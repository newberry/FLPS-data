On Saturday March 27, the Scandinavian Mixed Chorus invites its many friends to attend its spring concert at North West Hall, on North Avenue. The Scandinavian Mixed Chorus has throughout its five years of existence proven itself one of the best singing societies in Chicago, and the Chorus may be relied upon not to disappoint its friends this time.

The Magneten (Magnet), the singing society of the Danish Brotherhood, will assist. This singing society has made marked progress of late.
