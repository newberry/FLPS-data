p. 3.- The "Scandinavian Literary Society" which has existed for a number of years is about to be dissolved because of an ever increasing debt. The fee has been so high that only well-to-do people could afford to join. One of the reasons for the failure of the club was, that all three Scandinavian nations did not have an equal number of members on the board. More and more Swedes withdrew from the Society yet insisted upon having the same number of members on the board. The Norwegians strongly protested.
