Last Saturday the Danish colony was given a big surprise. Mr. [S. T.] Corydon, closed his department store. He laid off all his employees, paying them two weeks salary. All his merchandise was sold to Wieboldt's.

Mr. Corydon, who is sixty-nine years old, has retired from business life. He expects to go on a trip to Florida for the winter, and to take a long earned rest.

Mr. Corydon was well known on the Northwest Side. He was always very charitable. He was knighted by the Danish King.
