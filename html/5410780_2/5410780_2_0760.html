The Singing Society Magneten (The Magnet) has elected the following persons for its new board of directors: Rolf Rasmussen, president; Ole Berg, vice-president; Rahbeck Moller, secretary; Rosendahl Nielsen, treasurer; Knud Jensen, manager; and M.R. Ackerman, auditor.

The past year has been one of success; during it many new members joined, thus strengthening the financial standing of the Society.
