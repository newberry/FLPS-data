p.4...The Danish accompanist, T. Fredriksen, a graduate of the Copenhagen Conservatory of Music, who recently arrived in this country, is at present in Chicago, as an instructor at Drake's School of Music, Auditorium Building, Wabash Ave.

Mr. Fredricksen was present at Ebenezer Church, Rockwell St. and Wabansia Ave. last Sunday and played a couple of solo numbers on the organ, much to the pleasure of the congregation.

Surely, the Danish colony in Chicago has gained a valuable addition in this distinguished artist.

His home is at the North Cape Building, 2623 W. North Ave., where he receives pupils for instruction in piano, organ and violin.
