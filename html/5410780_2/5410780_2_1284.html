Danes on Chicago's north-west side are invited to join the "Freja" Gymastic Club organized on Friday, Oct. 22nd, with eighteen charter members. Training sessions are to be held on Tuesday and Friday evenings at Rockwell Hall, Rockwell and Noffat Sts., where all Danes will be welcome. For information regarding dues, Program, etc. apply to H. Olsen, 2510 Moffat St., Secretary.
