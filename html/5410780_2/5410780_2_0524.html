Mrs. Carrie Hasselriis [Dane], a well-known Chicago singer, gave a concert in the Handel Music Hall last Sunday.

Mrs. Hasselriis' program included songs composed by Thomas, Schubert, Ole Bull, Saint-Saens, Elgar, Heise, Grieg, Del Riego, Schumann, and Denza.

The composers, Felix Borowski and Adolph Boune, were Mrs. Hasselriis' teachers. She gave her first concert in Chicago when she was only nineteen years old.
