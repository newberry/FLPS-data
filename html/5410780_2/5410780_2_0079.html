Selskabet De Danske Vaabenbrodre (The Society of the Danish War Veterans) was organized April 2, 1876. It has 110 members, and it meets every second and fourth Wednesday of the month in Wabansia Hall, 1710 North California Avenue. It has a library of 600 volumes. The secretary is Alfred Hansen, 2121 North Kedvale Avenue. The aim of the Society is to keep alive the memories and comradship of the old days of war.
