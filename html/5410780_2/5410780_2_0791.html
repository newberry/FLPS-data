A new singing society has been started, the Scandinavian Socialist Singing Society. The Society has been organized by the Socialist groups on the West Side, and it aims to sing at street meetings and public entertainments arranged by any socialistic organization. The leader of the chorus is Mr. Berggren, who is also the leader of the Scandinavian Workers' Singing Society. The Chorus will have its first concert and dance at Wicker Mark Hall on October 8.
