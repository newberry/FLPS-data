p. 2. col. 4-5... The corner stone to the Danish Children' Home was laid Sept. 17 in the presence of about 2,000 Danes. Rev. H. C. Rordam, the president of the committee, bid welcome. The united Danish choir sang under the leadership of Kristian Nilsson. Rev. Victor L. Greenwood of Chicago Commons spoke in English about the importance of Danish culture. Rev. Bodholdt, president of the Danish Church in America, spoke about the spiritual values the emigrants had brought with them from Denmark. In the box that was placed under the corner stone was the following: The annual report of the Danish Church. A list of all donators to the Home. The names of all members on Hans Christian Andersen's committee, a medal with the picture of Hans Andersen. And the following papers: 1. Revyen, Chicago 2. Daily News, Chicago. 3. Den Danske Pioneer, Omaha. 4. Kirkelig Samler. 5. Dannevirke, Cedar Falls, Ia. 6. Chicago-Posten, Minneapolis, Minn. 7. Skandinaven, Chicago. There will be room for about 40 children when the home is finished.
