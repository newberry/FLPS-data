Heldt Henriksen, the well-known Danish sculptor, and Mrs. Henriksen celebrated their 25th Wedding Anniversary last Thursday.

When a delegation from D. B. S., No 18, called at their home to present them with the traditional silver dining set, the busy Mr. Henriksen was in his work-shop, "dressed" in overalls.....

Besides raising a large family, Mr. Henriksen has built up a considerable business with his much sought-after sculptures.
