Another young Dane died only 23 years old. He died from typhoid fever which he got in Cuba, where he went with the First Illinois Infantry Regiment.

Peter Damgaard was born in Kolding, Denmark, 1875. He came to this country with his father and mother in 1884; after about ten years farming the family moved to Chicago, where he joined the army.

He was buried with full military honors at Oakwood cemetery.
