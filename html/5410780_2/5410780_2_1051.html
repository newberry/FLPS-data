Christian Botker, Danish editor, was born at Gunslev, Falster, Denmark, March 4, 1866. Graduated as teacher in Public School from Blaagaards Seminarium at Copenhagen, 1885. Wrote for Aftenblndet. Assistant editor (Redaktionssekretaer) at Soro Amstidende and Venstrebladet, Copenhagen, 1889-91. Worked at a terra cotta factory at Perth Amboy, 1891-92. Editor of Chicago-Posten, 1892-1894. Publisher and editor of Revyen since 1895.
