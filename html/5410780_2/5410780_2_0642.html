page 4 col. 3-4...The Danish play "Ambrosius" arranged by the Danish actor, John Herskind from Copenhagen, was played on April 25th in the North Side Turner Hall for an unusually large crowd with 7-800 persons present.

"Ambrosius" was very well played by Mr. Herskind himself. Abigael was played by Mrs. Hartnack. The other characters were played by: Mr. and Mrs. Peter Boas, Th. Sommer, A. Frandsen, A. Rasmussen, Edw. Petersen and Hans. Didricksen.

The dresses and the decorations were beautiful, and the hall was fine for acting and hearing.
