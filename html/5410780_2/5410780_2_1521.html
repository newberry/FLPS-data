The guarantee fund has (October 1, 1899) United States Ponds to the value of $5,081.05. On October 1, 1899, the Brotherhood had 117 paid-up lodges with a total membership of 6,253, and insurance of, $6,620.16. There were 260 new members for the July quarter. In the July quarter $7,000 was paid out in insurance. Death Benefits totaled $250 to brothers who lost their wives; sick Benefits $3,371.94; and emergency assistance [Extra Hjaelp] to brothers in need was $820.65.
