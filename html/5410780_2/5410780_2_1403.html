Danmarks Sonner (Sons of Denmark) was started in 1894. On February 10, 1914, the society celebrated its twentieth anniversary at the Metropolitan Hall. Dr. Max Henius, who is an honorary member, spoke for the society, Christian Nielson for America, and S. Heilbuth for Denmark. The aim of the society is mutual aid in sickness and death, and entertainment.

Danmarks Sonner meets the second and fourth Thursdays of the month at Boyschous' Hall, at the corner of North and Western Avenues.
