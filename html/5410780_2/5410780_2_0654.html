The Danish community has finally agreed that its much discussed theatre will be officially opened this week.

Those who have taken active part in this project are Mrs. Bluhme, Mrs. Jensen, and Mr. Herskind. The manager will be Mr. P. G. Nielsen, whose experience guarantees the success of the enterprise.

The stock company attached to the theater will produce plays by Ibsen, some German and some English productions. They will also rehearse a great many light operas.
