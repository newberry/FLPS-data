On November 10, 1901, Harmonien celebrated its fifteenth anniversary with a concert in Studebaker Hall. Mr. F. Frandsen was the baritone soloist of the chorus. Miss Harriet M. Hertz sang several Danish and German songs. Mr. P. Marinus Paulsen played his violin. Among the selections which he played was a composition of his own, "Fantasie on Danish Airs".
