"Jens Jensen, the well known landscape artist, was born in Denmark in 1860. He came to Chicago in 1884 and was employed by the West Park System. Later he was appointed superintendent of Humboldt Park. He resigned in 1900 and practiced landscape architecture for six years, when he was recalled to the West Park System. After he resumed his profession he gained national reputation. He is now the most widely known landscape artist in the Middle West. Mr. Jensen is a man of pleasing personality. He loves nature, and its preservation and beautification is his life's hobby."
