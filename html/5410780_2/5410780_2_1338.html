Dr. Max Henuis on behalf of two hundred Danish-Americans on September 1st, purchased for 20,000 Kroner a piece of heather in Rebild Hills. The park is to be given to the Danish state and must never be cultivated; it takes in 1,000 acres of land. On the deed will be written the name of all persons who sent money for this purpose to consul C. H. Hansen, Chicago.

Rebild Park is half an hour's ride by railroad south of Aalborg and 15 minutes' walk from Skorping station.
