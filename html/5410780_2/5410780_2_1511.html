A new lodge of the Danish Brotherhood was started in Chicago Sunday, January 13, Lodge No. 140, Moreland, Chicago. Present were the supreme secretary, Viggo A. Danielsen, and Mr. W. Kikkebusch, president of Lodge No. 17.

The new board was elected from among the twenty-four charter members. Holger Stuhr was elected president; Rolf Rasmussen, vice-president; John Scroeder, secretary; and Andrew Jensen, treasurer.
