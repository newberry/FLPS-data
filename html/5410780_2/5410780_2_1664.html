The Danish Old People's Home Society celebrated its twentieth anniversary in Wicker Park Hall last Wednesday. The following outstanding program was rendered:

(1) "Salute to Denmark" Weldom
(2) "Le Postillion de Lorymean" Tobani by Orchestra
(3) "Valentine's Prayer of Faust" Gounod by Jorgen Dahl
(4) Festival Speech by Carl Antonsen
(5) "Honor March" (March of the D.B.S. [Danish Brotherhood] No. 17) Agnes Mathiesen by Orchestra
(6) "Valse de Consert" Alard by Miss Christine Nisted
(7) Selections from Sullivan's Opera "Mikado" by Orchestra
