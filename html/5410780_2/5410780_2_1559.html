The Children's Home, at 2846 Cortez Street, is owned by the Danish Church. The Reverend A. V. Andersen is the president and Mrs. Anna Petersen is the superintendent.

On January 1, 1914, the Home had 23 boys and 12 girls. During the year 8 children left and 11 were admitted. During the spring of 1915 four more boys were taken in. Three girls were confirmed.

Mr. Jens Nielsen, who recently died in Chicago gave his whole fortune to the Children's Home. The sum total is estimated to be about $13,000.
