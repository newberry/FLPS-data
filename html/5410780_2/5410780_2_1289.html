A wrestling match took place October 8 at Holter's Hall, 48th and Erie St., between the young Danish wrestler, carpenter Knud Petersen and Hamid Kalla Pasha, "The Terrible Turk." To the great surprise of many, the Dane won the prize of the evening, $100; he won on points after half an hour's fight. Knud Petersen lives at the Danish Young Peoples' Home on the West Side. He will soon be leaving for Coepnhagen, where he will take part in another wrestling match on November 5th.
