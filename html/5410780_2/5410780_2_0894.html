De Danske Folkedansere (Danish Folk dancers) was started June 5, 1913. Its aim is to further Scandinavian folk dances in America. Its president is A. S. Narkow, 3717 Wrightwood Avenue. Frederikke Sorensen is secretary. The fee is one dollar for every month in which dancing lossons are given by Mrs. Tousing-Thomsen. The ladies pay only fifty cents per month. Passive members pay fifty cents per year.
