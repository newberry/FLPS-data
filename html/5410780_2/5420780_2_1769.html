The report from the committee struggling to free Niels Kjar (leading Danish-American communist), is as follows: The resolutions sent to Scandinavian organizations, by the committee, in most cases, were adopted by acclamation.

Many organizations had protested that they would have nothing to do with politics, but later decided that this was not a political issue, and voted for the resolutions.

Niels Kjar, sends his thanks, through a number of newspapers, to the forty Danish and many other societies that adopted the various resolutions.

The Danish National Committee went into action wholeheartedly.
