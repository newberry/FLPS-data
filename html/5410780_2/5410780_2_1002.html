The little library of Trinity Church has not been used very much the last five or six years. The library has books by the following Danish authors: Hans Christian Andersen, Carit Etlar, Holberg, Hostrup, Ingemann, Kaalund, Paludan Moller, Zakariar Nielsen, Valdemar Rondam and C. A. Thyregod. The library also has a few English books.

Mrs. Johanne Petersen, 2846 Cortez St., is the librarian.
