As a part of its program of presenting the world's greatest usical works, executed by its finest operatic talent, the Grand Opera Company will give a special Scandinavian concert to-morrow at 3 P.M. in the Auditorium Theatre.

The soloists are the Swedish Prima Donna Mme. Julia Claussen, Rudolph Ganz, the pianist, and Joel Mosberg, the baritone. The local Swedish singing societies,with a selected choir of one hundred voices, and the Grand Opera orchestra will assist.

The program consists exclusively of Scandinavian music; Cade, Grieg, Svendsen, Bellman, Lindblad, and others, and the concluding number will be Land-Kjending (Sighting Land), in which the entire ensemble, choir, 2soloists, and orchestra, will participate.

Such a choice selection of Scandinavian music, interpreted by such outstanding dramatic and musical talent, has never before been offered in Chicago.
