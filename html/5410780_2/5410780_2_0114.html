Dr. Townsend of California, who with his Old Age Pension plan of two hundred dollars per month, has by far outdistanced "Utopia" Sinclair and "Papa" Coughlin. Townsend arrived in Washington with fifteen million signatures on the petitions in favor of the genial (?) discovery.

This sounds almost like a fairy tale, but truth is stranger than fiction, and it proves that people will "bite" on any bait which may prevent depression. A mass demonstration, of course, will have its effect on a more or less half-witted Congress.
