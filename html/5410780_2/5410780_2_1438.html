The Socialist Sick Benefit Society (S. S. S) has published its report for 1912, according to which its membership, as of January 1, 1913, is 489.

During the past year $2,957 was paid out in sick benefits, and $845.50 for funeral expenses. The building fund amounts to $1,835.43, and the library has on hand $186.68.

The general assessment for 1912 was $6. per member and $2. for the funeral fund.
