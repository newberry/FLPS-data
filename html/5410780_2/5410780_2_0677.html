Danis-Harmonien arrived in Copenhagen, Denmark on "Hellig Olav." With them were George P. Jensen, president of Dania; Hans. Chr. Erichsen, president of the Danis-Harmonien Committee, and many others.

June 10, 1930 Harmonien gave a concert in the Tivoli, Copenhagen, Denmark. The choir has only 22 male voices. They sang several Danish and English songs and were complimented on their good Danish pronunciation.
