p.2.col.4.... The Scandinavian American Medical Society convened at "The Tavern" on Thursday P.M.,Oct. 21st,for their annual meeting. Our countrymen Dr. Svenning Dohl and Dr. Andreas Klorstad were elected President and Vice-president, respectively.

An elegant dinner followed the business meeting and was attended by over forty of Chicago's prominent Scandinavian men of medicine. Dr. Dahl introduced the speaker of the evening, Judge Going of the Chicago municipal court. Judge Going spoke very highly of the ability of our Scandinavian doctors and their contribution to the betterment of life conditions even outside the medical benefits. An honored out of town guest was Dr. A.S.Hansen, Danish physician and surgeon from Cedar Falls, Iowa.
