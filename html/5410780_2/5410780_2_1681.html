The Scandinavian Socialist Singing Society is holding its annual spring festival in Wicker Park Hall Saturday, March 23. Co-sponsors of the festival are the 12th & 17th Wards' Scandinavian branches of the Socialist party. Mr. George Koop, the party's candidate for Mayor will speak. Soloists Eugene Ljunggreen, pianist, and E. Jensen, violinist, will play. The Scandinavian Workmens' Singing Society, German Langerbund and La Salle Frauen Choir will sing.
