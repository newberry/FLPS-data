S. T. Corydon, owner of a great department store, was born February 27, 1868 at Sleswig. Learned his trade in Haderslev. To America in 1889. Started his own business in Chicago in 1899. He is very patriotic and was treasurer for the committee for help to the Sleswegians.

(Mr. Corydon closed his store on North Avenue in 1936 and retired to his estate at Miami, Florida.) J. E.

He became Ridder of Denneborg for his work in connection with relief for the Danes in Sleswig.) J. E.
