The Scandinavian Song Festival arranged by the three Scandinavian consuls will take place at Orchestra Hall May 26 and 27. The following Danish singing societies will participate: Harmonien, Magneten, and Skandinavisk Arbejder-Sangforening. The United Danish Choir will, under the leadership of Joel Mossberg, sing Heise's Volmerslaget. There will be a thirty-five piece orchestra, with Hans Nelson as director.
