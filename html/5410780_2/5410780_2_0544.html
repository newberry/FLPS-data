Axel Skovgaard, the well-known Danish-American violinist, and his orchestra are on a tour through Wisconsin, Minnesota, Iowa, South Dakota and Nebraska during which they will give about eighty concerts. The tour is scheduled to terminate May 15 when Mr. Skovgaard sails for Europe.

From October 1, 1907 till April 1, 1910 Mr. Skovgaard will be engaged by the Mutual Lyceum Bureau of Chicago.
