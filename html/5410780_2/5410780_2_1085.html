There has recently been a change at the Chicago office of the Danish Pioneer, Omaha, Nebraska. The general agent, Rene, and the correspondent, Carl Antonsen, were fired or retired.

In their stead P. Thornum was appointed as general agent and A. C. Neilsen as correspondent.

[Translator's note: There has always been much jealousy between the Pioneer and Revyen. The Pioneer, which has eight pages and is written mostly for the farmers of the Middle West, has a whole page with news from the Danish colony in Chicago.]
