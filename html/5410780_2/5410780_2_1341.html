Chicago Lodges No. 18, 35, 58, 61, 107 and 140 of the Danish Brotherhood have the permission of the Supreme Board to collect money all over America for a monument for the late Supreme Secrerary, Mr. Viggo A.. Danielson.

The monument is to be erected in Mount Olive Cemetary at a cost of five hundred dollars. The committee has the following members: L.G. Cornelius, president; Claus Jensen, treasurer; and Christian Nielsen, secretary.
