M.N.Jensen Hundsberg, who has been a teacher in Denmark, will conduct a Danish vacation school in Ebenezer Danish Lutheran Church, Rockwell St. and Wabansia Ave. three days a week, Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 9:00 to 11:30 A.M. for Young children, and from 1:30 - 4:00 P.M. for older children. Danish, Religion, Song Music and drawing will be taught. All children are welcome, and the instruction is free.
