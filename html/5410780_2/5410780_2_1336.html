The new high school which is being erected at 114th Street and Stewart Avenue, at a cost of $700,000 is to be named Christian Fenger High School by decision of the Chicago Board of Education

Undoubtedly our countryman on the School Board, Dr. P. C. Clemensen, has been instrumental in causing the Board to pay this tribute to the memory of the famous Danish-Chicagoan.

Another public school is already named for another Dane, the author and poet H. C. Andersen.
