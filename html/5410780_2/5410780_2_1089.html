The Norwegian-Danish Press Association, which has existed for two years, held its annual meeting here in Chicago. Mr. Oppedal, Editor of Norden, is Vice-President. Christopher Botker, Editor of Revyen, went to the meeting as he wanted to join, but when Mr. Oppedal saw Mr. Botker, the former became angry, asked him if he was invited, and told Mr. Botker that the meeting was open to gentleman of the press.

(In the same article we are informed that there are only two Danes in the above association; C. Rasmussen and Axel Kringelbach, editor of "Spogefuglen". Both Editors are living in Minneapolis.)
