p.4............Mrs. Clara Weldon, nee Holm is dead in Chicago. Mrs. Weldon who was born in Copenhagen, was a member of The Old People's Home Society and was much interested in everything Danish. Her husband is the well-known American music director, Weldon. She was recognized as America's first lady cornetist. An orchestra of forty-five men played at her funeral. Many of the prominent Danish ladies were seen among the mourners.
