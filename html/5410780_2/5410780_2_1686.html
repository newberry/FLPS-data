A dedication festival for the Danish Old People's Home was held in Wicker Park Hall, December 3. The main speaker was Reverend Adam Dan from the South Side. He had also written a song that was sung by the audience. The entertainment of the evening was presented by prominent Danes. George Dupont-Hansen played the piano; Mr. Oscar Rubardt and Miss Dora Bartholdy sang; Marinus Paulsen played the violin. Music was provided by Ole Nielsen's orchestra. Mr. Monroe Fulkerson made a speech in English.

A fine printed program was given by Consul C. H. Hansen and sold for the benefit of the Home.
