Chicago's Expert Wig and Toupee Maker, the Dane, John M. Seerup, has moved his business from the Old Omaha Building, which is now being demolished, to a larger and up-to-date shop in the Consumers' Company building, 220 So. State Street.

Mr. Seerup is the originator and sole manufacturer of the ribbonless toupee with reinforced parting, which draws customers from every state in the union to his shop; even in foreign countries, highborn ladies and gentlemen are proudly wearing the Danish wig maker's creations.
