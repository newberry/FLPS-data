p.2...Ivan Kirkegaard's Norden, which for several years has been plugging along as a monthly, is now being issued semi-monthly, without change in size or price. The fact that Norden has been able to take this step is a sure sign the work of its energetic, able publisher and editor, has won the appreciation of the public; a feeling that is bound to grow now that the paper comes twice as often, and thus is in a position to offer, not only more, but more interesting and diversified reading material, such as letters and news from Denmark.
