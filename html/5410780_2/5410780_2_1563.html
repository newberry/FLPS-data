Next Tuesday, October 14, has been chosen as the annual Tag Day for the benefit of underprivileged children in Chicago.

The Danish Children's Home is represented as usual, and has been allotted certain districts and one hundred women workers, who will be active in the field.

A meeting was held last Thursday in the basement of the Trinitatis Church, in order to organize the Danish participation, and it is hoped that the home will benefit by two thousand dollars.
