The last issue of Norden (The North) contains the biographies and pictures of the well-known artists Miss Clara Jensen, soprano, and Miss Nathalia Jensen, violinist.

There is also an article accompanied by picture, about Miss Florence Bodenhoff, who came to Chicago about a year ago, and still is little known among the Danish public. She received her musical education in Copenhagen, and with her beautiful mezzo-soprano should be assured of a successful future here.
