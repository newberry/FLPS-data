The Norwegians, Swedes, and Danes held a big banquet in Scandia Hall in preparation for the concert to be given by the Scandinavians of Chicago at the World's Fair, in the big hall in Jackson Park. They must have at least one thousand singers for this event.

The banquet was arranged by a committee consisting of W. Colberg, I. Jager, Niels Juul, and John Riis. Eight singing societies were represented on the committee.
