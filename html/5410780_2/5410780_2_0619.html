T. Koefoed, Bjornsjold's impresario, will stop over in New York to play the leading part in "Jeppe Par Bjerget" (Jeppe on the Hill). It is said that Koefoed's portrayal of "Jeppe" is second to the Royal Danish actor Olaf Povlsen. We remember Koefoed's portrayal of "Elverhog" at the Chicago Opera House, which set the record for Danish professional theatricals in America.
