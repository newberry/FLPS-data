p.4.........The Cemetery Association for members of the Danish Brotherhood conducted its yearly stockholder's meeting last Monday. The president's report stated that the association had paid over$1300.00 on its cemetery lot at Mount Olive Cemetery. The debt is now only a little over $400.00.

The association has progressed during the last year. There are still quite a few unsold lots, and an option is held on additional 5000 square feet, bordering the property now owned by the association.

It is to be hoped that the plans concerning the erection of a beautiful and imposing monument, on the association's cemetery lot will be brought to an early completion.
