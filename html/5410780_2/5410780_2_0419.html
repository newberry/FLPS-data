p.2..........Ex-justice, attorney Valdemar Bauer has invented a device, which enables a musician to turn the pages of a musical note book, by touching a rubber pedal with his foot. The apparatus, which functions perfectly, will undoubtly be very useful and convenient, particularly for people playing the piano and certain other instruments, and a large sale can be counted on.

"Automatic Music Leaf Turner," has of course been patented and a company, the Alexia Manufacturing Company, capitalized at $10,000.00, has been organized for the manufacture of the device.
