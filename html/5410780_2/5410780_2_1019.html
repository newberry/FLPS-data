The late Dr. Fenger was the subject of a talk by Dr. Nils Johnson at a business meeting of the Scandinavian Physician Society. Among other prominent members at the meeting were Dr. Svening Dahl and Dr. Ludvig Herton. During the meeting the following were elected: Dr. Carl Nielsen, president; Dr. Oldenburg, secretary; Dr. Jern, cashier; Dr. Meyer, chairman.

The main speaker was Professor Wille of Christiania.
