The pianist Alice Petersen, daughter of Col. N. P. Petersen, of Pierce Avenue, who some time ago won a gold medal, has recently been awarded another one as first prize in the graduating class of the Chicago Musical College. This prize has won in competition with nine hundred classmates, comprising music students from all over the new and the old world.

Another testimony of Miss Petersen's ability is the fact that the is now instructing no less than thirty-two piano pupils at her home.
