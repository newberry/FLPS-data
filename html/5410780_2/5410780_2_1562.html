The Danish Lutheran Children's Home 3220 Evergreen Avenue, was started by the late Reverend A. S. Nielsen, in 1884. It belongs to The Danish Church. The Reverend Mr. A. V. Andersen is its president. Mrs. Anne Hansen is superintendent and Miss Martha Lund, the teacher. There were thirty-eight children in the Home in 1913.
