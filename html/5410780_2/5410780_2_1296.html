Hundestejlean, the prominent Danish fishing club at Fox Lake, held its annual meeting recently. Newly elected officers are C. M. Friis, president; C. O. Cortzen, vice-president; L. Ingwersen, treasurer; and Architect Carl Nielsen, secretary.

On January 29 the club conducts a ball and banquet, but in July it holds its really large event, which includes boat races on the lake.
