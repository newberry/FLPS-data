The concert given in the Bethel Church, Dickens Avenue and Humboldt Boulevard, was a great success.

Professor Alfred Paulsen is the choir director, the Church organist, and the conductor of the largest Scandinavian orchestra in Chicago. He has been the director of the Norwegian Glee Club and some of the largest Danish choruses.

Paulsen is a composer of orchestral music, and when we hear "Naar Fjordene Blaaner" [The Blue Fjords]. we recognize that he can write music.
