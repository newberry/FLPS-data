p.4.....Tonight the Danish Worker's Singing Society will give a concert and dance in Wicker Park Hall. The large choir consisting of good and well trained voices will be directed by Mr. Starch. A talented Danish-American couple, performing under the pseudonym "The McNiels," will contribute to the entertainment by presenting a sketch, said to be very funny, entitled "She came from Sweden."

Miss Elevira Petersen and Mrs. Mathilde Huseby will sing a duet, and Carl V. Petersen's orchestra will play.
