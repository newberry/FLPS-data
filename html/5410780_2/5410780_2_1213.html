Scandinavian societies held a meeting Sunday night and Theo. Johnson gave a lecture on the subject:

"Socialism and Brotherhood" followed by lively discussion pertaining to the possibility for Socialism to spread all over the world and unite all nations regardless of race or color.

Tomorrow at 10 a. m. Mr. Juhl Christensen will speak on the subject "Reform or Revolution?"

The meeting will be held at the hall on Noble and Erie Sts.
