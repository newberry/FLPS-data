June 1, 1911 Harmonien gave a Smoker for the Danish Student Choir from Copenhagen University. The choir had fifty male voices, and their director was S. Levysohn, choirmaster at the Royal Opera of Copenhagen, Denmark. The soloists of the Choir were: Royal Opera Singer, Helge Nissen, bass-baritone and Olaf Holboll, tenor. These student singers travelled all over U. S. A. during May-June in 1911. They did sing in Orchestra Hall here in Chicago on May 21 and 22.
