The young actor, Franklin P. Bendtsen, who is a son of John S. Bendtsen, a Dane living on Campbell Avenue, has just been engaged by Robert B. Mantell's Theatre Company, which travels all over the United States. Mr. F. P. Bendtsen won The Joseph Jefferson Diamond Medal at the Chicago Musical College, School of Acting, last fall. Last Saturday's Saturday Evening Herald described him as a young man of sterling dramatic talents, of fine intelligence, and splendid devotion to his work.
