An executive committee has been organized in Chicago to expand the work of the American - Scandinavian Foundation. The members of the committee are as follows: the Swedes: Henry S. Henschen, H. Lanquist, Charles S. Petersen; the Norwegians: Herman F. Gade, Oscar H. Haugan, Birger Osland; the Danes: Max Henius, Carl Antonsen, Henry L. Hertz; and the Islander, C. H. Thordarson.
