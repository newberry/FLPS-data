Mr. Oscar C. Tofte, secretary since 1892 of the Danmark Singing Society, of North Troy, New York, insists that the Danish-American press is wrong in stating that Harmonien is the oldest singing society in America.

The Denmark Singing Society was founded in 1885, or one year before Harmonien. Danmark celebrated its fiftieth anniversary in 1935. It still has fifty-three active and passive members, of whom five have been in the Society since 1885.
