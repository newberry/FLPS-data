p. 110. Den Danske Laesekreds af 1913 (The Danish Reading Circle of 1913) was started in October, 1913. It has 22 members. C.H.W. Hasselriis is the secretary. The aim of the society is to make its members acquainted with modern literature and more specifically Danish language and literature.

A free lecture is given the first Wednesday of the month during the winter. Dr. Max Henius, Librarian J. Chr. Bay, and Rasmus Herlov, editor, have promised to speak this coming winter.
