M. Salmonsen, marriage clerk in Chicago, has written an essay in the October issue of the Danish periodical: Tilskueren. It is called Skiftende Stemninger and it deals with the last years expansion of America into the field of world politics.

(In this article we are also informed that Mr. Chas. Woodman is so far the only Dane who ever sat in the House of Representatives at Washington, D.C.)
