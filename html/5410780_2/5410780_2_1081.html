The old Danish socialist, P. Geleff, has started to edit a new paper, "Illustreret Bibliotek" (Illustrated Library).

(O. Geleff was one of the first socialist agitators in Denmark. He had to leave the country, when, he, with several others, tried to held a public meeting in spite of the policy. This lead to a clash between the police and the crowd. The leaders got the choice between jail or exile.) J. E.
