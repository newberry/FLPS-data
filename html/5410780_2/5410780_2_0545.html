Sunday, March 10, an International Concert will be given at Orchestra Hall under the auspices of the St. Ausgar church congregation.

A committee appointed for the purpose of making arrangements, is in full action. Members of the committee are; Mr. S. T. Corydon, president; Rev. K. Gjorup, vice-president, Dr. J. N. N. Nielsen, treasurer; Mr. Eskild Madsen, secretary, and Mr. Edw. Nielsen.

Only first class artists from the musical world are going to perform and a great many nationalities will be represented. The committee has secured the assistance of several great artists already and promise us a most excellent musical entertainment.
