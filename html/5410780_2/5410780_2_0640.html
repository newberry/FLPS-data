p.4......Under the above name there was last Tuesday, in the La Salle Hotel, formed a company, which will start its activities next fall. Almost everybody in the company are Danes. Mr. Anton Blangsted is the director, and is also financing the undertaking. Early in September, "Children in Church" will be presented; in October a fall revue, written by some of Chicago's well-known writers; in November the operetta, "The Merry Widow".

Mr. Blangsted asked us to say that he is not worried about the various attacks to which he has been subjected by envious people, and that he is prepared to spend a good deal of money to see his ideas materialized.
