He has been conducting physiological experiments at the University of Chicago, and is leaving next week for a vacation in Denmark. The young scientist received quite a lot of publicity recently In papers throughout the country. He will continue his experiments when he returns.

The purpose of the experiments, which were begun two years ago, is first, to determine a man's normal consumption of food calories, and normal working capacity; next to determine the minimum on which a man can exist without having his physical or mental power impaired. The experiments are scheduled to last another two years.
