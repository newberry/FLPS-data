The well-known Danish clothing store at 947-951 Milwaukee Avenue is to be enlarged. The store, which is now one of the largest in town, is to occupy all of the four-story building in which it is located. The store is managed by Albert W. Rixon and George Benson, son of the late P. Benson. The head salesman is Arent W. Johnsen, brother of our famous poet, composer, and editor of music, Volkmar Johnsen.
