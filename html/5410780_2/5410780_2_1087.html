The following Danish papers are not yet members of the Norwegian-Danish Association: Den Danske Pioneer, Fremad (in Marinette). Folkets Avis, Dannevirke, Dansk Folkeblad, Arbejderen og Revyen.

Moreover, the two best journalists of the Norwegian press, A. P. Lange of Scandia and H. A. Foss of Nye Normanden, are not members, either.
