At Danish Young Peoples' Home another meeting and discussion of the topic, "The Art of Living," was held last Sunday.

Reverend Gjorup was the first to speak and led the discussion in a religious direction. Other speakers were: Messrs. Michael Madsen, C. H. W. Nasselrus, L. Larsen, P. I. Langelykke and S. Gjorup. Carl Jacobsen was toastmaster. The next discussion will be on the subject "Social Ethics," with Mr. Juhl Christensen as the first speaker.
