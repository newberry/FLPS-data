On January 1, 1910, the Danish Brotherhood had 268 lodges with a total membership of 18,797. During 1909 the Supreme Lodge paid out $98,700 in insurance and $43,374 in sick benefits.

On January 1, 1910, the Brotherhood owned $98, 700.63 in bonds and $33,741 in cash. The Supreme Lodge's income for 1909 was $21, 757.32; expenses totaled $13, 537.13. Cash on hand as of January 1, 1910 was $26,029.44.
