S.T. Corydon, Chicago, the treasurer of the fund for the people of South Jutland (Den sonderjydske Indsamling) has sent to Denmark 18,000 kroner this Winter.

[Translator's note: The people of South Jutland suffered a great deal during the World War. Even though they hated Germany, they were German citizens and were forced to fight for Germany on all fronts.

S. T. Corydon was made Ridder of Dannebrog by the Danish King for his work in connection with this fund.]
