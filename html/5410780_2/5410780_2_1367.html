At the meeting, July 5, 1892, it was decided that the well-known Danish sculptor, Johannes Gelert, should be commissioned to do the monument [to Hans Christian Andersen].

There were still many differences within the committee. It was finally decided that the present committee was too large and lacked unity. It was proposed that the committee be made smaller and that only the really active members should remain. No account has yet been given of the collections and the money on hand.
