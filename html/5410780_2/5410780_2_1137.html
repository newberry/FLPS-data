Mr. J. Christian Bay, librarian, and his son Axel, together with a number of book lovers have organized The Prairie Land Publishing Company, with the thought in mind of publishing old and new books and manuscripts of value to western American culture. The firm's first book will be Young E. Allison's,"The Delicious Vice:" pipe dreams and fond adventures of novel readers among great books and their people. Allison was a friend of the artist Rohl - Smith. His criticism of Robinson Crusoe is known throughout the world.
