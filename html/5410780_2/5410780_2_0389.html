Our countryman, Mr. Carstens, Chicago manufacturer of surgical instruments, is going to Detroit early next month to attend the convention of surgical instrument manufacturers. There are only nine factories of this kind in America. The Carstens Factory is the largest in the Middle West and carries a line of 1,200 different instruments in silver, brass and steel. Recently Mr. Carstens has started production of some exceptionally fine steel knives.

Mr. Carstens, who is a nephew of the famous Danish painter Carstens, was born in Schleswig. He is a 36th degree Mason, and a man of the highest principles.
