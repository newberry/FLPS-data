De Danske Vaabenbrodre (The Danish War Veterans' Society) started April 2, 1876. The aim is to keep alive the mutual memories from the years in which they were soldiers in the Danish or American army. The Society meets every second and fourth Wednesday of the month in Wabansia Hall, 1710 North California Avenue. On July 1, 1913, there were one hundred twenty-five members. Cash on hand amounts to $500. The Society has a library of six hundred volumes.
