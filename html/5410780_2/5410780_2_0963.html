p. III. Sydsidens danske Laeseforening (The South Side Danish Reading Club) was started on November 26th, 1894. It has 58 members, the dues are $1.00 per month. The club owns 400 volumes. Its president is Mr. Arnold Rasmussen, 6208 Honore St.

The original name of the club was "Klubben Enighed" (The Club Unity). In 1896 it joined the Socialist Labor Party and become known as "Scandinavian Section No. 3" But the party suffered a split and the labor paper "Arbejderen" had to be discontinued, and the name of the club was changed to its present name.
