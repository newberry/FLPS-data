page 2 col. 3-4... The famous Danish journalist, Jacob A. Riis, gave a lecture in the Auditorium Theatre, November 11. The meeting was arranged by the mortgage committee of the Danish Old Peoples Home in co-operation with many Danish societies. Mr. Riis brought a greeting from the Danish King and told about his recent trip to Denmark. The editor of Revyen did not like all this talk about the old king of Denmark.

The profit of the evening was $2,000, the bazaar gave a profit of $1,500, the debt on the home was $5,000, there is thus left only $1,500, which the committee hopes to collect by free subscription.
