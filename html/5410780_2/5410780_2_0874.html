p.4.col.3........The play,Den Kaere Familie, (The Dear Family) given at the Garrick Theatre by Danish-American Society Home Talent was well attended at both matinee and evening performances, and netted the society $169.00.

Of this amount the following donations were made to: Danish Old Peoples Home $50.00; Danish Orphanage $50.00; and Trinity Church Organ Fund $25.00.

The balance of the $169.00 has not yet been alloted.

After the play the cast was taken to the Red Star Inn for a farewell party and supper.
