The Northwest Side Commercial Association has sent out an appeal for help for the victims of the storm which on March 23 destroyed many homes and other property in the Cragin district.

The treasurer of the emergency committee is Chiltong Collins of the Pioneer State Bank and the Danes are represented by J. C. Hansen, of the Security Bank, and S. T. Corydon.

The following Danish societies have made contributions: Daughters of Denmark, $25; Sons of Denmark $10; Denmark $10; Dagmar Lodge No. 4 of the Danish Sisterhood $25; and St. Ansgar Parish $10.
