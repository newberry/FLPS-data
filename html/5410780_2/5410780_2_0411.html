Mr. H. J. Henriksen died October 24, at the age of sixty-nine. He was born in Thisted, Denmark. When he came to Chicago, in 1871, he was a cabinetmaker by trade.

In 1874 Mr. Henriksen opened an undertaker's establishment on Milwaukee Avenue near Carpenter Street. Last spring his son Thomas took over the business, and moved it to 1081 West North Avenue.

Mr. H. J. Henriksen officiated at more Danish funerals in Chicago than any other undertaker. He was a member of many Danish societies, including Dania, Danish Brotherhood Lodge No. 18, and Danmark Lodge of the Knights of Pythias.
