As a result of his article in Twentieth Century Magazine, "Judicial Usurpation," which was also discussed in Revyen, our countryman Otto Christensen has received a short letter from Theodore Roosevelt, in which the ex-president compliments him on the interesting article and intimates that he will comment on it in the near future.

Whether this comment will be published in Revyen, Twentieth Century Magazine, or The Outlook, Mr. Roosevelt did not say; probably in the latter publication, of which he is associate editor.
