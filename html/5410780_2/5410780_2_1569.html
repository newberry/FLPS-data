The Danish Lutheran Children's Home will be dedicated Sunday, March 4. It is a two-story red brick building. The architect is C. F. Sorensen. The mason work has been done by S. N. Nielsen, the carpentry work by a Swedish firm, the painting by Henry Petersen & P. Jorgenson (a Danish firm), the plastering by a Norwegian firm, and the plumbing by L. E. Clausen, member of Lodge No. 18.
