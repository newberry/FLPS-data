The ex-state representative, Mr. Charles Ryberg, has organized The National Chronograph Company, with a capitalization of $100,000, to market one of his inventions. The machine he has invented registers the time for the opening and closing of stores, factories, etc., Mr. Ryberg announced that he is now out of politics, and therefore, with his wife has moved from Bickerdyke St., and established residence in the Alexandria Hotel, Ohio and Rush Sts.
