Dansk Tidende (Danish Times.), the new "intellectual paper," is a beautiful-looking sheet, the front page is fine, and the entire paper is well illustrated.

The publisher, H. Franck-Philipsen, a well-known chemist and inventor, has lots of cash and lots of ambition; so we feel that the paper will survive.

The editor-in-chief, Kristian Baun, is a well-known author, educator, poet, botanist, and entomologist. Mr. Baun can write Ph. D., D. D., and D. S. after his name; so the editorship is in good hands.

The first issues of the new journal were the best papers that we have seen in Chicago, without exception.
