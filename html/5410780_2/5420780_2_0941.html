The sixth Danish National Festival was celebrated on June 7, 1914. It gave a profit of about $1,500. It was decided to give $600 to the Aid and Relief Fund, $360 to the Danish Old People's Home, $250 to the Danish Children's Home and $100 to the Dr. Fenger Memorial Fund.

J. Jensen is the president of the National Committee for 1915. Mr. R. Egebergh is secretary. To this committee thirty-seven Danish societies in Chicago have each sent a delegate.
