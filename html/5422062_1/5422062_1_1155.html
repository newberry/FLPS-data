Capt. Zacharias Papanikolaou, who fought in the Balkan war, announced yesterday that he had formed a company of one hundred Greeks in Chicago willing to fight for the United States against Mexico should the government issue a call for volunteers.

He has addressed a letter to President Wilson embodying his offer. Capt. Papanikolaou hopes that in the event of the government accepting his services he will be able to raise a regiment of 3,000 Greeks from Chicago. His address is 500 South State street.
