Last Sunday in Athens, Greece, the cornerstone of the American College, was laid with every imposition. After the sanctification, which was performed by the Right Rev. Chrisomos, Archbishop of Athens, many dignitaries eulogized the initiative and the noble endeavors of the Greek-Americans and Americans, he contributed to the noble cause. Amongst the dignitaries was the President of the Greek Republic, Mr. Kountourietis, who said be is certain, that the Institution will fulfill the expectations of all concerned, and will create citizens of a higher human type.
