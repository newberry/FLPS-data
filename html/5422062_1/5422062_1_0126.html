p. 4.- Walter G. Hjertstedt, teacher at Roosevelt High School, inaugurated the plan of having his students correspond with students of other countries. School problems, ideas, and suggestions are to be interchanged and discussed. Mr. Hjertstedt has chosen Pete Psiharis to supervise the writing and receiving of all letters from Greece. P. Psiharis was chosen because of his extensive knowledge of Greek and his outstanding scholastic record. Roosevelt High School is in Ravenswood vicinity.
