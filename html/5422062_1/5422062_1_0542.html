The Association of the Greek Community of Chicago wants to thank the Ahepa for helping out the Greek schools, Socrates and Homer, by giving a dance. $1,059 was given to Socrates School and $279 to the school, Homer.

Chicago, Illinois, March 18, 1930. Association of the Greek Community of Chicago--

N. Kokkinis, president

Socrates School--Archrev. A. Dimakopoulos.

Homer School of St. Nicholas Church--

President, John Marlas.

Principal, Archrev. D. Gambrilis.
