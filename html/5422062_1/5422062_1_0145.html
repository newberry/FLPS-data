The Publication of the Lane Technical School in its edition of 28th of November, tells us with distinct emphasis that amongst the twenty-one gradutes were two Young Greeks, Lazaropoulos and Kanglis, who excelled graduating with honors. Such news of joy the Saloniki publishes with pride, Saloniki congratulates the parents of the students, and urges the Greek Youth to remember that they are the sons of Ancient Greece, and as such, must excell in everything, thus honoring their two mothers, Greece and America.
