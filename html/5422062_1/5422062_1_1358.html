p. 1.- Some time ago, Gust Terzakis fed about one hundred unemployed. The Tribune gave him front page publicity.

Terzakis was certainly benefited. But what are our Greek Restaurant Associations doing? Three thousand Greek Restaurants in Chicago, if they undertake to feed ten people per day, can accomodate thirty thousand souls.

Is there a bigger boost for us Greeks? God only knows that we need it.
