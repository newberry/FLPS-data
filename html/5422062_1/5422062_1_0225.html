The Hierarchic Synod of America invited all the Greek teachers in the vicinity of Chicago to attend a meeting at the Evangelismos Church to organize a teachers' union, the purpose of which is to raise the educational standards of the Greek schools of the Central States. It will also raise the educational requirements of new teachers.
