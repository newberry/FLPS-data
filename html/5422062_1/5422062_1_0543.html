p. 6.- We extend our grateful thanks to Woodlawn Chapter No. 93 for its kind and gracious act in giving thirty dollars a month to the teacher who goes three times a week to teach the Greek children of the Chicago Orphanage Asylum. This shows the true Christian spirit and we are very proud of these Ahepans.

Archrev. M.E. Petrakis, principal.

James Chrisos, President.

Nick Kavouras, Secretary.
