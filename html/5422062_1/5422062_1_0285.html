p. 5.- We respectfully inform the parents, the directors of the school and all the Greek people of Chicago, that owing to the termination of classes, there will be a school holiday program given, Sunday, June 14th, in the spacious hall of the school, 733 S. Ashland Blvd.

For this occasion we cordially invite all to come and honor with their presence the rites of our school.

Pantelis Papardis

Teacher.
