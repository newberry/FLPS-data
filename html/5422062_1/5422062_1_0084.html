p. 5.- The annual election of the Helicon, a fraternity composed of Greek students attending Colleges and Universities in Chicago, took place last Sunday at the Sherman Hotel, and the following were elected: Basil Belogiannis, Pres.; George Livanos, Vice-Pres.; Elias Michalopoulos, Sec.; Demetrios Iatropoulos, Treas.; and Basil Nestor, Dean.
