p. 5 Demetrios A. Geroulis a law student at De Paul University, was chosen as member of P.G.M. Fraternity.

This students' brotherhood was founded six years ago by Leroy Allen, Professor of Economics at Winfield College, Kansas. It does not aim to create a special class of society, but simply takes as members, those who have distinguished themselves in colleges and Universities all over the country.

Mr. Geroulis who will be graduated from De Paul next year, is one of the four honored students, chosen for P. G. M. membership; out of eight hundred students enrolled at De Paul University.
