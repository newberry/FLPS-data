A new enterprise has been added to the great number of Greek stores on Halsted street. This new establishment is situated at 526 S. Halsted St., north of Harrison Street. It is located in an ideal place to attract the proper customers.

C. Eliopoulus, N. Mais, and A. Vakalos have opened a combination drug store and musical establishment known as the Trianon. Drugs, musical instruments, victrolas, records, radios, wedding and baptismalsupplies etc., will be carried.

To our newly established friends on Halsted St., we wish the greatest and speediest success and we are sure that they will be strongly supported by all countrymen in the neighborhood.
