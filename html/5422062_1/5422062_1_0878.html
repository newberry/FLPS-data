p. 4- On last Sunday at the residence of our well-known, highly-esteemed, and well-to-do compatriot, Mr. Nickolaos Stathopoulos, occurred the christening of the son of Mr. and Mrs. Stathopoulos, who received the name of John, sponsored by Mr. Constantine Antonopoulos, Mr. Stathopoulos's partner in business.

After the christening, at which many friends were present, a dinner was served, and the celebration continued to midnight.

Saloniki extends to the parents its best wishes and hopes that the son will grow up to be as good a man as his father and his godfather.
