The recently organized society of the Greek Women's University Club, at its last meeting, elected the following officers:

Miss Catherine Miller, President; Miss Irene Harvalis, "ice-President; Miss Sophia Pernakis, Secretary; and Miss Helen Demetriou, Treasurer;

The newly elected board will prepare a program of discussions to take place, next month, at the University of Chicago. The topic, speaker, and date, are to be announced.
