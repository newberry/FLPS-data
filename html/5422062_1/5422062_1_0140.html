The Sorority Epsilon Phi Alpha which is comprised of Greek coeds, will give its annual dance Sunday, Jan. 20th at the auditorium of the church St. Andrews Hollywood and Winthrop avenues.

Greek maidens as a rule always present rich and unusual programs at their dances, but the Greek coeds, being sophisticated, promised something new and a well seasoned program for their annual dance. Come and see. Price per ticket, one dollar.
