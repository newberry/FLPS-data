p. 5.- Peter Tatanis, editor and publisher of the Ethnikos Kyrikos, (National Herald) of New York, was graciously welcomed to our city. Hundreds of Chicagoans, desirous of meeting the man responsible for such a great newspaper, visited him at the Palmer House where he is staying.

He has been shown the sights of the city and has been guest of honor at many affairs. Among his hosts are G. Chiagouris, P. Demos, J. Raklios, N. Nimokos, and J. Papanastasiou.

Mr. Tatanis is very much surprised and pleased at the number of social gatherings we have in Chicago.
