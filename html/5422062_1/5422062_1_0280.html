In order to facilitate its educational program for the Greek-American generation, the executive council of the school Socrates has resolved to keep the school open during the summer months.

Parents and guardians are urged to utilize this opportunity and enroll their children for the summer classes. School hours are 8 to 11:30 A.M. Fees are very reasonable.
