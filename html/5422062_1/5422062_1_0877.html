The Bishop of the Diocese will officiate at the ornate Liturgy of St. John Chrysostom, which will take place in Saint Constantines on the South Side Sunday, December 2, 1923.

During the Liturgy Dr. Michael Galanos will speak on the Golden Rule, about which so much has been written in all newspapers.

After the Eucharist, a memorial service will take place in memory of the famous Greek literary scholar, Artistides Foutrides. This commemoration is sponsored by the Greek students of the Universities in Chicago and the Greek School Koraes. Speakers of the day will be the Bishop of Chicago, the Rt. Rev. Philaretos, and the doctors P. Harris and Stevens, Professors from the University of Chicago.

(The Executive Committee of Saint Constantine's Church)
