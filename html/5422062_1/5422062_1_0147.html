The idea of establishing in Athens a college according to the American system, as been enthusiastically from the contributions secured, so far, for the purpose and which amount to $450,000. We have, no doubt, that the Greeks of America also will hear the suggestion with the same enthusiasm and will liberally support it.

Athens was lacking such an institution, despite the fact that it is the capitol of the nation, and is considered to be the center of Greek letters. The Greek colleges do not import enough knowldege to their graduates because their main object is to prepare students for the University where they are taught a speech of profession.

For those, however, who do not desire to pursue a special profession there was no higher school of an encyclopedic education. For this reason, it was about time to establish such college of higher encyclopedic education.

Up to a few wears ago the diploma of a College was a token of wide knowldge, and the diploma of a University was the crown of knowlege. Today, however, 2the Greek college education is not enough to reader a person educated, and the American system of colleges is indispensable for the completion of the encyclopedic education. From this point of view the inecessity of such an institution of higher learning Athens was indispensable.

In addition, the teaching of the Greeks according to the American system in Athens, bring the two countries closer together. This must greatly stimulate the interest of the Greeks of America, who living for so many years were within America environment, ought to be interested for the development of American methods in Greece and, in fact, of everything beautiful that this country possesses.

The new Greek generation in America, in particular, who have had the opportunity to attend courses in, and graduate from, the American colleges will especially be pleased to hear of a collegiate institution in Athens and, with great enthusiasm, will also promote the completion of the job.

It should be the specific purpose of the Greeks in America to bring the two countries closer together, because such a contact would be beneficial to them both.
