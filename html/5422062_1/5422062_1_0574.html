The financial committee of the South Side Greek School, Koraes, in an effort to obtain funds for the School, visited various persons on the South Side and asked them to be generous and donate to this dignified and patriotic cause.

Their results are printed below.

N. Nomicus $15
Th. Prousianos 25
K. Giovan 25
H. Reckas 25

The committee wishes to express publicly its thanks to the contributors to this cause.
