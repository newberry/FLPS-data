p. 3 Nicholas Berkos, a graduate of Northwestern University, recently obtained his doctors degree. He distinguished himself in all his studies, and proudly wears his Phi Beta Kappa Key.

He owns his education to his proud father who immigrated here but adopted America as his future country, and worked hard to raise his family and educate his son, Nicholas.

Undoubtedly young Berkos, who is licensed to practice law in all the Illinois courts, will be one of the best lawyers in Chicago.
