The bootblacks of Chicago, recently organized, announced to-day plans for their first annual banquet, to be held in the Masonic Temple next Sunday night.

Speakers for the occasion will be Chairman P. S. Lambros, editor of the Greek Star, Judges Cook, Eberhardt, and O'Connell, and Alderman Funkhouser. The president of the association, organized four months ago, is George Prasinos. Other officers are A. H. Peponis, secretary, and George Kyriakopoulos, treasurer.
