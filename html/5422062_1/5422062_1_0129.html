The Greek students of the University of Illinois have formed an organization, which they call "Dades Hellenikou Politismou (Torch of Greek Civilization [the society is also called Delta Epsilon Pi]). Their purpose is to uphold the ideals of Hellenism and to preserve the Greek language and religion.

The organizers are Messrs. Mprouzas, Mbolas, Argyropoulos, Alexopoulos, and Kallianotis. At present the club has sixteen members.

In order to achieve the aims of their club, the students are giving a dance on Monday, December 30, which will take place at the Stevens Hotel. Every one should support this event as it is the first for the young club.
