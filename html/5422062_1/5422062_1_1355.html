p. 3.- Our well-known and well-beloved countryman, P. Moundreas, who has for four years furnished and decorated our restaurants, has obtained an important position in Jac Lederer, Inc.

Every Greek in Chicago know or has heard of Jac Lederer because he also has furnished thousands of our Greek restaurants in the last twenty-five years.

Both Mr. Moundreas and the Jac Lederer, Inc., will profit by this partnership.
