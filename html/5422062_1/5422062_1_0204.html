We are informed that the imputed sum of $10,000 for the Ahepan chapters in the Chicago district for the $100,000 scholarship fund is almost paid up. Up till today approximately $9,000 have been gathered.

In the coming week the various chapters selected their representatives for the coming convention in Kansas City, which will take place the latter part of August.

The representatives of the various chapters are as follows:
