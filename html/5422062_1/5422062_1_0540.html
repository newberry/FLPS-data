Contributions of $525 already sent in by various individual contributors, following an appeal sent by the executive board of the school Socrates.

The money is needed for the object of encircling the property of the school with an iron fence. The executive board, of this Greek-American school, is thankful to the contributors for their quick response and hope that the Greek community in general will assist in the present need of the institution.

Those who wish to contribute to the school, may communicate with the office of the parish, 1101 South Peoria Street.
