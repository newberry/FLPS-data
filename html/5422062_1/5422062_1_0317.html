A new school is being established on the South Side. The well-known Greek teacher, Mrs. Electra Zalouchos has furnished a schoolroom at 7909 South Park Avenue. It is to be called Likion o Socrates.

In order to make known to the public the scope and purpose of the school, an open meeting, to which all parents are invited, will be held Friday, Feb. 21, at 8 o'clock at the school.
