p. 5.- Fifteen-year old Anthony, son of James Smyrniotis, of Louka, Mantineias, who has attended Korais school, recently received the highest scholastic recognition in a class of 750 at the Sodus school.

During the entire school period Anthony has continually received the highest grades. His parents have every right to be proud of him. He is also a member of the Ypsilantes Chapter of the Sons of Pericles.
