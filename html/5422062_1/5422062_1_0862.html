We wish to announce to our pious fellow-countrymen that the litany for the miracle which occurred in the holy Church of St. Nicholas, will be recited next Sunday, May 13th, immediately after the holy mass.

Following the doxology, the litany will be recited with the saint's icon displayed, and the regular prayers will be said.

The salutations to the saint composed for the occasion by Archimandrite Ambrosios Mandilaris, will be sung. There will also be a sermon on the occurrence of the miracle.

Hymns to the saint will follow, sung by the well organized choir of women of the parish.

Every effort has been made that devout worshipers may enjoy spiritual peace and composure in the rich retirement of the Greek Orthodox Church.

Refreshments will be served to all immediately after the holy litany by the Women's Society of St. Nicholas Parish.
