Chapter "Hellas" of the "Daughters of Penelope" held its first meeting on Wednesday, June 15, at the Morrison Hotel, when twenty-five members were initiated. After the initiation, Miss Mary Pofanti, the President, spoke about the purpose of the club, which she outlined as follows:

To hold one dance every year, either at the Trianon or the Aragon, for the benefit of our Greek-American schools. Following this, plans were made to hold a dance on October 17 at the Trianon Ballroom.
