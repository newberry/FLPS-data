The Holy Trinity school committee, of which Mr. James Komninakis is president, has met and decided on the drafting of bylaws and a constitution for the newly organized Educational Society. The purpose of this organization will be to support the Greek schools.

Dr. Constantine Kallionzis, who has always been greatly interested in our schools and in every educational movement, has willingly undertaken, together with Messrs. Christ Damaskos and George Dedakis, to write the bylaws which will be discussed by the parents and guardians of the students at Hull House on December 7.
