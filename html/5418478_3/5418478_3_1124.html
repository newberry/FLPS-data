The theatrical group of Sokol Chicago selected "The Katakombs", a four-act comedy by Davis, for their first performance of the season. Mr. Weidner, veteran actor, directed the play in the auditorium of the Sokol Chicago, South Kedzie Avenue and 24th Street. Mrs. Slavka Svoboda, Mr. Lhotsky, and Mr. Weidner played the principal roles.

After the first act, Mr. Joseph Placek addressed the audience; he made a strong appeal in behalf of the Liberty bond campaign.
