There was a theatrical performance at V. Czech's Hall yesterday. This was a benefit affair for L.D. Vorak, who is a teacher at the Bohemian-American School.

The play would have been much better acted had there been less dependence upon cues.

It is also regrettable that so few members of C. S. P. S. (Czecho-Slovak Benevolent Society) were present in support of this worthy purpose.
