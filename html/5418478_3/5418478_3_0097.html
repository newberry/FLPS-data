The Klub Kollegu (Club of Colleagues), composed of our Bohemian businessmen of the Town of Lake district, held its annual meeting and election of officers December 9, and at this opportunity our charitable institutions were also remembered. Members of this club appropriated ten dollars for the Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage and a similar sum for the St. Joseph Orphanage of Lisle, Illinois. The following officers were elected: Mr. Adolf Metzl, president; Mr. Václav Čálek, vice-president; Mr. Pavel Beránek, secretary.....
