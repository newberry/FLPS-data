p. 1--The role of Denis in "Mamzell Nitouche," operetta, was played by Marie Ziegler yesterday at the Garrick Theatre.

She is a famous Bohemian opera star who has delighted the royal families, as well as people in large cities of Europe, with her wonderful singing and acting.

Marie Ziegler celebrated her triumph by proving that she really is a great opera star. Her performance fulfilled all expectations to the great satisfaction of the audience.

\2

She acts and sings in a natural way, with confidence in herself at all times while on the stage. Marie Ziegler played her role perfectly. Her singing and acting was outstanding.

Other roles in this operetta were played by Bohemian actors, but their performance could not be compared with that of Marie Ziegler.
