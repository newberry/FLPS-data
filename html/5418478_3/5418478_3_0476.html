The ambitious management of the Ceske Divadlo (Bohemian Theater Association) prepared for its public an unusual holiday treat last night by presenting the well-known operetta, "Almo, Kde Bydlis?" (Alma, Where Do You Live?), which some time ago ran so successfully on English and German stages, and for which even the police provided most effective advertising by prohibiting the performance for a time. Last night's performance was a fine success.....
