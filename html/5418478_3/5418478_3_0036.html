The American Czechoslovak Bankers Association of Chicago, Ill., held its annual meeting February 13th, 1924, at which the following officers were elected: -

Frank G. Hajicek, President,

Joseph J. Sabat, Vice President

John L. Movak, Vice President

John W. Jedlan, Secretary

Otto Kaspar, Treasurer

This organization has been functioning since 1919, and has rendered a large amount of service both to Czechoslovaks of this country as well as in Europe.
