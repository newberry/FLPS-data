The dramatic group of Sokol Chicago, so ably led by its director, Mr. Jindrich Weidner, has been particularly fortunate this year. The group's performances have constantly been given before sold-out houses, and the choice of plays has been pleasing to the public.....

In selecting the plays, Mr. Weidner likes to delve into the past of the Bohemian theater....as was demonstrated by yesterday's performance of "Jiříkovo Vidění" (George's Vision) and "Strakonický Dudásk" (Piper of Strakonice [town in southeast Bohemia]).....
