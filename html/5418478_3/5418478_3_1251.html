The board of the Cesko-Anglicka Svobodna Skola (Bohemian-English Free Thought School) on West 18th Street has made preparations for a bazaar beginning today and extending through November 9 and 10, 1922. There will be a rich program consisting of music. You may be able to win many beautiful and useful gifts for both young and old. Visit the bazaar, therefore, and rest assured that you will be satisfied.

The bazaar opened today and is for anybody who has money to spend. Wednesday is the day named for our merchants. Thursday is to be the day for the Bohemian Free Thought organizations, and Friday is for the Sokols. It should also be made known that an orchestra, consisting of twenty members of the musical department of the Sokol Slavsky, will perform gratis on Friday evening. On Saturday there will be a splendid ball to mark the ending of the bazaar. All friends of the Czech cause ought to attend.....
