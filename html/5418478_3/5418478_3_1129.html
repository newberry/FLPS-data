Chicago Czech amateur actors have united into a group which proposes to open a permanent stage in the hall of the Sokol Havlicek Tyrs gymnastic association, Lawndale Avenue near West 26th Street. The proceeds from the performances will be placed at the disposal of the Czech National Alliance for the cause of liberating of the old homeland. Mr. Krejci, a full-fledged actor, will be in charge of the artistic endeavors.....
