At the annual meeting of the Plzenska Pivovarni Spolecnost [The Pilsen Brewing Company], held January 25, a supplementary election of members of the Board of Directors was ordered and the following gentlemen were elected: Jan A Cervenka, Josef M. Skala, and Josef Hlavacek.

The directors then held a meeting and elected the following officers: chairman, Jan. A. Cervenka; vice-chairman, Tomas J. Seidl; secretary, Alois F. F. Jiruska; treasurer, Josef M. Skala.
