Last evening in the recital hall of the Auditorium, Miss Táňa S. Vojan, a daughter of the well-known journalist and present business manager of the J. Triner Wine Company, Dr. E. Salaba Vojan, formally introduced herself to our artistic colony as a remarkable pianist. That the young artist is really talented was known to her close friends, and was also indicated when, from time to time, she made public appearances as an accompanist. But yesterday's recital revealed to us the artistry of the debutante in a really splendid manner. Miss Vojan has at her disposal all the fundamental elements upon which the successful career of a virtuoso is built.....
