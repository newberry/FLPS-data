A new enterprise, called the "United Fish and Products Stores," operating on the order of the United Cigar Stores, will be established in the Bohemian settlements of the city. The law firm of Novak and Pollak sent the new company's articles of incorporation to the State Secretary yesterday. The company will be made up of Bohemians exclusively, and has a capital of $25,000.

A wholesale and retail business in fish, cheese, eggs, and all kinds of pickled meats, will be carried on.
