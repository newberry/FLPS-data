p. 1.--This morning at 8:30, Major P. V. Faltis, a prominent Bohemian, will arrive in Chicago on the Rock Island Railroad.

Major Faltis was formerly the court director of music and also an organizer of orchestras for the Egyptian khedive. Major Faltis is accompanied by eighteen Russian musicians who play the balalaika and wind-instruments. The orchestra will stay in Chicago for a week and will arrange to hold a number of concerts in Pilsen Park on West Twenty-sixth street.
