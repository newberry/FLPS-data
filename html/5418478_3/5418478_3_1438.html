On Nov. 14th, a Bohemian Day was held at the University of Chicago. It was preceded by a two-day exhibit of Bohemian art and books. Upon this occasion, a booklet was issued presenting in sixty pages Bohemia's contribution to civilization. Prof. Zmrhal discusses Bohemia's contribution to literature. Dr. Vojan writes on Bohemian music. Vojta Benes tells of Bohemian art and Dr. Fisher sets forth the ideals of the Sokols. Copies may be obtained from the Bohemian National Alliance, 3639 W. 26th Street, Chicago.
