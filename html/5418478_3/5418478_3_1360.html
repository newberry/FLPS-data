We hereby present a financial statement of the first Czechoslovakian Bazaar and Exposition which was arranged by the Ceskoslovenske Narodni Sdruzeni v Americe (Czechoslovak National Alliance of America), the Narodni Svaz Ceskych Katoliku v Americe (National Alliance of Bohemian Catholics of America), the Slovenska Liga (Slovak League), and the Cesky Umelecky Klub (Bohemian Arts Club) in Chicago, May 27-30, 1920.

The auditing committee, at its meeting on July 9, 1920, examined all the bills and items and prepared this statement, which is now being presented to the Czechoslovak people.
