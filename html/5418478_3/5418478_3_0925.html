A concert will be given by the music school of George Hrusa, on March 11, 1917, at 3 P.M. in the Masonic Temple Auditorium, Millard Avenue and 23 Street. The soloist will be Hattie Kovarik, piano; Louise Topinka, soprano. The orchestra is selected from mature pupils of the school and from members of the Chicago Federation of Musicians. The admission is fifty cents.
