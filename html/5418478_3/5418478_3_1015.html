P.1, Col.6--Our report of yesterday's successful concert, needs supplementing, and so it behooves us, to mention the solo, by Helen Lipert, which was one of the best numbers on the program.

Medals for zeal and talent were awarded to Antonia Maly, first gold medal, to Mary Ostrovsky, second, and to Josephine Petrzelka, third.

The silver medals were awarded to Bessie Kolar, first, to Mamie Horky, second, and to Florence Dill, third.
