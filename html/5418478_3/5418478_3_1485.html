The Patronat Ceske Svobodomyslne Skoly v Morton Parku (Board of Trustees of the Czech Free Thought Schools of Morton Park) are arranging a children's masked ball, to be held next Sunday at the hall of Sokol Karel Jonas.

The entertainment is to start at one o'clock in the afternoon. The admission charge is twenty-five cents for adults and fifteen cents for children. Every child presenting a coupon will get a prize.

It will be a splendid opportunity for all to have a pleasant afternoon, and the Czech parents in Morton Park will no doubt avail themselves of the opportunity.
