Yesterday afternoon there gathered in the home of Professor Vilim in Riverside a company of Bohemian artists. Among those present were Jaroslav Kocian, Boza Umirov, Frant. Spindler, Miss Clara Cermak, Miss Edna Crump, Frant. Horlivy, and Dr. Semerak. It is unnecessary to add that it was a most cordial entertainment.
