The convention of representatives of Illinois building and loan associations was opened at the Pilsen Auditorium yesterday....

President Frank G. Hájíček opened the program with an excellent, well-organized speech in which he dealt with the usefulness of building and loan associations....

After the appointment of several committees....and presentations by a number of out-of-town representatives....the convention adjourned until 2 P. M.....

....The afternoon session was concluded with an address by State Representative J. O. Hrubý on legislation concerning building and loan associations....

A magnificent banquet....in the park of the Pilsen Brewing Company....crowned the day's program.....
