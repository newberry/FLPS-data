p. 1 - col. 7.. Among Bohemian firms in all America the best known is the firm of F. Korbel & Bros., dealers in California wine and liquors. Their largest branch is in Chicago, where they are constantly expanding their place of business. During the past year they were compelled to construct a ware-house so that they could keep a supply on hand which their prosperous business demanded.

They ascribe their success to the quality of their product and also to the merit of their capable manager of the Chicago branch, Mr. J. Fucik, who is regarded as an outstanding Bohemian business man.
