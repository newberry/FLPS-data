In its last annual meeting, the Česká Vstřední Pevecká Jednota (Federation of Bohemian Singing Societies) decided to give a large-scale concert this spring. The size of the accompanying orchestra will be the subject of a discussion at the next rehearsal to be held February 17 at 7 P. M. in Mr. Hacha's hall, 26th Street and Springfield Avenue. The rehearsal will be the last one before the concert and will be open to any singers who have not attended previous meetings.

If the concert is to be a representative one, it is necessary that every singer appear. It has been a long time since we made an appearance before the public.
