The Chicago Art Institute has just arranged an exhibit of the works of Chicago artists. This exhibit is incorporated with another one consisting of etchings. ....Out of some 900 paintings and 150 sculptures, 280 pictures and 40 sculptured works were selected by the jury. Among the pieces on exhibition are the works of our countryman, Rudolf F. Ingerle, who is also a member of the art jury, and among the sculptures exhibited are those by our [countryman], Mr. Albín Polášek.
