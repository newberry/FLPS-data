The exhibition of the Gymnastic Unit of Sokol Progress (Telocvičná Jednota Sokol Pokrok), held in a hall on 47th Street and Winchester Avenue, in Town of Lake, will end today.

As customary--that the ends call for music--the close of the program will be the climax of the exhibition's splendid success.

Today the exhibition will open at two o'clock in the afternoon. A great number of people are expected to attend, partly to enjoy the program and partly to show their friendship toward this energetic Sokol group. Today's program is exceptionally attractive.

Lhotak's band will once more demonstrate that it is one of the best.
