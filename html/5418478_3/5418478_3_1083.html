The St. Vitus Sokol No. 48 of the Katolická Jednota Sokolská v America (Union of Catholic Sokols of America) arranged a theatrical performance last Wednesday evening at the Sokol Chicago hall. The general success attained by this performance will surely encourage the amateurs to further activity.

An effective picture from life called the "Svatojanský Dvur" (Court of St. John) was presented on the stage under the direction of Mr. Joseph Loner. The smooth ensemble and accurate characterization of individual parts gave proof of the intelligent study and attention given to the performance by all those who participated.....
