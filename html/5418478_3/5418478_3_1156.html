A most successful theatrical performance was presented in the hall of Sokol Slovanska Lipa last night. Under the direction of our noted teacher and national worker, Miss E. Zeman, our children staged the phantasy "Chytry Honza" (Clever Johnnie). The proceeds of the evening go to the Cesko-Americka Matice Skolska (Bohemian-American Scholastic Union). The splendid performance of our little actors was admired by a large audience.....
