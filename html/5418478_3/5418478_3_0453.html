The Jednota Pro Povzneseni Komorni Hudby v Chicagu (Society for the Promotion of Chamber Music in Chicago) will start its series of four concerts Saturday, January 24, at 3 P. M., in Stile's hall on 22nd Street and Millard Avenue.....

Our music critic, Dr. Jaroslav E. S. Vojan, will introduce each selection on the program with a brief talk about its history, musical significance, and other interesting matters connected with it.
