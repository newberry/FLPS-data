A meeting was held recently in the Pilsen Sokol hall by the members of the American State Bank, the new Bohemian enterprise, in which almost all of the stockholders participated.

As is known, this enterprise for which great success is assured by the number and names of the participants, will have its business place in the real centre of Bohemian Pilsen. It will occupy the premises formerly occupied by the Pelikan drug store at Blue Island and Loomis Streets, near 18th Street.

The following gentlemen were elected to the Board of Directors: Jan Karel, J. F. Stepina, Jan Fucik, F. J. Skala, Karel Kresl, \2C. R. Wallek, James Storkan, John C. Krasa, Emanuel Beranek and A. V. Geringer.

The Board of Directors then held a meeting and elected the following named officers: Jan Karel, president; C. R. Walleck, vice president and J. F. Stepina, treasurer.
