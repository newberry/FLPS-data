Every Saturday, beginning today, Miss Albina Sladek will give free instructions in Bohemian folk singing to our youth in Dvorak Park, May and 20th Streets. There will be two classes this year, one for children between six and ten years of age, and one for children between ten and fourteen. The classes begin at 4 P.M.

Miss Sladek is a piano teacher and for some years has been giving vocal lessons. She desires to devote her free time to our children and in their early youth acquaint them with the beauties of our old folk songs.....
