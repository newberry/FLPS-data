United Russian Social-Democratic Party. Proletariat of the world, unite!

Russians, come to the massmeeting of the Social-Democrats, dedicated to events in Russia!

The Red Bolshevik terror and the struggle for peace... for bread... for land and freedom ... for freedom and equal rights for all nationalities... for an immediate conference of the League of Nations. All these questions will be elucidated by the best orators of the Russian Social-Democratic colony.

The meeting is arranged by the Russian Social-Democratic Club on Sunday, January 13, 1918, at Hull House (Bowen Hall), corner of Polk and Halsted Street. It begins at 2 P. M. Admission free.
