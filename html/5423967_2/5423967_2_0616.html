Attention! Attention!

Saturday, May 24, 1919, at the West Side Auditorium (corner Taylor and Racine), a mass protest meeting will be held, demanding the opening of the border and a withdrawal of the army of the Allies from Russia.

There will be speakers in all languages. An orchestra will play.

It begins at 7:30 P. M. Admission free.

It is the duty of every worker to be at this meeting. The meeting is under the auspices of the Conference of Russian Labor Organizations.
