Sunday, Dec. 22, 1918, the second section of the Russian Socialist Party, in its permanent quarters at 1145 Blue Island Avenue, near 12th Street, is arranging a lecture on the subject of "The Worker Before the Revolution and After." The lecturer is Comrade Dr. Kopnagel. Beginning at 7 P. M. Admission 10 cents.

Tuesday, Dec. 24, at 8 P. M., at the same location, a comradeship party is arranged. Admission is free. Come and invite your friends.
