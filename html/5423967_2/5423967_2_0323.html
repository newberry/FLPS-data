Sunday, Dec. 1, 1918, two lectures are arranged on the subject of "How the Russian Workers and Poor Peasants have Obtained Freedom."

The first lecture is arranged by the Russian section of the Socialist Party of Melrose Park in the hall of Frank and James, corner 23rd Avenue and Lake Street. Beginning at 7 P. M.

The second lecture is arranged by the second section of the Russian Socialist Party of Chicago, at Hull House, Smith Hall, corner Halsted and Polk streets.

Beginning at 7 P. M. Admission to both lectures is free. The lecturer is comrade A. Stoklitzky. Come and invite your friends.
