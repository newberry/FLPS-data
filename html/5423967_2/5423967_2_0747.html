Saturday, September 28, there will be held at Smith Hall (Hull House, corner Halsted and Polk) a massmeeting dedicated to the recent events in the fatherland and our attitude toward the government in Northern Russia. The meeting is under the sponsorship of the Russian Social-Democratic Club. The speakers will be Pollack, Lobtov, Khinoy and others.

The meeting will begin at 8 P. M. Admission 10 cents.

Ed. Note: No year given.
