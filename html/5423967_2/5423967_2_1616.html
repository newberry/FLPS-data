Dear Editor:

We are sending our sincere greetings to you, to the publishers, and to all persons connected with Rassviet on the occasion of the tenth anniversary of this useful publication. We highly value Rassviet's most successful work in the field of education, culture, mutual aid, and general well-being of the Russian people in Chicago, and indeed in the entire United States and in Canada.

We wish you a full measure of prosperity and success for many years to come. May your good work in increasing the welfare of the Russian people continue unabated! To you and to all your associates, we wish the abiding health, vigor, energy, and courage necessary to keep up your great work.

\2

The Chicago Branch of the Russian Emigree's Children Aid Society.

I. F. Erin, chairman,

A. D. Dobrokhotov, secretary.
