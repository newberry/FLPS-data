The concert of the well known bass, V. Dylov, on April 27, 1929, was a big success. There was no end of encores. Mr. Dylov and Mme. Mirayev scored the greatest applause. The public was delighted by the artistic performance of Russian folk-songs by Mme. Mirayev.

V. Frotsenko, A. Frikin and B. Khmara also earned a well deserved success. This concert will be long remembered by those who attended.
