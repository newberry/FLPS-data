Professor N. A. Borodin, a member of the Special Mission, has accepted an invitation to lecture on the theme "The Sufferings of our Prisoners of War in Germany and Austria." The lecture, under the sponsorship of the Russian Club Znanie will take place September 16, 1917, at Bowen Hall of Hull House.

Screen pictures will be used to illustrate the lecture, the income of which will be devoted to the Russian prisoners of war.

The lecture starts at 8 P. M. sharp.
