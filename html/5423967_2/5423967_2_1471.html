On November 1, 1924, in the "House of Enlightenment". a circle of literary and artistic "boheme" will give an evening entertainment at which literary productions of local authors will be read. Some of the authors will read their own productions.

There will be music, singing and declamation.

Madame Soghie P. Stranden, who for some time has not been heard at Russian entertainments, will sing. Madame Sophie Stranden's soulful singing and her strong and melodious voice always attract a numerous public, and the best reports about her singing have appeared in the press.....

Her participation will be very welcome to all lovers of Russian songs.
