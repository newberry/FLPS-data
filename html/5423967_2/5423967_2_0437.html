The editors, in the first issue of the magazine, set forth its principles. It was dedicated "to the interests of Russian immigrants in America, regardless of nationality, religion or party." The New Era will strive to give well-rounded discussions of the problems of contemporary life, especially of those questions which are of interest to Russians in America.

"We believe in the necessity of a radical, basic revolution in contemporary social, sex, and national inter-relationship of people; in the necessity of establishing a social structure based upon the principles of social equality, co-operation and democracy; in the necessity of establishing the independence of women, based on the recognition of the full equality of the sexes; in the necessity of founding a federation of all nations, based on the principles of international solidarity."
