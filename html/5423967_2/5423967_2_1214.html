This week the artists of the club "Petrushka" will appear at the Palace Theater. They will sing their remarkable Chastushki (a peculiar kind of folk-song, picturing the life of various classes of people) and couplets illustrating night life. The troupe consists of more than eighteen--singers, dancers and rhymesters--conducted by I. Khmara and K. Sankarzhevsky.

The act consists of seven specially combined scenes. Among these there are such numbers as the March of the Wooden Soldiers, etc.
