A very interesting lecture on this gruesome subject will be given by a person who has recently returned from Norway, A. E. Galitzky, a famous Russian professor of the department of psychology at the University of Munchen, Germany.

As a member of the Social-Democratic Party, he has studied the Socialist movement thoroughly.

A. E. Galitzky is known not only as an interesting lecturer, but also as a first-class orator.

Come Friday, January 3, 1919, to the West Side Auditorium (Annex), corner Taylor Street and Racine Avenue. This lecture is organized by the Social-Democratic Club. It begins at 8 P. M. sharp. Admission 20 cents.
