On Novenber 23, 1929 there took place a literary debate on the theme: "The Literary Productions of the Colonial Poets Bondarenko, Kornosevich and Stotsky". The value and the significance of the above mentioned poets were expounded by N. Gribov and Dr. L. G. Pertsov. The defects and insignificance of their productions were pointed out by: E. Moravsky and N. Seoyev. K. Koszin was chairman.
