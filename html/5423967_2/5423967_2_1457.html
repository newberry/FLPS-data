A literary-dramatic farewell party was arranged on Saturday, June 26, 1926, at Division Hall, 2441 W. Division Street, for the well-known Russian writer, S. I. Goosiev Orenburgskii, before his departure to New York.

Two of his Miniatures of Soviet Life, "Samogonshchina" (Bootlegging) and "Sovietskii Diakon" (The Soviet Deacon) were performed, under his personal supervision, by a troupe of dramatic artists.

In the literary part of the program the author read from his unpublished work.
