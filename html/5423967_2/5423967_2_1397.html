Concert Program

1) Students of the Musical and Vocal Studio of Mr. G. Khrzhanovsky. Singing-solo by Mr. P. Aluyewich, baritone.

2) Students of the Musical Studio of Mr. A. Nakutin, singing-solos.

3) Mr. A. M. Aster, well known pianist, who has a studio in the Kimball Bldg., will play "Sheherazade" by Rimsky-Korsakov.

4) Miss Jean Koorey, talented pianist-piano solo.

5) Chorus conducted by Mrs. P. M. Maksakov.

6) Orchestra of students of the University of Chicago will play a pot-pourri and 2some other interesting numbers.

7) Mrs. P. M. Maksakov, well known Russian singer-Russian folk-songs and songs by Russian composers.

8) Mr. I. Kulgar, well known violinist.
