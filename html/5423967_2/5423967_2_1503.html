There were 46 active members. There had been 25 committee sessions and eleven general meetings, of which three were organizational and eight were scientific. Fourteen reports given were listed; these had been followed by discussions.

The income of the Society had been $393.84 and the expenditures $217.00.

Correspondence had been entered into with government departments relating to recognition of Russian diplomas.

The report was signed by Dr. G. Hassin, president and Dr. M. Sahud, secretary.
