In some of the department stores and large shops of the clothing industry there are many Russian tailors working.

There are many large shops where about a hundred Russians are being employed.

One of these firms is that of Conn and Rismann.

On the whole this firm employs about 500 men, among them 100 regular Russians, and, besides, more than 100 men and women born in Russia, but belonging to other nationalities.

This is a union shop. The tailors earn from $50 to $60 a week. During the busy season some earn as much as $75.

Among the hundred Russians employed by this firm there are 25 women.
