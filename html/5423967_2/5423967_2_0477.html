A meeting of this union (Russkii Krestyanskii Ekonomicheskii Soyuz) was held on June 8, 1913, at Workingmen's Hall, 1013 W. 12th St. The meeting was called for a discussion of questions "which should interest every Russian living in America".

The organizer of the union, M. Dorab; Comrade G. Belousov, a former member of the Second Duma; and Dr. H. R. Krasnow spoke.
