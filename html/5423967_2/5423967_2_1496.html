A Russian program will be given every second Wednesday, from 6 to 6:30 P.M., Chicago time, over the radio station W. M. B. I., Moody Bible Institute, Chicago, Ill. The schedule of programs is as follows: April 29, May 13 and 27, June 10 and 24, July 8 and 22, August 5 and 19, September 2 and 16.

In this program you will hear a choir, a string orchestra, solos, duets, trios, quartets and brief sermons.

The program is sponsored by the Community of Evangelical Christians, 2127 W. Crystal St., Chicago, Ill.
