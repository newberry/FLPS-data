On Sunday, November 19, 1916, at the Hull House (Bowen Hall), corner of Polk and Halsted Streets, a large meeting dedicated to the events of October 1905 will take place. (The general strike and manifesto, October 17th).

The meeting is organized by the Russian section of the Socialist Party and the Society for Aid to Political Exiles and Prisoners in Russia. Several speakers will represent these organizations. Admission free.

The meeting starts at 2:30 P. M.

Come and bring your friends and acquaintances!
