Mr. T. Y. Chen, was sent from the Canton government to Europe and the United States as a special investigator doing some political research work.

He arrived in Chicago from Europe yesterday, by way of New York. He will be here for about 3 days before continuing his trip West whence he will return to the homeland.

Mr. Chen's official position in China is that of assistant High Commissioner of the Kwantung municipality. He was, formerly Mayor of Swa-Taw and magistrate of many small cities.

He has just finished his research work in Europe and will continue his work in all the prominent cities of this country.

During his stay here in Chicago, Mr. Chen will be entertained by the leading Chinese organizations in Chicago.
