The local Chinese merchants, M. S. Stoo and C. H. Yee, have established a Chinese restaurant on South Clark Street, the old Chinatown. Hoe King is the name and the address is 410 South Clark Street.

In celebrating the grand opening on April the 9th, the restaurant owners entertained Mr. C. W. Chu, president of the Chinese Consolidated Benevolent Association, Mr. K. L. Chen, secretary of the same association and a few other intimate friends.
