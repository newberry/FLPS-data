Dr. S. T. Tao, returned to Chicago, yesterday from a propaganda trip in Mexico. Y. C. Moy, C. Tang, W.P. Lee, and C. H. Yu, executive officers of the Chinese Emergency Relief Society were on hand to welcome him at the 12th Street Station.

He was entertained by the On-Leong Chinese Merchants' Association at noon. The Chinese Consolidated Benevolent Association arranged a reception meeting for Dr. S. T. Tao at 3 P. M. During that meeting Dr. Tao will speak on his experiences and the Chinese patriotic work in Mexico.

Dr. Tao will leave Chicago, at 9 P.M. the same day.
