The twenty-third Ward Swedish American Republican Club has been incorporated under the laws of the State of Illinois. Its first regular members' meeting was held last Tuesday at Nelson's Hall, at which the following officers were elected: P. A. Sandelius, president; [gap] A. Johnson, 1st vice-pres.; Victor Lons[gap]n, 2nd vice-pres.; John A. Lynn, treasurer; Frank J. Chaiser, recording secy.; Gustave Claussen, [gap] secy.; Gustave Claussen, corr. secy. and A. [gap]Winberg, marshall. By-laws were drafted and adopted.
