The cooperative temperance cafe "Idrott" announces that a branch restaurant, will be opened at 5248 N. Clark Street a week from Saturday, Sept. 20th. The new place will have facilities for the public similar to the original at 3206 Wilton Avenue, although they will be on a smaller scale. They will comprise reading rooms, library, etc.

The increased migration of the Swedish people northward, from Lake View to Edgewater, Summerdale and Rogers Park, makes this addition very desirable.
