Swedish newcomers and others who are interested are offered free instruction in English by the Lake View Swedish Free Church, 3516-18 Sheffield Avenue. Classes are held every Monday night at 7:30.

The church has given such courses for several years, and hundreds of people have taken advantage of this opportunity to learn English under competent teachers.

The school is recognized by the Board of Education, and its diplomas have the same value as those issued by the public schools of corresponding grade.
