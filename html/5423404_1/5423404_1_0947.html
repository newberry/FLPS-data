John Ericsson Republican League of Illinois will hold its 32nd Annual convention, March 9th, at the La Salle Hotel. Delegates from Chicago and all over the state will be present at this convention. The convention will wind up in a banquet, which Secretary of Commerce Herbert Hoover, U.S. Senators Charles S. Deneen and Wm. B. McKinley, and Congressman Carl R. Chindblom, have promised to attend.
