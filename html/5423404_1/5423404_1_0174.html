"The Co-operative Temperance Cafe Idratt" celebrated the 15th anniversary of its organization last Saturday.

A banquet had been arranged in the large new assembly hall, and the guests numbered well over two hundred persons. A varied program was presented by the Northland Ladies Trio; Mr. Ivar Lundquist, violinist; Mr. Gunnar Sund, tenor; the Scandinavian String Trio, and Mr. K.H. Holton, pianist.

The principal address was delivered by Herman Cederblad, co-editor of the Swedish Tribune-News. His subject was "The Idealism of the Co-operative Movement". Master of ceremonies was Mr. Nils Linnberg.
