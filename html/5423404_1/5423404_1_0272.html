Our readers are reminded of the Julottan service which will take place Christmas morning at 5:45 A.M. in the Trinity Swedish Lutheran Church. It will be heard over radio station WMAQ. [Translator's note: Julottan is an early Christmas morning Church service, an old Swedish custom]. Dr. Gottfred Nelson will preach, and Christmas hymns will be sung by the church choir.

Another Lutheran service will be heard over station WCFL, and the Swedish part of it, with Dr. Julius Lincoln preaching, will begin at 6:00 A.M. and will last one half hour.

During a later part of the service the English language will be used and Mrs. Jennie Ekblom Peterson, president of the Swedish Choral Club, will sing.

At this service the musical accompaniment will be rendered by the noted Swedish organist, Professor John Hjertelius.
