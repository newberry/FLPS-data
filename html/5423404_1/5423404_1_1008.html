This club met recently in Clifton Hall on Belmont Avenue. The meeting was an unusually large one from the standpoint of attendance. The club set up new by-laws. It adopted the rules of the County Central Committee. The club is attempting to bring harmony to warring factions throughout the State. John Kjellander was elected president; A. J. Teig first vice-president; J. A. Barquist second vice-president; John B. Halsen treasurer.
