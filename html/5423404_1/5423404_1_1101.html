District Attorney Edwin A. Olson some months ago tendered his resignation to the Attorney General in Washington, D. C., requesting that he be relieved by Jan. 1st. Up to this date no successor has been appointed and Mr. Olsen again has written the Washington office asking immediate action.

Subsequent to the above writing it has been officially announced that our well-known contryman, Attorney George E. Q. Johnson, also a Chicagoan, has been appointed to succeed Mr. Olson.
