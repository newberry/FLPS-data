A new organization, the Swedish Women's Republican Club, was formed during a meeting at the Sherman Hotel on March 25. Its president is Mrs. Carl A. Evald, and Mrs. Othelia Myhrman is vice-president.

The purpose of the Club is to induce Swedish-American women to take out citizenship papers and become qualified voters. It endorses William Hale Thompson, Republican candidate for mayor, and all the other Republican candidates.

It is expected that before the election is over the Club's membership will reach ten thousand.
