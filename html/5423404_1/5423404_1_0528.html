The Union Bank of Chicago has elected the following directors and officers: president, Charles E. Schlytern; first vice-president Nils Arneson; second vice-president, John S. Rydell; cashier, Gustav Halltom; assistant cashier, C.C.Collins.

Among others chosen as directors, were: Arthur G. Bennett, John M. Ericson, Charles L. Johnson, C. W. Johnson, Hans P. Johnson, Thomas Olson, and Werner Wieboldt.
