The prominent Harry Olson, who is running for a judicial office, is now endorsed by the Chicago Bar Association, four hundred members of which have signed the endorsement. Most of these signatures were obtained after the Independence League had put up its ticket.

The Bar Association endorsement states:

"As members of the Chicago Bar and from personal knowledge of the ability, energy and trustworthy practice of law by Mr. Olson, during his career, we heartily recommend him for election to the position of Chief Justice of the Chicago Municipal Court. In view of the fact that his every trial in the Supreme Court has won him the highest esteem of the judges, his qualifications cannot be surpassed. His honesty has never been questioned, and his personality always wins him friends. We are without reservation, in favor of his election".
