At 2443-2447 West Kinzie Street we find an establishment turning out one of the world's best beverages, Lundin's En-Bars Dricka (Lundin's Juniper ale). Letters from five of the most prominent Swedish pastors, who recommend the use of this excellent ale and wonderful non-intoxicating substitute for liquors give wonderfully authentic advice for the welfare of the individual from infancy to old age.

We quote the letter from Pastor C. A. Bjork, principal of North Park College of Chicago and president of the Evangelical Mission of America: "All who have their own welfare and that of their fellow men [in mind] should welcome Lundin's Juniper ale in their home because it is the world's best aid in eliminating intoxicants."
