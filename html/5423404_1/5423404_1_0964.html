The Swedish-American Republican Central Club of Cook County held a meeting on May 21 in the offices of its president, Alfred A. Norton. The Club adopted a resolution pledging its support to all the judicial candidates on the Republican ticket.

The committee appointed at a previous meeting for the purpose of organizing the Swedish voters in Chicago reported that its work is progressing in a satisfactory manner, and organizations have already been established in ten wards. It was stated that the idea of unity is beginning to take hold among our countrymen, and indications are that voters' organizations will be built up not only in all the city wards but also in the suburbs.
