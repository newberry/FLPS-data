A meeting for the organization of domestics and others will be held next Thursday January 30, at 8 P.M., at Belmont Avenue and North Clark Street.

Housemaids, kitchen girls, laundry girls, waitresses, hotel maids, etc., are invited, together with all others interested. Among the speakers will be Miss Martha Biegler, who will speak in English. There will also be speaking in Swedish and Finnish, and a fine program of song, music and readings, by fine talent. Admission is free.
