The well-known gold mine corporation the Telma Gold Mining Company, with offices at 112 S. Clark Street, Chicago, seems to be winning ever-increasing popularity among the Swedes. The company owns a mine, which appears very valuable, in the American land of gold, Colorado, and to the many Swedes who have bought shares in the company we are inclined to predict a good income on the money invested......

Our well-known compatriot Dr. E. Nelson, is president of the company, and Mr. C. A. Vallentin is the secretary and manager.....
