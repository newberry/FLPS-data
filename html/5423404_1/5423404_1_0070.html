We are heartily in favor of the new system of choosing teachers for our public schools established by the Chicago Board of Education.

The new system requires every applicant to take five courses of study. Each course includes twenty-four lectures. [The studies should last] five years; the applicant may take the complete five-year course in two years in summer school.

The majority of the School Board is confident that this program will bring satisfactory results to all; both the applicants and their examiners will consider it a square deal for all concerned.
