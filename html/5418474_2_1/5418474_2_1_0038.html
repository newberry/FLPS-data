The Committee of Arrangements for the convention of the Saloonkeepers of the State of Illinois, has, at its meeting, in Harry Ruben's office, accepted the program and business rules of the convention.

Adolph George,M. Wasserman and William Clemens were appointed to the Press Committee. Jos. Miehle, E. Dieter and F. Knepper to the Hotel Committee; Peter Muller, N. Enders and Charles Heiser to the Committee of Amusements and the last named were requested to submit their report at the next meeting.
