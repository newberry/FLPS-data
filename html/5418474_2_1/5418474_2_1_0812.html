The steadily growing use made of the divorce courts the country over, the particularly rapid increase of divorce suits in Cook County, Illinois, and the uncovering of quite a number of actual "divorce mills" in various states of the Union have prompted both great and small minds to occupy themselves to a greater extent with the very weighty question of how best to put a stop to a condition that is by no means a happy one.

The campaign for uniform divorce laws for all states, which has been in progress for some time, is now being conducted with greater energy, and a large number of local reform organizations have, within the last few weeks, decided that this movement should be furthered and supported.
