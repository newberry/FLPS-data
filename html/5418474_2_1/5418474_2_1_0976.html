Mr. C. J. Gottschalk, the retiring manager of the Wieboldt Store, was highly honored as a result of his long association with that concern. Last night the department managers presented a surprise dinner for their superior, during the course of which he was presented with a loving cup, symbolizing their appreciation of his services. Mr. Gottschalk, was also presented with a diamond pin from all the employees of the store, as a token of their high regard for him.
