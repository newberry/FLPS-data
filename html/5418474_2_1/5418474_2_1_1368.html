We will find out very slowly the effects of the new tariff on our business. Apparently large quantities of goods came to this country as last hour shipments, before the new tariff tool effect. some of these shipments arrived too late and have to be auctioned, if the importation taxes are not paid. The new tariff will contribute enormously towards the protection of our home industry, which has a chance new to prosper and expand without any limitations.

It will block, of course for alwhile, the wholesale and retail business in many lines. which will have to get accustomed to the now situation.
