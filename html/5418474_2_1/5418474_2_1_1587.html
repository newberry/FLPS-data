Under the auspices of the Success Council No. 3, of the "Butchers and Grocery Clerks' Association" a meeting to intensify the demands of Sabbath observation was held at the hall at 1469 W. 35th Street. Every seat in the hall was taken. Chairman Otto Giermann reported that the association has been very successful so far in its agitation, and it is their belief, that they have won the general public and have their co-operation.
