The first general assembly in connection with the election of new officers of the German Waiters Union took place last night in their hall at 138 Michigan Street. The following were elected: President, P. Maulen; Vice-President, H. Ulrich; Treasurer, Wm. H. Jung; Financial Secretary, J. Schmidt, General Secretary, F. Huboi; Registrar, Wm. Hamann; R. Committee, F. Goedeke, H.W.Menke, J. Moenks.

The above union was established in 1878. It has a cash-capital of about $2,000, on hand and pays its members a weekly sick benefit of $5.00, and in case of death the amoutn of $100.00 to their respective families.
