To the German Women of the Southside. Mass meeting Thursday, April 2, 2 P.M., in "Freiheit Turnhalle". On Thursday, April 2nd, at 2 o'clock at the Freiheit Turnhalle, 34th & Halsted Street, a German Women's meeting will take place, under the auspices of the United Societies.

Good speakers, both in German and English will talk about the aims and purposes of the great liberty-league, and actors from the German Theatre, are going to delight the audience with a program of declamations and songs. Every German woman, who does homage to liberty, and intercedes for the principle, that Chicago should not become a bigoted hole, should attend this meeting.
