A very well attended agitation meeting with Comrade Schroder as Chairman was held last night. Comrade Back gave a complete weekly report emphasizing and condemning the law and brutal conduct of the French bourgeoisie, toward the courageous combatant for the Socialist cause Louise Michel. Comrade B. Rau then read the "Arbeiter Zeitung's" editorial dealing with the villany of French despotism.

This was followed by a speech given by Comrade A. Spies: "The revolutiona parties of Russia" and caused an animated debate in which a number of other comrades participated.
