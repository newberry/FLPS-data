The German-Democratic club of the 21st Ward held a meeting last night at the Heinen's Hall, 519 Larrabee Street and listened to the speech of Congressman Julius Goldzier, who predicted an overwhelming victory of the Democratic ticket, if the German Americans would give their full undivided support during the coming campaign.

Goldzier also expressed his satisfaction, that the German-Americans are organizing independent Democratic Clubs in every part of the city.
