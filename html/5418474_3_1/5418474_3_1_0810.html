The Social Club "Vorwarts" (Forward) held its well-attended agitation meeting in its locality, 120 Wells Street, yesterday evening. Comrade Perske was elected President. Comrade Neebe held his weekly review, which was very favorably received. Several of those present participated in a discussion, which followed. The action of the Comrades in Vienna against the police spies, was approved by the Club "Vorwarts."
