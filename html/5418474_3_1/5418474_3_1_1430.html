The German Democrats are forging ahead to organize and help carry the party banner to victory during the coming election. There will be another mass-meeting at the Northside Turnerhall. Governor Altgeld will be in Chicago next week to confer with the Democratic leaders.

Representative Julius Goldzier will preside over an exclusive gathering of German American business men at the Morrison Hall on Madison Street, this Saturday evening 8 o'clock.

The German-American Democratic Club of the 17th ward will meet coming Sunday afternoon at 3 o'clock at Hillinger's Hall, Sheffield and Belmont Avenue. Mr. Henry Rubens will be the principal speaker.
