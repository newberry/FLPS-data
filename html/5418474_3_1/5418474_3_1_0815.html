In regard to the proposition to invite Comrade Bebel to come to America on an agitation tour, they voted against it, with all but one vote. The delegate to the general committee was instructed to explain the position of the group and to induce them to assist in furthering the movement in the district. Comrade Hirsch submitted his weekly report, which caused a lively debate.
