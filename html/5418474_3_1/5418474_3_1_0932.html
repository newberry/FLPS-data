The Armed Organizations of the "Lehr and Wehr" Club and the Sportsmen's Club are asked to keep watch over the great Indignation-meeting to be held to-morrow evening in the Turner Hall. The presence of the people's Wehr is very much required as possible political vagabonds and followers could try to disturb and dissolve the meeting.

The sight of our vigorous warriors will be sufficient to keep in check all rough and rowdy elements who may get inside the Turner Hall.
