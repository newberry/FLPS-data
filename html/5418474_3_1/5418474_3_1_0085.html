The German Building Carpenters held a meeting yesterday at 71 W Lake Street, and made the following resolutions:

1. Founding of a new German Union.

2. Same should remain in the Brotherhood.

An temporary officers the following were elected: Zanger, President: Poche, Secretary. It was further resolved, to hold a meeting Thursday, June 19, in Seamen's Hall, 99, W. Randolph Street, to notify all members of the old German branch and to elect officers on that occasion.
