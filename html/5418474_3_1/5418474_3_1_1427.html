The local Republican Convention will take place Thursday 21st of this month at 11 A. M. in the Central Music Hall.

The German Republican Club of the 17th Ward has approved of the nominations of Mr. G. B. Swift, for Mayor Adam Wolf, City Treasurer, F. E. Erickson for West Town collector and S. P. Revere for Alderman in a meeting held at 331 Milwaukee Avenue.

The Humboldt Club, a German American Social Club has approved of Mr. Edward Haas as City Councillor. The first of the mass meetings which will be held under the auspices of the Civic Federation in various parts of the towns will take place Monday night in Douglas Hall, corner of 35th Street and Indiana Avenue. Lyman I. Gage. W. T. Onahan and D. E. Millner have been announced as speakers.
