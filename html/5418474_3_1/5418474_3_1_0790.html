At the business meeting of the Group Town of Lake last Sunday, the following officers were elected: Secretary, A. Kiesling; Finance Secretary, A. Watscho; Delegate to the General Committee, G. Braunsdorf; Librarian, W. Teschendorf.

It was resolved to hold the agitation meetings hereafter Saturday evenings. For the publication fund $3.00 was allowed from the treasury; further it was resolved to display a collection sheet for the family of Comrade Stellmacher at every meeting.
