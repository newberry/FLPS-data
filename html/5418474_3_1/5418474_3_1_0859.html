Under the chairmanship of Comrade Kiesling last Saturday, an agitation meeting of the Southside Socialists was held at Kostner's Hall.

Comrade R. Kiesling spoke on the subject:"The enemies of Socialism." The speaker depicted the class of people they belong to showing, that they alwyas professed injustice toward their fellowman and were not shy at employing any means to deceive the proletariat.

Comrade Braunsdorf submitted to the assembly his reasons for the founding of a new group in the Town of Lake. This received a warm welcome from the meeting and the delegates was advised to submit it to the Central Committee.
