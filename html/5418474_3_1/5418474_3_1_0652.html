Major Roche, Hubbard and Bonfield answered two days ago the request for an order to restrain asked by the laborers' association from Judge Tuley.

This lengthy document says among other things that the laborers' union is composed of anarchists who should not be allowed to meet, as they are dangerous.

The anarchists want to take their revenge on the officers who brought the murdered anarchists (Spiess, Parsons, etc.) to trial.

This is their answer word for word. It is remarkable that these people finally admit having murdered those anarchists.
