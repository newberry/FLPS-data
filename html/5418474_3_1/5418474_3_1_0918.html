The meeting of the Chicago Main Section will take place, Saturday, February 19th, at 8 P.M., 54 West Lake Street.

The purpose of this meeting is, to receive the organizations plan, as to the common procedure of the Party and the armed organizations, worked out by the organizations Committee. Further, all the business of this Section, which has not come up for discussion at the last meeting, will also be settled then.

All the members are requested to be present for this very important business meeting.

Frank A. Stauber,

Secretary of the Organizations Committee.
