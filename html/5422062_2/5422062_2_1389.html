A new business firm has been added to the lengthy list of Greek enterprises in this city. It is the Blue Goose Fruit and Liquor Store situated on the corner of State and Lake Streets in Chicago's Loop. It sells especially fine fruits and candies and imported liqueurs.

The managers of the Blue Goose are Mr. J. Katsivalis and E. and S. Krikas, all of whom have been in the same type of business for many years and are well known in the Loop.
