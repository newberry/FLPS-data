The young City Clerk of River Grove, Mr. Demetrios Kyriazopoulos, who is hardly twenty-one years old, recently celebrated his name-day by giving a wonderful party to numerous friends.

Our young countryman, who has a bright future in politics, was honored by his townspeople's vote because of the good character and reputation of his whole family. His father settled in River Grove a good many years ago and operates a restaurant there. The young City Clerk is studying law.
