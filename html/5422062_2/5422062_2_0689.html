Among Chicago Greek newspapers Loxias feels very, very proud. For Loxias does its duty to our country. America is our country. The following letter is one of many sent to Loxias for simply doing its duty.

Treasury Department, Bureau of Publicity,

Liberty Loan Campaign Committee,Federal

Reserve District No. 7

105 W. Monroe Sheet

Chicago, Illinois

To Chicago Loxias

Dear Sir:

Your hearty, unselfish, patriotic cooperation in the publicity work incident to the Third Liberty Loan campaign brought splendid results.

I wish to extend to you the sincere thanks, not only of this Bureau but of the entire Liberty Loan organization.

Cordially yours,

M. Sasbit,

Director of Publicity.
