The Aldermanic Election will be held in the City of Chicago next Tuesday, April 2, 1912. It is earnestly desired that every Greek voter in Chicago, who is a true Democrat, shall support the ticket which has been selected by Mayor Carter H. Harrison and the Progressive Democratic Organization. This ticket is backed by the only Democratic papers published in this city the Hearst papers.

Every Greek voter should go to the polls next Tuesday and vote straight the Democratic ticket in order to carry out the policies of Mayor Harrison.

Thanking you in advance for your support, which we know you will give us, we are D. S. Eutaxias, Peter G. Bitakes, George Machines, Louis Choromokos, John Cocales, Perry Hiotes.
