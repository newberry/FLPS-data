Mr. N. Dokos sent his plans for a church to the Orthodox Parish of Memphis, Tennessee, and a few days ago he was informed that his plans had been chosen as the best.

He deserves the heartiest congratulations of the community. Mr. Dokos' plans were also accepted by the Greek church in Little Rock, Arkansas.

Mr. Dokos also designed the plans for the hotel, owned by B. Georgakopoulos and D. Bogiantopoulos. This hotel has fifty rooms and eight stories, and is valued at $85,000.
