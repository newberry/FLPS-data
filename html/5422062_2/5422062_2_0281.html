Last week, at the Sherman Hotel, the regular meeting of the Chicago Grecian Circle took place, and the hall as usual was crowded. The Greeks of Chicago have become political minded and sooner or later their power will be recognized.

Judge Wm. Helander, as a speaker of the night, outlined the service and usefulness of the Grecian Circle. Senator Charles Deneen, who is honorary president of the Greek Club, brought out the future potentialities of the Greeks in the political arena, and eulogized them for being law abiding.
