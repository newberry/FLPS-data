George Damoularis has sold his two story brick building, located at 5245-47 Irving Park Blvd, and consisting of two stores, two offices, one flat and a two car garage, to John and Hariclia Sunders at a consideration of $41,000 subject to $30,000 mortgage.

The purchaser gave in part payment eleven lots of twenty five feet frontage, clear at 98th St. and Normal Ave.

Anton P. Tomaras was broker, George A. Jacobs represented the seller and George Askounis represented the purchaser.
