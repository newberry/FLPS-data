Known for twenty years to the Greeks of Chicago and other western cities, the Bouris Liner Supply Co. has once again opened its doors at 642 Blue Island Avenue.

Greek restauroteurs, who had used Bouris' tablecloths, napkins, towels, etc. were glad to hear of the firms revival.

The Company also carries a good line of Baptismal and Greek wedding supplies. We Greeks should continue to support this enterprise as we have in the past.
