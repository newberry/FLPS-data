Young Dr. Varellas, an honor graduate from the University of Illinois, after serving as an interne in various hospitals, will open an office to practice his profession at 35 South Dearborn Sts.

Dr. Varellas, who, in spite of his youth, is a noted pathologist, is a scion of an old and prominent family from Athens, and came to America in childhood.

Congratulations to Dr. Varellas and wishing him higher honors in his practice.
