p. 3.- We ask all Chicago citizens to vote for Collis Davis for Cook County Commissioner on April 8th. Mr. Davis is born and reared in Chicago and has attended the University of Illinois. He is a real estate agent with offices at Devon and Western.

Mr. Davis is a very good friend of the Greek people. He works in cooperation with John Mantas, 118 S. Dearborn Street. His election on April 8th will be a good thing for the Greeks so vote for him.
