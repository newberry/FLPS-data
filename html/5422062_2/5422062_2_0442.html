Mr. A. A. Pantelis, Sec'y

The Achaian League

616, #155 N. Clark St.

Chicago, Illinois

Dear Sir:

I am in receipt of your favor of the 19th instant and wish to thank you for the kind information contained therein.

I need hardly assure you that I keenly appreciate the action of your League in endorsing my candidacy, and desire to thank them, through you, for their kind consideration.

2

I might add that it shall be my constant endeavor, if elected, to justify the confidence implied by their endorsement.

Again thanking you and with kindest personal regards, I am,

Yours very truly,

Robert Sweitzer.

County Clerk.
