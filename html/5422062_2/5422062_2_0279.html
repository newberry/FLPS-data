All Greeks of Chicago, especially those living on the West Side, are invited to be present, today, Jan. 5, 8 P.M., at Olympia Hall, 748 S. Halsted street, where a Greek-American meeting will take place. This meeting is held under the auspices of the Greek-American Republican Political League of the 27th Ward.

It is the duty of every Greek to be present at this meeting because it concerns the problem of the political unification of the Greeks of Chicago. Various orators will speak in Greek and English. The main speaker of the evening will be the Hon. Charles Slater, general representative of the Hon. Homer K. Galpin. Chairman of Cook County Republican Central Committee and Ward Committeeman of the 27th Ward.
