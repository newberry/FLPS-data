Congratulations to the revolutionists of Greece for "throwing off the yoke of despotism" were cabled today by the Hellenic Liberal Democratic League of Chicago following a meeting of 300 members in its hall at 515 S. Halsted St., last night. The cable was sent to the "Chief of the Revolutionary Government," at Athens.

The league represents 30,000 people of Greek descent in Chicago," said Paul Demos, the President, with offices at 127 S. Dearborn St. "They are overwhelmingly in favor of a republican form of government for their native land. We believe Venizelos will return to take charge of the government. It is the only solution."
