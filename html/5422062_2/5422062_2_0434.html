Renomination of United States Senator Medill McCormick on the ground that he has endeavored to further the best interests of this country was urged in resolutions adopted by the American Association of the Greek Community of Chicago.

The resolutions were signed by Paul Demos, president; George Prassas, secretary and a committee composed of N. Nomicos, Andrew Karzas, T. K. Valos, E. M. Chirigos and J. Morton.
