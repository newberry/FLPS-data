A huge crowd was collected at the La Salle Hotel last Sunday to attend the annual banquet given by the Greek club, American Restaurant Ass'n. of America. All tables were filled with prominent people and well-known faces. Dinner was served at nine and speeches followed. Dancing started at 11 o'clock.

Officers of the club are G. Sotiropoulos, president; K. Tripilan, vice-president; H. Dinos, treasurer; G. Nicholson, secretary; and Messrs. Katasdiman, Kokonin, G. Kolliopoulos, T. Dimos, P. Kapsalis, G. Katsalin, Notin, and Patrinon, trustees. They are to be congratulated on this successful affair.
