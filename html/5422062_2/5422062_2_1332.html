The recently formed Club of Greek Professional Men in Chicago held its election for officers last Saturday. Fifty members were present at the election. The following were chosen: Dr. N. Salopoulos, honorary president; Dr. Spyro Zaph, president; Attorney John Gikas and Spyro Kotakis, vice-presidents; Geo Papanicolaou, secretary; Dr. Dem. Melidonis treasurer and Dr. Pan Chronopoulos, historian. Members of the Board: Attorney A. A. Pantelis, Dr. C. Thomas, Dr. C. Haloulos, Attorney Geo. Kyriakopoulos and Attorney Geo. Spannon.
