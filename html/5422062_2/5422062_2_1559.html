p. 5.- In the heart of the business district of Evanston is located the Davis Cafe. Messrs. N. Kallas, J. Theriopoulos and A. D. and K. Karahalis invite anyone in the vicinity of Evanston to drop in and visit them. They recently spent several thousand dollars in remodelling and redecorating their restaurant. The work was done by Pepas and Alex. It is now an aristocratic Evanston Cafe.
