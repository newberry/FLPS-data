p. 3 A new member is added to the Greek professionals class of Chicago. This addition, is not, as usually is the case, an attorney, but an Aesculapian.

The young doctor's name is Demetrios G. Hatzis, M.D. an honored graduate of medicine, from the Chicago Medical School. Dr. Hatzis, during his studies, was distinguished for his brilliant mind and undoubtedly, he will excel in his practice.

Congratulations to the young doctor.
