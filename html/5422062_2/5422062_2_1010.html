P. 1- Surely the world is not coming to an end, even if all values are destroyed. But the lives of men are being made hard,and society is suffering the consequences of the mismanagement of its affairs by men who are narrow in vision and ignorant of the eternal laws of change and progress.

The times call for sterner and sounder humanistic measures if social chaos is to be avoided, and the sons of man are to continue to breathe the air of freedom and security.
