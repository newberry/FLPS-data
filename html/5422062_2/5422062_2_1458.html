p.4. That the public likes the system of profit-sharing cafeterias lately established by Messrs. Panos Bemetrakopoulos of Sparta, for thirty years a resident of Chicago, and George Kouretsos of Tsipiana, who has had more than twenty years' experience in the restaurant business, is evident from the fact that they have opened three of them since last March.

Mr. Kouretsos states that experience has taught him what the public wants, and that he tries to satisfy it. He also says that recent government measures have not affected these cafeterias at all.
