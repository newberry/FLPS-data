H. W. Elmore and Company realtors 29 South La Salle St. sold real estate located at 79th st. and Center Ave. for a consideration of $20,000. to Mr. George Annes, restauratuer. Mr. B. Athanasopoulos was the salesman.

Mr. Geo. Annes, owner of the Annes Restaurant 55 East Congress, is considering the purchase of more real estate according to Mr. Athansopoulos.
