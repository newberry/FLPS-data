The newly organized Greek-American Club, whose chairman is General Thomas Hutchison, held an election of officers last week. Dr. Constantine Kallionzis was elected vice-president; Constantine Salopoulos (pharmacist), secretary; Mr. Lindas (attorney), treasurer; Messrs. Christ Damaskos (publisher), Constantine Mazarakis, and Thomas Mouzakiotis, advisers.

The purpose of this organization is to serve the political interests of our people. We hope that the membership will increase rapidly.
