p. 4.- A new doctor has entered our lists of prominent Greek professional men in Chicago. He is Evripides T. Nittis of Pafou, Cyprus. After graduating from the University of Athens, he went to Germany and attended the University of Berlin where he graduated with honors. He has also studied in Paris and at Oxford, taking up pathology, surgery and diagnosis.

In America he took up surgery in the University of Michigan and was situated at Harper Hospital for over a year. He has also been chief surgeon in a New York hospital. Deciding that he has a better future in Chicago, Dr. Nittis has come to join his brother, Saboas T. Nittis, doctor of Pathology.
