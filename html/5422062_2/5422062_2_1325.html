p. 2- Dr. Tassos Koliopoulos, nephew of John Koliopoulos, prosperous business man and president of Mortal Sleep of the Virgin Mary parish, has come to our city to complete his study of surgery.

This young physician, desiring to become a surgeon, prefers to pursue his studies in America because American hospitals have the most complete equipment of the latest surgical instruments, and their methods are likewise most modern.

We welcome him. We are glad that you are here, Dr. Koliopoulos!

G. F.
