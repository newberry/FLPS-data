Mr. S. Stavrakis and Mr. G. Katelanos, well-known Greek-American businessmen, have just opened a new, modern meat-and-fish market. Greek sausages will be available. The name of this market is the International. It is located at 614 South Halsted Street.

A barber shop was opened at 621 Blue Island Avenue by Mr. John Mavros. The name of this shop is the Minerva.

Mr. Pericles Camberis, very recently graduated from an embalming college, has opened a funeral parlor at 423 South Halsted Street.
