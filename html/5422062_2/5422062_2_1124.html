Churches, societies, veterans of all wars, even "Liberals" will gather today in Chicago to remember the 113th anniversary of Abraham Lincoln's birth.

P. S. Lambros, editor of the Greek Star, will compare Lincoln with Pericles before the First Methodist Sunday school at 10 A. M.

The great American and the great Greek had many points of similarity as statesmen, patriots, and orators, Mr. Lambros declares.
