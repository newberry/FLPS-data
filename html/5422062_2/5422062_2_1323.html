Hundreds of friends gathered at the railway station to bid good bye to Dr. Chronopoulos, prominent member of the Chicago Greek community, who goes on a two months visit to Greece.

Dr. Chronopoulos is known in the Chicago Greek colony as a distinguished professional, a true patriot, a strong advocator of Americanism, and is noted for his strong character. Saloniki joins with its many friends in wishing him bon voyage and a quick return.
