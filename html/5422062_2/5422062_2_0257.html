The United Greek-American Republican Clubs, comprising the Greek-American Republicans of Cook County, the North Side Greek-American Republican Club, the South Side Hellenic Republican Club, the West Side Greek-American Club, and the Greek-American Women's Republican Club of Cook County, unanimously endorse the candidacy of the Hon. Ruth Hanna McCormick, for United States Senator.

Mr. Ruth McCormick, as a public official and civic leader, has demonstrated that she is a real statesman and not a political demogogue.

Paul Demos, chairman.

A.G.N. Spannon, secretary.
