Sunday night at the La Salle Hotel a dinner was served in honor of Mrs. Ruth Hanna McCormick, candidate for Senator, at which sixty foreign-language newspapers were represented.

Girls dressed in their national costumes were the official guests of the evening. The spectacle was enchanting. Our nationality was represented by the charming Misses Katherine Milles and Pipitsa Kotaki. Those of our countrymen present were Mrs. Kotaki and her son, Messrs. P. Javaras, A. Georgopoulos, Paul Demos, Mr. and Mrs. Nickolson, and Mr. and Mrs. Valos.
