p. 8. John Raklios and Company Restauranteurs have leased the store east of the Randolph street entrance of the Capitol building, for an undisclosed number of years. The lessees will expend more than $50,000 in equipping the place as a high class restaurant. This will be the thirty-sixth restaurant to be opened by Mr. Raklios. He founded his business in 1900, and the thirtieth to be opened by the present system, which was organized in 1906.

Officers of John Raklios and Co. include Mr. Raklios, president; Andrew Chronis, secretary; William Cotsones, vice-president.
