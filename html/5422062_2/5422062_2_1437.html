A new Greek firm opened last Wednesday in Chicago under the name, C. E. Chipain and Company. This firm will handle imported and American liquors and wines. Its principal owner and manager is the active and progressive C. E. Chipianitis (Chipain), who is known for his social as well as his business leadership.

This week's Greek Press carries the firm's advertisement. Its offices are located in the Mid-City Bank Building, 803 West Madison Street.
