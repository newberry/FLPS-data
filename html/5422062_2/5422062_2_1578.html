The chocolate firm of Galanis Bros, which became famous for its Temptation Chocolates, has been reorganized. New and modern machinery has been installed to comply with the great production of the Temptation Chocolates. The reorganized firm will be knownas Temptation Company.

Galanis Bros. are to be congratulated for their great enterprise, which is a credit to themselves and to the Greek name as well. The firm is distinguished for its cleanliness, purity of quality and its secret method of making better chocolates.
