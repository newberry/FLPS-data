Last Sunday the Greek Professional Men's Club of Chicago held elections for the coming year. The officers elected are as follows:

Dentist P. Chronopoulos- president

Lawyer D. Parry First Vice-president

Lawyer K. Bouzanis Second vice-president

Lawyer C. Porikos-secretary

Lawyer K. Panoutsos-treasurer

Mr. K. Argyropoulos-historian

Trustees are J. Gekas, S. Zaph, E. Despotes, K. Koursoumis and P. Anasson.

The existence of the Professional Men's Club in our city is a great achievement and we are proud and happy to give its members first place, and they rightly earned it, above all other organizations.
