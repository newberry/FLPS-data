Last Monday, the 17th and 18th Wards chapter of Demosthenes political club held elections for the coming year. The officers elected are: J. Horitis, president; C. Karmiria, vice-president; E. Pikoulas, treasurer; A. Mitchell, Secretary; Mrs. K. Peppas, Recording Secretary; Advisors, P. Gargalionis and P. Pantopoulus. The Demosthenes club now has chapters in Wards 5, 6, 7, 8, 17 and 18.
