To the circle of the legal profession has been added, this year, Mr. Panos Anastasopoulos.

Mr. Anastasopoulos emigrated to America in 1906, attended American Schools to learn the English language, and having completed his courses at Hoffman Institute has entered DePaul University to study law. He graduated, this month, with the Degree L.L.B., and now is engaged in the office of the American Lawyer, Mr. Richard Hill.
