The Greek Professional Men's Club is glad to announce that His Holiness Damaskinos Korinthias will accompany the Greek Ambassador to the banquet on June 6. The Greek Consul, George Depastas will also be present. Anyone wishing to attend can make arrangements with the secretary, George Porikos, 134 N. LaSalle St.

The banquet will be given at the Palmer House, June 6, at 7 P. M.
