One of the most highly recommended of the sitting judges for re-election is the Honorable John P. McGoorty, who merits the vote of every citizen in the coming elections of November the 6th.

Judge McGoorty has a long and faithful career as a public servant in the thirty six years of his public life, first as a lawyer, later as Representative in the Illinois Legislature, and still later as Judge and chief Justice of the Appellate Court.

The Greeks of Chicago, all of whom are American citizens, and the Americans of Greek descent will, in a body, vote for the Hon. Judge John P. McGoorty, who is recommended by the Chicago Bar Association.
