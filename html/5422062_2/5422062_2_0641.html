The United States has issued a Victory Liberty Loan for the purpose of facilitating the return and discharge of our soldiers and sailors.

Onward, then, to duty again. Let us hurry and buy the Fifth Victory Loan in order to secure our money in the best way, and to accomplish our duty; and to thank and encourage our heroes who, seeing and feeling our gratitude to them, will show the same interest and willingness if--God forbid!--the same situation arises again.

Constantine Paleologos.
