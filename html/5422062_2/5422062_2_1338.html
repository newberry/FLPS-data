p. 4.--The Association of Chicago Greek Shoeshiners will hold its first annual picnic on Sunday, August 12, 1923. The location which they have chosen is the charming Forest Park grounds.

The committee in charge has made all necessary preparations, and those who attend will carry away very pleasant impressions.

The program includes athletics, dancing, and solos on various musical instruments.

The price of tickets is fifty cents each, and children escorted by parents will be admitted free.
