After five years of effort attending Marshall Law School and North-Western University, Mr. E. Koliopoulos, young and progressive member of our community, graduated with honors.

The young lawyer is one of the most popular young Greeks in Chicago a self-made man, hard worker, author of various books beneficial to the community and the first one among the Greeks in Chicago to establish a Greek-American printing shop during the year of 1902.

The management of Loxias sincerely congratulates Mr. Koliopoulos and holds him up as a good example of a Greek immigrant.
