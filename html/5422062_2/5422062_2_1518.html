Another new restaurant opened its doors yesterday. It is owned by B. Karagiannis and N. Selimus at 810 S. Wabash.

The restaurant owned by Mr. Karagiannis which had been situated there for eight years is no more. The new enlarged, modern restaurant has taken its place.

The beautiful furnishings are the work of Pappas and Alex Co., under direct supervision of Mr. Mazarakis.

Their grand opening day was a great success yesterday. We wish them the same success in the future.
