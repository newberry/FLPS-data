Mr. P. S. Lambros,

Publisher, The Greek Star,

130 North Wells Street,

Chicago, Illinois.

Dear friend:

Many thanks for your good letter of October 26, and for the copy of the Greek Star.

I wish to thank you for the active interest which you have taken in my candidacy for the office of governor, and I want you to know that I am deeply appreciative of the loyal support which you are giving me.

With best wishes, I am,

Sincerely yours, Louis L. Emmerson.
