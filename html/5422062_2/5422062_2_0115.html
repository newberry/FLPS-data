Leaders of Greek-American activities in Cook County, representing the churches, societies, business and professions of that national group, today unanimously endorsed Frank Knox, editor and publisher of the Daily News, for the Republican presitial nomination.

They took the action at a luncheon in the Hotel La Salle at which the publisher was guest of honor. Paul Demos, Chicago attorney, presided.

Mr. Demos related how the publisher, through the Carnival of Nations, which was sponsored by The Daily News, had aided all racial groups in metropolitan Chicago to raise funds for the care of their indigent and declared that, "Now we can show our appreciation to him for that help."
