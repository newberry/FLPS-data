p. 3.--Among those who seek the cool and refreshing breezes of summer resorts to avoid the heat of the city are those below listed who with their families are enjoying their vacations at John Maglaris's Emery Fruit Farm resort.

Const. Karydes, A. Stergiou, Bas. Englezos, Dem. Karakatshneos, the Nikolopoulos brothers, and N. Roussos.
