The ten apartment building located at the N. W. Corner of North Avenue and Rockwell Sts. has been sold by George Demos to Hilbert Nyman for $70,000 subject to $45,000 mortgage.

Mr. Demos took in exchange three parcels of vacant lots. A. H. Tomaras, 5705 N. Clark St. and Walter E. Prigge, 2460 Devon Ave. were brokers and Geo. A. Jacob, 139 N. Clark St. represented Mr. Demos.
