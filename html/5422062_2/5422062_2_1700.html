The Greek department of the Mid-City Trust and Savings Bank, located at Madison and Halsted Streets, is managed now by Mr. Peter Sarelas, who was employed by the bank for that purpose, taking the place of Mr. Geo. Stathopoulos who is leaving for Greece.

Mr. Sarelas is a graduate of Political Economy from the University of Chicago and has at his command four different languages. Greeks who are doing business with this bank will find Mr. Sarelas willing to offer his services upon request.
