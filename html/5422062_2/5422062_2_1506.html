J. Kokinatos, sponsor and organizer of the Chicago branch of the Bank of Athens and now a cashier of the branch in New York, has arrived in Chicago to temporarily fill the place of D. Poulakis, Manager of the Chicago branch, who has left for a trip in the country.

Mr. Kokinatos, a former citizen of Chicago, is known for his preponderance in banking affairs, and his many friends are delighted to hear of his return to Chicago.
