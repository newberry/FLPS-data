p. 2 . -- The Greek community of Chicago has been enriched by a new addition to the medical profession, Dr. G. P. Gavaris, the new Aesculapius.

Dr. Gavaris before he came to Chicago spent five years in Germany as associate physician in the famous clinics of Kraus, Lesser, and Czerny. He is a graduate of the National University of Athens and was an interne in the well-known Greek Evangelismos Hospital of Athens. He is a noted pathologist.

Dr. Gavaris recently obtained his license to practice here and has opened his offices in the Masonic Temple.
