p. 8.- The Greek Company, Indiana Village, whose president is the progressive Angelos Masouras, has closed a deal for a ten year rental at $200,000 with the Jackson Blvd. Corporation.

The new place, which will be opened as an up-to-date restaurant, is at 304-306 South Lills St. The broker for the transaction was George Poulos of the firm George Poulos and Co., 1535 Irving Park Blvd.
