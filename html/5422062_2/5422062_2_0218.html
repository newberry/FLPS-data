On Wednesday, March 27, a political dance was given by the Greek-American Voters' League in celebration of the Greek Independence and in honor of Mayor Edward Kelly. The dance was given in the Cameo Room of the Morrison Hotel and among the speakers were the guest of honor, Mayor Kelly, who said that we should re-elect him as Mayor. Mr. Stammon, who spoke in Greek, urged the Greeks to support the Mayor. The large crowd was entertained by Eddie Varzos' Orchestra and the vocal selections of Mr. and Mrs. Minas Booras.
