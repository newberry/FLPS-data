p. 2. Mr. Iason Kokinatos, of 30 N. La Salle street, representative of the Bank of Athens, informs the Greeks of Chicago that the above said Bank, is the biggest commercial bank in Greece. It has seventy branches within and without Greece.

In all its branches it issues checks in drachmas and in dollars. When you send money to Greece, tell our local representative, or your local bank, whether the money should be paid in drachmas or in American dollars.

Deposits in the Bank of Athens are: 1,200,000,000 drachmas.

Bank of Athens, 25 Pine Street, New York.
