p. 3--Last week the Greek Political Club of Cicero held its regular election, and the following officers were elected: Demetrios Betines, president; Dem. Fotakis, vice-president; Nickolas Karambellas, treasurer; John Dariotes, secretary; George Kokines, Constantine Lebesis, and Pet. Constantopoulos,members of the board, and Mickolaos Berkos, legal counsellor.
