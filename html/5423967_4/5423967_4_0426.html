Supplement to the Report.

For the first four years of our existence we have succeeded in collecting and sending for the exiled and prisoners, the victims of the tsarist regime, $2,795. It is true, we have collected much more, but half of everything collected was spent for the arrangement of our entertainments. If we had more members it would not be necessary to spend so much for the undertakings, and probably a large part of all collected money would aid the victims of i.

We do all in our power to make our affairs a success, but the results depend upon the public.
