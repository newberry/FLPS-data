The meeting starts at 10:15 P. M. without any chairman as Mr. Prokopov has refused to participate in the cultural work of the Center.

There is only one item on the order of the business of the day: the election of new officers.

After a short discussion the balloting took place and the following persons were elected for the period from October 14, 1927, to October 14, 1928:

Dr. L. G. Pertsov-Kaluzhin, chairman; Mr. L. J. Kruge, secretary; Mr. K. L. Kotsyubinsky, treasurer. The meeting was adjourned at 10:55 P. M. The next meeting will be held on Friday, October 21, at 7:30 P. M. in the same place.
