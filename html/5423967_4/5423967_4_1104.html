This convention was to take place at Chicago from September 15 to September 18, 1927. Russian, Ukrainian and some other Slavonic Evangelical Christians had decided to send their representatives to the convention. The following well-known Russian evangelical preachers were expected to go to the Convention: Mr. I. S. Prokhanov, who would come from Soviet Russia, P. Deneyka, I Kolesnikov and others. The Rev. John Johnson, preacher at the "House of the Gospel," was to represent the Russian Evangelical Christians of Chicago.
