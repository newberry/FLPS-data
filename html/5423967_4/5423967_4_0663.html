The Russians in Chicago have lived chiefly on the West. Side. One of these areas is that bounded on the east by Jefferson Street, on the north by Taylor Street, on the south by Sixteenth Street, and on the west by Racine. Other areas are Milwaukee Avenue between Halsted and Robey, Robey between North and Grand, south Grand Avenue between Robey and Ashland, and North Avenue between Ashland and Robey.
