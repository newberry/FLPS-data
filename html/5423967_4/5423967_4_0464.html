R.Novik, 24 years old, residing at 2330 N. Sacramento Avenue, is to be tried in court for an attempt to commit suicide. After a collision of an automobile with a truck, as a result of which Mr. Novik and his companion, Miss Lillian Leyton, 19 years old, were dangerously injured, the police found in Mr. Novik's pocket a note proving the intention of both young people to commit suicide.
