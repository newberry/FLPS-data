Three Gospels of John Johnson at the Home of the Evangelist.

1. "The Kingdom Where There is Frequent Crying." Sunday, November 13, 1921, 7 P. M.

2. "The Kingdom Where There is Eternal Crying." Sunday, November 20, 1921, 7 P. M.

3. "The Kingdom Where There is No Crying." Sunday, November 27, 1921, 7 P. M.

Come and hear these interesting themes, also music and beautiful singing.

The House of the Evangelist is located in Chicago, 2127 Crystal Street, between Hoyne Avenue and Leavitt Street, a block north of Division Street.
