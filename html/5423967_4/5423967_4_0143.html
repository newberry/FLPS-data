On Friday evening, being at the headquarters of the Russian-American Citizens' Organization, I had the opurtunity to pet from Miss Eve Thomas, secretary of the Russian Women's Club Rodina (Kative Country) information about this your organization, by talk with Miss Thomas lasted half an hour. I leaned from her that follows:

The Russian Women's Club Rodina was founded on April 30, 1935. The club received has charter on February 18,1936. The membership of the club consists of 28 full-fledged members, accurately paying their monthly dues.

The initiation fee is 50 cents; who monthly dues 25 cents.

Every summer the club arranges two pionics; in also gives every year several evening entertainments.

The object of the club Rodina is to give material and moral aid to 2its members in cases of misfortune, disease, etc.

Pecuniary aid is given and its amount is defined according to the decision of the general assembly.

The fund of the club consists at present of $235.65.

Mrs. Mary Kiersch is the president of the club. Miss Eve Thomas is the secretary.

The Russian Women's Club Rodina was organized at the initiative of Miss Eve Thomas.

D. Stranden.
