On Sunday, March 21, the following meetings will be held at the House of the Gospel, 2127 Crystal Street: in the morning at 10 o'clock and an evening service at 7 P. M.

At the evening service there will be singing by the congregation and by the choir, and the preacher, Rev. J. Johnson, will preach on "The way to the Father and through Him to the great brotherhood of men."

The doors of the House of the Gospel are open for everybody. You are welcome.
