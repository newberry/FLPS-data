The assembly is called to order at 9 p. m. Present: Mr. V. D. Maslov, Mr. K. L. Kotsyubinsky, Dr. L. G. Pertsov, Mrs. S. D. Pertsov, Mr. S. C. Prokopov, Mrs. Y. M. Prokopov, Mr. J. S. Zatsarinny, and Mr. J. Kashirkin.

Order of the business of the day:

1) Reading of the minutes.

2) Resignation of the chairman.

3) Elections

4) Financial report.

5) Admission of new members.

2

The secretary, Mr. Maslov, reads the minutes after the chairman, Mr. Prokopov, talked a long time concerning these minutes. After that Mr. Prokopov resigns the office of chairman, and Mr. Maslov, the secretary, takes his place and conducts the session right to the end.

As the membership of the Center is very small it is resolved to let Mr. Maslov, the secretary, fulfill also the duties of chairman and treasurer.
