The above named Society was organized in Chicago on June 7th of this year, with the direct participation of: A. G. Bush, Mrs. O. I. Lisov, Mrs. A. V. Meklashevsky and A. I. Iosefovitch. This organization was warmly received not only by the Russian Colony, but also by American groups. The constitution and by-laws have been already framed and approved.

With the opening of the season the Womens' Committee of the Society began its work. Let us hope that all those who understand the meaning and importance of this organization will do their utmost to come to its assistance. In the near future the official addresses will be announced, where donations may be sent, and where persons may enroll for membership.
