To-morrow, Mar. 31, representatives of R. I. M. A. S. (Russian Independent Mutual Aid Society) will speak over the radio for the first time. The first speaker will be G. Volos, president of the society, who will speak on "Ideals of the Russian immigrant in America." Tune your sets to station WEDC, 1210 kilocycles, at 9 o'clock in the morning.
