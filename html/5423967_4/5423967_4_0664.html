Mr. Pikiel is chairman of the Russian-American Citizens' Organization, of which Mr. George L. Percy (Pertsov) is chairman of the board of supervisors.

The aims of this organizations are: (1) To give information to Russian immigrants concerning the duties in connection with American citizenship, (2) To give free assistance in obtaining first and second citizenship papers, (3) To aid veterans in filling out forms, applications, etc.

Interview took place on October 29, 1936.
