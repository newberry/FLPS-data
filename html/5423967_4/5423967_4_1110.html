Mr. I. S. Prokhanov, leader of Evangical Christians in Russia, had already visited the U. S. in the autumn of 1925 and spoken at many Russian and American meetings. It is announced that, having come again to the U. S. from Leningrad, he intends to speak on June 12th, 1926, at the "House of the Gospel", 2127 Crystal St., in Chicago. Later he was going to visit the Russian farmers of North Dakota among which there are many Mennonites.
