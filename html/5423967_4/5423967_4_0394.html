Saturday, August 27, 1921, by the Chicago Committee of Relief to the Starving in Russia, a massmeeting is arranged at Douglas Park Auditorium, 3202 Ogden Avenue, corner Kedzie Avenue. There will be speakers in various languages. Moving pictures from Soviet Russia will be shown; there will also be a concert. Beginning at 7:30 P. M. Admission free. All proceeeds will be used to aid the starving in Russia.
