In this number Dr. Krasnow placed a short notice, stating that attorney Louis went to Granite City, studied the case, saw the lawyer, who was previously in charge of the case, also saw the prisoner proper, who "appears to be a typical, semi-savage son of Mother-Russia".

Dr. Krasnow, who signed himself G. K. feels confident of success, but stresses the need for more money--sixty to seventy dollars. He points out that "the 60 day reprieve will end exactly when the summer vacations of the Supreme Court will begin, thus 3 or 4 more months will be had for preparing a new defense.

"More funds are needed."
