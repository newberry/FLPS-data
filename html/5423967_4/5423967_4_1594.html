to a meeting called for the purpose of working out the program of a solemn celebration of the bi-centenary of the Russian Academy of Sciences.

The committee of the Russian Center of the city of Chicago wishes to believe that as to this question the Russian colony of Chicago will not be divided into various partisan groups. This celebration is a holiday for all Russians, not only for these who are at present in their native land, but also for these who are outside its boundaries.

The meeting will take place at the House of Enlightenment, 1080 W. 14th street, on Tuesday, Sept [gap] beginning at 7:30 P. M. sharp. We carnestly beg you not to be too late.

The Secretary of the Russian Center of the City of Chicago.
