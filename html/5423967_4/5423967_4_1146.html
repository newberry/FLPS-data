Third Mass Meeting of chosen (isbrannykh) Christians will be held Saturday evening, February 22nd, 1919 in the hall of John Smith Public School, on 13th Street, between Morgan and Blue Island Avenue. The topic of the gospel is: "The second coming of Jesus Christ." A special Russian choir will present a program.

The meeting will begin exactly at 7 P.M. All men, women and children are invited. Admission is free.
