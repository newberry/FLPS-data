Sunday, October 28, 1923, in the large hall of the South Side School, 1231 S. Morgan Street, at 4 P.M., a large White Russian meeting will be held. The speaker has recently arrived from Russia; he is a well known White Russian political leader, J. J. Voronko. White Russians of Chicago and suburbs, come to the meeting. Admission free. Hall open at 3:30 P.M.

White Russian National Society
