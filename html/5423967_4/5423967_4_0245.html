"Watching constantly the case of Nikolai Gevrilovich, sentenced to die on the gallows, we were happy to hear about the reprieve. Not giving up hope for further success of the case, I suggested to my comrades to help according to their possibilities towards the defense of Nikolai Gavrilovich."

Respectfully,

A constant reader of Russkoe Slovo

S. Voronzov.

Follows a list of such names, as Marinov, Shevchenko, Khaim Berman from Orchard Street--and so on--with a total, including previous entries, of $16.50 contributed.
