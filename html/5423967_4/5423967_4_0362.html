"On the first of February there was held here a very successful entertainment for the benefit of the Fund for Aid to Students, Writers, and Artists in Moscow. This fund, as is known, has existed for several years in New York, but had a few members in Chicago. Some time ago several Chicago members of this organization, in view of the increasing need for this fund, conceived the idea of forming a local committee which would be a section of the New York Fund.

"The best talent of the Russian community took part."
