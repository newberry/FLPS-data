In the Russian children's schools maintained by R. I. M. A. S. (Russian Independent Mutual Aid Society) preparations are being made for the concert and entertainment scheduled for Saturday, May 4, at 917 North Wood Street. The school committee requests the Russian people in Chicago to mark the date on the calendar and attend the concert arranged for the benefit of the schools.

Arrangements have been made for a good musical program.

All friendly Russian organizations are requested not to schedule any of their own entertainments for this date.

Tickets should be bought in advance.
