In Chicago was organized a new Lithuanian building and loan society under the name "Palangas Lietuviai," (The Lithuanians from Palanga). They have a permit from the state of Illinois to sell the shares. The first official meeting will be held Saturday, January 19, at 7:30 P. M. in the hall at 168 W. 18th St.

All Lithuanians are invited to come and learn about this society's purpose.
