It was announced in Lietuva that Sernas, editor of Lietuva, will deliver a lecture on geography.

Those who want to get a clearer understanding on what geography is, should come on January 13, 1907, to Freiheit Turner Hall, 3417 S. Halsted St., at 3 P. M. Admission is 10 cents.

You are cordially invited.

A. nn, Secretary.
