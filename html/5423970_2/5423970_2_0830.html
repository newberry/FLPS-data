A share-holder's meeting of the Vilnis Publishing Co., publishers of the semi-weekly newspaper Vilnis, was held on Jan. 31, at the Vilnis hall, 3116 So. Halsted St. Sixty shareholders attended the meeting. Ways and means for successfully conducting the approaching campaign to convert the Vilnis into a daily newspaper were discussed.

Over $3,000 worth of shares in the new newspaper company have been sold. It was decided to sell at least $8,000 worth of shares in Chicago before the launching of the new daily Vilnis.
