March 19, the Lithuanian Dramatic Circle presented a four-act comedy, "The Thief," at the Hull House Theater. The comedy was written in Latvian by Pleir. It was translated and Lithuanianized into a story of Lithuanian peasant life. The comedy is full of life and humor. The actors played well. The audience was satisfied.

We are glad to mention that the Dramatic Circle has made much progress in their performance of plays.
