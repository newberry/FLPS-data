The Lietuva Band is a truly Lithuanian musical society, composed of the finest musicians.

The Lietuva Band plays the latest dance music. It plays Lithuanian, European and American dance music, and the classic music of the best composers. Honorable patriots!

When you need music for any purpose, call the Lietuva Band. We practice every Friday, at 8:30, at Juozas Ridikas' Hall, 3253 Illinois Court.
