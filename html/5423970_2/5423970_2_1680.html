p.8.... The Lithuanian Improvement and Benefit Club invites all Lithuanians to attend meetings of the club, which will be held every other Sunday afternoon of each month, at 10501 So.Michigan Ave., Roseland, Ill.

All Roseland and Kensington Lithuanians are invited to attend the meetings and join this club, because this club is pledged to promote worthy and lofty ideals. It was organized Jan.11, 1915.
