The Lithuanian Alliance of America, a national fraternal organization, has ten lodges in Chicago with a total membership of six hundred twelve Lithuanians.

The numbers and membership of the lodges, as of July 1, 1909, is as follows: 36th, 225 members; 74th, 79 members; 109th, 55 members; 122nd, 102 members; 129th, 47 members; 139th, 25 members; 183rd, 17 members; 191st, twelve members; 226th, 39 members; 234th, eleven members.
