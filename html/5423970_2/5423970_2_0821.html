Mr. and Mrs. Gribinas gave a surprise party in honor of their son and daughter, William and Anna, at the home of Mr. and Mrs. Emparas, Feb. 13. Many relatives and friends attended the party.

During the course of the party, Mr. Gribinas spoke on the present nation-wide campaign to convert the semi-weekly Vilnis into a daily newspaper. He asked the guests to support the campaign with donations. A collection was taken and netted $36. The money was turned over to the Vilnis campaign committee.
