The Society of the Motherland Lovers, Number One, presented a program at the St. George Parish Hall last Sunday. A one-act comedy, "An Eye for an Eye, a Tooth for a Tooth," was presented by the artists of the Youth Circle.

The performance cannot be described as successful. It was hampered by the failure of the actors to raise their voices.

The infants which some women brought to the affair also distracted the audience's attention. A monologue, rendered after the play, was successful. There was dancing after the program. The attendance was large.
