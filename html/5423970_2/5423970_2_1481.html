Besides the Aurora Society which has its regular lecture forum, this winter the Lithuanian library on the west side is having its own lecture forum. The lectures will be held at the Lithuanian Library Hall, 2242 W. 23rd Place. On the lecture list, we see such prominent persons as Dr. Rutkauskas, St. Biezis, Mrs. Rutkauskas, C. Kasputis, and P. Zalatoris.

January 12, St. Biezis will deliver a lecture entitled, "The Sun, and the Eclipse of the Moon and the Sun".

Last Sunday, at the same Library Hall, Dr. Zimontas read a lecture on the subject of dental hygiene. The doctor explained the effect of bad teeth upon physical health.
