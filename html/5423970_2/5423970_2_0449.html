The Lithuanian Liberty Youth Musical and Dramatic Society, will present for the first time a farce, "The Actor of the Burned Theater," written by J. Uktveris, Sunday, October 19, 1913, at Freiheit Turner Hall, 3417 So. Halsted Street. Curtain at 5 P. M; admission twenty-five, thirty-five, and fifty cents.

This will be the first time this merry drama has ever been presented, and it should be very interesting to see why it is called "The Actor of the Burned Theater." After the performance, the choir of the society will entertain. Dancing will follow.

The Committee.
