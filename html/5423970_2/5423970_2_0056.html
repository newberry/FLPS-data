In recent days, Lithuanians have established a wholesale coal business, The Vilija Company, 3702 South Spaulding Avenue (office at 3815 South Kedzie Avenue), in the new Lithuanian colony in the district of Kedzie Avenue and 38th Street. The new company was incorporated at $10,000. The president and manager of this new company is Mr. M. M. Dudas.

[Translator's note: Vilija is the name of a river in Lithuania. It is a tributary of the river Nemunas (Niemen) at Kaunas, Lithuania. At present, Vilija has been changed to Neris.]
