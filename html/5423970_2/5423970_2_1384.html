On September 12th we will open the dancing school at the Szemaitis Hall, 632 S. Canal St. Every Wednesday and Friday at 8 P. M. we will give dancing lessons.

Every young man or girl who wants to learn to dance should come and learn. Admission for men, 25 cents; for girls, free.

J. Sabaliauskas and Ant. Rimgaila

Instructors.
