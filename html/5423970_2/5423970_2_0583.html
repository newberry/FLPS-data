The St. Francisco Society will present a play, "The Bethlehem Fellows," on Saturday, December 25, 1903, at Pulaski Hall, 800 S. Ashland Avenue. The performance will start at 7:30 P. M. After the play there will be a banquet.

Admission is 25, 35 and 50 cents.

All Lithuanians are cordially invited to come and see this beautiful performance, and to have a pleasant time at the banquet.

Committee.
