Last Sunday, the Birutes Choir presented for the first time in Chicago, the drama, "In the Mud". The actors enacted their roles very well, especially Miss Joanna Mickevicaite in the role of "Anna" and Julius Juska as "Michael".

After the performance the Birutes Choir, directed by Mr. Pocius, sang several songs. The Birutes Choir is getting better and better. Their singing that night was excellent.

April 25, the Birutes Choir will have a family evening at the Mark White Square Park Hall. They will present a one-act comedy, "Parody of Love". After the play, the choir will sing various songs. Admission free.
