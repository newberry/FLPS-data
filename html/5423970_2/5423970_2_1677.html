Last Sunday, October 17, the yearly anniversary celebration of the Simanas Daukantas Society was held. This organization is one of the largest and most popular Lithuanian benefit societies in Chicago.....

The program consisted of music and orations. Attorney F. P. Bradchulis and J. A. Chmieliauskas addressed the audience. The latter spoke on the cultural and political problems of Lithuanian-Americans; the former delivered a tirade against saloons. The musical program was provided by the chorus of the Young Lithuanian-American Nationalist Club.
