p.6.... The Federation of Chicago Lithuanian Societies has now established its headquarters at 3112 S. Halsted St. Starting Jan. 4th, the office of the Federation will be open to the public from 9:00 A.M. to 6:00 P.M. Lithuanians who are in need of relief, assistance to obtain employment, or desire any information, can now apply at this office.

Anyone who is able to donate money, cast off clothing and bedding for relief purposes may do so at the office of the Federation.

The Federation publishes a monthly publication entitled "Our Organization." All Chicago Lithuanian societies are invited to use this publication as a medium for the furtherance of their aims and activities.
