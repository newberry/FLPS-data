On Sunday, May 13, the Simonas Daukantas Society will participate in the 10th jubilee of the Duke Gedeminas Society. On that day, at 2 P. M., all the members of the Simonas Daukantas Society are to come to the hall at 106 Cleaver St, from where, with music and in full parade, we will march to the place of celebration. Every member is to wear the society's national emblem and white gloves. Any member not attending the parade will be fined $1.00, unless reasonable cause be shown for not participating in the parade.

S. Abromaviczia, President)
