We are requested to announce that the recently founded Lithuanian Music School at 33rd and Morgan Streets will be opened Saturday, September 5. All who are interested are invited to visit and inspect the school.

The enrollment period runs from September 5 to September 12. The scholastic year will begin the following Monday. The school will be divided into several classes, each of which will be provided with a special teacher.

The organizers of the school are A. Pocius and A. Alexandravicius. [Translator's note: The latter later shortened his name to Aleksis.]
