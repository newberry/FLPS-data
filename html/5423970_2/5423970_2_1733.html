The Lithuanian Tailors' Mutual Aid Club, which was recently organized in the North Side Chicago Lithuanian colony, is growing very fast in membership. This club now has 150 members. It is headed by J. Mickevicius.

The Club has launched a new membership campaign in an effort to get all the Lithuanian tailors of Chicago to join.

One of the main purposes of the Club is to promote, In every possible way, the intellectual advancement of our people. In this connection the Club is making plans to establish a public Lithuanian-American library.
