(1). December 17, at the Aurora Hall, 3149 S. Halsted Street, Mr. J. O. Sirvidas will speak on the future of our youth.

(2). December 17, at the Elias Hall, 46th and Wood Streets, Mr. K. Jurgelionis, will deliver a lecture entitled "The Mexican Indians and Their Language." Additional speeches will be made by J. Jankauskas, and K. Mikolaitis. Admission--ten cents.

(3). December 15, at the Aurora Hall, Mr. Akelaitis lecture on natural history will be delivered to the children. The adults are also invited. The lecture will be illustrated. Admission free.
