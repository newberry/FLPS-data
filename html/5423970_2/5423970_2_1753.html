On the Town of Lake district was organized a new society under the name of "St. Vincent Ferr." The society is a sick and death benefit one, with the aim in view of organizing a new Roman-Catholic parish, building a church and a school.

The first meeting will be held Sunday, March 9, at 3 P. M., at C. S. Radzeviczius Hall, 4514 S. Paulina St. All Lithuanians of good will are invited. The by-laws are ready and will be read at this meeting.

Committee.
