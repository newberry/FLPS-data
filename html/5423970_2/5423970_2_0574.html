The St. John the Baptist Lithuanian Society will present "Tarnas Be Kantrybes" (A Servant With out Patience), a comedy, on Sunday evening, February 7, at the Freiheit Turner Hall, 3417-21 So. Halsted Street. This will be the fourth theatrical presentation of the society.

Dancing will be enjoyed after the play. Admission will be twenty-five and thirty-five cents. All Lithuanians are invited to attend and spend a joyous evening.
