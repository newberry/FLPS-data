An affair "De Luxe," music superb a fascinating assembly of dancers and entertainment that will please all except those who fail to attend the annual dance of the year, will be sponsored by the well-known Knights of Lithuania, Council Four, on Saturday evening Oct. 18, 1930, at the Parish Auditorium, 18th Street and Union Avenue.

Again, we mention music. This time to inform you that, "Frank's Original Collegiate Nite Owls" and their entertainers are sure to please all. The above is only one of many events of the ensuing year which you will hear of, in fact, you will want to hear about them after you have attended "The Annual Dance."
