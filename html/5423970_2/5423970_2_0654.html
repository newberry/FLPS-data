The Aurora Society fair will be held on September 25, 26, 27 and 28, 1913, at the Aurora Hall, 3149 South Halsted Street.

Every day there will be dancing with music by the Sarpalius brothers, and each day there will be a different program. September 25 will be the evening of the Lithuanian Socialist Alliance of America, Branch 81, Choir. Friday, September 26, there will be soloists. On Saturday, September 27, the Birute Choir will sing; and on Sunday, September 28, the Liberty Youth Choir will sing. Admission will be ten cents. On September 25, 26 and 27, the fair will open at 7:30 P. M., and on Sunday, September 28, it will open at 2 P. M.

The variety in the program will satisfy old and young, boys and girls. We are inviting all of you to come in order to support the Aurora Library where you can get books to read free, and also to support the day and evening school.

The Aurora Committee.
