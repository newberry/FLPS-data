The board of directors of the west Side Lithuanian Public Library held a meeting last week at which it was reported that a donation of twenty-five dollars for the upkeep of the library had been received from the local Dr. V. Kudirka Society. This Society is one of several local Lithuanian organizations that are supporting the library.

A decision was made at the meeting to add newspapers printed in the Russian, Polish and English languages to the library. Thus far, newspapers in the Lithuanian language only were available at the library.
