The Birutes Society presents two beautiful and humorous comedies: "Do Not Oppose," and "The Bear" on May 4, at Meldazis Hall, 2242 W. 23rd Place. The performance will begin at six o'clock; admission, twenty-five cents and up.

This is the last Birutes production of this season. Come and see the humorous comedies and hear the Birutes Choir sing beautiful songs.

After the program, we will have a real Lithuanian banquet, a rose dance etc. The best dancers will receive rewards for such dances as the Polka, Hungarian and Round Dance.
