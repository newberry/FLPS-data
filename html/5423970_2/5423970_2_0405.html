The Alliance of Chicago Lithuanian Dramatic Societies held a social gathering at the Elijosius Hall, 46th and Wood Streets, last Saturday. The purpose of the gathering was to provide an opportunity for the members of the various societies to become acquainted.

A short program was also presented. The quartet (violin, cello, flute, and piano) of the Chicago Lithuanian Concert Club rendered three musical selections. Miss A. Ulkis gave a recitation and M. M. Juska related several laugh-provoking anecdotes. Mr. Strimaitis, B. Vargsas, Dr. K. Drangelis, K. Jurgelionis and several other representatives of various societies spoke.
