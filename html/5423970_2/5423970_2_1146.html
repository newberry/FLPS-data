The first number of Jaunimo Sapnai (Dreams of Youth) has appeared. Jaunimo Sapnai is the organ of the Lithuanian Students' Alliance. It was formerly published as a part of the Laisvoji Mintis. At the last Students' Convention, held in Chicago, it was decided to issue it independently..... Jaunimo Sapnai is to be published every two months. The price is $1.00 a year. The address: S. Biezis, 2310 S. Leavitt Street, Chicago, Illinois..... [Translator's note--Laisvoji Mintis was published in Scranton, Pa.]
