Saturday, September 2, at Pulaski Hall, 800 S. Ashland Avenue, there will be a first Lithuanian concert. There will be ten concertina players and players on other instruments. After the concert there will be a dance. Also a few Lithuanian spectacles will be presented by C. Zilewicze.

Admission 35 cents. All Lithuanians are cordially invited.

C. Zilewicze.
