The Drama Society presented Sudermann's four-act drama "St. John's Day" at the Hull House Theater last Sunday evening. The participating actors were B. Vaitiekunas, O. Stravinskiute, M. Montviliute, J. Briedis, M. Damijonaitiene, J. Sankunas, P. Sirvinckas, R. Slikiene, and A. Klovaite..... A. Pocius played the organ before the play and between acts.
