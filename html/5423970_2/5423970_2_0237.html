The Kanklu (Lithuanian Guitar) Chorus is now holding three song and theatrical rehearsals every week. Director Kvedaras and the entire chorus are making intensive preparations to present "Karnivalio Varpai" (Carnival Bells). A beautiful, large Chicago theatre has been rented for the presentation of the play.

This chorus, which is the most famous Lithuanian chorus in Chicago, is making preparations to present several plays and to participate in important programs.

2

The chorus is making plans to present "Is Meiles" (Because of Love), in Cicero, Ill. The chorus will present "Aukso Dievaitis" (God of Gold), in Roseland, I11., on April 24. The chorus will participate in the program of the International Labor Defense on March 27.

It is very pleasant to note how hard our proletarian artists are working for the benefit of the working class.
