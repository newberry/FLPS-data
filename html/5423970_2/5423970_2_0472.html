Last Saturday the Drama Society presented the comedy, "Matchmaking," at the St. Peter's Parish Hall, in Kenosha. The hall was crowded with people. The Kenosha Lithuanians were very much satisfied with the performance.

The Drama Society is preparing to present the tragedy, "Macbeth," which has been translated by K. Jurgelionis. As soon as they obtain qualified artists, they will begin rehearsing.
