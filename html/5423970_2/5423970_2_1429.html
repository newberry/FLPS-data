Whether lawbreakers should be punished will be the subject of a debate to be sponsored by Chapter 81 of the Lithuanian Socialist Alliance on Saturday, March 11. The debaters will be A. J. Karalius for the affirmative, and P. Galskis for the negative. All those interested in this subject are invited to attend. All members of the Chapter should be present, since the regular monthly meeting will be held after the debate.

The debate will begin at 7:30 P. M. in the hall located at 1182 Milwaukee Avenue.
