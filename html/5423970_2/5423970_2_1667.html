The South Englewood branch of the Lithuanian Socialist Alliance, Chapter 170, held its monthly meeting on February 13.

Among other things, the illness of our writer, Bruno Vargsas, was discussed at the meeting. The members agreed willingly to a suggestion that a collection be taken up for his benefit.

The following donated: [A long list of names follows]. The total sum donated, $11.05, was turned over to Bruno Vargsas.

Sincere thanks are extended to all the donors.
