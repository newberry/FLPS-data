According to statistics presented us, the Aurora Society presents the following picture: there are 180 library book-users; the reading room is used daily by about thirty to forty persons; during this summer season, the Aurora School has had a total of fifty-six students. The evening school is attended by forty-six persons, thirty-four of them studying the English language, twelve studying arithmetic. The day school is attended by ten students: six are studying English and arithmetic, four are taking drafting (on Sundays).
