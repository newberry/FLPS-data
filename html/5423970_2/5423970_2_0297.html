On November 30, at the Stancik Hall, 205 East 115th Street, the Lithuanian Musical Society "Aidas" (The Echo) will present a one-act drama, "Solomon's Dream" (by B. Vargsas). The Lithuanian Music and Dramatic Society and the Echo Choir will sing. There will be recitations and dances.

[The performance] will start at 4:30 P.M. Admission is twenty-five, thirty-five, and fifty cents.

All Lithuanians are invited.

The Committee.
