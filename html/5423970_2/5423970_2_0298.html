Program:

1. Overture: "Encouragement"....

Boettger. The Birutes Orchestra.

2. a. "Hope March"....P. Guido.

b. Moderato Cantabile....Ch. Dancla.

c. Barcarolle....Beethoven.

d. Turkish March....Beethoven.

Violin and piano: Sarpalius, Jereck, Bigelis, and Bijanskis at the piano.

3. Overture: "Lovers Return".....A. Nelson. The Birutes Orchestra.

4. a. "Moderato Cantabile"....Ch. Dancla. Three violins and piano.

b. Song from "Zauber Cote"....Mozart. Three violins.

5. "Hungarian Rhapsody"....M. Hauser. P. Sarpalius- -violin.

6. Overture: "Narcissus"....Schlepegrell. The Birutes Orchestra.

This concert is given by the Birutes Orchestra. After the program, there will be dancing and other entertainment. It will be given at the Aurora Hall, 3149 So. Halsted Street. Admission, 25 cents.
