p. 5... Lithuanian men and women. Now is the time to join the Society of the Lithuanian Flag in America, because the initiation fee will be reduced from $1.00 to 50 cents during the next two meetings of the society, Dec.13,1914 and Jan. 10, 1914.

This society is one of the best benefit societies in Chicago. It is based purely on a non-partisan basis. Men and women up to the age of 35 are accepted on an equal basis. All members are guaranteed religious freedom. A sick benefit of $5.00 per week and a various scale of death benefits are paid by the society.

Lithuanian workers, men and women, should join the society in order to have assistance during sickness. This society specializes in assistance to sick members.
