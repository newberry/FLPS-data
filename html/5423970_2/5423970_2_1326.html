Here now, with the long winter months upon us, the Women's Enlightenment Society has again prepared an evening cooking school at which, not only the preparation of healthful and economical meals is taught, but at which much useful advice, which can be very important to living, is given. Classes are held every Thursday evening in the Fellowship House, 831 W. 33rd Place, at 7:30 P.M. There is a fee of twenty-five cents a class for non-members of the Society.....
