On Dec. 29th will be given the last lecture of this year at the Fellowship House, 869 - 33rd Place.

The lecture will be given by Mr. L. Radiukynas from St. Louis, Mo. The subject will be "The Earth and Its History". This lecture should be very interesting to every one, so come without fail. It will start at 3:30 P.M., admission $.10.

The Aurora Society has arranged eight lectures after New Year's day on the following subjects:

(1) "Japan and the Japanese", by Mr. Kasputis.

(2) "Women's Future", by Mrs. M. Seskiene.

(3) "Woman and Her Place in Humanity", by Mr. J. Laukis.

(4), (5), (6) Three Lectures on "Hygiene", by Dr. K. Rutkauskas.

(7) "The History of Religion", by J. Jlgaudas.

(8) "The Mythology of Lithuania", by J. Sernas, editor of Lietuva.

As you see from the program, the lectures are very interesting and educational, so do not fail to attend all the lectures mentioned.

J. Jlgaudas, Secretary of the Lecture Forum.
