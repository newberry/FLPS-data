The Lithuanian women of the Enlightenment Society, have decided to establish a cooking school for Lithuanian women. The initiators are Mrs. Olsevskis, Mrs. Sestokas, and Mrs. Katkevicius.

The school will be held at the Aurora Hall, 3149 S. Halsted Street. As soon as the school opens, there will be a cooking exhibition. The men will have a chance to see what women can do in the preparation of food.
