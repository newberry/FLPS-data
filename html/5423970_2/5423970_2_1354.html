Mr. M. Petrauskas, on the 6th of May, at 8 P.M., will give a concert for the benefit of the Aurora Society. This concert will be given by the students of the Lithuanian Conservatory of Music.

The Program:

(1). "The Cowslip," Waltz....Smallwood. Piano...Miss Rose Monkiute.

(2). "Take Back the Heart"...Claribel. Trombone....Mr. Jeronimas Mockus.

(3). "In the Navy"....Gounod. Song...Mr. Zenonas Stogis.

(4). "Froelicher Landsmann"....Schumann. Piano...Miss Bertha Jodeikiute.

(5). "Perche Piangi"....Gounod. Song...Mary Viskoska.

2

(6). "Waltz," op. 62, No. 2.....Chopin. Piano....Antanas Olsevskis.

(7). "The Day is Ending".....Moniusko. Song....Miss Petronele Krisciokiute.

(8). "Chimes of Normandy"....Planquette. Violin....Miss Zose Olsevskyte, accompanied by A. Olsevskis.

(9). (a) "When I had My Native Home"....Lassen.

(b) "E Donne Mobile," from "Rigoletto"....Verdi. Song....Mr. Kazys Vycas.

(10). (a) "Mother Sends Me"....Mr. M. Petrauskas.

(b) "Gypsy Song," from "Carmen"....Bizet. Song....Miss Malvina Horodockaite.

(11). "Gavotte," op. 16....Godard. Piano....Vytautas Graiciunas.

(12). "Caro Nome," from "Rigoletto"....Verdi. Song....Mrs. Mary Janusauskas.

3

Mr. Petrauskas will accompany all the numbers, except No. 8.

After the concert, Mr. Brandukas will read his essay, "Dzuku Village, Past and Present." Dancing will follow. Admission 25 cents.
