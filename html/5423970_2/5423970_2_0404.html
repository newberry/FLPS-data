The Birute Society presented a one-act playlet, "Gaila Usu" (I Miss My Mustache), written by L. A. Dmusevski and translated by A. Vegele, at the St. George Parish Hall last Sunday. The actors were J. Vitkevicia, J. Stasiunas, A. Glebaitis, Miss P. Rudauskas, and Miss J. Aleknavicius, The Birute Chorus, under the direction of A. Pocius, sang a few songs after the play.
