The first Lithuanian musicians' club was organized on June 4th under the name of "Paderewski Concertina Club." It changed its name to "The Lithuanian Birute Concertina Club."

Meetings are held every Thursday at J. Petroszius Hall, 168 W. 18th St.

The administration is as follows: Stanislovas Kuczis, President; Antanas Ringai la, Secretary; Jonas Bijanskas, Treasurer.
