The following lectures will be given at 4537 South Ashland Avenue:

Sunday, December 22, at 3:30 P. M., "In What Manner Do People Accept Christ?"

Evening lecture at 7:30 P. M., "Why Do The Wicked Hate Light?"

Wednesday, December 23, at 3:30 P. M., "What Benefits Did Christ Bring Us?"

Evening lecture given at 7:30 P. M., "Who is Jesus Christ?"

Friday, December 27, at 7:30 P. M., "The Worthlessness of Man."

Sunday, December 29, at 3:30 P. M., "How Do We Show Our Gratitude to God for Past Favors?"

\2

Evening lecture given at 7:30 P. M., "The Moral Decline of People Today."

The matters to be discussed at these lectures will be interesting as well as beneficial to every person. Therefore, fellow Lithuanians, gather in large numbers! There will be no charge for admission, and there will be no collections of any kind.

Those who live far from the location of the lectures can reach it by either Ashland Avenue or 47 Street street cars.

Everybody is cordially invited to attend.
