The Dramatic Circle of the Lithuanian Socialist Alliance presented, for the first time in Chicago, B. Vargsas' three-act drama "The People," at the Hull House Theater, December 18...

The actors were: "Vagulis," Mr. Briedis; "his daughter," Mrs. M. Dundulis; "Petras," Sankunas; "Tubanski," Dundulis; "Petrof," Petraitis; "Borotko," Doviatas; "Mateusas," Strazdas.
