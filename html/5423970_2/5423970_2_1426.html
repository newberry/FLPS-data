The third of a series of forums sponsored by Chapter 22 of the Lithuanian Socialist Alliance will be held at M. Meldazis Hall tonight. The program of the socialists will be the subject of discussion. The speaker will be K. P. Gugis, and the audience will be given the opportunity to be heard.

West Siders, take advantage of this opportunity! As you can see, the subject to be discussed is a very interesting one. Therefore, everyone should try to be present. The discussion will begin at 8 o'clock. Admission is free.
