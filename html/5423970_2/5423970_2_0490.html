The Lithuanian Youth Circle, which has been very active recently in Town of Lake, presented J. J. Zolp's tragedy: "The Handsome Highwayman," last Sunday (March 24) at the Columbia Hall. Among the men actors, the best performances were given by J. Zolp and M. Barcius. Among the women, Izabelle Aleksandravicaite and A. Laurinaviciute merit mention. The acting of Bainoraite, Visgengaite, Pratapas, Sedemko, Ruibis, and F. Strzyneckis was not bad. . . . .
