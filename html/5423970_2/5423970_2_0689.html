Chapter 28 of the Lovers of the Fatherland Society gave a social on Saturday evening, October 16. The program, which consisted of orations, dancing, games, etc., was very interesting. About two hundred people attended.

The guests were addressed by Dr. A. Zimontas, supreme president of the Lovers of the Fatherland Society; Attorney F. P. Bradchulis; and B. H. Balutis. Among other things, the speakers explained the value of education and urged the guests to become members of the Society. As a result fifteen guests joined the Society.

Mr. V. Lenkauskas recited a monologue entitled "Kiemsargis" (Watchdog).
