The Birute Society presents a very good spectacle: "The Coat and the Over-coat," by V. Gutovsky, at St. George's Hall, at 32nd Place and Auburn Avenue on December 31, at 7:00 P.M. Admission is 25 cents. This play represents the real life of Lithuanians in Lithuania in the past.

It will bring sorrow and tears to you when you see the actual life of Lithuanians in the past, presented on the stage. If you want to see the real life in Lithuania, come to see this great performance.
