The question of support for the new Lietuva daily was raised at the last meeting of the Simanas Daukantas Society. After discussion, it was decided to purchase five shares of the Lietuva for the Society.

At the same meeting Anthony Ruginis bought one share (ten dollars) and Anthony Martinkus bought two shares. Several other members, who did not have money with them, promised to purchase some shares later.

The Simanas Daukantas Society is one of the oldest and most important nationalistic societies in Chicago. It never refuses to support worthy and important nationalistic causes.
