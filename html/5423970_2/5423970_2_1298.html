Through the efforts of Miss K. Persiukas an evening school for girls has been opened in Bridgeport. The English language is taught here. Classes were begun on February 21.

It is very encouraging to see that even the girls are not lagging behind in public and enlightenment activity.

It is true that there is another English language school in this vicinity; but the girls were not satisfied with this and organized a private school. There are fewer students in a private school, and therefore it is easier to teach them. The beginning was very good. Nine students attended the first class. It is expected that the school will be successful.

Girls who want to study English should take advantage of this opportunity. The school is open every Monday night, from 7:30 to 9:00 o'clock.
