The 36th Lodge of the Lithuanian Alliance of America will stage two comedies, "Velnias Ne Boba" (The Devil Is Not a Woman) and "Neatmez-Gamas Mazgas" (An Untieable Knot), on Sunday evening, April 18, at the South Side Turner Hall, 3143-47 So. State Street.

Both plays will be presented for the first time before a Chicago audience by the best and most reliable Lithuanian theatrical artists of Chicago.

After the stage presentations the floor will be cleared for dancing to the tunes of a first-class orchestra.

The profits from this affair will be used to send delegates to the twenty-fourth national convention of the Lithuanian Alliance of America. All Lithuanians are invited.
