The Lithuanian Press Association has been formed at a conference of Lithuanian editors and publishers who met in the Draugelis Hall, Brooklyn, March 17 and 18.

The following editors and publishers have become members of the new Association: A. Olszewski of the Lietuva, J. Tananevice of the Katalikas, J. J. Paukstis of the Vienybe Lietuvninku, V. Sliakys of the Darbininku Viltis, and S. Michelsonas of the Keleivis.

Laisvoji Mintis, Draugas, Kova, and Tevyne were also represented at the conference.

Of the Chicagoans, A. Olszewski was elected president, J. Tananevice, 2treasurer.

The organization, according to the protocol of the conference, "will have to help the newspapers in earnestly progressing toward one end (though not by the same paths): the enlightenment of the people and informing the readers of our activities and common problems."
