Chicago. The newly organized theatrical society of the Grand Duke Keistutis of Lithuania will present its first theatrical performance, "The Destruction of the Kaunas Castle in 1362." (Translator's note: This Kaunas Castle was destroyed by the Crusaders. At that time the Lithuanians were not Christians).

The performance will be on Sunday, April 14, 1901, at Vorwaerts Turner Hall, near 12th and Halsted Streets. It will start at 7:30 P. M. Admission 25, 35 and 50 cents. Children, 15 cents. After the theater there will be a banquet. All the profits will go for the benefit of the Providence of God Parish. We are cordially inviting all the Lithuanians to come.

Committee.
