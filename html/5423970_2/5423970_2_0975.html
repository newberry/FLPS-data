The Katalikas (The Catholic), a Lithuanian daily, is a general newspaper devoted to the interests of the Lithuanian public of the United States. It is a literary, political, and illustrated paper, giving the latest news of the war and other world events. It is always filled with interesting reading matter. The daily Katalikas adheres to a Christian-Nationalist viewpoint.

The subscription rates of the Katalikas are five dollars per year, three dollars for six months, one dollar and seventy-five cents for three months, one dollar and twenty-five cents for two months, and seven dollars per year to Europe. A sample copy will be mailed to anyone upon request.

We also maintain a large book and music store. A catalog will be sent free to anyone upon request.
