On Apr. 26, 1908 at twelve o'clock noon, a meeting of The Lovers of Fatherland Society will be held at Rugis Hall, 3301 S. Morgan St.

This meeting is very important, because we have to find ways and means for our society to publish the literary works of Dr. V. Kudirka. All members must be present at the meeting and bring new members to this literary society.

When our society will have a large number of members, we shall accomplish a gigantic task.

Committee.
