p.5............A commemoration program in honor of those two brave leaders of the working class, Carl Liebknecht and Rosa Luxemburg, will be held on Friday evening, January 8, at the North West Hall, corner of North and Western Avenues. These two working class leaders are martyrs of the world-wide labor movement. They were murdered by capitalists while engaged in the struggle for the uplift of the working class.

The program will consist of organization and theatrical presentations.
