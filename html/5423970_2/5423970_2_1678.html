The West Side Lodge (Number 109) of the Lithuanian Alliance of America held its semi-annual meeting on July 2, at Meldazis Hall, 23rd Place and South Oakley Avenue.

A decision was made at the meeting to assume some financial responsibility for the upkeep of the local Lithuanian public library.

A donation of ten dollars for Lithuanian war relief was voted at the meeting. The money will be sent to the Lithuanian Relief and Autonomy Fund.

It was decided at the meeting to hold a family picnic jointly with Chapter 28 of the Lovers of the Fatherland Society.

The lodge has about seventy members.
