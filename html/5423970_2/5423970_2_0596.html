The spectacle, "The Life of St. George during the Reign of Caesar Diocletian, "will be presented by St. Mathews' Society, Sunday, February 12, 1899, at St. George's church hall, 33rd and Auburn Avenue.

The play will start at 6:30 P. M. After the performance there will be a ball and dances. We cordially invite all the Lithuanians to attend.

St. Mathews Society.
