The Lithuanian Socialists Association of America, Local 137, is presenting a three-act drama, "Kriaucial" (The Tailors), written by B. Vargsas, May 21, at the Hull House Theater.

This play is about the recent garment workers' strike in Chicago. Is there anyone who does not want to see the strike [dramatized], its hope and its struggle, and the [final] victory of the strikers?
