The Lithuanian Organists Alliance will give a concert for the purpose of establishing a library of music. The concert will be held March 9, in St. George's Hall, 32nd Place and Auburn Avenue.

The cantata, "The Seven Last Words of Christ," by the famous Professor Theodore DuBois,of the Paris Conservatoire, will be presented. It has been translated into Lithuanian by Rt. Rev. F. B. Sarafinas, Chicago. The choir is directed by A. Pocius. Curtain at 8 P. M. Admission, thirty-five cents, fifty cents, and up.
