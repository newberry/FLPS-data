The Lithuanian Building and Loan Society was organized on Feb. 9, 1904, for the purpose of helping Lithuanians to buy and to build homes for themselves. Meetings are held every Thursday evening at the Providence of God Parish hall.

The Administration:

L. H. Getz, 168 W. 18th St., President.

J. Petroszius, 168 W. 18th St., Treasurer.

A. Lemont, 624 S. Union Avenue, Secretary.
