On February 11 at Mark White Square Park Hall, Birute will have its first practice in preparation for the concert which will be given in Lent. On the following Tuesday the Birute choir singers will begin attending the conservatory where Mr. A. Pocius will teach singing.

Those who want to participate in the concert which will be given in Lent, are to receive singing lessons at the conservatory free, are asked to come this Friday and join Birute and begin studying singing with the others. Birute singers must not miss a lesson; this applies also to those who want to join this choir.
