Our newspaper Lietuva (Lithuania) now has 12,000 readers. However, we would like to have 20,000 readers. Therefore, in order to attract 8,000 new readers for the Lietuva we have decided to give a prize to every new subscriber.

The prizes will consist of books, pictures, stereoscopes, or any other prize selected from our catalog.

Those who will pay $1.00 for a six months' subscription to the Lietuva will receive a prize valued at twenty-five cents; those who will pay $2.00 for a years' subscription will receive a prize valued at fifty cents.
