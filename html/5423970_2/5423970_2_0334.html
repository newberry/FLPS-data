The Lithuanian Singer's Dramatic Society has arranged a grand theatrical spectacle for Saturday evening, on the 10th of February, 1906, Pulaski Hall, 800 S. Ashland Avenue. They will present the tragedy "Keistutis", one of the most beautiful dramas of ancient Lithuanian life. Whoever wants to see a good production based on our grand and famous history of the olden times of Lithuania's greatness, can come and see this grand theatrical performance. After the drama there will be a banquet and good music. You are heartily invited.

Committee.
