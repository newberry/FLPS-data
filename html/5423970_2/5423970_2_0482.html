The Dramatic Circle of the Lithuanian Socialist Alliance presented "The Robbers," a four-act comedy, at the Hull House Theater, September 29.

In the cast were included: J. Sankunas, J. Uktveris, J. Buragas, F. Sirvinskas, E. Armulaite, V. Vilkeviciene, F. Kislauskaite and J. Prusinskas.

Monologues were presented by J. Prusinskas after the presentation of the play.
