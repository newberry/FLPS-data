The St. Francis Serafinian Society will stage "Apmaskolintas Jurgis" (Russianized George), a comedy, Saturday evening, December 26, at Freiheit Turner Hall, 3417 So. Halsted Street. Every year this society stages a comical theatrical play in connection with a musical program. This year's presentation promises to be better than ever.

Besides the play, the choir of the Providence of God Parish, under the direction of A. Limontas, will furnish a musical program of the latest songs. Everybody is invited to attend.
