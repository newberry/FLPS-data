The above association was organized on August 28, 1933, at A. Belskis' Drug Store, 2422 W. Marquette Rd. The purpose of this association is sports.

The first members were as follows: Dr. G. Blozis, Dr. S. Biezis, A. Belskis, Dr. U. S. Naris, L. Pivarunas, N. Pockul, L. Simutis, the editor of Draugas J. Vitkus, and Dr. C. K. Kliauga.

During the first year the membership dues were $1. Now the yearly dues are $2.

This year the membership is 165 members.

L. Pivarunas, president, 4602 S. Milwaukee Ave.

G. Blozis, secretary, 2201 W. Cermak Rd.

J. C. Miller, instructor.
