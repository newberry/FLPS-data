p. 8.... The 4th chapter of the Lithuanian Socialist League gave a lecture for men only on Dec. 30, 1914. The lecture was delivered by Dr. A.L.Yuska. The hall was crowded. Over two hundred questions were asked by the audience, requiring over three hours to answer them.

Another lecture will be given on Jan. 13, at Mark White Square. The topic will be: "Does the Catholic Church Aid Workers?"
