We have received the following announcement: "The fall and winter seasons are nearing. Gatherings and all kinds of affairs will again be held. It is best to prepare for them in advance.

"The University of the People will assume the responsibility of providing the societies and organizations with speakers and lecturers on all subjects: art, education, trades, insurance, health, religion, and others.

"Societies planning lectures or mass meetings can have good speakers, lecturers or declaimers by communicating with The University of the People, at the following address: University of the People, Dr. A. L. Graicunas, 3310 South Halsted Street."
