The Lithuanian Youth Circle arranged a free Lithuanian musical evening at the Davis Square Hall last Sunday, April 14.

The program was opened with a fitting address by Mr. J. Zolpis, and the following program was presented.

1. A speech on Lithuanian youth activities by Mr. D. B. Pratapas.

2. A Mandolin orchestra, which offered an encore.

3. A monologue: "Yearning."

4. A quartet composed of Miss Mossiej, Miss Pieskalsa, Mr. Strzyneckis, and Mr. A. Garcius.

5. A trio; sung by Gestautas, Strzyneckis, and J. Zolpis.

6. A quartet; sung by Zolpis, Gestautas, Strzyneckis and Misius.

It can be said that the whole program was fairly good.
