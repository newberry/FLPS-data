There was organized on June 27 a new Lithuanian Independent Club. Forty-eight members joined this club. The club's purpose is to help the sick, bury the dead, and help foreigners get their citizenship papers. The sick benefit is $5 per week and the death benefit is $50.

The club meetings will be held at the Peter Slakis Hall, 3321 Auburn Avenue. All Lithuanians are invited to join this club.

Petras Slakis, President

Frank Kudirka, Secretary

Frank Szcziuka, Treasurer.
