The Dramatic Circle of the Lithuanian Socialist Alliance presented "The Knight of Pilenai" at the Hull House Theater on February 27. This play, a five-act tragedy, is a dramatization by M. Siauleniskis of Krasevskis' important novel, The Priest.

The amateur actors were the following: M. Dunduliene, J. Briedis, A. Velykiute-Zagariene, J. Sankunas, B. Vaitiekunas, K. Ciuras, J. Girijotas, S. Danta, O.Cepaiciute, A. Zemaiciute, M. Kazenas and J. Levanas.
