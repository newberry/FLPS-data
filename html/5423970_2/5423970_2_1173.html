The Young Lithuanians Club, which was recently created by the union of the former Regal Club and the Young Lithuanians of America Social Club, voted at its last meeting to donate twenty-five dollars from its treasury to the fund which is being raised by the Lovers of the Fatherland Society for the publication of L. Sernas' written works.

It is said that the other Lithuanian societies of Chicago, also, are planning to donate to this worthy cause.
