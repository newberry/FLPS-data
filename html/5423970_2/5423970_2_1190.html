The following new books in the Lithuanian language were recently published in Chicago, Illinois:

R. Karuza, Tauta, Jos Esybe Ir Uzdaviniai (The Essence and Duties of a Nationality) (Chicago: Jaunoji Lietuva [Young Lithuania]). This book contains thirty-one pages and is priced at twenty cents.

A. Strazdo Rastai (Literary Works of A. Strazdas) is the twenty-ninth publication of the Lovers of the Fatherland Society. It contains seventy-seven pages including a biography and picture of the author. This publication was prepared for the press by J. Gabrys.
