The Ruta Theatrical Society presented Guzutis' play, "The Lord and the Peasant," at the Czech Hall, 1126 W. 18th Street, October 9.

The acting, compared with most of the Lithuanian presentations, was good enough. Some of the actors, for instance, A. Sidlauskas,.... J. Chmielauskas...., K. Balta, and J. Beliackas.... played their parts very well.....
