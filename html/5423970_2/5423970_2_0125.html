p.3........More and more favorable comments and compliments are being showered on Joseph Bobrovicius, Lithuanian tenor.

Herman Devries and Edward Moore, music critic authorities, have expressed approving words on the ability and talent of our Lithuanian songster. Now Mr. Bobrovicius is in Florida, and the St. Peterburgh Times greets him with a write-up wherein it calls him the "famous tenor."

All we shall say is that he is one of "our" stars.
