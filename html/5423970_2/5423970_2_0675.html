The Chicago branch of the Lithuanian-American Workers Literary Society will give a very interesting concert, Feb. 21, at the Chicago Lithuanian Auditorium, 3133 So. Halsted Street. A large number of musicians will participate in the program. Among those who will take part are: Ona Pocius, famous Lithuanian violinist and singer; Helen Peciukas; Paul Dauderis; a Jewish chorus; and a famous Italian singer.

This concert promises to be very interesting, and is expected to draw a capacity crowd.
