The following books in the Lithuanian language were recently published in Chicago, Illinois.

Dr. T. Zell, Gyvuliiu Protas (Brain of Animals) (Chicago: Katalikas [The Catholic]). This book was translated into the Lithuanian language by Joseph Laukis. It contains two hundred and twelve pages.

Reverend M. Gustaitis, Meile (Love) (Chicago: Katalikas). This is a twenty-four-page poem, priced at fifteen cents.
