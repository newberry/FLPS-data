p.3.... The 4th chapter of the Lithuanian Socialist League gave a lecture on the subject, "The National Awakening of "Ukrainia," Jan.13, at Mark White Square.

The lecture was read by Mr. Al. Dovidonis. It was interesting and highly appreciated by a large audience . Many questions were asked at the conclusion of the lecture and answered by Mr. Dovidonis.

The next lecture of the league will be held on Feb.10, at the same hall.
