Everyone who subscribes to the Lietuva (Lithuania) for one year will receive free the Lietuva Year Book for 1910 and a beautiful Lithuanian wall calendar. The Year Book contains much useful and valuable information which every person should know. One of the most important features of the book is the section entitled "Statistical Survey of Lithuanian-Americans." This section contains sixteen pages, and has a permanent historical value. The 1910 Year Book is a big improvement over all previous issues. The price of the Year Book is twenty-five cents. The price of the wall calendar is ten cents. When writing, address as follows:
