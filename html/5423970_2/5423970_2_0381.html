Branch 143 of the Lovers of the Motherland Society is situated in Roseland, and on January 9 it gave a performance at Strumilas Brothers Hall. This performance was in honor of the twentieth anniversary of the Lovers of the Motherland Literary Society, and also in celebration of the tenth anniversary of the first Lithuanian convention held in Vilnius, Lithuania.

The branch presented the one-act comedy "The Deaf Son-in-Law". After the performance, there were declamations a speech was delivered by Dr. A. Zimontas. Twenty-three dollars was collected for Lithuanian war-sufferers.
