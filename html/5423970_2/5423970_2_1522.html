The following Aurora lectures will be given November 27 at 3 P.M.:

1) In Bridgeport, at the Lithuanian Club, 3149 So. Halsted Street. Gudas will lecture on the history of culture.

2) In Town of Lake, at the Davis Square Hall, 45th Street and Marshfield Avenue. M. Petrauskas will read "The Music of various Ancient Nations and That of the Lithuanians."

3) In the West Side, on the second floor of the McCormick Hall. K.Jurgelionis will lecture on the theatrical arts: declamation, mimicry, and acting.
