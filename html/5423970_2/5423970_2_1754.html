There was organized in Chicago on February 14 a new Lithuanian mutual benefit society under the name of "St. Isidor."

At the first meeting twenty-four members joined it. They decided to hold a second meeting on Sunday, March 4, at 2 P. M., at K. Palszis Hall, 8737 Commercial Avenue.

We are inviting all the Lithuanians to come and join this society.

In the name of the society:

S. Puiszis, President.

S. Zilinskas, Secretary.
