p. 4.... The Kudirka Choral Society entertained Chicago Lithuanians on New Year's Eve with a concert at Meldazis Hall. The program consisted of songs, music and monologues. It was a most successful evening and everybody had a good time.

A large crowd attended. The audience was well balanced, because no intoxicating drinks were sold during the program.

At its pre-annual meeting on Dec. 27th, this society donated $25.00 from its treasurery for the relief of war refugees in Lithuania.
