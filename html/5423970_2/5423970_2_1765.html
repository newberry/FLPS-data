The Famous Name of Lithuanians Society was organized on March 25, 1906.

Synopsis of the Constitution:

1. The society is a benefit society.

2. (Its purpose is) to help students who are seeking higher education.

To increase the Lithuanian literature, the Lithuanian commerce, industry in all its branches.

Members are accepted from 18 to 45 years of age.

Every member pays 50 cents per month to the treasury of the society.

To the sick member it pays $5 per week. At death, member's beneficiary receives $200. It has about 165 members. The assets are $5,000.

President, P. Ukockis, 4444 S. Whipple St.

Secretary, S. Ivanauskas, 7008 S. Rockwell St.
