The Aurora school teaches: English, Lithuanian, bookkeeping, auditing, stenography, typewriting, business law, general history, United States history, geography, algebra, geometry, trigonometry, and drafting. School hours: from 9 A.M. to 5 P.M. Evenings, from 7:30 P.M. to 9:30 P.M.

The school is located at 3149 South Halsted Street.
