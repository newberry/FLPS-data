p.6... The distinction of being the only Lithuanian Riding Club in Chicago is not the only honor this club has attained.

The first Annual Dance was not only a financial success but an achievement of such magnitude that the club gained great social prestige within Lithuanian circles through that event alone.

At the last regular meeting the members enjoyed themselves thoroughly both before and after the business session.

Miss Julia Meyers was the Hostess. She served very delicious refreshments, and afterwards entertained by playing special games including Bingo and Bunco.

The date for the next ride was fixed and a program for winter activities decided upon. A to began party, sleigh ride, and a splash party are some of the other activities for which dates will be decided upon at the next meeting.

Having a social gathering after every meeting seems to appeal to the members and will be continued.
