A lecture will be given under the auspices of the Youth Alliance. S. Naujalis will read the lecture, which is entitled "Psychology". The lecture promises to be very interesting, and therefore will be beneficial to everybody who hears it. The place is Mark White Square Hall, and the time, 7:30 P. M. Admission will be free.

The Enlightenment Committee invites you to attend.
