The Simanas Daukantas Society held its annual commemoration of the birthday of Simanas Daukantas [1793-1864], great Lithuanian historian and patriot.

The main speaker at the affair was Dr. A. J. Zimontas. He spoke about the life of Daukantas. At the end of his talk, he urged the audience to contribute towards the relief of the war-stricken people of Lithuania. A collection netted a total of $23.14.

The program included a recitation by Miss Jadvyga Urbis, a monologue, and songs by the Biruta Chorus. The program ended with the singing of "Lietuva Tevyne Musu" (Lithuania Our Fatherland), the Lithuanian national anthem.

This Society always succeeds in furnishing very good programs at all its affairs. It is one of the largest Lithuanian mutual-aid societies in Chicago.

According to rumors, the Society is planning to make a large donation on Lithuanian Day (Tag Day), November I, 1916, for the relief of Lithuanian war victims in Europe. A member stated that the Society will donate at least one hundred dollars.
