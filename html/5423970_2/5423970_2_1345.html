K. JURGELIONIS, the Drama Society's director, began giving training courses for our amateurs. Elocution and declamation, mimicry and gesticulation, acting and the history of drama will be taught... One can register for the courses on Tuesdays after 7:00 P. M. and Sundays after 10:00 A. M.

A fee of three dollars a month will be charged for the courses. The place: the home of the director, 3327 Lowe Avenue, in Bridgeport.
