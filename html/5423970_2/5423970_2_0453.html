The Farmers of Lithuania Society gave a performance September 27, at Meldazis Hall. They presented a humorous one-act comedy "The Dearest Kiss." A little girl, K. Burduliute, gave a declamation. What the first actors were not able to succeed in doing, the Stepukas vaudeville (Mr. V. Brusokas) accomplished. At this time Stepukas tried to show himself as a magician, and he actually succeeded. The audience was satisfied with the program. After the program there was dancing and other amusements, such as the flying post office, Lithuanian amusement plays, etc.

The attendance was large; the performance was successful.
