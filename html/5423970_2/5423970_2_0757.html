The Lithuanian West Side Public Library gave a benefit performance on January 23 at Meldazis Hall. Mr. S. Biezis, a student of medicine, made a speech on libraries and the benefits of enlightenment. After the speech, The Lovers of Motherland Society, Branch 28, presented two plays: "One of Us Must Get Married," and "The Knave".

Local Lithuanians are paying very little attention to the support of this library, judging from the small attendance. The Lithuanian Library will have a deficit from this performance.
