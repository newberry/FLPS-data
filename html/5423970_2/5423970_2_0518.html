The Dramatic Circle of the Lithuanian Socialist Alliance presented Slowackis' five-act tragedy "Mindaugis," at the W. S. Auditorium, November 20. The following participated in the acting: Vaitiekunas, "Mindaugis"; Damijonaitiene, his mother; Briedis, the Pope's messenger; Mikolaitis, "Grand Duke Daumantas"; Dunduliene, his wife; Sankunas, "Trainitis"; Drangelis, "Knight Herman"; Doviat, "Liutovaras"; Sidiskis, "Vaisvilkis"; Kazenas, "Minykas." Therefore, the best talent among the Chicagoans was represented in this play.....
