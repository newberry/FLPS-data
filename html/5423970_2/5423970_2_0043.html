The Darius-Girenas Savings and Loan association at the beginning was organized under the name of the Knights Savings and Loan Association, on March 1, 1915. The association kept that name until 1933.

The first organizers were Julius Kaupas, John Kazanauskas, and W. Rutkauskas.

This association was incorporated for $1,000,000. The assets are $90,000.

President John Baltutis; Secretary Adolph Garuckas; Treasurer F. Gudas. Directors: F. Bastys, J. Puisis, C. Stulas, V. Stulpinas, J. Zakas, A. Overling.
