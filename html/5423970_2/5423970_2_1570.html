On October 20th at Valigovos Hall, corner North and Holt Avenues, there was held a public meeting. The speaker was K. Rutkus. He spoke for two hours on the Russian revolution and on the capitalist system in general; how the workers are oppressed and exploited for the profit of the few. The speaker said it is not enough to belong to the national societies - the workers must belong to the Socialist organizations, too.

The Socialist lectures were arranged here in order to organize the Socialist branch - the branch was organized.

Six dollars was collected for the revolutionary fund. Even though previously in this Lithuanian colony the Rev. Milukas denounced the Socialists, the people are laughing at the priest's speech.
