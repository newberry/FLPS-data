The Sacred Heart of Jesus Society of Cicero, Illinois staged the melodrama "Genovaite" (Genevieve) last week at Meldazis Hall, 23rd Place and South Oakley Avenue. Generally speaking, the performance was satisfactory. The cast of players included the following Lithuanian theatrical artists: "Genovaite", by Miss M. Daujotas; "Golius", by Mr. B. Liebonas; "Sigytas", by Mr. K. Matulis; and others. Miss L. Jucevicius recited a poem entitled "Kada Lietuva Kariavo" (When Lithuania Was at War) with much success.
