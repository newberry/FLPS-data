Last Sunday a meeting was held at Azukas Hall, 3301 Auburn avenue. There was organized a new society under the name of Simonas Daukantas. Thirteen members joined this new society.

The officers of the society of Simonas Daukantas were elected as follows:

Dr. Kadis, president

F. Mikolainis, vice-president

K. Pocius, secretary

S. Dargewiczia, financial secretary

K. Azukas, treasurer.
