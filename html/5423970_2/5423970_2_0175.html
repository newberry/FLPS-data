We are announcing that on the 24th day of June, 1900, was organized the Paderewski Concertina Club, which will play at balls, banquets, picnics and other amusement entertainments. It plays on various musical instruments and will teach any one to play on every instrument. The meetings are held every Thursday at Czeslowas Zilewiczia, 614 W. 17th St., corner Laflin Street. The club's administration:

Czeslowas Zilewiczia, President

J. Walantawiczia, Vice-President

A. Ringaila, Secretary

J. Bijanskas, Treasurer.
