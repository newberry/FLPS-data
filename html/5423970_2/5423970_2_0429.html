The Drama Society presented a four-act comedy, "With Pleasure," at the St. George Parish Hall, January 11. The play was orginally written in German by G. Moser and was translated into Lithuanian by Mariute.

The following actors were in the principal roles: B. Vaitiekunas, J. Sankunas, F. Sirvinskas, A. Dovidonis, J. Briedis, M. Montviliute, A. Samoliute, and Aleksandreviciute.
