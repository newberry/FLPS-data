The Lithuanian Conservatory [of Music], 3246 S. Halsted Street, began its new semester the first of the year. The Conservatory started new courses for the students. Every Wednesday Mr. K. Sliupas will lecture on aesthetics--Dr. Graiciunas on anatomy and Mr. K. Jurgelionis on stage technique and the art of declamation. The Conservatory's aim is to develop perfect actors and singers from its student body.
