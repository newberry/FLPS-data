Branch 27 will hold its annual meeting in Bierzinskis Hall, 4602 South Paulina Street, January 27, at 8 P.M.

At this meeting, the books, The Heart and The Mother will be distributed to the members of this society. The new members, who join this society at this meeting, will also receive these books. At this meeting we will discuss the announcements from the central board of the Lovers of the Motherland Society. Bring new members to this meeting.
