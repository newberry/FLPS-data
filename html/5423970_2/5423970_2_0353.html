p.8....The Kankliu Chorus (Lithuanian Guitar Chorus) is making preparations to present"Voratinkliai" (Cobwebs), a three act melodrama on Jan. 30, at the Chicago Lithuanian Auditorium, 3133 So. Halsted St.

The cast of the play will be made up of the most talented Lithuanian artists in Chicago. A leading role will be played by comrade F. Mockapetris, a newcomer in Chicago, who has distinguished himself as an able artist in Pennsylvania.
