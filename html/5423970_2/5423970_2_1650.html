The Lithuanian Western Star Club was organized in May, 1923.

The purpose of the club is to give help to its members. Entertainments and picnics are given in order to increase the budget of the club.

The membership dues are 25 cents per month. The sick benefit is $5 per week. The death benefit is $50, and every member pays one dollar.

Formerly the club had over 400 members; now there are 95. The assets are $600.

Meetings are held at the Petrauskas Hall, 1750 S. Union Avenue. President G. Chernauskas, 1900 S. Union Avenue; Secretary B. Zolynas, 2951 Emerald Avenue.
