The statistical survey of the Burnside Lithuanian colony, printed in the September 24 issue of the Lietuva (Lithuania), was not complete. At present, there is no Lithuanian saloon in that colony as it was previously reported. There was a Lithuanian saloon there before, but it discontinued business several years ago.

Altogether, there are about sixty Lithuanian families and a number of single people living in that colony.

There are three Lithuanian business establishments in Burnside: One food store, operated by A. Pocevicz; one candy store, managed by J. Skoots; and one barber shop, run by A. Briedis.

There are approximately seventeen Lithuanian home owners in this colony.
