The name of J. Zalpis (Zolp) has been known to, or heard of by Chicagoans as that of a playwright and the director of the Youth Circle. Under his direction the members of the Youth Circle presented his own original play "Who Is Guilty?" in Town of Lake January 18.....

Mr. Zalpis has written, it seems, about eight plays: "The Vagabond," "Without A Name," "Who Is Guilty?", and others....
