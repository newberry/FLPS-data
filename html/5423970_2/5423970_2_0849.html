While Lietuva was being organized as a daily, many of our friends--and some who were not our friends--asked us to state the type of program and platform which the new daily would support.

Numerous suggestions were received. We have been asked to publish some beautiful songs in the first edition so that everyone will be able to memorize them.

In response to these requests, we have published below our platform and a song. Every Lithuanian should make it his duty to memorize this song.

"Lithuania, our fatherland,

Band of heroes,

May thy sons draw

Strength from thy past.

"May thy children travel

Only the paths of virtue;

May they work for thy benefit

And the welfare of the people.

"May the sun of Lithuania

Remove the darkness,

And may light and truth

Guide our steps.

"May the love of Lithuania

Burn within our hearts.

For the sake of Lithuania

May unity bloom."

[Translator's note: This song is the Lithuanian Anthem. Vincas Kudirka wrote both the music and the lyrics.]

Lithuanians will benefit themselves by supporting the platform of our paper. All humanity will be enriched when nations regulate their activities according to these immortal words of Kudirka.
