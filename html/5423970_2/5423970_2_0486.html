On April 28 the Birute Society presented "The Chimney Sweep and the Miller" for the second time this year.

This time there were several new players in this operetta, among them J. Abramaitis, A. Misevicia, and J. Miseviciute.

Of the older artists, J. Ilgaudas and Stogis distinguished themselves. Brusokas rendered his monologues after the operetta.
