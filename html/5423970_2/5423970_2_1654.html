The Simanas Daukantas Society, a famous Chicago Lithuanian mutual aid society, held its annual meeting on January 7.

It was decided at the meeting to donate the sum of one hundred dollars for the relief of the war-stricken people of Lithuania. The money will be turned over to the Federation of Chicago Lithuanian Societies, of which the Simanas Daukantas Society is a member. That is a very worthy deed and a wonderful example for other Chicago Lithuanian societies to follow.

It was also decided at the meeting to purchase two burial lots in the Lithuanian National Cemetery.
