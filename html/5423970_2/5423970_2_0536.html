Chapter 58 of the Lithuanian Socialist Alliance of America sponsored a theatrical performance and a ball at the Columbia Hall, in this city, on April 16. The first play, "Neznaja Brodu, Ne Suisia V Vodu", was in the Russian language, the second, "Po Revizii", in the Ukrainian language. The actors were from the Progress Dramatic Club of Chicago.....There were about 300 people present, most of whom were Lithuanians....
