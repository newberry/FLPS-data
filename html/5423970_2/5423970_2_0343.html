We had announced in the last number of Lietuva that the exhibit of Mr. J. Sileika's paintings would be opened last Saturday. Now two reliefs by A. Aleksandravicius have been added to Mr. Sileika's paintings. There will also be two more Lithuanian artists participating in the exhibit, which will now open Saturday, July 9. It will continue to the end of this month. This exhibit is being sponsored by the Aurora Society and all the profit from the admission fees will go to the treasury of that Society. The admission fee will be ten cents for the men; women and girls will be admitted free. The exhibit will be open from 3 P. M. to 6 P. M. Saturdays and Sundays; all other days, from 3 P. M. to 5 P. M. It will be held at 3149 So. Halsted Street.
