A new Lithuanian periodical entitled Pazanga (Progress) appeared in Chicago this week. It is to be published once every month.

An editorial appearing in the new publication states that it will be a "journal of literature, learning, and politics, for the Lithuanian masses of America".

Reverend F. Kemesis is the editor of the Pazanga. It is published by the Mariavite Fathers, a Lithuanian Roman Catholic religious order, which also publishes the Chicago weekly Draugas.

The first number of the Pazanga consists of forty-eight large pages. It contains a variety of features. The new periodical will undoubtedly foster the ideals of the Lithuanian right wing faction (the Catholics), which supports the weekly Draugas.
