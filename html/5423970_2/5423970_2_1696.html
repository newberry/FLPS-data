The above mentioned society was organized March 15, 1914.

Extract from the constitution: "A person who has a husband not of the Roman-Catholic faith cannot be accepted in this society, or if being a member of this society would marry a man not of the Roman-Catholic faith she will be expelled from this society."

Every member who reads and keeps infidel literature against the Roman-Catholic religion, condemning the saints of God, all the holiness and the priests, will be expelled from the society.

The meetings are opened and closed with prayer. The members are accepted from 18 to 40 years of age. The membership dues are 25 cents per month, the sick benefit $5 per week. In case of death the members' benefit is $50, and the members pay one dollar each. The membership is about 80. The assets about $3,300.

The meetings are held at the church hall, 12225 Emerald Avenue.

President, Mrs. V. Tela, 12220 S. Union Avenue

Secretary, Dela Savich, 12352 Emerald Avenue.
