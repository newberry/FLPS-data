The Farmers of Lithuania Society sponsored an affair at the Meldazis Hall last Sunday night. A two-act farce, "The Expropriator," speeches, and recitations were presented. Outstanding by their good acting in the play were V. Brusokas, Mrs. S. Valonis, Miss A. Ulkis, B. Lenkauskas, and A. Matkus The recitations were given by Miss K. Zauris and Miss A. Jaukstas.
