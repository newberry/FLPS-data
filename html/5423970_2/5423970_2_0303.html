For the third time in Chicago, the Birute Society presented M. Petrauskas' operetta, "Birute," at the St. George Parish Hall, December 25. The actors who participated were the same as last time: Horodeckiute, B. Vaitiekunas, Kasputis, Duda, Vitkevicia, Zacharevicia, Janusauskis, Juska, J. Ilgaudas, and others.
