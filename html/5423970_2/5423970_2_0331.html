In Chicago, November 18th, a concert was given by the singers of Lithuanian Alliance of America branch 109. The public had an opportunity to hear good songs and beautiful melodies. The program consisted of a male choir the young girls' quartet the male quartet, and the mixed choir.

A considerable profit was made. $5.00 was donated to the Lithuanian National Home and the colony for the poor, and $5.00 was donated for the new church on the West side, the Sacred Lady Aurora Gates Church.

A.L. Miezlaiskis.
