The Guardsmen of Grand Duke Vytautas of Lithuania, a mutual benefit society, will celebrate the fifteenth anniversary of the founding of their society on September 12, 1909. Lithuanian societies and clubs are requested not to plan any affairs on that date. All the Lithuanian organizations of Chicago will be invited to participate in the celebration.

[Translator's note: Vytautas the Great (1350-1430) was the ruler of the Grand Dutchy of Lithuania until his death in 1430.]
