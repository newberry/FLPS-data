This coming Saturday, August 30, at J. Chutra hall, 9929 South Michigan Avenue, the Aurora society will present its performance for the benefit of the "Aurora" library, which will be established in the near future. The program will consist of declamations, dances, monologues, and prizes will be distributed to the best participants. At five o'clock in the afternoon, a sport play will be held outside, after which the main program inside the hall, will begin at 7:30 P. M. We are asking the public to attend this performance. All the profit will be given to the "Aurora" library.
