The first Lithuanian society which has been organized in Bridgeport district under the name Great Duke Keistutis Building and Loan Association No. 1, will start selling its shares, series VI, on February 22.

This society was established August 26, 1897, and has money to loan to everybody.

Meetings are held every Thursday 7:30 P. M., at L. Azuko Hall, 33 Auburn Avenue.

All Lithuanians are cordially invited to attend and to join this profitable organization.

Everybody, men, women and children, can join this organization.
