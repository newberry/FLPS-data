In the annual statement (October 1, 1916) of the ownership, management, circulation, etc., of the weekly Lietuva (Lithuania), required by the Act of Congress of August 24, 1912, the following information appears. It is stated that the publisher is Lietuva Publishing Company, 3252 South Halsted Street, Chicago, Illinois; the editor is L. Sernas, 636 East 38th Street, Chicago, Illinois; the managing editor is B. K. Balutis; 814 West 33rd Street, Chicago, Illinois; the business manager is J. Ambrosius, 7010 Eberhart Avenue, Chicago, Illinois; the stockholders are A. Olszewski, 3252 South Halsted Street, Chicago, Illinois; J. Ambrosius, 7010 Eberhart Avenue, Chicago, Illinois; and B. K. Balutis, 814 West 33rd Street, Chicago, Illinois.
