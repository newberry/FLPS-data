An affair sponsored by seven societies for the benefit of the Lithuanian Public Library was held at the Meldazis Hall, last Sunday night. The varied program of the evening was made up of speeches, recitations, a musicale, a theatrical presentation and, of course, Stepukas' Vaudeville.

Dr. A. Greiciunas spoke on the benefits of a library. In the two-act comedy, "The Manhunt", Mrs. Kaupas-Valonis, well-known to West Siders as one of our best artists, was outstanding.....
