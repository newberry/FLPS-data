On October 15, at 7:00 P.M., St. George's Parish Hall, 33rd Street and Auburn Avenue, the Aurora Society will give its theatrical performance and concert.

It is the aim of this society to give the public something different from and better than what it has been given before. The program will be given by the best artists. The comedy, "Revengeful Love," will be presented. The program will also include two quartets, two soloists, and a monologist. After the program, there will be a banquet and dance.

The admission will be seventy-five, fifty and twenty-five cents: half price for the students.
