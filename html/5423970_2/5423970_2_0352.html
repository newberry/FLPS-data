The Keistutis Lithuanian Benefit Club is beginning preparations for staging two comedies, "The Mother-in-Law and the Newlyweds" and "One of us Must Get Married." The presentation will take place on Saturday evening, Feb. 27, at the Mc Kinley Park Hall. Some of the most popular and talented Lithuanian actors of Chicago will take part in the plays. A very pleasant evening of entertainment is promised to all who attend.
