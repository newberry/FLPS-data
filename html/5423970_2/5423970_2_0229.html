This coming winter season, the Dramatic Circle and the local Lithuanian Socialist branch will work more intensively in order to uplift the theatrical art among Lithuanians. The Dramatic Circle has great prestige among the Lithuanian public for presenting good theatrical plays in the past several years. It is well-known that the Dramatic Circle society has the best players and artists.

The first theatrical scene, "The Heart of John," will be presented Oct. 14, at the Hull House hall. Also, this year there will be presented the well-known play "In the Deep" by Maxim Gorky, the "Resurrection," by K. Jasukaitis, and other selected theatrical plays.
