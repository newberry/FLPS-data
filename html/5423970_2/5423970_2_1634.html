The above named club was organized February 11, 1910. The club is liberal and supports all progressive movements.

Members are accepted from 16 to 40 years of age. A member who is not 21 years of age, or who is not a citizen of the United States, cannot take a position in the club's administration. Membership dues are 25 cents per month. Sick members get $5 per week. To the death beneficiary goes $50 from the treasury and one dollar from every member (of the club). There are about 60 members. Assets are $4,700.

F. Rasinskas, President

K. Statkus, 11928 Eggleston, Sec'y.
