This international concert will be given by the Lithuanian Socialist Party of America, branch 4, on New Year's evening, January 1, 1907, Freiheit Turner Hall, 3417 S. Halsted St.

This is the first international concert given by the Lithuanian Socialists. It will include choirs of the Americans, Germans, Poles, Russians, Latvians, etc. Speeches will be given in the Lithuanian, English, Russian and Polish languages. The profit of this concert will be set aside for the propaganda of Socialism.

Sons and daughters of the nation! Do not miss this opportunity! Such a concert you have never heard. Singers of the mentioned nationalities will sing beautiful songs. All are cordially invited.

Committee.
