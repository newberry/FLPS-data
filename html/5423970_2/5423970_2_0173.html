On October 24th there was organized a new club of musicians under the name of "The Chicago Lithuanian Orchestra Concertina Club." The president is C. Zyleviczia, 617 W. 18th St.; vice president, J. Valantianaviczia, 703 W. 18th St.; treasurer, D. Spuris, 656 W. 19th St.

Meetings are held at S. Lelaszius, 58 W. 25th St.

All Lithuanians wanting to have good music at their halls, should call our Musicians' Club.

M. Kanapeckas, Secretary.
