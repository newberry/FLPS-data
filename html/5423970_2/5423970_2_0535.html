The Women's Enlightenment Society held a ball at Pulaski ball on April 7, and in addition presented Zemaite's comedy "Our Good One."

The presentation of the play, it seems, did not take even a half-hour. As was advertised, only women participated in the play. They were: Katkeviciene, Moskiene, M. Damijonaitiene, Kalvaiciute, Montviliute, Miscikaitiene and Kenutiene.

The profit of the evening was intended for a student, Miss Balcauskaite...
