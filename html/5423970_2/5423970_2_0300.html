The Birutes Musical and Drama Society will present its last program of the season, May 21, at St. George's Parish Hall, 32nd Place and Auburn Avenue. Two operas will be given, "Sienapiute" (The Haying Season), by Mikas Petrauskas, and "Faust," by Gounod.

The opera "Faust," is presented for the first time to the Lithuanian public. Therefore it should be interesting to see the performance.

Admission: $1.00, 75, 50, and 25 cents.
