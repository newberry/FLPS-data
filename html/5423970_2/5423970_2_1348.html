Two evening schools are now established for women. Cooking and needlework is taught. Cooking is taught every Monday night at the Fellowship House, 831 West 33rd Place. On the first Sunday of every month, the meetings of the Enlightenment Society are held in this same place, at 2:00 P.M.....The sewing classes are located on the West Side, at Meldazis Hall, 2244 West 23rd Place, and classes are taught every Wednesday night. Classes in both schools begin at 7:30 P. M.....
