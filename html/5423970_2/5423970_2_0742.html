On January 30 the Gediminas Society held its annual meeting in the F. Shedvilas Hall, 341 Kensington Avenue. A letter received from an authorized committee of the Eighth District of the Lithuanian Socialist Alliance was read to the members. It concerned our ailing playwright, Bruno Vargsas.

The Society responded warmly to the plea for donations by voting to give ten dollars from the treasury. Besides this, donations were collected among the members. The following members contributed: [Editor's note: Here follows the list of names of those contributing, and the amounts.].....The total collected was $22.25.

A. Grebelis was appointed to deliver the donations. Societies, do not forget our distinguished writer!
