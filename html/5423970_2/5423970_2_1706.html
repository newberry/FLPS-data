The Lithuanian Alliance of America branch 129, held its meeting July 24. The membership decided to urge the Lithuanian Alliance of America, Chicago branches, to participate in the reception being planned for the Lithuanian representatives, Dr. T. Basanavicius, and M. Ycas. For this purpose, a committee was elected. They also decided to present a play, "The Slavery of Men," September 21, 1913 at Meldazis Hall. Two well-known Lithuanian artists, F. Motuzas, and J. Juknis, recent arrivals in this country, who have played in the theaters of Lithuania, will take part in the presentation of this play. These new artists have also joined the Lithuanian Alliance of America, Branch 129.
