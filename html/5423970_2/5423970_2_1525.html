The sixth Aurora lecture will be held at the Lithuanian Club Hall, 3149 So. Halsted Street, October 23. K. Jurgelionis will lecture on the language of the apes and the beginning of languages. The lecture will begin at 3 P. M.

After the lecture those wishing to will be able to register and choose branches of learning in the Aurora Evening Schools: Arithmetic, Geometry, Drawing, and the English and Lithuanian languages.... The classes will start Monday, October 24.
