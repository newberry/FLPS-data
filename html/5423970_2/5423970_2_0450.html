The Guards of the Grand Duke Algirdas of Lithuania Society, presented two comedies, "The Rubber Boots" and "From Ear to October 12, at M. Meldazis Hall on the West Side. The choir of the Sacred Heart Parish sang the Lithuanian national anthem and several Lithuanian popular songs. The Stepukas Vaudeville was also there. The program was varied, and the performances were very good. I am very pleased that this society takes care of national affairs. The attendance was large, and the conduct of the audience was excellent. The program was followed by dances.
