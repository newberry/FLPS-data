In the Book of Knowledge you will find facts on Mythology, History, Ethnography, Geography, Astronomy, Arithmetic, Medicine and on other scientific questions. There are suggestions for home remedies, for various tradesmen, for farmers, gardeners and others. These facts have been collected from various sources, by J. Laukis, Chicago. The book was published by Lietuva, 3252 So. Halsted Street. It contains 392 pages, priced at $1.50, with good cover, $2.50.
