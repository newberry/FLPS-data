The Leveskis Commercial and Language School, 3106 So. Halsted Street, closed for a one-month vacation. It will open again August 3. The past year was quite a year. The day school was attended by 78 students while 169 took the evening courses. According to nationalities, the students were: 1 Negro, 2 Germans, 3 English, 3 Poles, 4 Russians; the rest were Lithuanians.
