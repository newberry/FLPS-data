A total of 185 cuts of drawings and photographs were made this week in preparation for the publication of the Lithuanian translation of Gabriel d'Annunzio's book entitled Sirdis (The Heart). It will soon be published by the Lovers of the Fatherland Society. The cost of producing the cuts was $120.

A book entitled Motina (Mother) is now being published by the Society.

All publications of the Society, which has branches in Chicago and other parts of the United States, are distributed free of charge to all members, for the purpose of promoting the intellectual advancement of our people.
