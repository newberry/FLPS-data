The Lithuanian Harmony Singers will present Nagornski's five-act, eight-scene tragedy "Zivile" at the School Hall, 48th Street and South Honore Avenue, on Sunday, January 20, 1918. The performance will begin at 5 P. M.

The public has only to hear the name "Zivile" to know that it will not be disappointed if it attends. After the performance there will be dancing as usual. Admission will be thirty-five cents and up.
