p.8...F. Norvaisa invited the members of the Kankliu Choras (Lithuanian Guitar Chorus) and relatives to celebrate his birthday at his home on Jan.2. During the course of the party some of the guests delivered talks on the present economic situation and the need for a new social order. A suggestion was made to make a collection for the relief of class war prisoners in Lithuania. The guests contributed a total of $17.50, which was later forwarded to the Lithuanian Section of the International Labor defense.
