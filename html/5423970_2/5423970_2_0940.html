Mr. P. Brandukas, who was the editor of the Katalikas (The Catholic) for many years, is now the editor of the Draugas (The Friend). He Mr. Brandukas was the editor of the Draugas for a short time once before. This change indicates that the Katalikas has been adhering to the same political policies as the Draugas and the Darbininkas (The Worker), which is published in Boston, Massachusetts.

It is reported that Julius Kaupas, former editor of the Draugas, has become the business manager of the Katalikas.
