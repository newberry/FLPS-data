A lecture entitled "The End of the World Has Come; Millions Will Not Die" will be given Saturday, May 25, 1918, at the School Hall, 4758 South Honore Street--the large hall on the third floor. It will begin at 7:30 P.M. Admission will be free to all.

Notice: This lecture is very important and instructive and should be heard from beginning to end by everybody who notices this announcement. It has been delivered in various American cities by the New York attorney, J.F. Rutherford, president of the International Biblical Research Association. Certain explanations will be made in the Lithuanian language by S. Beneckis.
