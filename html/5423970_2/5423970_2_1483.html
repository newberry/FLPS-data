The Aurora Society will sponsor the following lectures:

November 10: "The School," by V. Niseika.

November 17: "The Life of Jesus, According to Renan," by A. Petraitis.

November 24: "Japan and Its Inhabitants," by Kato (a Japanese).

The above lecture will be delivered in English and will be illustrated with pictures and music.

December 1: "Insurance Societies and Companies," by K. Jurgelionis.

December 8: "The Workers' Movement In Japan," by K. Kvedaras.

December 15: "The Economic Foundations of Christianity," by Pruseika.

December 22: "The White Slave," by K. Valys.

All of the above-mentioned lectures will be given at the Aurora Hall, 3149 So. Halsted Street and will begin at exactly 4 P.M.
