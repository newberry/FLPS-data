On last Thursday, delegates of the Lithuanian National Cemetery organization held their meeting. They have made plans for a celebration of the installation of the bell at the Lithuanian National Cemetery on Decoration Day. The following persons were elected to collect money among the Lithuanians for the cemetery: Messrs B. Andriusiunas, Antanas Astrauskas, A. Andrikaitis, J. Simenis, K. Buozis, and Vincentas Gaudisius.
