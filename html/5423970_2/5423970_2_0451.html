Last Sunday, October 5, at St. George's Parish Hall, the Birute Society presented a three-act comedy, "Cannot Blow Against the Wind." The presentation of this play was fair. The artists performed their roles very well. If there had not been a mishap in one of the scenes of the third act, everything would have been perfect.....

After the performance, the Birute Choir, directed by Mr. A. Pocius, sang several songs. The program was concluded with the Lithuanian national anthem. After the program were dances.
