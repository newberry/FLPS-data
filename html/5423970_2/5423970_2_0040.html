Another Lithuanian drugstore has been recently opened at 3203 South Halsted Street. The proprietor of this establishment is Mr. Kartanas, who recently passed a state examination and became a registered pharmacist.

This indicates that our people are making rapid progress in the drugstore business. Mr. A. Jonaitis, who has just arrived in America from Europe, has obtained a position at the pharmacy of Mr. Gecas [a Lithuanian], 3159 South Kedzie Avenue. Mr. Jonaitis was a pharmacist in Lithuania. He plans to open a drugstore of his own after he becomes better acquainted with America.
