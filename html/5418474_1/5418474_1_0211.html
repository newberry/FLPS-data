The Chicago Committee of the International Congress for Education, held a meeting, presided by the Gymnast Charles Bary. Dr. Reuter was elected a member of the Board and the financial committee was formed by Mr. Phillip Greiner and the ladies Parker and Leewens. The question about the chances of a publication, as a propaganda for the Congress, has been referred to this committee.

The recommendations of Prof. Suder and the gymnastic teacher Kindervater to send invitations abroad, have been accepted. The debates for the Congress are limited to education, recreative and medical gymnastics. The next meeting will take place Saturday afternoon at 4 o'clock.
