According to the just published annual report of the Superintendent, Dr. Zimmermann, 44,270 pupils participated in the past School year in German instruction, which was given by 258 teachers. Of these figures 16 teachers and 1446 pupils come to the eleven high schools and 242 teachers and 42,824 pupils to the Grammar and Primary grades. Of the total 18,558 were of German origin, 13,677 of Anglo-American, and 12035 of Swedish, Bohemian, etc. descent. The average daily attendance at the primary and grammar grades in which the pupils could participate in the German instruction, amounted to 69,338, and 34,547 of these or 50 5 studied the German language.
