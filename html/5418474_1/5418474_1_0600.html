The school board was in session yesterday and Mr. C. L. Warner, chairman of the Finance Committee, submitted the budget for the fiscal school year. The net expense for school purposes will be $5,859,800...

Gymnastics.......$23,000.

German Language Instruction.......155,500.

Last year the cost of German instruction amounted to $150,979.95. This year $155,500. were provided and, as there is a $10,000 surplus available from the past year, Chairman Halle is fully satisfied with the present allotment....
