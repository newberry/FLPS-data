On Wednesday evening, Mayor Harrison will submit to the City Council the names of the newly appointed members of the school board. On July 1, the terms of the following school supervisors will expire: John McLaren, Thomas Brennan, Daniel N. Cameron, F. Goetz, S. A. Gunderson, Edward G. Halle, and N. J. Keane. It is rumored that the gentlemen Halle, Brennan, and Cameron will be reappointed not so McLaren and Gunderson who proved to be enemies of German lessons.
