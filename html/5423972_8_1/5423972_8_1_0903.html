A Herzl Memorial Meeting conducted by Zionist organizations, was held July 12, and consisted of two mass-meetings and a parade. The parade assembled in the Institute-grounds and marched, 1000-strong, from there through the principal streets of the neighborhood, returning to the Institute.

The older persons assembled in Annex Hall and the children in the Synagogue. About 1500 persons attended both meetings, and with music and oratory paid tributes to the character and achievements of the great Zionists leader.
