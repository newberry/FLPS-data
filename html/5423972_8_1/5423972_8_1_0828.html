A Hebrew organization for adults was started with the Zeirie Zion, 3317 W. Roosevelt Road, on August 16. The purpose of this organization is to create a Hebrew atmosphere and to revive Hebrew literature and culture by means of debates, lectures, and entertainments. The executive committee consists of Messrs. Rabinovitz, Schechtman, Liebenson, Milman, and Miss A. Lwirsky.

A special organization will be created on September 10, for those from fifteen to eighteen years of age, at 3317 W. Roosevelt Road. Officers will be elected.
