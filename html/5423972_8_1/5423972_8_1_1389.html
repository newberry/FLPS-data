Tomorrow evening, May 1st celebration in the West Side Auditorium, where the freeing of Eugene V. Debs will be demanded. The speakers: Joel Entin, well known journalist, brought specially from N. Y., William Nathan and M. Schuchter. The following will participate in the program: Ralph Beck, Jacob Allen-horn, Moshe Shore.

This meeting is arranged by the Poale Zion and the Jewish National Workers Alliance. Bezalel Sherman, Chairman.
