Edward Menkin, a prominent Chicago attorney, is doing his utmost to put the State of Illinois in line for a national rebuke to Russia, for the persecution of the Jews. A resolution to this effect was prepared by Mr. Menkin and introduced by Senator Niels Juul, on April 25, in the State Senate at Springfield.

Mr. Menkin is a member of the Oriental Lodge, I. O. B. B., B. M. Z., Home for the Aged Jews and the Isaiah Temple.
