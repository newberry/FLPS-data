Report of the Building Committee.

The argument has been advanced that as the drift of habitation was to so large an extent southward at the present time, that it might be a wise plan to sell our present building and erect a new structure. So far as the locality is concerned, in the judgement of your Board and Committee, it is believed that our present site is perhaps as good a location as can be secured, notwithstanding the fact that the great majority of our members are quite distant from our building.
