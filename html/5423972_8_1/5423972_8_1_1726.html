Mr. B. Hurwitz, chairman of the Reception Committee, who was appointed by Mayor Thompson to extend greetings to Prof. Chaim Weitzman and his Zionest Delegation to Chicago, has, yesterday, appealed, at a meeting of the Fraternal Orders, to the members to participate in a parade which is being arranged for the Zionest visitors this coming Tuesday. Those who signed to be present at the parade are:

D. R. J. Sultar, of the A.A.B.A.

Charles Bonus, of the A.B.A

Ike Shapiro, of the A.W.S.A.

Sam Epstein, of the P.A.W.

Sam Blier, of the A.A.B.S.
