Those who witnessed a gradual declining interest by our Jewish youth in things Jewish are not privileged to witness a changing scene. Our youth, who dissatisfied with our Jewish way of living in the other world, were fast leaving us seem to have found a new way of life in this new world of ours. Our radical friends have now accepted Jewish ceremonies and customs, holidays and festivals and are preparing to celebrate them within their new life with new interpretations. A gratifying spectacle indeed.
