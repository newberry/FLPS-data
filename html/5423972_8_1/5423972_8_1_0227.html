The sale of the building owned by Max Goodhartz has been completed. The property is situated on the northeast corner of Lincoln and Berteau Avenues, and has been purchased by Edith and Ida Cohen for an estimated price of $120,000. However, the apartment building located at the southeast corner of Albany Avenue and 15th Street and owned by the purchaser, has been taken in trade. The value of this property was set at $81,000.
