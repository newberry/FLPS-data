At the annual meeting of the Hadassah, Saturday evening, a beautiful rug was raffled off. The rug was presented to this society by Mr. Jacob Cohen, of 831 Ashland Boulevard. The raffle brought in one hundred and fifty dollars. Mrs. Feinstein paid the highest price and won the rug.

After the raffle, the elections took place. Miss Lillian Herstein was reelected president. Mrs. Benjamin Davis was elected vice-president, Miss Anna Levin, corresponding secretary, Miss Anna Robin, recording secretary, and Miss Nehemiah Horwich, treasurer. A new board of directors was also elected.
