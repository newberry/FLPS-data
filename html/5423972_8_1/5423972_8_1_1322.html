The Universal Club of Chicago, was organized this week at Kenwood Hall. The club is composed of young Jewish men of the city. It intends to provide for the educational and athletic, as well as the social welfare, of the members. Plans are to be formulated for a clubhouse to cost $25,000 to be erected in the vicinity of 51st street and Grand Blvd.

The officers are - Louis Grollman, president, Sidney Ware, vice-president, Oliver Salinger, recording secretary, David Hirsch, financial secretary, and Joel S. Flesh, treasurer.
