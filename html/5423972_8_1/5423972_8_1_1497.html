G. Snydacker reports that we have a proposition from Mr. Martin for the renting of his hall on Indiana Avenue, corner of 22nd St. for $1,200 per annum, for Saturday and Sunday service in the forenoon, five evening services, five congregational meetings, lighting, heating and janitor work, and he will furnish three-hundred seats, carpets for aisles, first three months to be paid in advance at once, then monthly payments.
