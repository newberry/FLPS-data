The Zionists in Chicago have begun a campaign to enlarge the number of registered Zionists in Chicago. This is in honor of their convention which will be held in Chicago, September 14th, (1919) and in which Justice Louis D. Brandeis, honorary president of the Zionist Organization of America, will participate.

The four Chicago districts have elected their officers and their delegates to the convention. Among the elected delegates are, Judge Julian W. Mack, Judge Hugo Pam, Max Shulman, Nathan Kaplan, Leon Zolartaroff, and others.

The presidents of the four districts are, Meyer Abrams, chairman of Lawndale district, H. Steinberg, Northwest district, H. L. Meines, West side district and Mr. H. Phillips, South side district.
