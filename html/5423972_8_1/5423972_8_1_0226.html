The twenty-six family apartment building at 4834-4840 West Henderson Street, including the plot, 100 by 125 feet, has been sold by M. Chambers to Annette G. Gruber at an undisclosed figure. The property is encumbered by a mortgage of $110,000.

The Stern Clothing Company acquired its third store at the southwest corner of North and Crawford Avenues. The premises have been leased for several years for $300,000. After the necessary alterations have been made, the store will be opened on or about February 1.
