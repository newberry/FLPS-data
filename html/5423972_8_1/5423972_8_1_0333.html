The firm deals in real estate in all its branches, rents and manages small and large buildings, make loans, sells a wide variety of sound securities, and represents the most important insurance companies. Mr. Greenfield, who has always taken an active interest in the Jewish community, is to be congratulated for establishing himself with this firm.
