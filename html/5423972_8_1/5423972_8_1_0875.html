Three meetings took place last night, on the West and North West Side. The Zionists celebrated the occasion that brought about a revival of the hopes that the Jewish people will again reclaim Palestine.

The twenty-second yearly convention of the Federate Zionist Societies of the middle west took place at the Hotel La Salle, during the day, where 400 delegates set plans to strengthen the Zionist movement, and the masses expressed hope and enthusiasm.

The meetings were held at the Hebrew Institute and the Sabin school auditorium. The speakers were Dr. Mosinzon, Louis Lisky of New York and others.

The convention will continue today, and will close with a banquet, tonight at the Morrison Hotel.
