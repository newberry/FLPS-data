All synagogues in Chicago are celebrating the "Chanukah" Festivals. Chanukah candles are being lit in all the orthodox and reform synagogues, and great pomp and ceremony is displayed.

In the Ohave Sholom Synagogue, Cantor Maizel and his talented shoir will perform the lighting of the Chanukah candles.

In the Hebrew Institute, Cantor Waldowsky will perform the ceremony.
