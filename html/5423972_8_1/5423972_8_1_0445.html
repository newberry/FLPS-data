These thereupon ensued a discussion as to the future location of Sinai Temple. After considerable deliberation, all of which revealed the recognition of the urgent need for the removal of Sinai Temple and Center from its present location at 46th and South Parkway, the President (N.E. Greenebaum) was authorized to appoint a committee to throughly investigate possible sites for the building of a new edifice which might serve the needs of the Congregation more effectively.
