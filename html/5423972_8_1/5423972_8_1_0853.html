The first woman's auxiliary to be organized under the new rules of the Order is that of David Fish Lodge No. 130, which began its career today with Mrs. Martin Ritter, president, and Mrs. Gottfried D. Bernstein, secretary.

The auxiliary was instituted by Benjamin Samuels, first vice-president of the Order, representing the Grand Officers, in the Lodge rooms at the Covenant Club.

Adolph Kraus, president of the B'nai B'rith, sent greetings and expressed regret that he was unable to attend the ceremony.
