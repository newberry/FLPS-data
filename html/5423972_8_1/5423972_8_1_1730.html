The Boruchov memorial meeting, under the auspices of the left wing of the Poale Zion, will take place Tuesday, December 21st, in the National Socialist Institute, 3322 Douglas Blvd. H. Nathanson, R. Risback, M. Schucter, will be the principal speakers, and Isenberg will act as chairman. Esther Yampolsky will play suitable music. The audience will participate in the songs.
