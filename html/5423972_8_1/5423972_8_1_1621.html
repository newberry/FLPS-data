At a meeting of the Chicago division of the Mid-Western Region, held yesterday in the offices of the Zionist Organization, the question was discussed as to what position the Chicago Zionists shall take [relative] to the Jewish Congress which will convene in October.

After a lengthy discussion, a resolution indorsing the Jewish Congress was adopted. Mr. Blitz of New York explained the objectives and purposes of the Congress. He also reported that Judge Hugo Pam had accepted the chairmanship of the Congress Committee in Chicago.
