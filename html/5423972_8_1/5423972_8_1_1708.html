All Ukranian Countrymen of Chicago will hold a Conference, Sunday, Mar. 19, at 10 A.M.

M. Rashkes, representative of the "Yidhezkom", in Russia, who arrived in Chicago, yesterday, will purposely remain in order to confer with representatives of the Ukrenian Countrymen and will explain to them the plans to transmit immidiate help to Jews in Ukrenia.

The Conference will take place at Mattz Hall, in the building of the 16th St. Bank, corner 16th and St. Louis Ave. The Countrymen are requested to elect their delegates to the Conference.

Rashkes will suggest plans of much important.
