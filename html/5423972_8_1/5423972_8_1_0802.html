A letter from Dr. E. Schreiber was presented and read as follows, to-wit:

Chicago, Illinois

April 23, 1902

Mr. Harry Hart, President

Sinai Congregation

Mr. President and gentlemen of the Board of Trustees:

Since February, I teach every Saturday afternoon a class of twelve Jewish boys, all from the Ghetto, in the Parental School of Chicago, situated on W. Berwin and St. Louis Ave., about eight miles from my residence..... The number of Jewish pupils is always increasing, as a new building has been added.
