Congregation Sha'areh Tfilah B'nai Reuben is at present making great preparations for a bazaar which will open Saturday evening, at their synagogue, 1105 S. Paulina Street, and will continue for one week. Mr. Meyer Abraham will lecture today on the life of our great Jewish Poet, Rabbi Yehulah Halevi, who lived centuries ago.

Mr. E. Tobin, the Vice-President of the Synagogue, will be the Chairman of this meeting.
