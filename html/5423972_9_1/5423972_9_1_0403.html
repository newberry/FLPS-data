Rogers Park is to have an important addition to its religious life in the Temple B'nai Zion, a synagogue which will be erected at 1439 Pratt Blvd. The structure will be built at a cost of $250,000 exclusive of the land.

B'nai Zion Congregation is only four years old and has a membership of three-hundred. Dr. A. L. Lassen is its Rabbi and the president is Morris Joseph.
