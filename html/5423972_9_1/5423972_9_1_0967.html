Plans for the Sinai Congregation new temple have been completed by Alfred S. Alschules, the architect. In addition to the temple there is to be a separate building for a social center. The cost of the two structures will be approximately $350,000. The construction work will begin as soon as a site has been selected. The temple will seat 1500 people.

The first floor of the social center building will contain an assembly room, accommodating 700 persons, a library, a domestic science dept., and a small stage.

The second floor will be given to schoolrooms. There will be seven with facilities for 350 students. Both the temple and the social center building are to be completed by April, 1911.
