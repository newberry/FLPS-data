The Congregation Ahavath Zion Ansh Tiktin, 1337 South Sangamon Street, united with the Congregation Atereth Israel, 1230 South Millard Avenue, and the name of the united synagogues will be Atereth Israel Anshe Tiktin. The membership plans to build a beautiful synagogue and hopes that all countrymen will come to their aid in the sacred undertaking.

The officers of the united congregation are Jacob Rukuzin, president; Chaim Rapilovitz, vice-president; Glicksberg, vice-president; S. Harris, treasurer, M. Liberman, recording secretary; Grass, financial secretary; and H. Karol, first trustee.
