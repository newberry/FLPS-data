The corner stone of Congregation B'Nai Berzales and center, will be laid on July 15. The temple and community cemter is located on Champlain ave. between 60 and 61st streets.

The building is a two story structure, the first floor will house a large community center which will seat between 800 and 1,000 people. The synagogue will occupy the entire second floor with a seating capacity, including the balcony, of 1,500.
