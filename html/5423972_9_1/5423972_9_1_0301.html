p. 3 - At yesterday's closing session of the Independent Order of B'nai B'rith, the district lodge No. 6 decided, to indorse the Chautauqua - system. For this purpose $400 has been appropriated. The district lodge announced its intentions, to arrange a course in Jewish history and philosophy. It has also made preparations for a series of lectures, by the most prominent scholars.

The Order of B'nai B'rith was founded October 18th, 52 years ago, - therefore that day is to be known henceforth, as the "B'nai B'rith Day."
