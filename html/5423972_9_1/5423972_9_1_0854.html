Rabbi S. Winograd, the rabbi of the Bnai Sholom Congregation, will soon occupy the pulpit at the Mikrah Kodesh Anshe Pinsk Congregation. A few days ago, this congregation gave him a rabbinical contract for several years.

Rabbi Winograd is one of the youngest rabbis in Chicago. During the time he has been here, however, he has attained prominence as a great scholar and a very energetic welfare worker, particularly in the Mizrachi movement.

Rabbi winograd is a native of Jerusalem and is a descendant of great rabbis and scholars.
