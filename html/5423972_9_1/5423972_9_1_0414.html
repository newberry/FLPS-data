The North Shore Congregation Israel announce that plans have been completed to erect a temple to cost $275,000.00. Members of this congregation number some three-hundred families and live in the strip of territory along Lake Michigan from Evanston to Highland Park.

The site of the new synagogue is at Lincoln and Vernon Avenues. Work will start at once with the expectation that it will be ready for religious services by October 1927. The congregation which is seven years old is headed by Rabbi Harvey E. Wessel.
