Samuel Meisenberg, son of Mr. and Mrs. Mayer Meisenberg, living on Racine Ave., is numbered among the sailors who lost their lives in the Vera Cruz engagement on April 21.

Secretary of the Navy, Josephus Daniels sent the following telegram to the bereaved family. - "Let me express my deep sympathy in the loss of your son at Vera Cruz, Mexico, April 21, 1914. His patriotic courage has placed his name high on the roll of those who lay down their lives for their country."

Samuel Meisenberg was a wireless operator and enlisted at the Chicago Naval Training Station, last June.
