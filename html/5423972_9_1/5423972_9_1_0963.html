Congregation Agudas Achim Anshe Janova, Kovna Gubernia, which has existed some twelve years, now announce that we have purchased a building to be used as a synagogue, at 13th and Loomis streets. We invite all our landsleit to be present at our grand opening ceremonies, Sunday May 8th, and avail themselves of the use of a synagogue intended especially for them.

(Signed) The Committee.
