A banquet in honor of all the leaders of South Side congregations was given last night at 114 East 59th Street. The toastmaster of the evening was Harry H. Blum and speeches were delivered by Judge [Hugo] Pam, Judge [Joseph] David, Dr. Ruben, Dr. Schmarya Levin, and Rabbi [Benjamin] Daskal.

Around $10,000 was pledged for the Keren Hayesod ["exchequer" of World Zionist Organization] after an appeal was made by Judge Pam. Mrs. Blum promised to donate ten per cent of the total sum which was pledged.

Cantor Cohen and Miss Bernstein contributed musical selections.
