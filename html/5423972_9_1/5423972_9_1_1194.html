Several Jewish soldiers and sailors met for the first time June 9, at 3512 W. 16th St.

Friend M. Schuchter spoke on the importance of establishing a veteran league, which should concern itself with the burning questions of today, whether general humane, labor, or Jewish.

It was decided unanimously to organize a soldier's and sailor's veteran league. B. Nimedinsky was appointed as secretary and H. Braus, J. Holtzman, M. Rozen, and I. Gold, in the executive.
