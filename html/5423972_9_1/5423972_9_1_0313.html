The American Congress of Liberal Religious Societies began its second annual session at 8 o'clock Tuesday evening, June 4th in Sinai Temple. The address of welcome was made by Dr. Hirsch. Thirty-two societies were actually represented by delegates and in addition there were some two-hundred greetings from those who could not attend. Leo Fox of Chicago, was elected treasurer for the ensuing year and Dr. Hirsch was elected one of the vice-presidents.
