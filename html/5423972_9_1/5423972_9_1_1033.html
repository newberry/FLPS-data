Dr. Emil G. Hirsch

My Dear Sir:

At Executive Board meeting held on Monday, January 27, 1896, the following resolution was adopted by a unanimous vote and I take pleasure in communicating it to you.

Resolved: That the Executive Board of Directors of Chicago Sinai Congregation recommend to the next annual meeting of the congregation the reelection of Dr. E. G. Hirsch as Rabbi of the congregation for a term of ten years at a salary of $12,000 - per annum.

Yours Truly

A. Benswanger, Secretary.
