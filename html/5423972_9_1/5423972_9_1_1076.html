The eighteenth annual convention of the Union of American Hebrew Congregations, will be held in Baltimore, commencing July 6. Congregations Anshe Mayriv and Zion, of this city, are members of the Union, and both will be represented at the meeting. The delegates from Anshe Mayriv are Dr. I. S. Moses, Jacob Rosenberg, Lazarus Silverman, H. A. Kohn, Henry N. Hart, Israel Cowen, H. E. Eliassof and J. Newman, Jr. Zion Congregation will be represented by Dr. Stolz.
