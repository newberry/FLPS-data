A meeting of Ezrath Chalutzim was held at the home of Mrs. A. Berger last Tuesday afternoon, July 5. Upon completion of business, Mrs. Joseph Hevish, the brilliant speaker, inspired the members by a stirring talk on our duty to the brave pioneers of Palestine.

Mr. Sigmund Schein, recovering from a recent illness, donated $10.00 to the organization. Upon adjournment, refreshments were served.
