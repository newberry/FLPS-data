Representatives of fourteen congregations met on October 16, at the Standard Club in answer to a letter sent by the Chicago Rabbinical Association. Dr. Stolz, President of the Chicago Rabbinical Association, opened the meeting and explained its purposes.

A constitution was voted upon and accepted. The action of the meeting is now to be reported to the various congregations. After seven have notified the secretary of their acceptance, the Federation of Synagogues will be declared duly organized. The work of the proposed federation includes synagogues and school extension.
