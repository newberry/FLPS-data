Dr. Emil G. Hirsch, of Sinai Temple, will give the eleventh of his series of sermons on "My Religion", on Sunday, December 5, at 10:30 A. M. His subject will be "The New Social Adjustment Suggested by the Implications of My Religion".

Dr. Hirsch will bring to his audience suggested avenues of approach, which might help to lead us out of the darkness of these days of misunderstanding and passion, into ways that promise co-operation and understanding.
