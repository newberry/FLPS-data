"A call has been issued by Dr. Hirsch and several other ministers of liberal denominations for a congress of Liberal Religious Societies of America, to be held in Chicago, when an adequate number of signatures have been obtained.

A gathering of such men should meet with the most hearty approval of all enlightened people. Through liberality and tolerance, we must look for the abolishment of religious prejudicial persecution, so prevalent these days."

- Hebrew Observer - Cleveland, Ohio.
