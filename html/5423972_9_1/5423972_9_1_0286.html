To Unite All Jews of America.

Dr. George Sultan of this city has received a communication from Mr. Max Stern, grandmaster of the Independent Order of the Brith Abraham, to the effect that he be present at a meeting of the grandmasters, to work out a plan to unite all the Jews and Jewish organizations in America.

The resolution was decided upon at the last convention of the Brith Abraham, on June 6, 1906. The meeting will take place at 37 Seventh street, New York City.

Mr. Stern states that were all Jewish organizations united into one large federation, it would have a membership of more than 2,000 Jews. Such an accomplishment would be a great achievement for our people.
