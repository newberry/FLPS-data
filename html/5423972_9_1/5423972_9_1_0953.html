Rabbi Edelsohn Lectures.

Saturday in Kovno Synagogue, Rabbi Edelsohn of the Denver Sanitarium, who astonished the Jews in Chicago with his wonderful addresses on former occasions, will lecture here again this Saturday in Kovno Synagogue, corner 14th place and Johnson St. at 5 P. M. Do not fail to be on hand for this lecture. He has a great message for the Jewish public in Chicago.
