"Selamed B'nei Yehudah", is the name of a new Jewish organization on the West Side, whose aim will be to study the Jewish history through the centuries of Jewish sufferings.

This organization was organized by Rabbi Isaac Caplan of Cong. Ohel Yakove Anshe Israel, which has existed as a Sabbath observing organization for the past eight years.

Meetings are held every Tuesday evening at Cong. Ohel Yakove, located at 15th and Homan Avenue.
