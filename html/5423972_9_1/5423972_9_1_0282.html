Among the most prominent members who will be inducted in the Gate are Rabbi Messing and Mr. Zelen Freund, Secretary of the "Bnai Brith". Mr. Leon Zolotkof is slated as Master Zionist.

The organizers expect to make this gate the largest in Chicago. Committee were appointed for a drive to secure their members. The reorganization will take place in the evening at 8 PM, in the rooms of the Zion Society.
