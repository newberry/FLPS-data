Yesterday afternoon, a large crowd of men and women witnessed the ceremony of the laying of a cornerstone for the new synagogue, North Shore Congregation B'nai Israel, 5025 Kenmore Avenue. Speeches were delivered by Rabbi [David] Almond, spiritual leader of the congregation, Judge [Harry M.] Fisher, Barney Stone, Rabbi [Saul] Silber, and Captain Weiss. Isidore Lieberman was chairman. Six thousand dollars was raised for the synagogue which will be one of the most beautiful in Chicago.
