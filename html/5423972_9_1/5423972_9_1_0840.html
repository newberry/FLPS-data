At yesterday's meeting the County Board of Commissioner's decided to give the necessary space in Oak Forest to establish a synagogue and center for the Jewish incurables.

President Peter Reinberg, Commissioner Bartly Berk, and Commissioner Frank Ryan were the strongest advocates favoring the grant of space in Oak Forest to build a spiritual home for the Jewish patients. Had it not been for their great efforts and support, the plan would have failed.

And now, as to these uncurable Jewish patients, the committee will set to work to obtain funds for this synagogue and center.
