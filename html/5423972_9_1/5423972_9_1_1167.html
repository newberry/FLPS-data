It was moved that the Board embodies in its recommendation to the congregation that the house standing on the ground bought of C. M. Culbertson, be tendered to our Minister for occupancy, except two rooms which should be reserved for the use of the congregation for Sunday School and meeting purposes, and that our Minister can either occupy said house on the first of May, or as soon as moved to its permanent site, subject however to the revocation of the congregation from year to year upon three months notice previously given, carried.
