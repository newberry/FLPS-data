An active campaign has been started to spread the Young Judea movement in all sections of the city. The plans call for the establishment of groups of boys and girls clubs in Hebrew schools, congregations and Jewish social centers over the city.

Young Judea is a national league of the Jewish youth. Its aims are to foster Jewish study and observance and to further the Zionist movement. In addition to conducting clubs, it publishes several periodicals, issues various Jewish booklets for the young, and celebrates the Jewish festivals by holding demonstrations.
