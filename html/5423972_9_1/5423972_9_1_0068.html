Atlantic City, June 17. In his message to the convention of the Order Brith Sholom, the Grandmaster, Dr. Louis Robinson reviewed the Polish pogroms and made a few proposals which would aid the victims.

One called for the levying of a ten cent assessment on each member of Brith Sholom. This resolution was adopted.

He also recommended that the Order erect a building in Philadelphia for its national headquarters and that it donate $5,000 to the Palestine Redemption Fund.

This Order has a membership of 52,000 in 400 lodges.
