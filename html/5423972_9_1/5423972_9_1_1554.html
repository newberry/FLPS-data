The organization, Ezrath Chalutzim, held a parlor meeting at the home of Mrs. M. Braude, last Tuesday. During the business session tickets for the coming card party were distributed among the members for immediate sale.

Rabbi Rosenbaum of Temple Judaea was the speaker of the afternoon. The rabbi, by his stirring talk and parable, encouraged the women to carry on their present Zionist work with greater zeal. Through the efforts of Mrs. Fred Lubin (The founder of Ezrath Chalutzim) an electric washing machine was presented and shipped to the [gap]hl School in Tel Aviv, Palestine.
