Congregation Anshe Sforad wishes to announce that they have appointed a new Shamas (Beadle) to take charge of affairs of the congregation. We ask that all monies due the congregation be forwarded to Mr. Nahum Silber, our new Shamas. We also wish to announce that our congregation is open every morning at 5 o'clock and that we have Minianim (Religious quorum) at three different times.

In the evening we have a regular study period between the Minchah and Maariv prayers.

Respectfully,

Congregation Anshe Sforad.
