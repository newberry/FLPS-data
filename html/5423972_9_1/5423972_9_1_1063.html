The new temple of the B'nai Abraham Congregation on Marshfield Ave., which was dedicated on September 9th,is a beautiful edifice, and takes rank among the fine synagogues of our city. It is Moorish architecture and presents a massive appearance. It has a seating capacity of 960.

Rabbi Levy delivered the dedication sermon, and Dr. Hirsch gave the oration.

The officers of the congregation for the ensuing year are, Chas. Klaussner, president, Louis and Chas. Levy, 1st and 2nd vice-president, Gust. Kassowitz, secretary, Adolph Norden, financial secretary and David Goldstine, treasurer.
