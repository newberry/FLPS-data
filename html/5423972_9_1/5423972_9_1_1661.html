An interesting and successful conference of representatives, of various Jewish organizations in Chicago, was held, yesterday, at Rosenthals' Restaurant, where the question of organizing a Chicago branch of the Ort was discussed.

Dr. Leon Bramson, who called this conference and came to Chicago, especially to organize a branch of the world wide Ort in an effective speech to those present, stated, the main aims of the Ort organization and clearly outlined the relief work Ort has done in the past and also, regarding plans for future activities.
