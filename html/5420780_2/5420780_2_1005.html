Yes, our people, the Scandinavian people, probably love Chicago as much as any other nationality does, if not more. From time to time poets write about her in verse, singers sing about her in song. Yes, we all believe that Chicago is beautiful--our Chicago.

Antoni Thomsen, Chicago's most distinguished Danish poet, writes about Chicago. Kristian Baun writer of excellent prose and poetry, writes about Chicago. Antoni Thomsen has just finished, a poem about our city. We feel that this poem is an example of the talented people whom we have here in "our city". We reprint Thomsen's poem.

The Song of Chicago

See her stand in all her splendor

When the early dawn doth break,

Brilliant, cheerful, bathed in sunlight

\2

Fanned by breezes from the lake,

See her parks and see her highways,

See her tow'rs against the sky,

And you'll feel her dauntless spirit,

And we'll love her, you and I!

Refrain:

Hail the City of Chicago,

Where your home, your future lies!

From her ever-growing beauty

Love and fame anew shall rise!

She shall rise! She shall rise!

Grand and greater she shall rise!

Here we stand in strong allegiance

To our city, to our homes,

While the marvel of her story

\3

Over distant oceans roams,

How she rose from fire and ashes

Phoenixlike--and oh, behold!

New and greater--always aiming

For a higher, vaster goal.
