George Dupont-Mansen, composer and pianist. Pupil of August Winding, Carl Attrup, and Orla Rosenhoff. Works: Six pieces for piano, edited in Denmark and Germany. "Thema med Variation" dedicated to Otterstrom; "Julekantate"; "Bornejemskantate" and "Kantate ved Trinitatis Kirkes Indvielse".

Since 1895 organist at the Danish Litheran Trinity Church, Chicago.
