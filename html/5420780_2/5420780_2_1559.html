De Skandinaviske Socialdemokraters Sygeforening (Scandinavian Social-Democratic Sick Benefit Society) has elected Mr. John Schmidt, 1282 North Talman Avenue, president. Mr. S. Huseby is the secretary.

The society now has one hundred forty members. In case of sickness members are paid $6.00 per week. The Society works through assessments of twenty-five cents; and the total cost to each member this year has been $2.25, or nine assessments.
