Hejmdal, a Danish singing society, was organized July 27, 1892, and re-organized February 20, 1914. It has 23 active members, and meets every Friday at Walhalla Hall. The secretary is Harry Kramer, 5201 South State Street.

The society had been practically dead for three years when it was re-organized in 1914. It is to give its first concert in the month of September.
