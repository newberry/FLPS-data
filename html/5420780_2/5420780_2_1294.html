A Gymnastic and Entertainment Club has been formed as an auxiliary of the society Walhalla, for the purpose of uniting young men of Danish birth or ancestry, for the cultivation of sports and athletics, such as gymnastics, boxing, wrestling, hand-ball, etc., and also music, song, and amateur dramatics.

Its official name is "Walhalla Athletic and Amusement Club," and meetings will be held in Walhalla Hall.
