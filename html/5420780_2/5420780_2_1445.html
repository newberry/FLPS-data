Den Danske Hundredmands-Forening (The Danish Hundred Men's Society) was started in 1902. Its aim is mutual aid in case of sickness and death. In case of sickness it pays six dollars a week for twenty-six weeks. It meets the second and fourth Wednesday of the month at Humboldt Hall, West North Avenue. The secretary is Mr. G. Bense, 1811 North Fairfield Avenue.
