p.4.col.3.........The question of old age benefits for members of the Danish Brotherhood Society is being discused by all local lodges. The plan has been submitted, by the officers of the supreme lodge, to the locals for consideration pending a decision to be made at the annual convention. Local lodges of Chicago are arranging a joint meeting for the consideration of the idea and delegates to the national convention will be instructed according to the joint decision. Leaders in Chicago, heartily favor an arrangment to care for veteran members and are confident that their united backing will bring about favorable votes at the convention.
