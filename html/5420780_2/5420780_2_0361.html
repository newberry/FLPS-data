Theodor Kofoed has been appointed general manager of The Co-operative Society of America. He announces that over one hundred and forty stores will be opened in Chicago this year.

The Society has its own dairy at East Troy, Wisconsin, and also owns a seventeen story warehouse at Wells and Randolph Streets. At Maplewood and Jackson Boulevards, they own an entire block where they will spend three million dollars for building a large warehouse.

There are to date one hundred and five thousand members in the Co-operative Society.
