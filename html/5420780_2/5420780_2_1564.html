p. 4.- Denmark Lodge No. 112, Knights of Pythias, has bought lots Nos. 114-115 in block N, Mt. Olive cemetery, or 830 square feet. The whole affair was arranged by the following committee: P. J. Noer, J. P. Olsen, A. Johnsen, Louis Andersen, John Rosenberg, and J. W. Hertz. The cost was $415.

This space is going to be used for the interment of members of the lodge.
