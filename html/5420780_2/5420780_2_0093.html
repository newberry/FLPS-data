A Dane, Niels S. T. Gade, died Nov. 7th. He was buried with full military honor as he participated in the Spanish-American war, Troop E. l. Illinois Volunteer Cavalry. He became ill with typhoid fever in Cuba and returned to Chicago in time to die. He was a member of Denmark Lodge, Nr. 112, Knights of Pythias.

(About his funeral see also Revyen, Nov. 19, 1898, page 4.)
