The delegates for the Hans Christian Andersen Jubilee had their final meeting in Dania, August 9. The money was turned over to the treasurer of the Children's Home, Mads Rasmussen. The total sum taken in was $9,233.57; expenses were $1,708.41, which leaves $7,525.16 for the Danish Children's Home. The Hans Christian Anderson Jubilee in Chicago brought in $4,739.18; other festivals in his honor all over the country brought in $2,500. The women collected $1,254.97. Gifts from different societies amounted to $538.03. In all these figures (sic) $2,038.73 from lodges of the Danish Brotherhood and Sisterhood is included.
