On entering any of the first class saloons or drug stores on the North Side, particularly in the Humboldt Park district, one can not help noticing the many displays of cigars by the names "Arthur Donaldson;" "Prince of Pilsen," and "Elect". The manufacturers of these cigars are the two well known Danish musicians Juergensen and Petersen, (The Humboldt Cigar Company) who started this business only one and one half years ago. During this time the undertaking has prospered and their sales indicate that they are offering extraordinary values in cigars.

The well known Danish musician Hans Nilsen, is the sole distributor for Chicago and besides, the company has two travelling representatives, who cover the entire United States.
