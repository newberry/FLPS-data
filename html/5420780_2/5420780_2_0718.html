On November 17, 1921, a group of fifteen ladies came together and decided to form Harmoniens Dameforening (The Harmonien Ladies' Society). Mrs. Otto Jensen was elected president, Mrs. John Petersen, vice-president, Mrs. Kaj Henriksen, treasurer, and Mrs. Kai Bennekon, secretary.

This board was only temporary; the society was regularly organized at a meeting on November 28, 1921.
