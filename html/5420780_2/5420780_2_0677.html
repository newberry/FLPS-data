The membership of Harmonien is divided into two groups, the active and the passive members. The active members are those who sing in the chorus. The annual dues are three dollars.

During the course of the Society's existence the number of singers has varied from thirty-five to sixty. The total membership was highest in 1924, when there were four hundred members. In 1922 there were three hundred members, and in 1936, when the fiftieth anniversary was celebrated, two hundred members.
