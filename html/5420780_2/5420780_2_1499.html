p.4.........Chicago's oldest Danish Society, "Danish," with a membership of more than two-hundred has called a meeting for the purpose of discussing the purchase of four building lots, near Humboldt Park, on which to erect a new building. The lots are said to be priced at $750.00 each, and the cost of construction will amount to $30,000. The society has a capital of $8,000. The president Mr. Halkier, is supporting the plan.
