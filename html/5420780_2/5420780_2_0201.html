Dania Society is liberally supporting the suffragettes and has granted them permission to use its hall for meetings. The next meeting is scheduled for tomorrow, Sunday Jan. 17, at 3:30 p. m. Miss H. Grimm and Mrs. May Wood Simons will each give a lecture presenting their opposing views on the subject: "Municipal Suffrage for Women." Miss Irene Benzinger and Mr. Paul Hendricksen will entertain with solos and music. Later there will be dancing, and refreshments will be served. There is no admission fee.
