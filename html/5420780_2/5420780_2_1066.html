Christian Botker, has been editor for Revyen twenty-two years. He was born 1866 in Gundslov, in Falster. In 1885, he passed his examination for teacher at the Blaagaad Seminary, and was for some years a teacher in Falster. Later, he was vicar at the communal schools of Copenhagen, and during that time he began to write for newspapers, and later as reporter for Aften Bladet (Evening Journal). He was secretary to the editor of the Soro Amtstidende (County paper). He next held the position as editorial secretary for Venstre Bladet (left paper) in Copenhagen, where he stayed until 1891. He then came to Chicago, and became editor of Revyen.
