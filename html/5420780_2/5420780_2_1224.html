Pastor M. F. Blichfeld will give a series of three lectures about the Danish people's high schools, in the assembly hall of St. Ansgar Danish Lutheran Church, 1639 N. Washtenaw Avenue. The schedule is as follows:

February 13--"How the People's High Schools Originated"; March 13--"Activities and Working Methods in the High Schools"; May 8--"The Importance of the High Schools to the Danish People." The lectures start at 8:15 P.M., and admission is free.
