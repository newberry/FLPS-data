The Scandinavian Social-Democrats' Sick Benefit Association of 1895 has published its financial statement for the year ending May 31.

The Society, which now has a membership of 523, paid out $1,948 in sick benefits during the past year. The assessment for the sick benefit and funeral funds was $7.23, higher than ever before.

The Society's building fund, having now reached $1,789.75, the building committee at last night's annual membership meeting, proposed that a committee be appointed to arrange for the purchase of a suitable building lot.
