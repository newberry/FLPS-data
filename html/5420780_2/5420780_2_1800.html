The report on the financial result of the joint concert which was given on Dec. 14 last year by Harmonien, Magneten, and the Danish Workers' Singing Society, for the benefit of the Danish Aid fund, has now been made public by Mr. Niels Ohlsen, committee treasurer.

The gross income amounted to $709.35, expenses $283.69, making the net profit $425.66, which has been handed over to Mrs. William Nelson, treasurer of the Danish Aid Society.
