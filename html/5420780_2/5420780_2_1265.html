The Danish-American Athletic Club was organized April 18, 1922. It was incorporated in 1923. The aim of the society is sport for sport's sake. The Club has taken up gymnastics, wrestling, boxing, soccer, and folk-dancing. It is at present affiliated with the Central Association of the Amateur Athletic Union, the Illinois State Football Association, and the Danish National Committee.

A business meeting is held the first Thursday of each month. The president is Mr. H. P. Halvorsen, 3043 North Kolmar Avenue. Mr. A. A. Friis, 3239 Palmer Street, is the secretary. The society owns its clubhouse at 1639 North Washtenaw Avenue. Its telephone number is Armitage 9248.
