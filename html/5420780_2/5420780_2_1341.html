The famous Danish wrestler, Bech Olsen, who is in New York, is coming to Chicago in the near future. The fight for the world championship is going to take place in New York March 21.

The Danish Gymnastic and Athletic Club here in Chicago is to arrange his visit to the Danish colony. The president of this club, Mr. A. J. Larsen, has just received a letter from Bech Olsen saying that he will let them know the date of his arrival in the near future.
