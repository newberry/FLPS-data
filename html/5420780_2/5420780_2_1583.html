Professor Dr. Thorkild Rousing, famous Danish surgeon, now visiting America, was the guest of honor at a banquet on July 4 at the Bismarck Hotel arranged by the Danish doctors in Chicago, headed by Dr. I. C. Clemensen. The professor was presented with an inscribed silver cup by his Chicago colleagues.

During his stay in our city, Dr. Rousing visited Dr. Svenning Dahl, at his home on Ewing Place and watched him operate at the Norwegian Deaconess Hospital.
