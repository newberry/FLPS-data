Den Skandinaviske Arbejdersangforening (The Scandinavian Workingmen's Singing Society) had a very successful evening in Schweim's Hall on April 13. The singers sang well under the able leadership of Mr. J. P. Jensen. Then the audience listened to a fine soprano solo by the Norwegian singer, Miss Eleonora Olsen; a violin solo by C. T. Moller; and a piano solo by Mrs. Laurine Harisen. Finally there was a fine exhibition by a German gymnastic club. The evening closed with dancing by all.
