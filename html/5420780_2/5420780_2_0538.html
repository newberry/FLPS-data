On Sunday afternoon, March 10, Mrs. Carrie Masseirus, who studies music under Madame Ragna Linne, the well-known Norwegian-American singer will give her first Chicago public concert in Mandel Hall, with Miss Prudence Nepp at the piano.

Mrs. Hasselrus is married to the secretary of the Danish-American Association, C. H. W. Hasselrus. She has an exceptionally beautiful contralto voice. Undoubtedly, her debut before a large American audience will be of more than great interest in Danish circled.
