The Echo Octet, a women's chorus which was organized only recently, gave its first concert October 13, in Wicker Park Hall. The hall was filled.

The members of the Octet are Mrs. Olga Meine, Miss Clara Fieldse, Miss Anna Tollefsen, Mrs. Ragna Albertson, Mrs. Mathilde Huseby, Miss Anna Larson, Miss Louise Larson, and Miss. Elvira Petersen.

During the evening Miss Brettsnyder gave a recitation, Mrs. Joel Mossberg sang, and Mr. Frank Winter played on the violin.
