Our well-known and beloved countryman, Mr. Axel Jorgensen, whose vocal performances have been generally enjoyed and appreciated around the Danish colony in Chicago, is engaged to sing at the Crystal Theatre, Friday and Saturday evenings. We most sincerely urge our readers to attend. Mr. Jorgensen will take care that you will not be disappointed. He has just returned from a concert tour through the Western States; the last states visited was Iowa.

His performances have received the most favorable comments both in the American and in the Danish press.
