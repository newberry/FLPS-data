Dannebrog was started August 10, 1889. Its aim is mutual aid in sickness and death, and social entertainment. It meets the first and third Saturdays of the month at 11526 South Michigan Avenue. The society pays six dollars per week in case of sickness and sixty dollars in case of death. It has sixty members. Dannebrog owns a library of three hundred volumes, all in the Danish language. The president is H. P. Littau, 11218 Michigan Avenue, and the secretary is Mr. Carl J. Andersen.
