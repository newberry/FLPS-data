On June 5, 1896, on the Danish Day of Independence, the Harmenien and Hejmdel Singing Societies gave an excellent concert in the Aurors Turner Hall. The concert was on the occasion of the visit of Miss Yalborg Andersen, an actress from the Dagmar Theater in Copenhagen, Denmark. Among the soloists were Miss V. Andersen, Miss Ella Dahl (on the piano), and Mr. Alfred Holmes (on the organ).

At the close of the concert the two choruses joined in singing "The Star-Spangled Banner".
