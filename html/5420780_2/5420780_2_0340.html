page 7 column 1... "William Harley came to Chicago in 1857, and in the course of time became a well-known builder and contractor. He built the Illinois building and several other buildings at the world's Fair. He built the Cook County building at Dunham. He had a contract on the drainage canal, in which he lost considerable money. It was Harley who "hung" the jury in the Luetgert murder trial."
