The young Danish surgeon, Dr. J. C. Johannesson, who for some time has been acting surgeon at the Loyola University, will leave next week for Los Angeles, where he will be assigned to the County Hospital.

Doctor Johannesson is a graduate of the University of Copenhagen. In this country, he is considered one of the most eminent bacteriologists and pathologists. He was temporary House Physician at the American Bridge Company of Gary, Indiana.

Ever since the Doctor's residence in Chicago, he has been a member of the Dania society.
