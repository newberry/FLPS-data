p. 2.col.6...The farewell appearance of Mr. Herskind, who for the past several months has scored a continual success in Danish-American theatrical circles, was a triumphal conclusion of his visit in Chicago and surrounding towns.

Mr. Herskind has shown great artistic ability and an energy and willingness that have won him a prominent place in the esteem of his local compatriots. His farewell audience was the largest turnout that has ever attended a Danish concert in Chicago, a real tribute to a real artist whose program consisted to some extent, of request numbers and encores.

The program was followed by a tasty lunch and dancing to music by Mrs. Mathiesen.

Mr. Herskind is scheduled to appear in Troy, N.Y., Philadelphia, Perth Amboy, N.J. and New York City, ere he sails for his home in Denmark.
