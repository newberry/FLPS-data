The memorial services held in honor of Mr. Max Henius, were sponsored by the Danish Societies of Chicago and his friends, on Sunday, January 12, 1:30 P. M. at the University of Chicago Chapel, 1166 East 59th Street.

The Danish Societies of Chicago and the friends of the late Dr. Max Henius wish to express their appreciation to the University of Chicago for the kind invitation extended to them to hold the memorial services in the chapel.

The following Danes spoke at the services: Reverend Oswald Helsing, F. M. Dupont, of the Wahl-Henius Institute, Dr. Emanuel Nielsen, Dr. J. Charles Bay, Carl B. Roden, and the Hon. R. Bauman.
