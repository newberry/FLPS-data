The society for a Danish Home for the Aged, during the year March 1, 1899 to March 1, 1900 had an income as follows:

Membership fees $459.50
Socials, etc. 735.74
Special gifts 94.25
Interest 585.15
Total $1,874.64

The expenses for the year amounted to $370.89, leaving a surplus of $1,503.75.

The total capital of the Society at present amounts to $13,106.06, placed as follows:

Mortgages $10,600.00
In the bank 954.13
Balance with the treasurer 51.93
Value of land 1,500.00
\2

In addition the Society will receive, in about one year, $1,000, willed to it by the late banker, Petersen.

The treasurer of the Society is Mrs. Geo. Olson.
