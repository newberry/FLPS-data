Four of our athletes participated in the Tribune Cross Country Race last Sunday. Robert Nussle came in as No. 20 for which he received a silver medal; Anders Schelde, No. 24, bronze medal; Niels Nielsen who is 40 was No. 50.

At the A.A.W Marathon race Niels Nielsen was No. 9 and received a gold medal.

The football match scheduled for last Sunday was postponed on account of unfavorable weather.

Next Sunday afternoon our team will meet a Norwegian team. We hope the Norwegians do not get cold feet.
