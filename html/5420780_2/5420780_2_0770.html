The Danish Workers' Singing Society's Concert last Saturday was a complete success as regards attendance, quality of performance and the appreciation shown by the audience. To watch Rolf Rammer direct his singers is in itself a real pleasure which even a deaf person could enjoy, for he uses his entire body, head, arms, legs, even his toes for a baton with a vivacity, elegance and technique which are inspiring to the audience as well as to the performers, and which have placed the choir among the first in the city.

The concert was followed by a delicious supper and an animated ball, which broke up in the wee hours Sunday morning.
