The National Theatre, 2639 W. North Avenue, has now acquired a selection of popular Danish films and presents tomorrow "The Great Circus Fire," a thrilling drama from Copenhagen, and on Thursday, Feb. 26, another Danish picture, "The Cry for Happiness," will be shown.

The Danish Pierrot himself will appear at Newell's theatre in the near future, and is sure to receive a hearty welcome from all Danes, both young and old.
