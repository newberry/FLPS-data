Last Thursday was Danish-Norwegian day at the Exposition. The Danish chorus sang the following numbers: America, Hoje Nord (Far to the North), Norden's Folkeaand (Northern Spirit), and Mrs. Harriet Hertz sang with much feeling, "Mother's Name." The Norwegian chorus was also very good.

There was a very good attendance, but the attendance should be very good, in order to cover the expenses estimated to be over $75,000.
