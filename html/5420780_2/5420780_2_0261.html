The Chemist, A. Franck-Philipsen, is without doubt, the outstanding inventor among Danish-Americans.Mr. Philipsen has made many important discoveries. One important one is a new method to dye wood, but the outstanding one is "Tarx," a new disinfectant in solid form.

Philipsen's latest discovery is an isolator bell from carbon, which takes the place of the fragile glass insulators on telegraph poles, etc.

Mr. Philipsen owns a gigantic laboratory on Lake Street and his main office is in the Peoples Gas Building.
