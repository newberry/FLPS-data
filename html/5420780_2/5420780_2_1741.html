The Society for the Danish Old People's Home had its meeting in Dania on April 18. John W. Hertz was again the chairman of the evening.

It was decided that anyone may become a life member by paying one hundred dollars. It was also resolved that quarterly meetings be held the second Wednesday of January, April, July, and October, at 8 P.M.

Only two paragraphs of the fifteen that make up the bylaws were thus dealt with. In order to speed up the business a committee was appointed, consisting of the following: Mrs. Toft, Dr. Henius, P. J. Noer, D. M. Frederiksen, Mrs. A. Christensen, Wald. Bauer, Charles Wilde, and Charles Ryberg.
