Jacon Enemark, Shoemaker and factory owner, was born July 28, 1876, at Skodborg, Denmark. He came to Bsbjerg when he was thirteen years old and to America in 1891. Worked at Perth Amboy and Boston, later he went to Portland, Me. where the last fifteen years he has been the owner of a large shoe store. He employes more than a dozen men. Was for four years the editor of the socialist paper, The Issue. He often has been candidate for public offices on the socialist ticket.

(Mr. Enemark is now the head of the Danish Passenger Department of the Gdynia American Line in New York.) J.E.
