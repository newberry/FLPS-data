On May 1, 1898, the first grand concert and ball by the United Singing Societies was given in Aurora Hall by Harmonien (Danish), Normaendenes Sangforening (Norwegian), and Teutonia Maennerchor (German).

Ortengren's Ladies' Quartet sang several numbers. Mr. Harry Dimond played the violin. All three choirs joined in singing "The Warriors," by N. W. Gade.
