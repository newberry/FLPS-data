p.2.col.4... The year 1908 shows a very good record for the Danish Socialist Society. The report shows: (a) Treasury balance of nearly $9,000.00. (b) 105 lodges. (c) a membership of 5203. (d) six new lodges during the year. (e) 633 new members. The order reports a general fund of nearly $36,000.00. During the year $8,763.18 has been expended in sick benefits.
