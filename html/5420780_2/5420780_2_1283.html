On January 10, 1914, the Danish-American Athletic Club was started, with Christian Christensen as president, Steen Rasmussen, secretary, John Steen, vice-president, and Rudy Hansen, treasurer. At present the society has 130 members. The dues are twenty-five cents per month for active members and three dollars per year for passive members. The motto of the society is "Mens sana in corpore sano".
