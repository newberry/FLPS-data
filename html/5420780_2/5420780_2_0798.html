The Scandinavian music festival will take place May 26 and 27 under the auspices of the newly organized Scandinavian Festival Association. Of this society John R. Lindgren is president, F. H. Gade and George Beck are vice-presidents, C. R. Chindblom is secretary, and Theodore Frooman is treasurer.

The aim of the Association is to further the mutual understanding between the three Scandinavian nations and to give all Scandinavians in America and all Americans a chance to hear and enjoy the masterpieces of Scandinavian composers. A guarantee fund has been raised from well-to-do Scandinavians. The sum is at present $3,000. The concert will be given in Orchestra Hall.
