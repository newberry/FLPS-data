p.4... Tonight the Singing Society "Harmonien" is giving its 25th anniversary jubilee-concert in Association Auditorium, Y.M.C.A. Building, La Salle St., near Madison.

Besides the choir the program offers three of our best known performers in the fieled of music and song: Miss Clara Jensen, soprano, Miss Nathalie Jensen, violinist, and the organist,Geo. Dupont-Hansen, who among other renditions will play a jubilee march, composed for the occasion.

Tonight's concert is a great event.
