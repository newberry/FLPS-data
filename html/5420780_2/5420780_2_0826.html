A new Danish singing society was formed March 1 at Lagoni's Hall by members of the Danish Brotherhood. Lodges 17 and 18 of the West Side, and Lodge 58 of Jefferson, were represented. The society has forty-nine charter members, active and passive.

The following board was elected: L. B. Larsen, president; Carl Hansen, secretary; Wm. Petersen, treasurer. A committee was also elected to work out the laws of the society. Violinist R. Knudsen Eriksen is candidate for the position of director of the new singing society. The society has not name as yet.
