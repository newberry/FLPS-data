The Ladies Song Club of Illinois, attracted a large and representative audience at its concert last Saturday night. The concert itself, as well as the dance following it, was a great success. The program was selected with good taste and a sense for lively variety, and the assistance could not have been better chosen.

The star of the evening was, of course, Mme. Ragna Linne, who was enthusiastically applauded.

The concert certainly was a credit to the club, which last Tuesday celebrated the second anniversary of its founding.
