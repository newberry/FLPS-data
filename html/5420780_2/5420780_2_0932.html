On September 15 and 16, 1928, the Associated Danish Singers of America held their fourth song festival in Wicker Park Hall, Chicago. The following Danish choruses participated: Bel Canto, of Detroit, Michigan, directed by Holger Jensen; Brage, of Kenosha, Wisconsin, directed by Otto Nielsen; Harmonien, of Chicago, directed by Joel Mossberg [Swede]; and Dansk Arbejder Sangforening (Danish Workingmen's Singing Society), of Chicago, directed by Mr. Holger Jensen. The orchestra was that of Ole Nielsen. Knute Hansen, of Chicago, played the piano.
