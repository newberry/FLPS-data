In 1920 the Dansk Arbejder Sangforening (Danish Workingmen's Singing Society) bought its own building, paying $4,000 for it. The building is located at 1652 North Rockwell Street. During the month of May thirty new members joined the Singing Society. For this reason the organization felt that it could risk the making of such an expenditure.

The result is that Harmonien is thinking of buying the hall which it now rents.
