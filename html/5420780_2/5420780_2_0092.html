The Central Committee on the South Side invited all Danes to a meeting in Wallhalla Hall on March 11 to protest against the English attack on the little Boer nation.

Revyen, Mar. 17, 1900.

The meeting was held and many people did attend. The president of the Central Committee, Mr. L. O. Larsen, opened the meeting. Mr. A. J. Nielsen, who was elected chairman, said that 250 Danes are fighting under the Boer flag. Representative Robert Redfield, Mrs. Catherine Toft, and James Skallerup also spoke. The last speaker wanted President McKinley to intervene in behalf of the little Boer nation, but the meeting felt that the American president could and would do nothing.
