The Danish author, L. C. Nielsen, is coming to America for a lecture tour, which will extend from New York to the West Coast.

He will arrive in Chicago on Feb. 10, and will speak in the Howard Theatre, on Van Buren Street, on the topic "Developments in Denmark during the last 50 years." The talk will be accompanied by lantern slides.

There will also be musical entertainment by the best local talent, and Chicago Danes may look forward to an interesting and pleasant evening.
