The Skandinaviske Socialdemokratiske Sygeforening (The Scandinavian Social-Democratic Sick Benefit Society) had its semiannual meeting June 27. Lauritz Olesen was elected president in place of John Schmidt. W. Lucius was elected secretary and L. N. Schmidt, treasurer.

The Society now has four hundred fifty-seven members. The sick benefit of six dollars per week has cost each member $3.25 during the past year.

Mr. Lauritz Olesen and Ferdinand Andersen are charter members of the Society.
