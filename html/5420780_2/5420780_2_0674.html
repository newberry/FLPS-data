Trinitatis has two choirs, a Danish and an English choir. Both meet every Friday evening for practice. The two choirs have about forty voices in all.

The choir leader is Mr. George Dupont-Hansen, who also has been the organist of the church for forty years. He is a fine musician. He was made totally blind by a snowball thrown into his face when he was a little boy. He has been decorated by the King of Denmark with the cross of Ridder af Dannebrog.
