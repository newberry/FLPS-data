The Danish Hospital, 752 Cortland Street, will be opened officially tonight. Mr. M. C. Sorensen is the superintendent of the hospital, and the chief surgeon is Dr. Theodore Nielsen, who has as associates Dr. Charles Adams and Bayard Holmes. Dr. Christian Fenger is also expected to join the staff when he returns from Denmark.

Unlike most hospitals in Chicago, the Danish Hospital is a private enterprise, and is not connected with any denomination.
