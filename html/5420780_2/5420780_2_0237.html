Jens P. Christensen is one of Chicago's leading builders. He has built, to mention a few: Hollenback sausage factory, warehouses for Werner Brothers, ice plant for Haegele Inc Company; Peninsula Bank, Ishpeming; Cragin State Bank, State Bank of Hammond, Exchange Bank, Arcade Building, Racine; Hollywood Theater, Chicago and Austin Boulevard Christian Church.

Proper building methods have been his life study.
