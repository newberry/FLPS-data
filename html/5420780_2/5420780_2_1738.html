The Society for the Danish Old Peoples' Home had its meeting in Dania on April 18. John W. Hertz was again the chairman for the evening.

It was decided that anyone can become a life member by paying $100 once for all. It was also resolved to have a quarterly meeting the second Wednesday of January, April, July and October at 8 P.M.

Only 2 paragraphs of the 15 that make up the by-laws were thus dealt with. In order to speed up the business a committee was appointed, consisting of the following: Mrs. Toft, Dr. Henius, P. J Noer, D. M. Frederiksen, Mrs. A. Christensen, Wald. Bauer, Chas Wilde, and Chas: Ryberg.

(John W. Hertz is a brother to the well known Danish politician Henry L Hertz) J.E.
