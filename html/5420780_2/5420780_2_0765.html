The three Danish singing societies, the Harmony Singing Society, the Danish Workers' Singing Society [now the Danish Glee Club], and the Magneten Singing Society, gave a benefit concert in Schoenhofen Hall last night. The proceeds were at once turned over to the Danish Aid and Relief Society. The Danish Workers Chorus was directed by the Norwegian, Rolf Hammer; Magneten, by the Swede, E. G. Berggren; and Harmony, by the Swede, Joel Mossberg.
