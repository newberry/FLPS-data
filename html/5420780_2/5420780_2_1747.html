Since the so-called mass meeting in Scandia Hall January 25, the paper has received so many proposals as to how the Home should be run that it is almost impossible to print them all.

A committee to submit plans for the Old People's Home has been appointed. This committee consists of the following members: Mrs. C. Toft, Mrs. Wm. Jensen, Mrs. A. C. Christensen, Dr. Max Henius, Peter J. Noer, Fritz Frantzen, L. M. Hoffenblad, Waldemar Bauer, and H. W. Rocatis.
