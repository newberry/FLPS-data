An entertainment was given in Aurora Hall for the benefit of the Danish hospital. [George] Dupont-Hansen gave a few excellent piano solos. Mr. Rubardt, a talented baritone, sang the new song, "The Day is Over".

Mr. Orckenholt made a speech in which he explained that for fifty cents a month one is entitled to ten weeks' hospital care, and for twenty-five cents a month, five weeks' free care.
