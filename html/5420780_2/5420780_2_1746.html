At the meeting in Lagoni Hall as to the future of the Danish Old People's Home the following recommendations were made:

1. That the committee on laws and the building committee be dismissed.

2. That all members have the right to vote from the day they join the society.

3. That a committee on law consisting of five women and four men work out a proposal in three days; that this be mailed to all members, and that a new meeting be called April 10.

4. That all meetings must be held in the evenings so that all may have a chance to attend.
