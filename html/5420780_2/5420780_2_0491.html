Opera Singer Poul Bay has obtained a position as teacher in music at the Bush Conservatory, a position previously held by the late Clark. Poul Bay will begin his duties at the Conservatory about September 14, and will thus become a member of a staff of teachers regarded as one of the very best in the country.

Most Danes have had a chance to hear Poul Bay's magnificent baritone and when he sings for us again in Kimball Hall on October 22, accompanied by Edgar Nielsen, he can expect a great audience.
