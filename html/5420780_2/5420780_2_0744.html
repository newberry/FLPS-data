The Harmonien Singing Society gave its first spring concert last Sunday.

The program was as follows:

1. Orchestra
2. a) "Der er et Yndigt Land" (There Is a Beautiful Land) Kroyer
b) "Bon for Danmark (Prayer for Denmark) Horneman Harmonien
3. a) Solo, Serenade Svendsen
b) Solo, "Maisang" Lembeke Holger Petersen
4. "Vocal Combaty Dudley Buck Harmonien
5. Orchestra
6. a) Solo, "Natten" (Night) Elberg Rudolph Brechel
b) Solo, "Violen" (The Viol) Holger Petersen
7. Duet, "In This Solemn House" Verdi Joel Mossberg and Holger Petersen
8. "Svenska Fosterjorden" Joel Mossberg and Harmonien
9. "Pirates' Chorus" from "Ernani" Verdi Harmonien and Orchestra
