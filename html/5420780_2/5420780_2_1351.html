The Danish Sharpshooters' Hall, 787-789 West North Avenue, was opened and dedicated May 9 with a great festival. This hall is located in the city's largest Scandinavian settlement.

On the curtain in front of the stage was painted a picture of Kronborg Castle, which is the first sight that greets any Dane who goes by boat from America to Denmark and sails down the sound to Copenhagen. Kronborg Castle is also well known from Shakespeare's play, "Hamlet".
