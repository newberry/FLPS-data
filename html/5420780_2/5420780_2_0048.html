The Defense Squad now includes 721 names that have been called to the colors. To date the D. B. S. has only lost one of those who are fighting for their country, namely, H. P. Anderson, from Lodge No. 70. The deceased was gunner's mate in the Navy. He was 25 years old, and was born in Grafton, Michigan. He was insured for $1,000 in the Lodge, with his parents as beneficiaries.

He lost his life on the United States destroyer "Jacob Jones" which sank recently.
