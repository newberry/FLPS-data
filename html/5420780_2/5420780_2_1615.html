The new Danish Children's Home will be built on the corner of Evergreen and Spaulding Avenues, where the committee has bought three lots with a frontage of one hundred feet on Evergreen Avenue. The price paid was $3,000. Architect Charles F. Sorensen estimates that the cost of the new building will be $11,000. The members of the building committee are S. J. Black, S. N. Nielsen, and C. H. C. Nielsen.
