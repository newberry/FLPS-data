It is with the greatest pleasure, that I take this opportunity of thanking you for your donation of $531.83. Your donations of last year amounted to practically one-third of the amount used by the committee during the year, which is a wonderful credit to your organization.

The needy, who have benefited by your kindness, are many, and by them surely the appreciation is greatest.

Allow me to extend all good wishes for prosperity to your society and thank you again.

Sincerely

The Danish Relief Fund Committee,

A. C. Nelson.
