The Board of Trustees of the American-Scandinavian Foundation held a meeting in New York City and passed the following resolution:

"Whereas a petition has been presented to the Trustees of the Foundation requesting that a stipend for study in Denmark be granted to Anton Kvist, a resident of Chicago, but of Danish birth, and,

"Whereas the Chicago Advisory Committee has approved this petition, providing only that rules governing Fellowships will not be violated, be it

"Resolved that the petition be referred to the Scholarship Committee with the recommendation that if any stipend be granted in addition to the five required by the terms of the Denmark-American Exchange, it shall be granted to Anton Kvist."
