"Kolorite" is a Danish product used to dye curtains and similar materials. It is imported by Oscar P. Rubardt, sole distributor, who has managed to place "Kolorite" in such stores as and Marshall Field's, where it is a big success. At the present, Mr. Rubardt is negotiating with a number of drug stores, drygoods stores, and paint stores, Anticipating a tremendous expansion of the market, he has prepared thousands of small display outfits, on which the effects of "Kolorite" tablets are attractively illustrated.
