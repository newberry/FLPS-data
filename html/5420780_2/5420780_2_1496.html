p.2.The.Sca dinavian Social Democratic Sick Benefit Society has elected new board of directors.

The society, which has a membership of some five hundred paid out $2,173.00 in sick benefits last year, and $370 for propaganda purposes.

The membership fee is $6.00 plus 50 cents for the funeral fund. The allowance for funerals has been raised from $50 to $100.

The society's building fund has now reached $865.32. The net profit of the picnic at Hoerdt's Grove, July 30, is to be added to this fund.
