The literary meeting of the Trinitatis Lutheran Church, held last Friday, drew a large audience in spite of the inclement weather. Reverend Rordam opened the meeting with a lecture on the poet Christian Richardt, after which Mrs. Rordam read several of Richardt's poems, and Mr. Johannes Hendriksen sang two of his songs.

These literary meetings seem to have won a hearty response from the public, which is informed in advance regarding the authors to be discussed.

At the coming literary meeting in April, the works of H. C. Hostrup will be discussed, and one act of his play, "Gjenboerne" (The People Across the Street), will be presented.
