The society Walhalla is sending out invitations for a meeting next Sunday in order to find out if there is sufficient interest and talent among South Side Danes to organize a singing society in that part of the city. State Senator Niels Juul, ex-president of the United Scandinavian Singing Societies, now an honorary member of Harmonien, and Otto Damm, one of the founders of the latter Society, will speak.

Go to it, singers!
