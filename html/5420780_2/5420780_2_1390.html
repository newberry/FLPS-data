The festival for Hans Christian Andersen will start at Lincoln Park at 3 P.M. Vice-Consul C. M. Koedt will make the introductory speech. Colonel Clark E. Carr will speak for Denmark, and Consul A. Petersen will present the statue to the Park Board. The Reverend Adam Dan will speak for the great poet. Rose Fugl will recite a poem written by him and Judge Gibbons will talk about Hans Christian Andersen's importance. The Hejmdal singing society will sing several songs.
