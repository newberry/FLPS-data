A woman suffrage meeting will be held tomorrow afternoon at 3 o'clock in the Humboldt Park parish house for the purpose of urging women, regardless of party affiliations, to exercise their newly acquired right to vote, and remind them that in order to qualify as voters, they must register within the given time limit.

The meeting will be addressed in English and Danish by Mrs. Lycinka Hansen; in Norwegian by Dr. Ingeborg Rasmussen; and in Swedish by Miss Rudd.
