Seven famous Scandinavians have been honored with a statue in America. Leif Erikson has two statues, one in Boston and one in Milwaukee. The following have one apiece: Linne and Hans Christian Andersen, both in Chicago; Ole Bull, in Minneapolis; John Ericson and Thorvaldsen, both in New York; Bellmann, in Brooklyn.

[Translator's note: Hans Christian Andersen, poet, and Thorvaldsen, sculptor, are Danes. Linne', scientist, John Ericson, engineer, Bellmann, poet, are Swedes. Leif Erikson, discoverer, and Ole Bull, violinist, are Norwegians].
