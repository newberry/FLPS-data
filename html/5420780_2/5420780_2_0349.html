The Park Theater Building at 3234 W. North Avenue is being remodeled to accommodate the Second Humboldt State Bank, which will have its offices in it. The new location will give the Danes an opportunity to make bank transactions in their neighborhood. The Humboldt State Bank, North and Fairfield Avenues, is the parent bank of the Second Humboldt.

Among the bank's officers and directors are, Jacob Nielsen, Oliver M. Ulvestad, and Soren T. Corydon.
