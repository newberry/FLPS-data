Musical director Ole Nielsen's orchestra, twenty men strong, will give a concert followed by a ball in Walhalla Hall tomorrow at 6.30 P.M.

This is an annual affair which has always been well worth attending, and for which Mr. Nielsen is making the most careful and exacting preparations.

As usual, several able soloists are on the program, among them the beloved singer Alb Hesselholdt, from Harmoniens's Choir.

"King" Jensen, Walhalla's popular host, will be in charge of the culinary department.
