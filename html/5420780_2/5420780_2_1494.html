p. 2.. At its last meeting, on Sunday, the Society Dania resolved to ask its architect to emphasize Danish style and building methods, in the construction of the society's new building, Dania Hall. Mr. Nielsen, the architect, informed the meeting that he planned to get in touch with two of the best known members of the Copenhagen Society of Architects, and possibly also with Professor Nyrop, in regard to the latest Danish ideas in building design.
