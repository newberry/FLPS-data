The Society Walhalla has published a financial statement for the year ending Dec. 1, 1912.

According to it, the gross income of the Hall was $3,840.95, while the operating expenses amounted to $3,567, including the redemption of bonds to the amount of $670. The building with grounds and furnishings is valued at $35,000 and the bonded debt is $8,500.

The society's assets, including sick benefit, insurance funds, and reserves amount to $30,015.36, or $58.51 per member, the membership, as per Dec. 1, 1912, being 513. During the year $2,460 was paid out in sick benefits and $4,100 in insurance.
