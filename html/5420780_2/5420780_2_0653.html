p.4.....In January, February, March and April, 1912, Mr. Blangsted will, here in Chicago, present six performances of plays by modern Danish authors, one of whom is Lieutenant Stellan Rye, who at present is under arrest, and whose play "The Faces of the Hie" has previously been introduced to Chicago by Oda.

Mr. Blangsted is considering Mr. Stellan Rye as scenic instructor, as soon as he is released.
