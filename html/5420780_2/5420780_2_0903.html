p. 2 col. 2....Henrik Ibsen's new play "Naar vi dode vaagner" was played at Scandia Hall, Sunday, Feb. 11, Practically all seats were taken.

The play was fine from the beginning to the end; this was a great surprise after having been "used to sippy training, sloven instruction and mediocre plays outside of the annual review and the lighter vaudevilles."

N. Henriksen, the sculptor, painted some fine decorations. Viking Marine Band played under the direction of Math. Petersen.

The play had a fine critique in the Chicago Tribune.
