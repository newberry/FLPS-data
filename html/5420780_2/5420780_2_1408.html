The Hans Christian Anderson Monument Association gave a concert in Central Music Hall last evening, to raise funds toward a $5,000 monument in Chicago to the Danish author. The concert was under the direction of August Hyllested; Carter H. Harrison made an address.

Those who took part in the musical program were: Clarence Eddy, August Hyllestad, Bicknell Young, Frederic Hess, and Elena Varesi Boccabadatio.
