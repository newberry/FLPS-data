The Society Walhalla has a membership of 514, as of Dec. 1, 1913, during which year it disbursed in sick benefits $1,366 and for life insurance payments and funeral aid $4,406.90.

The society's building, including furnishings and the land on which it stands, is valued at $35,000; the bonded debt is $8,040, and with its cash reserve Walhalla's net assets amount to $29,439.51, or $57.27 per member.
