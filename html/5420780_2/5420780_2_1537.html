The Danish Brotherhood of America will celebrate its twenty-fifth anniversary soon. All Danish Brotherhood lodges in Chicago will celebrate on New Year's Day with a big banquet at the 2nd Regiment Armory.

About eight hundred dinner guests are expected. The Supreme President, Mr. Vogt, of Davenport, Iowa, will be present.

A prologue has been written by Blickenberg-Jensen. The official speakers will be Martin Andersen, A. M. Petersen, and Carl Hansen.

A special ninety-six-page program has been printed, which contains many advertisements and a little about the history of the Danish Brotherhood in Chicago.
