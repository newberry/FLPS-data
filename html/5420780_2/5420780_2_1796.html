Emil Marcussen, Colonel in Salvation Army and Social Secretary at the Main Office in Chicago. He was born at Logumkloster, Denmark, August 1st, 1858. Frequented Melchior's School, Copenhagen. Took his Praeliminaereksamen (somewhat like graduating from high-school). Went to sea for 8 years. Joined Salvation Army in San Francisco in 1888. Started social work for Salvation Army in Denmark in 1890. Returned to America in 1903.

(Emil Marcussen was sent to Denmark in 1937 to represent the Salvation Army in America at the 50th year jubilee of the Army in Denmark)
