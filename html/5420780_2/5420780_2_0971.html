Viggo A. Danielsen and wife celebrated their silver wedding December 25. He has been a faithful member of Dania, De Danske Vaabenbredre, Harmonien and the Danish Brotherhood. The last 12 years he has been the Supreme Secretary of the latter society, and he had his part in the great success this organization is today.

Harmonien sang for them early in the morning. Committees came with gifts from the following lodges in Chicago: No. 17, 18, 35, 58, and 107; also from Danish Sisterhood No. 4.

Their only son, Axel died last summer. Their only daughter, Mrs. Olawes, just had a son, which made the grandparents feel very proud.
