Magneten ushered in the concert and Ball season last Saturday in Wicker Park Hall, with a program of singing and other entertainment which was greatly enjoyed by the hundreds of Danes which attented the affair.

We wish to express our admiration for the many excellent voices which the singing society now possesses, and for the perfect technique with which the forty men chorus executes its numbers.

The four clowns were really funn, and Mrs, Agnes Mathiesen's dance orchestra did a fine job.
