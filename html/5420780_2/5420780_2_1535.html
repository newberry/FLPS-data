Whereas the Scandinavian Socialists' Sick and Benefit Society has among its members one who has been nominated by the Socialist Party to represent the 28th Ward in the City Council of Chicago.

Be it therefore resolved: That we are honored by having so highly esteemed a member as Mr. Adolph Christensen in our midst, and that we shall exert every effort to secure his election; be it also resolved that this resolution be sent to the Danish press, The Chicago Socialist and Mr. Christensen, also that it be spread upon the records of the meeting.

J. C. Piehl

Secretary.
