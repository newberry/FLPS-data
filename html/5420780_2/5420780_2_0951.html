p.1..............The president, Mr. Bauer, informs Revyen : Tonight at 7:30 o'clock parade on North Avenue with two bands. We have six trucks for the committee, the bands, the national dancers, the children's choir and for advertisements a minature Danish farm; Concert from 1:00 to 3:00 P.M. But singing by "Braga" from Kenosha will from the climax.

At 8:00 P.M. folk dances, very inspiring! The dancers directed by Mrs. Tousieng-Thomsen, musical accompaniment by Mrs. Agnes Mathiesen. The restaurant concession is this year being operated by the Danish Children's Home. The more we eat tomorrow, the more clothing there will be for the kids. As usual, the net profit of the festival goes to the Old People's Home and to the Children's Home.

We expect an attendance of 15,000!
