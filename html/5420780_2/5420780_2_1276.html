The Danish Sharpshooters had their annual "Master Practice." Many unusual scores were chalked up, and several championships were made.....

The president of the sharpshooters, John Pooder, was in unusual bad form, but the others were fair. Exceptional scores were made by Christ Hermansen, Jens Lundsberg, Theodore Frydenlund, Christ Nielsen, Jack Nielsen, Julius Sorensen, and Christ Christensen, which gave the club enough lead to be considered the best in the Central States.
