The Society Dania held its annual general membership meeting last Wednesday night, and practically all officers were reelected.

It was resolved to join the Society for the Danish Old People's Home, for which the annual membership fee is $25.00.

After the meeting, a buffet supper and punch were served.

The year 1913 was quite a good year for Dania. The rent of its hall brought a net income of $739.22. The members were assessed $3.00 each for the retirement of $8.00 worth of bonds, and a $500 note was paid off.

Its building, including furnishings, is valued at $37,004.87, and the society's net assets as of Jan. 1, 1914, amounts to 13,589.16.

During the past year the membership increased from 180 to 220.
