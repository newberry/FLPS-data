Mr. Viggo A. Danielsen, secretary in chief of the Danish Brotherhood in America, has submitted to the Skandinaven the following report concerning the activity of the society from July 1 to October 1 of the present year:

On October 1 the Brotherhood had 126 active lodges with a total of 6,873 members: a net increase in membership of 165 during the quarter. During this period there has been paid in insurance to relatives of deceased members a total of $5,750.30. Burial aid paid to members whose wives have passed away reached a total of $450. During the same period $3,673.02 was paid in sick benefits, and $803.98 in extra benefits to ailing members. Since the last report three new lodges have been organized....
