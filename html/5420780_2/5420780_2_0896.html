Scandia Hall Theatre will present the Danish play, "Svend, Knud og Valdemar," directed by Emanuel Johnson. A prominent Danish-American actor, Franklin R. Bendtsen,will play the leading part. He is a famous actor on the American stage and he has even played in Europe. At present he is playing at the Dearborn Theatre. He will play in Danish. He is born here in Chicago, and his parents are still very popular with the Danish colony of this town.
