In the same building as the former Milwaukee Avenue State Bank, which recently went into the hands of the receivers, a new Security Bank has opened up. James B. Forgan, of the First National Bank, is the president of the board of directors. E. A. Eriksen is the president of the bank itself, and J. C. Hansen the cashier. Among the stockholders are such well-known Danes as Contractor S. N. Nielsen, Martin Ackerman, and News Dealer P. Nielsen.
