The Society for a Danish Old People's Home had a meeting November 21. Fifty members were present. It was decided to appoint a committee to see Consul C. H. Hansen and to ask him to get together the most prominent Danes and form a society of men to help in this great cause.

The Society for a Danish Old People's Home in Chicago has a chance to buy a fine building erected for this very purpose by a rich Italian. It is located near Humboldt Park and near the Norwegian Tabitha Hospital, and can be secured for twelve thousand dollars.
