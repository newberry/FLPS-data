p.2.col.5.......The Danish Old Peoples Home Society reports a very successful year with an income of $5000.00, a property valuation of $31,500.00, sixteen new entrants and a present membership of three-hundred.

That the Norwood Park Home is well and favorably known is shown by the many inquiries and applications for entry from outside of Illinois. The society looks forward to increasing popularity and support in the years to come and this it justly deserves.
