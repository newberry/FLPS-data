p.4. col.3. The Danish Old Peoples Home Society is making great plans for the celebration of their 18th anniversary on March 10, at Wicker Park Hall. The society has enjoyed the whole-hearted support of Chicago's Danish colony and boast a pretentious institution valued at $30,000.00 in Norwood Park, Illinois.

An extensive program of music, singing, declamation and other events has been arranged, and the outlook for the largest and best celebration in the history of the society is very evident.

A bountiful supper will be served at 10:00 P.M., and Schmidt's popular orchestra has been secured for the dancing program of the evening.
