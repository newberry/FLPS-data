The Scandia Theater Company was incorporated by Halvor Jacobsen, 192 W. Indiana Street, W. Dahlbom, 12 Austin Avenue, and Carl Antonsen, 829 North Avenue.

One floor of Scandia Hall has been rebuilt with a stage and auditorium. The stock company will produce Shakespeare, Ibsen, Bjornson and a number of French, German and Danish plays.

At last nights premiere, every ticket was sold and hundreds of people were turned away. This enterprise is sure to be successful.
