The Humboldt Park Branch of the Norwegian Society for the Home for the Aged held its regular monthly meeting Monday evening at the home of Mrs. Hoeck, 565 North California Avenue....The report from the Home was read, showing that good conditions prevailed there. A new members was received into the Society. The members were reminded of the annual picnic on August 23, for which tickets were distributed for sale....

Several speeches were made and refreshments were served. The meeting was very pleasant.
