Trinity Branch of the Society for the Home of the Aged had its meeting Monday evening in the cozy home of Mr. and Mrs. Smeby, 526 N. Sacramento Ave. Reports were read and accepted. Membership fees totaling $46 were received. A vote of thanks was given Mrs. Sivertsen for her excellent work in connection with the recent picnic for the home.

A tasty luncheon was served by Mrs. Smeby, and everybody agreed that the meeting had been most interesting and pleasant.

The next meeting is to be held on Tuesday October 8, in the home of Mrs. Sivertsen, 740 N. Humboldt St.
