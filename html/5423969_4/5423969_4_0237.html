The Scandinavian Painter's Union, No. 194, gave $3,000.00 to the poor unfortunate people in San Francisco. The delegates J. J. Johnson, 177 West Huron Street, told one of Skandinaven's reporters that Union No. 194 has voted an assessment of $1.00 for each member, the money to be sent to San Francisco. Union No. 194 is the largest in the U.S.A., having eighteen hundred members all Scandinavian.
