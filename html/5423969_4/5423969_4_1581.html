The United Committee, consisting of two delegates from each of the lodges of Sons and Daughters of Norway have now finished arrangements for the great picnic at Joe's Park. Morton Grove, Illinois, on Sunday August 5. There will be lots of fun for grownups and for children. There will be athletic exhibitions of various sorts, also other kinds of entertainment, and there will be dancing.

Coffee, ice cream, and various other refreshments will be available at the grounds, but for substantial food it is well to bring the lunch basket.
