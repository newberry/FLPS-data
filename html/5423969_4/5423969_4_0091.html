The meeting for August was held at the home of Mr. and Mrs. B. P. Johnson, Austin, and well attended. The reports showed that the home is in desirable condition. Preliminary arrangements were made for the picnic to be held at the home on August 24.

A mixed quartette furnished music and singing; refreshment, consisting of coffee and cakes, was served. Eleven dollars and fifty cents was paid in fees, and Mrs. Stevens of Oak Park invited the branch to gather at the Stevens' home for the meeting to be held in September.
