The Norwegian National League held its annual meeting last evening. The president, George Kramer, gave their report for the past year.

Lasse Grundeland, Mrs. John Thye, and [gap] represented the League at the centennial festival in Christiania, Norway. The report of the treasurer showed the cash on hand, January 1, 1914, to be [gap]. The income from the Seventeenth of May festival was $3,302.75; other income for the year totaled $3,891.63. The year's expense amounted to $2,470.89; the cash on hand, January 1, 1915 was $3,250.99. The employment office had a deficit this year of $431.57.
