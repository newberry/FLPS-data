The first lodge of the Daughters of Norway has been organized here, and it will meet at 1640 Humboldt Boulevard. The new officers are: Caspara Saerhim, president; Alfhild Nicolaysen, vice-president; Anna Mathiesen, guard; Helga Kringleland, secretary; Carrie Johnson, financial secretary; Selma Owen, treasurer; Kagot Larsen, master of ceremonies; Anna Olsen, drill leader; Julia Johnson, assistant drill leader; Sigrid Hassel Kristofferson, inner guard; Ragna Marie Solum, outer guard; Else Brown, Olivia Mathiesen, and Mary Enger, trustees.
