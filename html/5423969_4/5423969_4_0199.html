The Norwegian National League employment office will open its doors tomorrow under the direction of Lars A. Bergan. The office address is 897 North Avenue. Everything looks very promising Manager Lars A. Bergen has been very busy trying to make connections with factories, wholesale houses, farmers, etc. All have promised to send to him for help. When a client receives work, he must pay from $1.00 to $5.00 according to the type and salary of work.
