Normania Tent has just sent the following resolution to the South Park Board:

"Commissioners of the South Park Board.

Chicago, Illinois

"Gentlemen:

"We have followed with interest the campaign inaugurated by the Norwegian National League of Chicago aiming toward paving the new outer drive named "Leif Erikson Drive".

2

The recent action of your board, granting the request and thus conspiciously honoring the memory of the early discoverer of America, was a step in the direction of establishing in the minds of all a memory of the country's early history that has gladdened the hearts of multitudes of people, and earned your board lasting gratitude.

"Sincerely yours,

"Normania Tent No. 264,

The Maccabees.

"A. A. Scovill, commander,

"John Malmstrom, Record Keeper."
