The Wicker Park Branch of the Norwegian Society for the Home for the Aged held a monthly meeting at the home of Mrs. Henry Olsen, 517 N. Hoyne Avenue. The meeting was well attended and $46 was received as annual fees.

Arrangements for the twelfth annual bazaar were discussed. The bazaar is to be held in Schoenhofen's Hall, from Monday, Oct. 14, till Saturday, Oct 19.

After the meeting, Mrs. Olsen served coffee and cake.
