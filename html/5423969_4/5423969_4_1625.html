The Norwegian National Committee held its annual meeting at Kezdie Hall, Kedzie and North Avenue, last Monday evening.

The executive committee gave its report, which was followed by the treasurer's report.

The treasurer's report was as follows:

Income $2,493.82
Expenses 994.00
