It was clearly shown on Monday evening that the interest in the Norwegian Home for the Aged is not abating. The attendance was very good and the proceeds amounted to more than $172, nearly $80 more than the proceeds from the first evening of the bazaar last year, and $100 more than the receipts from the first evening of the Society's first bazaar (1896). A mass of articles of all kinds were attractively arranged about the hall that is so convenient for affairs of this kind....
