The Norwegian Society for the Home for the Aged opened its annual bazaar at Schoenhofen Hall last Monday. Norwegian and American a flags were placed on the platform and on a number of beautifully decorated sales booths, where the articles to be sold were exhibited.

Humboldt Park Branch was in charge of the bazaar on Monday evening.

The following branches are in charge of the bazaar:

Today (Tuesday), Bethlehem Branch; Wednesday, Trinity Branch; Thursday, Concordia Branch; Friday, Wicker Park Branch; Saturday.... Young Ladies' Aid assisted by the other women....
