The Norwegian-American Steamship Company recently donated $3,000 to be divided among the several Norwegian charitable institutions in the Northwest. The following Chicago institutions received a part:

Norwegian Old People's Home, Norwood Park $300
Norwegian Children's Home, Edison Park 150
Norwegian-American Hospital 150
Norwegian Lutheran Deaconess Home and Hospital 150
Norwegian Singers' League of America 150
Receiving and Home Finding Society for Children 60
