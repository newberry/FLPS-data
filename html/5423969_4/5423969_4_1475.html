Mr. Christian Olsen, president of the third annual Scandinavian song festival to be held at Riverview Park has received a letter from Dr. N. Remmen in which the latter endorses the idea of Scandinavian co-operation, and heartly recommends the festival.

"During the Scandinavian exposition at the Chicago Art Institute," Dr. Remmen writes, "the place was crowded from morning until night, proof of the appreciation of our art by the public."

"Let us awaken the public to our music and also show them that we Scandinavians are able to work unitedly for a good cause. Was it ever more necessary to do this than at present?"
