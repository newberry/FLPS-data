Nearly a thousand men and women attended the annual meeting of the Norwegian National Committee held at Kedzie Hall, 1621 North Kedzie Avenue. The meeting was called to order by the president, Carl F. Backer. After the reading of the minutes, the following committees, to serve for the entire year, were constituted: the work committee, the auditing committee, the credentials committee, and the Seventeenth of May committee. Carl J. Backer was re-elected president; O. C. Wold, first vice-president; Mrs. Marie Nielsen, second vice-president; Otto Berg, third vice-president; Hilmar J. Behrentz, secretary; J. Malmstrom, corresponding secretary; C. M. Berg, treasurer.
