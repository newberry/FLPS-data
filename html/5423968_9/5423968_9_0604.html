The United States celebrates today the 174th birthday of The Father of our Country, liberator, and first President. Courts, banks, public and parochial schools are closed. There are many banquets and programs being given in his honor.

Washington and his great achievements are popular subjects of discussion.

Poles are taking part in celebrating the birthday of the organizer of this Republic. They take pride in the knowledge that by the side of Washington, at critical times, stood the Poles Thaddeus Kosciusko and Casimir Pulaski who fought staunchly for freedom.
