Exercises to commemorate the November Insurrection were held on November 26 at St. John's parish and were preceded by vesper services at 2:30 P. M. After vespers, the societies and general public went to the parish hall, where the program was given.

Mr. B. F. Kowalski, chairman of the program committee, addressed the audience, asking the people to keep quiet during the program. He then called upon Reverend L. Grudzinski to open the exercises. Reverend Grudzinski, after opening the exercises, called upon Mr. A. Lipinski to act as master of ceremonies. [Translator's note: A detailed account of the program now follows.]
