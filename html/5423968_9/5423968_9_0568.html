Yesterday the eighty-first anniversary of the November Insurrection was commemorated at Mr. Narkiewicz's hall, corner Wolfram Street and Central Park Avenue.

Mr. T. Kozowicz opened the exercises with a few apt words and called upon Mr. F. Krocker and Aniela Moszczynska to act as master of ceremonies and secretary respectively.
