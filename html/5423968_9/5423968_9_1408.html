(We have received the following correspondence with a request that it be published in the Dziennik Chicagoski.)

We respectfully wish to announce, that the Krakus Society of St. Florian of St. Adalbert's Parish in Chicago decided at its last meeting, held January 19, to support the proposed Polish Catholic Congress in America with all its strength and to take an active part in it.

Maryan Plotkowski, recording secretary, 874 West 19th Street.
