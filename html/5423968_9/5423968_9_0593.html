This year, we have an opportunity to demonstrate our loyalty to this Great Country by taking part in the Independence Day parade. It is a well-known fact that Poles have contributed a great deal toward the cause of independence.

To celebrate this greatest day in the history of our Country, we Poles should participate in the grand parade on Tuesday,July 4th. The Polish division will meet at Wabash Ave. and 29th Street, on the East side, at 9:30 A.M.

Dr. W. A. Kuflewski

Parade Committee.
