The Polish Patriotic Organization of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish held a special meeting yesterday.

As the president of the society was absent, Jacob Mucha was made chairman of the meeting, and Thomas Krolik acted as secretary.

On a motion made by Reverend Vincent Barzynski, supported by [?] Ogorek, the members decided to dissolve their society and join the Polish League as the "First Agency of the Polish League", under the patronage of the Queen of the Polish Crown.

Furthermore, a committee was chosen to prepare a constitution for the new society. The following were appointed in the committee: Reverend V. Barzynski, the pastor, B. Clarkowski and Thomas Krolik.

Finally, a temporary set of officers of the Agency was chosen as follows:

Jacob Mucha, president; B. Klarkowski, secretary; Theodore Ostrowski, treasurer.

Thus, the League has gained already one agency with almost three hundred members.
