"The August Kordecki Society, under the protection of the Holy Mary of Czestochowa of the St. Adalbert's Parish in Chicago, decided, at its meeting held last Sunday, March 1, to support the projected Polish Catholic congress with all its strength.

"Anthony Janka, recording secretary."

"The St. Hyacinth Society, affiliated with the church of the same name in Avondale (Chicago), decided at its last meeting to take an active part in the projected Polish Catholic congress. The undersigned was selected to act as delegate.

"Joseph Grabowiecki."
