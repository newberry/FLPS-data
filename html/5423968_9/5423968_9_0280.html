A new resolution was adopted yesterday by the Polish Patriotic Organization at a meeting held at the Society's headquarters. It was decided that open-house meetings should be held every three months, with the purpose of spreading and fostering Polish patriotism. Guest speakers will be invited, among them, representative individuals and outstanding Polish priests. Readings will also be given. A committee was chosen to arrange these public meetings. Announcements will be made later. Everyone is invited to attend these quarterly sessions.
