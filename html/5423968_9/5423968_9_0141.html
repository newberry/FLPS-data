Yesterday, March 11, the Poles organized the Wells School Polish Society, the object of which is to promote education, spread patriotism, and furnish wholesome entertainment. The next meeting will be held on March 16, 9 P. M., at the Society's headquarters, 779 Milwaukee Avenue. Teachers, students, and women belonging to the Society are cordially invited to attend so that a group photograph of all the members may be taken. This photograph will be sent to the Lwow Fair in one of the albums specially prepared for this purpose.

John Jasinski, secretary.
