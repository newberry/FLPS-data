Commemoration exercises of the November Insurrection (Rising against Russia in November of 1830) were held on December 11 by the Poles of Town of Lake at the Columbia Hall. Reverend Zalewski, the local pastor, officially opened the exercises. Besides him, the following made speeches: Messrs. Szularecki, H. Ziolkowski, and S. Schmitt. Songs and recitations were interspersed throughout the program. At the close a collection for the Kosciuszko Monument Fund was made; $26.41 was collected. On the whole the affair was a success.
