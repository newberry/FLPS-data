On the occasion ot the 18th Convention of the Polish Singer's Alliance, a concert was arranged with the following program:

1- "Reveille of Nation," Orchestra

2- "Mazur," from the opera "Halka," United choir, with orchestra accompaniment.

3- "Miners' Serenade," P. Moos.-by the Choir Monwszko, from Pittsburgh.

4- Aria from the opera "Halka," Miss S. Jozefowicz.

5- "From Mansion to Mansion," Polish folk song by M. Segino. and United Choirs from Grand Rapids.

6- "The Watermen."A. Minhejmer United Women's choirs.

7- "Where is My Land," Wagner, by the Quartet Polonia.

8- "Polonaise, "F. Chopin, United Mixed choirs.

9- "Queen of Spades," Overture, Orchestra.

10- "Tales of Weldysz," humoresque, United Male choirs.

11- "Dumka," Kratzer Solo by S. Kuzniewicz.

2

12- "Folks song" M. Fibak, Harmony choir, of Cleveland.

13- "Polonaise," Krupinski, United choirs

14- "On Sunday After Vespers," from the opera "Halka" choir Filharmonia, of Chicago.

15- "Mazur", Maszynski, United Mixed choirs

The first prize was awarded to Monwszko choir, of Pittsburgh; the second to the Harmony choir of Cleveland.
