The Kosciuszko organization has the honor of inviting all organizations of the P. N.A. to a yearly celebration of the birthday of our here Tad. Kosciuszko, which will take place Sunday, February 14, at 3 in the afternoon, in the school hall of Holy Trinity Church.

The Kosciuszko organization has celebrated this event for a great many years, as it also celebrates it now. We invite you then to celebrate this occasion in honor to this brave and good son of our Fatherland.

Committee.
