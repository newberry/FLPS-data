At a meeting of the administrative body of the Polish Patriotic Organization yesterday at the St. Stanislaus Kostka School hall, the plans for the first January Insurrection [Rising of the Poles Against Russia in 1863] program by the club were adopted. The commemoration exercises will be held on January 21.

The program will be as follows:

1. Official opening of program.

2. Songs.

3. Historical speech.

4. Songs.

5. Recitation.

6. Drills by the Sokols.

7. Speech.

\2

8. Songs.

9. Declamation.

10. Drills by the Sokols.

11. Reading by Reverend Vincent Barzynski.

12. Polish National Song: "God Save Poland."

Further details will be published later.
