The opening of the convention of The Polish National Alliance took place yesterday at 102 West Division Street. About 200 delegates attended the meeting. The principal subject under discussion will be, the proposed change of the constitution, by which the life-insurance co-operative plan, shall be admitted into The Alliance's program. The second important discussion will be that, of admitting women to become members of The Alliance, sharing the rights and privileges equally, with the male members of the society.
