A patriotic exercise commemorating the Constitution of the Third of May was held Sunday, May 26, at Bridgeport. L. Czeslawski presided as chairman. The speakers were M. Mezydlo and M. Wleklinski, who also delivered a lecture on Thaddeus Kosciusko.

A collection for the Polish National Fund of Rapperschwil [a city in Switzerland] netted $9.28. [Translator's note: This fund was started by the Polish National Alliance about 1889.]
