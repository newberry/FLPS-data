At its last meeting, held in the Good Templars' building on Newpost Avenue, the Swedish Study League was addressed by Dr. C. G. Wallenius, who lectured on "The History of the Political Parties in the United States". The house was well filled by an appreciative audience, which followed the instructive talk with great interest. The lecture will be continued at the next meeting. which will be held October 24 in the same place.The Northland Trio entertained with song and music.
