The Swedish Vasa Society recently held its seventeenth annual meeting, at which time installation of new officers took place and an appropriate program was given.

The reports for the fiscal year just ended shows that the Society is steadily progressing and that is good work among the Swedish people of Chicago is markedly increasing. During the past year a sum of $1,436 in sick benefits has been paid out to fifty-six members; two deaths among the members had occurred during the year, making the total present membership 172. The income during the year was $2,742.87 and the disbursements $2,172.70. The total assets of the Society Vasa, in cash and first-rate securities amount to $3,570.07.
