Dr. John A. Enander, Nestor of Swedish-American newspapermen in Chicago, has been honored by the Swedish Academy of Stockholm with a scholarship of five hundred kroner [Translator's note: This is approximately $133.] in recognition of his literary activities in the United States.

Dr. Enander, who was born in Vastergotland, Sweden in 1842, came to America in 1869 and has worked for Swedish-American newspapers ever since.
