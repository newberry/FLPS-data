The regular monthly meeting of the Swedish National Association was held May 6 in its new, extensive place at 140 North Dearborn Street. The following directors were elected: H. S. Henschen, C. S. Peterson, Fritz Schoultz, Nils Hokanson, F. A. Larson, Gust. Hallbom, C. Ellison, Charles J. Erickson, and Frank Gustafson.

The association decided to help several persons in need, and to make arrangements for its annual midsummer festival to be held at Elliott's Park, July 22. A committee was selected.
