Odin Lodge No. 8, I. O. V. installed its directors last Friday, July 11. They are: And. Hemwall, chairman; Emil Pantzer, vice-chairman; and J. P. Carlson, recording secretary. The ceremony was conducted by the Grand Lodge deputies, Axel Holm and Aug. Person. Two new banners were dedicated and presented to the Lodge. The program for the evening consisted of Music, songs, and addresses.
