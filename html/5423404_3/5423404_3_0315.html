The Swedish National Society will give its Midwinter festival at the Garrick Theater Sunday, January 19, at 8 P.M. The Swedish Dramatical Society will present a historical play, "Gustaf II Adolph and Regina von Emmeritz," by Zacharias Topelius. "King Gustaf II Adolphs role" will be played by Prof. John R. Ortengren, and "Regina's" role by Miss Ida Ostergren, one of America's most prominent actresses.
