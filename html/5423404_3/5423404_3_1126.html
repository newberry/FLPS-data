The Sophia Benefit Society, connected with Washington Park Hospital, held its annual meeting January 6, when the following persons were elected to serve as officers during 1914: chariman: Mrs. C. E. Anderson; vice-chairman: Mrs. J. W. Hillstrom; recording secretary: Miss Olivia Hjarpe; financial secretary: Mrs. A. Ahlgren; corresponding secretary: Miss Emma Lind; and treasurer: Miss Nellie Hillstrom.
