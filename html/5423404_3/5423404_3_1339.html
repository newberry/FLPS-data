As has been previously announced, a group of Swedish women residing on the South Side volunteered to raise a sum of $15,000 towards the so-called Chicago Pavillion of the Swedish Tuberculosis Sanitarium in Denver, Colo. This amount has now been raised, and a few hundred dollars besides, which will be used for furnishings.

This announcement was made at a meeting of the executive committee last Tuesday, in the home of Mrs. C.L. Anderson, 924 Hyde Park Boulevard.

The following ladies have taken an active part in the work: Mrs. N.A. Nelson, chairman; Mrs. John A. Nylin, secretary; Mrs. Victor Bovik, assistant secretary; and Mrs. C.L. Anderson, treasurer.
