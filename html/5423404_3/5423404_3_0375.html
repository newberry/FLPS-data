p.12.......Carl Michael Bellman is a name which always will live in the memory of his countrymen, and festivals in his honor are celebrated annually among the Swedish people on both sides of the Atlantic. The Svithiod Singing Club and The Swedish Glee Club will give a Bellman-festival, Saturday, July the 13th, at Manila Grove, corner of North Clark Street, and Leland Avenue. The festival begins at three P.M. and continues all afternoon and evening.

Bellman's well-known melodies will be presented by both clubs and the orchestra, and a pleasant time is promised all who attend.
