Gladt Humor (Happy Humor) is the title of a collection of humorous tales in verse and prose by Gus Higgins, which has just been published by And. L. Lofstrom's publishing house, 113 Oak Street, Chicago.

Through the some one hundred pages, Wiklund tells why "He Was So Darned Happy"; Captain L. . m sits "In the Fox Trap"; then we hear the "Bjorneborg March" and Sigurd's "Mama," and "Proposal by Advertisement". We must also mention the soul-stirring poems "Fia Jansson" and "Steamboat Carl," etc.

Happy Humor is well worth reading, and costs 25 cents.
