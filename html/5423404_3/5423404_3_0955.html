The Chicago chess championship was won by the Swedish Chess Club of Chicago at the city-wide tournament held last week. The finalists in class one were the Swedes and the Commonwealth Edison Chess Club, and the outcome was 4-3 in favor of the Swedes.

The Swedish players were: H. Lundblad, G. Nystrom, F. Oberg, H. Gustavson, H.G. Reinhold, E. Gustavson, H. Gabrielson and W. Johnson.
