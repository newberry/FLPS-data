The Svithiod lodge during 1905 has experienced the most substantial growth of its history. No less than 828 new members have been taken in, which figure exceeds that of 1913, the former record year. The number lost or expelled is far less than that of 1913. The membership increased to 4,132, of which 4,073 are in good financial standing. On Sunday, January 14, another strong local was installed in Peoria by the Chicago branch.
