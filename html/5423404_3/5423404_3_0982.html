The Soccer football team of the Chicago-Swedish Athletic Club played its sixth football match of the season last Sunday with the Ogden Park team on the latter's field. The match was one of the hardest and most interesting in which the Swedish team ever has entered, but in spite of the English team's energetic attempt, the Swedish team was acclaimed the winner at the end of the match with a score of 3-0. The Swedish team up to the present unconquered, with six victories in six matches, whereas it has taken thirty-nine goals and only lost three, which is said to be unusual.

The next match will be played this coming Sunday against last year's champions, the Lincoln Park team, on their field at Winnemac Avenue and Robey Street.
