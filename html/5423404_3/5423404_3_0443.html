The winners of the Literary Contest, sponsored by the Swedish Journalists' Society, the headquarters of which is in Chicago, have now been announced. Seventy-one contributions have been submitted to the awarding committee, and of these thirty-five were in verse and thirty-six in prose.

First prize in prose was awarded to a Chicagoan, O.A. Linder, editor-in-chief of The Swedish-American. The winner of the first prize in verse was August Bergstedt, Quincy, Mass.
