Last Tuesday in his home, 719 Grace Street, Chicago, our old countryman, Cabriel Hjertquist passed to his reward. He came here in 1873, and was born in 1840. He was Sheriff of Konga, and was Kronobefallnings Man in Stranda. He attached himself to the Swedish Tribune, and with time became a factor in its life. In 1844, he, with P.A. Sundelius, and N.P.Nelson, bought the Swedish-American. He was in charge of the printing of this newspaper for twenty-five years, when he retired in dignified leisure, (Otium cum dignitate).

He was united in marriage with Sofia Beata Lundberg. Three children are living.
