The Independent Order of Svithiod will hold its eleventh annual May Festival this coming Saturday, May 2, at the North Side Turner Hall.

An elaborate program has been arranged, its component parts made up by the Svithiod Male Chorus, Struve's Quartet, the Swedish Cornet Band and Theodor Sjoquist, the tenor. Robert Lindblom will deliver an address, and there will be coronation of a May Queen.
