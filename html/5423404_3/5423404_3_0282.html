The Day of Youth is the name applied to the summer festival which will be held in Herm's Park, Western and Berteau Avenues, next Sunday afternoon by young members of Iclott, Venus, Ideal, which are affiliated with the Good Templars. Speaker Frithiof Werecskjold and singing by the popular singing choir Comrades. Music being furnished by the Good Templars Orchestra. Dancing and games will feature the entire day and evening.

Everybody of Nordic blood welcome.
