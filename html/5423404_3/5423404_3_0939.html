John G. Thunholm, President of the Swedish American Athletic Association of Chicago, is at present on a visit in Sweden, partly on business and partly to see his folks in his hometown, Stockholm.

He brought with him from Chicago a check in the sum of 5,000 Swedish Kroner (about $1,600.00) to be presented to the Swedish Olympic Games Committee as a contribution from the Swedish-American athletes in Chicago toward the Swedish participation in the games this summer at Antwerp.
