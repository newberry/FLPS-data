Its 75th Annual Jubilee was celebrated last week by the First Swedish Methedist-Episcopal Church at the corner of Highland Ave. and N. Paulina St. A series of commemorative services and festivals marked the Jubilee Week. The principal address during the Jubilee was made by Dr. C. G. Wallenims, professor at the Wesley Academy and Theological Seminary. An impressive banquet was given in connection with the Jubilee.
