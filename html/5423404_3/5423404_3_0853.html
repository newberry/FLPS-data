"Youth, Athletics and Alcohol" was the topic of a lecture given by the noted lecturer Carl Holmsten, before the Good Templar Lodge Svea, No. 8, in Link's Hall last Sunday night. The public had been invited, and the hall was well filled, in spite of the fact that several other important Swedish gatherings took place that same night.

The lecture was received with great interest, and many declared that they had never heard a more interesting and instructive talk on this subject.
