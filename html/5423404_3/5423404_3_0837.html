The Lake View Swedish Cultural Association opened its spring season last Friday with a meeting in Idrott's Hall, 3204 Wilton Avenue, at which Fred Leighton lectured on "Old Mexico, Its Indians and Prehistoric Civilization". Leighton has spent much time in Mexico, and is familiar with its geography and culture and with the life of its people.

The second part of this lecture will be given next Friday, and throughout the season other lectures dealing with educational topics will be offered every week.
