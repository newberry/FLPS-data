The Soccer Football Season in Chicago is now well under way and "our boys" have proven that they are in fine mettle.

Two weeks ago last Sunday the Swedish-American Athletic Association's team defeated the German Club by a score of 8-3, and the Sunday before last they proved their fortitude by beating the Waverly Ball Club by 8-0. Again last Sunday they came out victors with 5-0 against the Ulster United.

Last Sunday saw two other soccer victories for the Swedish colors. The north-side Liberty team beat the Lyons by 5-3, and the south side Linnia team handed the Chicago Sports Club a 3-1 defeat.
