The Svithiod Singers Club held its 26th annual festival last Saturday evening at its club house, 1768 Wrightwood Avenue. A splendid supper was served, after which business matters were attended to. Mr. Olaf Nelson announced that the net proceeds from the drama "Streets of Stockholm" was $1,100. At the same time he presented gifts to three of the performers in the play. Chairman Mrs. Olson and Erik Rosen were presented with gold medals, and little Miss Lindell with a large doll.
