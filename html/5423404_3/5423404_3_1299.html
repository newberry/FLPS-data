The Swedish Vasa Society of Chicago held its 15th. annual meeting the other day. Mr. J. F. Jerner was reelected to the presidency of the society.

The various reports showed that the Society's activities during the last fiscal year were larger than during the 1888-1889 season. A sum of $1,294. was paid out in sick and funeral benefits in the last year. The Society's treasury now has $4,025.86.
