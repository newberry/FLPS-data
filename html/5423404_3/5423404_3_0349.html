The Swedish National Association and the Swedish Singing Association have arranged for the presentation of an exceptionally enjoyable musical on Saturday evening. A chorus of 250 trained voices under the leadership of Professor John R. Ortengren is certain to prove very effective. Then we will hear the soloists, Mme Ragna Linne, Professor Gustave Holmquist, Miss Margaret Dahlstrom, and others. We also wish to call attention to the beautiful pantomimical sketches from Swedish life during times of peace and times of war, which will be presented as part of the program...
