The Illinois District Lodge of the Order of Vasa held its 20th annual meeting last Friday and Saturday.

The district secretary's report shows that 544 new members had joined the various lodges in Illinois during the year of 1927, thereby increasing the membership to 12,584. The combined assets of the lodges showed an increase from $163,294.05 to $178,507.95. In sick benefits during 1927 the lodges had paid out a sum of $43,666.55 and in funeral benefits $10,100.00, or a total of $53,766.55. The grand total corresponding figure for the twenty years of the Order's existence in Illinois is $445,283.68.
