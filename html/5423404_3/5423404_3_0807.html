Members of the twenty-fifth graduating class of Augustana Hospital Training School for Nurses received their diplomas during a ceremony held last Friday evening in the Irving Park Swedish Lutheran Church.

A program of song and music added color to the graduation exercises, and Dr. Wahlstrom, the chief of staff of the hospital, and two other doctors addressed the audience.

Forty-six nurses received their diplomas, certifying that they have completed the required three years of training.
