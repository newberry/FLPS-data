The Scandinavian-American Athletic Union of Illinois was organized here in Chicago and consists of four Swedish athletic clubs: Scandia, Brage, Eskil and the Swedish Gymnastic and Athletic Club and two Norwegian clubs: Sleipner and the Norwegian Athletic Club. The following officers were elected: president, George Odin; vice-president, B. Riestad; secretaries, H. Grevin and A. L. Hellstrom; treasurer, H. Gullogh; financial directors, J. Hanson and J. Johnson.
