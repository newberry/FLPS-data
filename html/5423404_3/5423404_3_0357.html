The Scandinavian Technological Society of Chicago celebrated the centennial of the birth of the Swedish inventor and scientist, John Ericsson, last Friday evening. The celebration took the form of a banquet at the Hotel Wellington. The participants were mostly technologists, but not exclusively so. The speakers of the evening were City Engineer John Ericson, Wholesaler Robert Lindblom, Rev. Herman Lindskog, Engineer O. M. Torrison, and General William Sooy Smith. General Smith was a personal friend of the celebrated Swede and gave a brilliant account of the achievements of his late friend.
