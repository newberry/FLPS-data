Two lectures will be delivered at the regular monthly meeting of the Swedish Engineers' Society next Saturday, March 28th, beginning at 7:00 P.M. and preceded by the regular dinner. The first lecture will be on "Methods of Communications" by Mr. S.C. Summerfield of the Western Electric Company and the subject of the second one will be "The Waterway Laboratory of the Stockholm Institute of Technology" and will be delivered by Lieutenant Eric Lindquist, a civil engineer from Stockholm on a visit here.

The annual banquet and ball of the Society will be given in the Club House, next Saturday, April 4th, at 8:00 P.M. This affair will be given in honor of the Ladie's Auxiliary.
