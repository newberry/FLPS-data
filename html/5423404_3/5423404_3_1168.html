The Swedish-American Old Settler Society held their yearly feast and banquet, Sunday, October 25, at 2:00 P.M. at Hamonybengen Club, 1535 Aldine Avenue. All those who have been in this country thirty years, who were born in Sweden, are eligible to belong to the society. All inquiries should be addressed to the President, Pehr. W. Nilsson, 1727 N. Clark Street, or Secretary E. G. Lubeck, 67 Dearborn Street.
