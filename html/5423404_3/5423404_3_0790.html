Graduation exercises were held last Wednesday in the Englewood Mission Church, 59th and Carpenter Streets, for fourteen nurses who have completed their three-year course in the training school of the Bowmanville Hospital.

The graduating class and their friends and relatives who attended the ceremony were addressed by Dr. O. Theo Roberg and Dr. Timmerman, of the hospital staff, and by Pastor Algoth Ohlson, the new director of North Park College. Between speeches, Professor Frank Earnest and the violinist Elmer Swanson, both of the North Park Music Conservatory, entertained the audience with song and music; they were assisted by the church choir.

Afterwards, refreshments were served in the basement dining room.
