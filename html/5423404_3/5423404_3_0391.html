p.12... Next Sunday at 3:00 P.M., a Swedish American festival will be held at Spelz Hall, to which all residents of Lake View are invited who are friends of knowledge. The purpose of the festival is to create an interest in free lectures, as an aid toward cultural development; and in the future, as a result of these interests, it is hoped that a Swedish-American cultural Institute, with headquarters in Lake View, will be the outcome.

The program will include speaking, discussions, declamations, singing and music. Free admission. The program begins at 3 P.M. sharp.
