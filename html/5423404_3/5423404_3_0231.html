An impressive Gustavus Adolphus Festival was held by the Chicago chapter of the Swedish Cultural Society of America last Sunday, at the Swedish Lutheran Central Church. Addresses were delivered by Consul C. O. de Dardel, the Rev. Dr. Peter Peterson and a guest speaker, the Rev. Dr. C. G. Klingner from Sweden. The Northland Ladies Trio, Nordstjernan Singing Club and Harry T. Carlson, organ soloist, furnished a highly enjoyable program.
