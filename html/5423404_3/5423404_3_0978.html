Great sports activity took place last Sunday in Brand's Park, arranged by the large Norwegian club "Sleipner," which had invited several other sports clubs to compete in sports of various kinds.

The weather was ideal, the audience large, and the results attained better than had been the case at similar meets during the past few years.

The "Sleipner" won the first prize in the general competition in general, but the Swedish Athletic Club of Chicago came in as a close second.

Five sports clubs participated.
