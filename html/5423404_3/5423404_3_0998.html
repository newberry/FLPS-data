Chicago has taken the initiative to found a Gymnastic and Athletic Society in the Western States similar to what the Swedish athletes did in the east three years ago. The club will try to build up a large and strong organization and through cooperation and yearly races it hopes to boost the interest for Swedish gymnastics forward, and to extend the working field.

Everybody interested in this matter is cordially invited to a meeting to be held Sunday, Mar. 22, at Glnecks Hall. A committee of two persons will be chosen to lead the meeting. All correspondence should be forwarded to Scandia Gymnastic and Athletic Club, 327 Sedgwick Street, Chicago, Illinois.
