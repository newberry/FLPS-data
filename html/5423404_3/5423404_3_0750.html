The Thrall of Leif the Lucky is the title of a novel about Leif Erickson's journey over the Atlantic to Greenland and America in the beginning of the Eleventh Century, written by Miss Ottilie Lilsencrantz, daughter of our well-known countryman Gustaf Lilsencrantz, Assistant Engineer at the U. S. Marine Department. A. C. McClurg & Co. is distributing the book.
