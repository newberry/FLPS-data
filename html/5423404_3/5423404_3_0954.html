The Chicago chess championship was again awarded the Swedish Chess Club by virtue of the Club's victory, the other day, over the German Chess Club. The final match comprised eight games, of which the Swedes won seven and lost one. The winning players were A. Bjuren, F. Strand, G. Nystrom, F. Oberg, I. Nordstrom, E. Johanson and H. Gustafson. The losing player was N. Hansen.
