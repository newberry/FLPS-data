The Svithiod Singing Club gave a euchre party last Saturday evening at their club-local, 1768 Wrightwood Avenue. A large number of club members attended with guests and partook in this especially pleasant affair.

After the card-playing refreshments were in order, and, thereafter, dancing, skittles, and other happy diversion were engaged in. Four prizes, two for men, two for women were given during the evening.

The popular club will give another euchre party at the same hall, Saturday March 16.
