The Scandia Life Insurance Company has its main office at 159 LaSalle Street, Chicago. It is the only Scandinavian corporation in America which issues life insurance of every type. On account of the care shown by this company in executing contracts, it is practically impossible for the insured to lose his rights. If for any reason he should wish to discontinue payment of premiums he may obtain Extended or Paid-Up insurance, thus being safeguarded, without extra expenses. Loan values and cash values are also connected with all the insurance policies.
