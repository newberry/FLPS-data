The final game for the Peel Cup was played last Sunday at the De Paul University Field between the Swedish-American Athletic Association's first team and the professional Sparta Athletic Club. The result was a 3-2 victory for the professional club, after a hard fought game, which displayed great skill on both sides. The deciding goal was made by Sparta on a penalty kick.

This game was a re-play of the finals for the coveted Peel Cup last Sunday at the Soldier's Field, at which neither of the above teams were able to score.
