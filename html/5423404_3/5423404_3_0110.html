The Swedish Theater Society will present a folk festival play called "Vermlandingarne," in six acts together with song and dancing. The play was written by F. A. Dahlgren, and is to be given on New Year's Day, January 1, 1908, at 8 P.M., at the North Side Turner Hall.

National Folk dances will be presented by the Philochorus Society, and a grand New Year's Ball will be arranged after the performance.
