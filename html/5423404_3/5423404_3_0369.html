A great festival was held in Sharpshooters Park last Sunday. Ten lodges attended. The Grand Chieftain, A. W. Johnson, gave the speech of welcome.

The Orphei Singing Clubs gave several renditions, which were received enthusiastically. Mayor Carter A. Harrison, Jr., also made a short speech. Attorney G. Bernhard Anderson spoke on the subject "Remembrance," which talk was punctuated by great applause.

The cash receipts are scheduled to go towards the Viking reserve fund.
