The Swedish Glee Club will give a concert next Saturday evening at its quarters at 470 La Salle Ave. It is a well-known fact that this club ranks first in Chicago when it comes to presenting programs of songs and music, and it is rumored that this affair will be an excellent one.

The club will be assisted by the Concordia Ladies Quartet, Miss Etta C. Levin, Wm. Dohlin, and Joel Massberg.

They will sing songs by Soderman, Myrberg, During, and Hedenblod.

The Concordia will sing: "Coming Thru the Rye" and "La Serenata." The tickets are now on sale at fifty cents per person. A large attendance is expected.
