The lodge Ljusbringeren (Bringer of light) is arranging for a great festival on Sunday December 13, at 7 P. M. The festival will be held in Volts Hall, corner of Chicago Avenue and Wells Street. Mr. Axel Ahlstedt will speak on the topic, "The Red Flag of Universal Brotherhood."

After the speech, a dialogue in drama form will be rendered. In addition there will be music and singing.
