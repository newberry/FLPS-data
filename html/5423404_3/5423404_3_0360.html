The Svithoid Singing Club presented a beautiful Spring Festival in its club rooms on Wrightwood Avenue last Saturday.

The festival was so arranged that only men participated. Their faces all shone with pleasure and with all the colors of the rainbow.

A great number of people participated in the singing and acting. Those who arranged the festival are: J. S. Meek, Fred K. Fransen, Ivar Hennings, and Wm. L. Ekwall.
