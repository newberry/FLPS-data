A large Swedish-American Festival in memory of the great Swedish-Finnish poet, Runeberg, will be held on Sunday, April 10, at Spelz Hall, Lake View. The festivities will start at 2:30 P. M., and continue till 6 P. M. Dr. J. A. Enander will deliver the speech in honor of the great poet, and in his speech will tell of the life and activity of Poet Runeberg. Joel Mossberg, the baritone singer, and Orphei Singing Club will assist, as will also Miss Dahlstrom.
