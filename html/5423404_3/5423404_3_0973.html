The annual membership meeting of the Scandinavian-American Athletic Union was held December 11 in the Sherman Hotel. The following officers were elected: A. W. Sundene, president; C. christensen, first vice-president; L. H. Rodde, secretary of protocol; B. Gundersen, corresponding secretary; Oseen Munson, treasurer; A. Shelstrom, athletic director.

The organization aims to promote co-operation in the sphere of athletics and sports among Scandinavians in this section of the country. During the three years of its existence it has obtained gratifying results, and Swedish, Norwegian, and Danish clubs are working together for the common cause. Scandinavian clubs are holding their own in competition with organizations of this kind operated by other nationalities.
