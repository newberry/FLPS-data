The Balder Lodge No. 12, I. O. Carlson; treasurer: Alfred Anderson; chief: F. G. E. Cedervall; secretary to the trustees: Gust Olson; examning Doctor: Emil Nels Larson. Charles E. Carlson and Gust Olson were elected delegates to the Grand Lodge.
