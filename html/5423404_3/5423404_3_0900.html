Mr. Erland Bjornberg, the organizer of the Scandinavian Fraternity of America, will speak at Belmont Hall, corner of North Clark Street and Belmont Avenue, on Wednesday May 31, 8 P.M. The topic of the lecture is the "Origin and Growth of the Scandinavian Fraternity." There will be music and singing. After the lecture, a lodge will be organized under the auspices of the Fraternity, which is the largest and most widely distributed Scandinavian organization in America. Both women and men are admitted to membership.
