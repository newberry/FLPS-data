The Svea lodge celebrated its forty-ninth anniversary on last Sunday at its hall on Chicago Avenue with a well-arranged social evening. Svea is the oldest Swedish lodge in Chicago. It has a present membership of fiftyseven. Svea's financial standing is very good, there being $4,215.10 in the treasury. Among the disbursements during the past year are two notable items; sick benefits to members amounted to $630, and furnishing a room completely at the Swedish Old People's Home in park Ridge, an expenditure of $100.
