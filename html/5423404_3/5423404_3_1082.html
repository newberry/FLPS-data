Irving Park Lodge No.20 of the Order of Svithiod celebrated its 25th anniversary last Saturday by a banquet at the Myrtle Masonic Temple.

From its modest beginning October, 1902, the lodge now has grown to a membership of 577. Its contributions to the grand lodge during these years total $62,000. Approximately $25,000 has been paid to its members in sick and funeral benefits. The lodge has in its own treasury a sum of $13,480.
