This lodge installed its officers on Dec. 29. Among them were: Alfred A. Norton, worship master; Edward H. Titus, senior warden; Charles H. Rosberg, junior warden; Edward A. Linn, treasurer; Nels J. Johnson, secretary; Nels O. Johnson, chaplain.

The meeting ended with a program composed of bass singer Gustaf Holmquist, soprano singer Mrs. Marie G. Zendt, alto singer Miss Barbara Wait, and a quartet.
