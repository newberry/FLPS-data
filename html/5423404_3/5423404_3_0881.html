Professor C. G. Wallenius is scheduled to lecture on the subject "the Road to Culture" on February 28 at 4 P.M. The lecture will be given at Eagle Hall, Springfield and North Avenues.

The second lecture in the series, "Tuberculosis and its Relationship to Alcohol", will be given by Dr. John Lindquist on March 7, 5 P.M., at Colonial Hall, 5436 Wentworth Avenue.

The final lecture of the series will be given on March 14, 7 P.M., at Unold Hall in La Grange. Professor Wallenius will speak on "Abraham Lincoln".
