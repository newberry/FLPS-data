The Swedish Singing Union will celebrate the birthday of Carl Von Linne May 23 by singing at his statue in Lincoln Park.

The Nordstjernan Society will give a May 28th festival at Turner Hall on the South Side, Saturday, May 4.

The Orphei Singing Club will give a Spring concert and ball in its hall, Saturday, May 11.

Balders Music Corps will arrange the first picnic of the season at World's Fair Park, Sunday, May 12.
