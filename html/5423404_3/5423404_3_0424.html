The Pegasus Club, an organization of teachers and students at the North Park College, has just published a little book containing a collection of poems entitled Pegasus. It contains thirty-two pages and forty-four poems, most of them are short ones. Some of them are lyrical while others are of a religious nature. In the collection are a number of Swedish poems, some original, others translated.

The book costs twenty-five cents and may be obtained from the Pegasus Club, North Park College, 3225 Foster Avenue, Chicago.
