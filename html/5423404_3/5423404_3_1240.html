A mass meeting, under the auspices of the lodges Vart Hem and Svea, of the I.O.G.T., held last Sunday, was very well attended.

The meeting was opened with music by the Bellman Trio, after which Dr. Jacob Bonggren read an ode to Mr. David Holmgren, a member of the Swedish parliament. Mr. Holmgren was afterwards introduced to the audience by Editor Frans Albin, and gave a brilliant speech.

Mr. Holmgren's speech was followed by songs by Mr. Robert Johnson, and by the Linnea ladies' quartette. Both Mr. Johnson and the ladies of the quartet deserve praise for their beautiful singing.
