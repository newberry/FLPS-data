The 60-game Inter-City Bowling Match between between Chicago and Cleveland, Ohio, was won by Chicago in the concluding game the other day. The finalists were our countryman, Adolph Carlson of Chicago, and S. Mercurie of Cleveland. Carlson won with 387 pins. The total paints for the 60 games were 13,201 for Carlson and 12,814 for Mercurie. Mr. Carlson also pocketed $1,000 as his prize for winning the match.
