Linnea Aid Society in the Englewood District held its annual meeting recently in the Swedish Methodist Church at 60th St. and Union Ave.

The reports show that the Society during 1927 distributed $3,925.96 in cash among sick and needy Swedish people, in addition to the distribution of 115 baskets at last Christmas. Besides, a subscription started among the members for the Swedish Ter-Centenary Jubilee Fund has to date brought $1,397.75.
