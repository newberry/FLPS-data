The Harmony Singing Club under the able leadership of Ernest Lindblom, has already won popular acclaim in spite of the fact that it is hardly one year old.

The Club's last concert in Spitz Hall Saturday drew a large and representative audience. At this concert the Club sang: "Hear Us Svea," "Romance," "Rose in the Northern Woodland," "Airs From Sicilian Vespers," and "On The Mountain Rings The Bell."
