The Scandia Life Insurance Company held its ninth annual meeting last Thursday at Hotel La Salle here. The reports which were read and approved, showed that the company has been very successful last year. There was $1,507,440.70 in the treasury December 31, 1913. C. H. Boman, E. F. Strandberg, Edwin A. Olson, and I.M. Anderson, all from Chicago, and Alfred G. Larson from Rockford, were elected members of the Board of Directors, who, in their turn, elected the following officers: president: N. A. Nelson; vice-president: E. A. Olson; secretary: C. H. Boman; treasurer: I. M. Anderson; Doctor: William A. Peterson.
