The Jupiter lodge of I.O.G.T. gave a well-attended entertainment last Saturday evening at Three Links Club House, North Clark Street. The program consisted of singing, music, speeches, and readings. Afterward there was dancing. The music was furnished by the Bellman Mandolin Club; Miss Maude Payne; Miss Elffi Peterson; Messrs. E. Renstjerna and H. Falk. The speech of welcome was delivered by Mr. J. E. Odell. The singing was done by Concordia Sextet and by Miss Ellen Eklund. Mr. S. Bergstedt read an ode written by himself for the occasion.
