At the election on April 5, 1898, Lauritz Olesen was the Socialist candidate for alderman in the Fourteenth Ward. He got 131 votes, but was not elected.

The only Dane that landed anything was C. W. Larsen, a fish dealer. He was the candidate for county constable on the Democratic South Town Ticket. It is to be hoped that with his constable's star Mr. Larsen will sell more cod and flounder.
