The Skandinaviske Socialdemokratiske Sygeforening (Social-Democratic Sick Benefit Society) had a crowded meeting August 5 regarding the expulsion of the Twenty-eighthWard branch of the Socialist party. Most of the members of this branch are not satisfied with the Socialist platform adopted by the national convention; they find it too tame and not radical enough. However, their leader, Mr. Jorgensen, did have a chance to set forth his point of view.
