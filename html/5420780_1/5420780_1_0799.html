Henry L. Hertz, who for many years has been Chief Clerk at the Board of Review was appointed last Monday, (March 25) by President McKinley to the post of Internal Revenue Collector for the First Illinois District. His predecessor was Fred E. Coyne, who, from April 1st is to be the new postmaster of Chicago.

Mr. Hertz is appointed for four years at salary of $10,000 per year.
