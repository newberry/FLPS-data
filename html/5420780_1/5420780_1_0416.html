p.2...........There is good reason to hope that the gas rate in Chicago is going to be reduced by five cents per 1,000 cubic feet, and later an another reduction is in prospect. Here is another illustration of practical socialism; why should the entire population of a great city be exploited for the benefit of a few monopolists and parasites?

With the steady growth of the socialistic movement, such reforms, which are as yet few and small and of minor consequence, will increase, wiping out monopolistic practices in all phases of production and distribution.
