Wahl-Henius Institute of Fermentology, 1135-1147 Fullerton Avenue was started in October, 1886 by Robert Wahl, Ph.D., and Max Henius, Ph.D. In 1891 the first class in theoretical and practical brewing was started. More than 1200 pupils have graduated from the institute and are now in important positions all over the world where brewing is regarded as a science.

The institute has published a manual for brewers: "American Hand Book of Brewing, Malting, and Auxiliary Trades."
