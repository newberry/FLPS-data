John Altgeld was not elected Mayor of Chicago, although he got 46,000 votes. The candidate of the Socialist Labor party, which stood for municipal ownership, got only 1,100 votes.

The capitalist party, or the Republican flourishes while our Democratic party is demoralized and divided, and the Socialist party, which should help the Democrats, fails and is no party to speak of at all.

Ninety per cent of the Danes voted for Altgeld.
