In earnest defense of sound morals a Chicago Hearst paper is expressing its indignation over the fact that a five cent theatre on the Northside is showing "The Unwritten Law" or the "Play of the Thaw Case." Well! well! In view of the fact the Hearst papers, like the rest of the capitalistic press have exploited the dirty Thaw affair for all its worth and in a manner that baffles description perchance we may be allowed to remark that their "indignation" is the limit of audacity; or is it business jealousy?
