The investigation into the magnificent swindle which was perpetrated by the Democratic machine at the last election in order to bring about the election of the Hearst-sponsored Maclay Hoyne, has resulted in a number of indictments, and it is to be hoped that proper punishment will be meted out to those guilty in the plot.

It is important to discover the real "brains" of the conspiracy. They seem to be more dangerous and nervy than even the old notorious "Tweed Ring" in New York, and undoubtedly the authorities, if they want to should be able to comprehend these crooks.

Turn on the screws, Mr. Northup!
