There must be very few thinking voters in some of Chicago's Wards to elect Seymor Steadman for Mayor, but there must be many of them in the Fifteenth and Twenty-seventh Wards, where Socialists were elected. William E. Rodrignez in the Fifteenth Ward and John C. Kennedy in the Twenty-seventh Ward, with a majority of four thousand votes. At any rate, there is new blood in the city council.
