The charge of Secretary Ickes that rugged individualism is a relic of the past is merely a confirmation by that gentleman that he is a moral coward. Sheltered under the wing of bureaucracy, this man would relegate to the ash heap the fundamentals of character that are essential to human progress, namely: honesty, courage, faith, initiative, thrift, and justice. By seeking to banish these finer traits of humanity, this man under presidential sanction, places a premium upon legalized plundering, political pilfering and exploitation of human misery.
