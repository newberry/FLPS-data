The Chicago Federation of Labor endorses Christian M. Madsen in the following words:

"A working man in the right place; is considerate, just, and true; was a member of the labor committee, and has worked hard to have all labor bills brought before the committee. A forceful debater, and a level-headed man. Should be returned and others of like qualities with him."

Madsen is also endorsed by the Legislative Voters League.

Otto Christensen, Socialist candidate for Senator of the twenty-fifth district, is endorsed also.
