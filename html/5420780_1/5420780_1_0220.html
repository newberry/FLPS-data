Mr. S. E. Gross, Chicago real estate firm, is giving a free excursion on the Illinois Central R. R. to Dauphin Park, where he has many fine residential lots for sale.

Many Danes of the South Side were out there Sunday,the 15th, and bought some lots. The location is only 24 minutes ride on the railroad from Randolph Street. The place already has a church, a public school and many stores.
