The Scandinavian Socialist Convention met July 2-4, at D.B.S., twenty-eight delegates were present. It was finally decided to organize a permanent Central Committee for all Scandinavian Socialists in America. A temporary board was elected to write up the laws of this organization; it had the following members: N. Juel Christensen, Wm. Petersen and Thv. Jensen, all of Chicago; K. A. Ihlsberg and P. Haugen, Rockford. Their work must be finished by October 1st; and the Central Committee will start to function January 1, 1911.
