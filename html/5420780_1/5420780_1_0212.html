The express companies claim that the government's "socialistic parcel post system" has deprived them of one-third of their revenue. We sincerely hope that soon they will be deprived of the remaining two-thirds for the benefit of business people and consumers all over the country.

The same procedure should be followed in regard to all other trusts. Why should society be taxed in order to enrich a few? They may get away with it for some time but not indefinitely.
