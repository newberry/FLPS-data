Ackerman's Book Store, which has always specialized in Danish literature, but at the same time also carried a considerable stock of good books in the English language has recently expanded its English department, and now offers a rich assortment of the best in this line.

One will also find a fine selection of Danish books in English translation, such as Karin Michaelis' The Dangerous Age, and H. C. Anderson's Fairy Tales. Drop in at the store. After the days work it is refreshing to browse among books!
