The presidential campaign's first great Scandinavian meeting will take place tonight in Bjorgvin's Hall, Artesian Avenue near North Avenue, under the sponsorship of the S.S.S. of 1895.

Attorney Otto Christensen, Socialistic candidate for congressman from the 7th District, and Frithjof Werenskjold, traveling Norwegian lecturer, will speak.

Enemies as well as friends of Socialism, are welcome.
