The S.S.S. of 1895 (The Scandinavian Socialist Society of 1895) was organized on June 7, 1895. Its aim is the spread of socialism and to provide help in case of sickness and death. It has 480 members. It meets every Friday night at Humboldt Hall, at the corner of Talman and North Avenues. It has a library of 1,500 volumes. The secretary is C. J. Ostergaard, 1938 North Albany Avenue.

On September 11 the Society held a protest meeting against the World War.
