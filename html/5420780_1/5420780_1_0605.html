p.2.......Most of the Danish and Norwegian newspapers in this country have chosen not to discuss the new military act of the United States, which entirely alters the old system, making the president the military dictator over every citizen. These papers follow the lead of the capitalistic dailies who maintain a stubborn silence concerning this far-reaching legislation.

However, the military act is an established fact, which is gradually becoming known, and it is reported from several stables that particularly members of labor unions are withdrawing from the state militia, in order to avoid going to war or to participate in mass slaughter of fellow workers, at least until the reserves are called.
