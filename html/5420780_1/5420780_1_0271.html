The Danish Cooperative Store of Racine, Wisconsin, has had a surplus profit of $710.95 during the period between Jan. 1st 1907 and July 1st 1907. The money, plus 8% interest of capital invested, is being paid to the seventy-six members of the Society. In accordance with cooperative principles, the $701.95 was distributed in proportion to patronage and amounted to nine percent of total purchase. Non-members trading at the store got a two percent rebate. Capital invested in the enterprise is $1670.00.
