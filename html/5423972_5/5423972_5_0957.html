The Jewish Record occupies an unusual position in its field. It is a weekly newspaper, printed in Yiddish, and is the leading literary publication in that language in the West. I. Shapiro, commissioner and secretary of the West Park Board, is the publisher.

Its regular contributors include K. Marmor, editor of the Chicago Daily World; Dr. Morris Reinhart; S. Greenblau, formerly editor of the Daily Jewish Press; Israel Blum, the poet; Dr. Israel Marcus; J. M. Wolfsohn, formerly, editor of the Daily Jewish Courier; Roman G. Lewis, and S. H. Album, the foremost orthodox rabbi in Chicago.
