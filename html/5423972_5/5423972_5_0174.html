"We have prepared a large repertoire, we have several beautiful dramas of O Ossys Dimov; we will produce his Bronx Express, with the same staging as in New York, and we also will produce Dimov's new drama "Stranger than Love"......We have secured the services of the Yiddish-Russian artists, Anastasin Orzhevska and Leomid Sniegov, who will appear in the outstanding works of Gorki, Artzibasheff, Andrew, and other Russian artists".

Mr. Jacob Cohen

Glickman's Theater.
