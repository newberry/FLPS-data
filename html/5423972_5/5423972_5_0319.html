The Gartner's Pavilion Theater has opened this season with a large troupe of actors. They have already presented the well known Jewish comedy of Shimer's "In Latineren" to a crowded house. They will not only present comedies, but also dramas that were very successful in New York, like "Die Harshendeh", "Shclaven" and "Broken Hearts."......

At the head of this troupe are Mr. and Madam Gartner, Herr and Mrs. Nathanson, Mr. Winestock, Mrs. Finklestein, Herr and Mrs. Leonsky, and others.
