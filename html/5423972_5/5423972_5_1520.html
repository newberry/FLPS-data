The opening of the I. L. Peretz Folk School in the National Socialist Institute, 3322 Douglas Boulevard, will take place on Tuesday, August 12, 10:00 A.M.

All parents of the Lawndale neighborhood, who have not as yet registered their children, are requested to do so on Tuesday morning, when the principal of the schools, William Nathanson, and the superintendent, A. Kipnis, will be present.

In the Maccabbean school at the Hebrew Institute, and in the Sholem Aleichem school of the Educational Alliance, the children are already attending classes from 10:00 A.M. to 1:00 P.M., until the public schools will open.
