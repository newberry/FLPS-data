The next meeting of the Liebling Amateurs will take place at Mr. Liebling's studio, Kimball Hall, 245 Wabash Ave. Santurday afternoon, April 11th. Mr. W. S. B. Mathews will deliver a lecture, with illustrations at the piano by Mr. Liebling.

The concert given by the Symphony Club Orchestra Tuesday evening at the Central Music Hall was well attended. A fine program was given. The soloists were Emil Liebling, Miss Grace Hiltz, Mrs. Claire Murray, Joseph Olheiser, and Leon A. Strauss.
