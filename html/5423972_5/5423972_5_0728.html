The priceless library of the late Gerson Siegel, which took a lifetime to collect, was presented by his widow and his children to the Beth Hamedrosh L. Torah.

This library consists of over 800 Hebrew volumes, some very rare out of print editions, others two and three hundred years old, will enable the young students of the Beth Hamedrosh L. Torah to acquaint themselves with such literature, which is nowadays almost extinct.
