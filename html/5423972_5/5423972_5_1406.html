Chicago Workmen's Circle Schools Open for New Children for a Short Time Only.

The classes arranged for new children, in these schools, are almost filled; therefore, the registration for new children must be limited.

The laws governing registration of new children, in these schools, are changed.

In the Douglas Park Workmen's Circle School, only children from 10 to 13 will be accepted.

On the Northwest Side children between 8 and 10 years only, will be accepted. The registrations of new children, will be made at the Douglas School, 1224 S. Albany Avenue, and the Northwest Side School, 2644 LeMoyne Street, Saturday and Sunday, from 10 AM to 2 PM, and the rest of the week, from 4 to 8 PM.
