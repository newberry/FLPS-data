The Herzliah Hebrew school graduation exercises for the year were held at the Institute Theater last Sunday Evening, October, 27. The proceedings were all held in Hebrew. The children all contributing equally intheir parts in that language together with their teachers and principal, Mr. Morris Liebman.

Twenty-five children were graduated on this occasion and will now continue with their work at the Central Hebrew High School. The large audience of parents and friends were highly elated with the fine program presented on this occasion and to the children, of course, it is bound to represent a milestone in their careers.
