A huge mass meeting, sponsored by the movement for a new Talmud Torah on the Northwest Side, was held September 3, in the Congregation B'Nai David Ohavah Tzedek. Eight synagogues of the Northwest Side were represented by prominent individuals who participate in all Jewish enterprises. The meeting was well attended. The chairman of the meeting was Mr. I. Miller who opened the meeting. He introduced Rabbi Saul Silber who delivered an inspiring message. It was reported that the West Side businessmen had contributed $800. Mr. D. Club donated $250.

M. Nathan, secretary.
