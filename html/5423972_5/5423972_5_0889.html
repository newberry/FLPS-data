$5,000 is waiting for someone!

Last week we issued a challenge to the Courier, which has not been accepted to date.

We are prepared to give $5,000 to any charity if the following statements are proven false!

1. That the Courier has no more than 1/3 of circulation which it announced in a notarized circulation-statement.

2. That the Forward has twice as much paid-circulation in Chicago as the Courier.

(Adv.)
