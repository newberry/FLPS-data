Empire Theater. Farewell performance.

Clara Rafala, Wednesday night, April 28th. This will be the last appearance of this great artist who is saying farewell to her thousands of friends and listeners, until next season.

On this occasion our artist will portray characters in the following 4 acts.

1. Shulamith - 1st act

2. The Stepchild - 2nd act

3. Where are our children? - 3rd act

4. Kreitzer Sonata - 4th act.
