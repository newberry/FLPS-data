Announcement: Grand Celebration. An Anniversary Night.

In honor of the ten years existence of the Literary Dramatic Society, a celebration will take place Saturday night April 5th, when the famous drama that made its success in New York - The Desolate Spot, in 4 acts, by P. Hirshbein - will be presented. The author himself, Mr. Hirshbein, is coming here especially to direct his drama, which will be given in the Central Music Hall, Van Buren and Michigan sts. Mr. Hirshbein will speak between the acts. Tickets can be purchased at the Forward, at the Cooperative restaurant, and from the members of the Society.
