The directors of the Jewish Training School, who are raising a fund of $300,000 for a new training school building, had the good fortune to receive a grand donation yesterday in the sum of $25,000 from Mrs. Emanuel Mandel.

Mrs. Mandel is an outstanding welfare worker, and for the last twenty-five years has tirelessly worked day in and day out for the good of Jewish institutions. With this liberal donation by Mrs. Mandel, the fund now totals $105,000. So, according to the latest financial report, it is understood that the necessary fund will soon be raised so that the building of the school can be started in the near future. It is understood that this training school building will be built some where in the Douglas Boulevard neighborhood.
