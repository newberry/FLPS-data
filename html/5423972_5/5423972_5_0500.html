The Officers of the free Jewish Shelter are inviting the Jewish Public of Chicago to its House warming at 1246 S. Sawyer Ave., Sunday Oct 24th at 3 P.M.

Prominent Rabbis will speak; an Orchestra will play.

Refreshments will be served, and a rich program will be presented.

Everybody is invited.

Respectfully,

Herman Elovitz, President.

Eliezer Adler, Vice President.

Jacob Tvery,Secretary.
