Mr. David Greenberg, the well known Jewish poet, who writes under the name of "Ben-ishiah," yesterday arrived to Chicago, in order to celebrate the 50th Jubilee of Mr. Aaron Heller, which will take place tomorrow evening at the Hull House.

Mr. Aaron Heller, who is celebrating the 50th anniversary, has attained recognition through his poetry. Those who read his work derived a great deal of pleasure. At the celebration a capacity crowd of business people is awaited, among whom Mr. Heller has made a name for himself, as well as all friends of Jewish literature.
