At the last meeting of the Fur Union, Local 45 was concerned with the question: What should the Union do in establishing a daily English Workers' Newspaper in Chicago? Comrades, M. J. Frenkel, and Sophie Rodigez addressed the meeting and pointed out the importance of the above-mentioned work.

A motion was made to withdraw $500 from the Treasury of the Union and purchase shares for the newspaper. The motion was unanimously accepted.
