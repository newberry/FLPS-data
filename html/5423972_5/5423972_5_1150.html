The following is a list of Jewish publication which have appeared here:

"Israelitische Temple Gesaenge Hymnen" by Otto Loeb, in 1876; a second edition in 1887.

"D'ma - an Yilmedoo", by a Hebrew reader, Dr. B. Felsenthal, in 1886.

"Songs of Zion", Souvenir of Jewish Women's Congress, Alois Kaiser and Rev. W. Sparger, in 1893, T. Rubovits, Publisher.

"Sabbath School Hymns", I.S. Moses, 1894.

"Hebrew Primer", second edition, Aaron J. Messing.

"Torath Emeth", Catechism for Instruction in Mosaic Religion, third edition, Aaron J. Messing.

Souvenir of the Centennial Anniversary of the birth of Sir Moses Montefiore: "A Hebrew Poem", H. Eliassoff, 1884.

Dr. Kohler's "Jewish Reader" for Sabbath Schools, which appeared in 1876.
