A Rabbi testified that the editor of the "Courier" had taken bribe, Is it possible?

When the news appeared that Rabbi Ephraim Epstein, Rabbi of the largest synagogue in Chicago, had testified at the Holy Altar that the editor of the Courier was given bribe, a great many asked the question, is it possible? Is the editor a "person" who would write praises, if paid a few klingers,and abuse and inveigh when he doesn't receive anything?

The answer to all questions concerning the money scandal in which the editor of the "Courier" is involved will clear the situation this Friday in the Forward.
