A school of Zionism has been organized in Chicago by the Knights of Zion. The object of this school is to describe the history and development of modern Zionism and explain the nature of this movement and its achievements.

The following have charge of the school: M. N. Friedland, chairman; David A. Pepp, secretary; Nathan D. Kaplan, Leon Zolotkoff, and Max Shulman, advisory committee.
