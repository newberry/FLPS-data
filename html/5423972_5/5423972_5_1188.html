On November 3, the Hebrew Theological College will lay the coner stone of its new library, which will be erected to the north of the present building at Dougles Blvd & St. Louis Avenue. The building will cost over $200,000. It will house one of the finest collections of Judaica and Hebraica in the country.

The new building will also furnish needed classroom space for the preparatory and elementary classes of the college.
