Chicago Jewry will assemble at three large memorial meetings to commemorate the death anniversary of Dr. Theodore Herzl, organizer and founder of the present great Zionist movement. The meetings will be held at the Herzl public school, Lawndale and Douglas Blvd., the Hebrew Institute, and the Jewish Educational Alliance, North Wood Street. Cantors, Milkowski, Lipner, and Reichlin will officiate at the meetings. A large attendance is expected in all three places.

Good speakers will address the meetings. Everyone is invited to come and honor the memory of the greatest Jewish leader of the present generation.
