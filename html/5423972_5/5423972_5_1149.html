Morris Salmonson has completed 29 years as Marriage License Clerk of Cook County. He had issued nearly a half-million marriage licenses. Mr. Salmonson is 60 years of age, and a native of Denmark.

He is the author of a number of philosophical workers. The most noteworthy of these are "Among Jews" and "From the Marriage License Window". He is also the author of a three-act comedy published some years ago called "We Mortals".
