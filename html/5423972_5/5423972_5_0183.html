At the request of the general public, The Big Prize by Sholom Alechem, folks comedy in 4 acts, will be produced in memory of Sholom Alechem, Sunday night, May 23rd at 8 P. M.

For technical reasons, this performance was postponed from the 16th to the 23rd of May.

Central Music Hall, Van Buren and Michigan Blvd. Produced by the Literary Dramatic Society.

L. Herman, business manager. Ben Zion Gordin, director.

There is a large demand for tickets.
