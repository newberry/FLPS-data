The Chicago Herald & Examiner of next Sunday (9-7-19) will contain a full page of sympathetically written articles by the most outstanding Jews of Chicago and the country. Articles will be printed by:

Judge Julian Mack. Judge Hugo Pam, Nathan D. Caplan, Samuel Phillipson, Max Shulman, Rabbi Saul Silber, Dr. Stephen Wise, Jacob DeHaas, S. B. Komarko, Mr. Benj. Davis, and others.

(Adv.)
