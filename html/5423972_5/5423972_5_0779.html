The Jewish Historical Society of Illinois met at the office of S. J. Rosenblatt and elected the following officers: President, S. J. Rosenblatt; Vice-President, Jacob. M. Loeb; Vice-President, Judge Harry M. Fisher; Recording Secretary, H. L. Meites; Financial Secretary, Meyer Rosengard; Treasurer, M. E. Greenebaum.

The first efforts of the organization will be to launch a campaign for members to the Historical Society and to publish "The History of the Jews of Illinois," which is being compiled for a number of years and is now nearing completion.
