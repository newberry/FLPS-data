After an absence of about ten years, Mr. Jay Leibner has returned to the Courier as a co-worker on the editorial staff. Mr. Leibner is one of the best-known men in Jewish newspaper circles. He has an eye for local interests and knows how best to present such material to the public.

Mr. Leibner knows Jewish Chicago and its institutions very well, and his return to the Courier will, without doubt, prove to be to the best interests of the Chicago public. His first article will appear tomorrow.
