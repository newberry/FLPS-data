The Lakeside masquerade ball, which took place last Saturday evening, was the most successful ever given by the club. Valuable prizes were awarded to seven members for unique and original costumes.

The Ideal masquerade ball was a success last Saturday night. Prizes were awarded to Miss Jennie Norden, who was masked as "Schatchen," and to Mr. Arthur Alschuler and Jacob Goldsmith, who appeared as polar bears.
