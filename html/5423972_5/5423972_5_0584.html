The Second Annual Jewish Art Exhibit opened officially Wednesday evening, March 18, with a reception for specially invited guests and for the artists. Over one-hundred-twenty-five people gathered for the reception. The program was short but very entertaining and appropriate. Mrs. Sarah E. Greco delighted the audience with specially selected folk songs. Next followed a piano solo by Miss O. Golnick, Rabbi Zonderling spoke on "Jewish Art," emphasizing the importance of Jewish Art and supporting the proposition that there is a specific Jewish Art.

After the program, Russian tea was served from a samovar. Mrs. Eva Ginsburg is the Chairman of the Art Exhibit, which is being sponsored by the Jewish Women's Art Club.
