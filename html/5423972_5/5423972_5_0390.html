Two Chicago children, Miss Gussie Gottlow, pianist, and Master Leon Marx, violinist, are just now attracting a good deal of attention in other cities.

Carl Wolfsohn's series of trio evening was concluded last Thursday at Bournique's Hall. Mr. Wolfsohn has certainly done his share of the work toward the musical advancement of this city, and the five concerts of this season give added emphasis to his services in this direction.

Levy, the well-known cornetist, is organizing a military band for the road.
