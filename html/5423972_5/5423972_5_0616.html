We announce to the Chicago Jewry that the library is already open to the public. The reading hours are from 7 to 11 daily and from 7 to 4 on Saturday and Sunday. The public is invited to visit the Library (of the Hebrew Speaking Society).

Services will also be conducted by a quorum of worshippers on Saturday.

Lectures on Jewish literature and history will be held every Friday evening.

Respectfully,

M. Sider, president.
