In Memory of Jacob Gordon.

Jacob Gordon's best drama - God - Man and Devil, will be presented Sunday night, May 6th, in the Central Music Hall, Van Buren St. and Michigan Blvd. under the auspices of the Literary Dramatic Society.

Tickets are 50¢, 83¢ and 1.10, including war tax. Tickets can be purchased at Forward office 1128 Blue Island Ave., D. Stein, 1012 S. Marshfield Ave., L. Herman, 1309 S. Karlove Ave., telephone, Lawndale 3143, and H. Shusterman, 1505 S. Ridgeway Ave.
