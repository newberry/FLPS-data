The children, whose parents are not members of the Workmen's Circle, are permitted to enroll in the Workmen Circle School.

The schools are so conducted that the children from 8 to 10 yrs. of age are in separate classes from those older than ten.

The schools are located in the three most populated Jewish workers districts in the city.

The children may register at:

Douglas Park district, 1224 S. Albany Ave.

Northwest side district, 2542 Lemoyne Ave.

Westside district, 1006 S. Ashland AVe.
