P.1, Col.3--Today at 2:30 P.M. the Bohemian Central Union of Printers' Trades is holding its regular meeting. The meeting will be held in their hall at 19th St. and Blue Island Ave.

Important business will be discussed, therefore all members are requested to be present. Numerous questions and incompleted business from the previous meeting will be settled also.

(Per) Mr. J. Novak (Secretary)
