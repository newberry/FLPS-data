The movement in opposition to the teaching of German in the Public Schools is getting more support. The Town of Lake Democratic Club in its meeting on the 16th of this month, made known, that it agrees to have steps taken by Chicago Sokols against the teaching of German in the public schools and adopted a resolution similar to the one drawn up by the central committee.

"Tel. Jed. Sokol Praha" (Gymnastic Union "Sokol Prague") also from Town of Lake, as a testimonial of their agreement with the present movement, elected a committee of three members to attend the meetings of the central committee, to support the movement for the abolition of the teaching of the German language in the public schools.
