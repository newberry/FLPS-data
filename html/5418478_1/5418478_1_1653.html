The "Lincoln" Republican club of the Eighth ward held a well-attended meeting yesterday afternoon in the "Ces. Ang. Svob. Skola" (Bohemian-English Liberal School). Lawyer Kriz was the chairman. The subsidiary committee was instructed by the Club to join with the committees of other Bohemian clubs and work for the nomination of some Bohemian on the township ticket.

Citizens Pechots, Kriz, Wallek, Vesely and Holy were elected as a committee for the obtaining of citizenship papers for our countrymen. Those wishing to obtain them are requested to get in touch with the above named committee.
