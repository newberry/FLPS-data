You will learn to speak, read, and write the English language Quicker and more easily in Soukup's School of English, located at 1646 west 18th Street. You may arrange for private instruction, or attend small classes which is a great convenience for you. Tuition for such instruction is very moderate. New pupils are still being accepted. Anyone who does not know English is lost in America. For that reason, do not delay in the least, but apply immediately any afternoon or evening, or Sunday morning.
