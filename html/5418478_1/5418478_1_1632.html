A meeting of the newly created 10th ward organization was held in Cervenka's hall on Blue Island Avenue, for the purpose of giving politics in that ward a new trend. The meeting was attended by about a hundred citizens. The meeting was called to order by Alderman Sindelar. An election of officers was held and the following were elected: Mr. J. Vistein, chairman; Mr. Karel Toman, secretary; Mr. Karel Vlcek, accountant; Mr. M. Krasny, treasurer. Among the speakers were Messrs. Jandus, Sindelar, and Cervenka.
