Bohemian Democrats of the Nineteenth Ward, met on Monday in the Catholic School Hall on De Koven Street, for the purpose of uniting the various Bohemian Democratic Clubs of the above mentioned Ward.

Until now there have been three Bohemian Democratic Clubs in the Ward. After the transaction of regular business, officers were elected. This new club was named the Bohemian Democratic Club of the Nineteenth Ward. After the election of an organization committee, citizen Kostner was recommended as a candidate for County Commissioner.

Country men desirous of obtaining citizenship papers should apply to the following members of the committee: Jos. Kostner, Vac Bartuska and citizen Kalal.
