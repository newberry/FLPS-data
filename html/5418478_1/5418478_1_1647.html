Yesterday's meeting of the 9th Ward Bohemian Republican Club was attended by a large number of our countrymen, who wished to be registered as seeking either first or second citizenship papers. More than 200 persons were registered. Citizen J. Kralovec, in a short speech, explained to those present the benefits of citizenship, after which, current political topics were discussed.

Then the meeting proceeded to the election of delegates to the convention, who will be recommended to the Central Republican Club of the 9th ward for confirmation. Each precinct is to have two delegates. Countrymen seeking citizenship, will be notified by the secretary of the club as to when they will go down town in a body.

This coming Saturday the Central Bohemian Republican Club of the 9th ward will hold it's meeting.
