A total of $29.65 was collected in the Bridgeport district of Chicago by Mrs. J. Zimontas, Mrs. M. Giraitis, and Mrs. M. Damijonaitis for the Lithuanian Relief and Autonomy Fund of Chicago. The following is a list of the principal contributors:

Dr. J. Kulis, five dollars; Dr. A. Zimontas, four dollars; Izidore Kulis, two dollars. The following donated one dollar each: J. Petrauskas, D. Gapshis, J. Skinderis, M. J. Kiras, Dr. K. Drangelis, F. Juozapaitis, B. K, Balutis, J. Vitas, and J. Skutas. Others donated smaller amounts.

A total of three dollars and sixty-five cents was donated to the autonomy section of the Fund. Dr. A. J. Zimontas donated one dollar; others donated smaller amounts.

The money was sent to and received by A. J. Berzinskas, treasurer of the Lithuanian Relief and Autonomy Fund of Chicago.
