Mr. M. Sipavicius, of Lodge 112 of the Lithuanian Alliance of America, collected a total of $12.15 in the Town of Lake district of Chicago for the Lithuanian Relief and Autonomy Fund. The principal contributors were: Mr. J. Bulota and Mr. B. Paszkiewicz, one dollar each; Mr. T. Atraska and Mr. P. Sipavicius, fifty cents each. Others donated smaller amounts.

The money was sent to, and has been received by, Mr. A. J. Berzinskas, treasurer of the Lithuanian Relief and Autonomy Fund of Chicago.
