The following important items were accidentally omitted from the statistical survey of the West Side, or McCormick Lithuanian colony, which was published in the previous issue of the Lietuva: The V. Kudirka Society, which is affiliated with the Lithuanian Alliance of America, and the 334th lodge of the Lithuanian Alliance of America should be added to the non-Catholic mutual aid societies of that colony. The following Lithuanian business establishments of that community were not mentioned: One photographing establishment, A. Zalatoris; and one general clothing store, by M. Palionis.
