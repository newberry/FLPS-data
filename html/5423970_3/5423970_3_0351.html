A section of the Lithuanian Relief and Autonomy Fund was established at a meeting of representatives of the Chicago lodges of the Lithuanian Alliance of America. The meeting was called by the officers of the Second (Chicago) District of the Lithuanian Alliance of America. The section will be known officially as the Lithuanian Relief and Autonomy Fund of Chicago. The following Chicago Lithuanians were elected officers of the section: M. A. Sarka, chairman; F. K. Strzynecki, secretary; A. J. Berzinskas, treasurer; Dr. K. Drangelis, trustee; A. Kundrota, trustee.
