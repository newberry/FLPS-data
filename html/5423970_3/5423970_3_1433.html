A new Lithuanian Roman Catholic Parish is being organized in the southern part of Chicago, in the community in which the Lithuanian (St. Casimir's) Convent is located [67th Street and South Washtenaw Avenue]. The organizers of the parish, as announced in the newspaper Draugas (Friend), are Reverend A. Staniukynas, A. J. Stukis, and Mr. J. Kabelis.
