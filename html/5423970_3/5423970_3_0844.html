Chapter 37 of the Lovers of the Motherland Society sponsored a debate September 28. The question was: "Is the Army Necessary to Humanity?" The necessity of the Army was defended by V. Valiukas, J. Vitkus, and V. Pukas; its worthlessness and uselessness was put forth by A. Sileika, E. Capulis, and V. Rimkus. The judges were Dasita, K. Juozapaitis, and J. Keturakis. They resolved that the army was not necessary.

The meetings of the .... Lovers of the Motherland are held at the Dom Hall, 1750 So. Union Avenue.
