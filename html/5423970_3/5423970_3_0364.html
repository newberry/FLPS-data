The Alliance of Dramatic Societies presented "The Perches" at the new Lithuanian Milda Theater Sunday afternoon.

The various actors revealed their best talents in this performance.

The proceeds of the affair were donated for Lithuanian war relief. Because of this, Mr. Olszewskis, who owns the Milda Theater, made no charge for the use of the theater.

The Sarpalius and Herman orchestras played during the performance. The musicians also donated their services.

It is estimated that about a hundred dollars profit was made.
