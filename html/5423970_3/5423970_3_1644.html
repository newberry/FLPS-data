The Society of the Providence of God will have its 10th anniversary banquet on April 20 at Freiheit Turner Hall, 3417-21 S. Halsted St. All the Lithuanians and all the Lithuanian societies are invited to participate. The societies that will take part in this celebration are asked to come to the St. George's parish hall, 33rd and Auburn Avenue. From there all the societies will march together to the hall. At the hall there will be speeches, declamations and music. There will be a banquet after the program.
