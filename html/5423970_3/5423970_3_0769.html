July 31, the board of the Chicago Lithuanian Societies Association, and other members of this organization, held their meeting. They decided to arrange a banquet for our honorable guests, Dr. J. Basanavicius, and M. Ycas before they leave Chicago. The banquet will be held August 30, at the Sherman Hotel, $2.50 per person. The profit, if any, will be given to the Lithuanian Science Society of Vilnus.
