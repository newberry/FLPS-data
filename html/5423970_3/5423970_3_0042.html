This society was organized in November 1914, in Brighton Park.

The society's aim: to help its members in sickness; to give their dead members the final services; to help their poor families; to work for their nation and church.

There are sixty five members. There is about $2,200 worth of property. Meetings are held in the parish hall, 2745 W. 44th St.

J. Valskis, president, 2651 W. Pershing Rd.

B. Nenertonis, secretary, 6540 So. Campbell Ave.
