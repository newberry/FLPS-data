July 28, the lovers of the Motherland Society, branch 28, West Side, held a meeting regarding the reception of the guests who came from Lithuania. For this purpose, a committee was elected composed of Messrs. M. Duda, and Jankauskas. This committee must come to an understanding with the central Lithuanian Committee of Chicago. It was decided to hold a meeting at the Sacred Heart Parish Hall where the honorable guests will speak on Lithuanian affairs.

It was also decided to hold a family banquet for the reception of these honorable guests.
