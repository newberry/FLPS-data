The July 4th celebration was held at the White Square Park in Bridgeport. Mr. J. L. McBean called the meeting to order and delivered a speech in English. After him, Mr. P. Butkus spoke in Lithuanian concerning American history. Mr. J. J. Hertmanavicia also spoke in English. The enjoyment of the evening was heightened by the Birutes Choir who sang folk songs, and finally some American and Lithuanian hymns. The Lietuva Band played.
