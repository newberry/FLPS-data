In connection with the intended nonpartisan convention, the officers of the Second District (Chicago) of the Lithuanian Alliance of America have taken the initiative in sponsoring a pre-convention conference of all the Lithuanian societies in Chicago.

The Meldazis Hall, 2244 W. 23 Place, has been selected as the place for this conference. According to the plans they have outlined, the conference will take place September 20 at 12:30 P.M. All societies are invited to elect two or three representatives and to send them to the above-mentioned conference.
