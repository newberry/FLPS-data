The Simonas Daukantas Society, with its memorial celebration of the birthday of Simonas Daukantas, historian of Lithuania, has also arranged the society's twenty-first anniversary celebration with speeches, songs, drama, and a banquet. The Drama Society will present the three-act comedy, "The Living Corpse". The Birute Society will sing beautiful national songs. This event will be held October 19, 1913, at St. George's Parish Hall, 32nd Place and Auburn Avenue. The celebration will begin at 5 P. M. The songs, the play, and the banquet will then take place. Admission will be seventy-five, fifty, and twenty-five, cents. All Lithuanians are cordially invited.

The Committee.
