A total of $119.50 was collected at the Lithuanian National Cemetery on April 2 for the benefit of the poverty-stricken family of Bruno Vargsas [Bruno Laucevicius], famous Lithuanian playwright, who died on March 31, after a long illness. [Names of donors omitted in translation.] The money has been given to Mrs. Eugenia Vargsas. [Translator's note: A photograph of Mr. Vargsas' burial appears in this issue of the Naujienos.]
