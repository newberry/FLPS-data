The Town of Lake lodge (Branch 122) of the Lithuanian Alliance of America held a meeting on March 2. A decision was made at this meeting to donate fifty dollars to the Lithuanian War Relief and Autonomy Fund. The Lodge held for this purpose on February 12 a benefit social which produced a net profit of thirty dollars. To this sum the lodge added twenty dollars to make the above-mentioned contribution.

Besides the donation, a committee of three was elected at the meeting to solicit contributions from local businessmen for the relief of war victims in Lithuania. The committee is composed of Mr. P. Mazutis, Mr. Frank Venckus, and Mr. Simanauskas.
