April 22, the Lovers of the Fatherland Society will give a program at the Aurora Hall, 3149 So. Halsted Street. The program will start at 7:30 P.M. Young V. Graiciunas will play the piano; J. Hertmanavicia will read an essay; Young Mr. Sluzinskas will play the piano; Miss Grigaiciute will give a declamation; Miss M. Horodeckiute will sing; Mr. F. Butkus will read extracts from the writings of Dr. V. Kudirka; Miss P. Kriksciokiute will sing; Mr. Vaitekunas will give a monologue; Mr. J. Laukis will read an essay.
