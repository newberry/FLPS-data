Reverend M. Krauciunas, for many years the pastor of St. George's Lithuanian Roman Catholic Church, 33rd Street, and Auburn [now Lituanica] Avenue, was retired on a pension last week on account of old age and poor health. He will reside in his own home in Cicero, Illinois. The new pastor of the parish is Reverend H. Vaiciunas.

During the pastorship of Reverend Krauciunas, the parish built one of the largest Lithuanian churches in America, and also established the largest Lithuanian parochial school in this country.
