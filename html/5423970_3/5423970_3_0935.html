After the examination of the budget of the Aurora society for the year 1905, we found the following results:

From the last part of the year of 1904 and of the year 1905, the receipts were $1,634.22. Out of this amount $1,121.74 was paid out.

In the budget from 1904 there was $962.48. For the year 1905 the receipts were $1,461.04. Paid stipends and various sums for the assistance of the students amounted to the sum of $746.37. Balance in the treasury was $714.67.

All the statements as to where and to whom the money was sent, as well as the receipts, are with the treasurer of the Aurora society.
