In regard to the Lithuanian parish of St. George, in Chicago, the Committee of Six Societies (St. Casimir, the Providence of God, Simonas Daukantas, St. John the Baptist, the King David, and St. Paul and Peter) is calling all the St. George parish members to a mass meeting to be held Sunday, April 23, 1899, at 2 P.M., at Turner Hall, 3417-21 S. Halsted.

All Lithuanians are welcome to this mass meeting.

The Committee of Six Societies
