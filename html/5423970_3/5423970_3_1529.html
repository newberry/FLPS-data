The parade will begin at two o'clock in the afternoon, and, at that time all the societies will be at the starting place between 45th and 47th Streets. The military guards will be in the front line at 47th Street; the societies will be divided into two sections. [Editor's note: A list of the organizations which will participate and their line of march, is included].

All the marshals, soldiers, guards, and members of societies are requested to be on time, to occupy their places, to march slowly, and to watch the lines.

Respectfully yours,

Rev. Alex. Skrypko; Josep J. Elias, parish president.

John Viscont, assistant president.

S. Anuchuskis, assistant president.
