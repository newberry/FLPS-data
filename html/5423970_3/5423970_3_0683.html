The Lithuanian Western Star Benefit Club held a meeting, Feb. 5, at Shattas Hall, 1843 So. Halsted Street. Three new members were admitted at the meeting.

According to a report of the financial secretary, the club is in a sound financial condition despite a steady increase in sickness and a high death rate among the members.

A decision was reached at the meeting to support the present campaign to convert the Vilnis semi-weekly Lithuanian working class newspaper, into a daily. Because a large number of members were at the meeting, it was decided to conduct a Vilnis subscription campaign then and there. Comrade T. Lekas conducted the drive and succeeded in obtaining 30 subscriptions.
