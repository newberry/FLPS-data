The Lithuanian Theatrical and Drama Society, Ruta, will present a four-act comedy, "Stupid George," by Zemkalnis, December 17 at Hull House Hall. [The performance] will begin at 8 P.M. Admission, 25, 35 and 50 cents.

The profit will be sent to the prisoners in Russia, who are imprisoned there for trying to enlighten the people by distributing books and literature to them.
