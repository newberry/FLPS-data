At the Gates of Aurora of the Holy Lady Parish, West Side, on Sunday, July 1, 1906, at 10 o'clock in the morning, the first Mass will be celebrated by the Rev. Joseph Klonauskas.

The following societies will take part in this celebration: St. Roch Society, St. Stephen, the Aurora Gates, and the Enlightenment of Brothers' Society. We are inviting all the Lithuanians to come to this festivity.

K. K. A.
