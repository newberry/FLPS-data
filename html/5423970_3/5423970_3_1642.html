On the north side of this city there was organized a new Lithuanian Roman-Catholic parish under the name of "Holy Lady of the Aurora Gates." With the permission of the bishop of Chicago, the first mass was held on Sunday, March 15, at 11 A. M. in the church of St. Stanislaw Kolegium, Division Street and Holt Avenue. From now on masses will be held there at 10 A. M.

A. Jasinskas, 100 Cleaver St.

Member of Committee.
