The name [of the society] will be: "The Lithuanian Music and Drama Society, the Birutes Society."

Its purpose: "To unite all the Lithuanians who love their national art; to urge the youth to study music, singing and the scenic arts. The Birutes Society will present musical and dramatic programs to revive the national spirit among our people, to make them acquainted with their national art. This Society, as much as possible, will help the students, who have musical, singing and other artistic ability. In general, the Birutes Society's aim is to improve and elevate the national art. The Society's [specific] aim is to acquire its own property."
