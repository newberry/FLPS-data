At a christening in the home of Mr. D. Varnas, 4316 West 37th Place, Mr. T. Zemaitis urged the guests not to forget our brothers and sisters who are suffering in Lithuania on account of the European war. All the guests reacted favorably to his suggestion. A collection netted a total of $4.10. In accordance with the wishes of the donors, the money has been sent to the Lithuanian War Relief and Autonomy Fund.
