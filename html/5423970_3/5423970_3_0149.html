The Christmas Gift for Lithuania Fund, sponsored by the Lietuva, has been augmented by the following donations:

The officers and members of the Simanas Daukantas Building and Loan Association contributed a total of $28.20.

A committee composed of four South Englewood Lithuanian societies sent in $21.23.

Among the individual donors was Joseph Elijosius (Elias), who sent a check for twenty-five dollars.

John I. Bagdziunas, treasurer of the Universal State Bank, Chicago Park Commissioner, and a prominent leader in Illinois politics, also sent a check 2for twenty-five dollars.

To date $1,940.61 has been received. This sum is only sixty dollars short of the two-thousand-dollar goal set by the Lietuva.
