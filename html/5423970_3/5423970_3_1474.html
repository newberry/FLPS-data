P. Mulelis read a manuscript on "The Future of Mankind," Another speaker delivered an address on "The European War" and its probable effect upon the future of the Lithuanian nation."

Rev. A. Lapelis outlined the principles and aims of the Catholic Alliance. He explained the various sick and death benefits that are enjoyed by members of the Alliance. He urged Lithuanians to organize and join only "good" societies such as the Catholic Alliance, the Knights of Lithuania, and the Lithuanian Temperance Society.
