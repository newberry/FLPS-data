The Chicago Lithuanian Independent Club will hold a picnic on Sunday, July 5th, at Bergman's Grove, Riverside, Ill. This will be the first picnic under the auspices of the club. All the profits will be used to maintain, improve, and enlarge the Lithuanian-American library which was recently established by the club.

The picnic will start at 9A.M. There will be good music, under the direction of E. Baksis. All kinds of refreshing drinks, aromatic cigars, and delicious sandwiches will be on sale. Various contests for both men and women, with prizes to the winners, will also be held. Everybody is cordially invited to come.
