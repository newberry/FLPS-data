The organized Chicago Lithuanian Evangelical Lutheran Parish of Zion intends soon to build its own church, according to information given us by K. L., the secretary of the parish. The lots, as previously announced, have already been purchased. A sum of $1,150 was paid for them. It is planned to start the building of the church this year. The pastor of the Zion Parish is the well-known Reverend J. J. Razokas.
