Branch 25 of the Lithuanian-American Nationalist League held a meeting last Friday in Milda Hall, 3140 South Halsted Street, at which the annual election of officers took place. The following were elected: Joseph Giraitis, president; Dr. K. Drangelis, vice-president; B. Lenkauskas, secretary; M. J. Damijonaitis, treasurer.

Several new members joined the Branch, which now has about forty members.

Plans were discussed at the meeting for raising funds for the Lithuanian Independence Fund.
