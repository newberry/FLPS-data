The above society was organized in August, 1913, in Brighton Park.

The society's aim: To help her members in sickness; to give their dead members the final services; to help their poor families; to work for their nation and church.

The first organizers of the society were: Stanislomas Anusauskas, Fr. Stasiulis, J. Tamasauskas, Juozas Maciutis and others.

There are about one hundred members. There is about $3,500 worth of property. Meetings are held in the parish hall, 2745 W. 44th St.

J. Gajauskas, president, 4525 So. California Ave.

G. Pakeltis, secretary, 4550 So. Western Ave.
