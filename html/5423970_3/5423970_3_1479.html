Reverend A. Briska has been appointed pastor of the new Roman Catholic parish which is being formed by Lithuanians living in the vicinity of Kedzie and California Avenues, between 35th and 49th Streets. In the meantime, until it has a place of its own, the new parish will hold all its services in St. Ann's Church, 38th Place and California Avenue. The name of the new parish is "The Immaculate Conception".
