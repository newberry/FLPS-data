The Lithuanian Evangelical Lutheran Society of Chicago will hold its fifth annual grand ball on Saturday evening, Oct. 31, at the Vladimir Klacel Hall, 19th and Leavitt Sts. The doors will be opened at 7:30 P. M.; dancing will begin at 8:00 P. M. and continue until 4:00 A. M. the following morning. Admission is twenty-five cents per couple.

Besides dancing, there will be interesting talks by prominent Chicago Lithuanians, and singing by the vocal artists of the fourth branch of the Lithuanian Socialist League. Everybody is invited by the committee.
