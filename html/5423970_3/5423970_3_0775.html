The Birutes Choir Society held a meeting, April 7, at Aurora Hall, 3149 South Halsted Street. Mr. P. Sarpalius stated that he would resign as instructor and director of the Birutes Choir.

The Birutes board decided to write a letter to Mr. Paul Ciurlianis, in Brooklyn, N. Y., and invite him to come to Chicago, to be the instructor and director of the Birutes Choir.
