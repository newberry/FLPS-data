Mr. V. Kalinauskas, S. Saulis, Miss T. Kislauskiute and Miss J. Zubriute have collected and delivered to me thirty-six dollars and twenty-one cents for the benefit of the imprisoned Lithuanian strikers, V. Katilius, and V. Stackus. Of this money, twenty-two dollars was given to the wife of striker V. Stackus.

Stasys Strazdas.
