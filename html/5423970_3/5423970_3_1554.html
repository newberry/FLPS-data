October 29, the Rev. P. Drignaitis, from Naugatuck, Conn., presided at divine service of the Lithuanian Lutherans in Chicago. October 30, a meeting was held by the Zion Lithuanian Lutheran parish, and Rev. P. Drignaitis delivered a lecture, discussing the origin of Christian dogma. [He also spoke of] protestantism and its influence upon the people. Rev. P. Drignaitis is a progressive. He wants to see the Lithuanians reach a higher level of intellectualism, and he urged the Lithuanian Lutherans to uphold Lithuanianism.
