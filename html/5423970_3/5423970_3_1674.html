The Providence of God parish has its temporary church at the Irish church, 19th street, between Halsted and Peoria streets. The holy mass is at 7 A. M. on week days, and at 11 A. M. on Sundays. The parish priest lives at 166 W. 18th St. Lithuanians having affairs in baptism, nuptials, etc., should go to the said address of the priest.

The Parish Administration.
