Chicago, March 14. The Liberty Society held its meeting. At this meeting it was decided to donate $10 to the Siberian martyrs for Lithuanianism. This society is urging the other societies to do likewise for the cause of the martyrs in Siberia. The society supports and votes for the nominated committee composed of Dr. John Szliupas, for president; Szernas for secretary; and A. Olszewski for treasurer.

Let the committee decide to whom the money shall be donated.
