[Since] the Prussian Lithuanians do not live in one place but are scattered in various parts of Chicago and vicinity, they ask us to publish the fact that Evangelical Lutheran divine services will be held at the following places: June 23, at noon, in the church at 25th Place and Canal Street; June 29, at one o'clock, 643 West 31st Street, and in the afternoon at the church on Lake Street, Waukegan, Ill.; July 4th at 612 North Horsman Street, Rockford, Ill.; July 6, at 643 West 31st Street, Chicago, at 10:00 A. M. After the divine services, parish meetings will be held.
