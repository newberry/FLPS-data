Last Sunday, at the Czechs Hall, corner Center and 18th Streets, the Liberty Society held its celebration of the displaying of its new Lithuanian flag.

At this celebration took part the Dr. Kudirka Singers Society. They sang three songs. The speeches were delivered by Dr. Szliupas, Mr. Masionis, Mr. Bradckulis, Mr. Bijanskas and Mr. Petroszius.

There were about 500 people in the hall including reporters from American newspapers.
