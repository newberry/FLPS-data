February 6, 1904, there was organized a New Lithuanian society named Keistutis. (Keistutis was the Grand Duke of Lithuania in the latter part of the 13th century-translator's note.) The purpose of this society is to help the sick to perform theatrical plays, and to carry out national activities. The next meeting will be held at Mr. J. Kovarkis hall, 1193 South Oakley Avenue.
