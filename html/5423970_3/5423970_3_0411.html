The local Simano Daukanto Society had a meeting March 3, at which, among other things, it was decided to aid Lithuanian strikers at Lawrence, Massachusetts. The Society appropriated twenty-five dollars from its treasury for this purpose, and members volunteered a collection of $5.50. In that manner a total of $30.50 was raised. The money was sent to A. Ramanauskas' address at 101 Oak Street, Lawrence, Massachusetts.

The other Chicago Lithuanian societies ought not to forget their brothers on strike, many of whom find themselves in a critical situation.
