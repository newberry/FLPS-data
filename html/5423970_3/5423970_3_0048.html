The above mentioned club was organized on November 12, 1918. Its purpose is to help members in case of sickness and to give the last services to deceased members.

The membership monthly dues are thirty-five cents. To the beneficiaries of a deceased member each member of the club pays one dollar, and $50. is paid from the club's treasury.

A sick member gets $5. per week. At present the club has twenty-seven members. The budget is about $2,000.
