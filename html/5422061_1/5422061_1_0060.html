p.2................The summer school will begin July 7. Al Hungarian children are welcome, but those whose parents are not members of the Church (652 East 92nd Street) will pay a tuition fee of $2.00 for the semester, also 50¢ for books. Children of members are taught free, only the 50¢ for books is charged each pupil.

If there are enough registrations we will establish a school in West Pullman also.
