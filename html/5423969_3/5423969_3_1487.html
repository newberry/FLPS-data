The Evangelical Lutheran Home Finding Society of Illinois held its annual convention at St. Paul's Church, North Avenue and Leavitt Street, last week. Reverend G.A. Gullixon presided. It was reported that the Society had bought the building next to the home at 4840 Byron Street. About forty children were cared for during the year. The Society has shown improvement again this year. The following statement will give the complete figures:
