The president of the orphanage reports steady progress.

Ten of the children have graduated to high school and thirteen have been confirmed. The work on the farm belonging to the home is bringing good results, and everything indicates a rich harvest. The debt on the home and the farm is paid. The whole property is now value at $45,000.

A shute given to the orphanage by the "The Friday Club" is being enjoyed greatly by the children, and our warmest thanks are due the club.
