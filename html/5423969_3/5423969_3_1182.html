The fourteenth annual celebration of the Deaconess Home and Hospital began last night. The program opened with graduation exercises for six deaconesses.

The program included hymns by the congregation; prayer by Reverend C. K. Solberg; songs by the Sisters' Quartet, William Klemm, and Mrs. H. B. Kildahl; and speeches by Dr. A. Holmboe, Adolph Larsen, and the rector.
