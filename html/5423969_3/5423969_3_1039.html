p.8...Nora lodge No. 1, Knights of the White Cross, is the oldest local in the entire order. On Wednesday they served a fish supper in their hall at North and Western Avenues. Several other locals sent representatives and the spirit of comradeship ruled the day. Mr. Solvesen, Arch Knight, has recently organized Nordkap lodge in New York City, and B. Pedersen represented that lodge at the supper.

Nora lodge will celebtrate the 50th anniversary of its founding in another two years.
