Nye Norge (New Norway), a Norwegian ladies' benevolent society, celebrated its fifth anniversary at Wabansia Hall on March [gap]. The committee had arranged a very good musical program by Sverre Rian, Miss Thea Olsen and Miss Jenny Torrison. The society now numbers seventy members, and during its five years of existence, it has paid out $775.[gap] in sick and funeral benefits. A tasty lunch was served and all were well pleased with the entire evening.
