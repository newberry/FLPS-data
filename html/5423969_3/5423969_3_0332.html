One of the things that will insure the success of the new "Norwegian Women's Club" is the fact that many of the "Colony's" leading people are interested in building up this organization.

Many of the people active in promoting its literary policy are well qualified for this task.

We will mention a few: Mrs. Julia G. Walther, Mrs. Scholdager-Armbruster, Mrs. Marie Wingard, Dr. Ingeborg Rasmussen, Mrs. Inger Scholdager, Mrs. Marion Wold, Miss Marie Ruud, Dr. Marie Olsen, and Dr. Helga Ruud.
