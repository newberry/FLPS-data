The Dramatic Club Bjorgvin will entertain tonight, giving a second presentation of "Til Saeters" (To the Saeter). This farce received thunderous applause when first presented by Bjorgvin, and one may be quite sure that the audience tonight will enjoy the play no less. In addition to the play, there will be music by Miss Anna Fosterad, singing by the ladies choir "Grieg," and finally dancing. The entertainment begins at 8:00 P.M.
