Clara Semb is considered an outstanding authority on the Norwegian folk dance. She and her group have been asked to perform at the All-American Exposition. Her program will be as follows: 1. Song Dans [Song dance]. 2. Spring Dans [Hop dance]. 3. Firtur [A square dance]. 4. Gammel Rhinelaender [Old Rhinelander].

Miss Semb has made a name for herself as the outstanding exponent of the folk dance.
