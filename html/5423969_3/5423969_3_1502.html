The Norwegian Lutheran Children's Home Society held its regular annual meeting at St. Paul's Evangelical Lutheran Church. Reverend Lars Harrisville presided.

At the end of 1913, 81 children were inmates at the Home; 28 of these were admitted during 1913. During the past year 39 children found permanent homes and were "checked out" of the orphanage.

The Home employs 6 attendants, 2 teachers, a hired boy, and a matron. The income for the Home was $16,377.79, including cash on hand as of January 1, 1913 amounting to $910.53. The expenses were $16,324.54, leaving a cash balance of $53.25.
