The Norwegian Lutheran Orphanage Society held its regular quarterly meeting last Tuesday in the St. Timothy Church, corner of 43rd and Dickens Avenues. Reverend Lars Harrisville presided. The chairman reported on the activities of the Orphanage....

The chairman proposed the construction of a new and modern building at a cost of $2,000, and the purchase of the adjoining three acres of land for $1,500.

It will be fifteen years on May 11 that the first Orphanage was officially opened.
