A number of open forums have been arranged at the University of Chicago by the Scandinavian Club of the University. Three forums [will be devoted to] Swedish lyrics; Clement Shaw and Adolf Hult will be the speakers [at two of these; the third speaker has not been announced]. [At another forum] Professor H. Larson will speak on "Glimpses of Scandinavian Art." Mrs. Gudrun Thorn-Thompson will be heard on "Norse Myths and Fairy Tales."

Other speakers, to whom no subject has been assigned, will be D. E. Wahlberg, Professor Arnoldson, and Dr. T. Foss.
