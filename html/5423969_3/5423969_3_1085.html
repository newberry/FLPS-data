The annual convention of the Royal Order of the Knights of the White Cross opened at Dania Hall in Chicago, today. The meeting was called to order by the Grand Duke, J. A. Arntsen, who then appointed a credentials committee consisting of Charles Lake, W. T. Brown, and one substitute. Several lodges were represented. [A list of the lodges represented at the convention is given.]

The officers constituting the executive board of the Grand Lodge are: J. A. Arntsen, Grand Duke; W. T. Brown, Grand Earl; Charles Lake, financial secretary; John Gran, treasurer; John Hellesvig, chaplain.

The minutes of the previous meeting were read and accepted, and the session was adjourned until the afternoon.
