The Singing Society, Harmony, had a full house last Saturday evening for its musical and dramatic entertainment.

The Singing Society, Hamlet, of Racine, Wisconsin was represented by thirty active singers. Under the leadership of their director, Mr. T. Elberg, they gave several numbers with great success. Similarly the joint choruses of the Hamlet and the Harmony Societies reaped great applause and had to give several encores.

As a closing number of the program a comedy "When We Living Ones Fall Asleep," was presented. The comedy was well presented and was very funny. The evening entertainment closed with dancing, and the entertainment was declared a great success by those present.
