The Norwegian-American Hospital gave a banquet and festival for the new graduate nurses. The program for the evening was as follows:

1. Organ preludium Mrs. Cecilia K. Munsen
2. Address Reverend O. Ingvolstad
3. Song, "Festival Te Deum" St. Paul Quartet
4. Piano solo Amanda Jorgensen
5. Songs, "Where My Caravan Rested" and"Morning" Hester Hoskins
6. Violin solo, "Cavatina" J. M. Dybdahl
7. Address J. U. Fowler
8. Piano solo Amanda Jorgensen
9. Songs, "Come, for It Is June" and "Harmony" Hester Hoskins
10. Violin solo, "Serenade" J. M. Dybdahl
11. Song, "Rock of Ages" Quartet
12. Presentation of diplomas Joachim G. Giaver
