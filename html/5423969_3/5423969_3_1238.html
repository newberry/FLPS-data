The Tabitha Hospital case is now before the courts. The case against Mr. Stoltenberg came up in Judge Tulley's court Monday afternoon, but a continuance was granted until next Tuesday. The attorney for the complainant is Mr. Frank A. Johnson; the attorneys for the defense are Johan Waage and Clarence Darrow.

Darrow proved to the satisfaction of the court that neither Dr. Carl Sandberg nor anyone else had started the movement to oust the directors representing the Synod. It is expected that a decision will be made in the case Tuesday when Clarence Darrow and Frank A. Johnson will sum up the case.
