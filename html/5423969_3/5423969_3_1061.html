Humboldt Tent #26, K. O. T. M., at its last meeting received 135 new members at Northwest Hall, 692 West North Avenue.

The Lodge now numbers 2200 members, three fourths of whom are Scandinavians.

After the meeting a banquet was held, three hundred of the members participating. Honorable D. P. Markey was guest of honor. Dr. G. M. Schaubel was toastmaster.
