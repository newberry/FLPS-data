The Scandinavian Brotherhood is a Scandinavian sick and death benefit society. The Brotherhood recently initiated five hundred new members into its eleven Chicago lodges at an affair held at the Coliseum last Sunday evening. The ceremonies were led by Chief Justice Harry Olsen [Swede].

The Brotherhood has eighteen thousand members throughout the United States. The national president of the Brotherhood is also the district president of the Chicago lodges, R. C. Hansen, [Dane].
