The Minde (Remembrance) Ladies Aid Society recently celebrated its nineteenth anniversary in Wicker Park Hall. The president, Mrs. Valborg Lund, opened the meeting, speaking briefly on the success of the Society. Mrs. Lund has been president for nine consecutive years. [This Society pays out funeral benefits only.]

The assets of the Society include: cash in the bank, $1,161.02; loans on property, $1,300; and cash on hand, $78.02--a total of $2,739.04.
