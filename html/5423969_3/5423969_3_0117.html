A Norwegian concert was given last Sunday afternoon and evening at Humboldt Park House. The audience was large and the program excellent. Mr. M. Pedersen was in charge of the program, and a number of works of Norwegian composers were presented, such as "Wedding March," by Emil Bjorn; "March", by H. Thinn; "Turner March," by Adolf Hansen; "Norway Home Echoes," by J. R. Hold; "Norwegian Peasant Life," by Holst; "Potpourri" (Norwegian March), by Alfred Paulsen; "American Military March," by M. Pedersen.
