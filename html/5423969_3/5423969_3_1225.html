The complete returns of the election of officers to the Tabitha Hospital Society show the following results: Dr. Marie Olsen, president; Mrs. Valborg Lund, vice-president; Miss Bella Olsen, secretary; Johan Waage, treasurer; executive committee: J. Waage, chairman; Mrs. W. Johnson, Mrs. K.M. Hagland, [Matthew] Pedersen and Dr. Marie Olsen; tenants' committee: Miss Bella Olsen, Dr. Valborg Sogn, and Mrs. M. Thompson.
