Several smaller publications by Norwegian-Americans have recently been put on sale. The Eighth Annual of Snofoika, by the Norge Ski Club, is on sale, being the most attractive issue to date.

Dr. Sandberg's The Money Trust has already passed the 3,000 mark. This book is well written and contains authentic information, which makes its reading worthwhile. Dr. Sandberg has been for many years an ardent student of political economy and monetary systems, being considered by many authorities as a reliable writer on matters of finance and banking.

There are rumors that a new paper, Journalen, is to be launched in Chicago, with Finn Rein Simonsen, the Norwegian-American violinist and writer of lyrics, as editor.
