The Scandinavian Medical Society held its regular monthly meeting on Thursday evening, March 11. Dr. T. Warloe functioned as president. Dr. Cornelius Gundersen gave an interesting lecture on ileus and reported on a case of this illness. Dr. E. E. Hendersen, Dr. W. J. Anderson, and Dr. N. Remmen participated in the discussion following the lecture.

Dr. N. Oldenborg told of his experiences with massage of adhesions in the posterior cavity, and reported several cases in which the patients had been completely healed by means of this type of treatment. A discussion ensued in which Dr. Hendersen and Dr. Gundersen took part. The meeting adjourned when this discussion was concluded.
