The Scandinavian-American Medical Society honored Dr. Ludvig Hektoen at a banquet held last night at the Swedish Club. Dr. Anton Holmboe functioned as toastmaster, and Dr. Anders Doe was the principal speaker of the evening. Dr. Thor Rothstein, spoke for Upsalla [Sweden] University; Dr. Suenning Dahl [Dane] spoke for the University of Copenhagen; Dr. Dawson spoke for the American Medical Society; and Dr. Nils Johnson, spoke for Mrs. Hektoen.
