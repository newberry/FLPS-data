The Sons of Norway Lodge held its annual convention a few days ago. The secretary reported that 31 new lodges joined the supreme body, and that the membership was increased by 1,640, making a total of 10,940. The cash on hand, July 1, was $109,842.16, compared to $72,347.66 for last year. C. J. Golee, of Chicago, was elected supreme vice-president.

An important resolution was passed which adds the Canadian flag to the Norwegian and American flags on the supreme charter.
