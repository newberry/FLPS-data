Yesterday the Norwegian Orphanage was dedicated. Thousands of people were present at the ceremony. The will of the late Mrs. C. Jevne was read; she has bequeathed five thousand dollars to the Orphanage.

The collection taken up at the ceremony netted $352 in cash and $177 in subscriptions. A bill of $4 was presented and paid. The collection, together with cash on hand of $560.59, brings the Orphanage Society's cash fund up to $1,093,59.

Among the speakers were Dr. N. T. Quales, Reverend N. J. Ellestao, A. D. Erile, and Reverend C. K. Solberg.
