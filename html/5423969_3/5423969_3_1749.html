The Bethlehem Branch of the Norwegian Old People's Home Society held their regular monthly meeting last evening. The vice-chairman, Jacob Helgsen, presided.

The recording secretary reported as follows:

Membership, January 1, 1911 77
Died in 1911 1
Resigned 5
Members joined in 1911 31
Total membership, January, 1912 102
Dues payments $245.75
Expenditures 9.00
Balance $236.75
