The concert of the Norwegian Singers' League, last Wednesday, in Bjorgvin Hall drew a capacity crowd and was both an artistic and a financial success. Such outstanding artists as Mme. Ragna Linne and Gustaf Holmquist participated, and also the promising baritone John W. Colberg, Jr. The large orchestra was directed by Math Pedersen, and the singing societies appeared jointly and individually.

The proceeds of the concert go the fund for the erection of a memorial on the grave of the great singer, John W. Colberg.
