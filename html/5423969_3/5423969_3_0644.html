Snorre Sturlasson, Norges Monge-Sagaer eller Heimskringla (The Sagas of Morway's Kings or Heimskringla) (Chicago: Skandinaven, 1901). This book was translated from the Icelandic by Professor P. A. Munchs and S. Ryghs. It contains twenty full-page, half-tone illustrations.

The book begins with the legend: "Thor's Battle with the Goblins," including a part of the interesting viking period up to and including Haakon Haakonsson and Helge Hvase. It also contains a short biography of the author, Snorre Sturlasson, written by Professor Munchs.
