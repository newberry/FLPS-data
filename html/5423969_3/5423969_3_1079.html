The Bethlehem Sick and Death Benefit Society presented one of the finest programs heard in Chicago for some time. The festival was held in the Bethlehem Church, corner of North Center and West Huron Streets.

The program was as follows:

1. Organ preludium by Miss Amanda Bjertnaes.

2. Welcome sermon by Reverend G. T. Rygh.

3. Orchestral selections, G. Paulsen, director.

4. Solo by John Rasmussen.

5. Church quartet, J. H. Rasmussen, director.

6. Report by the secretary, Mr. Lange.

7. Solo by Miss Anna Christensen.

8. Lecture by Reverend L. O. Foserum.

9. Orchestral selections, G. Paulsen, director.

10. Solo by Miss Anna Christensen.

11. Selection by the church quartet, J. H. Rasmussen, director.

Such well-known musical critics as Reverend J. A. Meyer and Miss Ragna Linne have given their approval of Miss Christensen's singing.
