To friends and admirers of Bjornstjern Bjornson, we bring good news. At the low price of twenty-five cents, they may now obtain a wonderfully instructive book of which this great man is the subject. The great chief, Norway's "Uncrowned King," as he is affectionately called, is herein pictured in his true character, and anyone reading the book will know Bjornson in all his greatness as never before. The book is on sale at Lund's Book Shop.
