The quarterly meeting of the Norwegian Orphanage Society was held last Tuesday evening in Bethel Church, Humboldt Boulevard and Dickens Avenue. The president, Reverend Lars Harrisville, reported that none of the children had been ill during the past three months.

Another teacher has been added to the staff.
