During the sports tournaments yesterday in Kosciuszko Park, the Norwegians won the first prize in the 880 yard race and second prize in the 440 yard race. A Norwegian also won second prize in distance jumping.

In Melrose Park the first prize in a bicycle race of fifteen miles went to a Norwegian. Also in rowing did the group of Norwegians more than held their own. The rowing took place at Lincoln Park. The various competitions in sports were international in scope, and multitudes of people were present.
