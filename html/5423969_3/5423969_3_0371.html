Lord of souls, I grant Thy power

Over gifts bestowed on me.

Show but mercy in the hour

When my song shall cease to be!

For my heart is trembling ever

Untold thoughts are resting never,

Pondering life's eternal riddle.

Take away the singer's garland,

Fling it to oblivion's shore;

If but in that vernal far land

Thou my voice improved restore;

If but there Thy throne before us,

I may join that lofty chorus

Swelling to the harps of heaven.
