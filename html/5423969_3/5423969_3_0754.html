p.2............The Norwegian Tennis-player, Miss Molla Bjurstedt, who holds the American championship; won Womens Single Metropolitan Championship at West Side Club's Courts Friday. Instead of the usual championship title and price Miss Bjurstedt will receive a certificate, issued by United States Lawn Tennis Association, as all the funds of this organization have been donated to the Red Cross.

Miss Bjurstedt defeated Mr. C. B. Hitchins 6 - 3, - 6 - 1, and was superior to her opponents at all times, winning twenty-three sets to Mrs. Hitsen's fourteen. Play for the Double Championship will start Sunday.
