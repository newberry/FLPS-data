The last presentation of Norwegian films for this year will be held in Orchestra Hall Sunday. There will be two films:

"Herr Arnes Penge" (Arnes Money), by Selma Lagerlof; "Presteenken" (The Pastor's Widow), by Kristofer Jansen.

The Northland Trio will sing popular Norwegian Songs. The new concert star, Julie Moser-Sporr (Swedish), and Karl Sporr (Swedish), the violinist, will take part in the program.
