The Norwegian Singing Society had its first "Tivoli Night" last Thursday night with a full house. The singers in America sing better than any singing society in Norway.

They showed St. Peterburg at night, the yellow house, the yellow fair and the city of Moscow. The Turner club Sleipner gave a fine exhibition. And Madame Newman Passner proved herself to be a fine singer. The price of admittance was 25 cents.
