"Skandinaven is planning to present a survey of the activities of Norwegian congregations and societies during the past year. May we therefore request that you kindly submit to us a brief account of the more important events within your organization during 1907?"

The above quoted letter has been sent to all Norwegian churches and societies in Chicago. If any organization has been overlooked, please consider this paragraph a personal request.

Please mail the report at your earliest convenience, and make it as brief and at the same time as comprehensive as possible. A brief outline of your organization's plans for the current year is also desirable.
