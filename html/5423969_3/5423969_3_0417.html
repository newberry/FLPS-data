The Scandinavian Medical Society held their monthly meeting on March 14th, at the Swedish Club rooms.

Dr. A. Holmboe delivered a lecture on what a physician and surgeon can do in the treatment of stomach ailments. Drs. Oyen and Anderson took prominent parts in the discussion which followed. The officers of the society were instructed to consider the idea of celebrating the 25th anniversary of the society, and to report at the April meeting.
