The Norwegian Society for a Home for the Aged held its fifteenth annual meeting last Friday in the Wicker Park Evangelical Lutheran Church. Anton A. Melum was elected chairman of this meeting. The first order of business was the yearly report by the president, Dr. N. T. Quales. Thirty-four men and women were admitted to the home in 1910.
