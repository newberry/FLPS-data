The annual meeting of the Bethlehem Sick Benefit Society was held yesterday in the Bethlehem Church, corner of North Center Avenue and West Huron Street. The vice-chairman called the meeting to order and pointed out that although the Society had been fairly active, there was much room for improvement. The secretary then followed with his report.

The report of the financial secretary was as follows:
