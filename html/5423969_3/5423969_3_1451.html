The Embla Club gave a concert last night for the benefit of the Norwegian Lutheran Children's Home. The program was as follows:

1. Welcome Speech; Hans E. Holman.

2. Song; Male Quartet.

3. Solo; Ella Bergh.

4. Recital; Ruth Dahly.

5. Solo; Edna Moe

6. Violin and Piano; Sigrid Hansen.

7. Speech; Reverend Ingvoldstad [now camp pastor at Ft. Sheridan].

8. Song; Male Quartet.

9. Solo; Jennie Andersen.

10. Solo; Ruth Dahly.

The Embla Club gives a benefit concert every year for the Children's Home.
