p. 208. In 1888 "court Normania," a social, benefit organization was formed and federated with Ind. Ord. of Foresters, until it became insolvent thru paying too many death premiums by 1899.

Survivors organized a "tent" and joined Knights of Maccabees.

p. 209. In 1893 the Norwegian Sick-Benefit Society, "Nordlyset" was organized.

p. 211. Skandinavian Women's Burial Benefit Association, organized 1879 with 10¢ initiation fee and 2¢ per week dues.

p. 213. Bethelem Congregation Sick & Aid Society formed 1893.

p. 213. "Enigheden", a Woman's Club & Benefit Society formed 1905.

p. 214. "Liberty Band" musical and social club organized 1904.
