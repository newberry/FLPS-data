The Norwegian Lutheran Orphanage Society held its annual festival at the Bethany Church last Friday evening.....Reverend Hetland, president of the Society, explained to those present the objectives of the Society. The speaker has been in the movement since the very beginning.....The Home was dedicated one year ago and during the past year its debt has been reduced, so that only $1,500 remains unpaid.

The festival was well attended and the program was excellently presented.....A donation party will be held at the Home on Saturday, May 19.
