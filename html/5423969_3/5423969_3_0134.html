Bjorgvin Singing Society never forgets to celebrate on February 5, Ole Bull's birthday. It was celebrated in the society's own hall. Mr. Richard T. Sartz was toastmaster. In his speech he made a proposition to raise a monument here in Chicago for Ole Bull. The suggestion received great applause.

At six o'clock a great surprise was given the party. The Northern Light Band whose members are all Norwegian, gave a serenade to honor Ole Bull's memory.
