The Bethlehem Sick Benefit Society held its seventeenth annual meeting last Monday in the Bethlehem Church, Central Avenue and Huron Street. The president, Haakon Thompson, called the meeting to order. The minutes of the previous meeting were read.

In the past year, twenty-eight sick claims, totaling $495, and burial assistance amounting to $140 were paid--that is, a total of $635 in benefits.
