Last Sunday, June 25, the Polish baseball teams of St. Vincent, and St. Leon XIII, met on the field located at Elston and Kedzie Avenue. It was a one sided battle, because the unbalanced and inexperienced team of St. Leon being no match for the powerful team of St. Vincent. Only teams representing Polish parishes can become members of this Polish Catholic baseball league.

All parishes are asked to organize teams and join, there is no entry or admission fee.
