Mr. T. M. Helinski, honorary president of Kosciuszko Statue Commission in Washington D. C., received a letter from the secretary of war, a member of the commission for that monument, which reads:

1038 Fine Arts Bldg.

Chicago

March 12, 1908.

Kosciuszko Statue Commission

Washington D. C.

Dear Sir:

I take pleasure in announcing that we, the committee, visited the studio of Mr. Antoni Popiel, yesterday afternoon, and inspected his full-size model for the Kosciuszko statue. We found it an admirable work, realizing well the promise of the sketch which is throughly worthy of your acceptance.

Respectfully submitted.

Charles J. Mulligan,

Lorado Taft,

Ralph Clakson.
