Yesterday afternoon at the baseball field located at Grand Ave. and Sacramento Ave., the Argo A.C., undefeated Polish baseball team, met the Mulveys, and the result was the most spectacular and hardest fought game this season. The score was Argo A.C. - 6 - the Mulveys - 4 -.

The game was marked by many beautiful plays; the most impressing plays and stops were made by Vincent Golubski, shortstop for the Argo A. C.
