The annual All-Slavic athletic contest will be held on May 25, from 3 p. m. to 11 p. m. at Davis Square Park, 45th Street and S. Marshfield Avenue. Polish Falcons No. 2, Jednosc Society and the well-known Polish choirs, Wisla and Druzyna will take part in this contest, as will Slovaks, Bohemians, Lithuanians, and Ukrainians. The most interesting part of the program will be the Polish dance Mazur performed by sixteen dancers.
