We have received an announcement calling a political meeting for tomorrow evening at Schutz's Hall.

Since the announcement is signed with the words "The Committee" only, and inasmuch as we do not know who compose this committee, for upon investigation we have succeeded in finding the name of only one of its members, the appeal is not published in full.

We wish only to inform all those who are interested that the appeal invites the officers of all Polish clubs of Chicago to be present at the first general meeting on July 25, 7:30 P.M., at Andrew Schutz's Hall.

This appeal is sent by Albert Nowak, 265 North Western Avenue.
