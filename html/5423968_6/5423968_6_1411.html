Polish members of the Chicago police force have organized a Kosciuszko Monument Club among themselves for the purpose of raising funds for the erection of a monument to honor the famous Polish hero Kosciuszko.

At a meeting on January 26, in addition to electing the Club's officers, it was decided that the Club would sponsor a ball at Shoenhofen's Hall on April 16, the entire proceeds of which to be contributed to the Kosciuszko Monument Fund. Five thousand tickets have been printed and our gallant policemen hope to sell them all, which would mean at least $1,500. The project is a commendable one.
