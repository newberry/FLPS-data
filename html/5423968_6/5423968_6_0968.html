Classes in the Saturday Schools conducted by the Women's Section of the Polish National Alliance, are held at the following places:

Town of Lake: Davis Souare Park, corner of 45th and Paulina Streets.

Holy Innocence Parish: Park No. 1, at Chicago Avenue and Noble Street.

Avondale: At the home of Mrs. Mroz, 2885 Milwaukee Avenue.

Classes are held every Saturday from 1 P. M. to 4 P. M.
