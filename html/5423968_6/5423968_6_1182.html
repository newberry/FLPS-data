Gospodarz--The Farmer No. 1 for 1906, just out, contains discussions of "Modernistic Adelbert," "Learn Husbandry," "Practical Advisor," "Home Doctor," "Home Hearth," remarks and practical advice for farmers also a poetry and literature section.

The publisher of this useful periodical is S. J. Napieralski, 1513 W. 22nd St., Chicago, Ill.
