The Polish physicians of Chicago, at their meeting of January 26, resolved to send the following telegram to President Wilson:

"To Honorable Woodrow Wilson,

"President, Washington, D. C.

"We, the members of the Polish Medical Society of Chicago, after reading your speech of January 22 before the Senate, wish to express our heartfelt gratitude for the kind reference you made therein in regard to freedom for a United Poland.

Dr. W. A. Kuflewski, president;

Dr. A. Pietrzykowski, secretary"
