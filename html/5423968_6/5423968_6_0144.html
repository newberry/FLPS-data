Contributions to the Library

Nineteen volumes, including several plays and a copy of "People's Handy Atlas of the World" were donated to the library by various persons during the month of November. In addition to these, the library received three almanacs and eleven brochures on various subjects.

Among the other books donated was a twelve-volume set of "A Thousand and One Nights", "Illustrated History of Poland", the first half year's volume of "The Illustrated Weekly", [cultural periodical published in Warsaw] 1887, and "The Album of Napoleon's Victories".

The directors of the library extend their sincere thanks to each of the donors.
