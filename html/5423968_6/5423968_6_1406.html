Up to the time of this writing, $4,100 has been collected for the erection of the Kosciuszko monument in Humboldt Park. The sum of $25,900 more is necessary. Can it be that the million and a half (sic.) Polish population of Chicago will be unable to collect this sum in time to erect the monument for the hundredth anniversary of the Kosciuszko Insurrection? That would be a disgrace.
