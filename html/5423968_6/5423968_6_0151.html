On Sunday, Aug. 13th, a picnic will be held at Kolzeg's Park in Dunning, Ill., for the benefit of the library and museum of the Polish National Alliance in Chicago.

Such picnics are held annually and are usually a great success. The proceeds of the picnics are turned over to the general library and musuem fund, from which new books for the library and exhibits for the museum are purchased. The library of the Polish National Alliance located in Chicago is one of the means of enlightening the Polish public.
