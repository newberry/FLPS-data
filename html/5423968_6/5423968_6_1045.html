A lecture on Poland will be given next Friday at 8 p. m. by Professor A. Janowski, at the Polish Roman Catholic Union hall under the auspices of the Polish National Alliance. The purpose of the lecture is to acquaint our Polish community with the most outstanding and noteworthy memorials, and picturesque views of our motherland, which will be shown on the screen with the aid of 100 slides. Such lectures were favored with a large attendance on previous occasions and it is expected that on this one too, the hall will be crowded to capacity.

To defray expenses, an entrance fee of 10 cents for regular seats, and 25 cents for reserved seats will be charged.
