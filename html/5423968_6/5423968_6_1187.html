During the annual convention of the Polish Falcons in South Bend, Indiana, bicycle races were also held.

Stanislaus Paczynski of South Chicago took first place in the ten-mile event by breaking the tape in 30 minutes and 20 seconds.

Of the various entrants the following received awards (ten-mile event):

The first to reach the tape was S. Adamkiewicz of Chicago, 30 minutes and 40 seconds (handicap, 2 minutes and 30 seconds); awarded gold medal.

Second place, J. Malewicki of Chicago, 31 minutes and 15 seconds (handicap, 2 minutes and 30 seconds); silver medal.

Third, J. Wojtecki of Chicago, 33 minutes and 13 seconds (handicap, 4 minutes); \2two tires contributed by Morgan & Wright.
