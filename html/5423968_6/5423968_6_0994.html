The annual examinations of girls and boys attending the Sisters of Nazareth School on Division Street took place last week in the presence of their parents and many priests.

The [results of the] examinations were truly marvelous and astonished everybody.

Particularly the examinations in Polish history, geography, literature, and Polish language were most satisfactory and commanded the attention of all present. The written examinations and handiwork were also excellent.

The public graduation exercises will be held in a few days, We will announce the date.

Tomorrow we shall publish a list of the students who deserve special credit for their wonderful showing.
