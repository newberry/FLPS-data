Heralded a long time ago, Dyabel, presumably a humorous newspaper, at last made its appearance today.

We have had an opportunity to look over the first issue. It is a satirical sheet of scandalous tendencies, which has no right to exist in Chicago.

Its special characteristics are soft, red paper, abominable orthography, a complete absence of sense or style, and millions of idiocies.

Stanislaus J. Seegers is editor of the Dyabel.

This newspaper will undoubtedly end where it started--in the gutter.
