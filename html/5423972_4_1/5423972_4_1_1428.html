Mr. B. Horwich, receiver for the Ashland and Twelfth Street Bank, will begin to pay a dividend of twenty per cent to all depositors today.

Mr. Horwich asks the depositors to bear in mind that today is not the only day on which dividends will be paid to depositors, but today is the first day. Payments will be made at the Blue Island Savings Bank, every day and evening until everybody receives his money.
