The L. D. Brandeis Zion Society will celebrate its fourth anniversary on Sunday, December 5, at 6 P. M.

A musical program has been arranged, in which Mr. Anshelovitz, the great tenor, who recently arrived from Europe will sing the best Zionist and folk songs. Chrideman and Wise, violinists, will participate in this affair.

Mr. Max Shulman who just arrived from the convention will address the meeting. Refreshments will be served.
