Dr. Joseph Zeisler, dermatologist and professor in the medical school at Northwestern University, died on Sunday. The deceased was born in Austria, sixty-five years ago.

He was the author of many lectures on dermatology. Dr. Zeisler was attending dermatologist at the Michael Reese, Wesley, and Mercy Hospitals.

He was also a member of the Cliff Dwellers, the Chicago Literary, and the City Clubs.
