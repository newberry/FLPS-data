Alexander H. Heyman is the Progressive candidate for State Senator. He is a law partner of Francis W. Walker. For the past fifteen years, Mr. Heyman has been active in the B'nai B'rith. He is ex-president of the David Fish Lodge, and a member of the Grand Lodge.

He was chairman of the Political Action Committee of the Marquette Club and is Past Sachem of the Tawawa Tribe of Redmen. At present he is a member of the Hamilton and Chicago Progressive Clubs.
