The Capital Hardware Manufacturing Company, incorporated, 3757 Wentworth Avenue has been organized. Capital is $10,000. The founders are Morris Nathanson, Samuel L. Cohen, and Philip E. Loeb.

The Henry Candy Company Stores, 4708 West 22nd Street, have been organized. Capital is $24,000. The organizers are J. Berlin, Harry Strulowitz, and Julius Segal. Samuel Micon, 127 North Dearborn Street, is the correspondent.
