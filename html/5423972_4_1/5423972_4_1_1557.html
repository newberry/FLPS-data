On December 28, at 3 P. M., the Chicago Civic Opera Company will present Hansel and Gretel with Miss Edith Orens and Miss Helen Derzback in the title roles. Both are young Jewish girls, who are making their debut in opera.

Miss Orens is known on the West Side, where she makes her home, as Miss Orenstein, and Miss Derzback resides on LaFayette Parkway.
