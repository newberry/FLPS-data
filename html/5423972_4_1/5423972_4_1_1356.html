Marcus Marx, one of the founders of Hart, Schaffner & Marx, died on August 4, in his 81th year. He was born in Germany, and came to America when he was 11 years old. He settled in Chicago in 1879.

Mr. Marx was a director of Hart, Schaffner & Marx, a member of Sinai Cong., and the Standard Club, and also a director of the Chicago Home for Jewish Orphans.
