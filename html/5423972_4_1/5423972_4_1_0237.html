Corporation Counsel, Samuel Ettelson, who is also State Senator, declared that when the State Legislature will assemble, January 5th, he will introduce a bill to have Judge David dismissed from the Superior Court of Chicago on account of discharging a suspicious character through a written "Habeus Corpus."

Judge David claims that the police have no right to arrest a person and hold him in jail, on suspicion or on account of his being a former criminal.

Judge David is being defended by the other judges.
