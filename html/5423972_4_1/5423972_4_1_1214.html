Dr. W. L. Baum was born at Morris, Illinois, in 1867. He studied medicine in this country and in Europe and in 1891 engaged in practice in Chicago as a specialist in skin diseases. He is treasurer and professor at the Chicago Post-Graduate Medical School and dermatologist at the Baptist, German and Chicago Hospitals. He is also the secretary of the Chicago Medico-Legal Society and the treasurer of the American Urological Association. He is a member of many organizations, including the Chicago Academy of Medicine, the Illinois State and the Chicago Medical Societies. Dr. Baum is also fleet surgeon of the Chicago Yacht Club.
