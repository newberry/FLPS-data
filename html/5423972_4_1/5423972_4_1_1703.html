"The September issue of the magazine 'Music' contains a well written and thoughtful article by Mr. Maurice Aronson of the piano faculty of the Chicago Conservatory of Music and Dramatic Art. Mr. Aronson is a teacher of uncommon ability, as well as a valuable addition to the meager list of many-sided musicians, who not only possess ideas, but also have the power to give them expression."

--From "Presto," a weekly journal devoted to music.
