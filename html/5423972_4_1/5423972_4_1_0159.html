Mayor Dever has appointed Samuel E. Pincus to the important post of City Prosecuting Attorney.

Mr. Pincus was born on the West Side and has lived in Chicago all his life. After graduating from Northwestern University with a degree of L. L. B., he was admitted to the bar.

He was Assistant Attorney-General under Patrick J. Lucey and attorney for the Industrial Commission of Illinois under Attorney-General Brundage. Mr. Pincus is a member of the law firm of Bear and Pincus.
