Drs. P. M. Le Mee and G. Illengworth Helie, French physicians attached to the American Hospital at Paris, arrived in Chicago this week to confer the French Legion of Honor on Dr. Isaac A. Abt, specialist in children's diseases and attending physician at the Michael Reese and Chicago Lying-In Hospitals. Dr. Helie made the presentation as recognition by the French government of Dr. Abt's work.
