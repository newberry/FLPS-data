The four-year-old struggle between light and darkness, between freedom and oppression, nears its conclusion. The hearts are beating, brains are impatient, and souls are aroused at the sight of this fifth and last act of the horrible bloody drama. We will soon witness the great peace act of this historical tragedy, which will seal the fate of the future generation, bring victory for freedom, victory for all oppressed and persecuted nations, victory for this country of liberty, and victory for the Jewish people.

The heroism of a group, nation or race, is shown not only in the struggle alone, but also in the patience of carrying the struggle through, in the strength of holding out till the last moment.

Now, in this critical moment of the universe, in this fateful hour, when the lot of mankind is being decided, it is the duty of every American citizen, of every American Jew to make the greatest sacrifices, sacrifices of blood and gold, in order to secure the victory of freedom.
