Judge Harry M. Fisher, for many years connected with law work in Illinois, has been reelected as Judge of the Circuit Court. In 1904 he was admitted to the bar. He was elected to the Municipal Court in November 1912 and reelected in 1918.

Judge Fisher was the author of the Juvenile Court Act of 1907, the Pandering Act, the Wife and Child Abandonment Act and the co-author of the Feebleminded Act.
