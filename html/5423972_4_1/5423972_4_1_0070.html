A Citizens' Campaign Committee to support the candidacy of Albert W. Cohen, who is running for the Republican nomination for lieutenant governor, will be organized tonight in room 303 of the Morrison Hotel. The most prominent citizens among the Chicago Jewry have become interested in the candidacy of this young Jewish man. A very strong campaign will be carried on in his behalf by this organization. Mr. Cohen is a West Sider who has been very successful in stock and bond transactions. Mr. Cohen's personal friends are very active in lending aid to his election.
