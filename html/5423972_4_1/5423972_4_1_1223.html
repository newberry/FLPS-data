Dr. Jacob Frank was born in Syracuse N. Y., in 1858. He has held various posts of honor in this city. He has been a surgeon in the German Hospital, the Marion Sims Hospital, St. Elizabeth, Columbus, and the Cook County Hospital.

He is a member of the Chicago Medical Society, the Illinois State Medical Society, the Chicago Surgical Society, the Chicago Pathological Society and other medical fraternities. He is recognized as one of the leading surgeons, not only of Chicago, but of the country.
