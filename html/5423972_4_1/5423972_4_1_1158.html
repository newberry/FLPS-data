Oscar S. Caplan has been promoted to assistant attorney of the Sanitary District. He has served as assistant city prosecuting attorney for the past three and one-half years.

Mr. Caplan is secretary and treasurer of the West Town Lawyers' Association. He is also a member of the A. F. and A. M., Silver Link Lodge No.909, I. O. O. F., Brady Chapter of the American Legion, and has been very active in Jewish and English journalistic work.
