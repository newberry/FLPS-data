The musical debut of Miss Bessie Birdie Kaplan is scheduled for Wednesday, May 31.

Her piano-recital will be given at the Fine Arts Music Hall, 203 Michigan Blvd., under the direction of Mr. Samuel B. Garten.

Ever since she was a mere-child, Miss Kaplan has given promise of a brilliant musical-career. She has, on more than one ocassion, earned enviable recognition and praise for her skill on the piano.

Miss Kaplan is the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. H. Kaplan who live at 3258 Douglas Blvd.
