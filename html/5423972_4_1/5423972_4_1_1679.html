Mr. Jerome S. Blum, of 4828 Grand Blvd., has arrived in Chicago from Peoria, where he has lived for the past five-years. Although he has specialized in portrait work, Mr. Blum has been very successful in winning for himself and his studio an enviable popularity as a painter of landscapes. He is a member of the Salon and his paintings have been honored at every session. For the purpose of exhibition, Mr. Blum is bringing many of his best work to the States.
