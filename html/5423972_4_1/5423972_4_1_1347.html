This modern three-story department store is located at 1613-21 W. Chicago Ave. Goldblatt Bros. started business seven years ago with a small 25 cents store at 1607 W. Chicago Ave. Due to their continuous growth in business, they have opened a department store with a 100 ft. frontage and 50 additional departments including a large drug department. An elevator was installed to carry 30 passengers. This new elevator is of modern construction with automatic Safety stop and locked doors to protect the passengers.

A squadron of six new delivery trucks is part of the new equipment. Free deliveries will be made in the city and Oak Part, and Evanston.
