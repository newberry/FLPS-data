Emil Reichel, seventy-two years old, of 1106 South Ashland Boulevard, died yesterday in the North American Hospital, after an operation. The funeral will be held tomorrow.

Mr. Reichel was a partner of Mr. Samuel Laudani. Together they founded and managed the Vienna and Vilno Sausage factories. Mr. Reichel was a businessman of great integrity and honesty, and won the friendship and respect of all those who came in contact with him. He was also a great philanthropist, and he was to go to Europe soon [on a mission], but an abscess formed behind his right ear and he was taken to the hospital. The operation was not successful. and he died.
