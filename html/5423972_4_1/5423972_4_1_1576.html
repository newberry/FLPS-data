For the first time in Chicago will the Lovers of Music and Hebraic Melodies listen to a concert, featuring 100 Cantors in the Medinah Temple.

This unusual concert is given this Sunday for the benefit of the orphans of the Douglas Park Day and Night Nursery, who are supported by that institution.

The noted conductor, Leo Leoff, came to Chicago to direct this concert. This alone is the best assurance, that is, will be one of the most interesting concerts ever heard by the Chicago Jewry.

The noted organist, Herr Verdritz will also participate in this concert.
