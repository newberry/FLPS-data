The series of three concerts to be given by the Quartette of the Chicago Musical College, under the direction of S. E. Jacobsohn, will take place at Kimball Hall on the following dates: April 28, May 5, and May 14. Season tickets will be sold for the series.

A piano recital will be given by Ch. F. Balatka, May 13, 1891.

The artists' recital given at Central Music Hall last Monday, under the auspices of the Amateur Musical Club, was one of the most brilliant events of the season. Madame Fannie Bloomfield Zeisler, Pianiste, and Mrs. Walter Wyman participated in a very interesting program.
