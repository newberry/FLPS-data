Dr. David Lipshultz was born in Mitow, Kurland in 1864. He studied music in Mannheim, Germany, under Gross Kapel Meister Ferdinand Langer and Karl Isenman, music director to the German Kaiser. In 1884 he came to Chicago.

He is a great musician and has composed many songs. Among the best-known are "Only Grief," and "Auditorium Concert Masurca." He was choir leader at the B'nai Abraham Congregation for eighteen years, and has composed many Jewish songs. He is at present sales manager of Stephen M. Meyers Piano Co.
