The well-known philanthropist and welfare worker, H. M. Barnett, received many felicitations yesterday upon the occasion of his entering into a new business with another well-known welfare worker N. H. Bolotin.

The two will be the only sales agents of the Sinai Kosher Sausage Company products. They have their office at 805 Maxwell Street. Mr. Barnett was in this line of business some years ago, and is well known as an experienced merchant.
