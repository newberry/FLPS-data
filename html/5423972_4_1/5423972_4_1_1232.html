Mr. Julian W. Mack was appointed by Mayor Harrison to the civil service commission. Mr. Mack occupies a chair of law in the University of Chicago. He will continue both his practice and his teaching at the University. He has been a member of the Civil Service Reform League, but has not been actively engaged in its work. Mr. Mack is the secretary of the Associated Jewish Charities. He has been engaged in the practice of law since 1880.
