You can obtain Red Cross Savings Stamps in our bank. With these coupons you can pay for the junk that you buy. You are thus helping yourself along as well as saving the lives of our wounded soldiers and sailors. Show your patriotism, prove that you stand with America in this terrible struggle for justice and freedom.

Every peddler who buys a book containing five dollars' worth of coupons will receive two "honor" card signs for his wagon.

Schiff and Company State Bank,
