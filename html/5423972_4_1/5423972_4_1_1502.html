This well known citizen, both of Milwaukee, and this city, passed away at his residence, on Monday last, at the age of sixty six years. Mr. Leopold was born in Baden, Germany, and came to the United States, and at his demise had been a resident for nearly forty years.

At Milwaukee, he was popularly known as the "Steamboat King." The deceased was largely interested in the Lake Michigan and Lake Superior Transportation Company, which interest, he sold to his surviving brother of the firm of Leopold and Austrian, whose docks are on North Water and Rush Street bridge.
