Martin J. Isaacs is the Democratic candidate for Judge of the Superior Court. He was born in 1871, studied law, and was admitted to the bar in 1891. He served as a member of the law department of the World's Fair.

In December 1912, Mr. Isaacs was appointed Master in Chancery. He was also a member of the Board of Managers of the Bar Association for three years and, during the War, served on the War Committee which handled the legal problems of the soldiers and sailors and their dependents.
