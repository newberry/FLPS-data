Solomon P. Roderick is a candidate for reelection as member of the State Legislature from the 19th District.

Mr. Roderick has been instrumental in the drafting and passing of important laws. He sponsored the bill compelling the Sanitary District to install bridges across the canal at Crawford, Cicero and 72nd avenues. He advocated and voted for the constitutional convention proposition. He assisted materially in the passage of the law to regulate private banks.
