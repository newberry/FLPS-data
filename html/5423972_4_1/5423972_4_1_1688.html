Prof. Simon E. Jacobsohn, one of the America's best known violin teachers, died on Oct. 3rd. The deceased was a native of Mitau, Russia. He was 63 years of age. Before coming to America, Prof. Jacobsohn was one of Europe's most famous violin virtuosos of his time. Prof. Jacobsohn came to Chicago in 1886.

His method of instruction was recognized in Europe as embodying all features of the violinist art.
