It will do nobody any good to hide himself if Uncle Sam should be in need of soldiers. Everybody must do his duty. But, if the war should end before Uncle Sam has time to re-enforce his army and navy, nobody will have to go to war.

It is our belief that the United States will have no time to send battalions across the ocean. But she must be prepared. The German press is of the opinion that the rumor in America about an army of one-half million men is a bluff. We will gain a lot in saving peace if we will prove that we are not bluffing.
