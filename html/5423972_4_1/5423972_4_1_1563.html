Tonight the first performance of grand opera in Jewish at the Ariograta Temple, 8th street and Wabash avenue! Tonight and tomorrow night:

The Jew In Spain

will be presented by a cast consisting of Morris Dalskaia, Jascha Rosenthal, and Mme. Westgate Dubin, Leon Gold, and Samuel Simon, with a special choir of 50 men and women, under the direction of Paul Lemkoff. This is the first opera played in Jewish in Chicago.
