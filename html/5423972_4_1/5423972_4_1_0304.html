Jacob H. Mark is the Republican nominee for the city Clerk He was born in this city and comes from an old Chicago famuly. His father, Jonas Mark, was in the dry goods business on the North side for 35 years.

Mr. Mark entered the government service in the Money Order Dept. for the past ten years he has been in the County Clerk's office. Four years ago he was promoted to minute clerk of the board of Commissionaers.
