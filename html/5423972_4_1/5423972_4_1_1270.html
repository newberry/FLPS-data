Julius Eli Levin, president of the North Side Sash and Door Co., died on April 3, at the age of 45. He was born in Russia, February 2, 1881, and came to Chicago with his parents in 1889.

He had been engaged in business as a mill-work and sash door manufacturer, the only one in Chicago.

Mr. Levin was a member of the B'nai B'rith, the Masons, and the Covenant Club.
