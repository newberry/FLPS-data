Mr. Max Blumenfeld is the Republican-candidate for County Commissioner. He was born in Roumania and came to Chicago in 1884. He has been employed by Butler Bros. and James H. Walker & Co., and in 1888 he went into the grocery business. He was appointed a deputy clerk in the Circuit Court of Cook County, a position which he still holds.

Mr. Blumenfeld is a member of the B'rith, Sons of Benjamin, and other fraternal orders, and is well known throughout the city.
