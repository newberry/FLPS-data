An editorial entitled "Jews and Peace" in today's Herald-Examiner indicates the fact that official records of Army and Navy Departments list two hundred thousand Jews who fought in the American Armies, this number being 5% of all American soldiers; the Jewish population in the United States being only 3% of the entire population.

They contributed 60% more in comparison to their proportion; therefore, the voice of American Jews against pogroms should receive a definite hearing in America. So says the Herald-Examiner.
