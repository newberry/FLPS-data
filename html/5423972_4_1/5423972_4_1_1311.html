Charles Kaufman, chairman of the board of directors of Charles Kaufman Brothers, Chicago, one of the largest manufacturers of men's clothing, died on Monday. He was born seventy-eight years ago in Germany, and came to the United States in 1866.

In 1871, Mr. Kaufman started a retail clothing store on Archer avenue, and developed a chain of retail clothing stores in the middle west. Thirty-five years ago he began liquidation his retail interests and developed the wholesale clothing business.
