Judge Julian Mack, the Democratic candidate for reelection as judge of the Circuit Court, has been endorsed by the United Societies, the Bar Association of Chicago, and the University of Chicago.

Hon. Abel Davis, recorder, in a recent address said that politics could not interfere at this time. Judge Mack's untiring efforts for the Jews at all times, his interest in Jewish charities and his prominence in seeing that laws were enacted that would be favorable to Jews, deserves the support of each and every coreligionists.
