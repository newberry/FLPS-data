Julius Howard Miner, one of Chicago's youngest attorneys, has been appointed Master in Chancery in the Circut Court of Cook County. Mr. Miner is 28 years of age, and has practiced law for 7 years.

Mr. Miner took an active interest in the Jewish People's Institute, West Park No. 2, and other settlements. He was one of the organizers and officers of the Young Men's Hebrew Association, and the Marks Nathan Junior Auxiliary.
