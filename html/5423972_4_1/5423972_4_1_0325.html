Henry Horner Jr. is the nominee for South Town collector on the Democratic ticket. He is 26 years old, was born in Chicago and has resided here all his life. His family is one of the oldest in the city, having come here in 1847.

Mr. Horner has practiced law for about four years, prior to which he spent four years in the study of law. He is associated with Frank A. Whitney under the firm name of Whitney and Horner, and they enjoy an enviable reputation in their profession. Their business consists principally of commercial and real estate practice.
