Samuel Ladanyi, pioneer sausage manufacturer, died last Monday. He was born in Budapest, Hungary, June 1863, and came to Chicago in 1893, during the World's Fair, where he opened a lunch room, and had later branched out in the manufacturing of sausages.

Mr. Ladanyi organized the Vienna Sausage Company, one of the largest of its kind in the country, and later the Vilo Sausage Company.
