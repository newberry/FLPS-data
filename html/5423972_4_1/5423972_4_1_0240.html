Michael Rosenberg a native Chicagoan, who is a candidate for a delegate to the Constitutional Convention, is a product of the Lawndale district.

Mr. Rosenberg is a member of the following organizations:

Wabasha Lodge No. 160 A. F. and A. M; Ramah Lodge I. O. B; Illinois Sportmen Club; and Covenant Club. He is also a director of the Marks Nathan Orphan Home, and the Home for Incurables of Oak Forest.
