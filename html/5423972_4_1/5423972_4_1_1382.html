The house of Samuel Philipson and Company (wholesale merchandise), in order to show its appreciation to its employees for their loyalty and co-operation in making the business a success, has provided them with life, health, and accident insurance policies, and will pay the premiums for them. Every employee will always be insured to the extent of his yearly salary, with the exception of those receiving more then $5,000 a year. For every new-born child, the employee will receive a gift of $1,000. He will get an increase in salary according to the number of years he is employed; and increase of five per cent after a year of service, and up to ten per cent for those employed more than five years.
