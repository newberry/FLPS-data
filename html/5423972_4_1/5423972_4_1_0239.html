When the rabbis of Chicago stepped out publicly against the cheap politics of the Jewish Courier, who wanted to deliver the entire community into the hands of a group of politicians, the Courier threatened to take revenge on these rabbis and drive them from the city.

Rabbi Saul Silber of the Anshe Sholom Congregation is one of the rabbis, whom the Courier wanted to drive out.

The congregation has just renewed his contract for three years and has raised his salary from one thousand dollars to four thousand and five-hundred dollars annually.

This illustrates how the Courier is driving out the rabbis from Chicago!
