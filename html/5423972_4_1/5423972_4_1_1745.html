Emil Liebling gave a piano concert last night at the new Kimball hall. The program included works by the following composers: Beethoven, Chopin, Raff, Emil Liebling, Schavwenka, Moszkowski, Lack, and Schytte. Mr. Liebling played the selections with his usual rare finish and brilliancy. Ludwig Marum and Theodore Reichman gave a violin and song recital this Friday evening at Central Music Hall.
