Dr. Bolotin, blind physician, has for four years been the examining physician at the Northwest Dispensary, in connection with the Municipal Tuberculosis Sanitarium. He is a lecturer on diseases of the heart and lungs in the Chicago College of Medicine and Surgery, Jewish Medical College, and in the nurses' training school at Francis Willard Hospital.

Dr. Bolotin is one of the founders and a member of the board of directors of the Lawndale Civic Center. Dr. Bolotin has been further honored with an appointment as a tuberculosis physician by the City Civil Service Commission.
