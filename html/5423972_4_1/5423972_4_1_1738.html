Emil Liebling's "Romance Poetique," dedicated to Fannie Bloomfield-Zeisler, and "Romance Dramatique," of which Harrison M. Wild is dedicatee, have just been published by S. Brainard's Sons Company.

A Grand concert by the advanced pupils of the Chicago College of Vocal and Instrumental Art, was given at Kimball Hall, Monday evening. The program embraced a fine collection of miscellaneous musical works.
