Mr. Solomon Golub will appear at Temple Zion, Odgen and Washington boulevards, November 17, in a recital of folk songs, composed by himself.

The evening is arranged by the Temple Zion Jewish Literary Society.

Mr. Golub has, for the past few years, entirely devoted himself to Jewish music. His task and object was to revive the Jewish folk-songs by creating suitable music for it.

In creating his Jewish compositions, Mr. Golub delved deeply into the soul of the Jewish race. Mr. Golub also writes the words to his music.
