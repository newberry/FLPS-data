Norman S. Florsheim, retired Chicago manufacturer, died on January 31, at the age of sixty-four. He was born in Chicago.

In 1882, he started in business with Sweet, Dempster and Co., cap and hat manufacturers. In 1894, he joined his father, the late Simon Florsheim, in the firm of S. Florsheim and Co.

Mr. N. S. Florsheim was made president of the company in 1908, when it was incorporated under the name of the Kabo Corset Co., and he continued as president of the firm until he retired from business in 1924.

He was a member of the Standard and Ravisloe Clubs.
