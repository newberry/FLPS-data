Comrade Samuel Holland, candidate of the Socialist Party for the 7th Congressional District which covers the Jewish Northwest side, was born 1892 in the small town Lutzen. He was raised as an Orthodox Jew; but in his young days he was attracted to the socialist movement. Comrade Holland came to America in 1905 with the Young Russian Jewish Revolutionary immigrants and became a worker. While working he studied law and graduated as a lawyer. He is a member of the Executive Committee of Socialist Party of Cook County.
