Mr. Joseph J. Merensky has been appointed Assistant United States District Attorney. He was born in Chicago, December 24, 1888. He attended the Chicago Kent College of Law and was admitted to the bar in 1912.

From 1909 to 1912 he was Secretary to Jens Jensen, former superintendent of West Parks. During the war he was assigned by the Liberty Loan Committee to factories and fraternal societies and to Moving-Picture Theatres as Four-Minutes Man and also served as legal advisor to the 81st District Exemption Board.

Mr. Merensky is a member of Welfare Lodge No. 991, A.F. and A. M., Beaconsfield Lodge, 448, K. of P.
