Collections for the unemployed have been received in the church of St. Stjefen, and brought a sum of $240. Gifts are received daily. Our relief organization is working hard in order to meet the great need. This organization is planning to open branches.

Headquarters for this relief organization have been established at 1852 W. 22nd Place, Chicago.

Chicago and vicinity are showing deep interest in this noble action.

Branch managers are asked to send their reports daily in order to coordinate the fast growing and complicated work.
