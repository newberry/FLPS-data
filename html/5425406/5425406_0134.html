We say it is news! Our newly organized Slovenian Catholic Dramatic Club gave us its first performance, and it was good! For a long time we did not have such opportunity to enjoy our native plays and hear our songs sung and we left the premises with a feeling that we spent a few delightful hours in our dear old country.

This performance brought us so close together that even our deadly enemies forgot their bad feelings. We felt that we were brothers and belonged to the same race and nation.
