At last we got good news that our hope came true, our Community House is almost ready to be open. We all know the great importance of having such a house. Our children will have airy rooms to play in; we will enjoy meetings with our friends; and at this house we will attend performances of our musical, singing and dramatic societies.

It is really a surprising fact that so many of our people cannot realize the priceless value of such enterprise and are refusing to subscribe to the Building Fund. We cannot improve our life unless we will readily donate for the educational organizations.
