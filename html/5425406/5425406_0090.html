The Organizing Comittee of the United Jugoslav Socialist Organizations in Chicago announced plans for a May-Day Manifestation, which will be held in co-operation with Bohemian organizations on May 1.

All organizations willing to participate in this manifestation should send their representatives to Bohemian Hall, "Spravedluost" at Loomis Street and Blue Island Avenue, from which place the parade will begin to move.

A11 organizations are urged to take part in the projected parade and to attend the mass-meeting following.
