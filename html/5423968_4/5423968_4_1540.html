Last Wednesday, February 1, a meeting of the board of directors of the Polish-owned White Eagle Dairy was held in Rusch's hall, 4800 South Wood Street. The following officers were elected: Michael Czworniak, president; Michael Zaczek, vice-president; Thomas Kozubowski, treasurer; and Louis Nowakowski, secretary. The following men constitute the board of directors: Messrs. Joseph Kukulski, F. Zaworski, S. Wollek, J. Wieduch, and F. Galecki. The attorney for the company is Mr. Stanislas Witmanski, brother of the Reverend Peter Witmanski of St. Josaphat's Church.
