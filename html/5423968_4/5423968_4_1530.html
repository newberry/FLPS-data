Recently a new building and loan association was organized, the Julian Building and Loan Association, located in the real-estate office of Mr. Bruno Mindak, 1400 North Ashland Avenue, corner Blackhawk Street, second floor.

All wishing to become members of this newly organized Association are asked to come to the meeting which will take place on Tuesday, February 22, at the above-mentioned address. At this meeting the officers of the Association will be elected. [Editor's note: February 22, 1922, was Wednesday, not Tuesday.]
