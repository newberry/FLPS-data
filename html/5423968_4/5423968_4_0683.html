Honorable Woodrow Wilson,

President,

Washington, D. C.

American citizens of Polish blood deem it a privilege to express to the President of the United States their administration for his masterful presentation to the whole world of the question of the establishment and preservation of peace, based upon equality and justice. They also desire to express to you, Mr. President, their profound thanks for the reference relating to the necessity of recognizing a free and united Poland as one of the conditions necessary to the establishment and maintenance of peace.

Please accept personally our love, admiration, and gratitude, and as President of the United States, our loyalty and devotion.

2

[Translator's note: These parentheses are part of the particle, not my own addition. The article does not name the organizations represented by the Central Polish Relief Committee.]

P. S. Individual Poles and Polish societies may send copies of this telegram in accordance with the appeal of the National Department of the Polish National Alliance, adding only their signatures and addresses.
