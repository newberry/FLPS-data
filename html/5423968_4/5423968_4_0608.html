Upon learning that a state of war exists between the United States and Germany, the Executive Committee of the National Department [of the Central Polish Relief Committee] sent the following telegram to President Wilson:

"The Poles organized in the Central Polish Relief Committee and its National Department, on the occasion of the announcement of the existence of a state of war between the Republic of the United States and Germany, hasten to assure you, Mr. President, of their deepest loyalty and their willingness to place at your disposal all the strength and resources at their command.
