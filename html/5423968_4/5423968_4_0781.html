Mr. John F. Smulski received the following telegram from Colonel Galezowski, Paderewski, and Styka, from Bordeaux, the temporary capitol of France:

Bordeaux, France, September 22, 1914.

2:08 P.M.

John F. Smulski

1201 Milwaukee Avenue

Chicago, Illinois

Compatriots send twenty thousand Poles to fight the Teutons. Have them land in England to join the British army in France. All our Pole sin France have already joined the colors. Show yourselves worthy of Kosciusko and Pulaski. The sacred cause of the homeland calls you.

Colonel Joseph Galezowski

2

Ignace Paderewski

Fortunat Strowski

Jan Hyka (Styka)

Villa Tade Garches
