It seems that the only firm that carries garments for the dead is the South End Burial Garment Shop.

This particular shop is located at 2959 E. 88th street and was established by a Pole by the name of J. Zacharias, in April, 1934, and is growing very nicely. The shop has a complete supply of garments for the dead, for men and women; also other necessary articles used for burial purposes.

Mr. J. Zacharias employs four people.
