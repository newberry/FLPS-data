Not by words alone, but by deeds as well, the people of Chicago support Wilson's stand in regard to Germany's predatory actions. The citizenry are showing their patriotism not only by speeches made at numerous gatherings and mass meetings, but by actions in the direction of energetic preparations for war. In case of need, Chicagoans will stand squarely behind Uncle Sam's Star-Spangled Banner in the struggle against foreign invaders.

It is the duty of every citizen of the United States to fight for international rights, in other words, his rights, which the Teutons violate. Those who cannot join the ranks to fight against the enemy should support those who can by helping them morally and financially.
