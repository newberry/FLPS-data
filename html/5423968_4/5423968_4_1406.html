A meeting of Polish technicians was held on Sunday, December 29, 1918, and after a prolonged discussion a new organization was formed under the name of Polish Technicians' Association. The new officers elected are: K. Szymanski, M. E., president, and R. Turczynowicz, M. E., secretary.

It was also decided to organize a trade school for members, under the management of K. Szymanski, M. E. and under the supervision of the association. In order to start the school members present made a voluntary loan to the association, of $10 each, which brought a sum of $300.
