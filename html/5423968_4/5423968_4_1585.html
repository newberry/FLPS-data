One of the oldest Polish dairies in the vicinity of St. Casimir's parish is the Consumers Dairy Company, 2216--22 South Sacramento Avenue. This dairy was founded by Sylvester Jaglowski in 1891. It has been in existence over thirty years.

The present officers of the company are:

Peter S. Jaglowski, president and cashier, Sylvester Jaglowski, vice-president, A. Jaglowski, secretary, Anthony Furmanek, assistant secretary and manager.
