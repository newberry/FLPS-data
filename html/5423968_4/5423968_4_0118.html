All Polish national organizations have combined with the aim of selecting a program to greet our friend and military genius, Marshal Foch of France, an honor that should not be neglected by any one of us.

Marshal Foch is coming to America at the request of the World War Veterans' organizations. He will be their guest during his entire stay.

It is our ambition to make this glorious fete in honor of this very dear friend a never-to-be-forgotten occasion.
