Just recently, Thomas T. Tomczak passed the state examinations for embalmer and undertaker. Mr. Tomczak was born in Chicago, November 14, 1892. He attended the Polish elementary school at St. Casimir's parish. Later, he attended the public schools. He received his professional training at John Spry School of International Anatomy and Embalming.

Mr. T. T. Thomas is the son of Mr. & Mrs. Francis and Josephine Thomas, old settlers of St. Casimir's parish. He has opened a modern office at 2232 South Sacramento Street.
