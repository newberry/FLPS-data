We have learned that Dr. Florian Znaniecki, who came from warsaw in 1914 and who has been working up to the present with Professor Thomas on a book on the psychology of the Polish people, was appointed Professor of the recently established Chair of the Polish Language at the University of Chicago. In addition to his lectures on Polish culture, he will give a cycle of popular lectures on Poland and her people.

We shall publish a more detailed program of Professor F. Znaniecki's lectures and a more detailed personal description of the first Pole in the Windy City to hold the chair of the Polish Language at the University of Chicago.
