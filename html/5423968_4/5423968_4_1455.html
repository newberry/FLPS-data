We wish to inform our esteemed public that we have organized and opened a new law firm under the name of Beilman, Czarnecki, and Koralewski.

Having long been convinced that there is a need for a Polish law firm that will serve the interests of the Poles, the undersigned have undertaken to establish a firm of this kind, patterned after the best American law firms which have built up a clientele through their business reputations. Under these principles we wish to serve our esteemed public.

Realizing that there is no Polish law firm in existence and being convinced that a group of lawyers can better serve the interests of the public, we anticipate that the public will seek our counsel, the counsel of a group and not of an individual.

2

We invite the esteemed public to support our new business enterprise, and we promise to do our utmost to serve their interests.

John J. Bielman,

C. B. Czarnecki,

F. N. Koralewski.

Offices are located at Room 501, 153-155 LaSalle Street, and at 604 Milwaukee Avenue.
