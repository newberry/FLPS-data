As is known Dr. W. Kuflewski received the nomination as a doctor on the County Hospital staff. He is the first Polish doctor to receive this position despite the fact that many well known doctors of Chicago were seeking this position.

Dr. Kuflewski will continue his work at the Surgical Clinic adjoining the Chicago Clinical School at 819 W. Harrison Street, across the street from the County Hospital, where he aids the sick and needy free of charge every Wednesday and Friday from 9 A. M. to 11 A. M.

Dr. Kuflewski is strongly in favor of giving surgical and medical aid to all classes and races of people, relieve their suffering ones and promote the happiness and health of the people.
