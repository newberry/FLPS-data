Conditions in the St. Hedwig Parish are looking brighter with each passing day. Respect and co-operation have been won by the new pastor from his flock, and peace now reigns in the parish.

Contributions to the church are also growing. Kochanowski's Dramatical Circle has contributed fifty dollars. The St. Hedwig Parish Choir has donated $192.15 for a new organ. Proceeds from the picnic given for the parish school children at Irving Park recently, netted $113.14.

All those who contributed toward these undertakings are tendered sincere thanks by the pastor.
