Out of sixteen donors to the St. John Cantius Parish fair eleven gave a total of ninety-four dollars. The other five gave merchandise. The fair was opened on July 27 and will continue through August.

Five church societies of St. John Cantius Parish made the cash contributions. The Sisters of Nazareth made an offering of three portraits and a covering for a piano. John Szczesny, 81 May Street, gave a gold watch.

The Fair Committee wishes to extend heartfelt thanks to the donors.
