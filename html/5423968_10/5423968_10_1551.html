Under the instigation of its pastor Reverend Nowicki, St. Michael Archangel Parish has organized two new military societies, the Cadets of St. Michael and the Kosynieri [soldiers armed with scythes, as in Kosciusko's peasant army]. Each will consist principally of school boys; those who do not attend school may be members only if they can prove themselves of exemplary character and are introduced by responsible persons.

Leon Machinkowski, a member of the Illinois National Guard, will command the Cadets, while Anthony Niewolski, who is an old soldier from Poland, will be in charge of the Kosynieri.

Judging from the large number of applications for membership, we can predict success for both of these societies.
