[We have received the following correspondence with a request for publication in the Dziennik Chicagoski.]

The St. Florian Young Men's Society of the Holy Trinity Parish decided, at their meeting yesterday, to invite all the presidents and secretaries of church societies of the Holy Trinity Parish, also the officers of national societies, to a meeting which will be held on Tuesday, January 28, at 7:30 in the evening, in the Holy Trinity hall.

The object of the meeting is to consider the question of commemorating the 150th anniversary of the birth of our great Thaddeus Kosciusko.

All the presidents and secretaries are requested to attend. The honor of our 2Polish name, and a great cause, is at stake.

The Officers of the St. Florian Society.
