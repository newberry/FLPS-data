The fair at St. John Cantius Parish opened Saturday evening, July 27, and will continue each evening, starting at 7:30. Admission is ten cents. A capacity audience attended during the first two days. The Fair opened yesterday (Sunday) at 4 P. M. Many took home prizes, valued at more than five dollars, which were won on a five-cent raffle ticket.

Admission will be free this evening, which is an evening dedicated to the members of St. John Cantius Parish. Various societies of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish will take turns in attending each evening.

Because the fair is free tonight, all those attending should be on their good behavior; otherwise they will be escorted out of the fair grounds.
