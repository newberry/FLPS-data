Forty-hour devotional services began yesterday in Holy Trinity Parish. Reverend M. Dorszynski, assisted by Reverend W. Czyzewski of South Bend, Indiana, officiated at Mass yesterday at 9 A.M. The sermon was delivered by Reverend Wojtalewicz of South Chicago.

In the evening, services were celebrated by Reverend F. Furman, and the sermon was given by Reverend Szkiarzyk. Reverends Gieburowski, Gielinek, Rapacz, Kuzyusz, and Wyzykowski were present to give a hand.
