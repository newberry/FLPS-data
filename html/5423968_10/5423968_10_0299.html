Last week, the most Rev. Father Archbishop Feehan authorized several important appointments in the archdiocese of Chicago.

Rev. Frank Barry was appointed a member of the cabinet, which is composed of six members, one of whom is our most Rev. Fr. Lange, Besides being a member of this cabinet our Rev. Lange also became a "Permanent Pastor", which means that he stays with his parish until death. For no reason can he be removed or sent to another parish.

The most important fact is that when the next balloting comes up for archbishop in Chicago, Father Lange's vote will be of value.

It is a great honor to us, because he is the first Polish priest in the archdiocese of Chicago to receive such an honor. so a \2good deal of happiness prevails among us, especially among those who know well the work and doings of Rev. Lange in the management and prosperity of his parish and community.

We heartily congratulate Rev. Frank Lange, and pray to God that he may live long and we in return receive his great help.
