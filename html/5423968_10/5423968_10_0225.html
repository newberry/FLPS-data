Seven hundred church representatives and priests gathered here in the Auditorium Hotel yesterday, and before them spoke Bishop McDowell, criticizing the public administrators and their oaths in fulfilling their duties.

The bishop entirely and clearly chided Mayor Busse with these words: "What steps shall we take toward a person who takes an oath on his promises, and who later sets a bad example by not fulfilling these promises?" Everyone understood that this was spoken about Busse. "If we have such a person in our midst, beware then, because he is just like a criminal, whose place is usually behind steel bars."

Bishop McDowell pretends that he forgot that politicians usually give us many promises during their candidacy, never thinking of ever keeping their word.
