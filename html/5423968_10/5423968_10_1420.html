Mr. W. Vesien, publisher and co-editor of several Polish newspapers in Chicago, received a telegraph notification of his appointment as a Translator in the Department of the Interior Bureau of Education at Washington.

The notice instructs him to appear ready for service at once. The annual salary for this work is $1,800. per year. He will leave for Washington D. C. this evening. This position is classified as professional; we are, therefore, happy to say that a Pole will represent our element in that capacity in Washington. Mr. Vesien is known and has acted in many cases without fee. This newspaper welcomes the opportunity to wish him success and godspeed in his new venture.
