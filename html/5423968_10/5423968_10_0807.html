Starting last Sunday, August 18, Forty-hour devotion services were begun in St. Michael Archangel Parish at South Chicago. The Sunday services were celebrated by the Reverend M. Fafara; the Sermon was delivered by Reverend Joseph Barzynski.

Other services were held as follows:

Monday: High Mass was officiated by Reverend J. Radziejewski; the sermon was delivered by Reverend Gieburowski. Vespers were celebrated by Reverend S. Cossimi the sermon was delivered by Reverend Florian Matuszewski.

Tuesday: High Mass was celebrated by Reverend J. Radziejewski; the sermon was delivered by Reverend Paul Rode [also Rhode]. Vespers were officiated by Reverend Kasprzycki; the sermon was delivered by Reverend J. Gieburowski.

Besides the above mentioned priests the following took part in the services: 2Reverends [gap], Borowski, Blater, Burdach, Lampka, Dickman and Adolph Nowicki, paster of St. Michael Archangel Parish.

The services were carried out very solemnly. All services were attended by capacity crowds. Several hundred people received Holy Communion. The parish choir, directed by Mr. Wiedeman, moved the congregation with its singing of religious music.

The parishioners are grateful to all the clergy who took part in this service.
