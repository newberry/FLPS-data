Crossing themselves very piously, the editor of Dziennik Narodowy kissed very reverently the apostolic paw of Mr. Rhode, the First Polish Bishop in America.

That ended the fight for "principle of the right of the people" conducted by Dziennik Narodowy, and from now on Mr. Rhode is no more a mere 'suffragan' or a 'painted bishop,' a man who didn't know his father, but Right Reverend Bishop Rhode, the First Polish Bishop, with a large capital B, in the United States of North America.

Bravo, Gentlemen!
