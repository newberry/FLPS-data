This organization was established in 1898 with a desire to be an organization embracing all Polish youth, but so far, it is still in its bud. It does not extend beyond the confines of its own parish. It belongs to the future and its history should be left to the future historians.

At this time it is enough to mention that the founder of this youngest Polish organization in America is Rev. Francis Gordon, a leader par excellence, whose work and activities we shall have the opportunity to mention many times.
