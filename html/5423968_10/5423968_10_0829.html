Sixty-six persons have contributed over seventy-five dollars in cash and a few hundred dollars in merchandise for the St. John Cantius Parish bazaar, which is to be held shortly. The St. John Cantius Brotherhood Society contributed $15. Benziger Brothers, 178 Monroe Street, donated a statue valued at $25.

Among the prominent persons who have contributed to the bazaar are Reverend Vincent Barzynski, C. R., pastor of St. Stanislaus Kostka, Parish, and Mr. Ignace Kowalski.

The committee in charge of the bazaar thanks all contributors for their kindness.
