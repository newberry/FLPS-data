The White Eagle Society is becoming every day more popular, and after hardly two years of existence it has become a strong society.

At every meeting we have the honor of presenting and admitting newly selected candidates and members. The ladies, members of this society, are rendering our nation a patriotic service; their activity in praising and uplifting everything is of much benefit to us.

The White Eagle Society is hoping to possess the greatest number of members, more than any other existing organization, and so we urge you to join this budding organization, a society of trustworthy sisters and young men, where you can find great aid and care in every need. You shall then help us in achieving our goal.
