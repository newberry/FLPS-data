The consecration of our Reverend Father, Bishop Rhode, will take place next Wednesday, July 29, at 10 A. M., in the cathedral located at State and Superior Streets.

All the clergymen will be present at the school hall by 9:50 A. M. From there they will march in procession to their designated places in the cathedral. After the church ceremonies, all the reverend fathers taking part in this consecration are cordially invited to meet in the church hall and pay respect to Bishop Rhode.

The public is invited to pay its respects to Bishop Rhode, but they must behave themselves at the hall the same as they should during the procession and the consecration.

Admission to the cathedral for the consecration ceremonies is by ticket only.

For the citizens committee, the newspapers reporters and for the officials and delegates, reservations have been set aside.

Law and order will be enforced by Polish seminarists.

Rev. Father K. Gronkowski,

Chairman of the Committee.
