The Most Reverend George W. MundeIein, Archbishop of the Chicago diocese of the Roman Catholic Church, published the changes to take effect in various Catholic parishes in Chicago, among which were many Polish....parishes. Due to the fact that ten new priests were ordained, twenty-nine priests have been moved from the posts which they formerly occupied.

We list here only the changes made in Polish....parishes:

The Reverend W. S. Kukulski, formerly pastor in Hanson Park, is now assistant pastor in St. Adalbert's Parish.

The Reverend Francis Marcinek has been transferred from Our Lady of Immaculate Conception Church to the Polish Church in Hanson Park.

The Reverend Ignace Mazarowski, formerly of St. Adalberts' Church, has been \2transferred to the post of assistant pastor of Assumption Parish.

The Reverend B. Szudzinski formerly of St. Andrew's Church in West Hammond, has been transferred to the post of assistant pastor of Our Lady of Immaculate Conception Church.

The Reverend W. Kozlowski (newly ordained), has been made assistant at St. Andrew's Church in West Hammond.

The Reverend Stephen Kowalski (newly ordained), has been made assistant at Our Lady of Czestochowo Church in Cicero.

The Reverend B. Nawrocki (newly ordained), has been made assistant at Holy Innocent's Church.

The Reverend Henry Pippenkotter (newly ordained), has been made assistant at Five Holy Martyrs' Church.
