Notice: On Wednesday, January 29, at seven thirty o'clock in the evening, there will be a meeting of the Pulaski Volunteers in the St. Adalbert Parish school hall, to which all Polish young men of eighteen years and older wishing to join this organization are cordially invited. The Volunteers have amended their constitution to permit payment of sick and death benefits. The initiation cost is one dollar, and monthly payments twenty-five cents. This is the ideal organization for Polish young men--and its aim is to work sincerely for the good of our country.

Joseph Walkowiak, president

Albert J. Danisch, secretary
