Contributions have been received from forty-two individuals and organizations by the St. Hedwig Parish Fair Committee. Of these, thirteen gave $38.80 in cash, while the others gave valuable merchandise.

The largest cash contributions came from John Rogalski and Max Drzemala, who gave five dollars each. The Young Women's Sodality of St. Hedwig Parish gave $4.30.

W. Herek gave a goat, valued a fifteen dollars, and two rabbits. Francis Dobisz gave four young pigs valued at fourteen dollars.

The parish committee extends sincere thanks to the donors.
