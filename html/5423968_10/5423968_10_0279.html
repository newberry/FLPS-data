Blessing of the Corner Stone for the Convent "Sisters of Resurrection."

Tomorrow afternoon at 4 o'clock Bishop P.Muldeon will arrive to perform the ceremony of blessing the corner stone for the new convent "Sister of Resurrection". The Societies of St. Stanislaus and St.Marion will march out as a reception for the arrival of Bishop Muldoon.

The Society of St.Stanislaus will gather in the convent courtyard at about 2 o'clock, and under the leadership of marshal Thomas Novak will march on Noble St., Blackhawk, Paulina and Wabansia; after forming with the Society of St.Marion under the leadership of marshal M.Henck, they will parade down Hermitage Ave. to North Ave., where they will await the arrival of Bishop Muldoon.

Then they will accompany the Bishop to the parish of "Blessed Virgin Mary", where he will dress in the episcopal robes, and then march in a procession to the convent's courtyard located at Hermitage Ave. and Clybourn Place. After the ceremony the Societies will escort the Bishop back to the parish of "Blessed \2Virgin Mary" where he will change back to his street clothes, thence to the auditorium of St. Stanislaus, where they will celebrated by singing and dancing. This ceremony is one that will remain in the minds of all Polish people as a day of great remembrance and celebration.
