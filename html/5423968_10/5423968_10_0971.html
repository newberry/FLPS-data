The St. Hyacinth Society has been recently organized in the parish of the same name. The new society has elected the following officers: Laurence Bialkowski, president; Theophilus Murzyn, vice-president; Theophilus Ratkowski, recording secretary; Anthony Jankowski, financial secretary; John Kochanski, treasurer; Francis Trochowski, marshal; Leon Bujak and Florian Orlowski, trustees.
