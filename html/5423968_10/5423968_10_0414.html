On St. Joseph's Day, Friday, March 19, beautiful and solemn services were held at the chapel of the Holy Family Home, 130 West Division Street. Eight Polish nuns were given their vows by Archbishop Feehan during the services. The Reverends Vincent Barzynski, J. Piechowski, T. Szypkowski, and Andrew Spetz acted as assistants.

The nunnery robes were given by Archbishop Feehan to the following:

1. Frances Witkowska (S. M. Cornelia), of Seranton, Pennsylvania.

2. Leckadia Ruszkowska (S. M. Margarite), of Pittsburg, Pennsylvania.

3. Sophia Fac (S. M. Martha), of Milwaukee, Wisconsin.

4. Pearl Prusakowska (S. M. Felicia), of Everson, Pennsylvania.

5. Helen Retmanska (S. M. Josephine), of St. Michael Archangel Parish, South Chicago, Illinois.

6. Anna Netkowska (S. M. Clara), of St. John Cantius Parish, Chicago, Illinois.

7. Agnes Zmich (S. M. Aloise), of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish, Chicago, Illinois.

8. Antonina Leszczynska (S. M. Celina), of Milwaukee, Wisconsin.

After the services were over, the Archbishop bestowed his blessing upon the entire congregation. entire congregation.
