Consecration ceremonies of the temporary chapel of St. Hyacinth Church took place yesterday.

A number of Polish societies, including military groups, left St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish early to take part in the services. The choir also took part. All were in full dress.

Reverend Simon Kobrzynski officiated, after which Reverend John Piechowski, pastor of the new St. Hyacinth Parish, sang the first Mass. A sermon was delivered by Reverend Vincent Barzynski, pastor of St. Stanislaus Kostka Church.

All delegates of societies made contributions for the new chapel, as well as all others that took part in the affair.
