Tomorrow evening at seven o'clock, all the orders of the Knights of St. Stanislaus Kostki's Parish are to meet in uniform at their headquarters, after which all will fall in and march to the Polish Hall near the Church. Drill exercises will take place.

St. Stanislaus Kostki's cadets will lead the march, followed by the Order of Knights of St. Martin's Society, a second order of cadets, Polish K. K. Guards, and others.

All members are requested to attend.
