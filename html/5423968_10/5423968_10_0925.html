Forty-Hour Devotion services will begin tomorrow at St. Stanislaus Kostka Church, and will continue in the following order:

Sunday, February 17:

1. The services will be officially opened by celebration of mass and a procession at 5:30 A. M. in the upper church [St. Stanislaus Kostka Church was really two churches in one: upper and lower].

A second mass will be held at 6:30 A. M. in the lower church.

2

2. The women and young ladies of the St. Francis Sodality will participate in the first hours of Adoration (sic), followed by Immaculate Heart of Mary Sodality, St. Joseph Society, Nos. 1 and 2, St. Stanislaus Kostka Cadets, and others.

3. Members of the ushers' staff will be in charge, and the same rules will be followed as have been followed previously, according to the Book of Adoration published in 1892.

The same rules will hold good for the women's and men's societies.

4. The parish societies will observe their hour of Adoration during vespers, Sunday at 3:30 P. M., in the upper church.

The marshals from the societies, with the assistance of the church ushers, will keep the order appropriate to the occasion of celebrating the services.

5. High Masses will be celebrated at the regular hours on Sunday. The ladies sodality organizations will participate in Rosary services in the afternoon at 3one o'clock; the young women's societies will participate at 2 P. M.

6. Vespers in the upper church for all other societies will be celebrated at 3:30 P. M.; the parishioners may take part in the Vespers to be given in the lower church at 3 P. M.

Monday and Tuesday: services will begin at 5 A. M. in the lower church, and at 7 A. M. in the upper church.

High Masses will be celebrated as on Sunday.

Concluding services will be held on Tuesday evening at 7 o'clock.

The school children will attend their regular mass services on Sunday at 7 A. M., and also on Monday and Tuesday. Adoration services will also be celebrated on Monday and Tuesday, in the following order:

4

Boys: from 1 P. M. to 2 P. M.

Girls: from 2 P. M. to 4 P. M.

The brother sexton (sic) will give the schedule and place to every church society relative to their period for participation in Adoration services.

Individuals will not be permitted to sing during the Forty-hour Services. The parish choir and the church societies will perform during the appointed hours.

The church marshals will keep order and quiet in the church.

It is expected that all church societies will refrain from holding any meetings; weddings and socials should not be given.

Reverend Vincent Barzynski, C. R.

Pastor of St. Stanislaus Kostka Church
