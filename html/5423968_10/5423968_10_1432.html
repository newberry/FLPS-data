I am proud to announce to the Polish public that I have been given charge of a postal substation in my home at 924 West 33rd Street. All services of a regular postal station are offered--money orders, registered mail, mailing of packages, post cards, stamps, etc. Polish, Lithuanian, and English spoken. Office hours: 7 A. M. to 9 P. M.; Sundays, 10 A. M. to 7 P. M.

The Polish public is invited to patronize this substation during the above-mentioned hours.

Anthony Olszewski
