Six Holy Trinity church choirs, under the direction of Antholy Mallek, arranged a concert program for tomorrow at 7:30 P. M. in the Walsh Hall, Noble and Emma Street. The income will go toward the purchase of an organ for the new church of Holy Trinity.

Mrs. Hedwig Smulska and Mr. John A. Mallek are on the program.

The program:

1. President T. Roosevelt's Inauguration March, accompanied by John A. Mallek's orchestra, under the direction of Mr. Anthony Mallek.

2. "To work, Together to Work!" music by Mr. Karasowki, words by S. Buszczynski, choir arrangements by Anthony Mallek; and will be performed by the entire parochial choir of Holy Trinity with the orchestra.

3. "And Do you Know, My Young Fellow?" by Sieroslawski, performed by the Holy Trinity Girls' Choir; and orchestra.

2

4. "Prussian Defeat under Miloslawiem," and "Sorrow for Ukrania," to be performed by the Trinity Male choir, solo parts will be sung by Mr. Jos. Szwerbel, H. Adamowski, K. Wronski, and Chester Kowalski.

5. "Arditien," (solo soprano), waltz, performing Mrs. Anna Mallek, accompanied by Mrs. Catherine Gorski on the piano.

6. "Ludomir," and "Naval Choir," from the opera "Condemned Island," by Zahajkiewicz, will be performed by the Trinity Male choir accompanied by the orchestra of Anthony Mallek; the solo part of "Ludomir" will be sung by Mr. J. Mallek.

7. Arditien," (solo soprano), waltz, performing Mrs. Helen Kempska, accompanied by Mrs. C. Gorski on the piano.

8. "Overture," played by the orchestra.

3

9. "The First Concerto," (Berriot's violin solo), performing John A. Mallek, accompanied by Mrs. Hedwig Smulska at the piano.

10. "Jubilee Polonez," by O. M. Zukowskis, the singing done by the female choir of St. Hedwig, with orchestra. Duet will be sung by Mrs. A. Kasza and Victoria Adamczyk.

11. A march in honor of His Excellency Archbishop Albin Symon. Music arranged by A. Mallek, words by Mr. Karol Wachtel. "Polish Nation," performed by the various choirs, with the orchestra. A duet will be sung by Mrs. A. Kasza and V. Adamczyk.

12. "White Roses," music by Wronski, words by Mr. Karol Wachtel; will be performed by Mrs. Wandas choir with accompaniment of the orchestra. Solo part will be sung by Mrs. Monika Szczepanska.

13. "Our Trinity," arranged by A. Mallek; performed by all choirs present with the aid of the orchestra.

14. "Polonez," orchestra.
