The Saint Mary Society of Saint Stanislaus Kostka Parish celebrated its tenth anniversary yesterday at 4 P.M. at the parish school hall.

James Tomaszewski, president of the society, opened the meeting and after a short speech called upon Reverend Vincent Barzynski, pastor of Saint Stanislaus Kostka Parish, to act as chairman. The pastor congratulated the society on its record and wished it many such anniversaries. He then called upon Mr. Horbaczewski, editor of Narod Polski (Polish Nation), to act as recording secretary.

Next, Leon Szopinski, editor of Gazeta Katolicka and member of the society, spoke of the principles and aims of Catholic societies. This was followed by the singing of "Polish Heart" by Mrs. Klarkowska, Miss Klarkowska and Miss Koppa, to the accompaniment of Reverend S. Cosimi.

The highlight of the program was the speech given by Reverend Joseph Gieburowski on the theme: "The Name of Saint Mary Brought and Will Bring Poland to Victory". The Poles always have Saint Mary in their hearts and upon their lips.

After a number of songs, the program concluded with a speech by Reverend Barzynski.
