We have received the following correspondence, with a request to publish it in the Dziennik Chicagoski:

"The St. Dominic Arch Sodality of St. Adalbert's Parish, in Chicago, Illinois, decided at its last meeting to give full support to the proposed Polish Catholic Congress in America, and selected Mr. John Wiszniewski and Mr. Joseph Susmarski as delegates.

"A. Preius, recording secretary,

"1042 W. 19th Street."

We are also informed that the St. Stanislaus Bishop and Martyr Society in Nanticoke, Pennsylvania, has decided to support the proposed Polish Catholic Congress in America.

It can truly be said that the cause of the Congress is progressing.
