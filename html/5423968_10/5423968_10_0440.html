The Jacob Wejer Society No. 1, under the protection of Saint Francis Xavier, of the St. Stanislaus Kostka parish in Chicago, at its regular meeting held on August 11, decided to take an active part in the Polish Catholic Congress to be held in Buffalo, New York, and donated the sum of twenty-five dollars to defray the expenses of the Congress. The president of the Society, Mr. Hubert Abraham, was selected as delegate to the Polish Catholic Congress.

T. Jarzombek, secretary,

5 Blanche Avenue.
