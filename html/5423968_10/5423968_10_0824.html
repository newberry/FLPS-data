The officers of all St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish Societies held a meeting on July 22 to determine the part they would take in the coming St. John Cantius Parish fair. Louis Kalisz presided as chairman and Peter Bocian acted as recording secretary.

The following societies agreed to participate:

1. St. Stanislaus Kostka Society, 230 members, August 3.

2. Holy Name of Mary Society, 260 members, August 10.

3. Holy Cross Society, 150 members, August 10.

4. St. Joseph Benevolent Society, 125 members, August 3.

5. St. Joseph Society, Number 2, 125 members, August 17.

6. St. Michael Archangel Society, 200 members, August 3.

7. John Sobieski III Society, 100 members, August 17.

8. S. S. Peter and Paul Society, 150 members, August 17.

9. St. Casimir Young Men's Society, 225 members, August 10.

10. St. Hyacinth Society, 60 members, August 17.

11. St. Hedwig Society, 115 members, August 11.

12. St. Stephen Society, 225 members, August 3.

Notice 1: If every member of the above-mentioned societies should spend \3two dollars at the fair, the St. John Cantius Church would profit by $4,030.

Notice 2: The St. Joseph Young Men's Benevolent Society of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish donated five dollars towards the fair.

Notice 3: If any other societies of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish desire to take part in the fair they are requested to get in touch with Reverend John Kasprzycki, pastor of St. John Cantius Parish.

Notice 4: The above-mentioned societies are to call at the rectory of St. John Cantius Church for tickets.
