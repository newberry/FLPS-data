The number of members of the Polish Roman Catholic Union is growing steadily.

Two new societies from outside of Chicago, numbering about one hundred members, have joined this organization.

The last assessment roll of the Polish Roman Catholic Union has been already sent out, Mr. Frank Wleklinski, secretary of this organization, assures us; the list contains names of 8,090 members. With this new hundred, the membership of the Union will reach close to 8,200 members. The Milwaukee split will soon be remedied.

More particulars about this matter will be published in the next issue of Wiara i Ojczyzna (Faith and Fatherland).
