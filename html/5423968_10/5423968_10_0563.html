(We have received the following correspondence with a request that it be published in the Dziennik Chicagoski.)

The St. John The Baptist Society, of St. Stauislaus Kostka Parish, has unanimously decided to support the Polish Catholic Congress and to take part in it. The Society has selected the following members as delegates to the congress: Mr. Jacob Mucha, Mr. John Buda, and Mr. J. Gniot.

J. Gniot, president.

(The second letter received by the Dziennik Chicagoski reads as follows:)

The St. Hyacinth Society, of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish, decided at their last meeting to take part in the Polish Catholic Congress and to send a \2delegate to represent them at the congress. Every Catholic Pole should support this cause.

B. Kraskiewicoz,

recording secretary.
