Yesterday, amid impressive ceremonies, the cornerstone was laid for the new Polish Catholic church, Saint Mary of the Angels, Wood and Cortland Streets.

Archbishop Quigley, assisted by Bishop Rhode, Archbishop Weber of Poland, Reverend Francis Gordon, and many priests from all parts of the country, blessed the foundation, after Archbishop Quigley had celebrated a High Mass in the old church in commemoration of the church's fiftieth anniversary. After the Mass, the faithful, twenty thousand strong, marched to the site of the new church, singing Polish hymns.

The cost of the new structure is estimated at two hundred thousand dollars.
