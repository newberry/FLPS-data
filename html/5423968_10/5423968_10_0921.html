The case of the St. Hedwig Parish agitators, which was to have been tried last Saturday, has been postponed by Judge Severson until Wednesday, February 20. The accused are Theodore Dimonowski, Joseph Tadych, Antonia Grams, and Catherine Lagodzinska.

Two other arrests were made in front of the police station last Saturday. Misses Julia Szablewska and Julia Smith were held for accosting Officer Klaft on the street.

The charges against the accused are not being pressed by the parishioners but by the police.
