(The following article was received by the Dziennik Chicagoski for publication.)

A new Polish youth society, Gymnastic Society under the Guidance of St. Joseph, was organized in Town of Lake. This society was organized on Sunday, December 1. Over twenty young men joined.

The officers are as follows: Michael Gapinski, president; Stanislaus Kucharski, vice-president; Andrew Chylewski, recording secretary; Peter Malecki, financial secretary; Vincent Stachowski, treasurer; Michael Rozplochowski, Anthony Slowik, and Chester Frackowiak, trustees.

These officers, however, are only temporary; an election of officers for the ensuing year will be held at a meeting this evening.

Andrew Chylewski, recording secretary,

5025 Justine Street.
