The new and magnificent St. Hedwig Church has been beautified with a splendid organ purchased for a sum of $9,000.

The blessing of the organ combined with a concert will take place on June 15. The following persons will take part in the concert: Professor A. Halter, at the organ; Miss R. Heiman, on the violin; Mr. R. Drott, tenor soloist; Miss R. Kwasigroch, soprano soloist.

The local organist, Mr. Wiedeman, will conduct the choirs. The concert will begin at 7:30 P. M.
