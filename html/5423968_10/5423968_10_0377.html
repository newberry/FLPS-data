Although the weather was perfect last Sunday, it did not have any effect on the attendance of the Sunday-school class in the hall of St. Stanislaus Kostka's School. A large crowd of Polish youth was on hand to hear the Reverend Vincent Barzynski, pastor of St. Stanislaus Kostka's church, lecture on "The Soul of Man".

Tomorrow the discourse will be continued by the pastor along the same lines. It is anticipated that a capacity crowd of Polish youth will be present, for the topics of the other speakers are not only interesting but instructive. The school begins promptly at 10 A. M.
