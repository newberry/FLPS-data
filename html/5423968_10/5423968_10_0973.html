Forty-Hour devotion services began in St. Mary of Perpetual Help Parish December 11 and continued until December 13. High Mass was celebrated at 10 P.M., and the sermon was delivered by Reverend Gutkowski of Detroit. Vespers were celebrated in the evening at 7 o'clock.

The concluding sermon was given by Reverend Kozlowski of Manistee, Michigan.

During the three days, the church was constantly visited by parishioners and visitors from other parishes. Over thirty-five hundred persons went to confession and partook of the Blessed Sacrament. This is sufficient evidence that the parishioners heeded the call of their good pastor.

The following priests came to the assistance of our local clergy: Reverend Gutkowski of Detroit, Michigan, Reverend Matkowski of Bay City, Michigan, 2Reverend Skory of Gaylord, Michigan, Reverend Lipinski of Saginaw, Michigan, Reverend Kozlowski of Manistee, Michigan, Reverend Szukalski of Milwaukee, Wisconsin, Reverend Balczewicz of Chicago, Illinois, Reverends Koblynski and Wojtalewicz of Hammond, Indiana, Reverends Tyszka and Brodowicz of LaSalle, Illinois, Reverends Lange, Laczynski, Sztuczko, Truszynski, Sedlaczek, and Matuszewski of Chicago, Illinois, and Reverend Kolesinski of Terre Coupee (sic).

Sincere thanks are expressed for the kind help and co-operation of the clergy and parishioners.

A Parishioner
