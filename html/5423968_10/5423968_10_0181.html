A meeting of all right-thinking citizens will be held at the hall of the Polish Hospital, at Thomas and Leavitt Streets, on Friday, September 9, 1910. The purpose of the meeting is to consider the question of organizing a new Polish parish in the locality.

The presence of all is urgently requested as very important matters will be given due consideration. The committee extends its invitation to all those residing in the neighborhood and urges them to participate in this very important event.
