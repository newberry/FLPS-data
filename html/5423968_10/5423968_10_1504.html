The youth of the Holy Trinity Parish, according to a report received by the editor, recently organized a new military society under the name, "The Sons of Kosciusko Knights". The purpose of this society is to give military training and to take part in national and religious celebrations.

All Polish youths of the parish are cordially invited by the committee to attend the next meeting, which will be held on Wednesday, February 3, at the parish school hall, starting at 7:30 P. M. The entrance fee is only fifty cents. An election of officers will be held at this meeting.
