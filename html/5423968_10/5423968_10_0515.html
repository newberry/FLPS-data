(We have received the following correspondence with a request that it be published in the Dziennik Chicagoski.)

The Brotherhood Of St. Barbara has been organized in the St. Hyacinth Parish in Avondale. The first induction ceremony will take place in church on Monday, April 6, at 9 A. M. Whoever wishes to join the Brotherhood may apply before the services or after the services to the undersigned. The initiation fee is fifty cents, and monthly dues are ten cents.

Joseph Krystan, 822 West George Street.
