The case against the Reverend John Piechowski, unjustly accused or criminal libel, was finally called yesterday by Judge Eldred (of Logan Square), who then gave his decision.

The charge was dismissed.

We have already printed a few items about this case. The accuser was a certain Mr. Kowarzyk, an undertaker belonging to the independent sect of the Reverend Anthony Korlowski, who felt insulted by the innocent [Polish] word "Drab" (strong man), which, he alleged, referred to him.

This case will go no further, and it will not be appealed to the Criminal Court. Those who started the proceedings will themselves be judged.

In the trial the facts were disclosed, in spite of the objections of Mr. 2Kowarzyk's attorney, who is the excommunicated priest Kozlonski, and what the purpose of the accusation was.

Attorney John King, defending the Reverend Father Piechowski, spoke very well. He condemned the entire independent religious movement and showed clearly its sorrowful aim and beginning.

Further particulars of the trial and a reprint of this speech, full of wisdom and of logic, will be published in tomorrow's paper.

The trial lasted all afternoon and was finished late at night.
