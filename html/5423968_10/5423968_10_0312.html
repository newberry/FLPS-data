The death of Rev. Father Barzynski, a member of the Resurrection Provincial convent, and the Most Rev. Father at St. Stanislaus, caused a change in three parishes.

Rev. Father John Kasprzycki, of St. John Cansius, will take over St. Stanislaus parish; Rev. Father Eugene Sedlaczek, of Avondale, will take over the parish of St. John Cansius, while Rev. Father Anzelma Babski will occupy Father Sedlaczek's parish.

Therefore, three parishes will have new rectors. We have high hopes that the church members will feel the same towards the new rectors as they have for their former ones.
