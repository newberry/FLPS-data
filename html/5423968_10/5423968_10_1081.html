At eight thirty this morning, the papal delegate, His Excellency Monsignor Satolli, celebrated High Mass at the Holy Trinity Church on Noble Street, according to a promise previously given. He came in the company of two other Italian priests. Also present at his invitation were the Resurrection Fathers F. Zwiardowski, Legrand, J. Kasprzycki, and J. Piochowski. Monsignor Satolli left immediately after the solemn High Mass was over.

Later in the day we expect to receive news of when, and under what conditions Holy Trinity Church will be opened to the public.
