In Chicago a new Polish society has been organized under the name of the Polish Catholic Church Extension Society.

We were informed of this society by a circular sent to us, that this new organization was formed to protect the Catholic church's interests and extend Catholicism in America.

This society will endeavor to strengthen all newly organized churches needing support.

The next meeting will be held Wednesday, Dec. 13th, at Archbishop Quigley's palace. Reading of the constitution and the enactment of laws will be accepted and the document of incorporation will be signed. The admittance of these new members will take place: Archbishop Messmer and Archbishop Farley; Bishop Allen and Bishop Maes; Mr.Edward L. Hearn, Nicholas Gonner, Arthur Preuss, Warren Cartier, Stanley Szwajkart.

The press is informed that the election of vice-presidents, and a formal opening of the society will be held shortly. Work well. Begin immediately.
