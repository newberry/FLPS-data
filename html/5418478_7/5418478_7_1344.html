All invalid Czechoslovak Legionnaires are to take notice! Legionnaires who are entitled to a pension are requested to call at the office of the secretary of the local post. Do this as early as possible so that your case may be attended to. This matter concerns every Czechoslovak Legionnaire whether he is a member of the local post or not.

For the Druzina Ceskoslovenskych Legionarju,

Mr. Albert Houska, secretary, 3222 West 23rd Street, Chicago, I11.
