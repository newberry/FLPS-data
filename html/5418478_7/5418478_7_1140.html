The regular monthly meeting of the Druzstvo Jubilejniho Kusova Domu (John Huss Memorial Society) was held in the Cesko-Englicka Svobodomyslna Skola (Bohemian-English Free Thought School) last night....

Many lodges and societies, both from the East and west of Bohemian America, reported on the condition of their Huss, funds, some sending in sums of money which, in a few cases, exceeded one hundred dollars.....

In view of the fact that the Huss celebration will be held next month, July 6, no regular monthly meeting will be held in July. The Annual meeting of the Druzstvo is scheduled for July 15.....

Receipts reported in this meeting were $1,249. Another $1,000 payment on lots purchased for the Huss memorial was approved.....
