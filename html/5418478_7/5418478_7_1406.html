A committee, the members of which are mostly professional and business men, is taking good care of the soldiers recruited from Town of Lake, which is a district dominantly Czech. The committee is headed by Charles V. Janovsky, well-known businessman and chairman of the recruiting commission for the 67th Division of the United States Army.

Soldiers in Camp Grant, in Rockford, Illinois, and also those in Texas, will be benefited by Christmas boxes filled with tobacco, pipes, and other small articles which help to make a soldier's life easier. Doctor Belsan is one of the most active members of the committee.
