Yesterday's conference lasted through the forenoon only. The report of the committee on the proposed merger of all the brotherhoods was formally submitted to the delegates. After a lively debate on this proposal, more on the form of the transaction than on the principle of the unification, the committee retired to discuss details.....

Child insurance, with age limits of two and sixteen years, was adopted.....

The bylaws of the organization were discussed and improved. This took up most of the session, which closed at 12 noon. A sightseeing tour followed, which took the delegates over the boulevards, to the Bohemian National Cemetery, the Bohemian Old People's Home, and other Czech benevolent institutions.
