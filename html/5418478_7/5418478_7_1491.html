The Bohemian Volunteers held a meeting yesterday which was attended by a large number of those willing to offer their services in the war against the Spaniards.

The minutes were approved, and a motion made that the Bohemian Regiment be formed as an independent military unit. Among those present were many youths from the town of Lake, Bohemian California, and from the North side.

After the roll call, 41 new applicants were enrolled.
