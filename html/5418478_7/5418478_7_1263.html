p. 1, col. 7.. The representatives of the Bohemian National Cemetery held their regular meeting last night. They discussed several vital questions concerning the cemetery. Most important of these was the permit to build a public waiting room at the cemetery. Other questions concerned the surveying and landscaping of the grounds and the purchase of certain equipment essential for the maintenance of the cemetery.

Prominent Bohemians function as representatives for this great Bohemian enterprise. This cemetery serves as a permanent memorial.
