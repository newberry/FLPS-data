p. 1 - col. 2.. After a thorough investigation the congressional committee was convinced that Mr. A. Michalek, the first Bohemian congressman, is a respectable citizen and very capable to hold his office. The committee which consisted of both democrats and republicans, voted unanimously that the election of our young countryman to the national legislative body, was perfectly legal.

The question of Mr. A. Michalek's citizenship prompted this investigation, the outcome of which proved that he was elected to his office under all legal requirements.
