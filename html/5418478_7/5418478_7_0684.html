The Cesko-Americky Spolek Pro Spalovani Mrtvych (Bohemian-American Society for the Cremation of the Dead) will hold its regular meeting on Sunday November 5, 1922, at 2 P. M. The meeting is to take place in the Jubilejni Husuv Dum (John Huss Memorial Building), 4236 West 22nd Street. All members are hereby informed to attend this meeting.

Josef W. Egermayer, president, 1148 South Crawford Avenue;

Vaclav Rehak, secretary, 1709 West 15th Street;

Wubert Stary, accountant, 2120 South St. Louis Avenue.
