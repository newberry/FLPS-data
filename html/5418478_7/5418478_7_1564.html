Our countryman, Mr. Frank J. Beran who is employed in the office of the Clerk of the Superior Court, announces that he is willing to offer advice and assistance to all countrymen living in the Tenth Ward who wish to become citizens, and also to those who had their citizenship papers but have lost them. For that purpose he will serve you free of charge every Friday evening from 8 to 9 P. M. in Lackovich Hall, 18th Place and Fisk Street.
