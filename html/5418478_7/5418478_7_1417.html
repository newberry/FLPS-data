In last Friday's meeting, presided over by Anton J. Cermak, it was decided to appeal to all Czech Grand Lodges to use their influence to urge their branches to join the Red Cross, and also to become active as collectors of funds for the organization.

The Benevolent Society of Bailiffs of the Municipal court collected $1,233 from Czech societies; $495 came in for the Czech Red Cross branch. The money was deposited with Mr. Hajicek, treasurer of the branch.
