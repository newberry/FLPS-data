The Dorostovy Klub' Cechoslovak' (Junior Club Czechoslovak) will stage a play this Sunday for the benefit of the T. G. Masaryk [Bohemian Free Thought] school of Cicero, Illinois. The title of the play is 'Pro tu lasku (All for Love), and the play itself will be performed in the Sokol Karel Jonas. The curtain will rise at 8 P.M. During the intermission the junior club orchestra will play, and since it is its intention to surprise everybody with its art, it is desirable that you do not make any engagements for next Sunday but come to Cicero to see this "All for Love" play.
