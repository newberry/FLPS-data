The crematory of the Cesky Narodni Hrbitov underwent an important test yesterday afternoon. It was the final test to make sure that it is in a condition to cremate the remains of such of our departed friends who wish to be cremated, or whose relatives agree to such methods of interment. The test was fully satisfactory, and the remains of the first Bohemian to be cremated in the new incinerators will be, by some strange chance, none other than the Bohemian poet and writer, Josef Bezdek, who died yesterday in the County Hospital.....
