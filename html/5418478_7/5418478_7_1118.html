Yesterday's meeting of the Ceska Svobodna Obec (Congregation of Bohemian Freethinkers) of Chicago was a festive one. Its main purpose was the installation of officers elected at the recent annual meeting of the Svobodna Obec.

After the ceremonies, the members discussed matters pertaining to the John Huss Memorial. The clear deed for six lots at 22nd Street near 42nd Avenue was presented. The lots were purchased for $6,500 for the Memorial, and the amount was paid in full.....

The laying of the cornerstone of the building will take place next July 6.....
