A regular meeting of the board of representatives of the Cesky Narodni Hrbitov was held yesterday......The board approved the pay roll of last month amounting to $4,084 for seventy-nine employees....

Chairman Nencl of the building committee reported on the progress in the building of the crematorium which was not very rapid during the month because the work with the concrete that is being done at present requires more time. When that is finished, the progress will be more rapid.
