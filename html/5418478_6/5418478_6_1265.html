Sbor Karolina Svetla, Cislo 15, Chicagska Jednota Ceskych Dam (Karolina Svetla, Number 15, Chicago Association of Czech Ladies) elected the following officers for the year: chairlady, Filomena Andrle; vice-chairlady, Antonie Malecek; secretary, Antonie Sticet, 1014 West 20th Place; financial secretary Marie Zdenahly, 1903 May Street; cashier, Antonie Denemark, 1920 Racine Avenue; speaker, Marie Kosik; carekeeper, Anna Vondriska; retiring chairlady, Marie Jedlicka; guard, Katerina Opatrny; executive committee; Filomena Andrle and Antonie Natejicek, auditing committee, Marie Jedlicka, Marie Kosik and Antonie Matejicek.

The meetings are held every first Friday of the month at Dvorak Park, May and 20th Streets.
