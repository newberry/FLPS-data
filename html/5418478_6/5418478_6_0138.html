The well-known circle of ladies of the Pilsen Brewery Park gave an entertainment last Tuesday, the proceeds of which will go to charity.

Mrs. Ant. Cervenka is at the head of the organization. She led the ladies who presented songs and humorous sketches. Mr. John A. Cervenka delivered a speech in the Czech language; Mr. Joseph F. Bolek spoke in English. Rubringer's band played dance music.
