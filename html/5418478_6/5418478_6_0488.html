That our Ustredni Matice Skolska v Cechach (Bohemian Scholastic Society in Bohemia] is not forgotten in our circles was again proved last Saturday when the Krouzek Zapadlych Vlastencu (Circle of Modest Patriots) at Mr. Zastera's Coffee House, sent in a contribution of twenty dollars collected by its members on various occasions.

It is truly a pity that we have such a small number of those "Zapadlych Vlastencu".
