A regular weekly meeting of the Ceska Dobrocinna Spolecnost (Bohemian Charitable Association) was held in the offices of the Pilsen Brewing Company last Friday night.....

The ball committee is not yet able to give an exact report on the financial outcome of the ball, because it has not been able to collect money for all the tickets sent but by mail.....

According to a rough estimate, the income from the ball will exceed $2,000.....
