Lodge Bohemia, No. 943, of Free and Accepted Masons, founded with most impressive ceremonials, and because of public interest in our city two years ago, celebrated its second anniversary last Friday night. The celebration took place in West Chicago Masonic Temple and was participated in by the Grand Master of the Grand Lodge of Illinois, Mr. Henry T. Burnay, and all other officers of the Grand Lodge of Illinois, with representatives of all seven Masonic districts of the State. Many Master Masons of other Chicago lodges, as well as several hundred influential visitors from outside were present.....
