Dr. Harry Pratt Judson, president of the University of Chicago, also a well-known friend of the Czech people, has extended an invitation to the Czech National Alliance to meet at the University for the purpose of making arrangements for a "Czech Day" at the University. The cultural level of the Czech nation is to be shown there to the American public, and ample information will be given about our people.

It is being contemplated to give an exhibition of Slovak art, be it handwork or dances. Mr. Vojta [Adalbert] Benes, schoolteacher, [brother of Dr. Edouard Benes, president of the Czechoslovak republic] got in touch with the Slovak Educational Club to discuss details of the exhibition.
