The Klub Svoboda Ceskych Zen A Divek (Liberty Bohemian Women's and Girls' Club Svoboda) arranged an evening of entertainment which is to mark the beginning of the new season. The entertainment will take place in the University Hall, 4630 Gross Avenue. There will be a lecture, solo pieces for violin, and a minstrel show. The Klub decided to meet at 7:30 P. M. so that the program could start at 8:30 P. M.
