When Joseph Spika, one of our Siberian legionnaires, who at the present time is lying in the County Hospital waiting to undergo an operation, returns to his home in Czechoslovakia he will gratefully remember compatriots in Chicago. In answer to our call, he is being remembered by many of our countrymen.
