The fourth annual ball of the Ceska Dobrocinna Spolecnost (Bohemian Charitable Association) was held in the profusely lighted and beautifully decorated pavilion of the Pilsen Brewery last night. It was, as in previous years, the event of the season--an evening when the very best of our Chicago Bohemians meet to spend a few hours in dancing, social intercourse, and partaking of an excellent dinner. In this way, a considerable amount of money is raised for the Association's charitable purposes. The attendance was enormous.....
