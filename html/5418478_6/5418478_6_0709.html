Pauline Plotka did not shoot and kill Dr. Anton J. Jindra with malitious intent, but under the impulse of a fit of unconquerable jealousy. This was the verdict of the corner's jury, and it was borne out by the opinion of twelve unbiased and unprejudiced men in Judge Joseph Sabath's Court. This jury required almost four hours to reach a verdict on the aftermath of a tragic love affair.....
