The Garden City Brewing Company, a Czech enterprise, held its annual meeting yesterday afternoon in the company's office on Albany Avenue. A board of directors for the coming year as elected.....

Our charitable institutions were remembered, as is customary. It was resolved that one hundred dollars be given to the Utulna A Sirotcinec (Old People's Home and Orphanage), and a like amount be given to the Catholic Orphanage at Lisle, Illinois. A donation of twenty-five dollars was made to the Ceska Dobrocinna Spolecnost (Bohemian Charitable Association).
