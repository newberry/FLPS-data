The Ceska-Americka Tiskova Kancelar (Bohemian-American Press Bureau) held a meeting last Tuesday which concluded the third year of activities of this organization. The result of the elections, in which almost all the members participated, was that the present executive committee, consisting of Messrs. James F. Stepina, Karel Beranek, R. Jaromir Psenka, Frantisek Skala, and Fucik, were re-elected by a large majority. A more detailed report containing a summary of the Bureau's work during the past year will be issued to the press within a week.
