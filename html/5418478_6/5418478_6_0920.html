Judge Pinckney of the Juvenile Court is evidently afraid of hurting the Catholics if he allowed the transfer of two Czech orphans from the institution of a foreign nationality to the Bohemian Orphan Asylum. He may rest assured, however, that nobody is minding the affair save the Czechs, and these, whether Catholic or Free-Thinker, know that the Bohemians care about each and every one soul belonging to them, and that any one will ingratiate himself to the Czechs who will help to save two Czech children for their native tongue.
