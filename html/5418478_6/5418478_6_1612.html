P.1, Col. 2--The arrangements committee of the Czechoslovak Catholic Foresters, invited all the delegates of the Association of the Czechoslovak Patronage of the Bohemian American Hall to an outing, which was held today at Jestrom Grove in Bellwood, Ill. At this outing, an expensive emblem, engraved with the symbols of the various Bohemian lodges, was raffled off, for the benefit of the Patronage. This prize was donated by Mrs. Meek.
