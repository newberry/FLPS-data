The census figures show 38,570 Czechs in Illinois, however this figure represents only the number of Bohemians who were born in Bohemia. The total number of all Bohemians is two and one-half to three times as great. This can be judged for instance by the Chicago school census, which found 100,000 Czechs, while the government figures show only 36,362 Bohemian immigrants living in Chicago.
