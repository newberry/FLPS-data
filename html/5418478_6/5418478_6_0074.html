Lalla [first names not given in full], is arranging a vaudeville and musical program, to be given for the orphans and the old people on Sunday, February 22, and will pay all the expenses themselves. A delicious lunch will be served free by Mmes. Frank J. Petru, Charles A. Mrizek and J. J. Lalla. He also is inviting all the representatives to attend.....
