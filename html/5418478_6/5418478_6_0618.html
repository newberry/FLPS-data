Several days ago, we brought a report of the misfortune of the Hruby family of 2631 West 20th Street. Their son was killed by a wagon. Today, we have to mention the kindheartedness of two Bohemian women, who did not hesitate, but undertook a collection for the unfortunate family. To be sure they could not return the lost son to them, but they contributed financially to alleviate their great difficulty, and again proved that no family here remains forsaken when fate strikes it in such a fearful manner as happened in this instance.
