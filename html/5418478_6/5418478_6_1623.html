As in the past, so this year also the Bohemian Club, numbering among its members some of the most prominent Bohemians, held its "Elite Ball" in Libuse Hall on 12th Boulevard.

The Grand March was led by Mr. Charles Vopicka, accomapanied by his wife, followed by Attorney J. O. Hruby, accompanied by Miss Camilla Triner.

The attendance was numerous and those who danced enjoyed the music of Mr. Rubringer's orchestra.
