The plight of two little Slovak girls deserves the attention of our Chicago Czech community. Elizabeth and Anna Pecka, nine and seven years of age respectively, were driven out by their stepfather. They now live at 1625 South Union Street in the home of Mrs. Mary Kyjacek, who was a friend of the deceased mother.....

"The stepfather, Frank Grejewski, a Polish fellow with a brutish disposition, was sent to the Bridewell for nonsupport and child desertion. The Denni Hlasatel received a letter from the elder girl in which she begs for help. Whether these poor children be Czech or Slovak does not matter. They ought to be helped."
