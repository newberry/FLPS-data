Last Thursday, at the meeting of Navarinon Chapter of Gapa, which has existed on the West Side of Chicago for the last two months, the Supreme Governor of the 5th District, Mr. A. [gap] duly installed as the new president, Kleomenos Papatheodoron, replacing Mr. G. Drosos, who has resigned. The entire membership of the Navarinon chapter was present as well as officers and members of the Alexander Ypsilantes chapter.

After the installation, Mr. [gap] and the president of the Alexander Ypsilantes chapter, Mr. K.Sotirakopoulos, spoke on the wonderful purpose and aims of the Gapa organization.
