p. 11 The Chicago Ahepans decided to give a grand ball at the Trianon ballroom on Monday, June 18th, as a benefit for the Corinthian earthquake refugees. The price for tickets has been set at $2.00 per individual and there is no question that the floor of the Trianon, will grow under thousands of Hellenic and Philellenic feet.

The combined chapters of the Ahepa in Chicago, boast something like 2,000 members, whose loyalty to the organization and love of the old country cannot be questioned. For this reason the affair on the 18th.will prove a huge success. For this accomplishment the officials of the Ahepa chapters should be congratulated.
