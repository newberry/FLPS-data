E. F. Wollis has sold and conveyed the property at the Northeast corner of Washtenaw and Ainslie Streets, improved with a ten apartment building, to Peter and Christ Kartsunis, for a consideration of $80,000. subject to $56,000.

The purchasers give in part payment, the vacant business corner 151x125 at Fullerton and Melvinia Avenues, for a consideration of $28,500. subject to $4,000. Anton Tomaras was the salesman.
