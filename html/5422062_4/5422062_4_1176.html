The celebration for Washington's Birthday which the North Shore Chapter gave last Monday was a great success. A large crowd attended. Peter Alfers, president of the Educational Committee was chairman. The first speaker was Charles Giannopoulos past president of the Lord Byron Chapter of the Sons of Pericles. Young Mr. Giannopoulos speech will do down in Ahepa history. Lawyer G. Kyriakopoulos, District Governor of Ahepa was the next speaker. He was followed by Lawyer J. Michalopoulos, who spoke in English. The last speaker was Dr. C. Antonopoulos. Refreshments and dancing followed.
