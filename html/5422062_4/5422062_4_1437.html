At the last meeting of the great nationalistic and patriotic Macedonian Society, the following members were elected to the board of officers for the next year:

Attorney Nicholas Kyriacopoulos, was elected president; James Papajimas, vice-president; K. Raptis, secretary; C. Kotsirilos, treasurer. A motion was passed to the effect that twenty-five per cent of the money collected from dues and other sources during the first three months of 1908 is to be donated to the victims of Rachovos, Macedonia.
