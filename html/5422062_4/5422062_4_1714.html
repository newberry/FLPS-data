Next Sunday, Feb. 2, is the holiday of Ypapantis Tou Kyriou Imon Yisou Christou. Archrev. E. Tsourounakis, priest of St. Andrews church, will take that occasion to lecture on the life and work of the three hierarchs.

Great crowds come to hear Archrev. E. Tsourounakis lectures every Sunday, because they are religious, instructive, and interesting.
