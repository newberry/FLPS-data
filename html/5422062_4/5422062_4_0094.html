p. 4 - The St. George Greek Society of Taniates of Chicago hereby announces that the retiring officers of this fraternal organization have left a bank deposit of $2,518.80 and a stock of books and gold emblems valued at $927.00. Total, $3,445.80. It further informs the public that $3,850.00 has been sent to the National Bank of Greece to be added to the funds to be used in the erection of St. George's Church in Tania. A balance of $509.10 collected for that purpose is on deposit in the First National Bank of Chicago.

The St. George Greek Society of Taniates.
