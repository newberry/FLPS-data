At the last convention of the Order of Gapa which took place at the aristocratic Medinah Club, the following officers were elected:

Dr. Asteriades, Supreme President, Akron Ohio; Attorney George Triantafilakos (Rose) Vice-Supreme President, Gary Indiana; Mr. A. Maroulis, Secretary; Mr. Stamos Grinias, Treasurer; Commanders are the following:

A. B. Mountanos, San Francisco; Geo. Demetracopoulos, Boston; Geo Kounes, New York; K. Sateracopoulos, Chicago; P. Stamos, Charleston S. C; K. Kontogianis, Pittsburg, Pa; P. Economopoulus, Detroit; John Darlas, Akron, Ohio; K. Economopoulos, Denver; Dr. MaLavazos, Alabama; Stylianos Stagopoulos, Utah; Hercules Papamanolis, Canada and Mrs. Olga D. Kallimachos the wife of Mr. D. Kallimachos editor of the National Herald, as General Commander of the woman's lodges.

Mr. Augerinos Soupras, Counsellor at Law.

The next congress of the Order for 1931 will take place in Canada.
