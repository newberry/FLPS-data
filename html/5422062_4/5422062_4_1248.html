Last Tuesday, Supreme Governor A. Kapsopoulos installed the new Gapa Chapter, Thermopilai. This beautiful event took place at the Masonic Temple of the South Side Chapter and was attended by members of all the chapters in Chicago. Speakers were: A. Kapsopovlos, president, K. Augerinos, vice-president, G. Patarias and Archrev. Daniel Gabrilis.

Vice- President- G. Patarias
