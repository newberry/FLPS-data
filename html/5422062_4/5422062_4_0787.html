Harry A. Reckas, Elias Theodoropoulos and Aristides A. Reckas have sold to J. J. Gibs the Southwest 134 ft, vacant corner at E. 71st. St.and Ridgeland Ave for the price of $70,000. The sellers of this property have made a profit of over $30,000 within a period of one year.

Tutules and Dignan represented both parties.
