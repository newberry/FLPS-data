The progressive and well-known John Demaras and N. Karnesis have leased the store at 612 Blue Island Avenue. They are going to open a first-class restaurant which will cost them at least ten thousand dollars. The work will be done by Pericles Skourtis and George Primbas.

Mr. N. Karambelas has opened a beautiful confectionery store in Cicero, Illinois.

Mr. Koufakos, well-known Greek, has opened a Greek Kafenion (Cafe ) at 14 South Halsted Street.
