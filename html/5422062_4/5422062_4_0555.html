p. 2.- Last Sunday, the Minister from Greece made his farewell address, at the Masonic Temple, to the people of Chicago. Lambros Koromilas spoke very beautifully. He praised our city, our people and our progress. He left yesterday morning to continue his trip.

The net proceeds of the affair at the Masonic Temple were $559.37. Following the minister, Mr. Rifakis made a speech. George Rementsoulias arranged the program.
