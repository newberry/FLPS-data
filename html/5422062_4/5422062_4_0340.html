Mme. Anna Papadopoulos, called the mother of the Greek Soldiers, and who at one time was connected with the Red Cross Nursing Service of Greece, recently called at Red Cross National Headquarters here.

She is in this country for the purpose of raising money from Greek-Americans for equipping a tuberculosis Sanatorium for ex-service men in Greece.

During her visit to the Walter Reed Hospital, Major Simpson, Superintendent of the Army School of Nursing, extended her every possible courtesy at his command.
