At eight o'clock last Sunday night the dance of the Liberal Society took place in the Ashland Auditorium. The council acted as the reception committee. A program was offered by the music students of Mr. [Eddy] Varzos. During the intervals between dances, the crowd partook of refreshments. The receipts of the dance will go into the treasury.

The dance was conducted in a very dignified manner, and on all sides expressions of pleasure could be heard. It was well attended despite the number of other social affairs.
