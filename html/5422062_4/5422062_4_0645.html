Peter Stavropoulos and two of his schoolmates had eighty dollars for which they had not worked. They had stolen this money from the purse of their teacher.

They had a wonderful time spending the money. Shows, confectionery shops, and other places of amusement were visited during the two days following the theft.

The boys were caught by the police, and Stavropoulos' two companions claimed that he was the ringleader and had suggested the theft.
