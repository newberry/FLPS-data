Next Wednesday, Feb. 12, in the luxurious Aragon ballroom the initiation of officers of Ahepa's (American-Hellenic Educational Progressive Association) eight chapters will take place, and a dance will follow for the members and guests of the organization.

There is no room for any possibility that this great organized association of the Greeks will ever commit itself to any act contrary to the views and ideals of Hellenism.

In greeting, therefore, the new Executive Councils of "Ahepa", we heartily wish them success in their aims and purposes, in full confidence that they are working for our national preservation in America.
