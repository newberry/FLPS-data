The Greeks of Chicago and vicinity received an appeal from the Near East Relief to contribute, generously, for the continuance of the philanthropic work extended to hundreds of thousands of orphans and refugees in Greece.

It is known that the Near East Relief rescued and saved one million souls, amongst whom were 150,000 orphans. The noble work of the Near East Relief is undoubtedly known to every Greek in this country, it is not necessary to emphasize this well known fact. Send your contributions in without delay.
