p. 6.- The Lodge, Kypseli, composed of young girls of Gapa, showed its strength, March 9th, in the Rose Room of the Harrison Hotel, where they gave a tea. Everything was orderly. The room was beautifully decorated, the lighting abundant and the music excellent. The program was exceptionally good. Various speeches were heard and songs were rendered, after which Mr. Vournas played the mandolin. When Mr. Vournas finished, dancing was in order.

The affair was given with the object of facilitating acquaintanceship among the members.
