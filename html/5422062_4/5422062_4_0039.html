p. 3- We hereby notify the members of the Messinian Brotherhood that on Sunday, March 4, 1923, will occur the election of the executive committee of the Brother-hood, in accordance with article no. 8 of its constitution.

The election will take place in the offices of the Brotherhood, Minerva Hall, 522 South Halsted street.

Applications for candidacy will be accepted up to February 26, 1923.

The auditing committee:

P. Katsavos, chairman.

Th. Spiropoulos, secretary.
