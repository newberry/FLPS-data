With delight we hear that Koliniates, established in Chicago, have come together and founded a society to be known as "Holy Virgin Mary."

Officers of the society are: Mr. K. N. Shiones, president; Mr. Elias K. Koulouris, vice president; Mr. S. Stanthas, secretary; Mr. George Remendjoulas, treasurer, and Mr. H. Layandreas, collector.

The goal of the newly organized fraternity is mutual assistance and its scope of operation is not only local but national as well.

As usual The Star wishes the Koliniates society, success and harmony.
