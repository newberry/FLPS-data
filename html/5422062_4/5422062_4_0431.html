The Society of Liberals gave a dinner in the Sherman Hotel for His Holiness Metropolite Meletiou of Greece. His Holiness spoke of the greatness and patriotism of Venizelos, and the liberal aid of the Greek Americans to their mother country.

Mr. Salopoulos then thanked His Holiness, and urged the audience to keep in mind the great need of the children and women in Greece, and urged them to contribute to the fund for their aid.
