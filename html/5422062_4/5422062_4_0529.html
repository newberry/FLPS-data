p. 1.- Sophocles' "Electra" will be given by Raymond Duncan at the Garrick Theater, Randolph and Dearborn. The affair is sponsored by Mr. Duncan. Proceeds will go towards the purchase of arms for the Greek Legion in America. The performance will be given Sunday, June 26, at 2 o'clock. All Greeks are urged to attend. Tickets will be sold at the offices of Loxias, 58 West Ontario Street, Third Flat, all day Saturday and till noon Sunday.
