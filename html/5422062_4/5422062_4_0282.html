If we remember correctly, the most successful dance during the last six years was that given by the Young Ladies South Side Club, Nea Genea. Over 5,000 people were present at the Trianon that night.

This same club is planning another dance for February 24, and expects a Larger number of people at the Trianon this year. The purpose is again philanthropic and the girls are working hard to make the evening a success.
