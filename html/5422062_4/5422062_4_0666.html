The Greek community around Blue Island Avenue and Halstead Street was encircled by the police who were looking for two Greek counterfeiters. They found the men they were seeking, in a Cafe at 610 Blue Island Avenue.

These men were K. Trigonis and Miltiades Sakkos. They have been circulating counterfeit twenty-five cent and fifty cent pieces. They were put in jail, and a search is being made for their accomplices.
