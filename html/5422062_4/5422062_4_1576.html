p. 6.- We remind all friends and members of the Chicago, Oak Park, and Evanston Chapters of the American Hellenic Educational Progressive Association (Ahepa) that March 25th is the hundredth anniversary of our Independence. Everyone should go to church on that day and give thanks for our liberation.

For this event, the united chapters of Ahepa are giving a celebration on March 30th at the Masonic Temple, 32 W. Randolph Street, at 7 P.M. Dancing will follow the program. The admission is free, so everyone can attend.
