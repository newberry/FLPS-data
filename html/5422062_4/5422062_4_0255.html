p. 5 We recently received a letter of thanks from Oak Park where many of our countrymen are confired in the sanitarium for tubercular diseases. They sent a letter thanking us for publishing an article concerning their plight. In that article we asked the various philanthropical clubs of Chicago to help our needy countrymen.

We are informed that aside from those suffering from tuberculosis there are many others there with other diseases.

We think it is a good idea for the various clubs to get together and give an affair the proceeds to go entirely to our suffering countrymen.
