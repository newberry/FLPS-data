p. 7.- The Greek firm, Chicago Pie Company, has under construction a two story building at 2430-42 West Congress St., at a cost of $200,000. The new addition to the company will serve as shops and will be equipped with modern improvements, where 25,000 pies a day will be made.

The Chicago Pie Co., which is under the successful management of Mr. Karalis, owes its tremendous business to its motto which stands for quality excellent service and cooperation of its workers, who take their work at heart.
