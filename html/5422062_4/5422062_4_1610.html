p. 3. Last Thursday the Greek churches of Chicago were overcrowded in celebration of the 25th of March, the day of Greek Independence.

At the cathedral of St. Constantine, Bishop Philaretos was officiating in a splendor of ecclesiastic solemnity. The Greek General Consul, Mr. Depastas and his wife, honored the celebration with their presence.

After the ritual, the Right Rev. Mr. Philaretos in an euphonious and well composed speech, so enthused the people, that hundreds of them were weeping.
