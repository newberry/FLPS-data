Elections for the year 1932 have taken place at the Gorgo chapter of the Gapa organization. President is Basiliki Bousious, Vice-President, Katina matsini; Secretary, K. Piloulas; and Treasurer, Olga Karopoulos. Board of advisors are: B. Prim , M. Koubelis, K. Brisiotis, A. Barouni and A. Nikolaou.

We know that the newly elected officers will do all in their power to make the chapter one of the biggest and best in the city.
