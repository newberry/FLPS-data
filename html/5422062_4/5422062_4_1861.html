Dr. John Volicos and Rev. E. Tsourounakis representing the various Cretan societies of Chicago, today left for Detroit Michigan, to participate as delegates, in the Pan-Cretan Congress, which opens its sessions next Monday.

Eleuterios Venizelos, prime minister of Greece, himself a Cretan and honorary president of the fraternity, wired felicitations to the convocation of the Pan-Cretans.
