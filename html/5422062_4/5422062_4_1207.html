p. 5.- On Dec. 19, the Navarinon chapter of Gapa held elections for the coming year. The members turned out in a body to vote, thus showing their interest and enthusiasm in this progressive chapter.

Newly elected officers are: James Athanasopoulos, president; E. Chioles, vice-president; J. Petro Hilos, treasurer; A. Tsetse Nekos, secretary; J. Kaskambas, master of ceremonies; K. Katsiroumbas, assistant to the master of ceremonies; and K. Stamatopoulos, A. Agrapidakis and G. Louis, trustees.
