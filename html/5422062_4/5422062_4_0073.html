p. 3.- On February 4th the Kosmitans had an account of $1,218.42. Three hundred and fifty dollars was deposited on March 8, and $100 on March 24th. The total sum deposited at the First Trust and Savings Bank of Chicago is $1,668.01. The treasurer has $92.41. The money is deposited under the name "Association Kosmitan St. Anargyros" with the signatures of the president, treasurer, secretary and four trustees.

Chicago, July 8, 1910

A. Apalodimas

5853 Wentworth Ave.
