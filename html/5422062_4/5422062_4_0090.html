P. 4- Natives of the town of Aphisou who have settled in Chicago met last Sunday at 12 South Clark Street and founded a fraternity to be known as the Holy Sunday Society of Aphisiotes.

A collection was taken up among those present, and the sum of one thousand dollars was raised immediately.

As temporary officers until a regular election can be held Messrs. Constantine A. Honoros, Pan Elias Liapacos, Theodire D. Colovos, Pan John Alemeses, and Charalampos Synadinos were appointed.

An Appeal was sent out to all Aphisioted in America to join the newly- formed Society.
