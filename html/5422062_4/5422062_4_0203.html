p. 5.- The dance, which was given by Nea Genea, a philanthropic society which is composed of Greek young women,at the Blackstone Hotel, was very successful.

Nine hundred Greeks, some of them very prominent, took part in the brilliant affair. The organizing committee of the society, comprising some of the smartest Greek young women, presented an unusual program.

Proceeds of the dance will be utilized for the benefit of needy Greek families in Chicago.
