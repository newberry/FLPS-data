Among the noteworthy events of the past week, is included the dance given by the Messinian Brotherhood last Sunday at Turner Hall. The dance was well attended and Mr. and Mrs. Xanthopoulos, Mr. and Mrs. C. Salopoulos of the Saloniki, Mr. and Mrs. K. Theodore and....were among those present.

....The dance committee....worked very hard and succeeded in making this affair a success. Its members acted as a reception committee and saw to it that a good time was had by all.
