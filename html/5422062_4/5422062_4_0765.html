Bruno J. and Elizabeth Krause have sold the property located at 2524 Ainslie St., 30 x 160, improved with a three flat brick building, containing three five room apartments to George and Elene Alexander, for a consideration of $28,000. subject to $18,000.

The purchasers gave, in part payment, the vacant business property, 25 x 125 sq. ft. at 2018 Cicero Ave., Cicero, III. for a consideration of $7,000. Anton H. Tomaras and James L. Holmes, 10 S. La Salle St., represented the purchasers.
