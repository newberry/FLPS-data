p. 5.- On Wednesday, February 3, the installation of Woodlawn Chapter No. 93 took place at the South Side Masonic Temple. Over one thousand Ahepans were there to witness this great spectacle.

Officers of the new chapter are P. Vasilos, president; C. Ganas, vice-pres.; A. Peponis, secretary; and A. Paziotopoulos, treasurer. An entertaining program and dancing followed the induction.

We are extremely proud of the Ahepans for their enormous progress and wish them continued success in the future.
