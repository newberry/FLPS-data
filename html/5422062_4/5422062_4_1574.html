p. 5.- Next Monday night at Evangelismos church will be celebrated the Esperinos of our national holiday. Tuesday morning Doxology will be said.

On the evening of March 25th, the church is giving its annual dance at the hall of St. James church. A program arranged by the children of the school will be presented before the dance. We are sure everyone will be satisfied and well-pleased with these arrangements. We know you will have a good time.
