Last Sunday, February 29, at 8:00 P.M., the performance of "Genovefa" took place. The theater was filled and the dramatic group scored a success.

During intermission, Father D. Hatzidimitriou gave a speech concerning the Greek orphan children in Constantinople. Mr. C. Mammonas spoke a few words at the close of the Father's speech. He appealed for money to aid these orphans. Contributions amounting to $295.45 were made after the performance. The money was sent to the Red Cross.

The actors and the people who attended the play are to be commended for supporting such a worthy cause.
