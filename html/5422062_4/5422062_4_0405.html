The Young Greek Ladies' Philomusical Society, whose efforts and activities are willingly supported by the noble Greek community of Chicago, gives its fourth ball on Thursday, November 16, at eight o'clock in the Cameo Room of the Morrison Hotel. The purpose of this ball is to [raise money] for the relief of the Asia Minor refugees. We are confident that all the Greeks of Chicago will honor this soiree with their presence. They should attend the dance not only because the program prepared is excellent, but [also] because the purpose which it aims to fulfill is sacred and philanthropic.
