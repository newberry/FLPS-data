Morris Limpemas, a notorious counterfeiter and forger of bad checks, attempted to cash a forged bank check at the confectionery store of our prominent countryman, Stylianos Georgakakis, 1551 West 13th Street. He was arrested and sentenced to fourteen years in the penitentiary.

Mr. Georgakakis was warmly congratulated by the chief of police for discovering this dangerous forger, who will now learn a good lesson. It is well for other Greek businessmen to be careful about cashing doubtful checks. Saloniki congratulates Mr. Georgakakis for reporting this criminal to the police in time.
