My dear Mr. Editor,

We, the Greek patients at Oak Forest, Ill. publicly express our gratitude and appreciation to the Greek ladies society St. Basil. for the kind visit they paid us and the many presents they brought to us.

We cannot find words, to fully express our feeling, for the courtesy extended to us sick boys by the honorable Greek ladies' society. This philanthropic society indeed is a credit to its name and to the Greeks in general.

We beg to remain,

Sincerely yours,

Greek Patients of

Oak Forest, Ill.
