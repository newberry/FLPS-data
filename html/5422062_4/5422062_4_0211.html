The philanthropic society, "Selasia", founded twenty years ago, has celebrated its anniversary. The activities and humanitarian deeds of Society are very well known. Since its founding tens of thousands of dollars have been expended to assist the members of the society, and thousands of indigent families were aided, widows and orphans receiving special attention.

The "Selasia" on July 4th will hold its 9th annual picnic and it has invited members and others to come. Admission is free. The picnic will take place at Brighton Park, 3619 S. Kedzie Ave.
