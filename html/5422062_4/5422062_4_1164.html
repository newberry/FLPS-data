A farewell party was neatly arranged for last Saturday evening, December 9, by the Greek ladies benevolent society "St. Helen," for t

he Hon. and Mrs. Depasta, who are leaving for Greece. The occasion was honored by the presence of the new consul, Mr. Paraskevopoulos, who is succeeding Mr. Depasta, the Rev. Tsourounakis of St. Andrew's Church, Rev. Petrakis and Deacon Glynos, and also numerous guests who are well known to the Parish, including Mr. Desfis, a soloist. Mrs. Zaharia Pappas, president of St. Helen's Society, spoke at the affair.
