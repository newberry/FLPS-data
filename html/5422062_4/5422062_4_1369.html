Chicago, March 14, 1911

Dear Sir:

You are cordially invited to attend the first installation of the first chapter of the Pan Hellenic Union. The installation will take place on Sunday, March 19, at 2 o'clock, at Bowen Hall of Hull House.

Among the honored guests will be N. Salopoulos, Greek Consul, and Spyros Matsoukas.

Organizer

J. Cheronis.

Secretary

P. Javaras.
