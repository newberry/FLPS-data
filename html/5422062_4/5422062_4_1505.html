p. 5.- Doxology for our National Greek Independence day will be said at Annunciation Church, next Friday, March 25th. The service will not start till one o'clock, to enable all local priests to be present after their local church services. George Depasta, General Consul of Greece in Chicago, and Bishop Callistos will be present.

The Greek Press urges all Chicago Greeks to be present at this special service which is in honor of Greece's independence from Turkey.
