p. 2. The Greek mystic society, Ahepa, in its last meeting, resolved to give a dance at the Trianon for the benefit of the Greek Refugees in Greece.

The dance undoubtedly will be a success, due to the fact that many Greek societies, the Near East Relief and civic authorities, have consented to participate in this philanthropic affair.

This dance banquet will take place the 10th of May, at the luxurious ballroom, the Trianon, 62nd street and Cottage Grove Avenue.
