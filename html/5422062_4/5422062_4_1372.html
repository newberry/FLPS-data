The Greek Consul has decided to hold a general meeting at Hull House on Sunday, January 1, 1911 in connection with the Panhellenic Union. Isn't it a shame to go to so much trouble to establish a Panhellenic Union in our midst!

We are not fellow of the Greek Consul and do not approve of all his plans but the Panhellenic Union is one thing which should have the cooperation of every Greek in Chicago.
