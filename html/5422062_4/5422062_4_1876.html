The highlight of the social part of Gapa's convention took place Wednesday evening. Over 2,000 people thronged the Aragon ballroom to show their loyalty to the Gapa organization. A pleasant surprise came in the form of Greek dancing by the members of the young Ladies "Icypselis" club in native Greek costumes.

Distinguished guests were the Ambassador of Greece, the General Consul of Greece, who had just arrived from New York and J. Kallimahos, editor of the Ethnikos Kyrikx (National Herald) of New York.
