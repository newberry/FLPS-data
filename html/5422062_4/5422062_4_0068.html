One of the most progressive and most active Greek organizations of Chicago is the Vervainon Greek Brotherhood. One of the aims of this organization is to build a new church on the ancient site of the Koimisis Tis Theotokou church in Vervainos, Greece.

The society presents the following financial report:

Assets for the year 1913 were $1,800.

Income was $1,242.50, and expenditures $69.05.

The sum of $1,067.05 was sent to the Church committee in Vervainos, Greece, on January 9, 1914. This left a balance of $173.45. A total of $17,000 has been sent to Greece for the aforesaid purpose during the period from 1901 to 1914.
