p. 5.- Harry Bouras, Supreme President of the Greek-American organization, Ahepa, arrived in Chicago, and was received at the railroad station by a committee headed by attorney Geo. Kyriakopoulos.

Mr. Kyriakopoulos, as Commander of the 21st circuit of Ahepa, sent out an announcements to local chapters for a general meeting inviting all to come and hear, for the first time, the recently elected Supreme President, who is to deliver a speech in the hall of the "46" chapter.
