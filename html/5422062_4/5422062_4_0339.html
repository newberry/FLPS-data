Last Monday night, the Greeks of Chicago congregated at the Trianon to honor "Manna", the Greek soldiers mother, and to contribute donations for the erection of a Tuberculosis Sanatorium for the Greek Soldiers of Greece. All the Greek societies of Chicago were represented. The Greek American Legion, sent a huge body of Legionnaires to honor and escort her. The Greek Consul of Chicago, as a speaker for the occasion explained the distinguished visitor, and complimented the crowd for their adherence to undying principles of generosity, patriotism and progress.
