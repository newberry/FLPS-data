On August 19, the anniversary of its foundation, the association of the Tsatistas of nothern Epirus, called the Society of the Transfiguration, celebrated the Transfiguration of Christ.

On this day a sumptuous dinner was served in the restautant at 511 North Clark Street. Music was provided with the banquet. Many toasts were proposed for the success of the Society. At the conclusion of the dinner a collection was taken, and the contributions amounted to $635. This sum will be augmented by collections soon to be taken in other cities of the United States.
