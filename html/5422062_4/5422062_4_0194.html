The Greeks of Chicago who have undertaken to present the first entertainment program for the unemployed of Chicago, already have complete preparatory arrangements for the most beautiful presentation ever given.

Monday night, at the Aragon, a rich program will be staged with Miss Matli, dancer, recently arrived from Greece and forty Grecian girls in ancient costumes who will enact an old tragedy, the Antigoni of Sophocles, under the direction of Mr. N. Tsoukalas, dancer.

Another group will give various songs. The Greek program will inaugurate the starting of the International programs for the benefit of the unemployed. Other nationalities will follow.
