Owing to the impending celebration of the centennial of Greek independence, a doxology will take place March 25, at the Cathedral of S. Basil. An invitation is sent to all the Greek societies, fraternities, veterans of the Balkan and World Wars, and all the Greeks of Chicago and suburbs to officially participate in uniforms.

The hour of doxology is 12:30 Noon.

The Consul General

G. Depastas.
