Phoenix, is the name of a Greek Society in Chicago. It is the biggest Greek organization in America, with 1,000 regular members and $4,137.89 surplus money in the bank.

The object of the Phoenix is to promote friendship, cooperation and mutual assistance. Every Greek in Chicago is invited to become a member of this society, which is ready and willing to assist its members in every way possible.

Mr. Andrew Vlachos, is president of this organization and Mr. Ep. Petropoulos, treasurer.
