p. 5.- Gapa invites all the Greek societies, organizations, veterans of the war, and all other Greeks of Chicago to attend the celebration of Greek independence, which will be held Sunday, March 30th, at 2:00 P. M., in the Majestic Hall of the Masonic Temple, 32 E. Randolph St., 14th floor.

The united lodges of the Order have prepared a highly enjoyable and varied program for the celebration. Free admission to all.
