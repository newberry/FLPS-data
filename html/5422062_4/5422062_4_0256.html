p. 4 The Ladies Filoptohos Club , (Friend of the Poor) of St. Basil Church, render thanks to the St. Andrews, St. James and Nea Genea clubs for cooperating with them in aiding needy families for Easter. Personal thanks are extended to Mrs. Bekares, Mrs. Duros, and Mrs. Petrakos of St. James church and Miss Floudas, past president of New Genea for their visits to hospitals where they cheered up and encouraged many patients.
