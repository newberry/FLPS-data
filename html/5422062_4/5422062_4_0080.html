p. 1.- Last Friday at 47th and State, the Kosmitan club elected officers for the coming year. These officers are C. Rigopoulos, president; A. Apalodimas, vice-president; J. Chamales, treasurer; E. Kontzias, secretary; N. Libanos, recorder; and advisers are A. Lambres, L. Kotsonis, N. Kilavos, A. Zografos, K. Lioanos and K. Roris. Speeches followed the election and the program for the coming year was discussed. The meeting lasted three hours.
