p. 1.- The national holiday, March 25th, which every single Greek home celebrates no matter in what part of the globe it happens to be located, was celebrated most splendidly by the community again this year. The holy masses were held, as usual, in all churches.

The stores of Halsted Street, this excellent Greek center, were decorated with flags showing the love that the Greek proprietors have for this exceptional national day. The "Kentrikon Book Store" was especially decorated for the occasion. Many were thrilled because this great Greek holiday that might have passed unnoticed.
