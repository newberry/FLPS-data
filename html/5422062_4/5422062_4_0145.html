Hull House arranged a celebration in honor of Miss Alexandrakis, who will soon leave the House. It was attended by a large number of people who came to bid Miss Alexandrakis goodbye. Refreshments were served to all the guests. A summary showing the benefit of Hull House to the Greek community was read.

A program for an entertainment consisting of Greek dancing and singing was arranged for December 3, to be held in Bowen Hall of Hull House. The entertainment was planned for the young Greek people mostly.
