Joseph W. O'Connor and his wife, Edith, sold the lots located at 1635-39 East 79th Street to Theodore Liapes, Samuel Andreas, and Peter Tatules for the price of $100,000. This property fronts 60 ft. on 79th Street near Stony Island Avenue and is improved with a two-story brick building containing three stores, six offices, apartments, and garages, and the site for the new theater which will soon be built with a 2,500 seat capacity.

Tutules and Dignan represented both parties.
