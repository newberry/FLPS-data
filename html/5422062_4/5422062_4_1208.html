The young ladies of Kipseli Branch of the Greek Society, Gapa, will give their first dance on Sunday, Jan. 19, 1931, in the Cameo Room of the Morrison Hotel.

The program of the dance will be in two parts. The first part will commence at 7:30 P.M., with famous classical and modern Greek dances by Constant Nichols, who will appear through the courtesy of R. Sparks, the producer of Lysistrata. Mr. Nichols will dance eight classical dances. The second part of the program will be devoted to the modern dance.
