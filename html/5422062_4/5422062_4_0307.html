In order to systematize the collections for earthquake stricken Corinth, a committee of one hundred formed to accelerate the collection work.

This laity Corinthian Relief Committee is composed of business men, professionals, journalists and many enterprising Greeks of Chicago.

The President of the committee chosen was Mr. Constantine A. Paleologos Mamonas, banker; Mr. Paul Demos, attorney, as secretary; and as treasurer, Mr. John Raklios, president of the firm, Raklios and Company.
