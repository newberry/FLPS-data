Wor. Bro. Thomas George Leventis, Installing Master. Assisted by

Right Wor. Bro. Albert T. Thompson

Wor. Bro. Demos J. Melebes, Marshall

Wor. Bro. George K. Johnson, Chaplain

Wor Bro. Voselios C. Vaselopoulos, Secretary
