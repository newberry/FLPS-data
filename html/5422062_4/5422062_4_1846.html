The annual elections of the Greek-American organization Gapa, of new officers, took place last Sunday at Gary, Ind.

After an all night session, at which four ballotings took place the following officers were elected: Attorney Geo. Triantafilakos of Gary, Grand President of the organization; A. Mountanos, Vice-President; Dem. Theophilatos, Grand Secretary; George Kounes, Grand Treasurer;

Mrs. Olga Kallimachos, was elected Grand Counselor of the Womens' chapters. Commanders of the circuits are:
