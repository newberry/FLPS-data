The directorate of the Chicago Branch held its regular monthly meeting at the Bismarck Hotel. President Walter thanked the departing directors, Dr. Rudolf Wagner, Max Wild, and W. Fischer and greeted their successors, the newly elected staff, Emil G. Brill, Dr. H. Gerhard, and Charles Heckl. Several clubs and a number of individuals became affiliated with the Alliance. A protest against an impending prohibition law in the District of Columbia was sent to the Senators and Representatives in Washington. A special session has been arranged for next Monday evening in order to discuss questions still pending and to make the necessary preparations for a practical, energetic, and effective political campaign.
