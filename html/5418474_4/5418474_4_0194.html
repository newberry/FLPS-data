The German Democrats of Chicago had last Saturday afternoon a lively mass-meeting at Jung's Hall, Belmont and Lincoln Avenue. The main speakers were A. S. Trude, S. Goldzier and H. Rubens.

Several German Singing Societies were also representad at this meeting and gave intermittedly very fine Chorus-song recitations Mr. Trude closed the meeting with the announcement, that Democrats would be victorious in every part of the State during the coming election.
