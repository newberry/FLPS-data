Lies have fulfilled their aim. Knavery has won. A. C. Hesing has been beaten. The man who always took the part of his fellow countrymen has been forsaken by them, at least by a great number of them.

Many Germans have believed the infamous lies of Medill and Storey and have in great number voted for a tax cheater. Neither have the Irish helped Hesing. Although they did not vote for Huck, they gave most of their votes to Keeley.
