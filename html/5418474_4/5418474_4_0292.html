A number of German-American citizens of the Fifth Ward held a meeting yesterday at 2311 Wentworth Avenue, in order to establish a German-American Democratic Club.

Mr. S. Schrenk opened the meeting with a brief address, in which he emphasized that many German-Americans would be much more interested in politics, if political affairs could be discussed in German. It is the expressed purpose of this organization to attract such Germans as members to this club.

After this the officials of the club were elected, and then the meeting was concluded with the announcement that the next meeting will be held at the same place on the following Saturday.
