A new German-Democratic League has been formed and incorporated in the State with Joseoh Happ, Pres., Herman Fieldl, Sec.,Eugen Niederegger, Treas. Mr. Niederegger is the well-known President of the Schwaben Club.

The organization will have branches in all counties of the State. "The object is to maintain Germanmanners and morals and to see that German interests in public life are not neglected. There will be no job-hunting but we want recognition of German activities in the Middle West in culture, science and industry."

Mr. Niederegger announced yesterday.
