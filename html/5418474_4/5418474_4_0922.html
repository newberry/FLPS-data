Our taxpayers had a sweeping success at yesterday's city elections. Never before had a party received in Chicago such a large majority of votes as was the case yesterday.

The taxpayers chose officials whose program was:

Reform and Economy. Previous to this, in 1873 Colvin received the largest majority of votes. Then, the Germans and Irish joined forces; this time the Germans and Americans were allies.

Both times the Germans were credited with casting the majority votes...
