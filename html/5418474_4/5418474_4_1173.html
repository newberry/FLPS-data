A. C. Hesing has through the following letter tendered his resignation as Lincoln Park Commissioner to the governor:

"To Hon. John L. Beveridge, Governor:

"I herewith tender my resignation as member of the Lincoln Park Board. Said resignation is to take effect at once.

A. C. Hesing."

As Governor Beveridge is away, he has not been able to answer as yet. During his two years as Lincoln Park Commissioner, A. C. Hesing has done much to help beautify the park. Once the park will be finished, his fellow citizens will think of A. C. Hesing in grateful remembrance.
