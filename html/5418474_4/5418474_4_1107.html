The Democratic Party offered Raster $22,500 for the presidential campaign. As this is not enough for the Staata Zeitung, Raster declares vast majorities for the Republicans all over in his paper.

Raster admits that the principle of his old sweetheart, the Democratic party is to make money and not to serve the commonwealth. It is time for the Cleveland followers to raise their bid to the demanded $50,000, otherwise Raster will join the agitating bums.
