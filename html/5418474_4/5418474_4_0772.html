In a letter Mayor Swift informed the City Council that in place of Mr. Wm. Boldenweck, who wanted to resign already under Mayor Harrison, but could not get it granted until now, he has nominated Louis O. Kohtz, the well-known member of the "Chicago Turngemeinde" and the"Germania Maennerchor" as member of the School board.

The nomination was ratified by the City Council without any delay. Mr. Kohtz is a long time resident of the 25th ward.
