P. 3 - The Central Committee of the German-American Democrats, composed of representatives from every German-American club of Cook County, resolved at its last meeting, to indorse Frank Wenter for re-nomination. His ability as a public servant, in his present capacity as president of the drainage department, is so well known, that we recommend his re-nomination. The Central Committee of the Democratic party of Cook county, urges the nomination and election of this deserving public servant.
