The Executive Board of the "Amnesty Association of Illinois" accepted in their meeting at 225 Dearborn Street yesterday, a resolution, to publish a history of the Amnesty Club. Same will be published in 50,000 copies in the German and English languages.

Each copy will be sold at a low price and the proceeds shall go to the families of the three pardoned anarchists. The report will contain several petitions and letters, which were dent to Governor Altgeld and will before all describe the efforts, that were undertaken, to obtain the Amnesty for the three Anarchists. Final resolutions will be made in the next meeting of the Board in Tuesday evening.
