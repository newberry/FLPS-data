This evening, Saturday, July 19th, at 8.P.M. a meeting for election reform will be at the Sherman House, at which occasion the Central board of the league will be represented. If any branch organization of the League wish to be represented at this meeting, they are requested to send delegates, but not more than 3 each. Their names should be sent to room 19-24 in the Staats zeitung Building, where the delegates papers are made out.
