The German-American Republican Central Club of Cook County met at Schiller Hall last night. German Republican delegates from Will County attended the very animated meeting in large numbers. The purpose was explained by the chairman, Mr. Franz Amberg. He said, that the formation of a State Association of German Republicans of Illinois, is the present plan of the Central Club. Speeches enlightened the meeting as to the necessity of a German State's organization. Thereafter, Mr. Joseph Brucker proposed the nomination of a special committee, to whom the task of the preparations for an agitation program shall be assigned.

At the meeting called for May 6th, this committee will submit its plan, for the proposed organization, to the assembly for acceptance. According to the report of the delegates from Will County, the German-Republican Club of Will County has already more than 600 members.
