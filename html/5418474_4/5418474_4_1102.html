In Baumann Hall at the corner of Blue Island and Lincoln Avenue, a German-American Citizen's League has been formed, and up to the present one hundred and forty seven members have been initiated.

The League intende chiefly to deal with municipal affairs. The following officers have been elected. Hermann Scheth, Pres. Henry Baumann, Vice President; Capt. Sindig, secretary and Carl Bounker, treasurer. Founders of the League are Edward Hofling and Charles Braun.
