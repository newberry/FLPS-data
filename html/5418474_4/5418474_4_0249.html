The German Democrats of the 24th Ward have organized and have elected the following officers: President, Joseph Schlenker, Vice President, Richard Von bach, Secretary, Prank Arndt, Financial Secretary, George Miltzer, Treasurer, O. L. Woback. Also an agitation Committee was chosen.

Everybody who wants to become a citizen or wishes political information, can call at the Saronia House, corner of Wells and Ohio.
