The German Democrats of the 26th Ward held a meeting yesterday at Post's Hall, which was presided by C. E. Zimmermann and the following resolution was passed:

The German Democratic Club of the 26th Ward, "expresses its disappointment, disapproval and contempt at its regular constitutional meeting on the action of the Congress and the Senate in the World's Fair question."

It is the wish of the German Democratic Club, that this resolution should be published in the whole German Press and that a Committee should translate same into the English language, and also have it published in the American press.
