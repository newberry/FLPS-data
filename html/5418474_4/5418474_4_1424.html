Has the Washingtonian Home any right to accept yearly help from the municipal authorities in the amount of $20,000 Without giving the city any king of rights whatsoever?

There are twelve members of the board of directions and who are getting paid for mismanaging the foundation's million dollar fund.

The expenditure for food alone during the last year amounted to $12,500 which average, for the 73 inhabitants of this delirium tremens asylum, $14 per month.

The broom of reform should be put into action in this mismanagement and the whole scandalous affair, including its managers, be cleared out.
