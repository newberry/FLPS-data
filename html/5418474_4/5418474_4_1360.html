All the German citizens to whom the right to vote was denied for any reason whatsoever, at the recent Democratic primary election, are urgently requested to report immediately, either personally or by letter, to the Illinois Staats-Zeitung, Rooms 32 and 39, in the Staats-Zeitung's building. We wish to hear from all the citizens, who have been insulted or attacked by the Carter H. Harrison clique, as well as from those who can give any information about fraudulent actions and procedures at the primary election. The Staats-Zeitung's building is located at the corner of Fifth Avenue and Washington Street.
