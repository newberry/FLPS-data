Editorial endorses, enthusiastically, program of seven points that South-German "Friends of Liberty" have mapped out for their activity in the New Reich:

1. Addition of a declaration of fundamental rights to the constitution comparable to the first ten amendments to the U. S. Constitution.

2. A responsible ministry.

3. Army budget passed annually by the Reichstag.

4. Salary for members of the Reichstag.

5. Shortening of compulsory military service.

6. Make Reichstag co-responsible for declarations of wat and treaties of peace.

7. Far-reaching support for invalids, war-widows, etc.
