Under the presidency of Mr. Emil Hochster, a meeting was held yesterday evening in the Northside Turner Hall of the delegates to the "United Societies for Local Self-government," at which Mr. Mathias Huss acted as secretary.

A permanent Organization will be effected at the same hall on August 4th. The secretary of the United Societies will be requested, to ask the Northside societies, to send their delegates to the next meeting.
