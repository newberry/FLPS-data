Chief of Police Hubbard is said to be about to resign his post. His principle in employing policemen is to select them for ability and not politics.

The Bosses of the Chicago Republican Party, Swift, Swift, Davis & Co., do not agree with those principles and have been trying, since Mayor Roche came into office to put the orthodox - Republican stamp on the whole police force.

Hubbard is in many ways too honest - he is called in some authoritative quarters "Sleepy Head".
