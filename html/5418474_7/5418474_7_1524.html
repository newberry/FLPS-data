As previously reported, a great bazaar will be held at the Coliseum, beginning tonight and lasting till Friday, for the benefit of the Guardian Angel Orphan Asylum in High Ridge. The proceeds are to be used for the erection of a new building, so that the overcrowing of the orphan asylum will be prevented. Therefore an appeal is made to the Catholics and the general public of Chicago, to participate actively at the bazaars so that this human work may be carried out to its fullest extent.

There will be no lack of enjeyment,because a very interesting and rich program has been mayed out. On the eyeing of the opening of the Bazaar Archbishop Quikley will make a speech, the St. Michael's Men's Chorus, under the direction of Professor Edelmann will sing, and three Catholic Societies will meet at a "Club evening" For the three afternoons of Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday a children's festival and a maiden's club coffee party is in Prospect. Tuesday evening the Consuls of Germany, Austria and 2Switzerland will visit the festival, and Thursday evening Governor Dineen will make a speech. Friday evening the Festival closes with a raffle and general joyfulness.
