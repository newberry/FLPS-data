Der Gesangverein Liederkranz von Avondale (The Song-club "Songwreath" of Avondale) held its bi-annual meeting, including election last Friday, Jan. 17th. The following officials were elected. President Wilhelm Schroeder, Vice President Peter Busch. The secretary states that during the 9 months of the clubs existence, membership mounted to 27; 16 active; 11 passive. Although the association is still in its infancy, it has accomplished much and is quite ambitious.
