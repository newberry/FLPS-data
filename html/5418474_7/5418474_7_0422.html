Tonight at 7 P. M. the monthly entertainment of the Reich Flag Black-Red-Gold takes place at the organization house, 1347 Otto Street.

In response to many requests, this entertainment will start with a concert. Classical music, played by a quintet under the conductor Johnk, and aided by Richard Blassee, will be given. After the concert there will be a dance. Admission price is thirty cents per person. Every one is invited.

Following is the program:

1. Opening march "Treue um Treue", by Teile

2. Fantasy from "Faust" by Gounod,

3. Hungarian dances Numbers 5 and 6, by Brahms.

\2

4. Intermezzo: "Ein Fruehlingstraum", by Micheli

5. "Largo" from Xerzes", by Haendel

6. Recitation: "Die Buergschaft", by Richard Blassee

7. Fantasy: "Reminiscences of Schiller's Works", by Johnk

8. "March of the Reich Flag", by Blankenburg.
