The well-known Song and Mutual Benefit Association "Harmony" held its seventh annual ball last Saturday at the Mesters hall. The climax of the evening was reached when the former vice-president of the organization, Mr. P. Windbiel gave a splendid speech which came from his heart and went to the hearts of the hearers.

The financial conditions of the association are favorable and its membership is steadily increasing, having now a total of 85.
