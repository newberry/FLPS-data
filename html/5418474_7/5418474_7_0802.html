At Northwestern University, on February 12, the German Society and the German Literary Society will give a concert, consisting exclusively of German songs, to celebrate Lincoln's birthday. The Germania Maennerchor, Mrs. Theodore Brentano, and Mr. Charles Dupie (baritone) have agreed to give their co-operation, with the result that an exceptionally rich and solid program can be prepared.

Speeches will be made by President Henry Wade Rogers, Dr. Carl Buentz, the German Consul, and Judge Theodore Brentano.
