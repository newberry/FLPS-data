Henrik Ibsen's play "A Doll's House" was presented in German last Sunday evening at Powers Theatre. The play drew a full house. The German actors who give plays at Powers Theatre on Sunday evenings are, on the whole, able people, and the presentation of "A Doll's House" was praiseworthy.

Nora's role was presented by Pauline Wirth who appeared to have taken her task very seriously and executed it with great ability. Advocate Helmer was presented by Ludwig Lindikoff.

The German translation by Wilhelm Lange was used.
