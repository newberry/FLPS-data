The first concert of the "Schleswig-Holstein Glee Club" took place last night at the Yondorf Hall. Music and song were under the direction of Mr. O. W. Richter. Members and guests showed a lively interest in the performances.

Following the concert, which had began at 3 o'clock P.M. a ball was held which lasted until the early hours.

The Club was founded in 1882 and has 80 members.
