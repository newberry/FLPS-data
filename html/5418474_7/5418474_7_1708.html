In Folz' Hall the German Engineers' Club, No. 23, held its 3rd masquerade hall, last Saturday evening, which gave full satisfaction to all attending. The attendance was splendid, most of them remaining till early morning. No disturbance spoiled the enjoyment and the members of the Committee did their very beast to make this an outstanding festival, which will remain for same time in the memory of the guests.

The following members of the Committee have earned the full appreciation of all those who attended this enjoyable celebration! Otto Ruhr, Paul Hoffmann, Charles Schlinkert, Wilhelm Holzbauer and Max Weifsmueller.
