The Socialist, Mannerchor of the North side will hold their next meeting Sunday, May 4th at 2:00 o'clock in the afternoon at Bleimour's Hall, 58 Clybourn Ave. The members are requested to attend in large number. The active members are requested to attend the ball which the Lehr and Wehr Verein is giving Saturday, May 3d at the Aurora Turnhalle. It is expected that the members will meet in the Tavern, net later than 8:15 o'clock in the evening.
