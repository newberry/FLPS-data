Helene Wagner, the famous recitalist, will open tomorrow in Hershey Hall, Madison Street opposite Mcvicker's Theatre, a Recital cycle. Her program is: Sunday May 18, 8 o'clock P.M., "Tannhauser," by Julius Wolf. Followed by "Humoristicals," Saturday, May 24, half past 2 o'clock in the afternoon, 1st part: "Mrs. Holde" by Rudolf Baumbach. 2nd part: "The Visit in Jail," Humorous sketch by E. Eckstein. Sunday, May 25, 8 P.M., "The Wild Hunter" by Julius Wolf. After this came a humorous piece. The beautiful young lady is preceded by an excellent reputation and we hope that the Germans will fully support her.
