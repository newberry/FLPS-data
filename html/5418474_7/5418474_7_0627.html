The performance of "Paradise and Peri," which was given for the benefit of Uhlich's Orphans' Home, was exceptionally successful.

The director of the Chicago Singverein, Mr. Ernst Kruetgen, president, and Miss Meta Rathjon, secretary, informed the Abendpost today of the splendid success of the recently arranged performance. Almost $2,000 clear profit was handed over to the Uhlich's Orphans' Home.
