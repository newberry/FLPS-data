The ladies' club of St. Elizabeth Hospital will hold its annual sale tomorrow, Thursday, from 1 P.M. until 10 P.M., in the lecture hall of the hospital, 1410 North Oakley Boulevard.

One will have there the opportunity to acquire, at low prices, not only useful things for the coming Christmas festival, but all sorts of entertainment. From 5.30 until 7.30 P.M., a delicious supper will be served for only fifty cents. The "lords of creation" are also welcome, and they will be able to obtain beer and buttered bread at any time.
