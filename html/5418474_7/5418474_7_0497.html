The Vereinigte Damenchoere von Chicago (United Ladies' Choirs of Chicago) will sing this afternoon, between 3 and 4 P.M., at the World's Fair in the Food and Agricultural Building. The part of the concert to be given between 3:15 and 3:30 P.M. will be heard over the radio--station WMAQ.

Under the direction of Mr. A. H. Rehberg the following songs will be rendered:

1) "Santa Lucia" (a Neapolitan folk song) ......

2) "Grossmama" (Minuet) ....................... Beethoven

3) "Schnsucht nach den Bergen" ............... Abt

4) "Lorelei" ....................................... Silcher

5) "Der Spielmann ist da" ....................... Stein

6) "Ans weiten Fernen" ......................... Somet

7) "Rosebuds" .................................... Geibel
