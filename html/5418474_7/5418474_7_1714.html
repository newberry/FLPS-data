The Labor Aid Society gave an entertainment with a ball last Saturday evening at Yondorff Hall.

The program was a selection of pleasing songs.

The monologue " Das Schneeweisse Haar" (The Snow White Hair), recited by Mr. Ch. Keiter, was a remarkable performance and should be mentioned particularly. All festivity-arrangments were in the hands of Messrs. C. A. Thielmann, Wm. Farmendt, H. F. Siebach and Wm. Klein.

A dance kept the folks together till late in the night.
