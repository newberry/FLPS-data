There was pleasure and enjoyment yesterday evening at Folz's Hall such as can seldom be witnessed. The "Sozialistische Mannersgor," which held its Mask-ball , must be given credit for the success. Everything went smoothly.

Amusing were the Mask-jokes: "The Mixup in the Mill," "The Barber of Seville," and the "Animal Quartet", when our reporter left the locality at a late hour the pleasure temperature of Prince Carnival rose above 100 degrees.

That he, under the circumstances, would have preferred to linger somewhat longer at Folz's Hall, instead of returning to 5th Avenue on such a cold night, needs no further explanation.
