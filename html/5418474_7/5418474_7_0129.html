As we have announced already, Director Wormser will open the second theater-season of the Graper Opera House, at State and 40th Streets. He has done his utmost to make it a success. Also he has increased and improved his troupe of actors considerably. As usual, the Henschel Orchestra will furnish the music.

The first play under Wormser's direction will be, "Die Schoene-Ungarin" (The Beautiful Hungarian). Wormser will fill the cast of Mieseberk, himself.

The charming actress Bella St. Claire will play the title role. All other roles are in complete hands.
