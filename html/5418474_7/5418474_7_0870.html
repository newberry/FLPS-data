A large Music Festival will begin on May 27th in the Exposition Building and continue till Saturday. Those participating as Soloists are: Materna, Winkelman, Scaria and Mrs. Nilsson. The program consists among other of Haydn's "Creation" with Mrs. Nilsson; parts from Wagner's Tannhauser, Berlioz's Requiem Mass, Handel's Te Deum, etc. Matinee concerts will be given Thursdays and Saturdays.

The choir consists of 900 voices. The music festival will be under the direction of Theodore Thomas.-
