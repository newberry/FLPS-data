This affair, arranged by the Northside Lodges of the order of the "Harugari," will take place Saturday, March 1, 1884, in the Nordseite Turn-halle. As a special attraction, a large stage performance by the dramatic club of the Lassalle Lodge, called "Prince carnival's Roundtrip about Chicago in 80 Minutes" (containing 5 pictures and one tableau), will be performed. Everything is being done to offer the public an enjoyable evening.
