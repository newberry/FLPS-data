Last night at 8 o'clock the members of the Song Club, "Eintracht Liederkranz", met at the hall at West 12th St. No. 376. They went accompanied by their ladies to the residence of Mr. Phillip Kastler, 241 Blue Island Ave. one of their members. They surprised him with a serenade in honor of his 10 years of wedded life. After that they were invited into the house and enjoyed themselves with music, dancing and singing till early in the morning. The jubilant pair responded freely with their splendid hospitality.
