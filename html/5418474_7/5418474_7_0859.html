The German councils "Evergreen No. 16 and Concord No. 41, of the order of selected friends will install its elected officers, Sunday 19th at 3 o'clock in the afternoon. Prior and after installation a grand concert under the capable leadership of Prof. S. Werteim has been arranged, so (that) the Germans of the Southside will have a suitable opportunity for a social gathering at Livores Hall, corner 43rd & State Sts.
