The Singing Circle will hold its annual Mask-ball this evening in the Vorwarts-Turn-halle.

The program arranged for same contains a Tableau with large shifting scenes, a dance of the Bears and a picture: Gambrinus (God of Beer) in the midst of his drinking companions with song, also "The Harper Bill" to "Who Gets the Milk"? and lastly: "A Modern Patient" or "The Doctor in Trouble."

It will be as usual the best of amusements.
