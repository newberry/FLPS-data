This recently organized society is composed of members of the St. Theresia community. Although in existence only one month. it gave its first concert last evening at Yondorf's Hall, North Avenue and Halsted Street. The well selected program included "Brotherhood" by Abt, "The Hymn to the Night" by Beethoven, "Springtime" by Becker, sung by the talented young singer Miss Agnes Rapp, and the "Serenade" by Lombard, executed by the song section of the St. Michael's Casino under the excellent direction of Albert Sieben.
