Dr. Faust's "Zauberkappchen" (Magic Cap) was played last Sunday at Freiberg's Operahouse and had a full house.

Moritz Hahn, Leon Deutsch, Alexander Bauer, S. Franz and the ladies, E. Nosta, von Bergere, Richter, Wagner, were excellent in their individual casts.

The audience was pleased and did not spare with applause.
