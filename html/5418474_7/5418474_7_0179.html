The comedy "Wo ist die Katz?" (Where is the Cat?), is one of the best plays, we have ever seen at the Labor Hall. Last night's performance was a real success, as indicated by a full house and the warm applause of the cheering audience.

Particular praise must be accorded to the ladies Wagner, Rossi, Kraus and Winter, also to Messrs. Roesecke, Sispel, Alvin and Tempel.
