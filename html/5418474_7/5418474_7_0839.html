Last Saturday, Mr. Katzenberger received applications for voice tests at the North side Turnhalle, 350 children applied and it was decided to have 310 reports back.

Sometime during the coming week, Mr. Katzenberger will test the voices of the other applicants, which by now has numbered almost 1000 and at the same time he will organize various classes.

Further information as well as applications can be obtained at all times at the above address or at Mr. Katzenberger's residence.
