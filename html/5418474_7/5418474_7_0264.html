Under the direction of Mr. William Pelissier in the Summer Theatre in Miller's Garden, corner North Avenue and Clark Street, there will be presented, towmorrow afternoon and evening, three very attractive plays. They are: "The family Discord" and "The Jealous Wife," comedies by Kotzebue, and the one act Singing farce, "The Saxon Village Schoolmaster," by Jacobson.

The Summer-Theatre has earned the German Public's appreciation, and Mr. Pelissier may well depend on a large attendance at tomorrow's performances.
