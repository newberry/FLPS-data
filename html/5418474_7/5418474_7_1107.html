On Thursday and Friday there will be a bazaar for the benefit of the church in the parish hall of the Evangelical Church of St. Paul, Fullerton Avenue and Orchard Street, announces Pastor Dr. George L. Scherger. Opportunity is offered to acquire there not only useful objects of all kinds at moderate prices, but also to meet Miss Elinor Huesman in person, who won the beauty contest between various nationalities at the World's Fair as Miss Germany and was later crowned as Miss Century of Progress.

The Uhlich Orphanage, in which more than two thousand children, during the last sixty-seven years, have found shelter and faithful care, is a St. Paul parish foundation.
