The "Wildieb" (Game Thief) is the title of a play which was given for the first time yeaterday at this theatre had an unusual success, as in all other places where this play has been shown. There was a full house and the main roles were played by good actors.

"Burhanna" was performed by Mr. C. Alvin, "Ebert" by Mr. Rosesecke, "Gruener" by Mr. Liepel, "Frieda" by Miss Liebig. The smaller parts were also filled conveniently. It was a perfect and enjoyable evening.
