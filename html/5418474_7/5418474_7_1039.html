The German Society will give a big presentation on December 6th in the North Side Turner Hall. The Society needs money on account of the impending winter. Need and unemployment are widespread.

It is true that the German Society was founded to help the immigrants, but during the last years it also has had to take over the care of the needy Germans already established.

The Wurster troop will cooperate with the German Society and produce the comedy "Ein Schutzgeist".
