The German Waiter's Club held this year's fancy dress ball in Brand's Hall. It was very well attended. The masks were numerous and costumes well chosen. Numerous guests were in attendance. Nothing was lacking as the Arrangement Committee had done all that was possible. Unlimited joyfulness reigned the whole evening till the early morning hours; and with certainly it can be said that the vistors of the festival counted the time spent at the masquerade as one of the joyful and agreeable of the season. Messrs. Paul Mahlen, Emanuel Marx, Joseph Cohen, Henry Sebach, H. Ulrich, Aug. Mansel, Aug. Overdick, the members of the Committee, can be congratulated upon this great success.
