According to the theatrical column, five German stock companies functioned at that period, this includes traveling and resident enterprises.

(1) Ernst Tossart in "The bloody marriage (Karl IX) at the Auditorium (yesterday)

(2) German Theatre at McVickers (Sunday) Miss Marie Kronan, residence theatre of Berlin.

(3) German Popular Theatre, Aurora Turnhall, "Lumpaci Vagabundus."

(4) Apollo Theatre on Blue Island Ave. Next Sunday: Mr. Hepner's company in the forceful play "The discharged Convict."

(5) Freiberg's Opera House, Sunday 12th "From step to step," by Dr. Hugo Mueller.
