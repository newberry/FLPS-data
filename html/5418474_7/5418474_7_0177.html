"Die Tochter der Holle" (The Daughter of Hall), by Rudolf Kmeisel, was played last Sunday at the Labor Hall. The main casts were filled by C. Alvin and F. Rothmeyer, who deserved the repeated applause of the audience.

Also Messrs. Felshaus, Henke and Tempel were at their best, while Mrs. Raabe was playing the main cast excellently.
