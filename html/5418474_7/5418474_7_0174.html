The project of a new home for the German Theater is approaching its realization. During the last two weeks, $14,500.00 have been collected for the building funds. The total of all collections is to-day $331,600.00.

The meeting of share-holders of the New Theater Association will be to-night at the Orpheus Hall, 49 La Salle Street. Among other objects, the purpose of the meeting is the election of new Directors.
