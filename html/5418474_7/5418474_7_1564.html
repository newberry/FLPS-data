New Ulm, Minn. Sept. 25th,- Today was the main festival day in connection with the Convention of the order of the "Hermannssoehne" by which the beautiful Hermann monument was consecrated.

The election of officers had the following result; Grand Lodge President: Julius Schuetze, the well known German-Texan Editor, First Vice President: Aug. Behrens of Illinois, Second Vice President: Waldemar Stein of Montant, Grand Lodge Trueasurer: Knnrad Walther of Illinois, Grand Lodge Secretary; Richard Schaefer of Connecticut.
