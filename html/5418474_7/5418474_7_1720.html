Yesterday constituted the second evening of the National Turn Verein's great fair and the multitude was in the best of humor. The ladies were entrusted with the raffling of prizes.

Miss Fuellgraff, piano virtuoso, and Heinken's orchestra were the featurs of the evening. For tonight the program will be:-
