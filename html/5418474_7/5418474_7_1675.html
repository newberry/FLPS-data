To-day the celebration of the erection of the German House took place in the grounds of the World's Fair, an affair which is observed in many parts of Germany. The foreman of the Masons went to the roof in the afternoon, held a speech and handed the building over to the Fair Commissioner, who had the German Flag hoisted and turned over the building to the General Superintendent, after which the American Flag was waving besides the German.

The Workmen, who took an active part in the erection of the building, were festively entertained and the festival will be concluded this evening, with a banquet in the home of the German Commission, corner 32nd Court and Graceland Avenue.
