Through the initiative of the John Sobieski Political Club of the Tenth Ward, a meeting was held Sunday, February 8, in St. Casimir Parish. Many things were discussed and the following resolutions were adopted:

1. Considering it our duty to support all Poles, regardless of partisan following, we endorse the young and energetic Stanislaus Kuflewski, brother of the well-known Dr. Kuflewski, as a candidate for the office of West Town Clerk.

2. Realizing that during the past two years the alderman of this ward only made promises sans actions, we, therefore, have indorsed the well-known Peter Biewar as a candidate for alderman of the Tenth Ward.

J. Rosinski, secretary.
