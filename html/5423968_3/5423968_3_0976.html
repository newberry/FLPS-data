Two Polish Democratic mass meetings were held yesterday in South Chicago. The first was held in Templin's Hall, 88th Street and Commercial Avenue, at 4 P. M. Speeches were made by A. J. Kowalski, John Koziczynski, and Mr. White.

The second meeting was held in the evening at Retman's Hall, 84th Street and Buffalo Avenue. John Kondziorski, Frank White, and J. Koziczynski were the speakers.

We understand both political meetings were successes.
