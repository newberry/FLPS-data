At a meeting held on Tuesday, March 20, the Polish Saloonkeepers Society, Local 13, unanimously endorsed Stanley Adamkiewicz and Stanley Kunz for aldermen of the Seventeenth Ward and Fifteenth Ward.

The Fabian S. Klonowicz Society, Group 641 of the Polish National Alliance, unanimously endorsed Stanley Adamkiewicz for alderman of the Seventeenth Ward at its meeting of March 17.
