The primary election of delegates to the West Town Convention will take place today from 1 P. M. to 7 P. M. All Poles in our ward ought to vote for the delegates chosen by the Central Democratic Club of this ward.

As we see by the list, twenty-three out of twenty-nine candidates are Poles. The convention which meets tomorrow morning at Bricklayers' Hall will choose candidates for (West Town) assessor, supervisor, clerk, collector, and constable.

Joseph Gieszkiewicz is seeking the nomination for clerk. All Polish delegates and Polish citizens should support their countryman.
