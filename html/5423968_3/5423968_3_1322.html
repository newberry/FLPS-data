At the heavily attended meeting of last Sunday evening, several speakers indicated the necessity of developing the Democratic Club of Polish-American citizens of the Sixteenth Ward. Some thirty-four new members enrolled in the club, which now numbers one hundred and eight members. A special committee was also assigned to take charge of the introductory work of the club, and to call a large citizens mass meeting for the coming Sunday. The following were members of this committee: Peter Kiolbassa, John Kwasigroch, John Biniak, Stanley Kunz, A. J. Kowalski, and Louis Roland.

Mr. John Biniak presided at the meeting in the absence of the president, Mr. Anthony Rudnicki, who arrived later.
