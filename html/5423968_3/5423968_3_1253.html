We have been requested to publish the following report in our newspaper:

Last Sunday the Poles of the 15th Ward held one of their largest political meetings. Approximately three hundred persons attended the gathering, which was held at Columbia Hall by the Polish-American youth of the 15th Ward.

Of the many speakers who addressed the gathering, the most energetic ones were John Nowicki, S. Fruzyna, and J. B. Pallasch, our candidate for alderman of the 15th Ward, all of whom spoke in Polish. [gap]. Sarazinski, who desires to run for the post of clerk of West Town, spoke in English Francis Cutowski spoke in German.
