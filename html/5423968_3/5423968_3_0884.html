Members of the church choir of St. Stanislaus gathered at yesterday's meeting a passed the following resolution:

Our citizen Vincent J. Jozwiakowski is seeking re-election for the aldermanic office, an office which he has held faithfully for the last two years. During his term he built himself a good name as a man of social standing, trustworthy and one of integrity. Even the organizations of other nationalities are supporting him in the coming election.

He was a reliable member of the Choir of St. Stanislaus for many years, at present he is an honorary member of this organization.

He was a great help to the youngsters of this parish, while an honorary member he approved of sports, athletics and different kinds of amusement, and was liked by them all.

We, the members of this choir, support him whole-heartedly, as candidate for the 16th ward, and beg sincerely the Polish citizens, in the name of solidarity, to give their votes for V. J. Jozwiakowski.

2

Please, citizens of Polish clubs, societies and organizations, consider our plea, and give it your utmost consideration, and vote for the man of our own flesh and blood.

St. Stanislaus Choir
