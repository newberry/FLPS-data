We have been requested to publish the following announcement: The Sobieski Democratic Club of the 29th Ward, at a meeting held on January 21 at 4527 Page Street, elected the following new officers: Mr. W. Piasecki, president; Albert Dropik, vice-president; F. Wilhelm, recording secretary; John Kuminski, financial secretary; Thomas Peplowski, cashier.

The executive committee consists of Mr. W. Piasecki, Mr. Albert Dropik, and Mr. John Kuminski.

Regular meetings are held every first Saturday of the month at 8 P. M. at 4527 Page Street.

Citizens residing in the 29th Ward are invited to every meeting. The purpose of these meetings is to devise ways and means whereby Poles living in this ward \2may get better political offices.

In behalf of the Club,

W. Piasecki,

4527 Page Street.
