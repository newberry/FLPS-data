The recently organized Citizenship School at the St. Hyacinth parish at present boasts of sixty-five members. But, considering the size of the parish this is indeed a comparatively small number.

Instruction is given by attorney John Rybicki, Francis J. Kempa, and Herman Wisniewski.

Those that do not have their citizenship papers are urged to attend the class of any Tuesday at the parish hall on 17th and Paulina Streets. Class starts at 7:30 P. M.
