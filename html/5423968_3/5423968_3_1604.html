The Polish Democratic Club of the Sixteenth Ward held a meeting yesterday afternoon at four o'clock in the lower hall of St. Stanislaus Kostka School. The purpose of the meeting was to obtain positions for Polish persons in the Cook County and City of Chicago administrative offices. Peter Kiolbassa, J. A. Kowalski, and S. Kunz made speeches. Mention was also made about securing mail carrier positions for some Polish individuals.

A committee of three was chosen to deal with the matter; it was made up of the men named above. The resignation of S. Kwasigroch from the executive body of the organization was accepted. He was replaced by S. Kunz. The committee was also instructed to prepare a constitution for the Club.
