At the last meeting of the Polish Roman-Catholic Union which took place on the 18th instant, they decided to call upon all the Poles and members of the above mentioned association to tell them to vote for all our members who are candidates for offices.

Citizens Wisniewski, South Chicago, for member of the canal commission; J. Giersza, for alderman of the 16th ward; and J. Gieszkiewicz for alderman of the 11th ward.
