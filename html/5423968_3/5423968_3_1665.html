Stanley Kielczynski, who is a candidate for the office of alderman of the 17th ward, was born in Chicago in 1881, attended the St. Stanislaus parochial school and college and is at the present time engaged in the coal business.

He is married and is the father of two children. His family belongs to the pioneers of Chicago, having settled in Chicago in 1876. He is energetic, loyal, and courteous, in all of his business transactions, is a worthy candidate for the office of alderman, and he deserves the support of all Poles residing in the 17th ward.
