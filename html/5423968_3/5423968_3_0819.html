All Poles in Chicago can and should vote for Peter Rostenkowski for county commissioner.

His name will be found on the Democratic ticket and because it is Polish and long it will be easy for every one to find and place an X in front of it.

The county commissioner is the father of the poor, a guardian of the ill-treated and neglected, also master of public establishments.

Peter Rostenkowski is known throughout all the Polish neighborhoods as kind-hearted, a hard worker and honest. It would be very good for us Poles if he is elected county commissioner.

2

We can have him if all Poles in various sections of Chicago can remember that a good an reliable fellow citizen, Peter Rostenkowski, is seeking the votes of all Polish citizens so that he may be elected county commissioner.

Remember, fellow citizens, that you were first a Pole before you became a Republican or a Democrat. Vote for your own fellow brother, that will be enough.
