Through the efforts of Mr. Peter H. Schwaba, Mr. Michael Dominowski, and several other citizens of the Avondale district the Milwaukee Avenue streetcars now stop at Central Park Avenue. Prior to the passage of the new city ordinance the above-mentioned streetcars had failed to stop at the most important street in Avondale.
