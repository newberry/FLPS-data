Polish women citizens of all wards of Chicago will meet in the hall of the Briggs House Hotel on Wednesday, January 27, 1915, at 2 P.M., at which time Mayor C. Harrison will speak to them. On Friday, January 29, 1915, at 2 P.M., at the same hotel, the Mayor will speak before the Polish businessmen of Chicago. The election campaign among the Poles in behalf of Mayor C. Harrison is conducted by Mr. Frank Rydzewski, in the absence of Mr. Frank P. Danisch, president of the Polish Democratic League. Mr. N. L. Piotrowski is the secretary of the League.
