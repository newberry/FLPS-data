The Polish youth of the Fifteenth Ward gathered yesterday at Columbia Hall, 12 Hamburg [now Park Avenue]Street, to organize the Polish-American Democratic Youth Club. About two hundred young men attended the meeting. Officers of the new club are as follows:

Michael Sarazinski, president; John Jaszkowski, vice-president; Frank Klatecki, secretary, Albert Nowak, treasurer; and W. Glowaczewski, sergeant at arms.
