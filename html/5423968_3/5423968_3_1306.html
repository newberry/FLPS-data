Bear in mind that on Sunday, August 7, 1892, at 7 P.M., there will be a mass meeting of the Polish-American Democratic Club of the Sixteenth Ward in Hall No. 1 of the school building near Bradley Street. All present will receive election cards containing the names of the candidates for County Commissioner, according to the Australian system of voting. With the aim of electing a candidate from the Sixteenth Ward for this office, everyone should attend, and everyone should vote. The candidates are all well-known Polish citizens of the Sixteenth Ward
