Polish Democrats in Saint Casimir Parish held a meeting last Sunday afternoon at Kluczynski Hall, 22nd and Albany Streets.

M. Marcinkowski, president of the John Sobieski Club, opened the meeting and called upon John Rosinski to act as secretary.

J. Rosinski, J. F. Dankowski, and W. Laskowski spoke in Polish; Coody, Teuffy, and Hayes, in English. The speech in Bohemian was delivered by Mr. Herdlicka.

The meeting was very orderly, and the participants showed great enthusiasm for the Democratic platform.
