Members of the Sixteenth Ward Polish Youth Democratic Club held a meeting yesterday at Wiza's Hall. The meeting was quite interesting, and the hall was filled with Democrats.

Polish speeches were delivered by Alderman S. H. Kunz, Mr. Roland, Francis Bieszki, John A. Kwasigroch, Stanislaus Piasecki, Albert Tomasik, S. F. Cywinski, J. Czekala, and John Biniak.

Senator Edward T. Noonan delivered a speech in English, which was received with great enthusiasm. The meeting was a great success. The Polish youth is loyal to the Democratic party, which it will always support.
