Last week there was held the inaugural meeting of the new city council. Eleven new aldermen participated in it, and among them were three Poles. The public was well represented. The Poles entered into the following committees:

Streets and alleys on the West side, Nowicki; harbors and bridges, Nowicki, Golombiewski; taxes, Schermann; Health, Golombiewski, Nowicki, Schermann; City Hall and public buildings, Derpa; printing, Derpa, Golombiewski.
