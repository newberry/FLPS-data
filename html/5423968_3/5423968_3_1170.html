A grand concert, banquet and dance, sponsored by the citizen's committee will be held by the friends of the Dziennik Narodowy, at the Schoenhofen Hall, Milwaukee and Ashland Aves., Octover 23, 1912. Two choirs will compete for a silver trophy.

The entertainment committee is composed of twenty people from the various parts of Chicago. They have worked very earnestly, to make this program a very rich and successful one, and have invited many prominent citizens of Chicago to take part.

The committee has arranged for many surprises for the guests.
