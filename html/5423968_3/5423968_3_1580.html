The Sobieski Democratic Club of the Seventeenth Ward held a meeting yesterday at Kalisz's hall, Sangamon and Front Streets. Polish and English speeches were delivered.

A. J. Kowalski, who spoke in Polish, explained some of the political problems confronting us today and urged his listeners to support the Democratic party. He was rewarded with great applause.

The English speech was delivered by Mr. Noonan, candidate for congressman. The other Polish speeches were delivered by H. Nagiel and W. Tomasik. The second English speech was delivered by Mr. Goering. All expressed good will toward the Democratic party and wished it success.
