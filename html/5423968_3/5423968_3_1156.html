At its meeting of March 28, the Citizens' Club of St. Innocent's Parish resolved to support the following candidates for aldermen: Mr. Stanley Adamkiewicz (Seventeenth Ward), Mr. Edward J. Kaindl (Fifteenth Ward), and Mr. George M. Maypole (Fourteenth Ward).
