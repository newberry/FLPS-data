The first convention, of the Polish Democratic League of Illinois was held last Sunday, August 14. Mr. G. T. Kozlowski acted as president in the absence of the provisional president, Mr. Peter Kiolbassa. Mr. [gap] J. Szameit was the secretary.

The chief purpose of the convention was the organization of the League and the election of an administration. The following persons were elected: Peter Kiolbassa, president; G. T. Kozlowski, [gap] Stanley Fruzyna, vice-presidents; [gap] J. Szameit, correspondence secretary; John Kowicki, recording secretary; Joseph Wilkowski, treasurer; and Paul Karnowski, sergeant at arms.

For the executive committee: Peter Kiolbassa, president; M. J. Szameit, secretary; Frank Kelichowski, Victor Bardonski, Vincent Pescinski, Louis Roland, L. G. Kalisz, Walter Kledzinski, G. T. [gap], Michael Gryszczynski, and Theodore Wikaryasz.

With the approval of the assembly, the presiding officer named the following committee to draw up a constitution: A. Michalski, [gap] Szameit, Stanley Szwajkart, G. T. Kozlowski, and Louis Roland.

On a motion by Mr. M. J. Szameit, the assembly adopted a resolution to endorse Grover Cleveland, E. Stevenson, John [gap].Altgeld, and the entire Democratic ticket, recommending that the secretary inform the above of this resolution.

The next delegate meeting will be held on Wednesday, the 17th of this month, at 7:30 P. M., in the hall at 137 [gap] Street.

M. Szameit,

Secretary.
