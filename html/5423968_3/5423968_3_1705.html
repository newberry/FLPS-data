Kiolbassa's plurality was 63 votes. So Mr. Peter Kiolbassa was elected alderman of the Sixteenth Ward by the small plurality of 63 votes.

The Democratic West Town ticket was victorious in the Sixteenth Ward also. We have no information as to the results in other wards.

In the Fifteenth Ward Mr. Anderson, the Repubican candidate, was elected alderman. The Polish candidate, Mr. Pallasch, received very few votes.
