The Polish Democratic Organization of Chicago was organized last night at A. Schultz's Hall by representatives of nine Polish Democratic clubs of Chicago. J. Nowak of the Fiffeenth Ward opened the meeting and called upon G. J. Krajecki, of the same ward, to preside. M. Derengowski acted as secretary.

After a number of speeches it was decided to organize the new group. A committee of five was chosen to draw up a constitution and prepare a publicity plan.

All Polish organizations desiring further details should get in touch with the secretary, M. Derengowski, 132 Cleaver Street.
