Professor M. Szymczak of DePaul University, with a newly formed Polish organization and two other candidates were presented to the head of the Democratic party as their choice for candidates for the office of county treasurer. Professor M. Szymczak was officially indorsed by George Brennan, head of the Democratic party in the state of Illinois, and will be supported by that organization.

This news turned the wheel of politics in Chicago, especially in the Republican party, where Mr. Szymczak is regarded as a dangerous opponent. Professor Szymczak is popular with young Americans.

Mayor Dever, who again will be a candidate for mayor, accepted the news with great enthusiasm. Being on the same ticket with the professor, it is certain that he will be supported by Polish voters.
