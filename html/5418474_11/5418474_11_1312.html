The funeral of Conrad Feige, 939 Montrose Avenue, who for more than 15 years was in the employ of the Abendpost, will take place tomorrow afternoon from his home to the Montrose Cemetery. The deceased was born 60 years ago in Kassel, Germany. He came to America at an early age.

While employed at the Freie Presse he learned the typographer's trade and later, in 1886, was accepted as a member of the German-American Typographical Union No. 9. After a long period of activity with the Catholic Weekly paper, he became connected with the Abendpost, where he was active until his death.
