Dr. Hermann Kirschstein died yesterday in his residence, 2941 S. Park Avenue, at the age of 77 years.

He had graduated from the University of Heidelberg and then emigrated. He had lived since in Chicago and 40 years ago he built the house, in which he died yesterday.

Fifteen years ago he retired. Dr. Kirschstein was a very well known personality in German circles and a regular guest at the festivals of the "old Settlers." Two sons Albert and William survive him. The funeral will take place at the cemetery "Graceland."
