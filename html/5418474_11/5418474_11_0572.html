Again a member of the ill-famed brotherhood of "hotel runners", one Nicolaus Schultz, has been caught red-handed at night. This scoundrel working as solicitor for the "Rock Island House, snatched away an immigrant's luggage on Saturday night and forced him to come to his Hotel. When Mr. William Meyer who had charge of the immigrants, objected he was knocked down by the ruffian.

The police took care of him.
