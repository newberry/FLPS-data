Yesterday a committee of prominent German-Americans held a conference in the interest of the planned candidacy of Gustave A. Brand for the office of city treasurer. They conferred with representatives of the Democratic Central Committee, among them being Judge Patrick Sullivan and Alderman Jacob Arvey.

The speakers for the German-Americans favored the candidacy of Mr. Brand quite warmly, and obtained the assurance that the subcommittee will recommend his nomination.

Fred Z. Brummel, member of the tax revision office, presided over the conference.
