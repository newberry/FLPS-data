Johanna Bethke, who for more than fifty years had been active in Chicago as Kindergarten teacher, was laid to her rest in Graceland Cemetery yesterday afternoon.

Miss Bethke, who died of a heart attack at the age of ninety-three, came from the Deaconess Institute in Kaiserworth Germany, in 1864, and was first active at the Berlin Charity. She came to Chicago in 1873 and was engaged for almost twenty years as director of a Congregational Church Kindergarten.

From 1893 to 1920 she directed her own Kindergarten on Magnolia Ave., until she retired to the St. Paul's Home about eight years ago.
