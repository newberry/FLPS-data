The German-Hungarians also find zealous co-workers here.

The first American group for the propagation of Germanism in Hungary has been founded at Sieben's Hall. The movement for the preservation of Germanism in Hungary is constantly growing stronger. Supporting this movement in its fight against the perfidious, hostile Magyar forces, are their compatriots. After several hours of deliberation, the Chicagoan, an organization first of its kind in the United States, has been founded. The profound interest in the cause as was shown yesterday, may be indicative of future progress of the new society.....
