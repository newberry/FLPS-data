Another old German settler of Chicago died yesterday at his home, 1723 Frederick Street, Mr. Dietrich Moeng, 82 years old.

He was born 1812 in Landsburgen, Hannover (Germany), and came in 1844 to Chicago, where he had for many years a dry goods store.

Moeng served in the Northern army during the Civil war, and become an army captain. Also he belonged to several German socities and was known all over Chicago. Moeng leaves a widow, four sons and three daughters.
