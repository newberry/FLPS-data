The Bavarian Handicraft Association is exhibiting at the present time an interesting collection of its products at the Steuben book store. It consists of leather goods, which are treated according to a special process and, therefore, produce an exceptionally beautiful and elegant effect.

This exhibit comprises handbags, cigarette-cases, portfolios, etc. There are also dainty glasswares and porcelains, silver, and paintings, and enamel, as well as ivory ornaments. It is worthwhile to inspect the exhibited goods, because it brings new proof of the high standards of German handicraft industries.
