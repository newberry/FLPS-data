After eight weeks of illness, the well known German business man, Mr. George A. Weiss died in his home last night at the age of 52 years. He was born in Frankenthal, Rheinpfalz and came to the United States thirty years ago.

He settled in Chicago in the malt business and formed later on the American Malt Company, over which he presided till his death. His wife, nee Schneider, and his daughter are his survivors.
