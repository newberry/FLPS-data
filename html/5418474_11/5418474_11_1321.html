After getting in touch with a number of friends last Tuesday, to start a new relief action for the sufferers in Germany, Mrs. Bertha Junge died yesterday in her home, 4540 So. Michigan Ave. at the age of 75. The deceased, who lived in Chicago for 60 years, has through her constant readiness to help all those people, who suffered through the terrible war in Germany, in an unselfish way, acquired many friends, who deeply regret the passing of this excellent woman.

Mrs. Junge was a widow for 22 years. She was a member of the Altenheim and was one of the founders of the Erholung (recreation) Society. The deceased leaves seven children and eight grandchildren. The funeral takes place Monday at 2 o'clock.
