The former collaborator of the Illinois Staats Zeitung, Hans von Meding, passed away this morning at the age of 68 years. He was born on January 10, 1860, in Mecklenburg, and came to America in his youth. At the beginning of the nineties, he became an editor of the Illinois Staats Zeitung. He occupied this position for a number of years.

Later he devoted himself to different business enterprises, and lately, was employed by the Foreman National Bank.

The deceased leaves two brothers and two sisters in Germany, his widow, Ida von Meding, 4226 Greenview Avenue, and his sons Victor and Bodo. The oldest son, William, lives in Pittsburgh. His only daughter died several years ago.
