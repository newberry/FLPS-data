Lillian E. Weide, who had been active as a teacher in the Chicago schools for more than fifty years, died Saturday at the age of 72 years.

Miss Weide was born in Frankfurt on the Kaine, Germany, and came to Chicago as a child with her parents. She was a teacher at the Old Dearborn-Seminary, the Loring School the Sieboth-Kennedy School, the Kenwood Institute as well as in the Fauhur school for Girls.

During the last twenty years she was a teacher of German and Spanish.
