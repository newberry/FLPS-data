At the ripe old age of 85 years, Mr. Louis Lutz died at his home 4100 Greenview Avenue. He belonged to the oldest settlers of Chicago. He was born in the year 1846 in Weingarten, Baden. On May 4th 1871, he married Miss Caroline Pauli, a Meklenburgian, with whom he lived happily for more than 60 years. A few weeks ago, he celebrated his diamond wedding with his faithful companion.

Mr. and Mrs. Lutz came to Chicago 56 years ago. They settled on North Avenue, where Mr. Lutz opened a butcher shop. Thirty-five years ago, he became active in the brewery business, which he continued till his retirement. He leaves two sons and three grandchildren.
