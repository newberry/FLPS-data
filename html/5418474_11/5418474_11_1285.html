After many years of suffering, Charles F. Vogel, attorney at law, 5929 Midway Park, Master of the Independent Order of Foresters of America, died yesterday at the age of 57 years. Attorney Vogel came from Germany to Chicago as a young man. He studied law at Kent College of Law, and then practiced law for over 30 years.

Under his leadership the new Forester's temple on North Dearborn Street was planned and built.
