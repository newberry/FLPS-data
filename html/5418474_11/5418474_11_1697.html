After a long period of painful illness, Mr. Edward Rummel, died yesterday at his home, 1413 N. Halsted Street. Mr. Rumell came from Germany, when he was only 18 years old, and worked hard, until finally he became Superior of the Postoffice at Lakeview. Also he published for a long time The German Weekly, "The Postillon", which was read by many Germans of Chicago.

Mr. Rummel was only 59 years old, when he died, and never was married. We also learn, that Mr. Edward Rummel has been State Secretary of Illinois for one term of the Legislature.
