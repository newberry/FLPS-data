St.Louis, April 8- In his residence at Belleville, Illinois, died Gustav Koerner, former Vice Governor of Illinois. ( He was born 1809 in Frankfort o.M. and came 1833 to Belleville; from 1845-1849 he was Judge of the Illinois Superior Court and in 1852 he was elected Vice Governor. He helped to nominate Abraham Lincoln as candidate for the presidency. 1862 he was sent to Spain as American ambassador. He was in Madrid for two and one half years and then he resigned. 1872 he supported Greeley as candidate for president and since that time he was a Democrat.)
