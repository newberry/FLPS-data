Last Wednesday evening a well known German fellow citizen, Dr. Kurt Peters, 4410 Greenview Ave., died in Grant Hospital after an unsuccessful operation.

Dr. Peters was born in Berlin and spent his youth in Husum, Schleswig. Having been a veterinarian in Germany, he came to Chicago, where he worked in several chemical factories. A short time ago he got appendicitis and peritonitis. Complications set in, which finally caused his death.

He is mourned by his wife, Mrs. Dotty, nee Schumann, and a seven year old daughter. Funeral notice will be given later.
