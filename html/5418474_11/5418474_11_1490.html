Mr. John Weil, who after a ten years illness died in the family home, 109 Winchester Avenue, will be buried in Rosehill tomorrow morning.

Mr. Weil, was for many years, till he had to retire on account of ill health, president of the Weil Feather Company, 184 Fulton Street. He was born in Baden, [gap], and came to the United States in 1852, a few decades later to Chicago.

Besides his wife, he is survived by four children.
