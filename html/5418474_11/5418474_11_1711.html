George Meumeister, one of the oldest German citizens of Chicago, died yesterday, 75 years old. Born 1819 in Germany, George Meumeister came already 1850 with his parents to Chicago, where his father became a successful business man.

George Meumeister himself speculated successfully in real estate and retired from business 10 years ago. He belonged to several German Societies and was requested by all, who knew him. He is survived by his widow and a son, John H. Meumeister, who is a city hall secretary.
