Peter Schoenhofen Theurer, president of the Schoenhofen Beverage Co. 1928 W. 18th St., and former treasurer of the Illinois Manufacturers Association, died yesterday in his home 232 E. Walton Place of a heart attack. The deceased, a Chicagoan by birth, is mourned by his widow Ilena and two children, John Peter and Ilena Jane. The funeral will take place tomorrow forenoon 10 o'clock at the Graceland cemetery.
