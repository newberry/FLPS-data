Ernst Herman Pachmann died very suddenly this morning at his home, 1122 Eddy Street. He was born in 1852 in Loschwitz, Samony, and emigrated to America in 1823 with his family. He was active for 30 years in the circulation department of the Abendpost and filled his position, true to his duty at all times. He was industrious and reliable.

Besides his wife, Minna, and his two sons, Hugo and Willie, he will be mourned by a large circle of friends and acquaintances, among them the association of German Comrades, and the Hoffening Lodge of the Odd Fellows, to both of which be belonged. The Tuneral took place Thursday from the home to Nontrose cemetery.
