Dr. Hermann Stein, a good friend and fighting pal of the deceased Karl Schurz, died yesterday at the age of 80 years in his home at 1761 Magnolia Avenue. Dr. Stein was practicing for many years in St. Louis and Denver; twelve years ago he retired and moved to Chicago. But up to a short time ago he was very active in the literary world.

He leaves two children Dr. Otto J. Stein and Miss Pauline Stein.
