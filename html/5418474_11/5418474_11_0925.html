Accompanied by Mr. F. Gudbrod, the technical attache of the German Embassy in Washington. Dr. E. C. Fleck, under secretary of the ministry, for public works arrived here yesterday to make a trip through the United States of America to study principally the American Railway System. They were received by the German Consul, Dr. Wever and visited this morning the Stock yards. This afternoon they will view the tunnels; tomorrow they will inspect the Pullman factories and other great manufacturing industries.
