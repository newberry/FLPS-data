Up to 10 o'clock this morning the Abendpost, has received $47 in contributions for a donation to be made to Dr. Eckener, Commander of the Zeppelin that flew the Atlantic. H. Wollenberger, the treasurer, has received so far a total of $462, which with the receipts of the Abendpost raises the amount to $509. The grand total collected by different sources amounts to $11,705.13.
