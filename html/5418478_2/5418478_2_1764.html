The Bohemian Lying-In Hospital reports that several women can now be admitted there for confinement. They will also accept Bohemian women, who wish to train for mid-wifery. Their school is specially suited for women of small means, who wish to improve their position. Those who apply now will pay only fifty dollars for the complete course of instruction, and they can pay this amount in small installments. The Hospital also takes care of confinement cases in the home, and needy women who apply in advance, will be well taken care of free of charge. Application may be made at 3639 West 22nd Street, or to Doctor Soukut at 1646 West 18th Street, near Paulina Street.
