Czech women in Town of Lake are doing valuable work in the registration of women for auxiliary service in the war. Registrations in that Czech section of Chicago are being taken in the Robert E. Fulton School, 53rd and Wood Streets; Seward School, 48th Street and Hermitage Avenue; Hedges School, 48th Street and Winchester Avenue in the Czech parish school, 50th Street and Hermitage Avenue; the Slovak parish school, 48th Street and Winchester Avenue; and in the offices of Mr. Felix H. Janovsky, 47th and Honore Streets. More than thirty Czech women are helping out at the places of registration at this time.
