"It is an old saying that there are three things needed to carry on a war: money, then again money, and finally money. We all can see today that this is true. For the second time Uncle Sam is asking us to lend him money. The new Liberty Loan is a demand for money so that the enormous expenses for the Army and Navy can be defrayed. The Liberty bond is a direct promise by the United States Government to pay back the money in gold after a certain time....."

[Translator's note: Detailed explanations about the advantages and security offered in the Liberty bonds are given.]
