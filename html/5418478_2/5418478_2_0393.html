Large subscriptions came in yesterday, so that there is no doubt that the quota for Chicago will be reached. The Tenth Ward with its Czechoslovak population has subscribed $561,850, or 112 per cent of its quota. The Twelfth Ward had subscribed $801,400, or 80 per cent of its quota of $1,000,000 up to last night. It is sure to reach 100 per cent. The only question remaining is how much will be oversubscribed.

Two of our banking institutions, the Lawndale State Bank and the Lawndale National Bank each subscribed $100,000 yesterday. This of course, does not include subscriptions made by their depositors. Reports from other Czechoslovak wards indicate that their quotas will be reached.
