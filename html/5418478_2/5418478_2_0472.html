Some of our readers may be wondering why they get their newspaper in reduced size now. They have had no inkling that the government regulates printed matter to such a degree that it prescribes the quantity of paper which may be used. The press is not the only one to attract close attention from the Government. There is more than one reason why the authorities should give to it even sharper consideration than to other enterprises..... [A column and a half of explanations of government rulings follows.]
