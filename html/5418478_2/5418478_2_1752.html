Doctor Leo Zelenka-Lerando, now of the German department of the Ohio State University, Columbus, Ohio, is going to teach the Czech language at that school of high learning.

He is a native of Prague, where he graduated from the University, and also from the Conservatory of Music. Continuing his studies in the United States at Northwestern University, he was graduated, and also from the University of Chicago. He has been connected with the University of Ohio since last fall. He is an excellent harpist as well as pianist.
