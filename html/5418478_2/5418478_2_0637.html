Mr. Anton J. Čermák, chief bailiff of the Municipal Court, has engaged in laudable, beneficial activities, as a result of which a remarkable number of men who tried to avoid the draft have been exposed. The chief bailiff has ordered his deputies to question every man in their custody in regard to registration for military service.....Mr. Čermák will see to it that draft dodgers, after serving their jail terms, are again turned over to the proper authorities.
