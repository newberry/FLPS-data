The second issue of the newspaper Na Zdar (To Success), published by Paris Bohemians to keep French political circles informed on the desires and aims of the Bohemian nation as well as on her position in the present war in which our fate will be decided, has reached our office. In addition to the editorial, the number contains several articles by French publicists and authors, friends of the Bohemian nation. There is a wealth of material in this number....
