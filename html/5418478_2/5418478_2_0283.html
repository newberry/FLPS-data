Last Saturday, Mayor Harrison named one hundred twenty-eight delegates to the convention of the Lakes to the Gulf Deep Waterway Association, which will be held in Chicago on October 12, 13 and 14 .....

Among the delegates named by the Mayor are the following Czechs: Anton J. Cermák, Dr. J. F. Chvátal, Felix B. Janovský, Adolf Kraus, Otto J. Novak, Adolf J. Sabath, Frank Skala and Charles J. Vopicka.....
