Mrs. Wm. G. McAdoo, wife of the secretary of the United States Treasury, has sent a letter to the publishers of the Denni Hlasatel, in which she expresses her appreciation for the work done by that Chicago Czech paper in behalf of the Second Liberty Loan. Enclosed was a final report on the total subscription, which reached the figure $4,617,532,300, or fifty-four per cent more than the quota of three billion dollars.
