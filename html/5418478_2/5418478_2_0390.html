The executive committee of the American Czechoslovak Board met in Chicago, Jan. 18th. Secretary Vojta Benes announced that the Bohemian National Alliance had sent one million francs to Paris to be employed for the relief of war suffering in Bohemia.

Among the actions taken at this meeting was the decision to send a delegation of six Czechs and six Slovaks to Czechoslovakia as early as possible. One of the matters to be taken up by this commission is the exchange of students between America and Bohemia.
