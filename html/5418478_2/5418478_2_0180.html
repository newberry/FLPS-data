Impressed by our report yesterday about the shortage of water in Town of Lake, Alderman Felix B. Janovsky of the Twenty-ninth Ward put pressure upon the proper departments in the City Hall and today we can assure the inhabitants of the Town of Lake that they will no longer suffer from a shortage of water. Alderman Janovsky has been assured that in order to overcome the shortage of water, two pumping stations would be combined into a single unit and the Town of Lake would have plenty of water.
