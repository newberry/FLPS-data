Bohemian physicians, dentists, pharmacists, and lawyers held a meeting last night at the La Salle Hotel at which the Bohemian Professional Club of Chicago was founded. The purpose of the Club is to promote better acquaintance and closer social contacts between Chicago Bohemians of the above-mentioned professions. The Club is also open to artists and similar groups.
