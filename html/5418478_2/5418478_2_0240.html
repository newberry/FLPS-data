Mr. Morris Sabath, who is a brother of Congressman Adolph J. Sabath, was appointed as a revenue assessor for the district of Chicago, and his nomination was approved by the United States Senate, according to the report received here yesterday from Washington D. C.

Mr. Morris Sabath will take possession of this office, which is at present vacant because of the appointment of the former revenue assessor to the office of collector of revenue, as soon as he, himself, is officially notified.
