Mayor Harrison yesterday submitted to the City Council his nominations for three members of the Chicago Board of Education. They are to replace the members who have been ousted because they refused to vote for Mrs. Ella Flagg Young as superintendent of Chicago public schools, and voted instead for John D. Shoop.....

Fifty-eight members of the Council were present at the meeting, which unanimously approved the Mayor's nominations.....

The new members of the Board include Josef A. Holpuch, who takes the place of J. B. Dibelka [a Bohemian].....
