The priceless heritage of civilization is peace. Only in times of peace can people and nations grow strong. War only weakens and devastates.

The strong alone can strive successfully to alleviate the sufferings of mankind, or to add the pleasures and benefits of living. To work for peace; to strive to maintain peace is a sign of strength, not of weakness. The world needs peace. We wish to see the world at peace.

The most powerful country in the world today is the United States. Everyday that we remain at peace with the world and with ourselves, enables us to grow stronger.

2

May our President, our Government, our people, be strong enough to initiate a movement and to carry it to a successful ending, a movement for peace. The war must end and peace again must reign over the now fighting nations of the world.
