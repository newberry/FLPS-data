[Lieutenant Berge and his crew captured an English liner on the high seas and brought her to Norfolk, Va., evading the British navy. The Abendpost started a collection to reward the brave sailors. Transl.]

As previously stated in this column, the collection for Lieutenant Berge and his brave men was discontinued last Saturday evening. Altogether, a nice sum of $333.25 was collected and we shall mail a check for this amount to-night. As soon as we receive Berge's reply, we shall publish it in this space.

The final contributions received up to Saturday night amounted to $13, which added to $320.25 previously collected, makes our Chicago contribution $333.25.
