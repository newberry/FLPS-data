Because President P. Zimmermann of the Evengelical Lutheran Synode of North America, following the induction of Pastor Schaube of Mohana, Illinois, gave a warning against Oscar Keltsch, the present minister of the St. Nicolai Church, as a borrower and as not regularly ordained minister, Mr. Keltsch lost his position.

He has now filed a $15,000 suit for slander against Pastor Zimmermann and will also act in a similar way against Pastor Schaube.
