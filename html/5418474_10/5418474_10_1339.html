At the grand pavilion of Oswald's Grove today an assembly was held of earnest men who foster these conferences for the benefit of the Plattduetsche (Low-German) Guilds of the U. S. of America. From the State of Illinois there are represented 60 guilds with 6505 members. Grand master H. A. Linnemeyer opened the convention with an address, Grand secretary Louis A. Brandt read the reports. The total membership is 7269, the total assets $31,500. During the last year the lodge had 72 deaths, $36,000 were paid for death benefits for which every member was taxed $5.50.
