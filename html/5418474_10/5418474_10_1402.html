Pastor Koenig, who for fourteen years has been reporter for the Sonntagpost covering the Evangelical parishes of Chicago, has decided to follow the call of the Evangelical Cross Parish in Seguin, Texas, near San Antonio. For the last seventeen years he has been active in the Chicago Zion Parish, and also has served as assistant in various Chicago parishes.

Pastor Koenig will preach two more times in Chicago; on Sunday, October 16,at the Golden Jubilee of the Evangelical St. John Parish of Pastor Leesmann, on Moffat Street and Campbell Avenue, and on October 23 at the thirty-sixth anniversary of Pastor Fruechte's Bethlehem Parish, Magnolia and Diversey Avenues.
