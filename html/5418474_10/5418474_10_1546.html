The Protestant Lutheran Jerusalem Parish has had a very pleasant development under the newly nominated Pastor, Rev. H. G. Michelmann. Church services are now held regularly each Sunday, at 10 A. M. and 7:30 P. M.

The newly formed church song club, under the direction of Miss H. Bode, adds much to the beauty of this service.

The Sunday School lessons are held from 2 to 3 P/M. The new Pastor also has established a German School for Catechism, history of the Bible, reading, writing, and singing, which is held every Saturday.

During the vacation, the school is open daily. The Collector of the Parish is Wilhelm Schmidt. This church is situated at 101 Burling Street.
