Last night began in Flory's Hall, 3609 S. Halsted Street this years great Fair of the German Lutheran St. Marcus Parish. This festival lasts till Saturday inclusive, and plenty opportunity is offered to the members of this Parish and their friends, to enjoy a good entertainment and some happy days. Numerous useful and valuable presents are exhibited and will be raffled. A well balanced program has been arranged for each of the three nights, consisting of living pictures, recitations, songs and musical performances, so that there will be no lack of entertainment.
