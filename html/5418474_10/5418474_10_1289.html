p. 8. The German Catholic Central Association of Catholic Young Men will celebrate Catholic Day to-day and tomorrow. At the St. Franciscan Church a festive pontifical service will be held at 10 o'clock and for Wednesday morning, at 9 A.M., an imposing pontifical requiem has been announced.

For the latter the capable St. Johannes Young Men's Association has made extensive preparations to provide for an elaborate and adequate service in honor of their departed members; their meeting hall has been decorated also, preparatory to the reception which will be given to out of town delegates.

The St. Joseph School building has been profusely and beautifully decorated in anticipation of the arrivals of the Youth Association's friends.
