In Irving Park a new German Evangelic parish has been established. The pastor is Louis Von Roque', 719 W. Irving Park Blvd. President of the Parish is Kornelius Goldmann and President of the Women's department, Augusta Fiedler. Church service will be held temporarily in the store 639 Irving Park Blvd, corner of Kimball Avenue. The sermon is held at 11:30. At 2 o'clock in the afternoon a Sunday School is held.
