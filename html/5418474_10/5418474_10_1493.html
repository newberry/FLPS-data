Today the entirely renovated church of the Evangelical St. Jacobi Congregation, N. Park Avenue and Florimond Street has been reopened. In the forenoon, service conducted by Prof. Kraft the confirmation service takes place. Three boys and three girls will be confirmed.

At the evening service, Prof. Oberhaus of the Chicago Theological Seminary will made a speech in German and Dr. Armstrong, secretary of the Chicago town Missions Association will make a speech in English. For the festival service in the forenoon a strong delegation of the Evangelical Germans of Winnetka is expected. On the 27th of April the church choir will give a concert in the Church.
