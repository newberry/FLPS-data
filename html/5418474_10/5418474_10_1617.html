The Baker Free Turning Society held a meeting last evening at its hall, No. 723 Ogden Avenue. The object of the meeting was to raise funds for a hall to be built for the society. Two hundred shares of stock in the building were subscribed and paid for, and the following officers were elected: President, Chas. Brenel; Vice-President, Jacob Portz; Treasurer, H. Von der Heydt; Secretary, Martin Grenbel.
