Complying with several requests, the Friends of the New Germany have revived an old German custom, and reintroduced Sunday afternoon concerts with coffee and cake and other refreshments. They take place regularly, beginning at 8 P.M., at von Themen's restaurant, Western Avenue and Roscoe Street, across from the Riverview gate.

The music is played by members of the Bund orchestra, conductor Ernst Heinecke. Admission is free. Guests are cordially welcome.
