The Schwabenverein held its meeting, yesterday evening at the lower floor of the Northside Turnhall. Attendance was good and the official report of the annual general assembly showed, that the Club progresses in all of its endeavors, as never before; in fact it achieved a membership increase and now boasts the number 1112. Another 24 gentlemen made application to become members. The Club passed a resolution, an assessment of "10 cents per head" for the benefit of the Milwaukee Teacher's Seminary: As candidates for the coming election on Wednesday, the following names have been tabulated, as future club-officials: Pres. E. Niederegger, vice-pres., F. Demmler and 20 others in divers capacities.
