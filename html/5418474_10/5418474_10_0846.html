Yesterday at 2 P. M., the "German Military Association" of the Southwest Side, held their first meeting at Schmidt's Hall. After the meeting was called to order, the officials were elected.

The purpose of this Association is to extend assistance in case of sickness and to take care of funeral expenses in case of death of their members.

There will be another meeting Wednesday, September 23rd, at the same place.

All those that have been soldiers in Germany are accepted as members.
