Tonight, - in the gigantic hall of the Auditorium, the Chicago German-Americans will hold their great Protest Meeting! Its purpose is to give a typical German reply to those malicious attempts and miserable insinuations now being circulated by the subsidized press of England, and our yellow Press, which is so desirous of destroying our friendship and adherence to our adopted fatherland, to which we have been faithful for a century. Above all, the local German - Americans wish to proclaim and prove to the world, that they are bona fide - loyal citizens of this nation; in unity they voice their demand to be recognized and enjoy equal suffrage; - with stentorian tones of warning, which shall reverberate like thunder, we will protest against a perfidious alliance with Albion.

There is no doubt, the meeting will be a grand demonstration and will not fail in its purpose. All those, who wish to obtain cards of admission, may procure them at William H. Jung's hall, 106 East Randolph Street, or from Mr. E. H.
