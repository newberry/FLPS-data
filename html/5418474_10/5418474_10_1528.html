The Liberal Church on the Northwest side is reopening their Sunday School and also their sewing school.

Instructions in sewing, embroidery, etc. will be given every Saturday from 2-4 P. M. Sunday-school is held every Sunday morning from 10-12 o'clock A. M.

This congregation appeals to all parents in sympathy with their plans to send their children to these classes. At the same time they ask parents to join the church. They promise to put forth every effort to establish a home of their own. The monthly fee is .10Â¢.
