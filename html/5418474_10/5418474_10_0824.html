Natives of the Provinces of Alsace-Lorraine now residents of this city met at Jungs Hall, No. 106 East Randolph Street, yesterday afternoon to organize a social and benevolent society. I. M. Schmitt was elected President; Joseph Shall, Secretary, and Charles Leraux, Treasurer. Seventy members were enrolled being about equally divided between Germans and French, the business being transacted mostly in the German language. A weekly benefit of $5.00 is to be allowed members when sick and disabled. Another meeting will be held at the same place two weeks from yesterday for the reception of new members and to take action in reference to completing the adoption of by-laws now in the hands of a special committee who will then report in full.
