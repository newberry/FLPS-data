About one half of 600 outside Veterans arrived last night, in Chicago for the Silver Jubilee and annual convention of the German Veterans' Club of North America. The first were the delegates from Denver, Colo., who arrived on Friday. From Pittsburgh, came these clubs, Allegheny Arms Association, and the Veterans Club made up of the Southside clubs, St. Louis, Cincinnati, Toledo, Bloomington, Davenport and Dubuque. They were met at the station and conducted to their respective quarters. Today, the Cleveland Club will arrive, being the eighth in line. Besides the clubs, delegates arrived from Kansas City, Fort Wayne, Louisville, Akron and other places. Today, delegations will arrive from New York, New Orleans, and many more towns.
