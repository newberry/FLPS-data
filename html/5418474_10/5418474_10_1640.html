Yesterday's Easter festival of the free thinking school Verein of the Northwest Side proved morally strengthening and mentally animating for all visitors young and old.

After being in existence for four months, the Verein has two hundred children attending the freethinking Sunday school and hopes to acquire its own building in the near future.

The principle is to give children of the working class a good education in contrast to the so-called Christian education, so as to raise them in the sense of enlightenment.
