The Germanistic Society announces the following lectures in the Fullerton Hall at the Art Institute:

Tonight, 8:15 P. M. "Germanism in Austria. Given in German with slides by Professor Eugene Oberhummer of Vienna.

Monday, November 14th, 8:15 P. M. "Whims of English Grammar." Given in English by Professor L. Morsbach of the University of Goettingen.

Monday, November 21st, 8:15 P. M. "What do we know about Shakespeare?" German Club-house at North Clark Street and German Place. The members of the Germanistic Society are cordially invited.
