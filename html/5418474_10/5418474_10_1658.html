The Evangelical Johannes Congregation celebrated last night their twenty years existence in their church at Mohawk and Garfield Avenue. About three hundred persons took part in the festival, consisting of a dinner, musical performances, speeches etc. Mr. J. J. Roessler spoke on "The Pioneer Period", Pastor Klein about "Our Women", Mr. A. C. Dittmann about a "Review", Mr. L. N. Knoop about the men, Mr. H. Nuttman about youth, and Mr. A. Thinger about "The Children". All speakers mentioned the deceased founder of the Parish; Dr. Zimmerman, who was Pastor of the congregation until four years ago. The church choir conducted by Mr. Scherer gave several songs. Tonight a celebration for the young people will take place.
