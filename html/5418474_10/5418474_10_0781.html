The Soldiers Club of the South Side, a young, but ambitious association, which unites former German soldiers and sailors, men who are not affiliated with other similar organizations, held a public meeting yesterday afternoon at 3 P.M. Installation of officers and a new membership drive were on the agenda.

The club will celebrate its fourth anniversary next Saturday at Freiberg's Hall, 182-184 Twenty-second Street. Other veterans associations will also share in the festivities. Whoever has been present during any of its former festivals knows how effectively they have been arranged. The committee will spare no efforts to provide enjoyment during the forthcoming celebration.
