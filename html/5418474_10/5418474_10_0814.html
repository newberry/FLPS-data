The "Schwaben-Verein" has now disposed of the surplus of its picnic to the amount of about $1500. The following donations were made: German Hospital $50; Alexian Hospital $50.00; German Old People's Home $50; Uhlig's Orphanage $50; Rose-hill Orphanage $50.00; Michael Reese Hospital $35; German Society $25; becises a donation of $50 was agreed on for the planned "Goethe Monument."
