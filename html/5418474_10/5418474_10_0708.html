The Verein Der Yereinigten Oesterreicher Und Baiern (Society of United Austrians and Bavarians) has mobilized all members to appear in full uniform at the Auditorium on Wednesday evening, in order to participate in the mass meeting which has been called by the Deutsch-Amerikanischer Nationalbund to protest against the attitude and conduct of the English-language press.
