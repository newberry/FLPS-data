German-American National Association. A branch of The above Association held its monthly meeting yesterday, at the Schiller Bldg under the leadership of its president. According to Association reports, there is considerable progress in organizing local and state groups in the South and Western sections and the association is preparing to fight the prohibition proposals now before congress. The" (All-German) Protective Association" and "German Sentinel" (Deutsche Wacht) joined.

Other clubs announced new delegates. In the near future the presidium will address a proclamation to all Germans, regarding the "teaching of German in the public schools and furthermore to take active steps for the promotion of this cause. Next month, the executive board of the general assembly will give recommendations concerning the observance of "German day."
