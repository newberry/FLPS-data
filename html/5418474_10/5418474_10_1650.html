The annual meeting of the Chicago Conference of the Methodist Church, under the chairmanship of Bishop J. M. Walden, started yesterday morning at the German Methodist Church, Portland Avenue & 28th Street.

The district of the Chicago German conference covers a part of the state of Illinois, Wisconsin, and Indiana. This territory is divided into three separate districts, and in each district are from 20 to 22 congregations. The presiding elders of these districts are appointed and taken from the ranks of the ordained ministers. They serve four years in this capacity and from the cabinet of the bishop during the annual conference.
