In the Club house of the "Germania Maennerchor", a farewell party in honor of Mr. Euegene Seeger was given. Mr. Seeger was appointed Consul of the United States in Rio De Janeiro, and will leave, shortly, for South America.

The party was arranged by his personal and political friends and was presided over by William Vocke. Speeches were given by Judge Brentano, Dr. Theodore Bluthardt, F.J. Dewes, Louis Hirsch, and others.
