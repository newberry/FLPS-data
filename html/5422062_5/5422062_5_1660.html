More than five hundred people had the opportunity of meeting A. Metaxa, general manager of the Metaxa Exporting Company of Greece, who has been visiting the liquer and wine convention hold at the Sherman Hotel. This opportunity was given them by the Deligiannis brothers of Chicago, who gave a dinner in his honor at the Edgewater Beach Hotel last Sunday night. Many social leaders and notables were seated at the table of the guest of honor.

When the director of the orchestra announced the presence of the General Consul of Greece, and Mr. Metaxa of the exporting firm, all the American people in the room applauded vigorously.
