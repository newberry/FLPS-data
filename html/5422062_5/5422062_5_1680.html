These compatriots of ours left a few days ago for the dear old country: Mrs. Demetras S. Campas, Mrs. Billa C. Tziovane and her beautiful daughter Suzanne, Mr. and Mrs. Spyros Koukoulomatis, and Mr. and Mrs. Eugene Naslides.

Dr. Christopher Mubayas, a prominent Greek dentist of Detroit, has arrived in Chicago to visit some of his relations and friends.

Dr. Mubayas formerly resided here. He expects to spend more than a week in Chicago.
