The Sons of Pericles, a junior organization, is making preparations for a unique program of entertainment, to take place at their dance given at the Blackstone Hotel. The committee, composed of well known Greek youths, decided to make the entertainment of special interest to the fair sex, by featuring a popularity contest.

The contest will be decided on the basis of the selling of the most tickets, together with the vote taken at the dance, the girl who has the best rating on these two points will be acclaimed the most popular Greek girl in Chicago.

Dancing will be the featured entertainment of the evening and some unusual stunts will be performed by the members of the organization.

This is the first Ball of the Sons of Pericles and proceeds from this Ball will be given to needy Greek families.
