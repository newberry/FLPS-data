p. 4.- The ladies of St. Andrews gave their third annual banquet last week in the hall of the church. About 130 members and friends were present. The banquet committee was made up of Mrs. Papanastasio, Mrs. Rifakis and Mrs. Chamales. Mrs. Lambros, president, welcomed the guests and introduced Mr. Depasta, Consul; Mr. Dritsa, lawyer; I. Tanagra Kopadis, wife of the Serbian Consul, and Mrs. Rifakis, who recently returned from Greece. A program followed the dinner, in which Mrs. Kopadis gave a recitation. The entire afternoon was a great success.
