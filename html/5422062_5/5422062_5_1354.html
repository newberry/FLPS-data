Very few Greeks were absent from the dance given by the Epsilon Phi Epsilon Club of Young Ladies which was given at the Sherman Hotel.

The young ladies were more than satisfied at the enthusiasm shown by those present. All the members of the club were glowing with pride and joy at the success of their first dance.

The officers of the club are:

Miss Nestoridos- President

Mary Lempeisi- Vice-president

Miss Siepis- Secretary

Miss Michalopoulos-Treasurer
