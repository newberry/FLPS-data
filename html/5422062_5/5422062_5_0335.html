Last Friday, the choir of St. Constantine and Helen church gave a small banquet at their hall. Miss Simaois, president, Miss Katsambis, treasurer, and the Misses Manes, Salikis, and Brouba organized the affair.

C. Dimopoulos, director of the group was guest of honor made a short speech, thanking the members of the choir and praising their good work. Misses Sabelis and Sarmoukou played a few selections on the piano. Dancing followed.
