It is with much pleasure that we announce the organization of a Young Women's Club here in Chicago. The purposes of this group will be: to retain and promote the customs and mores of Greek family life; to help the community assimilate the best part of the American way of life; to help make the new generation of Greek girls the finest type of womanhood from a social, cultural, and domestic viewpoint.

The club will also support all philanthropic movements.

An organization of this type can do an unlimited amount of good, and we are sure that the Greek girls of Chicago will rush to join its ranks.

We will soon publish the names of the girls who organized this club.
