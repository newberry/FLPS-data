p. 5.- The administrative council of St. Nicholas' parish calls all the parishers to a general meeting to take place on Nov. 1st in the auditorium of the Church, 5957 So. Peoria St.

The object of the meeting is to choose a layman representative, who with our Pastor, will represent the parish in the forthcoming Congress of laymen and clergyman, which will take place in New York on Nov. 16, 1931.
