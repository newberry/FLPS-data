EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE.

Malapanes, Tasiopoulos, Georgetsis, Alex, DeLevois, Gust Patsios, Dr. Joannides, Kolias Thomas Leventis.

Installation of the new officers will take place on Jan. 30th 8 P.M. at the Red Room, Hotel La Salle.

Post national commander R. P. Savage and Post Department Commander F. C. Watkins will be the installing officers.

A sergeant bugler and a drummer from the Illinois National Guard will land military color to the ceremonies. Jim Bowers, of Advertising Men's Post, will recite his famous selection "40 Hommes and 8 Cheveaux." A well known singer will sing a few selections. Refreshments will be served and a dance will follow the installing ceremonies.
