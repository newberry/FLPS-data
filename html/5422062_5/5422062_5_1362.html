The Ypsilanti chapter of the Sons of Pericles, founded but a few months ago, is preparing for its first ball to be held in the ball-room of the South-Side Masonic Temple, located at 6400 S. Green street, on Oct. 30th.

The committee in charge is promising in addition to the dancing program, a unique and unusual entertainment which will be long remembered in the future. Music will be provided by Varzos Orchestra.
