The united clergy of the Greek Orthodox Church of Chicago makes known to all Greeks of Chicago and suburbs, that Tuesday, January 27th St. Gregory's holiday mass will be said in honor of the Matropolitan of Syros and Tynos, the Most Rev. Philaretos, who leaves for Greece.

The liturgy will take place at the Cathedral of St. Basil. Forty-five members of the clergy will participate.
