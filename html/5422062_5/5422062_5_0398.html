p. b.- Over 4,000 people participated in the Seventh Masquerade Ball given by the progressive society of Greek ladies, Nea Genea, South Side Division at the Trianon.

Awards in gold were given to the best disguised, by the following judges: Paul Demos, George Kyriakopoulos, Stylianos Rekas, Mrs. Pafanti, Mrs. Tzovani and Miss Stavroulas J. Floudas, President of Nea Genea. The first prize was won by Miss Stamos.

The dance continued to the early morning hours.
