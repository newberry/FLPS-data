p. 2.- On Sunday, January 31, the holiday of the Three Hierarchs special mass, will be said at Holy Trinity Church. Bishop Callistos will be present.

In the evening of the same day, the Mother's club of the church and the Socrates school is giving a dance at 742 Sibley Street. At that time a Greek program will be presented by the pupils of Socrates school. A whole turnout of West Siders is predicted as well as many Greeks from other parts of the city.
