Notice is hereby given to the South Side Community members, that, as per article 45 of our constitution, the ones willing to put up a candidacy for counselman of the church or school, should do so by submitting a petition during the period 15th to 31st of October. Petitions ought to be submitted in writing by every one desiring to do so, or, by three members of the Community in behalf of a fourth one.

ex-Counsel.
