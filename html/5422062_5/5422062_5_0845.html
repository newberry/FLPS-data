The ladies in the St. Constantine Parish have formed a society. This organization was formed for the specific purpose of helping the Greek school and the church.

Reverend Theodore Prousianos suggested that this society be organized. The following officers were elected at the last meeting: Mrs. Maria Contos, president, Mrs. P. Rekas, vice-president, and Mrs. A. Zoe, secretary.

The ladies are going to raffle off a silver fruit dish in order to raise enough money to buy a bishop's throne for the church. They will need about $450 for this purpose.
