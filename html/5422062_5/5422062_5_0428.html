The society Nea Genea composed of Greek young girls is organizing a unique dance in the open to be given on August 27th, 8 P. M. at Pilsen Park, 26th Street and Albany Ave.

These young girls always have had success with their social affairs, due to the fact that they always present something new and interesting.

Nea Genea is known for its philanthropic doings and financial assistance to our Greek schools.
