p. 3.--The Greek Post of the American Legion, which, by the way, is one of the most active in the State of Illinois, will install its new officers on the 16th of this month at 8 p. m. in the Capitol Building.

The committee on arrangements for this affair has invited Greek musicians to play for the occasion. It has also invited the baritone Mr. Leonidas Kardnes to sing, and Mr. P. Vournas with his orchestra will entertain those who attend with popular music and Greek folk-songs.
