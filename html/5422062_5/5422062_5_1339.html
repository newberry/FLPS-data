p. 4.- Last Sunday the members of the Young Ladies Philomusical Club on the North Side held elections for the coming year. Those elected are: A. Galvert, president; K. Argyropoulos, vice-president; E. Vournazous, treasurer; G. Ladas, secretary; and M. Karambis, recording secretary. Trustees are: Misses B. Floros, F. Argyropoulos, A. Laris, E. Oikonomopoulos, A. Karagiannis, and E. Giannakopoulos.

We wish the utmost success and progress to the new officers.
