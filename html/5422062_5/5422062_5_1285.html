Those Greek men in Chicago who became twenty-one years of age between June 5, 1918 and August 24, 1918 must register today, Saturday, between the hours 7:00 A.M. and 9:00 P.M., at the local boards of their district. Anyone needing any guidance or information in regard to this matter may call at the nearest police station.

Disregard for this order is punishable by law. Any evaders are liable to immediate, compulsory conscription.
