Mr. Spiros Dimitrios, an Athenian professor and general representative of "Panagios Tafos" has been in Chicago for several days.

His purpose in Chicago is to establish chapter of Panagios Tafos here. This Athenian organization has chapters in all parts of Greece in order to make pilgrimages to Jerusalem and the Holy Land.

Chicagoans are invited to the Sherman Hotel, Friday evening, 8 o'clock, in order to hear Mr. Dimitrios explain the aims and purpose of the club and how he has already started a chapter in New York.
