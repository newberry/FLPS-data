p. 3- Nea Genea (New Generation) an association of young women, will give its third annual dance on Monday, September 22, in the magnificent and luxurious Trianon dance hall.

The young ladies of Nea Genea, who in past years have displayed such activity and zeal for the success of their dances, will surprise us this year with their "Autumn Breeze," an affair that will live in the memory of the community for a long time. Therefore, all to the Trianon on that evening! For the proceeds will go to build a school on the North Side.
