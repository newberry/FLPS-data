The dance given by St. Helen's club of St. Constantine Church and Korais School was a great success. Among those present were: Mr. Seriadis, Mr. Kalpakin, Mr. Sarlas. and his sister Athanasia, Mrs. Trakos, Dr. Touloupan and his sister, the Hatzopolos sisters and many others. We congratulate Mrs. Zathas, the President Mrs. Koutsoalis, and the Secretary Helen Dalkos for their indefatigable work.
