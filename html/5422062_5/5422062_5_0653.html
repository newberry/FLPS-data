p. 1 - Constantinople, Nov. 14. Ecumenical Patriarch Gregorios, who had been ill for more than a month, died today after an operation.

The deceased Patriarch, who exercised authority over the Greek Orthodox Church of America, was the very well-known former Metropolitan Karchedonos. He succeeded Patriarch Meletios and was elected for his many virtues, his ability, his patriotism, and his good judgment. The Greek churches of Chicago will hold masses for the deceased prelate of the Greek Orthodox Church.
