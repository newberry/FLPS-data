p. 3-We hereby notify all the Greek communities of our district of the contents of the following communication from the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Greece to the Greek Minister at Washington, D. C.:

"The Holy Synod of the Ecumenical Patriarchate, convened as the highest ecclesiastical court, has degraded the Metropolitan of Haldea and assigned him again to the rank of layman."

L. Matles, Consul General of Greece in Chicago.
