What a beautiful word "church" is. It is a word that should have a beautiful spiritual meaning yet our churches have sunk to the level of our coffee-houses. There are still a few people left who say, "Let us go to church and say our prayers." These people are becoming more scarce every day.

If Jesus were living today and saw the number of collection plates in our churches he would say "Stay home and say your prayers - this is robbery."

The Greek communities of America are breaking up because the clergical desire for money is surpassing the desire for Orthodoxy and Christianity.
