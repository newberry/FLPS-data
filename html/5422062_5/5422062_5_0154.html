p. 5.- After a final threat from receivers that the two churches of Annunciation and St. James will close immediately unless paid for, the officers got together and held a meeting at Solon school. Bishop Callistos was present.

Various plans for raising the nessary money were discussed. By donations and other means $1,555.00 were raised and the churches are once more saved. They are not out of danger yet, but are safe at the present time. Another sum of $503.000 has just been added to the first amount.
