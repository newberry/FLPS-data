On January 19 the installment of the new council of the Hellenic Post of the American Legion took place in the Morrison Hotel. Various speeches were made by officers of the American Legion Post.

Mr. Ganan, retiring president, received a valuable gift from the Post in appreciation of his fine work. Mr. D. Nickolopoulos, owner of the Ritz Laundry, was elected president. A. Pantezis, organizer of the Post, was in charge of the installation ceremony.
