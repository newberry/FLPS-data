p. 1.- Last Friday, at 5100 Wentworth, a meeting of all South Siders took place in order to solve the church problem. At 8 o'clock the hall was crowded to capacity. C. Petroulas was chairman of the evening. He spoke on the need of another church, especially to the South Siders who had to go so far for services. He was followed by speakers T. Koumoungis, G. Bright or Xanthos, A. Apalodimas, P. Roris, J. Orpanopoulos. All spoke in favor of another church. The members of the community were given till the next meeting to think it over.
