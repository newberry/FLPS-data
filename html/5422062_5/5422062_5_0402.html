This coming Wednesday, February 26, at 7:30 P. M. St. Andrews is holding an open meeting at the church hall, 5658 Winthrop Ave. Mrs. Tanagra Kopadis is going to speak, J. Niklopoulos and D. Bournas are going to play mandolin duets, and J. Papageorge will sing a few songs. Everyone will want to be there. Admission is only one dollar.
