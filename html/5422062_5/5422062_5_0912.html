The Greek Orthodox Church of the Annunciation, on Chicago's North Side, reports that income for the period June 1, 1914-May 1, 1915 was $10,091.23. The total expenses for the same period amounted to $10,322.81. In the years between January 1910, when the church was founded, and May 30, 1915, the total income of this institution has been $100,930.80. The expenses in the same period of time were $100,930.80.
