During the meeting which took place on Wednesday, December 17, it was decided to appoint a committee of ten whose duty would be to enroll new members. To be eligible a man must have served in either the American Army or Navy during the last war.

The dues were fixed at three dollars, with a small charge of twenty-five cents for enrollment. The members of the committee are A. A. Pantelis, president; D. A. Tasiopoulos, secretary...
