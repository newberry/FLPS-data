p. 5.- Last Friday the following women were elected officers of St. George for the coming year: Mrs. Karkazis, president; vice-presidents, Mrs. Chamilomati and Tzanetakou; secretary, Mrs. Panagiotarou; and treasurer, Mrs. Argyropoulos. Trustees are: G. Kordopatis, E. Tulupan, P. Siama, D. Mbartzali, P. Bouloukou, K. Zaphiropoulos and E. Kypiazi.

Election officers: M. Kominos, K. Bookes, and A. Vombrak.
