p. 5.- For the sake of unity, cooperation, understanding, and harmony, in the Greek communities of Chicago, the Pastor of St. Andrews and St. Constantine, will change places this coming Sunday.

The Rev. M. Petrakis, Head Priest of St. Constantine Church, will hold mass at the church of St. Andrews and the Rev. Eirinaios Tsourounakis will hold mass at the church of St. Constantine.

The diocese of Chicago informs the parishers of the respective churches of this interchange in pastors for next Sunday.
