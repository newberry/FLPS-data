The election of new officers for the New Generation, society of Greek maidens, took place last Thursday. The following officers were elected; Penelope Petropoulus, President; Maria Hoida, Vice-president; Stavroula Katradis, Treasurer; Constantina Soulanzis, Secretary. Executive Board: Chrysanthe Dilberakis, Vasiliki Batsakis, Aglaia Varellas, Athena Batsakis, Helen Bardavlia.

The new board decided to give a dance, May 10th, at the Winderemere East Hotel, 1642 East 56th Street.
