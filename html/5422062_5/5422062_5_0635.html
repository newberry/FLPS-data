p. 1. The Metropolitan of Thessaloniki, His Holiness, Mr. Gennadios, with his entourage, arrived in Chicago. The reception committee, headed by the president of Y.M.C.A., Mr. Francis, escorted the distinguished visitor to the La Salle Hotel. A dinner was given by the United Greek Societies in honor of the Prelate and his entourage. The dinner was attended by 700 including the Mayor of Chicago and many personages.

Noted among the speakers were the Most Rev. Mr. Philaretos, Bishop of Chicago; the Mayor, Mr. Paleologos Mammonas, and Mr. Francis the Master of Ceremonies.
