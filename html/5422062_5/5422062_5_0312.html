p. 5 Last Tuesday the Eusebia club, of St. Basil Church, held a banquet in the hall of the church for all members of the parish. A program followed the banquet Mrs. Agriostatmis, chairman, presented Mr. K. Mammounas, who spoke a few words and then introduced Miss M. Lembesis who played the piano. Then G. Dedakis recited a monologue. The three Missess Husmon gave us a few selections. Two played the violin and one the piano. Bishop Kaclistos spoke and was followed by Miss Agriostathis who played the piano. George Alexander spoke. His wife accompanied his son on the piano while he played the violin. The evening ended with everyone thanking Mrs. H'Dimitriou, president, for a lovely evening.
