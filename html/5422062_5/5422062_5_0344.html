After ten months' absence, Mr. Spyros Kotakis, the manager of the Greek Daily, has returned from Greece.

Mr. Kotakis, was delegated, as is known, by the Greek communities of Chicago to go to seek a solution of our church problem in Athens and Constantinople, and as a result, we have the Right Rev. Metropolitan Bishop of Corinth, Mr. Damaskinos, sent as a Legate.

The Greek community of Chicago has expressed its greetings and enthusiastic congratulations for the success of the mission.
