Last Sunday the cornerstone of the new church of St. Constantine was laid by the Holy Rev. Alexander of New York, Archbishop of all the Greek churches of North and South America. The thousands of Greeks after listening to the words of the Prelate rushed to the donation desk, and the result was that $16,000 were raised for both the church and the Greek School, which latter will be in the same building. The Prelate praised the Greeks for their devotion to both their adopted country, America, and to their Mother country, Greece. Archbishop Alexander also maintained that Greek Religion and the Greek tongue indispensable to the Greeks of America.
