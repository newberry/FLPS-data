The Spolek Ceskych Lekaru (Association of Bohemian [Man] Physicians) held a meeting last night at which a discussion took place as to what would be the most suitable way of honoring the memory of Dr. Jan M. Kara, a Bohemian-American physician and member of the medical expedition to Serbia, who has become a victim of his own noble vocation, having died of a contagious disease contracted while he was working for the Slav cause.

After some discussion the meeting appointed a committee to work out the best way of honoring Dr. Kara's name.....
