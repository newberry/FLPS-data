The body of Mr. Otokar Ludvik, son of Mrs. Bohumila Ludvik, director of the Bohemian theatre, and famous dramatic artist, will be cremated at Montrose Cemetery, and the remains buried in the Bohemian National Cemetery. He died Wednesday morning, and was twenty years and six months of age. It is not long since his father, Mr. Framtisek Ludvik, also a director, died. Much sympathy is being expressed to the grief-stricken Mother, in the hope that it will at least, partially alleviate her twofold sorrow.

The funeral of the deceased will take place this morning from Mr. Radovse's hall at 18th and Laflin Streets.
