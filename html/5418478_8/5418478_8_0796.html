P.1, Col.2--Under this heading, we wish to submit a report of yesterday's contributions, from various individuals and the Pilsen Bando-Concertina Club, for a million penny fund for the Scholastic School in Bohemia. Contributions were made by the following: Joseph Rabusitz, Joseph Strecha, John Malecek and the Pilsen Bandonian Concertina Club.

Mr. Martinek, 2754 Central Park Ave., and Mr. James Supa, 416 W. 25th Place, also donated their their share.
