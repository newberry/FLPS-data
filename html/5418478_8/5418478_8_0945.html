Springfield, Illinois, April 2. Governor Dunne today sent to the Senate further appointments of State officials, among whom we find that Mr. James B. Dibelka of Chicago has been appointed State Architect. Our countryman, Dibelka, is a wellknown member of the Bohemian community in Chicago, a prominent architect, and a member of the Chicago Board of Education. Now a recognized expert in architecture, he has the added distinction of being appointed State Architect of the State of Illinois. He was sponsored for this office by Mayor Harrison and a number of Bohemian organizations in Chicago.
