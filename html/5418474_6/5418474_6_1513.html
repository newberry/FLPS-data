The Teutonic Players of America, sent the following information to the editor of the Abendpost: For the benefit of the German Societies of Chicago, who were severely affected by the business depression and through unemployment, two performances of Johann Strauss' "The Bat," will be given at the Chicago Civic Opera house, Sunday Oct. 3th.

On this occasion the extremely popular and prominent opera singer Lya Beyer, will appear as guest artist, in the role of "Rosalinde," the well known "Reinhardt" arrangement, will be performed.

The cast for the remaining roles will be announced later.
