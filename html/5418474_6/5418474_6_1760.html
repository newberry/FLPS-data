A society of German artists has been formed in Chicago with the aim of offering German art and German artists the place to which they are entitled by reason of their importance in public life. The society takes it upon itself to bring to light the best that German artists have produced. This will be accomplished by arranging annual and semiannual exhibitions and by participating in public displays of German works.

At the head of the organization stands a superintending board consisting of the following gentlemen: John Nordinger, H. Bartsch, Otto R. Niebuhr, Dr. Karl Bunge, and Karl Vitzthum. The acting secretary is Otto R. Niebuhr, 812 Barry Avenue.
