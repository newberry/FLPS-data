Music, Ludwig Becker, as successor to Leopold Kramer, concert master with Thomas's Orchestra, will not renew his contract, which terminates in the spring, instead he expects to function as assistant director and principal of the violin department at the "Columbia School of Music." Mr. Becker said yesterday, that the only reason for his resignation from the orchestra is his wish to increase his financial emoluments. For 14 years, Mr. Becker has been second and finally first concertmaster of the orchestra.

According to reports, Leopold Kramer is to be concertmaster of the Chicago Opera Orchestra, next fall.
