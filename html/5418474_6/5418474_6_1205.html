The latest issue of a periodical published a list of citizens of Chicago who have the distinction of being millionaires. It stated also that there are at least one thousand individuals whose wealth is estimated at $500,000 to $1,000,000.

The following Chicago citizens are German-Americans, and are worth from $1,000,000 to $5,000,000.

E. J. Lehmann,

Conrad Seipp,

Jacob Rosenberg,

Otto Young,

George Schneider,

Jacob Rahm,

Conrad Fuerst,

Henry A. Cohn,

Michael Brand,

Peter Schoenhofen.
