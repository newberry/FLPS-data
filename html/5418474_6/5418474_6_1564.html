The last of the three Jubilee concerts of Bruno Steindl in celebration of his twenty five years of membership in the "Chicago Symphony Orchestra", took place in Orchestra Hall.

The artist, assisted by his wife and Mssrs. Harry Weisbach, Franz Weisbach, Franz Esser and Otto Roehrborer, played the "Quintette in F", by Cesar Frank, and "Es-Dur, Op. 49", by Schumann.
