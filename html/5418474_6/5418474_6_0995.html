The National Association of German-American Teachers sent a circular letter to all German-American teachers of Chicago; requesting them to come to a meeting at the Northside Turnerhall, June 17th, 2 P.M.

The purpose of the meeting is, to develop a detailed travelling schedule in regards to the German Teachers' concention, which will be June 10th to June 14th in Newark, N.J.

The described circular letter was signed by M. Schmishofer, 601 St. Elmo Street.
