The National Congress of Music Teachers, met at 9 A.M., at Hershey's Halle, under the Chairmanship of Arthut Mees of Cincinnati. Mr. Mees opened the Congress with an address, after which the "Women's Quartet of Chicago" gave a song recital, which was followed by a speech by Dr. Thomas. The subject of the Afternoon session will be, "Modern Church Music.

The discussions are of an entirely religious character.
