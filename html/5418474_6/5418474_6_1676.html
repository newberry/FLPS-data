The May Music Festival begins next Tuesday, and we will have the opportunity to hear the following solists: Mrs. Amelia Friedrick, Materna, Miss Emma Juch and Mrs. Christine Nilsson, Sopranos, Miss Emily Winant, alto; Messrs. Hermann Winkelman and Theo Toedt, tenors; Messrs. Frantz Reunnertz and Max Reunnertz and Max Heinrich, baritones and Mr. Emil Scaria, Basso.

Mr. Theodore Thomas conducting.
