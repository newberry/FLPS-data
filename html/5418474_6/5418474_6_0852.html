The National Fancier's Association is holding a poultry exibition at Dearborn and 16th Streets. It was there, that the recently organized National Association of German-American Poultry Farmers met for the first time last night, to appoint officers for the organization. The membership is rapidly increasing, having one hundred members already. Mr. Andreas Simon, editor of the agricultural supplement of the Illinois Staats-Zeitung, delivered the opening address.
