Otto Wasmansdorff, secretary

Messrs. Fraser and Chalmers are building in Chicago the ore-crushing machinery for the reduction works which are to be installed in the Company's property, which consists of twenty-two of the richest silver mines in the Globe district, Pinal County, Arizona.

A large supply of rich ore has already been mined, and is available for treatment as soon as the mill is completed, thus assuring an immediate income to the Company.
