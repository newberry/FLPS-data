p. 5 - 6 A German society of engineers, architects, and chemists, known as the Societe Technique, met at No. 106 Randolph Street last evening and took steps for establishing a bureau of information for societies and professional men of the United States and Europe during the World's Fair. This bureau will be quartered in the new German Theatre Building. H. F. Wallman is President of the society, and Charles Otterstedde, Secretary.
