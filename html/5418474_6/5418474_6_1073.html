A syndicate headed by Nathan Shefner, an attorney, will build a four-story hotel on 4820-4822 W. Chicago Avenue. Raymond Gregori, architect, drew the plans for the $160,000 building, which will have 80 rooms, each with a private bathroom. Wollenberger and Company have financed the venture. The new hotel has been leased to Jacob Siegel, well-known hotel owner. The lease is for twenty-five years and calls for $288,000.
