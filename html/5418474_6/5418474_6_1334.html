The Wirthsuerein (Tavernkeepers' Association) held its general meeting at the Turnhalle on the North Side yesterday, at half-past two in the afternoon. Mr. Feldcamp was the chairman.

After reading the minutes of the previous meeting, Peter Mueller, chairman of the Committee on Legal Affairs, submitted a report of the Collection Committee. Thus far, $440 has been collected. The recommendations of this Committee will be discussed at the next meeting.

Philipp Dieter, treasurer, declared that the Association's fund amounts to $668.
