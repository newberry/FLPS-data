Annual Statement.

The Italian Women's Benefit Society has issued the following financial statement to the State Board of Charity in Springfield, Ill., during the year, 1912.

Income:

Ready cash in Treasury beginning of year $ 801.24
Profit on benefit dance 1,047.91
Dues received from members 176.50
Rummage sale 86.00
Total $2,111.67

Sums on Hand and Donated.

Ready cash in Treasury after the year $1,407.46
Expense for correspondence 24.40
Donations to Columbus Hospital 50.00
Donations to poor 629.79
Total $2,111.67
