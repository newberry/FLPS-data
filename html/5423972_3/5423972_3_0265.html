A Victory for the Ladies Tailors in Blum Shop.

Local 104 of the Ladies Tailors Union was victorious in Blums Ladies Tailor Shop. Mr. Blum recognized the union and granted a 44 hour week to all employees. Salaries are as follows: Cloak-makers $33.00 per week, for girls a minimum of $25.00 per week, alteration workers $28.00 per week. The firm must employ union help only.
