Today from two to six o'clock in the afternoon, there will be free theater entertainment, at the Metropolitan Theater, for Emanuel M. Abrahams, candidate for Alderman of the 9th ward.

Congressman Sabath's letter of recommendation, recommending the voters of the 9th ward to elect Mr. Abrahams as Alderman of that ward: "In all my years as Judge of the Maxwell Street Court, I have found Mr. Abrahans to serve only the best interests of his people. He has in his previous public position, clearly demonstrated his ability to fill a position of such importance."

Adolph J. Sabath.
