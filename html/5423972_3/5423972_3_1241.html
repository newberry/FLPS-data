This organization, from a small beginning three years ago, includes at present over 300 women, banded together to fight the "white plague" among the Jews of this city. Originally planned for the support of patients at the sanitarium of the Denver Jewish Consumptives Relief Society, it gradually invaded the local field and is at present a powerful co-operative agency in dealing with the general situation. Its program includes visiting the Consumptives in their homes, the placing of patients in various institutions, and supplying milk, and eggs, etc.
