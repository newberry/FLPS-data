Max Levy breaks agreement with the United Garment Workers.

Max Levy of 507 Jackson Blvd. employs 150 tailors and cutters, all members of the United Garment Workers Union.

About a week ago, Mr. Levy notified his workers that he was through with them and their lable, and closed his shop. He wanted to retain the cutters but when they found out what Mr. Levy was up to, they all decided to strike.
