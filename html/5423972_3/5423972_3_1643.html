Invitations have been sent to all the members of the order B'nai Brith in this city to attend a reception to be given by the Covenant Culture Club.

The Club is an institution created under the auspices of the B'nai Brith lodges of our city for the purpose of fostering the growth of the order and promoting the social, moral and intellectual welfare of its members. The organization also furnishes aid and encouragement to benevolent and philanthropic efforts. The club is to be the headquarters for the B'nai Brith and also of all Jewish philanthropic organizations.

The officers are Herman Felsenthal, president, Max Stern, vice-president, Adolf Kraus, treasurer, Dr. A. R. Levy, secretary, and Arnold Wolf, financial secretary.
