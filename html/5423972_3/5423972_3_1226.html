The Northwest Cooperative Association, which has a cooperative grocery in its neighborhood, will be celebrating soon the opening of a new store at 2732 Division st.

The Northwest Side Cooperative is not content with its activities, and wish to do more. They have decided therefore, to unite with the Worker's Cooperative Society in the Douglas Park district, which has a grocery store at 330 W. 15th st. and a restaurant at 3058 W. 12th st.,and together to control the three undertakings.
