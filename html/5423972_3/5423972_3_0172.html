The large firm, Cling Bros. manufactures of clothing and raincoats have signed a Union agreement with the raincoat makers Union and the employers, after striking for several weeks, returned to work under Union conditions.

Cling Bros. wanted to enforce an open shop and thereby had placed his shop under the managerment of a contracor whose object was to break the Union. The Joint Board cooperated with the workers and called a strike.

The Cling Bros. must now abide by the following agrement:

The employees will work 44 hours instead of 48.

A preferential Union shop. The scabs were dismissed from their positions.
