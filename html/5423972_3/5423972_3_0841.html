The strike which was declared by the Joint Board of the Cloak-Maker's Union at J. B. Berman, dress manufacturer, 337 S. Market St., was settled through an agreement, which was signed by the union and manufacturer. Mr. J. Berman granted all union conditions.

The other strike, which was declared yesterday by the Joint Board at Aldriger and Franklin, of the same building, is in full swing and will continue until the bosses will grant the union conditions.
