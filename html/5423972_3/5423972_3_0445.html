May Festival of the Women's Trade Union League.

The Women's Trade Union League is sponsoring a public meeting and festival this afternoon at the Federation Hall, 275 La Salle St., 2nd Floor.

Starting at 3:00 o'clock and on, Miss Page of the Chicago Kindergarten Institute will conduct Spring Songs and the Choir will offer some very fine selections. Everybody is welcome. Refreshments will be served.

Miss Raymond Robins

Emma Steighagen, Sec'y.
