All the shareholders are invited to come to the general meeting Wednesday Feb. 26th at 8 o'clock sharp, at the Talmud Torah Hall, corner Rockwell and Hirsch st.

Those who want us to progress with the work of the Cooperative bakery on the Northwest Side must come and do practical work. Until now, the work brought wonderful results, and now we must start doing constructive work which you will partake in.

If you have moved since you became a shareholder, please let us know personally, or write to M. Ladanski, Fin. Sec. 1133 Washtenaw ave.

With regards

The Board of Directors.
