A representative of the Cap-Makers Union, Local 5, notified the Board of Arbitration, that the union decided to demand a raise in wages. The Board of Arbitration, of which Prof. Mullenbauch is chairman, will deal with the demand of the cap-makers in the approaching few weeks.

The cap trade is now in a good condition. The cap manufacturers held a meeting in the La Salle Hotel and from their reports it was evident that the condition of that trade is good and several shops are working overtime.

A report comes from the general office of the United Cloth-Hat and Cap-Makers Union showing that the general executive board will begin a mighty organizational campaign in the Western states of the United States and in Canada.
