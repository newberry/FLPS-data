Comrade Benjamin Schlessinger, the president of the International Ladies Garment workers is now visiting Chicago.

His presence here will be of great benefit to the Skirts and Dress makers. The Joint Board of the Cloak maker locals decided to carry on a campaign to organize the dress and skirt makers. A meeting was held at 180 Washington St., last Monday where the plans of conducting a campaign was reported by organizer Greenberg to an audience of 200 chairladies and a price committee.
