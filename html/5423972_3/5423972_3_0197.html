Local 39 of the Amalgamated has made the beginning of a realistic cooperative movement in Chicago.

Comrade L. Waldman, the director of the United Cooperatives addressed a meeting of the local, to whom he presented the necessity for cooperative activity. Comrade Waldman made a strong impression on the membership, who decided to organize a credit union or a credit bank.

The matter was referred to the Board of Directors, where the sentiment for the project is very strong.
