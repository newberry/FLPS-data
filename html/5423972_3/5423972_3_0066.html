The Amalgamated of Chicago is making feverish preparations to celebrate the First of May, the international labor holiday, with a big demonstration, a concert, and a massmeeting in the big Coliseum, Wabash Avenue and 16th Street.

The program of the First of May celebration will consist of two parts: a concert and a massmeeting in the afternoon, and a dance at night. The committee selected to arrange the First of May celebration is now working out the details.

The Chicago Amalgamated has rented the big Coliseum specially for this year's First of May celebration, to accommodate the big number of tailors, so that they may attend the concert in the afternoon and the dance in the evening.
