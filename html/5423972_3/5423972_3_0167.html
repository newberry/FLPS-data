Chicago Federation of Labor determines to help the United Hebrew Trade Unions in the work against spies.

In accordance with the decision to help the United Hebrew Trade Unions raise a requisite sum to unveil the spies and the spy system of the detective agencies, the secretary of the Chicago Federation of Labor has given a credential to Morris Ziskind, representative of the United Hebrew Trade Unions, in order to visit the Unions of the Chicago Federation of Labor and appeal for money for this purpose.
