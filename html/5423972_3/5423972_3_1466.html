The first large campaign meeting of the Socialist party will be held in Chicago to-morrow, Sunday, at two o'clock in the Labor Lyceum at Kedzie and Ogden Avenues.

The principal speakers will be as follows:

William P. Kraus, congressional candidate in the 6th congressional district.

James T. Frankel, candidate for United States Senator from Illinois. S. P. Kramer, managing editor of the "Forward".

Morris Siskind, Labor editor of the "Forward".

The citizens of the 6th congressional district should come and be informed of the vital political and economic issues confronting us today.

Issues to be discussed will be

1. The high cost of living

2. High rents

3. The shortage of coal

4. The cause of Eugene V. Debs present jail sentence.
