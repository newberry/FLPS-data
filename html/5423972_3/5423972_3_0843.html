Cloakmaker's Union Declare Strike at Rubloff and Co.

The firm, Rubloff and Co., 309 Monroe St., attempted to operate a non-union cloak shop on the South Side, but the union became aware of this and hindered them in their progress.

The Cloakmaker's Union will not permit, under any circumstances, any firm to produce cloaks under non-union conditions.

The cloakmakers are requested to support the union in order to make the strike successful.
