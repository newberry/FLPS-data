Wednesday evening Nov. 17th, a general meeting of all Jewish Socialist Party members will be held at 3322 Douglas Blvd. The following party problems will be considered:

1. Reports of the officers: a) recording sec'y. b) financial sec'y. c) secretary organizer.

2. Jewish Daily Forward.

3. Organizational problems of the Branches - introduction by K. Marmar.

4. How our Socialist Campaign is conducted - introduction by H. Schneid.
