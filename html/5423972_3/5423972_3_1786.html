Hugo Pam elected Judge.

The Jewish voting public of Chicago marched to the polls in masses yesterday to cast their votes for the Jewish candidate, Mr. Hugo Pam, for Superior Judge of Cook County. The election returns show a large majority for Mr. Pam.

With many thanks, in a statement to the Jewish public of Chicago, Mr. Pam faithfully promised that he is at any time ready and willing to serve the public to his best ability.
