The young Jewish Baker Drivers Union, won a victory without a strike. It is only a few months since the Jewish bakery drivers have organized. They have already scored a victory and deserve to be in line with the aggressive trade unions. When they presented their demands to the employers, it was thought that it would result in a strike, but the employers, as it appears, realized that the workers presented just demands, and they signed an agreement with the union, with the following conditions:

1. Recognition of the Union.

2. A raise in pay of 20 per cent.

3. A six (6) day working week, excluding Saturday.

4. No work Labor Day or May 1st.

5. Pay for all Jewish Holy days.

The Bakers Drivers Union deserve congratulations from all organized workers on their speedy victory.
