The organizer is Comrade Levitas, the renowned lecturer and journalist, who came to America in 1914. He has been back in Russia since, and returned to Chicago a few months ago. These last few years in Russia afforded him a great deal of new experience and new possibilities to develop his talent and the comrades expect to accomplish a great deal under his management.

Comrade Levitas was editor of the Rabotshni Gazete, published by the Socialist Democratic party, in Vladivostok, he was also secretary of the party of Moscow in 1918 and was arbitrator in Kiev and other cities.

In the near future, announcements will be made of the plans which the Alliance will undertake under the leadership of Comrade Levitas.
