The United Hebrew Trades discussed the conditions of the thousands of Jewish workers who are employed in districts, and determined to start a campaign to organize them in Unions, where there are no such organization.

For this purpose, the United Hebrew Trades decided unanimously yesterday to call a conference of all executive boards of the unions, belonging to the United Hebrew Trades and to invite the Joint Board of the Amalgated Clothing Workers of the joint board of the Clockmakers Unions, and the City Central Committee of the Workmen's Circle, discuss the importance of carrying on a great organizations campaign for these Jewish Workers of the factories and stores.
