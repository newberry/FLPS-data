The Chicago Shoe-Makers Association had a meeting yesterday at Priticken's Hall, corner, Maxwell and Halsted streets. Mr. Sam Miller was the speaker, and spoke very convincingly of the importance for the shoe-makers of Chicago to organize themselves.

A suggestion was made to establish a cooperative leather store in which all members will be able to purchase needed leather goods at the right prices. Three other well known speakers also spoke. They are as follows: Mr. Rosenberg, Mr. Marcus and Mr. Piser.
