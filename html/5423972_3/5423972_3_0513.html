The Amalgamated Clothing Workers will open their educational season in the Ashland Auditorium, Friday evening, October 3.

The noted lecturer, Comrade Scott Nearing, will speak on "The Worker's Education,"Alexander Zikowsky together with his symphony orchestra, will render classical music and Miss Lillian Sandel, famous opera soprano, will sing. It will be an evening of spiritual and cultural enjoyment and there is no doubt that thousands of members will be present at the opening of the educational season. Sam Levin, manager of the Joint Board, will be chairman of the evening.
