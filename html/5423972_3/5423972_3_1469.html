This Tuesday (September 14, 1920), two open air Socialist meetings will be held.

One will be held on the north west side at 3:00 P. M., corner Washtenaw Ave. and Division St. The speakers will be W. M. Fergenbaum, M. Bahal and George Coop. The other will be in the Douglas Park District at 8:P. M., corner St. Louis Ave. and Roosevelt Road. The speakers will be Morris Siskind and Dr. M. Green.
