The 25th annual convention of District Grand Lodge No.6, was held in this city of Feb. 5th, 6th, and 7th. There were present about 60 delegates representing lodges in Illinois, Wisconsin, Mich., Minn., and Neb.

Israel Cowen of Chicago was elected and installed as the new president.

On Sunday evening, the local lodges gave an entertainment in honor of the visiting delegates. Sinai Temple was filled by member to hear the address of Mr. Jacob Furth. On Tuesday night, an elaborate banquet was given at the Great Northern Hotel.
