All salesman of dry goods, clothing, shoes, and groceries in department stores are invited to attend a meeting of the newly organized "Jewish Progressive Salesmen Association of Chicago" on Sunday April 10th at 7:00 o'clock in the evening, Rosensteins Hall, 634 West Twelfth Street. Attend our meeting and become acquainted with our plans.

The Jewish Progressive Salesmen Associated of Chicago

B. Halpein, Sec'y.
