The meeting of the co-operative conference, which was held last Sunday in Workers Circle Lyceum, has taken an important step in the work for cooperation.

The Central Committee of the conference recommended the following :

1. The conference should elect a committee that shall be united with the Central Committees of the existing cooperative society. They should manage the co-operative undertakings which are in existence and are to open new co-operative enterprises.

2. The conference should decide to open a co-operative grocery store on the West Side.

3. The conference should start a campaign for shares. The delegates discussed the recommendations and with a small exception, decided on the following:

To elect a board of directors of seven, who together with six members of the co-operative societies, should be the administrators of all co-operative enterprises.

The conference decided to instruct the new administration board, to discuss at its first meeting, the question of opening a store on the West Side. The report which the delegates of the Workmen's Circle branches given showed that many branches had members who belong to unions. These members pay a dollar to their union for co-operation, and they think that they should not have to give another dollar to the branch.

The delegates accepted the decision that Workmen's Circle branches should be able to join the conference, when they buy two shares for the branch, and secure voluntary contributions of a dollar each and think those who give a dollar through a union, shall not give any more.
