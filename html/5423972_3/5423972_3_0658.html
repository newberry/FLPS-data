Hilman Captures Large Meeting of Local Amalgamated.

Sam Rissman read the first list of the shops which have brought their contributions to aid the (New York) strike. The employees of the Royal Tailors gave from $5 to $10 each - a total of $500. Hart, Schaffner and Marx factory B gave $1,200, another factory $600. Stay Tailors, $120, Shoenfeld and Yoder, $228, Alfred, Decker and Cohen, $1,600. 28 spongers and examiners of Kuppenheimer's West Side shop, $384. The first day of collections brought in a total of $4,800.
