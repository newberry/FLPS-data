Who was Lenin? What were his achievements? How was Russia governed during his regime? Professor Hurwitz, will lecture on all these questions tonight at the W. C. Lyceum, Kedzie and Ogden Avenue.

Professor Hurwitz, has recently returned from a visit to Russia, where he had the opportunity to study Lenin, his life and influence. Therefore, today's lecture will be very interesting to every person interested in Lenin and his personality.

The lecture will start promptly at 8 P. M. The lecture was arranged by the Educational Committee of the Workmen's Circle.
