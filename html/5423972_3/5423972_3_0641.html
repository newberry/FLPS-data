The Dress and Skirtmakers, members of Local 100, International Ladies' Garment Workers' Union, yesterday successfully ended their negotiations with the representatives of the Manufacturers Association, with whom an agreement had existed for the past two years.

The Dress and Skirt Manufacturers had demanded a 25% decrease in wages.

Mr. Schlesinger, President of the International, stated that under no circumstances would the Union accept such a proposal.

Yesterday morning, after the representatives of the Association had discussed the matter another time, they withdraw their demand for a cut in wages and signed a contract, extending the previous agreement another five months.
