Great Preparations are Being Made for the Fifth Convention of the Amalgamated in Chicago.

The Convention Committee of the Amalgamated is already engaged in preparing the work for the fifth semi-annual convention of the Amalgamated Clothing Workers of America, which will open in Chicago, Monday May 8, in the Ashland Auditorium.

An assemblage of 375 delegates from various sections of the country will be present at this convention. The amount present will reach about 500, including the invited guests. The fifth convention of the Amalgamated will open Monday morning May 8, and continue for one week, closing May 13.

2

The first session of the convention will be held in the Ashland Auditorium and the remaining sessions will be held in the Oriental Consistory, Dear-born and Walton Sts. The convention will be the most important one ever held by the Amalgamated and the delegates are sharpening the "weapons" for this epoch making convention with much work to accomplish and many important problems to solve.

Prior to the convention on May 3-4, the general executive board will hold a conference, preparing the first and most important work for the convention. The headquarters of the executive board will be in the Morrison Hotel.
