Tens of thousands of Amalgamated members of Chicago yesterday celebrated the international [workers'] holiday, May first, with mass meetings, concerts, and balls. All Amalgamated shops were closed on account of the holiday and tens of thousands of workers filled the various halls where the Union had arranged entertainments for them.

The largest crowd gathered in the afternoon at the mass meeting and concert in the Auditorium Theater, and in the evening, at the ball at the Ashland Auditorium.
