At a special meeting of Local 237, Jewish Bakers Union. ... a report of the negotiations regarding day labor and higher pay for night labor was given.....

The members voted to accept the proposals of the bosses. The conditions are as follows:

The bakery workers who work until 11:00 P. M. will receive the same wages as day laborers, and those who work after 11:00 P.M. until 5:00 A.M. will receive an additional 25 cents per hour.
