Tonight, at 5 P. M., the children and parents of the Workmen's Circle schools will hold a meeting with Comrade Ziebert, delegate of the Jewish working class in Poland, who came to America to raise money for the Medem Fund. The League is now being built in Warsaw in commemoration of that great Socialist leader, Vladmir Medem.

Comrade Ziebert, who is connected with the Jewish secular schools in Poland, will tell the children about the life of Jewish children there and will also tell them interesting incidents of the Jewish worker's life in that country.

This meeting will take place at the Workmen's Circle Lyceum. The children will sing a few revolutionary songs and there will be speeches.

Admission for the parents of the children and their friends is free.
