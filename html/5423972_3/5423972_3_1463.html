The first large Socialistic campaign meeting in the sixth Congressional District will be held tomorrow at 2 o'clock in Workers Labor Lyceum. Speakers who will address the men and women of the Lawndale district will be William B. Krause, candidate for Congress from the sixth Congressional District, George F. Franklin, candidate for United States Senator and Andrew Lopin, candidate for Governor. Mr. Levinson, the Jews from Missouri and S. P. Kramer, Managing Editor of the Forward and Morris Ziskin, Editor of the Forward, Will be present.

Therefore, we ask the citizens of the sixth Congressional District to come in masses and hear the Socialist candidates discuss the issues of the campaign.
