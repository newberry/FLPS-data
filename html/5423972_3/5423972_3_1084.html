The strike at the Congress Raincoat Co., 506 S. 5th Avenue, which was declared last Wednesday because the firm dismissed one worker and refused to recognize the union, ended with an agreement signed by the firm, and the reinstatement of all workers.

SHEAF WORKERS WIN STRIKE

The local sheaf workers, who have recently begun organizing, have already scored a victory over their bosses. The 125 workers of I. Irvin and Co., 1055. Division Street, who walked out on strike last Monday, demanding a one dollar raise, a nine day, and time a half for overtime, have returned to their work Friday after winning all demands. The strike was carried on with the aid of the Chicago Federation of Labor.
