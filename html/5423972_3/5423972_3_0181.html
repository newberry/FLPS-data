The Jewish Bakers, Local 237, announce that Shirowsky and Hurvitz, 3646 W. 15th St., have settled with the Union and the Union employees are, again, working under union conditions.

Julius Levinson, 14th and Loomis, settled with the union and signed a union agreement. His son proposed to become a member (union) in order to work in the Shop.

Both Shops use Union Labels.
