A large Mass Meeting of the Chicago-group of Social-Revolutionists will be held in honor of their comrade and great champion of socialistic ideas, Gregor Gershoni, and Comrade Katz of New York, Wednesday, January 9th, 1907, at 8 PM, in Orchestra Hall, 168 Michigan Avenue. An excellent musical-program has been arranged for this occasion.
