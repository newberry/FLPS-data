A complete report was listened to, at the last meeting of the Douglas Branch Socialist Party, concerning the plans to open large club rooms and a library and great center for all Socialist activities on the West Side among the masses of Jewish workers and Socialists.

Comrade L. Schaffer reported that the committee had already engaged the entire floor of the building on 22nd and Albany avenue, where the Workmen's Circle schools are located. The floor must be reconstructed to be suitable for club rooms. Plans are being made for constructing one large room for social gatherings, reading of books, journals and papers, playing chess, discussing current events, etc.

A library and other rooms, where meetings will be held, will be built.
