Ladies' Tailors Union - Local 104 declared a strike in Moshinas Shop - 1348 47th Street, because Moshina fired the shop Chairman. The shop chairman tried to obey the rules of the union and the boss did not like it. The union demanded that the boss reinstate the shop chairman and the boss refused. Yesterday the shop chairman passed by the shop and was speaking to workers who were asking for work. The boss became excited and started a fight with the chairman because the chairman told the workers that Moshinas Shop was on strike. Ladies' tailors who are looking for work are invited to call in the union.
