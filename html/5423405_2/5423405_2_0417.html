It is very important to observe and study from the social point of view, the mental unbalance and doubts caused to the minds of our people by campaigns undertaken from time to time by the different denominations, to win converts; but more interesting would be if it could be possible to obtain the statistics that might reveal more or less the number of human beings that, due to the same propaganda and to the erroneous methods used, get farther from all, with the painful doubt whether any of them are real and deserving to be proclaimed by them and defended at all costs, under all circumstances.The fault is not with those who lend shelter to doubt, but the blame must be placed on those who have brought on the doubt.
