We have been informed of the death of Mrs. Lucia V. de Aranda. She was the founder of the Sociedad Femenil Mexicana, and therefore, we lament her departure.

Sunday, March 31st, at 2:00 o'clock our secretary was informed of her sickness and immediately sent a representative to bid her farewell in the name of the society. We sympathize with Mr. Aranda in his bereavement. The members of our Society extend to him and to his family their most sincere condolences.
