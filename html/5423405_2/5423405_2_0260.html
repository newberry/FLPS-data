To-night at 8 P.M. the members of the Pan-American Club will meet to make plans for the social activities of the winter season.

The Pan-American Club has the pleasure of inviting you to attend this meeting. You will enjoy to associate with American people who are interested in the language and the customs of the Latins.

The Secretary of the Club, Mr. Manuel G. Garcia, will gladly furnish any information you may desire at the office in Room 300, 160 North LaSalle Street.
