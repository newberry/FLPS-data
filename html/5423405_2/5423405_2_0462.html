Beginning with the second Sunday of this month, the worship time in the church "El Buen Pastor" will be as follows:

Sunday School, from 7:30, till 8:30 A. M.

Predication Worship, from 8:30 till 9:30 A. M.

With this change of time during the summer we hope we shall have a large attendance.

Temple open at 7 A.M.
