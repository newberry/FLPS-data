A group of enthusiastic young boys has joined the Conference of San Vicente de Paul, of the San Francisco de As is Church, in order to organize a charity celebration. The aim of this celebration is to help the disabled Mexicans who are confined in the County Hospital. This festival will take place on the 16th of the present month, at 7:30 p.m. in the Salon de Actos of the San Francisco School.

The Colony is invited to attend.
