A great festival and dance is being organized by the Mutual Societies Miguel Hidalgo Y Costilla and Jesefa Ortiz de Dominguez. This event will take place Saturday July 18th at 8:00 P.M. in the large and comfortable Emporium Hall, 1010 South Halsted Street. The Societies are very pleased in presenting to the colony for the first time the magnificent orchestra Los Charros, who will delight the patrons with their excellent repertoire of tangos, rhumbas and Mexican music. We shall have the best of services, at the bar and restaurant. The admission for gentlemen is 35 cents, and 25 cents for ladies.
