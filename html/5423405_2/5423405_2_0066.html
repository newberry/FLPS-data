The Alianza Fraternal Mexicana has organized an atractive feast for tonight which will consist of an animated dance and refreshments to take place in the official residence of the society 808 W. Roosevelt Rd., at 7:30 p.m. and will continue until the last couple should depart. The admission will be to the convenience of every body, and the music will be in charge of one of the best known orchestras in the colony.
