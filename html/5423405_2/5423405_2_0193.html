The Spanish Society has planned a dance for Saturday, May 28th. This affair will be held in the dance hall of the Capitol Building, 159 North State Street. It is for the pleasure of the Society and its friends.

The dancers will be entertained by the Calencian Serenaders, who will play the type of music which will please them until 1 P. M.

It is felt that this affair will be of the type always promoted by this distinguished Society.
