September 5th is the anniversary of our society. In order to celebrate this our third year of existence we are giving a dance, and a selected program at the Steuben Club, 188 W. Randolph St., beginning at 8:30 P. M. We shall have two orchestras on that night, Los Cubanos, and the Royal Castillians, both of the orchestras are very famous. The admission is sixty five cents for ladies, and eighty five cents for gentlemen. Tax is included.
