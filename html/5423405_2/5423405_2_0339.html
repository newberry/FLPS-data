The Mexican Congregational Church of South Chicago, 9135 Brandon Avenue, will celebrate for the first time, under the supervision of Reverend Lineweaver, the Mexican holiday with a literary and musical program.

The following persons will take part in the program:

Dr. J. B. Medina, will be a speaker; Mrs. Yolanda Diaz de Rangel will recite poetry; Tenor James Paterson will sing the best of his repertory; Misses Brown and Miss Mary Bottigliero will play the piano and will sing respectively.

Some other persons have been invited to take part in this festival, which will be held September 16th, 7:30 P. M.,at the mentioned church.
