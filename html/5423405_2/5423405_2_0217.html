The Ideal Club of Chicago will carry through, a spring festival, today at 8:30 P. M. in The Debs Hall, Ashland and Van Buren, for to honor the ladies of the Mexican colony.

The dance will be a competitive contest between dancers, for dances and flowers; but the greatest event will be the election for a queen of May, who will be crowned at once.

The Ideal Club invites the Mexican colony to assist to this social feast.
