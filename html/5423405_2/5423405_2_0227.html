Last Monday, Mr. Bernardo Pastor and Salvador Trejo, both members of the Propaganda and Investigation Commission respectively, of the Miguel Hidalgo y Costilla Society, came to visit our Editing Department. Mr. Pastor, told us among other things that this renowned society will hold a very important meeting in which they shall discuss a plan for the society's future activities.

The Miguel Hidalgo y Costilla counts with a large membership and is one of the most popular organizations in Chicago.
