Herein we publish an article by Mr. Ignacio Valle, well known merchant of our colony. The substance of the article is of extreme interest to all Mexicans residing in Chicago and, in general, to all those who live in the United States. This office wholly adheres to the proposition made by the author of the article because of its great transcendence to the Mexican people and because some day we shall demonstrate to foreign countries that we know how to be united and form part of the civilized communities to which we have a right to belong.
