As we have mentioned repeatedly in other editions, a grand benefit ball, will take place to-day at the Chicago University Settlement House. This dance has been organized by the Cultural and Necaka Clubs of this city, which will also give a [gap]festival at the same location, in which the best of our Mexican artists will take part.

These two evenings of entertainment are the results of preparations over a longs period of time. Indeed, the organization [gap]determined, to omit no detail which would detract in importance from this benefit, given for the Chicago University Settlement.
