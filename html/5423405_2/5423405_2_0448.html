The Methodists Ladies Society, from the church of the Good Shepherd, gave a social function, in one of the halls of Mercy Center, in honor of the Methodist Bishop, Reverend Juan N. Pascoe, of the Mexican Republic.

The Bishop presented a beautiful literary program and sang a beautiful spiritual composition, entitled "El Lugar Para La Mujer".

Among the distinguished persons present at this social affair, were the wife of the pastor of the church and of Reverend William B. O'Neill.

The colony is grateful for the social work which these respectable ladies are doing.
