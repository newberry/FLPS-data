Last week the Atlas Sport Club incorporated a new basket ball team, headed by the young sportsman Mr. Angel Soto, who will act as manager, and captained by agustin Mendoza. The team will make its debut to-day at 7 p.m. in the Principal Gymnasium of Hull House, 800 South Halsted Street. There will be a dance at the Cuahtemoc Athletic Club after the game.

This affair will be attended by Mr. Rafael Aveleyra, Consul of Mexico, and the high fuctionaries of Hull House as invited guests of honor.
