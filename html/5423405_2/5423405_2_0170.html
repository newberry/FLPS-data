On the 12th of this month the Club Latino-Americano met at 4630 Gross Avenue, and elected new directors for the next fiscal year. Those elected were as follows: J. Jesus Flores, President; Cornello, Vice-President; Abraham Gomez, Secretary; Mrs. Lupe Plaza, Assistant Secretary; Mr. Santiago, Treasurer; Maria Louisa Garilay, Assistant Treasurer; Fidel Paramo, First Voter; Rosario A. de Flores, Second Voter.

The Club is to give an initiation affair which will be the beginning of a series of festivals which it will offer to its members and the general public.
