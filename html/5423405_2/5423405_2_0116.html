The members of this society announce to the honorable Mexican colony in South Chicago, that their first event, an artistic dance, will be given on April 22nd.

They will celebrate the glorious day of 5 De Mayo; (the 5th of May) and, for this purpose many talented artists will be engaged.

We shall inform you shortly where this festival will be performed.

You may await more details and the program "Club Plutarco Elias Calles",

by: Leopoldo Escobarete
