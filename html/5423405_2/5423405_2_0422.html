One hundred and fifty delegates, representing the Mexican Baptist Church from the Middle-West, are attending the Convention. The convention is taking place in the Mexican Baptist Church at 4658 Gross Avenue from April 18 to 22, 1934. The program is as follows: April 18, 7:30 P.M. Devotional Service, conducted by Lucio P. Avila. At 7:45 special music by Century of Progress orchestra 8:00 P.M. Opening sermon by Rev. Ramon Tolsa. April 19, at 9:00 A.M. registration of delegates, Ministerial Conference presided by Rev. Frank Martinez and a theme in charge of Dr. R. C. Schell. 10:00 A. M. special music and singing. 10:10 A. M. Government of the Church by Dr. J. E. Davies. 10:40 A. M. Business meeting. 11:00 A. M. Pauline Ecclesiology by Dr. Carlos L. Detwieler. At 2:00 P. M. A program by the Feminine Association. 7:00 P. M. Service of Praise by Rev. Reinaldo Olivares. 7:15 P. M. Chorus by brothers from Indiana Harbor, Ind. 7:30 P. M. Introduction of visitors. 8:00 P. M. Mexico Today, by Senor Carlos Palacios Roji, Consul of Mexico.
