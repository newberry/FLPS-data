The Mexican National festivities of September 15th, will be celebrated next September 15th and 16th, at Pilsen Park, which is the most convenient place with its large auditorium well ventilated for the civic ceremony, and spacious gardens.

The official program will be very interesting and there will be besides a great parade of Mexicans girls who will wear Mexican regional costumes, and will sing vernacular songs.

There will be also dances on the stage. The program will end with a dance.
