The Societies "Miguel Hidalgo," "Josefa Ortiz de Dominguez," with the Cooperation of Local No. 36 of the Unemployed Workers Alliance will present a literary and musical program which will be presented in the Bowen Hall of the Hull House situated on Halsted and Polk Streets. The program consists of ten numbers and is to begin at eight in the evening. One of the best known Latin-American orchestras will be in charge of the music. The admission is absolutely free.
