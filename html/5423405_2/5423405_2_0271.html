Local 36 of the Illinois Workers Alliance has organized a dance for next Wednesday at the West End Women's Club, 37 Ashland Blvd. This splendid dance will begin at 7:30 p.m. and continue until 2:00 o'clock of the next morning. The music will be in charge of one of the most popular orchestras, and during this festival, a Turkey and one electric clock will be raffled. Tickets for gentlemen 40 cents and 20 cents for ladies.
