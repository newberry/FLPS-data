Much enthusiasm prevails in anticipation of the benefit dance which will be given by the Sociedad Femenil Mexicana of Chicago. This ball will be held in the West Side Auditorium and will commence at 8 p.m. to-night and last until 2 a.m. to-morrow morning.

The purpose of the affair is to secure funds so that the Society may continue its aid to the poor.

The promoters of this function are doing everything possible to give to the public some real entertainment.
