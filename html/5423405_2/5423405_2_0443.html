The Mexican Social Center (Iglesia Methodista del Buen Pastor) which made it a practice to celebrate the September Feasts in former years, will again have a special affair September 18th, at 7:30 p.m. in the Halsted Institution Center, located at 1935 South Halsted Street.

The program includes great artists so as to make it a great success. The Honorable Mexican Consul will deliver the official address. Miss Mercedes Figueroa will play a violin number. The Juventino Ramas Orchestra will play native pieces and there will be other musical and literary numbers of much artistic value.

The Colony is invited to take part in this splendid function which will be headed by Reverend William B. O'Neill. There will be no admission charge.
