Saturday March 3, at 8:00 P. M. El Club Educative de Trabajadores (Educational Workers Club) will give a dance once more in their hall at 410 South Halsted Street, where the sympathetic "Rainbow Ramblers" will play the latest musical compositions. This Club wished and expect the cooperation of the colony in their exertion to obtain funds to defray the expenses of the organization among our working class which is an imperative necessity.
