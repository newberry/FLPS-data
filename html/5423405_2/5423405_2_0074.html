A dance was organized by the "Casino Latino-Americano", which took place in the reception room of the "Knickerbocker Hotel", last Saturday night. It was a complete success and an elegant social affair, as usual it was patronized by the best elements of the different Spanish speaking colonies in Chicago. The number of Americans who attended this festival was very considerable for the "Latin American Casino" has known how to introduce itself to the best elements of this Metropolis, where they count many sympathizers.
