A sumptuous dance will take place next Saturday, May 20th, at the West Side Auditorium, corner of Racine and Taylor Streets. The orchestra "Los Aztecas, will furnish the music and the dance will begin at 7:30 P.M. and will last until midnight.

The admission prices are low and in consequence we expect the public to take advantage of such an opportunity and to spend an agreeable night.

If additional information is desired, please consult our program already in circulation.
