A dance organized by The Mamerto Club, for the inauguration of the feminine social club "Venus", will be given today, and will also be the beginning of all celebrations for the 5th of May, which is a glorious day in Mexican history.

The Mamerto Club, according to its official bulletin, has invited the Mexican ladies so that they can also enjoy the dance in the company of the beautiful girls of the "Venus" club.

Mr. Escobarete, will sing "Elisa," musical composition by Mr. E. Medina, when the persons who form the new club appear.

The dance is absolutely free. All may assist, because this dance is not for financial purposes.
