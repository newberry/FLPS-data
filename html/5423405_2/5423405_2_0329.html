Next Sunday, September 27th, a musical and literary festival will be held at 1107 South Halsted Street, in order to celebrate the CXXI anniversary of the consummation of the Mexican Republic's independence.

This festival is prepared by the "Artistas Unidos Mexicanos",(Mexican United Artists,) with the cooperation of the Mexican colony. They have hired Professor F. Facio's orchestra, and Professor Justino Sanchez, pianist, for the purpose of making the act more impressive. Speeches will be made by the best orators in the colony.

The festivities will be concluded with dancing. The festival will start at 8:00 P. M. on the indicated date.
