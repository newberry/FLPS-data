Our distinguished Chicago aviator Senor Guillermo Jara, will pilot his own plane through the state of Illinois, and Indiana, as a patriotic manifestation in honor of the memory of the hero General Ignacio Zaragoza. Such act will be effected Sunday, May 3, at noon. In case of bad weather, the flight will take place May 5th, at the same hour. This flight has met with the unanimous approval of La Voz de Mexico, the Patriotic Committee, Pro-Mexico Society, Beniot Juarez of Indiana Harbor, and our sister societies from Gary, Indiana.
