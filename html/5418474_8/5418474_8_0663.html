The latest number (February edition) of Deutscher Vorkaempfer. published by L. Viereck, 1175 Broadway, New York, contains the famous German speech of Professor Burgess and also an article of Dr. P. Ilgen about Prohibition.

We recommend to all Chicago Germans to obtain the February copy of the said edition, which also has interesting novels, written by German authors, living in this country. The price is $1.00 per copy.
