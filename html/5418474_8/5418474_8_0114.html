The festive procession then, proceeded to the Picnic Grounds, where a lively entertainment soon developed.

In the afternoon Ex-Alderman Sommer, Dr. Busch and William Eschenburg addressed the excursionists in Low German.

The rest of the program consisted of Concert, Dance and a Peasant marriage.
