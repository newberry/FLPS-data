Before an audience of 15,000 people the 2500 German Turners, who came from their League games in Milwaukee, showed what they can do. The exercises were led by George Brosius of Milwaukee and the seats of the Arena were filled to the last place. After the grey uniformed, muscular turners, accompanied by about 100 flags paraded through the fair grounds, the exercises took place in model orderliness admiest the cheers of the public and were closed with the singing of the beautiful song: "Rally Around The Flag."
