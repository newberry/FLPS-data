The founders and directors of the German-American Medical College have also acquired the right to train midwives.

For this purpose, a special department for this college has been established. Also the necessary reservation of rooms has been made, to make the treatment and care of incoming patients possible.

One of the College directors, Dr. Hagenow, will be the leading practical instructor of the nurses.

New applications from German girls, anxious to become nurses and midwives, are accepted in the office of the College.
