With $12,000 of city treasury money, a number of aldermen bought the support of the noble Mike, Neue Freie Presse, (New Free Press).

The working class will keep this transaction in mind, and will remember this selling out by the paper when and where ever it is going to canvass for votes.

As some city officials, among then the city treasurer, are stockholders of the Printing plant of this paper, we cannot concede them the right to accept municipal contracts. But is not the purpose of laws to be circumvented by sharpers.
