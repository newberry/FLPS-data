Mr. Adolf Kratz is the announcer and manager of the German entertainment programmes over station W C F L, which can be heard daily at 9 o'clock in the morning.

Mr. Kratz is well known in Chicago and in German circles, especially in society circles; he is also well known as an experienced manager of festivals and other entertainments where good music, singing, cheerfulness and German cordiality prevail.

He is a member of local German Freemason lodges; also of Commandery & Shrine, of Turner, Singing, Philatelic, and other societies.

Mr. Kratz was the manager of the German house in Detroit, manager of the Turner Summer Home in Lake St. Clair, manager of the Birmingham Golf club, and finally also manager of the German House in San Francisco.
