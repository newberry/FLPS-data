An Association has been founded under the above name, Saturday afternoon at 182 Evergreen Avenue, with the object of organizing and developing the principles and teachings of the teacher Froebel. The propaganda will chiefly consist of sending out literature and establishing kindergardens in different parts of Chicago.

A free kindergarden for the Northside has been established in 793 N. Robey Street. The next meeting of the Association will take place on March 7th at 2:30 in the afternoon at 793 Robey Street.
