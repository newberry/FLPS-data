The German Press Club had a festival arranged in honor of the ladies and the invited members and friends came in countless numbers.

This fact must be considered as an evidence that the better educated "Germans" understand and appreciate what the club is trying to do.....

The concert started at 8 o'clock. After music and song followed the comedy, "I Married My Daughter." This play was immensely enjoyed by the guests and there was no end to the applause.

After this entertainment, the guests participated at the banquet and the dance.
