The "Aurora Turnverein" held its regular intellectual meeting yesterday evening. The program was very copious and consisted of Zither productions of Messrs Wm. Krause and Chas Kobelli, declamations by Messrs. Knocke and Rosenow, also a lecture of Mr. Waterstaat, same was read from a book of Max Nordau "Conventional Lies". It was also resolved, to install a reading room and clubroom.
