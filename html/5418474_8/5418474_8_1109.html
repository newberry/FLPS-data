Under the presidency of Comrade Fischer, the Club Jefferson held its regular Agitation meeting yesterday. A lively discussion of Comrade Fehlings' lecture, which he held day before yesterday, ensued, in which Comrades Meeske, Blum, Hirsch, Fehling and Fischer participated.

After this they proceeded to finish the reports submitted. It was resolved, to hold a Picnic in cooperation with the Northwest side club, in the near future, and for this purpose, a Committee was appointed to get in touch with the aforesaid club. Furthermore it was resolved, to procure agitation pamphlets and distribute them gratuitously, also to subscribe to three copies of the "Freihet."
