P. 5 - The "Baers" of the local turner societies met at the Aurora Turner Hall yesterday, to give proof of their excellent co-operation, as it will be exhibited at the convention to be held in Phildelphia. The program consisted of dumb-bell exercises, performed by four groups. Mr. Dreisel, the turner teacher from the Aurora Turner Society, and Mr. Burger of the Social Turn Society were in charge of last night's exercises. Mr. Burger was chosen to direct the turner exhibitions in Philadelphia....

Practically all the turner societies were represented at last night's exhibition of the "Baers"....
