The Socialistic Club "Vorwarts" held last night its regular meeting at the Samonia House, 120 Wells Street. The members were present in large numbers. After Comrade Wehner's election, Comrade Heinemann, Sr., made a speech about "Cholera and Epidemic Diseases in General" in which, starting from the present epidemic in Egypt he discussed the causes, symptoms and consequences of the cholera as well as the means of prevention.

The explanation was listened to with great interest...
