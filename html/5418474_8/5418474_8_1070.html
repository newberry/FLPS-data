Professor Camillo von Klenze, head of the German Department of the University of Chicago, criticized in a lecture, which he gave yesterday, before students of the summer school, the method of teaching at American Universities which, in his opinion, interfere with the field that should be left exclusively to Colleges. The duty of these teaching institutions is to teach facts and general knowledge, while it is the concern of the Universities to further scientific research.
