The German-American Medical college opened its winter session last night. The president of the faculty, Dr. Carlstein, introduced the speaker for the occasion, the Rev. Lambrecht. The audience was pleasantly entertained by song and music, and the significance of the evening was pointed out by speeches of several professors. The institution starts with the best of prospects. The number of students enrolled is beyond expectation. The professors of the faculty are men who know the importance of their calling and who are capable to meet their responsibilities.
