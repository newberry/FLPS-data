As shown by the last annual report submitted to the Secretary of State in Springfield in accordance with regulations the Grand Lodge of the Hermann Sisters had, at the end of the year, a fortune of $74,615.56 which was invested as follows: Real estate property, $11,500.00, mortgages $61,950.00, cash in banks, $1,165.56.

During the year reported the income from assessments, contributions, and dues from certificates was $11,963.77, from interests and other sources, $3,218.68. Thus the total income is given as $15,182.45. Against this income stood expenditures totalling $20,884.43. Included in this sum is $14,333.32 which was spent for funeral services.
