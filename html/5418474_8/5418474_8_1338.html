P. 1.. After Tuesday's festival of the German Allied Gymnasts which proved such a great success at Milwaukee, about 3,000 of its active members travelled to Chicago in three special trains. Upon arrival they formed into 12 divisions under the leadership of Mr. John Koelling of the Chicago Turner District.

The huge parade started on Market Street, between Randolph and Washington. The route of this veritable army brought the assembly to its final destination, the Exposition Grounds...
