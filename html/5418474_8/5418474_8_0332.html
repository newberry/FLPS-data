The union of action of the German organizations of the South Side maintains a German lending (circulating) library, in the South Side Ballroom, which is accessible to every one. Every German who wants to read a book in the long winter nights should make use of the library. The library was assembled by the communicating office for German libraries in foreign lands and comprises today 1500 volumes (novels, books on travel, classics, books on the World War, German history, and the New Germany).

The books are let out every Friday night from 7 to 9 P.M. to everybody who leaves a small deposit.
