The German Society of Northwestern University in Evanston gave last Monday evening an entertainment, dedicated exclusively to German singing and German literature. The well arranged program was opened by a chorus song. Then followed several fine song recitals by German students of the University.

Finally Mrs. Amalie von Ende gave a lecture on all literary achievements of Annette von Huelshoff, the noted German poetess. The entertainment closed with a jolly dance of the younger folks.
