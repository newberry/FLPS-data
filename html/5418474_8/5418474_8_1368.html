The National Athletic Club will start its winter semester of training with a well-prepared entertainment Monday night at the Apollo Hall, 260-262 Blue Island Avenue. The Gymnasium, under the supervision of the capable and untiring teacher of athletics, Fritz Krinnel, has attained an excellent reputation since the club was organized. Parents living in the neighborhood of this well-ventilated, and well-equipped gymnasium should make the advantages of a physical training by practical gymnastics, available to their children. Applications are accepted by the teacher in the hall.
