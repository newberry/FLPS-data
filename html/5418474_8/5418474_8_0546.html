The German Press Club of Chicago plans to establish a comfortable home. It has entered negotiations for this purpose with the management of the Schiller Theater Building, and it wishes to rent the 13th story of this building, which is already arranged for such purposes. The German Press Club has decided to increase its membership by admitting special members, which will enable it to carry out its plan....

According to the revised statutes, the club elects a manager annually, who takes care of its financial affairs. The manager is elected from among the members of the Board of Directors, consisting of six regular and nine special members....
