The best-known representative of the Germans in the United States, Carl Schurz, will be honored by the School Council of the City of Chicago in a worthy manner. One of the High Schools of the City will receive his name. Following a motion of the President of the School Board, Otto Schneider, the School Board Committee for Real Estate resolved yesterday to name the new Irving Park High School, Carl Schurz High School.

The motion was accepted unanimously, and also in the School Board there is no opposition expected.
