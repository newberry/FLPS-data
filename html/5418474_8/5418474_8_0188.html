The old settlers had a successful picnic yesterday in Wright's Grove. Prizes were won by: William Lobbeke, 1833, as oldest settler; as oldest married couple, John Weekler and wife, nee Berg, 1841; Leo Mayer, as oldest American born son of German parents, Mathias and Blondine Mayer, 1834; as oldest American born daughter, Johanna Peters, daughter of Franz and Appollania Clark, 1839; as oldest singer, Christian Kappes, 1839, now and Orpheus member; as oldest marksman, Louis Wagner, 1862; as oldest turner, Albert Bose, 1854.
