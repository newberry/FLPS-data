Lively attendance prevailed at the Schuetzenpark (Marksmen's Park) in spite of bad weather. Of course, the crowd--in as far as visitors were concerned--was comparatively small, but the out-of-town marksmen, as well as the local contingent, were participating eagerly, and rivalry was keen.

Rifle teams--consisting of six members of each club--were given a special target. Their performances were indeed exciting. The results were as follows:
