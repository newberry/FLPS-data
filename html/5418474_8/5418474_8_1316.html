Before the Club Lincoln and its friends, the instructor in Gymnastics, Mr. Meyer took the yearly fall-examination of the different Turn-Classes under his supervision.

The examination was passed splendidly according to the unanimous judgment of all experts attending. The cleverly arranged program offered to all Turners-male and female, young and old, the best occasion to show their abilities and the that followed each number was well earned.

A pleasant dance entertainment followed the official program.
