Friends of the "German" poet, Reuter, assembled last night at the "Old Quincy." It was reported that the collected sum for the Reuter monument had increased considerably. The total amount collected is $2272.00 and another $800.00 had been pledged.

It was also announced at the assembly that, starting tomorrow, the western issue of the "New York" Platt deutchen Post" (Low German Post) will make its appearance here. This newspaper will effectively promote the project for the erection of the monument.
