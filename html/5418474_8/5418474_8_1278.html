The annual swimming contest arranged by the Illinois Athletic Club was held this afternoon. There were sixty-eight contestants, many of whom were of national repute. The mouth of the Chicago river was the starting point, ending at the Jackson Boulevard bridge.

The winner of this swimming contest was J. Stener, a non-member of the club. He reached the point of destination in forty-eight minutes; the second winner in the race was T. W. Ninans, both of whom out-distanced E. Heath, from the Missouri Athletic Club of St. Louis, who despite being the favorite, came in fifth.
