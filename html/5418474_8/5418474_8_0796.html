The Frobel's Kindergarten Society held a general meeting yesterday at the Lincoln Turnhalle, and elected the following new officers:

President, Mr. Beyersdorf,

Vice-President, Dr. G. F. Boucsein,

Treasurer, H. Engholm,

Corresponding secretary, J. Sommer,

Kindergartener, Mrs. R. Schroeder and Miss T. Sommers.

It was decided to take in twenty children of poor but respectable parents free of charge. A special committee will be in charge of this. It was also arranged to receive passive members into the society with an annual fee of $3.00. It was also agreed upon to publish an appeal for funds in all German newspapers.
