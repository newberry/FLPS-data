(The Gymnastic school of the club "Clear the way"--right of way.)

The above school will be inaugurated on Saturday, Jan. 11th, at the Turnhall, 723-25 Ogden Avenue, near 12th Street. Instruction to girls and boys under the direction of Wilhelm Zoeller and Otto Tromsdorff. Fee: 75 cents quarterly, each child. Regular classes for all members in active standing.
