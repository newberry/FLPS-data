The Minerva Institution, the well known school for girls of Mrs. Amalie Ende in Ravenswood, starts on September 5th, a new school year. Girls over 6 years will be admitted. The Institution includes all elementary and secondary classes, education equally in the German and English languages (French, if desired), declamation, singing, gymnastics, drawing, painting and female handiwork.

By the more advanced pupils special attention is paid to history, literature and written expression of thoughts.
