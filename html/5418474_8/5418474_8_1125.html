The Group "Einigkeit" held a well attended Agitation Meeting, at which several women also took part. Comrade Kiesling presided. The discourse about "Equal Rights" by Comrade Fehling was greatly acclaimed. Several new members were admitted, after which the meeting was adjourned till the next Agitation Meeting, May 5th.

Order of the day: lecture by Comrade Henley on "The Religion of the Christians" which promises to be very interesting. The comrades are requested to appear in large numbers. Bring your wives and friends with you.

New members will be admitted. Everybody is welcome, no admission charged.
