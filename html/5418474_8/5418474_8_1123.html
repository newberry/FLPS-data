The Group "Bridgeport" held their regular business meeting last Wednesday. Comrade Hacker presided. Among other business matters, there was an accounting of the receipts and expenditures of the last evening entertainment. Part of the net proceeds was used for the founding the of a library and the balance given to the clubs fund. The committee, which undertook to arrange the peasant affair was discharged and it was decided to express thanks to the Southwestside Clubs, Men's Club, Comrades Kroening and Pollmaker, Mrs. Sellhorn and Mr. Becker for their kind participation.

After winding up several smaller business matters, the meeting was adjourned.
