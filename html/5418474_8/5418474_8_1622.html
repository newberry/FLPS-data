The Pastorius Monument is to be erected at Vernon Park, Germantown, Philadelphia, near the soot, where the first ground was broken for the first German settlement in the United States. It is to be the German American National Monument. Congress, at its next meeting, will grant $30,000 for the monument, provided the German-Americans will raise a similar sum, if not, then---

The raising of this fund has become a matter of honor for the German-Americans. What will the large and strong German population of Chicago contribute?

Received until yesterday------$598.75. The Chicago Abendpost gave $500 at the beginning of the drive.
