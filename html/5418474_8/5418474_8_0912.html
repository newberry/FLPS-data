Under the auspices of the German department of Northwestern University, German lectures are held every fourth Tuesday of the month, at the Harris Hall of the University, Sheridan Rd. and Chicago Ave, Evanston.

The next lecture will be held Apr. 26th, by Mr. A.J.K. Zieglschund. The subject will be: Franz Schubert. The necessary musical illustrations accompanying the lecture will be given by Prof. W.A. Stults, and songs will be rendered by Miss Anne Marie Gerts. The accompaniment will be taken care of by Prof. L.N. Dodge.

A film presentation illustrating Franz Schubert's life is connected with the lecture.
