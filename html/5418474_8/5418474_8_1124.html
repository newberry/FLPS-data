The Socialist Group "Vorwarts" held its monthly business meeting last night at 130 Wells Street. Comrade Nememan presided and in the absence of the Recording Secretary, Beron, Comrade Altherr took his place. As the expected speaker did not appear, business matters were discussed.

As delegates to the General Committee, comrades Perschke and Grottau were elected was librarians, Comrade Knieser as Comptroller, Comrade Altherr, the Financial Secfetary made his report of the cash conditions, which was accepted. $5.00 was placed at the new Librarian's disposition, and he was instanced to order 25 copies of "The Nemesis", 2 copies of the "San Francisco Truth," I cop of the "Freiheit" and 1 copy of "The Social Democrat." Comrade Greb reported on the last two meetings of the "Seneral Committee, which was followed by a length debate. An invitation of the Southside Group to a festival was received and accepted. Next Wednesday, May 7th, Comrade Grottkau will lecture.
