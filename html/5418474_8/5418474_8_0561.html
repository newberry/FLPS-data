The "German Press Club" held its first general assembly yesterday. President Janssen read the annual report, which mentioned above all the steady increase and the splendid success of the club. Emphasis was laid on the fact that a more friendly relationship among the members of the club now existed, which no doubt would be of social and material advantage to the. The Financial Secretary reported a cash balance of $918.00. There are fifty-eight ordinary and eighty-one extraordinary members of the club.
