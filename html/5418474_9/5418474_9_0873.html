Under the leadership of President Eberhardt, the above society held its monthly meeting. Agent W. A. Thielepape gave his monthly report.

Support was given to 39 families with 120 children and 31 single persons, aggregating $174.25 cash, $28.50 for coal, $20.88 food and lodging, total $223.63.

Besides, work was found for 110 laborers, through the society's efforts. Collection of yearly contribution dues, $268.50. A list of people and brewers who voluntarily increased their contributions for the following year was appended.
