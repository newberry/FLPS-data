The building located at 1118 Lake Street, containing four apartments and six stores, and mortgaged for $17,000, was traded by Hermann Schmidt to Margaret Evans for the two-story frame building located at 1837 Fremont Street.

The twenty-four-apartment building located at 806-16 Montrose Boulevard, and mortgaged for $40,000, was bought by Oscar C. Hagen from Caroline D. Ordway for $95,000.

The six-apartment building located at 6133 Woodlawn Avenue, and mortgaged for $12,000, was sold by Edward M. Bertha to Linus C. Roder for $20,000.
