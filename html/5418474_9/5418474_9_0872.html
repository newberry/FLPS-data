The administration board of the German Society gathered this afternoon for its regular monthly meeting. According to the report read, 28 families with 65 children and 12 single adults received help during last month, amounting to a total expense of $144.67.

Furthermore 314 persons were put to work.

The Society recieved $436.00 for dues last month and a gift of $4.00. The following gentlemen were accepted as new members: John Schlenker, Ch. Bohlans, E. Hecht.
