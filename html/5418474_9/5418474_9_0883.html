The German Society has granted relief and assistance to 14 families, with 32 children, and 10 single persons during the past month...

The society has also succeeded in placing 250 unemployed, thereby saving a total amount of $500.00 which the unemployed, would have been forced to pay to employment agents.

The total of $65.20 was spent during the month for relief; cash $55.25; for coal $2.75 and for meals $7.20

Members contributed in fees $462.00 during the month and as new members were added. Messrs. Foreman Bros. and Mr. Ch. von Helmolt made annual contributions of $10.00.
