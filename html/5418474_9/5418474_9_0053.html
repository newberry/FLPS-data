The annual meeting and election of officials of the Schiller Women's Club took place last night at the "Northside Turnhalle". The president of the club Mrs. Knaus, gave a report about the club's activities which was excellent in every respect. Other officials made reports of their respective activities. Then the election took place with the following results:

The club has at this time 108 members and a capital of $1,475.00. The amount of insurance paid in case of death of a member was increased from $75.00 to $100.00. In case of sickness a member receives a weekly benefit of $4.00.
