The German Hospital Society held its regular general meeting last night, presided over by Mr. A. Henning. The Society has now 506 members and took in during the year $23073.22, whereas the total expenses amounted to $22,722.19. The Hospital took care of 665 patients during the same period, while 1437 cases were treated in the Hotel Dispensary. Of the patient cases, 477 were Germans and German-Americans, who seem to favor the German Hospital.

The following new officers were elected towards the end of the meeting;

Adolf Sturm, 1st Director,

D. Bakenhus, 2nd Director,

J. Hormann, Secretary,

Henry Metzger, Treasurer,

John Koenig, Adviser,

Whereupon the meeting adjourned.
