The German Society of Chicago gives free Advice, information, and aid to all immigrants from Germany, Alsace-Dorraine, Austria, and Switzerland.

Employers are requested to notify the Society of vacancies.

Office location: 51 and 53 South La Salle Street. Office hours: Daily excepting Sundays, from 9 to 12 A. M. and from 1 P. M. to....[Translator's note: number obliterated]. Max Eberhardt, secretary. Office in the Teutonia Building; Chas. Endres, agent.
