The property located on the west side of Dominick Street, 700 feet south of Webster Avenue, was sold by the Greenebaum Sons Bank and Trust Company to the Gerber Gutmann and Company for $50,000.

The unimproved property located on the west side of Green Street, south of Fulton Street, and adjacent to the alley, was sold by Bernard A. Eckhart to Carl D. Robinson for $20,300. The latter will erect a commercial building on the plot.
