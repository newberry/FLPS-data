Mr. F. H. Henning, in the name of the directors of the German Hospital, is expressing herewith his deepest, sincere gratitude for the check of $826.00 received from the Committee of the "Humor"Bowling Club, Messrs.Wm. Swissler, Jacob Birk and John Eisner.

The amount of $826.00 resulted from the profits, occasioned by the recent Bowling contest of the said club.
