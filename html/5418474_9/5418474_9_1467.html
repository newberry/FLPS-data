Anton Wille bought the unimproved property located on Sherwin Avenue, 401 feet east of Sheridan Road, from Mr. and Mrs. L. O. M. Taylor for $30,000. He will erect a thirty-six apartment building on the site at a cost of between $150,000 and $200,000.

George Stange bought the apartment building located on the south side of Margate Terrace, 394 feet east of Sheridan Road, from R. Guenther for $31,000.
