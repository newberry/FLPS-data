To work 50 years in peace and love is admirably. The association of the Uhlichs Orphans Home was able to celebrate this rare, beautiful festival. In the spacious institute are stationed at the present time, ninety children, at the ages of 3 to 18 years. Young people who outgrew the institution, but consider it still as their home, are coming back for a visit from time to time.

The association was established in 1866 by a loving woman, who considered it her duty to sew for needy children. Out of the purpose originated two years later, the orphans home, an association consisting now of 250 members. The Board of Directors, and the members of the association are prominent German-Americans of Chicago.
