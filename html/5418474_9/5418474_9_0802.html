According to the report of Mr. Charles Spaeth, business manager of the German Society, 124 families with 407 children and 232 single persons were helped during last month, with a total expense of $800.45.

Furthermore 125 persons received meals and rooms, 25 persons were given shoes and 8 families received coal. The Society collected $200.00 in monthly dues from members, but did not receive any donations recently.

There were 141 jobs, mostly on farms, available for applicants. The demand for factory help was very weak.
