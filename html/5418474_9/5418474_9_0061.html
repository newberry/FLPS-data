Members for the Order "Germania". This organization existed in the East for 21 years. It has a membership of 6000 and a Cash balance of $30,000.00.- It pays from $250.00 to $1000.00.- in case of death and $6.00 per week during sickness besides free medical treatments....Men and women between 18-50 years of age irrespective of religion and race are accepted as members.
