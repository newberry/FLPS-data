The Adolph Gohl Pickle Company has purchased the new three-story commercial building, located on the southeast corner of Belmont Avenue and Herndon Street, mortgaged for $22,500, from Frank W. Sheppard for $62,500. The building lot is 58 by 125 feet.

John A. Lundstrom, a contractor, purchased the 100 by 125 foot lot, located on the south side of Balmoral Avenue, 335 feet east of North Clark Street, for $85.00 per front foot from J. Krohn.
