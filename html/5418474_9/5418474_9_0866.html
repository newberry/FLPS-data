The monthly meeting of the "German Society" took place yesterday. Mr. Theilepape read his last month's report and stated, that, fifty two families, one hundred and five children and thirty six single persons received help amounting to $285.80.

Also one hundred and sixty applicants found employment through this Society. They had to elect a new agent in place of Mr. Thielepape as he is retiring next week. The nomination of the new agent will take place next Tuesday at 5 P.M. by special appointment.
