The two-story commercial and residence building located at 3312 West Twelfth Street was sold by Samuel Maureme to Bessie Greenberg for $15,000.

The property located on the north side of Twelfth Street, 307 feet east of Spaulding Avenue, and mortgaged for $7,500, was sold by Samuel I. Greenspan to Jakob Shapiro for $23,500.

The residence at 5330 Dorchester Avenue was sold by Burt O. Baker to William F. Krahl for $10,000.
