It could not be over-looked that the administration of the Relief and Aid Society was almost exclusively in American hands while three-fourths of the fire victims were Germans a large number of whom were not able to speak English.

The destroyed North Side formed the center of Germandom in Chicago. Here the oldest and strongest Turn, singing and shooting associations had their home. In many streets Germans lived, house by house, and one heard no talk but German. It was our task to assist these people with aid and advice.......
