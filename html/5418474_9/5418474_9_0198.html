The German Hospital, 754 Larrabee Street, held its regular director's meeting last night in the Hospital office. During the months July, August, September, 126 patients were admitted, while during the same period 813 persons were treated in the dispensary.

According to the same report, the German hospital took in $4341.71 against total expenses of $4710.34 for the three months. No donations to the German Hospital were made recently.
