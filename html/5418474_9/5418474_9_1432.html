Henry C. W. Schrage sold the six-flat apartment building at 1554 East 66th Place to Louis C. La Belle for $30,000.

Milton H. Callner bought the one story building at the northeast corner of Irving Park Boulevard and Central Park Avenue, and the ground on which the building stands, for $350,000. Estelle Heller received $350,000 for the property, it is said. The land is encumbered by a $108,000 mortgage. The old building is to be remodeled and will contain 13 stores.
