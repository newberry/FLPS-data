The Hamburg Club is holding a concert, for the benefit of the distressed in Hamburg, at the Northside Gymnastic Hall, Thursday, October 13th, 1892, at 8 o'clock P. M. The concert will be given by 35 of the best musicians of the Hugo Weege Orchestra with the assistance of a Section of the Singing Club of the gymnastic association, and of the Singing Club, "Vorwaerts."

We want to point out, that the Northside Gymnastic Club and music director Weege, have met us in a most charming manner. The gymnastic Club has loaned us their locality for the evening free of charge and Music Director Weege has given us the use of his famous Orchestra likewise without cost.

We appeal again to the charity of our patriotic clubs and citizens, to support us as much as possible.

Hamburger Club.
