At the annual meeting of the officers of the German Old People's Home its President, Mr. Koelling, gave his annual report. He complains in the report that the Germans of this city manifest very little interest in this institution and stressed the necessity of better financial support.

The interest bearing endowment amounts to $80,000.- At this time, and the interest is not sufficient to take care of one hundred inmates. Contributions during the year amounted to $1766.- exclusive of the cost of collecting.

A number of men were elected to the Board of Directors.
