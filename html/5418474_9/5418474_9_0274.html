(Organized in 1868 because of distress arising from Civil War; one of Chicago's first children's homes; founders and administrators were of St. Paul's including Pastor Hartmann, Conrad Fuerst, Wm.Knoke, John Baur, Louis Haas, J.H.Muhkle.)

After the fire had consumed the temporary building occupied by the children, a new and spacious Home was erected at Burling and Center Streets and occupied by the dependent children until the year 1928.
