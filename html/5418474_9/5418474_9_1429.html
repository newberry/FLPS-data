John Zimmermann and Ignatz Mente bought the plot of ground at 451 E. 83rd Street from David L. Sutton, for $12,500. They intend to build a three-story apartment house in the near future. The lot is 50' x 104'.

Max Clamitz, tailor, deeded the plot on the northwest corner of Madison Street and Central Avenue to the Austin Merchants Building Corporation. The property was encumbered by a $200,000 mortgage. The size of the ground is 224 x 160 feet.
