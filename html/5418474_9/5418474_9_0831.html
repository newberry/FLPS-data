Presided over by Mr. Max Eberhardt the regular monthly meeting of the administration of the German Benevolent Society took place. Mr. Oscar Kuehne read the report about the activity of the administration in the month of April. Twenty eight families with eighty six children and seventeen single persons have found support. The majority of applicants were widows with children and such, who have become needy on account of illness. The amount of $776.15 has been spent for support. In eight cases Board and lodging was given and in three cases shoes have been provided.

The demand for workmen was rather good, Three hundred seventeen persons found employment.
