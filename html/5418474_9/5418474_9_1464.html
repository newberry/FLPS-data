The commercial building located on the west side of Clark Street, 297 feet north of Division Street, was sold by Joseph Mayer to the Federal Fireproof Storage Company for $55,000.

The building located on the southwest corner of Grand Boulevard and 38th Street, and mortgaged for $22,000, was sold by Mrs. Anna Morrill, of Milwaukee, to Mrs. Elizabeth Schramm for $40,000.

The apartment building located at 1226-30 Hyde Park Boulevard, and mortgaged for $63,500, was sold by Gustav Anders, contractor, to Louis Geyler for $102,000.
