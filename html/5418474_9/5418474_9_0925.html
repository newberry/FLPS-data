Although many people will regard last Friday's concert at McCormick Hall as a failure because of the scant attendance, there are others, experts in such matters, who say that the hall would have been overcrowded if the weather had been cooler. And these "gentlemen who know" did not believe that the purpose of the concert, the benefit angle, was the sole drawing card; to the contrary, they were of the opinion that the featuring of the simpler German songs met with great popular approval.....[Translator's note: Here follows a dissertation advocating popular German songs instead of classical selections--several long paragraphs.
