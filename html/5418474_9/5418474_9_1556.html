Mrs. Auguste Lehmann increased her State Street holdings by purchasing the five-story building at 118 South State Street from Benjamin Rosenthal for an undisclosed amount. The building is valued at $17,650, and the plot at $336,430.

Dr. Herman A. White sold the three-story apartment building located on the southwest corner of Sheridan Road and Winona Street to H.C. Dovenmuehle for an undisclosed amount. The property is mortgaged for $63,000, and the selling price is said to be above $150,000. As part payment, the buyer deeded to the seller the two apartment buildings located at 610-616 Arlington Place and valued at $25,000.
