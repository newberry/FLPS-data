Nathan Glicksman, an attorney and theatrical producer of Milwaukee, purchased the 118 by 90 foot property situated at the Northwest corner of Sheridan Road and Byron Street from Julius Teich, the owner for and undisclosed price. The building consists of six stores and thirteen offices. The buyer assumed a $20,000 mortgage.

Herbert N. Rose purchased the 122 by 40 foot building lot, situated at the Southwest corner of Stone and Scott Streets, from August Kirchoff, at the price of $20,500.
