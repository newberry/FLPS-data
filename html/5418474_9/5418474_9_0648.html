The Welfare Committee for War Prisoners, through its chairman, Adolf Kuttroff, 24 North Moore Street, New York City, requests the Abendpost to open a depot where money and clothing for German prisoners of war and interned Germans can be deposited.

The Abendpost gladly complies with this wish and requests that all who wish to help bring their gifts, money or clothing, to our place of business, 223-225 West Washington Street. Day by day we will acknowledge contributions in the Abendpost and send the money and clothing to the Committee in New York at once.

This is a good and urgent cause. "A dollar today is worth ten tomorrow."
