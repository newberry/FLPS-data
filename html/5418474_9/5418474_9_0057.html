The well-known Song and Mutual Benefit Association "Harmony" held their seventh annual ball last Saturday at the Mesters Hall.

The climax of the evening was reached when the former Vice-President of the organization, Mr. P. Windbiel gave his splendid speech which came from his heart and went to the hearts of the hearers.

The financial conditions of the association is favorable and their membership is steadily increasing, having a total now of 85.
