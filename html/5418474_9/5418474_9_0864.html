The Swabian Society held their general meeting the night before last. Again they manifested their famous liberality by making the following donations: Goethe Monument Fund - $500.00, Reuter Monument Fund - $30.00, German Old People's Home - $50.00, German Hospital - $50.00, Uhlich's Orphanage - $50.00, Rose Hill Orphanage - $50.00, German Society - $75.00, total amount - $825.00.

The fund for the Goethe monument of the Swabian Society, managed by O. H. Wacker, the treasurer, now contains the sum of $1500.00.
