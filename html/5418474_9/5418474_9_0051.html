In August the German Benevolent Society gave aid to thirty-two families and eighteen single persons, and found employment for 435 applicants. The expense of the aid furnished was $195. At its monthly meeting yesterday the following new members were elected: F. L. Greenwald, Dr. W. Wyl, Joseph Mendelsohn, Dr. W. Bochert, W. H. Hafuer, Wm. Ganschow, Geo. M. Baumann, Sherer Fisher, F. C. Christy. Dr. F. A. Hunning, M. R. Mandelbauer, and Otto Diederich.
