A permit was issued to Jacob Kesner, to add a super-structure of four stories to the City Court Building, located at Michigan Avenue near Adams Street. The cost of the erection will amount to about $100,000. Mr. Kesner has also become the rightful owner of the ground adjoining Gunther's Candy Store, on State Street, measuring a frontage of 82 1/2 feet on State Street, and 144 1/2 feet on Quincy Street. He contemplates erecting a large building there.
