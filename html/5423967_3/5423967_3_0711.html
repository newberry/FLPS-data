St. George's parish, 917 North Wood Street, has made arrangements for two lectures which will be delivered by the Reverend P. Nirvanna. The first lecture will be given on Saturday, March 7, on the subject, "Individual, Family, and Church in the Ancient Christian World". The second lecture, occasioned by the recent demise of the academician Pavlov, the famous Russian scientist, will have for its subject, "Academician Pavlov: Conditional Reflexes and Their Significance for Psychology". Typical operations of Pavlov's will be shown as illustrations.

Admission is free to both lectures.
