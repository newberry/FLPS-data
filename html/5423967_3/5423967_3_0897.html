On Sunday, April 18, at the quarters of the YMCA, 1621 W. Division Street, near Ashland Avenue, Dr. Mark Slonim will give his last lecture, organized by the Russian Center of the city of Chicago. The subject of the lecture will be, "Whither is Russia Going?"

After the lecture there will be an open discussion.

The lecture will begin at 2 P. M. sharp.

The Committee.
