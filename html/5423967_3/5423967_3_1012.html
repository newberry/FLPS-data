"Our Days in the World of History and Prophecy," a popular lecture in the Russian language, Sunday, March 30, 1919, at 3 P. M., in the large hall on 12th Street, 1032 W. 12th Street, near Blue Island Bank. The important issues of the lecture are: "The World Crisis and Its Causes," "Is There a League of Nations?"

The lecturer is C. G. Burley, founder of the first Russian Christian high school in America, where he taught history, the Russian language and Bible doctrine for eight years. Admission free. All are invited. Students of the Broadview Seminary will sing.
