On May 29, 1923, an extremely interesting lecture on the theme "Russian Literature and Art of the Twentieth Century" will be given. The lecturer will be the recently-arrived Professor A. I. Ghidoni. In Russia his lectures had a great success, and the criticism of the press was very favorable. Several of his lectures in Chicago were attended, and great interest was shown.

The lecture will take place at the Women's City Club, 16 N. Wabash Avenue, 17th floor.
