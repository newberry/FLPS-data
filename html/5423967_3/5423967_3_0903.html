The Courses for General Education of Teachers announce a lecture for Saturday, March 27, 1926. Dr. Nedzelnitsky will be the lecturer. The theme will be: "Infectious diseases (typhoid fever, plague, small-pox, scarlet fever and others)." The lecture will be illustrated by pictures. It will take place at the House of Enlightenment, 1080 W. 14th Street. Beginning at 8 P. M. The public is requested not to come too late.

I. Osipik.
