On Sunday, February 7, at the auditorium of the American church, corner Leavitt street and Haddon avenue, near the Leavitt Street Russian Orthodox church, V. Narkevich will speak on the following subject: "Glimpses of a new life in the Russian colony." The Russian public is invited. Beginning at 4 P. M. sharp. The doors will be open at 3:30 P. M. Admission free of charge.
