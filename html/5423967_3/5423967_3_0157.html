Russian radio programs in Chicago, inaugurated in 1933, are broadcast three times a week--on Sundays at 9 A.M. from Station WEDC (1210 kilocycles), on Wednesdays at 5:30 P.M. from Station WGES (1360 kilocycles), and on Saturdays at 3 P.M. from Station WSBC (1210 kilocycles).

For all matters pertaining to these programs apply to the Russian Radio in Chicago, 2009 Cortez Street.
