FIRST WORK OF THE RUSSIAN SOCIAL-ECONOMIC CLUB

The first meeting of members took place on Sept. 25 (undoubtedly 1909). It was well attended. The executive committee consisted of M. Dobrov, H. Krasnow, M. Kuznetsov, L. Porter, Peter and Dr. Yarus, H. Krasnow was elected secretary and M. Kuznetsov, treasurer.

On Sept. 26 the executive committee decided to open a library and reading room; and organize a series of lectures.
