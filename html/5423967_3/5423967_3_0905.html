On Sunday, March 21, in the large hall of the House of Enlightenment, 1080 W. 14th Street, Mr. Grigoriy Senkevich will read a paper on "The Economic Situation in Modern Russia" (based on material derived from Soviet sources). After the reading of the paper, there is expected to be a lively discussion in which representatives of all shades of political views existing among the members of the Russian colony of Chicago are invited to participate.

Beginning at 2 P. M. Admission free of charge.

The Secretary.
