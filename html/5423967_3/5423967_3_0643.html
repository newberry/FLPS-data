If you want to know how to become specialist in any field or acquire an education without quitting your job, or losing time from work in order to attend school, come over and you will be told about this by Mr. Belous in his lecture. Should you happen to be in the commercial field, you will find out how it is possible to increase your income from 25% to 100% during this year, which promises to be quite a prosperous one.

At the lecture there will be presented a movie-drama in five acts.

The lecture will take place on Sunday, January 18, at 3 P. M., in the Educational Home, 1838 W. Division Street. Admission free.
