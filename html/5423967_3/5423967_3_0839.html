Znanie Society (Knowledge Society) seems desirous to live up to its name to be, in the true sense of the word, a distributor of knowledge among the Russians in Chicago. It is known to us that there are schools connected with this Society. We now also that there is in existence a fair chorus, dramatic circle etc.

And now, we found something new and inter sting. Znanie intends to arrange on its premises scientific lectures. For this purpose, the society will invite experienced lecturers and illustrate their lectures on the screen with slides or moving pictures. We are sure that these lectures will be a great success.
