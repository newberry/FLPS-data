The school committee wishes to advise Russian parents that the schedule of the Bunin school for Russian children has been changed. At present the classes are held on Wednesdays from four to seven o'clock and on Saturdays from twelve to three o'clock. In connection with the school, a string orchestra has been organized under the guidance of a talented musician. Instructions are given on all instruments except the violin. Music lessons and rehearsals are held on the school premises, 2548 Cortez Street, on Thursdays at six o'clock. All those interested in the school or in music are requested to apply to A. Bernov, 1048 North Mozart Street, or directly at the school during class hours.
