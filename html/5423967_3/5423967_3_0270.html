"He (Borodin) was an old Bolshevik, that is, he had been a member of the Leninist school since its underground days before the war. His exile had been spent in the United States, where he acquired a better first-hand knowledge of the industrial system than was common among Bolshevik intellectuals. He returned to Russia in 1917 to "party work" and had been entrusted with the Chinese mission in 1924."

(This note is background material on Michael Borodin. T. R. Hall.)
