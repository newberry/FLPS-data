On Wednesday, February 24, 1926, at 8 P. M., the Russian Progressive Women's Mutual Aid Society arranges a lecture. The subject will be "The family hearth and woman's social position." The lecturer will be the well-known writer and public worker, Miss Antonina Sokolich, who has recently arrived from Poland. The lecture will take place at the quarters of the House of the Workman, 1902 W. Division street. Both men and women are invited to attend this lecture. Admission free of charge.

The Secretary of the Society.
