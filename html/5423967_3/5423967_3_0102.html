In Lisle, Illinois, the first issue of a new church publication printed in English and Russian has just come off the press. The name of the magazine is the Church Voice. It is edited by Mr. P. Neuzhil.

The first issue presents a pleasing appearance, but the articles in Russian suffer with illiteracy.

In Chicago also the latest issue of Our Way, a church magazine published by the Russian Orthodox Church on Leavitt Street, has appeared.
