It is more than a month since G. D. Grebenshchikov came to Chicago, and he will spend his last evening in our city as the guest of the Young Russians and their friends. Our noted writer at this literary evening arranged in his honor will read aloud his latest novel in rhyme, entitled Czarevich. The new work will be illustrated by lantern slides.

The literary evening will take place in the library room of the YMCA at 1621 West Division Street on Friday, March 13, at 8 P.M. The price of admission will be twenty-five cents.
