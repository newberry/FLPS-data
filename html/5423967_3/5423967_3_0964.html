Come all! Saturday, February 8, at 7:30 P. M.

The Club of Russian Workers is arranging in its own quarters, at 1400 S. Union Avenue, corner 14th Street, a lecture on the subject: "Why are we organizing."

At the same location, Sunday, February 9, at 2 P. M., Professor Lomonosov will lecture on the subject, "The Russian Revolution and the Present Moment." Admission free.

Ed. Note: The year was 1919.
