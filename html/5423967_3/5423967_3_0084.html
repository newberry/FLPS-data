Mr. Savicki has been the publisher of the Russian Review since the very start, i. e., since November 1927.

He was born in Galicia and has been a resident of Chicago since 1920. He was graduated from the Northwestern University, where he studied especially all that pertains to commerce and foreign trade. He was one of the organizers of several of the foreign sections of the Republican Party, and is a member of the Northwestern University Club and of the Pen and Pencil Club.

Mr. Savicki is the author of the book in Ukrainian Moye Zhitiye v Mexico (My Life in Mexico).
