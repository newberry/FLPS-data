We are informed by the Board of Directors of the Union of Russian Intellectuals in the city of Chicago that on September 1, this union had 24 full fledged members. Among these we find the following names: Prof. A. A. Maximov, Dr. Nodzelnitsky, Dr. S. Novakovskaya, [gap] Gugis, Dr. [gap] Sahud, Dr. I. Shokhat, Dr. [gap] Piett, and others.

At the next regular general assembly of the union there will be examined over 40 applications of persons who desire to become members of the union.
