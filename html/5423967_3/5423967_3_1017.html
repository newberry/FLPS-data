Does it pay to believe in God?

"It pays," says John Johnson.

"It does not pay," says D. L. Orlovsky.

Saturday, Nov. 12, 1921, at Bowen Hall of Hull House, 800 S. Halsted Street, debates will be conducted: "Does It Pay to Believe in God?" Beginning exactly at 8 P. M. Doors open at 7 P. M. You are requested to be on time.
