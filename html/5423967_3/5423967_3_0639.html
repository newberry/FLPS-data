The Progressive Preparatory School was located from 1907 to 1917 on Hoyne and Division streets. Mr. Michael Rosenberg was its director and prepared students for admission to high schools and colleges; they also taught languages. Mr. Rodriguez was an instructor in citizenship and constitution.

Mr. Rosenberg was known as Michael Berg and went back to Russia, to the city of Moscow, and is connected with the Moscow News, a daily and weekly newspaper. His absence from Chicago dates since the Russian revolution. He has two sons in the army there.

He also visited China. A record of this trip with his wife is recorded in Vincent Sheehan's Personal History.
