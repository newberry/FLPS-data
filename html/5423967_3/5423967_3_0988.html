The first meeting of members took place on September 25 (undoubtedly 1909). It was well attended. The executive committee consisted of M. Dobrov, H. Krasnow, M. Kuznetsov, L. Porter, Peter and Dr. Yarros. H. Krasnow was elected secretary and M. Kuznetsov treasurer.

On September 26 the executive committee decided to open a library and reading room, and organize a series of lectures.
