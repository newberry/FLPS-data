Chicago, June 12, 1928.

Dr. H. R. Krasnow

Dear Sir: The Chicago Union of Russian officers has the honor of inviting you and your friends to be present at the ceremonial conference to honor the memory of the chief commander of the Russian army, Lieutenant General Baron Peter Nikolaevich Wrangel, which is to take place Saturday, June 16, in the YMCA library, 1621 W. Division St.

THE PROGRAM

Part I.

1. Opening of the conference and report.

The biography of General Wrangel, by Major General A. M. Uzefovich.

2. The Crimean period of White struggle. Dr. L. G. Pertsov-Kaluzhin

3. Gallipoli, Captain R. J. Khomko

Part II.

4. A word in memory of Dr. V. G. Telesnik by Dr. A. C. Gabriliantz.

5. A word in memory of K. J. Barsov, lieutenant of the fleet, by V. J. Antsiferov.

6. A report of the president of the Chicago Committee for Aid to Invalids in Foreign Lands. Captain V. V. Shumkov.

7-9. Russian songs. Mrs. S. J. Shukhvostova

10-12. Balalaika Quartet. G. Kaminsky, D. Gribanovsky, E. Tupikov,

V. Antsiferov.

Beginning 8 P. M. Admission free.

General M. A. M. Usefovich

President of the Union
