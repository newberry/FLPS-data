The Russian-American Citizens' Club wishes to let our members and persons, who are interested in our club, know of the following newly organized and functioning schools:

1. Ballet school - managed by Mr. Kosteluk, of the Chicago Civic Opera (Mondays and Tuesdays)

2. String Orchestra (with twenty members already enrolled) - managed by Mr. Saturnov and Mr. Chernishov. (Fridays)

3. School of the English language and helpful instruction in obtaining citizenship papers - managed by Mary Moravsky. (Tuesdays and Thursdays)

Enroll now in these classes.

V. Rishun - Chairman

D. Sleznik - Secretary
