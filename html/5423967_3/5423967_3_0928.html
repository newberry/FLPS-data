At the courses for general education, 1080 W. 14th St., it has been announced that a series of lectures on hygiene will be given, starting on Jan. 1, 1925.

Dr. A. I. Nedzelnitsky, formerly assistant professor of the Novorossiysky University, has been invited to give this course of lectures. The lectures will take place on Wednesdays from 9 P. M. to 10 P. M.

Outsiders interested in hygiene are invited to join this course of lectures, which they can do free of charge.
