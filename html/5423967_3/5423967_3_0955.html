On October 20, 1923, at 8 p.m., in the hall of the school of the Independent Society, at 817 N. Wood Street, will be given a very interesting lecture on "The days of the revolution in Russia." The lecturer will be Miss F. P. Barzeilo, a journalist who has come recently from Russia and has lived there through all the most important periods of the great revolution.

Come and hear Miss Barzeilo give this interesting lecture. Before the lecture the chorus of the Independent Society will sing several revolutionary songs.

Admission--25c

The Committee.
