Such an enormous stream of emigrants from Gothenburg, Sweden, to America has not been seen for the last ten years. Certain steamship lines have passengers booked many weeks ahead. The largest emigrant office in Gothenburg sent no less than two thousand persons to America a short time ago.. It is usually young men of eighteen to twenty years of age, who emigrate. The supposed good times in the United States, is said to be one of the causes for this emigration.
