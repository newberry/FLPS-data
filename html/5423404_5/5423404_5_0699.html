Mrs. Adolfina Lindskog, wife of the Rev. Herman Lindskog, died last Thursday in the Rectory of the Saint Ansgarius Swedish-American Episcopal Church, at 59 years of age.

She was born in Sweden, and married Pastor Lindskog in 1874. Five years later she came with him to America. She became very interested and active in her husband's parish work, especially within the Women's and Young People's Societies and among the needy. Her funeral was conducted in St. Ansgarius Church by the Rt. Rev. Charles P. Anderson, Episcopal Bishop of the Chicago Diocese, and at the grave in Graceland Cemetary by Pastor Carl A. Nyblad.
