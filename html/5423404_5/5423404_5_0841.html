Memorial services for Mrs. Adolphina Lindskog, who died some years ago, will be held September 17. Her surviving husband is the Reverend Herman Lindskog. Rector of St. Ansgarius' Swedish-American Episcopal Church at Sedgwick Street, Chicago. Her memory will be celebrated on her birthday, September 17, with an outing at the Old Swedish Home at Evanston, Illinois, the women of St. Ansgarius Parish Participating. They will gather together at Pastor Lindskog's home at 2 P.M. where he will conduct a brief service, and then proceed to the Home where the old residents will be entertained and refreshments will be served by the ladies.
