Appointment after appointment has been the good fortune of Dr. Arthur Emil Swanson of the Northwestern University School of Commerce. He has lately been appointed dean of the same school, where he has been a teacher since 1911, and was at the same time asked by the Federal Marine Board to dedicate some of his time to it during the year.

Leave of absence has been granted him by the University, and on Saturday he left for Washington, D. C., to assume his duties there.
