Two more Chicago Swedes honored by the Swedish King, namely,Dr. Carl G. Wallenius, Professor of Theology and Rector of the Methodist Seminary in Evanston and Mr. Adolph Lindstrom, the well-known contractor, who is also the president of the Central State Security Company and a director of the Congress Trust and Savings Bank. Professor Wallenius has been honored with the Order of the North Star and Mr. Lindstrem with the Order of Vasa.
