Dr. David Nyvall, Professor and Rector Emeritus at the North Park College, has been honored with the Order of the North Star by the King of Sweden in recognition of the fine work accomplished by Dr. Nyvall and the high standards reached at this comparatively new Swedish institute of learning.

The order was presented to Dr. Nyvakk by Consul Carl O.deDardel at a banquet in his honor last Thursday at the Drake Hotel.
