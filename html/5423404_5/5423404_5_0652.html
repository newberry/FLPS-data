Rev. Clans A. Nyren, who has been elected president of the state conference of the Swedish missionary church ministers, is pastor of the Bethany Swedish Missionary Church at Fifth Avenue and Garfield Boulevard.

He was born in Sweden thirty-nine years ago, and was educated at Kristedahamn. When a young man he came to America and has lived in Chicago eighteen years. He has been actively interested in the Swedish colleges in and near Chicago. Rev. Mr. Nyren is married and lives at 5743 Princeton Avenue.
