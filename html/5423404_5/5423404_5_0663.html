The most fashionable wedding of the season in Swedish circles, took place last Tuesday evening, when Miss Grace Rognhild Hedman, daughter of Director Carl Hedman, and his wife was united in Marriage with the Architect, Robert Charles Ostergren.

The wedding took place in St. Peter's Episcopal Church, which was completely filled. A wedding banquet was held in the evening at the La Salle Hotel, in the Red Room. The couple will live at 538 Roscoe Street.
