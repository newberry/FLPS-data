The well-known theatre director, Ernst Behmer, was honored last Saturday by the Swedish Dramatical Society with a banquet at the Swedish Club.

The finance director of the Society, Mrs. Othelia Myhrman, opened the program with a speech, and thanked Mr. Behmer for the tireless work he has done to keep up the dramatical art in Chicago. She presented Mr. Behmer a valuable watch with a gold chain on behalf of the society. Mr. Behmer expressed his hearty thanks for the gift.
