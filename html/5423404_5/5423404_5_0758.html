Dr. J. A. Enander, editor-in-chief of the valued contemporary, Hemlandet, was honored by the Swedish King the other day with the medal, "Literis et Artibus," given for valued service. Svenska Nyheter congratulates the greying veteran of the press upon his receiving such well-earned recognition. In connection with the award, a banquet will be held in Dr. Enander's honor on Friday evening, at the Swedish Businessmen's Club, 171 Washington Street.
