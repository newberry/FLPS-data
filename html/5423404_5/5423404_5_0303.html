Knut Nyblom, Stockholm, Sweden, has sent a letter of thanks to those in charge of the Wennerberg Concert, held here on September 2, for the purpose of raising money to be sent to Sweden as a contribution of the Chicago Swedes to the erection of a statue of the great composer Gunnar Wennerberg,at Upsala, Sweden. The net profit from the concert amounted to 1723 Swedish Kroner.
