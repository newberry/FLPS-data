The remains of our great countryman, John Ericsson, will be transported to Sweden on board the U.S. steel cruiser "Baltimore", according to an order issued by the Secretary of the Navy.

August 23 has been set as the date for the transfer of the sarcophagus containing his body from the American mainland in New York Harbor onto the "Baltimore". Solemn military ceremonies will accompany this act, at which prominent naval, diplomatic and consular dignitaries will be present.
