Charles E. Kronlof died last Sunday, April 10th in his home, 5326 Foster avenue. He was born in Sweden in 1866 and came to Chicago about forty years ago.

Mr. Kronlof enjoyed the reputation of being the most active person in Swedish fraternal circles. He was an ardent friend of temperance, and the I.O.G.T. had honored him with the highest office within that organization. At one time he served as Grand Master of the Order of Vikings, and for a number of years he was District Master of the Order of Vasa.
