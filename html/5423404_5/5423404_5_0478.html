One of the best known and beloved Swedes in Chicago, Christoffer Brusell, died Friday, May 22nd. He was singer, amateur actor, theater director, and an "aide-de-camp", whenever entertainment in Swedish Lodges and Societies was needed. He was born in Stockholm, in 1861, and in his youth studied at the Royal Dramatic Theatre there. He was President of the Swedish Theatrical Society of Chicago and had directed and filled parts in countless Swedish dramas and comedies played by the Society.
