Mrs. Charlotta Muchau, nee Bolling, of 5922 N. Fairfield Avenue, died last Friday after a brief illness, at the age of ninety. She was one of the best known Swedish masseuses in Chicago and was active in her profession until shortly before her death. She possessed a remarkable vitality and mental alertness. She was born on the Isle of Gotland and was a sister of the famous Dr. Gustav Magnus Bolling in Sweden.
