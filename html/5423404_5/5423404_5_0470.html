Of Prominent Chicago Swedes, who have been vacationing in the Old Country and have just returned to Chicago are Dr. A. J. Carlson, Professor of Physiology at the University of Chicago; A. G. Hallbom, President of the Builders & Merchants Bank; the Rev. Dr. C. G. Wallenius; Pastor Elof; K. Johnson; Helge Erikson, jeweler; Hjalmar Ebbeson, singer; Hugo Oberg, Secretary of the Swedish Consulate and Mrs. Othelia Myhrman, President of the American Daughters of Sweden.
