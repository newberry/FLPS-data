Henry S. Henschen, cashier-director at the State Bank of Chicago, also former Swedish Consul here, and Nils A. Nelson, director for Scandia Life Insurance Company, have been appointed Knights of the Royal Swedish Order of Vasa, first class, by His Majesty, King Gustaf V. The insignias of the Order arrived last Tuesday from the Swedish Ambassador in Washington, D. C. to the Swedish Consul, Gosta Puke here, who delivered them to the new knights the same day.
