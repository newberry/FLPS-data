The Scandinavian-American Line has arranged three Christmas excursions this year to the Scandinavian countries. The S.S. "Oscar II" is leaving New York November 23, "C. F. Tietgen," November 30, and "United States" December 7. The Scandinavian-American Line is the only direct Line between New York and the Scandinavian countries. Good Scandinavian meals are served on board.
