Carl Max Hedman, president of the Hedman Manufacturing Company, whose plant and offices are located at 1158 Center Street, died suddenly last Wednesday night in his home, 618 Waveland Avenue.

He had recently returned from a vacation trip to Colorado, and had remarked to friends that he did not feel quite right, but nobody expected that his indispostion would take such a turn. Death was caused by a heart attack.
