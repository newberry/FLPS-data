Our countryman, William A. Peterson of Chicago, has been honored by King Gustaf of Sweden, who has just conferred upon him the Order of Vasa. Mr. Peterson is a director of the State Bank of Chicago and president of the Peterson Nursery Company.

He received the order from the Swedish vice-consul here, Henry S. Henschen, in the presence of a number of local dignitaries, among whom was City Engineer John Ericson, who also received the order some time ago.
