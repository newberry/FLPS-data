Mrs. J. F. Smulski, wife of Alderman Smulski, was presented with diplomas yesterday at Holly Trinity Church school hall, 540 Noble St., conferring upon her honorary membership in the United Polish Singers of America and the Wanda Ladies' Singing Society. A gold medal set with diamonds was given Mrs. Smulski by the Queen Hedwig Polish Ladies' Singing Society.

Mrs. Smulski is the first Polish woman in Chicago to graduate from a musical conservatory.
