A concert will be given by the noted young violinist, Miss A. Zebroski, at the St. Stanislaus hall, on Wednesday, October 25, at 8 P.M.

Miss Zebrowski is a pupil of Prof. J. H. Chapek. Others participating in the program of the concert will be Mrs. Rose Kwasigroch, soprano; Miss J. Dyniewicz, pianist; Mrs. A. Haevel-Chapek,pianist; Prof. J. H. Chapek, violinist; M. Lusk, violinist; E. Chapek, violinist; and the well known choir Filareci, under the direction of B. Zalewski, This program is select and varied.
