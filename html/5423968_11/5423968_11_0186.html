The editor of the Catholic Newspaper, Mr. W. Smulski, invited the editors of various Polish publications with the intention of discussing in what way the task of Mr. Emil Habdank Danikowski, professor and dean of a university could be made easier and enable him to receive the necessary information in regard to Polish immigration into United States.

Participants in this conference were: W. Smulski, T. Wild, H. Nagiel, S. Szwajkart, L. Szopinski, and S. Nicki.
