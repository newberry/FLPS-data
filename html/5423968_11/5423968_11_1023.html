Not only the English newspapers but also the German are not always well informed of our affairs.

In an item found in Weston about the consecration of the St. Hedwig's Church, the writer--some Vaterlandsverteiger, no doubt--says that besides, the Polish people there are many Deutschpolen from Prussia, Silesia, and Poznan in St. Hedwig's Parish. We know nothing about any Deutschpolen living in St. Hedwig's--but the Weston knows about them. It probably got a whiff of the odor of Prussian Roast.
