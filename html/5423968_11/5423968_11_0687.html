A patriotic gathering was held Sunday, April 28, at University Hall, Town of Lake, by the Poles of that community.

Mr. Zaleski delivered a lecture on the fall of Poland. This was followed by a lively discussion on this same subject by Dr. T. Kodis, C. Zurawski and Mr. Marchwicki. The insurrection of 1863 against Russia was also referred to.

Recitations were given by little Miss Bauer and a boy, Janiszewski; another was given by Mrs. Krzyzanska.

The musical portion of the program was filled with the piano-playing by Misses Wilson and Ron. Miss Hoffer entertained with several songs.

The sum of $2.50 was collected for the National Fund.
