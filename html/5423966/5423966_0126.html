The Serbian Orphan's Society in Chicago organized a tea party at the church hall on October 4th.

The humanitarian aim of this society brought in hundreds of guests who were interested in rendering help to unfortunate orphans of Serbian parentage.

This noble society, during the past quarter of a century, gave a home and education to hundreds of children.

The president of this society is the well known and oldest member of Chicago's colony, Mr. John Vuchetich.
