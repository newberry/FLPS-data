The Jugoslav Song Festival, which was held on April 18, 1937, at 5 P. M. at the church, 1905 W. Schiller (formerly Fowler) street, Chicago, Ill., was a huge success.

Jugoslav music lovers and leaders of our colony were present and many out-of-town came here for the occasion. Participating were soloists from the Chicago Civic Opera and the Savine Vocal Ensemble.
