Dr, Dushkin Discusses The New In Jewish Education

At the Five O'clock Forum on Sunday, January 8, Dr. Alexander Dushkin, Director of the Board of Jewish Education, told the story of Jewish Education and its present status in Jewish life.

From the educational standpoint, he divided American Jewry into five main groups and discussed the curricula advocated by each group. He expressed the opinion that a unification is taking place in Jewish life and that the Jewry of the future would be an amalgam of the elements that are culturally and educationally active at the present time.
