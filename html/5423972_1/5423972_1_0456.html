At a recent meeting of the Board of Education, permission was given Rabbi Israel Klein, Milton H. Twehlich, and B. M. Engelhard of the Chicago Association of Jewish Religious Teachers, to secure from the various teachers a list of all pupils absent from school on September 12 last, the date of the Jewish New Year.

The purpose of this list is to locate the home addresses and names of parents of the children, so as to provide a dequate religion schools wherever they are lacking.
