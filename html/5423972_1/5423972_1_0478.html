The School Committee....reported that it held a meeting on October 28, 1903, and opened the West Side Mission School; and further recommends to the Executive Board that the sum of fifty dollars be paid to Dr. Schreiber for the maintenance of the religious school at the City Parental School, to be known as the Sinai Mission School.

Upon motion by E. B. Foreman, seconded by S. W. Straus, the report of said committee was concurred in and said recommendation adopted.
