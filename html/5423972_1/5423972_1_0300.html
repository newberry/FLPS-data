Mr. E. Zeligman, 2789 Augusta Street, is the first Jew in Chicago to pass the examination given by the Board of Education as high school teacher of Hebrew.

This step was the result of the work of Mr. H. Lipsky, who four years ago started a campaign for Hebrew as one of the subjects in the examination of high school teachers.

Mr. Zeligman is a Ph. B. of the University of Chicago and a teacher in the Hebrew school at the Home for the Aged.

It is expected that for the next semester he will be selected as Hebrew teacher for one of the Chicago high schools.
