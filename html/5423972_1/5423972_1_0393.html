Extraordinary interest was displayed in the circles of the students of Jewish religious schools in this country in the debate which took place in Chicago on September 7th at the Jewish People's Institute between teams representing the Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary, the Yeshiva, the leading institution for higher Jewish learning and the team representing the Hebrew Theological College of Chicago.

Over 1,000 persons were present at the debate presided over by Rabbi Simon J. Kramer at the Jewish People's Institute when the team representing the New York Yeshiva argued the affirmative for the following resolution "Resolved that the Parochial School System is the sole solution to the problem of the permanency of traditional Judaism in this Country."
