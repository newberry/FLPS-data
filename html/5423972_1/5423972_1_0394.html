The Chicago Jewry is anxiously and enthusiastically awaiting the forthcoming epoch-making debate between the Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary of New York, and the Hebrew Theological College of Chicago. This unusual event, which will be the first inter-seminary English debate in Jewish history, will be held in the new Jewish People's Institute, Douglas Blvd. and St. Louis Ave., Wednesday evening, September 7, at 8:15 P.M.
