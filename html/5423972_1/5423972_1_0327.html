The University of Chicago has announced the creation of the Lasker Foundation for Medical Research with an initial endowment of $1,000,000. The fund is the gift of Albert D. Lasker and his wife, Flora. Mr. Lasker is former chairman of the United States Shipping Board and head of Lord and Thomas Advertising Agency.

While a definite program has not yet been developed under the foundation, Dr. McLean, head of the University's Medical Department, stated that the first efforts would be made against Bright's disease and heart ailments.
