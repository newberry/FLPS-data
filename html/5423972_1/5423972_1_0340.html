The Commercial Club which is at present carrying on extensive propaganda for trade school training in Chicago public schools, last evening discussed the question of trade training, at a large banquet at the Blackstone Hotel.

In addition to all the members of the Board of Education, the following prominent Jews of Chicago participated on this discussion: Rabbi Emil G. Hirsch, Mrs. Hanna Solomon, Mr. Edwin G. Foreman, Mr. Harry A. Lipsky, Prof. Ernest Freund, Congressman A. J. Sabbath, Dr. David F. Mones, and Mr. T. Rubovitz.
