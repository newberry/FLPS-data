A meeting of young women and men was held in the vestry rooms of Sinani, in answer to a call by Dr. Hirsch. It was resolved to organize a Sinai University Extension Center. Mrs. Sophia Haas, Mr. Simon Yondorf, Dr. E. G. Hirsch and Mr. Jalian W. Mack were elected the Board of Directors.

The plan of the organization is to receive courses of lectures by the professors at the University, in the various departments of Science and Literature.
