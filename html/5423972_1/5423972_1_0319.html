The will of Harry Hart was filed in the Probate Court this week. Among the donations were: The Jewish Charities of Chicago, $50,000, the Art Institute, $25,000, Women and Children's Hospital, $25,000, and Northwestern University, $30,000, in honor of Mr. Hart's nephew, Dr. Isaac Abt.

The will provides that if the University should erect a children's hospital with which the name of Dr. Abt could be associated, the money may be used in either the construction, maintenance or operation of the institution.
