The Pulaski Pleasure Club was organized on April 22 in St. Adalbert Parish. The following officers were elected, John Kowalski, president; Anthony Jezierny, vice-president; Thomas Ronowski, financial secretary; Valentine Astomski, recording secretary; and Stanislaus Szmergalski, treasurer.

The next meeting will be held on May 5, at Sobolewski's Hall, 19th Street and Hoyne Avenue, at 1 P.M. All those desiring to join are requested to attend the meeting. Entrance fee is only fifty cents.

Stanislaus Szmergalski, treasurer.
