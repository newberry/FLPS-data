The Polish League has gained eighty-two new women members. The Polish Women's Patriotic and Benevolent Society, from St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish, has decided to join the League in a body, and pay the special assessment for the benefit of the Polish National Fund.

Moreover, it was decided to hold a large meeting for this purpose in one of the halls in the school building on Bradley Street. All ladies are invited.

Additional hundreds of ladies will join the League on Monday. Today we will give a cheer to those who already have joined it.

Long live patriotic Polish women!
