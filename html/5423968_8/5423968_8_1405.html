Telegram sent by management of the Polish Roman-Catholic Union to private secretary of President McKinley is as follows:

"George B. Certelyen,

Secretary to the President,

Buffalo, N. Y.

"The Polish Roman-Catholic Union of the United States deplores sincerely and profoundly the dastardly attempt to assassinate our beloved President McKinley. We pray that the Almighty may spare his life. Kindly extend our heartfelt sympathy to Mrs. McKinley in her hour of sorrow.

Anthony Polenz, President.

Thomasz Krolik, Secretary.

Rev. Francis Lange, Chaplain."
