A permit to build a three-story flat-building at 34 Kosciusko Street, for the sum of $4,000, was issued to Mr. Blasius Slareyor.

A. J. Golowski was granted a concession to erect a three-story flat-building at 800 Elk Grove Avenue, for the sum of $5,000.

Henry Nowacki was allowed to construct a two-story flat-building at 4432 Wood Street for $2,500.
