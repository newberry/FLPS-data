A drill contest for a gold medal was held on August 2 by the cadets in the town of Lake district. The contest was arranged under the auspices of the Polish White Eagle Society. The following societies participated in the contest: A battalion of Pulaski Volunteers from St. Adalbert's parish and the Kosciusko Guards under the protection of Saint Florian of South Chicago.

The gold medal for the best drill was awarded to the company commanded by John Szymonajtys of the Kosciusko Guards.
