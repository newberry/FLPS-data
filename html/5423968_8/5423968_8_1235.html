The Bell of Freedom Society, group 1103, of the Polish National Alliance, is sponsoring a drama and dance on October 27, 1912, at the Wachowski Hall, Milwaukee and Gresham Streets. The net proceeds are to be turned over to the fund of the Polish National Alliance College at Cambridge Springs, Pennsylvania.

A one-act play, under the title of "God be Praised for Our Welfare," will be given by many of the well-known Chicago Amateurs. Following the drama, the dance will take place. The committee has arranged very many surprises for this evening. One of the main surprises of the dance, will be the selection of a queen. The lucky girl will receive a bouquet of flowers as an award.
