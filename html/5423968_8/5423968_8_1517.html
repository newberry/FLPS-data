Last Saturday, August 3, the branch of the Polish League of America in St. Adalbert Parish held its first annual meeting. The president, Clement Belinski, presided.

Joseph Napieralski, J. I. Migoalski, F. Czerwinski, Reverend John Radziejewski, and President Clement Belinski spoke at the meeting.

The following contributions were received for the Polish National Fund:

The Sorrowful Heart of Mary Society $6.00
The Holy Family Brotherhood 4.80

Joseph Plewa, Regina Tarnowska, Victoria Sikora, John Kroplewski, John Kowalski, and Joseph Napieralski gave twelve cents each. Others gave lesser amounts.

Contributions were also made for the Polish League. The total contribution for the Polish National Fund amounted to $12.15, and $1.91 was contributed for the Polish League.

The next meeting of this branch will be held on Sunday, August 11, at 4 P.M., in St. John Cantius Parish hall, 16th Street and Paulina Avenue. All members of the League are requested to attend, for an election of officers, as well as an election of delegates for the coming convention at Toledo, Ohio, will be held.

L. Jastrzemski, secretary,
