All architects wishing to prepare and submit plans, sketches, and estimates of cost on the new Polish Alliance Home to be erected in Chicago are requested to call at once at the office of the Central Administration of the Polish National Alliance, 574 Noble Street, Chicago, where the general secretary will give further particulars and more information.

The Central Administration reserves the right to choose one of the submitted projects to be completed, or to reject any and all of the submitted propositions. All of the plans and sketches must be ready and deposited in the office of the Central Administration not later than the twenty ninth day of January, 1896, twelve o'clock noon.

By order of the Central Administration.
