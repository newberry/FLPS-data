An embezzlement accusation against a Pole will be heard before Judge LaBuy on Wednesday. The defendant, Mr. Adam Magdziarz, 91 Chapin Street, was a collector for the Casimir Jagielle Society (of the Polish National Alliance). The president of the Society, Mr. Roman Czerwinski, 123 Cleaver Street, signed the complaint, alleging, that Mr. Magdziarz had collected some money for Hely Trinity Church and some assessments, all together forty dollars, and had not turned ever the money. Magdziarz was placed under bond.
