A building permit has been granted to Mr. Charles Kusiates to build a three-story building at the corner of 38th Street and S. Albany Avenue. The building will be 24 X 67 feet and will cost $12,000. Mr. Anthony Karczewski was given the contract to do the bricklaying work. The metal work will be in charge of another contractor, Mr. Graczykowski, of Division Street, and a certain Lithuanian contractor has the contract for the carpentry work.
