The following contributions were made to the Polish National Fund of the Polish League:

Walter Zwierzynski, thirty-five cents; Anthony Rozewicz, Francis Fryza, and Joseph Tyszper, ten cents apiece; and Joseph Tigalski, five cents.

One dollar and twenty cents came from F. H. Kowalski, secretary of the St. Hedwig Society of South Bend, Indiana.

Stanislaus Szwajkart,

secretary of the Polish League
