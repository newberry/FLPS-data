The Polish League of America, which was organized in Chicago last year, has recently opened a new branch at Stevens Point, Wisconsin. Reverend Jachimiak is largely responsible for its opening, for he made all the plans.

A dedication ceremony took place on March 21 in the school hall. Officers were also installed. M. Kaliszewski was appointed president.

This is the nineteenth branch to be opened in less than a year. Long live the Polish League!
