President W. H. Taft has accepted the invitation from the central headquarters of the Polish National Alliance to the dedication of the new Polish National Alliance College at Cambridge Springs, Pennsylvania. His reply to the invitation is as follows:

Beverly, Mass., October 11, 1912.

Mr. K. Zychlinski, President,

Polish National Alliance,

1406 W. Division Street,

Chicago, Ill.,

My Dear Mr. Zychlinski:

I have your and Mr. Czechowicz's letter of October 4, and have much pleasure in accepting your invitation to participate in the dedication of 2the new Polish National Alliance College at Cambridge Springs, Pennsylvania, on October 26.

Please accept my thanks for the compliment you pay me, and an assurance of my appreciation of your courtesy.

Sincerly yours,

W. H. Taft.
