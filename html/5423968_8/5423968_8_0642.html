The grand opening of the Polish Hotel, at 531 Noble Street, will take place on Saturday, Sunday and Monday, December 2, 3, 4.

The undersigned cordially invites all his friends and acquaintances to this grand opening. Remember it will last three days, Saturday, Sunday, and Monday. There will be excellent liquor, cigars,and lunch--really not a lunch, but good old-fashioned Polish refreshments.

Sigmund F. Czaplinski,

proprietor of the Polish Hotel
