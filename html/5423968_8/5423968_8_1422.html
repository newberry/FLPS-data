The present committee, elected by the P.N.A. and the P.R.C.U. board of directors, at their last meeting March 10th, decided to invite all delegates representing independent and Catholic, Polish and Lithuanian societies to be present at the next meeting Saturday April 20, at Schultz's hall, at 8 P.M. located at Noble and Blackhawk Sts. The purpose of this meeting is to get as many societies interested in this great movement that is possible. Their main object is to make plans for the celebration of the 100th anniversary of the birth of Adam Mickiewicz.

M.A. LaBuy, Pres.

S.Barszczewski, Sect.
