At yesterday's meeting of the Central Board of Directors it was resolved to send the following telegram to President Wilson:

Chicago, Illinois, February 5, 1917

His Excellency Woodrow Wilson,

President of the United States,

Washington, D. C.

In the present great crisis involving the United States, the members of our organization, numbering one hundred and twenty thousand, are with you to a man in supporting your manly stand for the vindication of the rights, honor, and dignity of our country. They are ready and willing at all times to render any service and make any sacrifice that their duty as American citizens may require of them.

Polish National Alliance of the

United States of North America:

K. Zychlinski, President

J. S. Zawilinski, General Secretary
