The popular Jednota Cesko-Americkych Lesniku A Lesnic (Bohemian-American Men and Women Foresters) , who have proved their largesse and their patriotic spirit many times, have not been idle in these fateful times, but have shown their munificence by a splendid donation for war relief in the old country. The grand court of the organization recommended and all courts approved the collection of a special tax which netted $1,150.75.....
