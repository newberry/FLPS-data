Respecting the wishes of the deceased Mrs. Anna Vrba that instead of floral offerings all the money be utilized for contributions to the Ceska Utulna a Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage), and the Sirotcinec Svateho Josefa (St. Joseph's Orphanage) in Lisle, Illinois, her children and friends have acted accordingly.

Thus, the sum of $186 was equally divided among the two above-named institutions.....
