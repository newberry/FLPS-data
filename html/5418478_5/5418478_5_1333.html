Beginning today, the offices of the Ceska Dobrocinna Spolecnost (Bohemian charitable association) located at 2603 South Kedzie Avenue will be open every day between the hours of 9 A. M. and 1 P. M. The offices will be closed on Saturdays until September 9, 1922, when they will be reopened, and will continue to be open on Saturdays as usual.
