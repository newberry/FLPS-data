The regular monthly meeting of the board of representatives of the Cesko-Americka Nemocnice (Bohemian-American Hospital) was held in the premises of the Ceska Dobrocinna Spolecnost (Bohemian Charitable Association) yesterday. It was very well attended.....

After Mr. John A. Cervenka had opened the meeting....Mr. Blodek reported on the profit the Nemocnice had made on the "wheels of fortune" installed at various places on the occasion of the Businessmen's carnival on 26th Street. According to his report the gross income was $223.20, from which $15.29, in expenses conncected with the decoration of the booths must be substracted, leaving a net profit of $207.91.....

The report of the directory committee is ready for presentation at the next meeting.....
