This is to notify all Czechoslovak women who had to resign from their societies because of exorbitant dues that we are going to organize a club which will satisfy the needs of both our elderly and our young women. There will be a meeting at Mr. Novak's establishment, 25th Street and Homan Avenue, on January 20 at 7:30 P. M. Women between the ages of twenty and fifty years will be accepted for club membership.
