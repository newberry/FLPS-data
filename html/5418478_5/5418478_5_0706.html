The most worthy of present Bohemian enterprises in Chicago, the Cesko-Americka Nemocnice (Bohemian-American Hospital) was remembered yesterday on the occasion of the wedding of Miss Sofie C. Legat with Mr. Josef G. Blaha at Mr. Legat's restaurant on Crawford Avenue. A collection was taken up which yielded $165.00. The money was immediately forwarded to the treasurer of the Cesko-Americka Memocnice....
