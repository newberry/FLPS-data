p. 2, col. 3 - At a social gathering given by Mrs. Matous, at her home, 535 West 20th Street, a suggestion was made by Mrs. Nemecek, to take up a collection for the benefit of the Old People's Home and the Orphanage. This proposal was well taken into consideration and the amount of $4.50 was collected. This money was then deposited at the office of this newspaper for the authorized official.

Both Mrs. Matous and Mrs. Nemecek thanked the guests for their donations.
