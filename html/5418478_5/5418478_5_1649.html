Recently, a collection was undertaken by members of the Ludvikova Divadelni Spolecnost (Ludvik's Bohemian Dramatic Association) for the benefit of an unfortunate actor, Mr. Frantisek Bohuslav, who lost his sight in action. The following countrymen contributed to this collection: [There follow the names and amounts.] Total, $99.

The sum of 8,300 Czechoslovakian kronen was sent through the Skala State Bank. The president of the Skala State Bank, Mr. F. J. Skala, contributed 700 Czechoslovakian kronen toward this collection. Thus, Mr. Frantisek Bohuslav will receive the full amount of 9,000 Czechoslovakian kronen.
