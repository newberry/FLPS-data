The Rad Jungman, Cyslo 20, Cesko-Slovanske Podporujici Spolky (Lodge Jungman, No. 20, Bohemian-Slavonic Benevolent Societies), celebrated a memorable occasion Saturday--its thirty-fifth anniversary.....

The hall of the Cesko-Slovenske Podporujici Spolky was filled with a good share of the 606 present members of the Lodge and their friends.....

The main speech of the evening was delivered by Dr. Frantisek Iska.....His address was followed by a Lohrbeer comedy, "Joseska Tragedie" (A Josephine Tragedy), played by a group of amateurs....all of whom are members of the Lodge.....

At midnight an excellent supper was served, and all of the guests remained in good fellowship until the early hours of the morning.....
