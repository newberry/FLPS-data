We have just received from the office of the Ceskoslovensky Cerveny Kriz (Czechoslovak Red Cross) at Prague, the acknowledgements for two shipments of money, the first of 875 Czechoslovak crowns, the second of 210 crowns. The money which was collected here was for the benefit of the Dr. Alice G. Masaryk Fund [for Czechoslovak war orphans]. The Cerveny Kriz in making the acknowledgement expressed its most sincere thanks to all the countrymen who contributed to this collection.....
