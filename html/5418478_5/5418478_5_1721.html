At the last meeting of the arrangement committee of the Bazar Svobody (Liberty Bazaar) which will be held in Chicago from September 22 to September 26, 1920, and which is sponsored by our national organizations, societies, and Czechoslovak legionnaires of Chicago, a great interest was manifested in this project by our Bohemian people, thus indicating that the Bazar Svobody will meet with success.

Many monetary contributions are coming in from all sides, and according to the report of the financial secretary, the following contributions have been received so far: [There follow the names and amounts.] Total, sixty-six dollars.
