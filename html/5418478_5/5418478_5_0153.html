The directors of the Bohemian Charitable Association held their monthly meeting on Friday, February 2, 1920. Mr. Stanislav J. Loula, presiding... The total receipts in this meeting amounted to $577.50. The total expenses for the poor were as follows: For rents $169.50, for fuel $12.60, for food $55.50, for clothes $11.98, other relief $5.50. Total $255.08. For one Bohemian family whose four children died after being stricken with influenza, within the short period of three weeks, $20.00 for relief.
