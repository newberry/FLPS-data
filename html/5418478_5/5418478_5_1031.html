At the surprise party arranged in honor of Mrs. Josephine Glajsky, 2648 West 21st Street, by her friends and neighbors, our important humanitarian institution, the Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage, was remembered by a collection undertaken among the guests. The proceeds of the collection, amounting to $4.75, were deposited in our office by Mrs. Antonie Sterba of 2652 West 21st Street.
