During the celebration of the thirtieth wedding anniversary of Mr. Frank and Mrs. Katerina Kalivoda, Mr. Vaclav Fridrich remembered the Utulna a Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage) by collecting $21.70 and depositing it for the aforementioned cause.

Miss Libuse Hajek of Riverside, Illinois....deposited four dollars with us for the benefit of the Ceska Dobrocinna Spolecnost (Bohemian Charitable Association)....Three dollars and seventy-five cents were mailed to us by Mr. Karel Nechansky of Crystal Lake, Illinois, Mrs. S. Jelinek of Beaufield, Saskatchewan, Canada; Mrs. Clara Pavlik of Allentown, Pennsylvania; and Mr. Fred Holubar of Council Bluffs, Iowa, for the benefit of the Brnenska Utulna (Old People's Home at Brno, Czechoslovakia).

Mr. M. Doucha of Antigo, Wisconsin, sent us twenty-five cents as a flower offering for the grave of Frank Habbart.
