A meeting was held yesterday in the Pilsen Pavilion by a committee of the Bohemian Charitable Association. The meeting was called for the purpose of making preparations for the so-called "Tag Day." The following named ladies arrived for the meeting: Miss G.Jerabek, Mesdames C. Ring, Fatrev, Fuerst, Sistka, Turek, Sedlacek, Cervenka, and Uhlir. Messrs. Cervenka, Petru, Turek and Docekal were present as an executive committee for the Bohemian Charitable Association. It was decided to tag in all communities settled by Bohemians. Mr. Cervenka was chosen chairman by the ladies present, and it was agreed that the next meeting be held next Wednesday afternoon in Pilsen Park.
