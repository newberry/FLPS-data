Mr. Jan Hruska, 1621 South Loomis Street, recently received a communication from his friend, Mr. Frantisek Hyrak, an invalid, of Nova Ves, near Uhersky Ostroh, Czechoslovakia, in which he asks for aid. He is in a very sad situation, and therefore in his behalf Mr. Hruska undertook a collection to which the following countrymen contributed: [There follow the names and amounts.] Total, $13.50. Any of our countrymen who would be willing to contribute toward this cause are requested to send contributions to the above-mentioned address.
