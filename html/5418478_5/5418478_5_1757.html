The Czechoslovak Socialist workingmen's society Fresl, which agreed to collect donations for the benefit of suffering Bohemians in Vienna, Austria, received further contributions.....[Translator's note: The names of the contributors and the amounts are given] amounting to $10.70. Up until today, the amount collected is $30.20. Further contributions are being accepted by the above-mentioned Society.

Antonin Podlipsky, secretary, 1829 South Taroop Street.
