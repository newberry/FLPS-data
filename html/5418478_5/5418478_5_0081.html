The true Free Thought and progressive Jednota Taboritu v Americe (Taborites of America) is founding a new lodge in Cicero. The first meeting of the organization committee will be held Sunday, February 27, at 2 P. M., in Sokol Karel Jonas hall, where it will be possible to apply for member-ship. No initiation fee will be charged for Sokols and the sons and daughters of members of our organization.
