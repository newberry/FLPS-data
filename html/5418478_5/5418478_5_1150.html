The meeting was held on Wednesday evening and the attendance was enormous. The minutes of the previous meeting were read and announcement made of the joining of five lodges as new members of the home association.

The representative of the "Ceska Vlast" (Bohemian Nation Club) turned in $86.25, profit from a dance given for the benefit of the home.

The receipts of the meeting were $737. If as much was received at each meeting, we would soon have the home and the orphanage in operation.
