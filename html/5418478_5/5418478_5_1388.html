The social and benevolent society, Plzenske Fesacky (Chic Women of Pilsen), arranged a surprise party for one of the members, Mrs. Berta Rydl, on April 11, 1922. The occasion served also for a collection which was made for the benefit of the Utulna a Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage). The proceeds, four dollars and fifty cents was deposited with us. One dollar by an anonymous giver was mailed to us to be transmitted to the Utulna.....
