Upon the request of the secretary of the Teleocvicna Jednota Sokol Chotebor (Sokol Chotebor Gymnastic Association), Czechoslovakia, I have undertaken a collection for the erection of a Sokolovna (Sokol Hall) at that place, to which the following countrymen have so far contributed: [There follow the names and amounts.] Altogether I have collected the sum of forty-one dollars. On September 20, I sent the sum three thousand Czechoslovakian kronen to Chotebor. In the name of the Sokol Chotebor, I most cordially thank all of you who contributed.

I would like to ask those countrymen who are still willing to contribute to the above-mentioned cause to send their gifts to Mr. Jan Matejka, 2345 South Kedzie Avenue, Chicago, Illinois.
