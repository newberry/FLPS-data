Our office received $16.35 for the benefit of the Brnenska Utulna (Old People's Home of Brno, [Czechoslovakia]). [Names of contributors are omitted in translation.]

On the occasion of the celebration of the silver wedding anniversary of Mr. and Mrs. Vaclav Heninger, Mesdames Josefa Kropik and B. Soucek made a collection for the benefit of the T. G. Masaryk Free Thought School of Cicero, Illinois. The total amount collected was $18.20.

For the benefit of the victims of the Fisk Street fire $2 was deposited with us by Mr. A. J. Martinek.
