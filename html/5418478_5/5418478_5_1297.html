At a postnuptial party arranged in honor of the bride and groom--Mr. and Mrs. Frank Cerny--at their home located at 2646 West 21st Street, a collection was made for the benefit of the Utulna a Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old Peoples Home and Orphanage). Ten dollars was collected, and was deposited with us by the groom's brother, Mr. Josef Cerny.

The following contributed to the Popelka Bilian fund: Mrs. Marie Janacek of New Bedford, Massachussetts, fifty cents; Mrs. Anna Princl-Krejci of Manitowoc, Wisconsin, fifty cents.
