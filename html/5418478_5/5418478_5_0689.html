On behalf of the Infant Welfare Society, the director of Dvorak Park wishes to thank all those who have contributed to the Society's fund for the benefit of sick children of poor families.

The total amount which the director of Dvorak Park has collected for this noble cause from individual Bohemians and philanthropic organizations amounts to $100.50.

[Translator's note: Among the contributors were twenty-eight firms, five lodges, and twenty-five individuals.]
