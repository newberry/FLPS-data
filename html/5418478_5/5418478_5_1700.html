Messrs. Karel Bersik and Joseph Miksik undertook a collection for the benefit of orphans and widows of fallen legionnaires of Pitin, near Bojkovice, Moravia, Czechoslovakia, and the following countrymen contributed: A. Janecka, $8; L. Miksik, $5; L. Manak, $5; L. Janecka, $5; F. Smola, $5; L. Pavelka, $5; [there follow the names and amounts of smaller contributors]. The total amount collected, one hundred dollars, was sent to its destination.
