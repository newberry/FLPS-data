Miss Ludmila Halik, a fourteen-year-old girl, whose parents reside at 2833 South Union Avenue, read in our newspaper a report about the sorrowful fate of a soldier, Mr. Alois Aubrecht of Komorany, Czechoslovakia. She undertook in his behalf a collection that totaled $20.72. [Names of contributors omitted in translation.] This amount was deposited in our office and will be sent to the unfortunate soldier.
