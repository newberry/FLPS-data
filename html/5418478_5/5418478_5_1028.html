We hereby present a financial report of the concert which was arranged for the benefit of the Ceska Utulna a Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage) on Wednesday, September 29, 1920, in the Pilsen Park pavilion.

Receipts:

For tickets sold at the door 48.50
For advertisements 677.50
For flowers 63.35
For coffee, cakes, etc. 34.75
For chips sold in the park 127.19
Gift of the Pilsen Brewing Company 50.00
Gift of the Svornost 6.00
Gift of Mr. Kotrba $5.00
Gift of Mr. J. F. Cihak 3.00

We hereby take the liberty to extend our gratitude to all those who helped in any way to make this concert a success.
