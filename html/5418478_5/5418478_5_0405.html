The semiannual report of the Jednota Ceskych Dam (Society of Bohemian Ladies) offers some very interesting data which shows the growth and progress of this important benevolent organization: During the past six months the Jednota took in 652 new members; there were 102 deaths, 46 resignations, 68 expulsions. Total membership is 20,786. The total amount of paid-out death benefits was $612,000 (sic); sick benefits, $12,313.67; contributions to various charities, $1,002.87; contributions to Bohemian schools, $1,613.63; to patriotic organizations, $1,001.95.

The reserve funds amount to $33,197.23; total assets, $344,040.50.
