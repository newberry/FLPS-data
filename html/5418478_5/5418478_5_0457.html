The semiannual report of the Jednota Ceskych Dam (Bohemian Ladies' Unity) contains a number of interesting facts which indicate the Jednota's popularity. There were 678 new members taken in during the past six months, 95 members died, 40 resigned, and 53 were expelled.

Death benefits paid out during that period amounted to $57,000; sick benefits, $10,613.64; charity donations, $1,711.20; donations to Bohemian schools, $1,421.75; and for various national purposes, $825.45. The Jednota has assets amounting to $325,984.37; its reserve fund is $29,079.92.
