The Spolek Skutecskych Rodaku a Okoli (Society of Natives from Skutec and Vicinity) in its past meeting appropriated the sum of seventy-five dollars which will be taken to Czechoslovakia by Mr. Otto Rychtarik, a member of the Society. Mr. Rychtarik has been instructed to divide the gift as follows: The sum of twenty-five dollars shall be given to the Telocvicna Jednota Sokol Skutec; the sum of twenty-five dollars, to disabled soldiers; and the sum of twenty-five dollars to school children, all of the Town of Skutec, Czechoslovakia.
