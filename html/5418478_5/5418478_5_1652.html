Mr. Vladimir Kriz, a principal of the agricultural college of Bystrice nad Perstynem, Czechoslovakia, is appealing to a great number of countrymen from Bystrice residing in Chicago to contribute toward the fund for the erection of a long-proposed Sokolovna (Sokol Hall). All contributions will be published, and the total list of donors will be sent to the proper place. Send your contributions to Mr. A. Prosecky, 1536 West 18th Street, Chicago, Illinois.

Mr. A. Prosecky contributed the sum of $10.
