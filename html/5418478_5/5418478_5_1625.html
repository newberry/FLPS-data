A new regulation was introduced in the restaurant and coffeehouse managed by Mr. Karel Psenicka, 1651 Blue Island Avenue. Under this new ruling, any guest who happens to throw a ball off the billiard table while playing a game of billiards shall pay a voluntary penalty of five cents. Every nickel collected in this manner shall be donated toward Hoover's relief fund for the benefit of children in Czechoslovakia.

In the course of two weeks, the sum of $2.50 was collected in this manner. The above-mentioned amount was deposited in our office, and we were requested to send this, together with other contributions, to Mr. Hoover.
