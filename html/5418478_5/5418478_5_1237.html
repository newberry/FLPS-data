Mr. and Mrs. Jan Vicha, former Chicagoans, now residing in Braidwood, Illinois, celebrated their silver wedding anniversary last Saturday. To that celebration a great many of their Chicago friends and acquaintances were invited.....Mr. Vlach suggested, in the midst of the festivities, that a collection be made for the benefit of the Utulna A Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage). The collection netted $25.
