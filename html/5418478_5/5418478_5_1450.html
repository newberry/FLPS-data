The annual meeting of the stockholders of the Garden City Brewery was held recently. In this meeting an election of the board of directors and officers of the company took place. Most of the old officers were re-elected.

During the meeting, various charitable and national organizations were remembered. One hundred dollars was set aside as a gift to the Ceska Utulna A Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage in Lisle, Illinois). The Ceska Dobrocinna Spolecnost (Bohemian Charitable Association)Received $25 and the Narodni Dan (National Tax) received the same amount. At this time the unfortunate Vilem Koukola was also remembered with a gift of $25.
