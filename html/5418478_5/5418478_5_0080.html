We want one thousand men and women who would like to become members of a new lodge of the Cesko-Slovanske Podporujici Spolky (Czecho-Slavonic Benevolent Societies) which is being organized now.

The physical examination is free, and there is no initial fee charged. Therefore, let everybody come and bring his friends. Make use of this opportunity!

Apply at the following places: J. Veselka, 2535 South Crawford Avenue; A. Hass, 2524 South Spaulding Avenue; A. Dvorak, 1747 West Roosevelt Road; A. Leitner, 2508 South 58th Court, Cicero, Illinois; Fr. Blaha, 1305 South Komensky Avenue; J. Horna, 1301 South 56th Avenue, Cicero, Illinois.
