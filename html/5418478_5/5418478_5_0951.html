The gifts and contributions received by the office of the Utulna a Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage) from March 1, 1922 to April 1, 1922 are hereby acknowledged. [Translator's note: The names, addresses and other identifications of contributors and the amounts contributed in each case are omitted in translation. An entire column was devoted to this information. The grand total was $1,776.06. The amounts were deposited with the Denni Hlasatel and the Svornost, which transmitted them to the Utulna; others were mailed directly.] The Utulna is grateful to all the generous givers and asks for continued favors.....

Mr. Bohumil Fiser, Business Manager.
