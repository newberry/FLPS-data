An important meeting of the United Societies of Chicago was held last night in the Bismarck Kotel.....

All of the present officers were re-elected by acclamation, in spite of their reluctance to accept their offices again.....

The United Societies of Chicago are entering the new year of administration under the presidency of Mr. George Landau....with Mr. Anton J. Cermak, secretary; Mr. K. Rosestiel, treasurer; and Mr. Leopold Neuman, organizer.....
