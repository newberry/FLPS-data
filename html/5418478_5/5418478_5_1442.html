The following contributions were deposited with us: From the ladies of the social and benevolent Klub Ceske Dobracky (Club of Bohemian Goodfellows), $2.50 for the benefit of the Utulna A Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage). Mrs. Marie Matousek, 2466 Blue Island Avenue, deposited the sum of $2.50 with us for the benefit of the Sirotcinec Svateho Josefa V Lisle (St. Joseph's Orphanage in Lisle, Illinois). There were contributions of twenty-five cents and fifty cents sent to us, the former for the Alice Masaryk fund, the latter for the benefit of the starving Russian children.
