To honor the memory of the deceased Mrs. Fanny Faraizl of Ennis, Texas, who was a correspondent of the Denni Hlasatel, her friend Mrs. Toman, also one of our correspondents, deposited with us the sum of three dollars for the benefit of the Utulna A Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage). The management of the aforesaid institution is requested to make arrangements to collect this amount together with other money deposited with us in its behalf.
