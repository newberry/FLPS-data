The board of directors of the Ceska Dobrocinna Spolecnost (Bohemian Charitable Association) held a meeting last Friday in its offices at 2603 South Kedzie Avenue. The meeting was opened by the chairman of the ladies' auxiliary, Mrs. Josefa Beranek. The minutes of the last meeting were read and approved. The meeting was later attended by the first vice-president of the Ceska Dobrocinna Spolecnost, Mr. John A. Sokol, who took the chair for the remainder of the meeting. It was decided to abandon the idea of having an independent tag day and instead to become a member of the Universal Tag Day Association. It was further decided that a campaign for contributions from the members of the Charitable Association should take place in June instead of in January. Upon a motion made by Mr. Anton J. Cermak, it was decided to make a regular card index of all members and contributors. The next annual meeting will take place on the third Friday in February. After listening to the reading of the annual report on the activities of the newly elected members of the board, an announcement was made about a soiree to be given for all members.

The treasurer, Mr. Malecek, reported that the the disbursements of the Ceska Dobrocinna Spolecnost for the past year amounted to $10,000 in cash and $2,000 in gifts, totaling $12,000. Thus, the disbursements of the Ceska Dobrocinna Spolecnost average $1,000 per month. The chairman of the ladies' auxiliary, Mrs. Josefa Beranek, reported that the auxiliary will turn over its surplus from last year, amounting to $1,600, which will be deposited in the joint treasury. The president thanked the auxiliary for their exemplary work and for this monetary contribution. The ladies, under the chairmanship of Mrs. Beranek and with the co-operation of other officers, worked diligently throughout the year.

The following gentlemen were elected to the nominating committee: Messrs. Anton J. Cermak, Frank G. Hajicek, and Josef Bolek. Collections in this meeting amounted to seventeen dollars.
