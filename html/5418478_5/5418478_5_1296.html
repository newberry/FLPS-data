The superintendent of the Brnenska Utulna (Old Peoples Home at Brno [Czechoslovakia]), Miss Marie Steskal, the directress of that institution, Mrs. Marie Slavik, and its accountant, Miss Marie Lazinka, sent us a letter of thanks dated September 28. In this letter they acknowledged the receipt of 365 [Czechoslovak] crowns sent to the aforesaid institution by the readers of the Denni Hlasatel. The management of the institution heartily thanks all the generous contributors who have thus remembered it.
