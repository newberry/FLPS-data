At the annual banquet arranged on Saturday night, February 11, by the Joseph Triner Company [a Bohemian enterprise] for its employees, to whom it wished to show its appreciation for their conscientious work, the unfortunate poor who need our help were not forgotten.

During the height of the entertainment, Mr. Lupe, who is a shipping clerk for this concern, suggested that a collection be made for our orphanages. The collection netted $40. Of this sum, one half was sent yesterday to the Sirotcinec Svateho Josefa V Lisle (St. Joseph's Orphanage in Lisle, Illinois) and one half to the Ceska Utulna A Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old Peoples Home and Orphanage) in Chicago.
