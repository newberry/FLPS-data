The convention of the Cesko-Slovanska Bratrska Podporující Jednota (Bohemian-Slavonic Fraternal Benevolent Society) held only a morning session yesterday.....

The delegates remembered various national organizations with donations and voted, among others, a gift of twenty-five dollars to the Cesko-Americka Tiskova Kancelar (Bohemian-American Press Bureau), seventy-five dollars for the Ceska Utulna A Sirotcinec (Bohemian Old People's Home and Orphanage), and fifty dollars to the Buckeye Road Bohemian Free Thought School in Cleveland, Ohio, which, until now, had functioned in the building of a public school, but through pressure exerted by certain parties, had been evicted.....

The afternoon was devoted to a sightseeing tour of the city.....
