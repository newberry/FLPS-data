Assisted by the Hartmann Lyric Society, the female singing society, Illinois, gave a concert Saturday evening in Wicker Park Hall.

H. C. Schmidt's Orchestra opened the concert with Lebar's overture to "The Gay Widow," a lively operetta which has won great acclaim in Norway and Denmark.

The main interest in the concert was the fact that Miss Clara Jensen, the soprano, was the director of the Illinois Singing Society. She did her work very well.

After the concert, there was dancing.
