The Bjorgvin Singing Society held its annual program in Bjorgvin Hall, 876 West North Avenue. The Bjorgvin Ladies Chorus, Hans Schmidt's orchestra, Mr. August Anderson, Mr. Henry Anderson, Olaf Olsen, and Grace Svensen assisted in the program.
