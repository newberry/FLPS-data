The Bjorgvin Singing Society has reached the ripe old age of forty years. As usual, on its anniversary the singers give one of their outstanding concerts, and for those people who worship at the shrine of the god of melody, the concerts are a treat.

Our soloists who appear on the program prove to us time and time again that the Scandinavians not only love music but live in an atmosphere of melody and song. By that we mean that whenever you or I attend affairs given by any one of the many Scandinavian clubs or organizations, we always find some music or song on the program.
