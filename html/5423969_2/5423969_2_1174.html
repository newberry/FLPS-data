S. S. Xania arrived in Chicago directly from Norway. It took thirty-five days to cross and carried a cargo of three thousand three hundred barrels of herring. It was chartered by the Chicago Export firm, O. A. Thorp & Co. The captain of Xania is Mr. Johannesen.

It is a great thing that we now have direct communication between Norway and Chicago. It is the second arrival of a non stop steamer from Norway to Chicago and the best news is that it is a paying proposition.
