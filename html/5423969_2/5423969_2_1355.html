The talented painter, Dorothy Anderson, has opened her art exhibition at her studio, 3314 Wrightwood Avenue.

Miss Anderson paints still life, and her "Flowers" are of the best. She has done one or two marine paintings which have been exhibited at the Art Institute.....She was appointed teacher, some time ago, at the Art Institute.....

Her teachers were Vanderpool, Freer, Charles Francis Brown, and J. O. Carol......

She received honorable mention on several occasions at the Art Institute.
