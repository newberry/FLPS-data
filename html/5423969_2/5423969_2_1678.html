The Lake View Norwegian Lutheran Church gave a concert yesterday evening, at the Belmont Auditorium. The artists presented were: Miss Marie Helgesen; The Imperial Quartette; W. J. Brown, tenor; J. S. Buce, second tenor; B. T. Tufts, baritone; J. M. Worthington, basso; Miss Bremmer, alto; and several lesser lights.
