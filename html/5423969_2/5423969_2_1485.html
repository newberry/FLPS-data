The Norwegian Ladies' Singing Society, Grieg gave its second great annual concert Sunday October 14. All the seats in the great hall was taken, and the enthusiasm was great. In their beautiful Norwegian national costumes and under the leadership of their director Mrs. S. Huseby the ladies won their public from the very start and earned great applause. The ladies' chorus was ably assisted by a number of soloists, singers and readers, and also a piano soloist. In addition, the Norwegian Glee Club gave two splendid numbers, and the concert ended by the joint execution of a couple of beautiful numbers by the singing societies, "Heimdahl" and "Grieg."
