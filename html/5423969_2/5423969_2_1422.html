Last Tuesday, the Scandinavian Students' Singing Society was organized, by twenty student singers, at the Chicago Norwegian Club. The following well-known men were elected on the executive board: Harry Kellerman, president; Per Wendelbo, first vice-president; Professor Bjornson, second vice-president; S.H.S. Leonard, secretary; J. H. Hille, treasurer; master of ceremonies, Hjalmar Lundquist; librarian, Mr. Boberg.
