p.8........Three of our young Norwegian-Americans are about to start on a trip Westward earning their way by a series of entertainments on route. Miss Alice Walden, pianist, Miss Eleanora Olsen, vocalist and Miss Ethel Olsen (a sister), comedienne in Norwegian-American dialect readings. The young ladies are very talented in their respective lines and should score a success at every stop, making the trip one of both pleasure and profit.
