The Norwegian Singer's League will give an open-air concert tomorrow in Riverview Park Grove. The league includes the Norwegian Glee Club, Norwegian Singers' Society Bjorgvin, the Larch, and the Norske Klub's Singing Society. In addition, the Singing Society Harmonien, directed Emil Biorn, will participate.

A large and festively decorated printed program entitled "Laata" has been issued for the occasion.
