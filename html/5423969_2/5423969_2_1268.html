The Mendelssohn Conservatory of Music celebrated its fifth anniversary last night in Kimball Hall. The well-known first violinist, Hjalmar Rabe, a member of the faculty, has been a member of the Thomas Orchestra for twenty-five years. The director of the Orchestra is Alfred Holmes; he is fast becoming the outstanding orchestra leader in Chicago.
