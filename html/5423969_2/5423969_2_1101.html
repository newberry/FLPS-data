Ole C. Land & Company will sell their stock of jewelry, etc., at auction.

Ole C. Land & Comapny having conducted a jewelry business in Chicago for 38 years, most of this time on Milwaukee Avenue, now intends to close out the whole stock.

Mr. Land intends to retire from active business, and is giving his customers the advantage of getting $25,000.00 worth of jewelry for such prices as they wish to pay. A souvenir will be given with every sale.
