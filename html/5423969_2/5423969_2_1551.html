The Scandinavian Socialist Singing Society held its regular fall concert last Saturday at Wicker Park Hall. The following artists and organizations appeared on the program: Miss Irma Brynelson, pianist; Andrew Ratzer, violinist; the Danish Workers Singing Society, under the direction of Rolf Hammer; the German Socialist Singers League; and Lawson's Orchestra.

Robert Korsang sang "Sangen Har Lysning" (The Light of Song), "Lovsrring" (Spring Buds), and, as usual, the "Marseillaise". Rolf Hammer and the Danish Workers Singing Society sang the well-known "Arbeiter Marsch" and the "Socialist March".
