Rolf Hammer, Carsten Woll, and H. Roren, well-known Norwegian singers, can now be heard on Victor Records. The following reproductions are available:

b) "The Norwegian Flag" H. Roren
2. a) "Ja Vi Elsker" (Yes, We Love the Land) C. Woll
b) "Syng Mig Hjem" (Sing Me Home) C. Woll
3. a) "Gamle Norge" (Old Norway) Rolf Hammer
b) "For Norge" (For Norway) Hammer
4. a) "Mainat" (Night in May) C. Woll
b) "En Svane" (A Swan) C. Woll
