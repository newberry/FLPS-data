Ouse & Peterson Co., the Norwegian-American importing cncern of Chicago do not confine its imports to foods. The Zoo, at Milwaukee recently welcomed four polar bear cubs obtained through Ouse & Peterson Co.

The Ouse & Peterson Co., has connections in Aalesund, Norway, with whaling concerns active around Greenland. The whalers secure the polar bears on order from the Ouse & Peterson Co., through the Aalesund concern. The Ouse & Peterson Co. is being accused of an attempted popularizing of polars for meat.
