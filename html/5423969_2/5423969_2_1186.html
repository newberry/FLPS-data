Mrs. Josephine Mamen, of the Norwegian colony, sails April 25th on the Hellig Olav for a visit to Kristiania and Fredrikshald, after which she will visit Sweden, Denmark, England, and France. Mrs. Mamen is a well-known artist whose specialty is painting on porcelain, and on this trip she intends to study the latest methods and motifs of foreign lands. Her daughter will accompany her.
