p.5...Dr. A. Hektoen, who practiced medicine in Chicago for a number of years, passed away, at the age of forty-three, at the Kankakee Insane Asylum, where for several years he had been a member of the medical staff.

Dr. Hektoen was born in Wisconsin, and was a brother of Prof. Ludvig Hektoen. He was an outstanding physician, and a man looked up to by all. His wonderful personality will be sadly missed by a host of friends.
