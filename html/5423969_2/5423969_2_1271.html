Miss Bronten presented an excellent program last night at the Humboldt Park Parish House. She was assisted by Miss Marie Helgesen, singer; John Dybdahl, violinist; and Mrs. Selma Ansan and Miss Alyse DeMuth, pianists.

The artists on this program are well-known in the concert world, not only here in Chicago but from coast to coast.
