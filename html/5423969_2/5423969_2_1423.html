Now comes the seventeenth Landssangerfest [National Song Festival] of the Norwegian Singers' Association of America, under the auspices of the Norwegian Singers' League of Chicago. The Festival will last two days, May 28 and 29. We write ever so often that this or that is the best yet, but every year our singing societies improve in every sense of the word. We must say again, "this is the best." The following program will prove our statement.
