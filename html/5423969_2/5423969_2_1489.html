During the Scandinavian exhibit at the Chicago Art Institute the house was packed from morning till night, indicating the public appreciation of art. What a grand thing it would be if twenty thousand Scandinavians could meet at Riverview Park on July 29 and listen to the Scandinavian singers, five hundred strong.

Let us call the public attention also to our music and show them, both at home and abroad, that the Scandinavians can work in harmony for any good cause.
