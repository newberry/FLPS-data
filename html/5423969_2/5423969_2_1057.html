Wilfred Holmboe and wife, nee Signe Gerner, came to Chicago, yesterday, from Colorado. Their little boy died in Colorado, and for that reason they decided to leave the western state. Together with his brother, Harold, Mr. Wilfred Holmboe, has bought Temple Garage at Robey St., and Irving Park Blvd., where they have already started in the automobile business. The garage and automobile business was bought from an estate. Attorney Andrew Hummeland made the legal arrangements connected with the transfer.
