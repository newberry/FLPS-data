p.8. Birger Osland, prominent in the Chicago Norwegian colony, has resigned as general manager of the Chicago Heights Land Association. On November 1, he assumed the position of agent for the General Western Norwegian-American Line and has established offices in the Marquette Building at Dearborn and Adams Streets. On the occasion of his resignation from the Chicago Heights Land Association he was presented by President Chas. H. Wacker (of the association) with a fine gold watch.
