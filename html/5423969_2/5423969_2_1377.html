The Norwegian Theater will produce Sigurd Ibsen's, "Erindringens Tempel" (The Temple of Memories), with Mrs. Olivia Bakke, Consul Stillesen, and Hiss in the leading roles.

A second play, on which rehearsals have started, Gustav Nielsen's "Pea Familie-Bal" (The Family Ball), will be presented with Miss Bjergsjo and Sigurd Thomle heading the cast.
