The following Scandinavians were elected to offices in Chicago banks:

State Bank of Chicago: vice-president, Henry A. Haugan; assistant cashier, C. E. Carlson; directors, H. G. Haugan, A. Lanquist [Swede], William A. Petersen [Dane], and J. J. Dau.

Union Bank of Chicago: cashier, G. Hallbom [Swede]; directors, John M. Erickson, Charles W. Johnson [Swede], Hans P. Johnson, Tom Olsen [Dane].

Northwest State Bank: directors, Alfred Anderson [Dane], Peter Orbesen [Dane], Peter J. Reisenhus [Dane].

Pioneer State Bank: president, A. H. Greenberg; directors, C. N. Strom, and T. A. Siqueland.

Second Security Bank of Chicago: president, Charles H. Meyer [Dane]; vice-president, J. C. Hansen [Dane]; assistant cashier, Martin J. Grau; director, Thorvald Johnson.

Security Bank of Chicago: Meyer and Hansen, as above; director, T. Johnson.
