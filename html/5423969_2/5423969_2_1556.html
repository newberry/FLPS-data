The Norwegian Glee Club won second place at the International Singers' Congress last Monday, Labor Day. Fifteen singing societies participated, representing eight different nationalities. The Richard Wagner Maennchor, a German chorus, won the first prize, and the Danish Workers' Singing Society was third.

More than twenty thousand people attended this festival. We should have more of these international get-togethers. Alfred Paulsen [Dane] directed the Norwegian Glee Club, and he was, as usual, the outstanding director of the entire group.
