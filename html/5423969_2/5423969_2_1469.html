A Grand Sacred Concert was held at the Christ Norwegian Lutheran Church last Friday. J. Rode Jacobsen, the well-known organist, and Mathias Pedersen, the cornet soloist, were the principal performers. The program was as follows:

1. a) March from "Sigurd Jorsalfar" Grieg
b) "In the Morning" from "Peer Gynt" Grieg

J. Rode Jacobsen, Organist

2. "Praise Ye the Father" Gounod

Christ Church Chorus

3. Fantasie, "Nearer My God to Thee" Belstedt

M. Pedersen, Violinist

J. Rode Jacobsen, Organist

4. "Palm Branches" Faure

Soprano solo with cornet obligato

5. "The Morning Star" Wetterling

Christ Church Quartet

2
