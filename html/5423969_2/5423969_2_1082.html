p.8.......Union Bank of Chicago, a Norwegian-American institution, has purchased the property at 25 Dearborn, which it has occupied since its organization. They have also leased the ground on which the building stands for one hundred ninety-eight years at an annual rental of $30,000 during the first five years, $32,000, the second five years, $34,000, the third five year period, and $36,000 during the balance of the lease term.

The Union Bank is a highly recommended banking institution, and its service to the community has won the confidence of all.
