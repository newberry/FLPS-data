p.5. Doctor Boyesen, well-known throughout the Chicago-Norwegian colony, has placed a new article on the market. He has for some time devoted a lot of thought on the subject of producing an article that not only appealed to the sweet tooth of the public, but possessed usefulness and benefit as well. The result is what he calls "Tooth Violets," a candy that contains ingredients beneficial to the teeth and gums. "Tooth Violets" are on sale at drug stores throughout Chicago.
