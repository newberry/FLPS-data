The Norwegian Methodist Deaconess Home at 3261 Cortland Street will celebrate its 8th anniversary tomorrow with a social affair in the Kedzie Avenue Methodist Episcopal Church. The Rev. E. O. Sellers, who has been working for the Y. M. C. A. among the soldiers in France, will speak on "The Life of Our Soldiers in the Trenches in France." There will also be other speakers, and a musical program.
