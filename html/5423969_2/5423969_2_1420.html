Under the leadership of Mr. Joel Mossberg, the singing society, "Harmony," gave a concert Saturday evening in Schoenhofen's Hall. The concert opened with "Overture to Martha," played by H. C. Schmidt's Orchestra. Pianist Eugene Lyunggren sang "Caprice Norwegienne," for which he was warmly applauded. Miss Clara Jensen sang Strauss' "Prince Vera" and Hartman's "Cradle Song."

Joel Mossberg, the director sang Schuman's "Grenadierma" and "Den Rode Nase" (The Red Nose).
