An outstanding Norwegian concert was [recently] given at Cedric Hall under the auspices of the Norwegian National League.

The League went to great expense in obtaining the artists, and of course [hiring] Cedric Hall was quite an expense.

Ole Theobaldi, and Mlle. Helene Kellere and Mme. Stitzel of the Metropolitan Opera Company, were the headliners on the program, which was as follows:
