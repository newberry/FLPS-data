Club West, a ladies' organization, announces that its annual benefit concert for the Children's Home in Edison Park, will be held at Bjorgvins Hall, on May 5th, at [gap]. Club West always puts on worth-while concerts, and Scandia urges its readers to the sure to attend, not only for the sake of the national treat, but for the little ones the are to benefit from the affair. This is Club West's 3th annual concert for this purpose and they are to be of commended for their work.
