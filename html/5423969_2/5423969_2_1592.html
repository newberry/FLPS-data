Last night Bjorsvin Hall was filled with a great mass of happy people. This was a gala night; the Scandinavian Socialist Singing Society presented its annual concert. As we entered the Hall we heard the strains of a stirring workers' march, played by Ratzer's orchestra.

The active group of singers, under the direction of Rolf Hammer, gave us one "musical surprise" after another. This group sang the songs of freedom for the workers, seemingly, with a hope that some day the workers would obtain economic freedom.
