The Norwegian Society for a Home for the Aged sponsored a concert last night in Wicker Park Hall. Dr. N. T. Quales, after eighteen years of activity in the Society, was elected honorary president. He was highly commended in a short speech by Mrs. Julia Walther.

Mr. Thorvald Woll, who is recognized as a great tenor, sang a large number of songs.....Mr. Woll was for years a member of the Student Singers of Christiania.

The well-arranged program is again a credit to Norwegians; it shows that they are not only music lovers, but that they are the intelligentsia of Northern Europe. The program was as follows:

b) "Chimes of Normandy" Planquette
