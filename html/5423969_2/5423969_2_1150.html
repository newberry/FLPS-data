Karl Michelsen, 804 Grand Avenue has made a model of a flying gas baloon which is far superior to anything on the market. His faith in flying is great; he states that in the near future he will fly to the north pole. He is offering his baloon to Fridtjof Nansen.

The model is three meters in length and has three propellers and a rudder. The three propellers can be used for driving power and for steering purposes. It can turn left and right, up and down.

Karl Michelsen was born in Norway; he is twenty-seven years old and has been in America only three or four years.
