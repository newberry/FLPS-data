Now the Norsemen's Singing Society has surpassed itself in concerts. The Society gave a concert a few days ago that was considered by music lovers to be the outstanding concert of the year. The program was as follows:

1. Potpourri of Scandinavian Melodies Anton Pedersen Orchestra, Anton Pederson, Director
2. a) "Aftenstemning Reissiger
b) "Vaar Gjaede" Ahrenssen Norsemen's Singing Society, Christian Nilson, Director
3. Sextette from "Lucia" Cheshire Mr. Walfried Singer
4. "Solveig's Song" Edvard Grieg Miss Anna Nelson, Soprano
5. "The Young Man Waited" Russell (Sung for the first time in Chicago) Miss Portia Carness
6. "Hunters Chorus" from the opera, "The Poacher" Weber Chorus and Orchestra Norsemen's Singing Society, Christian Nilson, Director
7. Overture Leautner Orchestra, Anton Pedersen, Director
8. a) "The Wedding in Hardanger" Kjaerulf
b) "Pelle Goes A-courting" Kuntze Norsemen's Singing Society, C. Nilson, Director
9. "Nearer My God to Thee" Mr. Walfried Singer
10. "Min Sol" D. Capula Miss Anna Nelson
11. Scene from "Ben Hur" Wallace Miss Portia Carnes
12. "Missere" from "Il Trovatore" Verdi Duet, Miss Anna Nelson and Mr. Charles C. Hansen

Grand Chorus and Orchestra

Norsemen's Singing Society,

Christian Nilson, Director
