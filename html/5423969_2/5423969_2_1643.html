The Bjorgvin Singing Society held its annual concert last night in Bjorgvin Hall, 1639-41 North Artesian Avenue. The Swedish Ladies' Quartet also appeared on the program and was so well received by the audience that the ladies were compelled to give several encores.

Joel Mossberg, director of the Bjorgvin Chorus, was especially honored...
