The Oselio and the Grieg Singing Societies have combined. This amalgamation has increased the number of active singers and facilitated the formation of a large chorus, a chorus that will, we are sure, make a name for itself.

A concert was given by this group last Sunday at the Logan Square Auditorium. The program was as follows:
