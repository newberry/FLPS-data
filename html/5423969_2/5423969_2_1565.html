P. 4-"Club West", in faithful adherence to its principles announce an annual concert for the benefit of the Norwegian Children's Home at Edison Park, Illinois.

The concert will be given on May 11th at Bjorgoin Hall at 2:30 P.M. Assisting will be the Socialist Singers, with solos by their director, Rolf Hammer, Nora and Ethel Olser, Mrs. Dr. Young and Hartlein Thinn. Let us all support "Club West," in this laudable effort to help.
