p.5... Prof. Chas. Stangeland, formerly a well-known member of Chicago's Norwegian colony, who, during recent years has been a member of the faculty of the State University at Olympia, Washington, stopped off to visit Chicago on his way to a new appointment.

Prof. Stangeland has taught political economy at the University of Washington, and has recently been appointed Secretary of the American Legation in Bolivia; he will take up his duties in March. Scandia congratulates.
