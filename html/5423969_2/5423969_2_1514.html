The Christ Church gave an organ concert last night. The program included the following numbers: "March" by Semmens, "The Nun's Song" by Lefebure-Wely, "Humoresque" and "Silhouette" by Dvorak, and "Prayer Lullaby" and "March Religieuse" by Geaulmants. Reverend Kittelsby gave a short, interesting talk on Lutheran Church music.
