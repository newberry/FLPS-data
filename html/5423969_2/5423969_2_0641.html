Halvdan Koht has just published a new book. The title is ultra-modern, Social Democratie (Social Democracy).

The book is filled with new ideas....The content are as follows: Background; Class Struggle; The New Order; Revolt of the Workers; The Revolution of 1848; The Communist Manifesto; LaSalle and the German Social-Democratic Party; The Internationale; Germany; France; Great Britain; The Scandinavian Countries; Syndicalism; The Social-Democratic Parties Will Capitulate.

This book should be read by every student of political economy. It is an analysis of the political world.
