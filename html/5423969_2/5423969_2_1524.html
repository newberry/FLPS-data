June 5, 6, and 7 will be red letter days for the Chicago Norwegian colony. On June 5, the great singing Festival will open, a festival in which singing groups from all over the United States and Canada will participate. There will be a combined chorus of over one thousand voices. Emil Bjorn will be the principal director, and we look forward to the part of the program when he will direct the combined choruses.
