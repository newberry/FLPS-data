President James B. Thorsen, of the long-established firm of Thorsen & Passady, formerly dealing in sports articles on Wabash Avenue,....has applied in the United States District Court for release from a debt of $225,539. The assets of the firm amount to $19,000, mainly consisting of the stock of the firm, Thorsen Company, of which he is president. The Company is dealing in bicycles at 56 Fifth Avenue.
