A Golden Wedding was celebrated on Thanksgiving day, November 30, by Captain Martin A. Gundersen in his home at 100 Park Avenue. There were over one hundred guests as the Captain and his wife are very prominent settlers.

Both Mr. and Mrs. Gundersen were born in Norway. He came to Chicago in 1848; she came in 1853. They were married in 1853. The Captain is not only a shipmaster but also posseses several ships. He was harbormaster for eight years.
