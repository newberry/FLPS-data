On Labor Day, September 7, a great festival has been arranged in Soldier's Field and the Mexican societies have been invited to take part. The Hidalgo and Dominquez societies accepted and will represent the Mexican group. They count on the Consular's support. Miss Chiquita Rangel distinquished Mexican artist will be in charge of organizing and presenting our group.

Our people will be the tenth in the festival and we know what a tremendous interest this invitation has created in the colony.
