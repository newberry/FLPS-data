A delegation representing the Confederation de Trabajadores Mexicanos of Mexico, gave a conference under the auspices of El Frente Popular Mexicano, at Union Hall 777 West Adams St. About two hundred persons attended the meeting; it was presided over by Leon Lira, general secretary. The delegates were Dr. Manuel Villasenor, Eduardo Inez, and Dr. Rafael Carrillo, who spoke concerning the progress made in the labor movement and the conditions prevailing now in Mexico. Several questions were asked and answered satisfactorily to the listeners. The Mexican Consul Antonio Schmidt and the Vice-Consul Manuel Aguilar were among those present.
