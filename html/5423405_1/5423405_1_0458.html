The inauguration celebration of the Spanish Flag took place yesterday, Friday, July 7th, on the World's Fair Grounds.

Besides the Honorable Consul of Spain, Mr. Sebastian Romeo, were present the, Sociedad Espanola of Chicago, the Union Espanola of Gary, Indiana, as well as numerous prominent persons of the Spanish Colony of Chicago.

Mr. Rufus Dawes, president of the World's Fair Exposition, as well as the Fair's Exposition Committee, took part in the inauguration.
