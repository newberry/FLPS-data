Three young Mexicans, Pascual Servin, Gilberto Jaime and Antonio Ojeda, who are members of The Old Town Boys' Club, 1300 Newberry Avenue, received the first prize in Brass Hammering, during a competition which was held at Washington, D. C.

More than 250 "Boys' Clubs" resident in the United States, competed in this contest.

Two Mexican teachers, Mr. Emeterio Mendoza, 1313 Newberry Avenue, and Antonio Servin, 1236 Newberry Avenue who have a great interest in these boys, supervised their work.
