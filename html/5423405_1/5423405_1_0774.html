Interview: Secretary, Henry Booth House, 14th Pl. & Union Ave. Canal 1672.

This Community Center founded 38 years, houses the activities of Poles, Mexicans, Lithuanians, Jews and Russians. Its head assistant is Miss Meisner. There are two Societies that hold meetings in the building, also Spanish classes, dramatic groups, and a library of 100 books, mostly fiction and a few in history.
