Two men whom it is believed, robbed Frank Gosieski of 40 chickens last Wednesday night, were arrested yesterday with two women. When approached,they were eating a chicken dinner in their home, at 10722 Torrence Avenue. The four were taken to the police station where Gosieki said that the chickens, found in the yard of the robbers' home, were his. Those apprehended were Remigio Trevino, 30 years old; Louis Rottus, 26 years old; Mrs. Amelia Orvontiv, 26 years old and Mrs. Isabel Cervantes, 35 years of age. The accused maintained that they bought the chickens in May of this year.
