The Peruvians in Chicago number about fifty and came seeking higher education. Due to reverses of economical conditions at home some of them got jobs and entered industry. He states that most of them go back to Peru after they obtain their purpose. The majority come from the better class of their respective communities.

The Peruvian is individualist does not love music, singing or sports, so they do not have clubs, societies etc. A club was formed and dissolved the day it was formed.
