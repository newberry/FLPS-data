Francisco Juarez, 29 years old, was conveyed last Thursday evening, to the South Chicago Hospital in a very serious condition, as a result of fighting his fellow-roomer Salvador R. Gutierrez.

Juarez and Gutierrez lived in the same room at 8436 Greenbay Avenue, and according to the aggressor's statement, Juarez came home intoxicated and started abusing him.

Juarez has two wounds in one of his arms, one in the chest near the heart and one in the back.

The police of South Chicago seized the attacker a few minutes after the tragedy, he told the authorities that he had acted in self defense.
