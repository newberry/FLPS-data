The Fireman House's Board of Directors most cordially invites you and your friends interested in the work the Church is conducting in behalf of the Mexicans to visit Fireman House and St. Mark's Church in the new location at 1213 Gilpen Place (near Racine and Taylor) at 3:00 P.M. on Sunday, November 29th, 1931, in honor of the relocation of this work.
