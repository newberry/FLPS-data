EXECUTIVE BOARD

President - Emilio Garay

Secretary - A. Diaz

Treasurer - Jose Garcia

Note: They inform us that the clubs Mayas and Flechas will celebrate a ballet tomorrow at the Lincoln Hall, located at 91st and Commercial Avenue.

It is expected that the ballet will be entertaining to everybody.

There will also be as an added attraction, two games of basket-ball \2which will be played before the ballet begins.
