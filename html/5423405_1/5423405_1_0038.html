About 450 Mexican children attend this school, ages ranging from 5 to 16 years old. They get schooling up to the 8th grade. The language background handicaps these children a lot but in the average they are alert, artistic, and musically inclined. 3% of the pupils are irregular in attendance. Compared with other national groups as the Polish, they are not below then in their I. Q. The parent-teacher association is in the process of formation and the prospects are it will be a success.
