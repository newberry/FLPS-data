The Sociedad Espanola is planning a fiesta for Saturday, November 9th. The two outstanding attractions of the program will be the drama "Amor Salvaje," and a dance.

The actors taking part in the presentation of this drama are well known among the public as a result of the fine performances in which they have participated before.

The dance music will be furnished by Los Diablos Rojos (The Red Devils,) an orchestra which is well known to all dance lovers.
