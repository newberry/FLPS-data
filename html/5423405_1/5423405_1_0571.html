Beginning April 8th, and continuing every Wednesday and Friday from 9:00 A. M. to 9:15 A.M., thereafter, there will be heard over station W.G.N., the Chicago Tribune station, a program of Mexican music given under the auspicies of the United Fruit Company. For this purpose the Tipica Garcia orchestra has been engaged.

It is composed of two mandolins, a mandola, a mandocello, a guitar, a banjo and violins.
