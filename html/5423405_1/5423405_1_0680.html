The foot-ball team, The Pan-American, defeated The Italian Republic last Sunday. This match was the most important of the season.

The Italian people were surprised, because they thought that their opponents were novices, instead of efficient and could be easily defeated.

The public applauded the Italians in the beginning, but later favored the Mexicans.

The heroes among the Mexicans were: Ranos, Robles and Gallardo.
