The new orchestra Tipica Mexicana was organized to-day at the Chicago University Settlement. It is made up of distinguished young women who are lovers of music. The orchestra was organized by the well known artist Pablo Baquero under the auspicies of the University of Chicago Settlement.

Among the artists who are members of the orchestra we wish to mention, Misses Anita Munoz, Maria La Garibay, Manuelita Franco, Carmen Flores, Magarita Guzman and Juanita Martinez.
