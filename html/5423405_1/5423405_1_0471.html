A festival will be carried out by the Mexican Kermess, to-day at 7:30 P.M., at The Mexican Social Center, 1335 Newberry Avenue.

The program includes the following artists: The Morales sisters, Silvano Ramos, tenor; Leopoldo Escabarete, Salvador Mendoza and Professor Justino Sanchez, who will play the piano.

This festival is for the Mexican children's benefit.
