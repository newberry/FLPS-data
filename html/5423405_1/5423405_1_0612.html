Tomorrow afternoon at 3 o'clock a conference will be given by Mr. Manuel Aguilar, vice Consul of Mexico, in the Hull House. The subject to be presented is: "The woman's place in Modern Society." The conference, which will be highly educational, has been organized by Sec. No. 3 of "El Frente Popular Mexicano," and according to the information we, have received they are initiating an intense educational campaign among our people. The sympathy manifested to Mr. Aguilar, by the members of our colony, and the important subject that he is to present make us to augur a large attendance. The admission is free.
