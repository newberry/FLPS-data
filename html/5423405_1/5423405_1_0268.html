This manufacturing company was founded thirty years ago and has changed hands a couple of times. The president of the company now is Mr. Modesto Cortina; Jose Lumia, vice-president, Modesto Alonso, treasurer.

The company imports tobacco from Cuba. It employs twelve persons, uses 800 sq. ft. of space and manufactures 300,000 cigars annually. All are sold in Chicago. The name of the cigars is Lucius and sell from 5 cents to three for one dollar.
