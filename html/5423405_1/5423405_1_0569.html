Mr. Rafael Aveleyra, Mexican Consul in Chicago, will speak by radio to-day at 4 P.M., during the transmission of the program of the Worlds' Fair, using the radio station W C F L. Mr. Aveleyra was invited by the Exposition's President.

The radio audience will have an opportunity to listen to Mrs. Milla Dominguez, Mexican soprano, and Mr. Francisco Tortalero, Mexican tenor.
