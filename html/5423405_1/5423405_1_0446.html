The Society "Miquel Hidalgo y Costilla", in cooperation with the mutualist society "Josefa Ortiz de Dominguez" is organizing a carnival which will last nine days at Peoria St, between Roosevelt and Maxwell Streets, to begin September 11 and last until the 20th of September. According to the plans now made there will be a series of games of Mexican style and such as roulette, lotteries, besides the stands, fruits, refreshments, and national dishes. A Mexican orchestra will play for those who like to dance,for the children there will be a marry-go-round, and a ferris-wheel
