English classes will be held in the Chicago Boys' Club, 1300 Newberry Avenue from 6:30 to 8:30 P.M. Monday, Tuesday and Friday.

Elementary classes are being conducted by a competent teacher Mrs. Odga Aid, for those not more than 18 years of age.

In Marcy Center: (for girls only) Monday, Tuesday, and Friday at 3:00 o'clock in the afternoon. English and Spanish classes, carpentry and polishing.

Please enroll today. Bring your friends and form a club. We will be glad to cooperate. The address of Henry Booth House is 701 West 14th Place, Chicago, Illinois.
