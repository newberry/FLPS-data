A dance will take place at the West Side Auditorium, Satarday December 29th, from 8:00 o'clock until 2:00 in the morning. Come with your people and be happy for a moment in a large and comfortable hall.

On this "Galla Night" the orchestra of Professor Jose Reyes will delight us with its best melodies. Admission for gentlemen is fifty cents, ladies will be allowed to come in free of charge. The Hall is located at Racine and Taylor Streets.
