On Saturday, November 16, the Union Benefica will present a play at the West Side Auditorium, Racine and Taylor Streets.

Featuring this affair will be a dance and the presentation of the drama, "La Justicia del Puello." This is one of the most notable plays ever shown on the stage.

Because of the effort expended in arranging this performance, as well as on account of its entertaining features, it is felt that its success is assured.
