Mr. Saboya estimates the number of Brazilians in Chicago at five. He came here fourteen months ago and before taking charge of the office there were no records in the office.

Mr. De Moreas is export manager for the Belmont Radio Corporation; Mr. Henly C. Hill is secretary of the Rotary Club and lives at 636 Addison street; his office telephone number is State 4020.

These are the only Brazilians that are known to the Consulate.
