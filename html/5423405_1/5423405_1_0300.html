On Saturday, September 10, the orchestra "Musas Latinas," made up wholly of women, will make its debut at the Auditorium Hotel. This orchestra is under the direction of Belinsky and the names of the artists are as follows:

Beatriz Macias Paulina Ortiz, Juanita Martinez, Maria Guadalupe, Martinez, Tula Rios, Anita Munoz and Maria Luisa Martinez.
