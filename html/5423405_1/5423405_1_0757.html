The Union Benefica Espanola held a meeting the 8th of this month at 10:30 A.M. This extraordinary session was very well attended. Mr. Serrano opened the meeting. The main item was the nomination of candidates who would direct the destinies of that society during the year of 1929. Mr. Jose Amigo, Francisco Abeldua and Pedro Ballesteros were nominated as candidates for President; Juan Serrano, for Secretary; Francisco Garcia and Alfredo Quintana, Assistant Secretary; L. Jose Varela, Treasurer; Jose Cendon, Assistant Treasurer.

Be on the look out for the new undertakings which will be outlined by the new officers in the very near future.
