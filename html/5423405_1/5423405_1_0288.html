The highly credited and well known commercial firm of S. Esteiro and Company, which has occupied the location at 813 South Halsted Street, for some time has moved to 1024 South Halsted Street. We have been informed that this new move was for the purpose of giving better service to the many customers of the Company.

The store of Mr. Esteiro is fully stocked with diversified Mexican products which are of the highest grade.

This firm is one of the principal importing and distributing houses in the Colony. We wish this outstanding business much success in its new location.
