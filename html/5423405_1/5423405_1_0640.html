The newspaper El Nacional has offered a trophy to the winning Basket-Ball team in the tournament which is to take place in the Hull House, 800 So. Halsted Street, Saturday 21st, at 8:00 P. M. The participant groups will be; Flechas Azules, Flechas del Sur, Hidalgo, Necaxa and Cuauhtemoc. Whoever may be the winner does not matter, the question is to play fair and square.
