As a result of a police raid on the Poolroom at 4537 So. Ashland Ave., thirteen Mexicans were arrested. The arrests were made last Saturday night at 2 o'clock when one of the agents who was searching the "parlor" for illicit alcohol discovered four bottles of beer in a hiding-place. Those detained were taken to the police station and were freed after a brief hearing.

The proprietor of the billard-parlor, Mr. A. Castro, was fined $100 and costs.
