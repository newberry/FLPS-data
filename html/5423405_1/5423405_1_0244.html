Mr. Banios said: There are about 25 Guatemalians in Chicago and 20 years ago there were none. The majority came to study but all of them are working people now. I know of Jose Garcia, export manager for the Continental Products Co., 21st St. and Michigan Ave. and of Garcia Hurera, 2135 N. Racine; Dona Julia De Figuerro is a midwife.

I remember of two marimba bands that played in Chicago, Don Carlos in the Morrison Hotel in 1933-34 and the Hurtado Brothers in the Drake Hotel in 1921.
