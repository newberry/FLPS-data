The well known writer, Miss Angelica Arenal, who was delegated by the Mexican writers to a Congress held in New York is now in Chicago as a guest of: El Frente Popular. Miss Arenal, spoke last night to a group at the Hull House, and tonight will speak at--Fuller Park situated at 45th and Princeton Avenue. Tomorrow she is scheduled to speak to another branch of El Frente Popular, at the University of Chicago Settlement at 2 o'clock. Also at the meeting to be held tomorrow Miss Consuelo Garcia Moran, a Mexican teacher will address the people of that vicinity. Miss Moran, has been among us for some time.
