Born in Lugo, Spain, he came to the United States in 1913 and to Chicago in 1920. Started the wood carving manufacturing in 1926 in partnership with another man, but in 1932 bought him out. He does business with (?) Milwaukee, Wis., besides the state of Illinois.

He uses 2500 sq. ft. of space for his business.

Former vice-president of the Spanish Club in 1936.
