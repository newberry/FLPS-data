The first meeting of the Sporting Club Excelsior, was celebrated on Sunday, March 4th, with the purpose of electing a new executive board and of finding out if this club could organize a base-ball team. It seems that the club has enough players to form a team.

Mr. Nardo Stevens, noted sportsman, was appointed organizer and Mr. Nicolas Garcia, president.

On Sunday, March 11th, this club will have another meeting to appoint the other officials of the executive board.
