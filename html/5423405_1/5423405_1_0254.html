Dr. Eliud Garcia Trevino, was elected president of the Chicago Homeopathic Medical Society, for the 1932-33 term and will immediately take charge of the Medical Society's work in connection with the 1933 Worlds Fair.

The Mexican Colony feels proud each time that a fellowman contributes more and more to the prestige of Mexicans in this country.

Our friend Dr. Trevino will leave Chicago,this week on his vacation and will be back in his office by the first of June or a little later.
