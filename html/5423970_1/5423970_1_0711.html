The Keistutis (name of Lithuanian king) Building and Loan Association is selling new shares, the 33rd series, which began on Sept. 12. All Lithuanians, men and women, are invited to join the Association. Meetings are held every Thursday evening at L. Azukas hall, 3301 S. Auburn Avenue. The secretary of the Association is K. F. Pocius, 938 W. 33rd St.
