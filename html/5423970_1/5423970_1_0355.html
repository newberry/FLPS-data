All members of the 269 local of the Amalgamated Garment Workers of America are requested to attend a meeting of the local which will be held Saturday evening, January 16 at Cernauskas Hall, 19th and Union Ave. At this meeting a report will be made by our delegate who attended the national convention of our union at New York. There will be other important matters of interest to members of our organization. It is the duty of all members to be present. Also, do not forget to bring new members, because applications for membership are accepted at each meeting.
