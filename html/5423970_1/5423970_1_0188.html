March 6 at Aurora Hall, 3149 So. Halsted Street, a meeting was held of Lithuanian intellectuals in Chicago. The purpose of this meeting was to duscuss the the question of education for the Lithuanians--how to attract more of them to the Aurora lectures, etc. Thirty people were present. The discussions were lively and interesting. The meeting lasted until late. Many valuable suggestions were made and a decision was reached to have further discussion of this question.
