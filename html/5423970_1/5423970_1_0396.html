Comrade St. Laurinaitis was married to Stella Jurevicius on Jan. 16, at Cicero, Ill. Comrade Laurinaitis is a capable and active leader among the working class. He is well known for his successful efforts in popularizing the Daily Worker, organ of the American Communist Party, and other working class literature. The bride is also an active worker among the same groups.

The guests at the wedding did not forget the needs of the working class movement. Comrad Laurinaitis suggested that a collection be made to assist the coal mine strikers and their families, and $24.00 was collected and later forwarded to the strike field.
