The Gediminas Building and Loan Association was organized on Jan. 28, 1914.

The first organizers were: S. Anusauskas, A. S. Preczinauskas, P. Bludzins, J. Bazdonas, J. Sandars, P. Zabella and others.

Total assets in the year 1936 - $164,000.

Directors:

Joseph C. Eucher, Anton Lukosius, Peter Knietkus, Rose Encher, Zigment Kukalis, Vincent Paukstir, John Sandars, Joseph Klapatauskas, Alex Ercius, John Zeuberis, Slanley Norzaila, George Lukas.

Officers:

Joseph C. Eucher, president

Rose Eucher, secretary.

Vincent Paukstir, treasurer.
