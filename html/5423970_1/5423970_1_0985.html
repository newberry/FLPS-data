The Marquette Park Home Owner's Association was organized in the month of April 1930.

The purpose of this organization is to see to it that the taxes should not be too high, that the vicinity should be kept clean, etc.

The membership is over three hundred. The meetings are held at Holy Birth Parish Hall, 6812 So. Washtenau Ave.

J. M. Mickeliunas, president, 6747 So. Artesian Ave., J. Czeikauskas, secretary, 6642 So. Sacramento Ave.
