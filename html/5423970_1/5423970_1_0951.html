Friday, November 7, 7 P. M. at P. Koreckas' Hall, 1193 S. Oakley Avenue, the Lithuanian Republican Club of the Tenth Ward will hold its meeting.

At this meeting will be shown Spanish-American war pictures. All Lithuanians are welcome. The president of the Club of the Tenth Ward is extending thanks to all the club members for coming to the meeting.

We are proud to say that four Lithuanians of this club have city jobs. We are inviting all to come to this meeting.

J. Juszka

President.
