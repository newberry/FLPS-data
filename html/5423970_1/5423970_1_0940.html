The Lithuanian Political Democratic League will have its banquet Saturday, May 24, Pulaski Hall, 800 S. Ashland Avenue, at 7 P. M. Admission for men and women is 50 cents. At this banquet there will be good music. There will be present all the Democratic politicians and Mayor Harrison, who will lead the Grand March Dance. This will be one of the greatest Lithuanian banquets in Chicago. All Lithuanians are invited to come.
