The Bridgeport branch of the Lithuanian Catholic Temperance Society is conducting a health and temperance exhibit on Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday of this week at St. George's (Lithuanian) Parish Hall, 32nd Place and Auburn (now Lituanica) Avenue. Pictures, charts, and statistical data pertaining to health and temperance make up the display at the exhibit.

A program is presented every evening at 8:00 P. M. It consists of a lecture by a Lithuanian doctor, the showing of motion pictures and slides, etc. There are guides at the exhibit to explain the various features of the show to visitors. Admission is free.
