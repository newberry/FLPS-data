The eighty-first chapter of the Lithuanian Socialist League, Chicago, will present for the first time "Miljonai Vandenyje" (Millions in Water), a three-act drama, Sunday evening, at Hull House, Halsted and Polk Streets.

This drama was written by Mr. Bruno Vargsas. It is educational as well as highly entertaining. The plot of the play portrays the unhappy life of a greedy Lithuanian farmer, who denies himself the comforts of life to hoard money.

Besides the play, the program will consist of recitations and monologues. Admission will be twenty-five thirty-five, and fifty cents. Everybody is invited to attend.
