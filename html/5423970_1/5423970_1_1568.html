The Midland Building and Loan Association was organized on June 6, 1914.

The first organizers were: John Alexandravice, G. Povilaitis, A. Balchunas, G. J. Wesdzunas, J. Gustaitis, J. Balchunas, J. Waydzunas and others.

Total assets in the year 1936 - $45,144.99.

Directors:

G. K. Budris, A. Saldunas, Bruns Shunis, A. Grima, B. Radzins, Paul Ozolas, Adam Markauskas, K. Strelcunas, John Thomas.

Officers:

G. K. Budris, President.

Bruno Shukis, Vice-President

A. Saldunas, Secretary,

Anton Grina, Treasurer.

John T. Zuris, Attorney.
