The Butchers' and Grocers' Association was organized, February 8, 1914.

The purpose of this association is to act in common business interests, to help each other in various incidents, to have friendly festivals and buy a wreath for the members who die. There are thirty-six members.

The organizers were K. Beinorauskes, J. Balciunas, K. Berenkis and K. M. Minetas.

H. Urnezis, treasarer, 3428 Lithuaania Ave.

A. Budfris, secretary, 939 W. 33rd.St.
