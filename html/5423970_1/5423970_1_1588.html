The Chicago Lithuanian Savings and Loan Society was organized in December, 1925. The first organizers were: A. Dargis, J. Brunzas, J. Kratkus, B. Mastauskas, M. Marvides, S. Shambards.

The capital is $1,000,000. The assets are $45,000.

B. Nemartonis, president 6540 S. Campbell Ave.

A. Valonis, secretary, 2201 W. Cermak Rd.

A. Dargis, treasurer, 2425 W. Marquette Rd.
