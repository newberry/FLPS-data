We, a group of Lithuanian residents of the West Side district, held a meeting and came to the conclusion that a co-operative Lithuanian food store must be established in this community. Therefore, we invite all the residents of this district to come to a meeting on April 27 at Meldazis Hall, 2242 West 23rd Place. The establishment of a Lithuanian co-operative food store will be discussed at the meeting. The meeting is scheduled to start promptly at 7:30 P.M.
