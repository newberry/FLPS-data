Lodge 81 of the Lithuanian Socialist Alliance will sponsor a debate to be held at 1182 Milwaukee Avenue on February 12. The subject will be: "Can Women Have Equal Rights with Men?" A. Vasiliauskas will take the affirmativas side; Dr. A. J. Karalius the negative.

The Lodge plans to have similar debates immediately preceding each monthly meeting, these debates to begin at 7 P. M. Members and prospective members are urged to attend. We have many important things to discuss, among them the nominations of the Socialist party.
