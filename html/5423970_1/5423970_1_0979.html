We are announcing to all Lithuanians that on the 8th day of January, at 7 o'clock in the evening, at Mr. P. Rykowas Hall, 4458 S. Wood Street, was organized the Lithuanian Political Club No. 2 (independent).

At this first meeting forty-five members joined the club.

President J. Kowakskis

Secretary J. Kazlauskis

Committee until the second meeting -- Z. Rykowas and Antanas Norciko.

The second meeting will be held on Sunday 22nd day of January,4 P. M. at Z. Rykowas Hall. The speakers will be our citizens John Grinius, S. Rokosz and Zaleskis.

Committee.
