On February 25, Mrs. Olsevskis and Mr. M. Jurgelionis, for a students' benefit, arranged a banquet at the Aurora Hall.

Miss Horodockaite sang Schubert's "Serenade" and "Gipsy Song." Young Mr. Olsevskis and Miss Dundulis played the piano.

Mr. Jurgelionis gave a humorous imitation of public speakers of Lithuanian, as well as other nationalities. After the program, the Lithuanian dances were performed.
