The Singers' Circle of the fourth branch of the Lithuanian Socialist League, Chicago, will stage "Audra Giedroje" (A Storm in Fair Weather), a three-act farce, farce, Sunday evening, November 22, at Hull House, Halsted and Polk Streets. This will be the first presentation of the play, which deals with an illicit love affair.

Besides the play, the program will consist of popular songs, working class songs, recitations, and monologues. Admission prices will be twenty-five, thirty-five and fifty cents per person. All Lithuanians are invited to attend.
