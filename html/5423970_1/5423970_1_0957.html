On August 26 at Kareskas Hall, 4612 Atlantic street, there was organized a new Lithuanian political club of fifty-five members.

The administration of the club consists of N. Urbonas, president; P. Kareckas, vice-president; J. Jowaisza, secretary; J. Mongirdas, treasurer.

The meeting will be held every Saturday evening at Kareckas Hall.

J. Jowaisza, secretary.
