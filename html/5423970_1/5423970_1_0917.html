The 21st Ward American Lithuanian Citizen's Club was organized in 1934.

The club's aim is to take active part in politics, to help its members to get political jobs, to help the Lithuanians to become citizens, etc.

The membership dues are 10 cents per month. The membership is about three hundred. The meetings are held at the Petraushas Hall, 1750 So.Union Ave.

G. M. Chernauskas, president, 1900 So. Union Ave., G. Jakubauskas, secretary, 1904 Canalport Ave.
