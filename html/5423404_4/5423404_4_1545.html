Members of the Marine Corps, who participated in the Chateau-Thierry and Belleau Woods offensive have just returned from France. Among them are A. M. Benson, 223 E.55th Place, Charles A. Lindquist, from Maywood; and M. A. Carlson, 1903 Berwyn Avenue. Mr. Benson, who as is known was a boxing instructor, has lost the sight of his right eye. Lindquist was gassed and Carlson severely wounded.
