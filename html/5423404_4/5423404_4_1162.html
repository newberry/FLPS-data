The Mimer Lodge of Independent Order of Svithiod celebrated its 20th anniversary on September 23 with a banquet at Wicker Park Hall attended by some three hundred fifty persons. Mr. Frank Lindquist acted as toastmaster. The principal speaker for the evening was Mr. Carl R. Chinblom. The Mimer Lodge was the fourth of forty-six lodges of the order which was organized October 3, 1891 with twenty-six members. Four of them still belong to the Lodge.
