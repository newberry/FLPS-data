The Orphei Singing Club arranged a concert and dance at its club house last Saturday. It was assisted by The Swedish Glee Club tenor, John Chellman, Newstrom's Orchestra, and Ernst Ekberg. The concert hall was filled to capacity.

The Glee Club sang fifteen numbers on its program. Carl Lonnerblad directed the chorus. The public was very well pleased, and rewarded the artists with loud applauses.
