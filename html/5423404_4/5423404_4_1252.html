This lodge was incorporated as the Brahe Pleasure Club last Oct. 28. The following eight persons are charter members: Nels J. Lindstrand, Axel Borg, Alfred Anderson, John W. Youngberg, Chas. Bylin, Frank Swanson, Edward Isberg, and John A. Kling.

The Lodge Brahe has 461 members, all in good standing, and has a reserve in its treasury of more than $4,000.
