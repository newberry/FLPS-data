Axel W. Anderson, the well-known builder, bought the lot on the southeast corner of Elmdale and Greenview Avenues for $75,000. The purchaser is planning to erect a sixty-apartment building on the site at an estimated cost of $400,000.

Benjamin Franklin Olson, architect, has delivered the drawings for a church which is to be erected on the northeast corner of Washtenaw Avenue and Crystal Street at an estimated cost of $60,000.
