The firm of Larson & Lindgren will erect a building at 4857-59 North Drake Avenue, at an estimated cost of $100,000. A. W. Anderson has contracted for the brickwork. The drawings have been prepared by Architect G. C. Larson.

N. Severin will erect a three-story brick building at 1442 Elmdale Avenue, at an estimated cost of $25,000. A. W. Anderson has contracted for the brickwork. Architect Larson, has made the drawings for this building also.
