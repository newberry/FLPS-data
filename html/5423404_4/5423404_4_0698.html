J. Z. Anderson has sold his eighteen-apartment building on the northwest corner of Granville and Lakewood Avenues to Richard H. Boericke for $60,000. As part of the deal he is taking in trade Boericke's apartment building on the northwest corner of Southport and Belle Plaine Avenues, at a valuation of $35,000.

Frederick A. Anderson and Axel W. Anderson have bought the southeast corner of Kenmore Avenue and Argyle Street for $121,000.

Gustaf Hallberg has sold his house, located at 1407 Ridge Avenue, Evanston, for $60,000.
