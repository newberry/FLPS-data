The Society for the Swedish Home for Aged held its quarterly meeting last Monday at the Sherman Hotel. Reports were read and approved. The program for the annual Summer visit to the Home July 19 was presented at the meeting. There will be speeches by representative leaders, and songs by the Swedish Singing Society. Refreshments will be served.

The Society has now 180 life time members, and there is over $15,000 in the endowment fund treasury.
