The First Swedish Methodist Church of Ravenswood dedicated its new church building last Sunday. It is located at the corner of Paulina street and Webster Avenue. It is a frame structure and costs $3,000.

Three dedicatory services were held: one in the forenoon in Swedish by Pastor H.W. Eklund from Worcester, Mass.; one in the afternoon, also in Swedish, by Pastor R. Cederberg from Evanston; and one in the evening in English by Pastor J.P. Brushingham.
