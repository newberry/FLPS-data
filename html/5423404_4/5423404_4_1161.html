A music festival was celebrated last Friday by the Viking and Nordstjernan's Music corps, and Nordstjernan Singing Club at Larsons Hall. A "Smorgasbord" was served. The Singing Club sang several numbers under the direction of Emil Larson, and the music corps played, lead by director Gustav Carlson. Speeches were made by Mr. Albin Swanson, chairman of the Swedish Societies Home for Aged, director Olof F. Nelson, A. P. Olson, and others.
