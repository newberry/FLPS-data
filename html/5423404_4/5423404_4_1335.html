Three hundred Chicago Swedes 'go home' for Christmas on the s/s "Gripsholm", according to information given out by the Chicago office of the Swedish American Line. A special train via the Lehigh Valley R.R. will leave Chicago, Dec. 7th. It will arrive in New York just in time for the departure of the ship.

Among these three hundred Chicagoans who will celebrate "Yule" in the old country, are County Architect Eric E. Hall and Dr. Olga Gustafson.
