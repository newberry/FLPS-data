The May feast of the Svithiod Lodge No. 1 was held last Saturday at the North Side Turner Hall, which was filled to capacity by an appreciative audience. Music and songs were presented by the Svithiod Military Band and the Svithiod Singing Club.

Concluding the program, came the crowning of the May bride, held on the stage with about thirty young ladies dressed in white participating. Miss Ethel Nelson was crowned the May bride, and Mr. Carl R. Chindblom delivered the address.
