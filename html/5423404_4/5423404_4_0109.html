The 1914 yearbook has just been published and contains the usual reports, among them being a list of those that hold life memberships, and of those that are now living at the Home, as well as a financial report. The latter shows that income during the past year was $14,743.28 while total expenditures amounted to $14,013.41, leaving cash on hand as of January 1, 1915 to the amount of $729.87.

Mr. and Mrs Dahl superintend the Home, and Reverend Alf Anderson is the president of its board of directors.
