The delegates were called to a meeting at the Sherman House on January 28, to decide what should be done with $560.70 that was in their treasury. It was decided that it should be split into five equal parts and given to the following named; old people's homes:, Swedish Lutheran,Joliet $112.14; Swedish Baptist,Morgan Park $112.14; Swedish Methodist Bethania, Chicago $112.14; Swedish Mission's Home $112.14, and Chicago's Singers Organization $112.10.

After this was done the choir disbanded from further work and the management offered them a very heartfelt thanks.
