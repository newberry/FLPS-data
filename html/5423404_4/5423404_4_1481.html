Last Sunday was an unusual day for the Swedish Lutherans in Chicago, for on that day High Mass services were held. Bishop Scheele, from Sweden preached.

Long before the services began, the huge hall was packed with worshippers. The complete High Mass was then celebrated. The preludium was given by Prof. Emil Larson.

The collection totalled more than $600, which was given to the Augustana Hospital's general fund. The Bishop has no doubt ever preached to a more attentive audience than the one found listening in the Chicago Auditorium.
