Adolph Lindstrom, the well-known builder and contractor, bought a lot at Dearborn Street and North Avenue for $60,000, and plans to erect an eight-story apartment building on the site. Mr. Lindstrom also bought a lot on the southeast corner of Ridge Boulevard and Noyes Street, Evanston, from Northwestern University for $43,500. He will erect an apartment building on the site at an estimated cost of $350,000.
