At the annual meeting of the Swedish National Association, the following officers for 1905 were elected: president, F. A. Lindstrand; first vice president, Gotthard Dahlberg; second vice president, Oscar Olson; recording secretary, Frithjof Malmquist; corresponding secretary, Obelia Myhrman; treasure, K. E. Ostergren; police officer, Edward Fiellander. The directors are: A. Beckman, Axel Ahlstedt, Theodore Hessell.
