This club will hold its first family social at the Verdandi Hall, Sunday, Feb. 20, at 3 P.M. All men and women born in Calmar, or who received their schooling there, are invited.

The purpose of this gathering, is that people from Calmar, now living in Chicago, may renew acquaintances with dear friends of old.

A fine program will be rendered followed by dancing. Coffee, cake, and "Smorgasbord" will be served. None except those from the city of Calmar will be admitted.
