The Ladies Department of the Swedish Club had its yearly meeting, and the following were elected for the year 1919: Mrs. Ivar Holmstrom, president; Mrs. Eric Wegner, vice-president; Miss O. C. Nylund was elected treasurer. Mrs. C. Max Hulman (or Heilman) was elected cashier, and the corresponding secretary is Mrs. Carl G. Swanson.

A very fine luncheon was served after the business session.
