The new church of the Swedish Methodist Bethany Congregation, located on the corner of Winnemac and North Ashland, was dedicated last Sunday.

The ceremonies were performed by W.C. Blomquist, pastor of the congregation, and Pastor Alf. Anderson, District Superintendent.

The Congregation was organized in October 1901, under the name of "Webster Avenue Congregation." The cost of the church was about $35,000, and it has a seating capacity of 1,000.
