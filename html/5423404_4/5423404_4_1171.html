The Swedish Club held its annual meeting January 5 and elected officers as follows: president: C. S. Peterson; vice-president: Doctor A. Frick; treasurer: F. Winship; secretary: Axe Hulten. Six members were elected to serve as a Board of Directors. Mr. Carl Lonnerblad was chosen librarian. The membership is now 250, but it was decided to add fifty persons who would like to join the club. The annual dues will be $18 from April 1.
