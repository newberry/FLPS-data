The Swedish Society for the Home for the Aged held its annual outing last Sunday on the lawn outside the Home at Evanston, Illinois. The program started at 3 P.M. Dr. John A. Christenson, president of the Society greeted the crowd. The festival address was given by Dr. Gustav Andreen, president of Augustana College. The Swedish Singing Society, directed by Ernst Lindblom, sang several Swedish folksongs. Refreshments were served.
