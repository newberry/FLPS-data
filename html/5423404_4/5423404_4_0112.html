A grand Christmas concert will be given Thursday, December 10 at Orchestra Hall for the benefit of the Swedish Home for Aged, in Evanston, Illinois. A chorus of 350 voices will sing directed by Prof. Joel Mossberg.

Others who will appear on the program are: Miss Minnie Cedargreen, violinist; Hanna Butler, soprano; Emil Larson, organist; Mrs. Carl R. Chindblom, pianist; and Mr. Rudolph Engberg.
