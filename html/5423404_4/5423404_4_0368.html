The police force has a very meager Swedish clientele amongst its law-breakers; and we are proud of it. Therefore, it hurts us to tell of the fate of Otto Bjerkman, who has descended the downward path and is now a graduate burglar. Several burglaries have been committed by him, but most of the stolen goods has been recovered.

What makes this case so sad is the fact that Bjorkman always has been well liked by his friends, and was looked upon as a well-merited man. His services in the World War were rewarded with the French Croix de Guerre and the American Military Modal, a special distinction.
