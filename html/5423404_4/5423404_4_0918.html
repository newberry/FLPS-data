Permits for the erection of new buildings in Chicago were granted last week to certain Scandinavians as follows:

G. A. Carlson: Two three-story houses, 5333 Cottage Grove Avenue. Total estimated cost, $25,000.

Lindholm and Johnson: Two three-story houses, 5827-29 Calumet Avenue. Total estimated cost, $19,000.

Erickson and Isaacson: Two-story house, 3325 North Ashland Avenue. Estimated cost, $5,000.
