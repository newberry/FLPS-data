Through the union of the great organizations, the "Scandinavian Fraternity" and the "Scandinavian Aid Society", the "Scandinavian Fraternity of America" has been organized these past days. The union was arranged at the combined meeting here in Chicago, of the two organizations mentioned.

The convention lasted four days and throughout, the enthusiasm for the union was strong. The total membership of the new Fraternity is about 25000. It was resolved to start the publication of a magazine representing the organization, to be issued monthly under the name, The Scandinavian Fraternity Review.
