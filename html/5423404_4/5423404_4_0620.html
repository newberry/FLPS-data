C. M. Hedman and Winthrop O. Evans, president and vice-president, respectively, of the Hedman Manufacturing Company, have bought the property on the northwest corner of Cass and Huron Streets from Frederic Delano for $45,000. The firm is manufacturing a check protecting device, and is planning to move its offices to the newly acquired building.

Martin Larson has bought the building at 1243-45 Foster Avenue from John T. Shay for $36,500. The building contains six apartments.
