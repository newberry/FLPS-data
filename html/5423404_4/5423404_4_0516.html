Axel Johnson and Adolph Johnson, who have been engaged for some time in contracting and building under the firm name of Axel E. Johnson and Company, are planning to build a large apartment hotel next spring at Main Street and Maple Avenue, Evanston, at an estimated cost of $600,000. Drawings for the structure, which will be named Ridge View, have been prepared by Ronneberg & Pierce, architects. The building will be seven stories high and will contain 137 rooms. The ground floor will be occupied by six stores. It will be ready for occupancy next October.
