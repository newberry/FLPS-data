The Annual Convention of the Swedenborgians is in progress in Chicago during the week June 14 - 21.

This coming Saturday, the 18, all delegates and guests of the convention will meet at the bust of Emanuel Swedenborg in Lincoln Park, at which an appropriate program will be rendered.

As we all no doubt recall,this memorial of the great Swedish philosopher and religious writer was erected some years ago through public subscription among the Swedish people of Chicago.
