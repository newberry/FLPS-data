A Gustaf Adolphus festival was held in Ausgau Church, on Sedgwick Street under the auspices of the church. The baritone, Joel Mossberg gave several numbers. The tenor, William Dohlem sang, and the violinist, Mr. Frank Winters played "Rose Marie," a duet was rendered with great ability and feeling by Mossberg and Miss Ida Linn. The attendance was greater than had been anticipated.
