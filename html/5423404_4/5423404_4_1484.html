A joint religious service will be held by the Swedish Lutheran Churches in Chicago on June 24 in the Auditorium. The services will be conducted by Bishop Von Schiele, who is visiting in the United States.

A mixed choir will sing with soloists, including the bass singers, Gustof Holmquist, and the tenor, William Dohlin.

All Lutheran ministers have been sent admission cards, which will be honored until 10:15 A.M., at which time all reservations will be thrown open to the public.
