A jolly concert and dance will be given on Saturday, December 30, at Spelz Hall, 1630 North Clark Street. The income is to be turned over to the Comic Ernst Schycker. The Orpheus Singing Club, Messrs. Chr. Brusell, John Lindhagen, and members of Swedish Theatrical Association will assist. The jolly one-act play, "Pelle Jonson's Rivals," will be one of the attractions.
