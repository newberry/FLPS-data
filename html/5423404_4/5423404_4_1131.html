The Swedish American Woman's Club which during the past twenty years has done excellent work in aiding the poor among the Swedes in the city, is arranging a social and dance at Lake View Hall, 3143 North Clark Street, on Saturday evening November 6. All the income from the affair is to be used to help the Swedish poor here in the city during the cold months of the winter. The members of the club are therefore expecting lively interest on the part of the public.

The admission fee is only 25 cents.
