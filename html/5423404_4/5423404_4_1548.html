Mr. Nels Hokanson, of the State Bank of Chicago, as secretary of the "Foreign Language Division at the Liberty Loan Committee" has been very active and exceptionally successful in the war fund drive.

Mr. Felix Streyckman, chairman of this committee and Mr. Hokanson were honored at a large dinner which was given at the Hotel Morrison Tuesday evening. Mr. Hokanson was presented with a gold watch as a memento and in appreciation of his unselfish work in the interest of the Liberty Loan.

Due to sudden illness, Mr. Hokanson was not present at the dinner.

Late reports indicate his sickness to be of a grave nature.
