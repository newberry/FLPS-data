The Swedish Old People's Home organization has sold its home in Park Ridge to F. Gillich, of Park Ridge, for $8,500. The home will be vacated August 15. The organization now has in the treasury not less than $20,000. The well-known architect, Andrew Sandegren, is now drawing the plans and ascertaining the cost of a new home. It may cost $25,000, and will be a credit to the Chicago Swedes. A committee of twenty-two men has been appointed to bring in plans for this new building.
