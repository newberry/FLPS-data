From Augustana College, Rock Island, Ill., we are informed that 100 young men will receive training to be officers in the army. The government has invited all youths of over 18, to take this course.

The government furnishes the uniforms, pays for sustenance and lodging, and all school expenses in addition to a salary of $30 per month for each student.

The government will assign a special "Commander" at Augustana College, as leader of the military training, which begins October 1, The government expects these college students to become excellent army officers.

Only young men over 18, who are pursuing an academic course, can take advantage of this offer. At the Augustana Synod's other institutions of learning such as, Bethany College, Lindsborg, Kans., and Gustaf Adolphus College, St. Peter, Minn., and others, military training courses have been instituted.
