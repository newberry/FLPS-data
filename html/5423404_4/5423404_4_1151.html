This united concert held in Orchestra Hall, on March 9, was very successful, and brought a net income of $995,38, which was equally divided between these two societies. The Governor of Minnesota the Hon. Adolph O. Eberhart, who was the guest of honor at this concert, sent the following letter to Mr. F. A. Lindstrand:

"My dear Sir: The entertainment given Sunday afternoon at Orchestra Hall, Chicago, by the Swedish National Association and the National Chorus was one of the most pleasing and interesting I have attended and I wish to thank you, and through you, Mrs. Myhrman and Mr. Dahlen for the courtesies extended to me."
