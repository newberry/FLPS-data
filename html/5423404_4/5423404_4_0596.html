Joseph and Maria C. Sandahl bought the thirty-family apartment building at 5042-50 Glenwood Avenue from Martin Grosby for $140,000.

Arthur Berg sold the apartment building at 6646-54 Kenwood Avenue to George R. Richter for $105,000.

The block bounded by 46th Court, 47th Avenue, 18th Street, and 19th Street has been sold by Carl A. Carlson to the Curtis Leger Fixture Company for $75,932. The company is planning to erect a large factory building on the site as soon as conditions in the building industry permit.
