The Vikings Valhalla Society elected the following to serve as a Board of Directors, namely; chairman: Frithiof Malmquist; vice-chairman for the South side; Mrs. Hilda C. Anderson; for the West side; Mrs. John P. Carlson, for the North side; Mrs. Oscar Magnuson, financial secretary; John A. Linden, treasurer; Elias Olson. A committee of five was chosen to find and recommend a suitable place for Valhalla Society's Home for Aged. It prefers one near a farm, with at least eighty acres, and situated at a lake.
