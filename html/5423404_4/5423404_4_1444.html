The Augustana Luther League of Chicago, representing some thirty congregations and societies, held its annual membership meeting last Tuesday in the Boston Oyster House. Pastor Carl Christenson, of Saron Lutheran Church,who is chairman, presided. The vice-president,W. F. Carlson, gave a brief but instructive outline of the League's activities during the past year. According to his report, the organization accomplished much in 1909, and the outlook for the current year is encouraging.

Several members addressed the meeting, and in addition, there was entertainment consisting of song and music.
