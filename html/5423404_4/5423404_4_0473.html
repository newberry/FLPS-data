G. Pearson has contracted for the brickwork on a three-story apartment building which is to be erected at 1618-22 Wallen Avenue for C. Hogberg at an estimated cost of $125,000. The drawings have been prepared by P. P. Olson, architect.

The firm of Olson Brothers has contracted for the carpentry work, and A. Thulin for the brickwork, on a building which is to be erected at 6513 South Halsted Street.
