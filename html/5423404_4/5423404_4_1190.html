The Viking Lodge No. 1 will give a Christmas and New Year's feast Saturday, December 28, at 8 P.M. at the North Side Turner Hall. Joel Mossberg, Ernst Ekberg, Otto Nelson, the Orphei Singing Club, and I.O.V, band will appear on the stage. A play called "Frithiof's forsoning med Angantyr," with songs interspersed, will be presented. Gifts will be distributed by Santa Claus.
