Saturday evening May 2, there will be an entertainment at Svenska Klubben 1258 North LaSalle Street. This entertainment to be known as "The Spring Song".

A program is being arranged which will include interpretative dancing, singing, music, and readings, It will be followed by a dance.

Svenska Klubben was organized in Chicago forty-five years ago. It has a membership of eight-hundred. Officers of Svenska Klubben are as follows; President C. S. Peterson; vice-president, John E. Erickson; secretary, Carl Lonnerblad; treasurer, Francis Winship; board of directors, G. B. Anderson, Carl Swenson, Charles Theodore Green, Frank Gustafson, Ivar Holmstrom, S. P. Norman.
