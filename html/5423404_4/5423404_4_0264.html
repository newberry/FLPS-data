Swedish Aid Society, Linnea, held its annual meeting last week in the Grace Evangelist Lutheran Church at 70th Street and Union avenue.

From the various reports made, it was shown that the Society had disbursed $3,799.48 in cash to orphanages, old people's homes and charitable institutions, in addition to other works of mercy. Last Christmas 85 baskets of food were distributed. Seventy-four new members were gained during 1925.

New officers for 1926 were elected, as follows: Mrs. N.A. Nelson, president; Mrs. O. Osterholm, vice-president; Mrs. Elmer Johnson, recording secretary; Mrs. John Nylin, financial secretary.
