More than one hundred delegates from the forty-eight subordinate lodges met on Friday and Saturday for the 24th regular convention of the Svithiod order, at the Verdandi Hall, N. Clark St: The income of the order during the year was $107,921.32. One thousand and seventy new members had been admitted into the lodges of the order.

A change in the constitution was adopted, admitting women as members equally with men.
