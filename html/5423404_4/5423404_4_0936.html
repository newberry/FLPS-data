Mr. C. J. Blomstrom has taken out permit for the erection of a four-story building at the corner of Ellis Avenue and 42nd Street. The estimated cost is $75,000, and the architects are Ostling Bros.

Architect L. G. Hallberg has completed the approved plans for a building at the corner of Drexel Boulevard and 47th Street. The contractor is Albert Wisner, and the estimated cost of the building project is $60,000.
