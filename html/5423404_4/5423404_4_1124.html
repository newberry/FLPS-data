The American Scandinavian Foundation, with headquarters in New York, has selected an advisory committee in Chicago. This committee favors Henry S. Henschen, A. Lunquist, C.S. Peterson, as Swedish members; F.Herman Gode, Oscar H. Hougan, Berger Osland, Norwegian representatives; Max Henins, Henry L. Hertz, as Danish members; C.H.Thordorson for the Islander section.
