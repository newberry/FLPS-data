This new old peoples home is located at Grant Street and Evanston Avenue in North Evanston, Ill.

It will be dedicated on the 26th of this month. Some of the speakers on this occasion will be Joseph Paden, of Evanston; Congressman Frederick Lundin, Attorney Edwin A. Olson, and Pastor Ludwig Holmes.

Governor Deneen will be invited to attend the opening ceremonies. It will be a memorable event for those who attend.
