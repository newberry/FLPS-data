Erick Nelson Sten, well-known Swedish old-timer, has passed to his reward. Sten came to America in 1854 with 333 other immigrants, of whom 155 died en route.

The entire group settled in Moline, Ill., Mr. Sten came to Chicago in 1862 and was appointed to the police force. He was a guard at the County jail for many years, and despite his advanced age he kept his post to the last.
