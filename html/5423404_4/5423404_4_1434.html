The Rt. Rev. C. P. Anderson, D. D. Bishop of the Episcopal Diocese of Chicago will visit Immanuel Swedish-American Episcopal Church, Englewood, Sunday November 26, and officiate at the Rite of Confirmation. This church is a sister church to the old St. Ansgarius Church on the North Side. The prayer book and hymnal of the Church of Sweden are used at its services. All persons of Swedish descent are most cordially welcomed to the services.
