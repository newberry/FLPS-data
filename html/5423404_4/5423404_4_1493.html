p.11........The Rector and Regimental Pastor Gustaf Floden, one of Sweden's most noted ministers, will preach on Sunday, Whitsuntide, at St. Ansgarius Church here. The rector, who at his own expenses has undertaken a study-trip to America to obtain a closer knowledge of the state of church affairs has already visited New York, Boston, and Providence, R.I., and from here will go to St. Paul and Minneapolis. During his visit here he is the guest of Rector Herman Lindskog.
