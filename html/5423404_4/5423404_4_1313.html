The Swedish May Festival was celebrated on Saturday, May 5, in the North Side Turner Hall, by the Svithiod lodge number one, and, as usual, this lodge put over another decided success. Hjalmar Hedin extended the welcome to the gathering, and Judge J.G. Sheldon was the speaker of the evening. Miss Bessie Swanson was crowned as the May Queen and music was furnished by Svithiod Military Band and Hunt's Juvenile Orchestra.
