The Swedish-American Women's Club held its annual meeting January 11 at the home of Mrs. Tekla Kjellberg. The following report was read: $820 has been distributed to charity; the following officers were elected, namely; Mrs. Othelia Myhrman, chairman; Mrs. Bertha Estborn, vice-chairman; Mrs. Martha Hall, recording secretary; Mrs. Alma Lindblom, corresponding secretary; Mrs. Hulda Johnson, treasurer. Trustees: Mesdames Ellen Wennerberg, Matilda Larson, and Carolina Lindmark. Finance Committee: Mesdames Bertha Hakanson, Olga Sandstedt, and Hanna Frost.
