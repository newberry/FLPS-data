Last week the Elim Swedish Lutheran congregation observed the thirtieth anniversary of its founding with special services in the church, which is located at Forest Avenue and 113th Street.

The late Pastor C. A. Evald and Dean M. C. Ranseen were instrumental in organizing this congregation among our countrymen and countrywomen in the Pullman district.

The present pastor, G. K. Stark, assisted by members of the church, has written, for the occasion of the anniversary, a history of the congregation.
