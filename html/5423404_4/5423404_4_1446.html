The Swedish Lutheran Messiah Congregation held a bazaar last week in Claremont Hall, 3143 N. Clark St. The affair was well attended every evening and business was good, which can best be demonstrated by the fact that the proceeds amounted to $2,000 net. A splendid entertainment consisting of music and songs was given every evening. The bazaar wound up last Friday evening when Corporation Counsel Edward J. Brundage gave an address on "The Women's Right to Vote."
