Herman G. Peterson has sold the apartment building on the northwest corner of 70th Street and Paxton Avenue to J. Rubin at an undisclosed price. The building contains thirty apartments.

Frank J. Johnson has sold the property on the southwest corner of Clark and Maple Streets to Louis Guenzel for $47,000.

F. A. Johnson has bought a large building lot at Adams Street and Cicero Avenue at an undisclosed price. He is planning to erect an apartment building on the site at an estimated cost of $200,000.
