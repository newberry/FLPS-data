The Swedish Home for Aged, in Evanston, and its old residents were visited by the women of St. Ansgarius' Parish and its Rector the Rev. Herman Lindskog last Wednesday. All of the visitors brought gifts for the old folks living in the home. Pastor Lindskog conducted a service and preached a short sermon. Refreshments were served. This visit was made to honor the memory of Mrs. Adolphine Lindskog.
