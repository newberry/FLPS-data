N. T. Nielsen will build a two-story brick house 4139-43 Long Avenue at an estimated cost of $10,000. G. E. Pearson is the architect.

Architect A. G. Lund has delivered the drawings for a one-story brick building to be erected at 5953 South Whipple Street at an estimated cost of $6,000.

J. & G. Nelson, carpenter contractors, will build a three-story brick building at 3042 Belle Plaine Avenue, which is estimated to cost $16,000. L. Erlanson has contracted for the brickwork.
