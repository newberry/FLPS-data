Sven A. Danielson sold the building on the southeast corner of Clark Street and Arthur Avenue to Bessie Leavitt for $89,000. The building contains stores, offices, and apartments.

William F. Krahl sold the vacant lot at 331-39 East Ohio Street to A. E. Swenson for $55,000. Hedberg & Bister were the brokers. Mr. Swenson is planning to erect a large factory building on the site.
