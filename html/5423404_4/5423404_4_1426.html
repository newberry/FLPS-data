We present above a picture of the Swedish Lutheran Bethania Church, 9118 Houston Avenue, South Chicago, and its pastor, F. A. Engstrand.

Next week the congregation will begin the observance of the thirty-fifth anniversary of its founding, and special programs will be given every night of the week. The male choir of the church, together with other organizations, will take part. The church was built in 1881, and the congregation now has 515 members.
