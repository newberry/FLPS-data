Johan Peterson has sold the apartment building at 4718 Bernard Street to Anias Sorenson for $105,000. It contains twenty-four apartments. Title to the property located at 2104 Ballou Street was accepted as part payment.

Hilma Hillstrom has sold the apartment building located at 1430 Thome Avenue to Hilma M. Lind for $35,000. As part payment, the seller accepted the title to the property located at 1654 Winona Street.

John V. Carlson and Elida Akerlund have sold the apartment building located at 1449-51 Summerdale Avenue to Ralph Michilini for an undisclosed price. It contains six apartments.
