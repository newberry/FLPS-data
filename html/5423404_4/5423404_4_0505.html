R. Erickson has contracted for the brickwork on a three-story brick building which is to be erected at 6334-36 Kimbark Avenue, at an estimated cost of $70,000. O. G. Lund is the architect.

A. E. Dahlberg and E. Peterson have contracted for the brickwork and the carpentry work, respectively, on an apartment building which is to be erected at 6110-36 North Fairfield Avenue at an estimated cost of $50,000.

The Harvard-Yale-Princeton Club has engaged architect C. A. Eckstrom to make the drawings for a large club building which the Club is planning to erect on Federal Street, next to the Engineers' Building.

A building permit has been issued to J. Lind and A. B. Schillins for a three-story apartment building at 1200-02 Wellington Street. The cost is estimated at $80,000. R. C. Ostergren is the architect. The brickwork will be done by E. Edlund.
