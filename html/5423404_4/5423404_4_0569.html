Dr. Abel P. M. Sandahl has bought the apartment building located on the northwest corner of Wayne and Granville Avenues from Mary V. Loftus for $61,000. The building contains fifteen apartments.

Frederick A. and Axel W. Anderson have sold the apartment building located on the southwest corner of Indiana Avenue and 45th Street to Almon Brooks for $75,000. The sellers received title to the property at 4643 Lake Park Avenue, which is valued at $40,000, in part payment.
