Two hundred people attended the concert given by the young Greek violinist, Nickolas Mavrikes, in Kimball Hall.....

Mr. Mavrikes is master of his instrument, and this may account for the fact that this is the first time that no one either came in late or left early at a concert attended by Greek people.

After the concert the young artist was feted by a group of music lovers at the Ranch Cafe of Mr. C. Terzakis.
