p. 2. -- To our great delight we have been informed that Greek friends of music in Chicago have organized a club of their own under the name "Greek Philomusical Society." The following officers have been elected: S. Becatoros, president; N. Docos, vice-president; George Georgopoulos, secretary; B. Mouzakiotis, treasurer, and A. Vappas, dean. The council: D. Chalchipoulos, A. Alexopoulos, P. Somos, H. Kanafas, G. Kachiolis, and Mr. Achilides.

The installation of officers will take place tomorrow, Sunday, at 748 Blue Island Avenue.
