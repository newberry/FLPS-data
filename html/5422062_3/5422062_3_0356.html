At the Symposium of Gapa at the Medinah Club, the well known baritone Mr. John Lysandrou, sang the famous Greek song "O Gero Demos" which enthused and electrified the great gathering.

Mr. Lysnadrou, who begun taking phonetic lessons in Egypt, Completed his studies at the Chicago Musical College. And later perfected himself at the Studio of Lina Bertussi, the famous lyric tenor, whom he married.

The Greek families of Chicago have the opportunity to hear the immortal Greek songs, on Sundays, over the W O R D station.
