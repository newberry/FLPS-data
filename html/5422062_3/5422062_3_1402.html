The Ahepans are inviting everyone to be present on Thursday, Nov. 13, at the State and Lake Bldg., headquarters for chapter No. 46, to hear a lecture by the county engineer of our city, Mr. Gayton.

Mr. Gayton's theme will be "Chicago's Lake". As engineer of our city, he is well qualified to speak on this subject and will give as many interesting facts. His lecture will be illustrated with fifty unusual slides.
