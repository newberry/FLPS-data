In the recent bouts which took place in the Y. M. C. A. gymnasium, two young Greek men won the wrestling matches. The lightweight (105 pounds) match was won by Spyros Vorres, who threw his opponent in three minutes. Bill Tseligas won the 145 pound class, throwing his opponent in thirteen minutes and fiftysix seconds.

More than two hundred athletes took part in the evening's contests.

The winning Greek youths are members of the progressive Greek Olympic Athletic Club. We heartily congratulate them for the ability and courage they exhibited.
