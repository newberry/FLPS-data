Last Saturday afternoon the ladies of the Estia Chapter of the Gapa organization held a meeting and tea in the hall at 528 South Halsted Street. Bunco and bridge were followed by refreshments.

Then the business meeting opened, and the president announced that the Chapter had voted to pay the tuition fees of four needy Greek children in the Socrates Greek-American School. They finally selected four out of the ten children who had applied for the scholarship. The lucky ones are....

It was also announced that the Chapter was going to send three orphans of Greek parentage to an orphanage in Greece. The Estia Chapter gave $30; the Kypseli Chapter, $5; and the St. Barbara Chapter, $25 for this philanthropic purpose.
