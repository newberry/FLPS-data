The first national amateur Baseball championship game of the Hellenic Youth Organization will be played at Wrigley Field tomorrow as one of the events on the opening of the convention of order of Ahepa, a patriotic and fraternal organization of Greek-Americans.

Before the game a committee in charge of the convention will present an American flag to Mrs. George T. Maniatis, president of the Hellenic unit of the American Legion Auxiliary. The Cook County Council Drum and Bugle Corps will act as escort to the flag.
