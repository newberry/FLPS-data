The famous drama "Lagiarni," will be shown in Cinema at Temple Hall, 332-338 S. Marshfield Ave. Jan. 10th and Jan. 11th.

The film of this drama brought from Greece, is 9,000 feet has 9 acts, and the scenario is by John G. Loumos. The picture will be shown at 7 and 9 and 11 P.M. Admission seventy five cents.
