p. 5.- The Ahepa is preparing a huge celebration for the Panigirismo tis Ellinikis Anexartisias, (Panegyrics of National Independence). It will take place Sunday, March 30, at the Masonic Temple, 32 W. Randolph Street, at 7 o'clock. Lawyer George N. Spannon will be chairman. Speakers will be Consul Depastas, Lawyer A. Pantelis and Andrew Vlachos. Three sons of Pericles will recite poems, and dancing will follow to the accompaniment of Nick Varsos' orchestra.

Ahepa recently donated $5,000 to the Greek Schools of Chicago. Keep up the good work!
