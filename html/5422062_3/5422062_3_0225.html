p. 1.- A new Greek club has just been organized. It is the Association of Greek Cooks and Waiters. Mr. C. Ballis is president, A. Simos is vice-president, and D. Kostakos is secretary. The board includes Messrs. Androutsos, Stratigos, Kolombakos, Ekonomou, Lambropoulos, Georgopoulos and Marinakos. Mr. T. Chamales is treasurer.
