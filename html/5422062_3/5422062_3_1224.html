We have received the second number of the magazine issued by the organization, Ahepa, which is published in Washington. Rich in content and artistic printing skill, it is truly a wonderful piece of work.

Mr. Meletios Meletiadis has undertaken to supervise and edit the Ahepan periodical. Mr. Meletiadis is well-known in our city and has been editor of the periodical Greek-American Review for many years. Well acquainted in the business of editing magazines, Mr. Meletiadis has put into the Ahepan periodical much select reading matter of interest to everyone.
