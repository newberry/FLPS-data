The Greek Women's Club, St. Helen, founded 12 years ago, is one of the oldest Women's Clubs in this city. For many years and on many occasions it has rendered its services for philanthropic purposes and has contributed to the adornment of the church by donating the pews.

On Wednesday, May 23rd, at 8 o'clock at St. Constantine's Hall, the club will hold its Annual Dance. The admission is 40 cents and the members are anticipating a large attendance.
