The first annual basket ball games of the seven teams, all Greek, took place last Sunday at the gymnasium of De Paul University.

The athletic team of the "Centaurs" captured first prize, a silver cup was presented by Mr. John Raklios. The chapter, Sophocles, of the Sons of Pericles, came second, the Ypsilantis chapter, third and the chapter Lambda Delta, fourth.

The games were witnessed by a great and enthusiastic crowd.
