With the coming of winter, interest in theatricals has been revived by the people. Chicago never before had the chance to present a Greek troupe, as New York has been doing for the last three years.

Mr. Gerasimos Kourouklis has asked Christ Taboularioes to give an Athenian performance in Chicago this season. Mr. Tacoularides replied that he will be in Chicago in a month and will be ready to present "Dio Lohion" in November 3. Our Greek community is awaiting his arrival with anticipation.
