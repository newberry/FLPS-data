A dramatic performance was given last Sunday night by the dramatic group of Mrs. Polixeni Papachvistov. The drama "Kolokotronis" was a complete success. All the members of the cast played their parts very well.

Before the drama a comedy called "Giantes" was given, which made the audience hysterical with laughter. The theater was packed, giving evidence that the people will support a dramatic group, providing it is capable.
