p. 5.- The ball which was given last Sunday by the Society of Zakinthos, at the Morrison Hotel, was a great success.

Hundreds of people of both sexes danced vivaciously to the melodious strains of two bands.

The success of the ball is attributed to the secretary of the society, H. Antipis, and the whole-hearted cooperation of all the members. The ball was the first major event given by this well-organized society.
