p. 5 - The Thermopylae chapter of the Greek-American organization, Gapa, (Greek-American Progressive Association), will celebrate its anniversary on Oct. 14th, at their hall, 150 West 69th St. The program of the celebration includes a lecture by Dr. K. Theodorou on "The Ideology of Gapa."

Honoring the celebration with their presence will be G. Triantafilakos, President of the Organization,,and K. Soterakopoulos, Grand Commander of the 5th circuit. Free admission to all, members and otherwise.
