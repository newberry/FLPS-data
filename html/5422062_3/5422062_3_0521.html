p. 5.- The gifted five year old, Johana K. Lembesis, daughter of Mr. Constantine Lembesis, can sing and dance with such grace and technique that the American press is beginning to comment on her.

This young butterfly represented our mother Greece in Symposium given by the Y.W. C. A., and again will personify Greece in another congregation given by the high schools. The little girl is so popular in American and Greek circles that her fortunate parents have little opportunity to be with her.
