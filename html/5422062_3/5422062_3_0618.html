The first theatrical performance of the season will be presented by Lord Byron Chapter No. 11 of the Sons of Pericles on Dec. 7, Sunday, at the Eighth Street Theater.

"Esme H. Tourkopoula" will be presented and will be followed by many poems and recitations.

All seats have been sold so the affair promises to he a huge success. Corinthian Damaskinos has promised to attend if he is still in America at that date.
