The concert given by the famous Mr. Constantine Nickolaov before an audience of seven hundred people was a tremendous success.

This Chicago baritone enthralled his audience with his fine voice. He sang various popular Greek songs such as, "O Gero Demos", "E Lafina", and "O Aetos". These well-loved songs were enthusiastically applauded by the audience.

He was assisted by Mr. C. Petropoulos with songs of Smyrna....

The proceeds of the concert are to be sent to Greece for the relief of the widows and orphans.
