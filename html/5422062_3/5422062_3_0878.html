The ten year old Eptanisian club, Allilovoithiatan is giving an outdoor picnic at Marvel Inn Grove (the end of Milwaukee-Devon street car line) on Sunday, July 28.

The Board of Directors of the organization are working hard to give their guests the best of everything--food and drinks, shade and coolness, Greek and American dancing--and above all, a radio, valued at $120 is to be given as a door prize. The admission is $1, women and children are admitted free.

The Greek people of Chicago who have always supported the Eptanisian club, are once more called upon to help it in its endeavors.
