Last week the Greek Confectioners Union of Chicago held its elections. The members elected to office are some of the finest business men of our community.

Mr. D. Papantoniou is president; Mr. N. Johnson, vice-president; and Mr. J. Adinamis, who is also an undertaker, is the secretary.

Saloniki congratulates the above persons upon their election, and hopes that by their energy and progressiveness the Union will become even more successful than it has been in the past.

This Union, when organized and governed in a better manner, can become a solid foundation for business to be built upon.
