The board of trustees of the Central Trust Bank of Chicago met last week to arrange for the promotion of one of the leading Greek citizens of Chicago. This man is Constantine Palaeologos Mammonas. He is head of the foreign department of the bank, and is the first Greek professional man to secure such a high position in the short space of four years.....

He was showered by congratulations and flowers when this became known. Mr. Palaeologos is greatly admired and respected, not only by the Greeks, but also by Americans.
