The Greek Association, Kosmeton St. Anargyros, will hold its tenth annual picnic at the World's Fair Park, 67th and Stony Island Ave., on Monday, August 29. This picnic is looked forward to with great anticipation by the Greek community of Chicago. Kosmetans must pay fifty cents admission to the park. All others will be admitted free.
