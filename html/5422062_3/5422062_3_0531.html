p. 5.- The members of the Liberal Arts Club (Filomouson Despinon) held elections for the coming year last Sunday.

Those elected were: A. Kaloidas, president; N. Hatzopoulos, vice-president; E. Bournazos, secretary: H. Likouris, rec. secretary and ex-president; A. Touloupa, treasurer. Trustees are: N. Perdikes, M. Floros, H. Laris, M. Cokins, D. Sotiriadis and S. Calvert.

We wish them continued success.
