A notable group of clergymen from the Chicago Greek diocese and other prominent Greeks of Chicago, headed by the Most Rev. Philaretos. Metropolitan of Chicago, will visit Oct. 20th, the Greek cemetery and inspect the progress of the construction.

The cemetery is located at the intersection of Fullerton Ave. and Wolf Road, and is dedicated as a burial place of the followers of the Eastern Orthodox Church. The inauguration of the cemetery will take place as soon as it is completed. A temporary chapel is erected to serve the purpose, until a new and beautiful church is built.
