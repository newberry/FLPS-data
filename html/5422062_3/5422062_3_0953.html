The White House, Washington

My dear Mr. Lambros,

The President has received your letter of February 10, and he greatly appreciates your kindness in sending him the copy of your article on Lincoln and Pericles. He will be interested in reading it.

Sincerely yours,

George B. Christian,

Secretary to the President.

Mr. P. S. Lambros,

130 North Wells Street, Chicago, Illinois.
