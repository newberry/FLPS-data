p. 2 -- Mr. Anthony K. Livicianos, linguist and graduate of the Commercial Academy of Cairo, Egypt, has entered the service of the Fort Dear born National Bank as manager of the Greek department.

It is to the advantage of the Greek communities in Chicago and to the advantage of the Greek name to deal with Mr. Livicianos, who is well posted not only in the banking business but in the commerce of the East as well.

The Greek department of the bank of which Mr. Livicianos is manager includes under its heading other nationalities of the East.
