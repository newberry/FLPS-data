The Masonic Temple,where Clarence Darrow was invited to speak before Ahepans, at the invitation of Woodlawn chapter No. 93, was overfilled with thousands of people. At 9:00 P.M. Louis George, A. Peponis, and P. G. Sikokis welcomed the honored guest. Everybody applauded enthusiastically.

Darrow's theme was "My Philosophy of Life". The chairman, Mr. Louis George, thanked the lecturer for coming; Pres. Peponis thanked him on behalf of Woodlawn chapter No. 93 and presented Mr. Darrow's wife with a huge bouquet to show the appreciation of the chapter for her husband.
