Dear Sir:

We cordially invite you and your family, on August 15, 1929, the holiday of Koimisis Tis Theotokou to come to our picnic given by the North Side Greek School, Solon, at Kolzes Electric Park, 6354 Irving Park.

The purpose of this picnic is to celebrate the day and to raise funds for our school.

We are expecting every one of you to come and help us carry out the high hopes we have for our school.

Solon School.

August 2, 1929

Chicago, Illinois.
