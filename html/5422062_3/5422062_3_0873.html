The Tegeatikan Club will have its third outdoor picnic this coming Sunday, July 21, at Irving Park Blvd. and Desplaines River.

This club which is affiliated with the Tegeatikan Club of Greece, promises great things for the Tegeas country.

At the gathering will be shown movies of Greece and in particular parts of Athens, Tripoli, and Tegea.

The Board of Directors of the club, whose president is George Gatsis, broker, will consider it an honor to have present the Arkadikan Clubs and the other Greeks of Chicago at this festival.
