p. 4.- Under the auspices of Navarinon Lodge of the Order Gapa, a public lecture will be held on November 17th, 2 P. M. at Hull House.

Mr. George Kyriakopoulos, well known attorney, journalist and former assistant states attorney, will deliver the lecture on the theme "With What Means Will the Greeks of America Maintain Their Race."

As this lecture is of paramount importance all the Greeks and their families are invited to attend and enjoy it as a spiritual recreation and as a national initiation. Free admission to all.
