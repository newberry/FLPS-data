p. 5.- The filming of the Hundredth Anniversary of Greece's Liberation is ready to be shown at the Milo Theater next Monday evening, by Mr. J. Boumbakis. For over two and one half hours the audience will see Ahepans and Gapans of America taking part or witnessing the various festivals and performances which took place in Greece.

Messrs. Kolovos and Athanasakos are expecting a huge crowd and great success at this showing, so let's not disappoint them.
