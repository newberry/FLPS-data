Sing, O New Generation!

Life's sweet melody,

With flowers of joy

Bespangled exhilaration.

Thy common Grecian start,

O Maid, do not throw aside;

Learn to cherish with pride

"Mother Greece" in thy heart.

For only then thou wilt be

O Maid, a good American

When thou wouldst honor, O Grecian

The "Mother" of the free.

2

The "Mother" who gave birth

To light that the ages endowed;

The "Mother" whose renowned

Parthenons fill the earth.

With glory and grace abide

In song, O New Generation!

Of Greece, the exultation

America's own pride.

Thine the beauty of the dawn

And glory's beauty thine;

Thou, O New Generation the shrine

And our country's hope to own.

3

Glory laurels will scatter

Whenever thou wilt cross the land

If, O New Generation, thy hand

In thy oath will not falter.

Hail! O Grecian maid. Hail!

Greco-American glory;

Hail! Thou too, our new country,

And thou, O "Great Mother", hail!
