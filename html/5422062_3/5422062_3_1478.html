p. 3.. Tomorrow, Sunday, a preacher from the North Side will speak and has selected as his subject the miracles of the Church of Christ from the time when the Church was founded to the present. Another speaker of the day will be Mr. Espen, a graduate of the Philosophical School of Calcutta, India, who is now a student at the Western Theological Seminary. His subject will be the Orthodox Church in India, which is known as the Church of the Apostle Thomas, and its connection with the Greek Orthodox Church.

Everybody is invited to attend with his esteemed family at 7:30 p. m. in Hull House, Halsted and Polk Streets.
