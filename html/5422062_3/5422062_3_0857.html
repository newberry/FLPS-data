For the first time in the annals of the Greek Community in Chicago a family lake excursion to Milwaukee will take place on August 18th and sponsored by the committee of St. Basil church.

The boat Christopher Columbus has been chartered by the committee and the fare is $12.25 the round trip.

Dancing, music and other entertainment are included in the excursion. Enjoy the hours of sea breeze, and forget your troubles and worries. Get your tickets early.

Many prominent Greeks and members of various societies have registered for the trip.
