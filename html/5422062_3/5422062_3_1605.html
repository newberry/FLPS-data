p. 4- September 3, Labor Day, will be celebrated in Chicago with the usual festivities and ceremonies.

On this day, a Marathon race will be run, sponsored by the Metropolitan Athletic Club of Chicago. Two Greek boys, Lamoros Lambrakis and K. P. Savoyas, will compete for the prize. Lambrakis is the young Greek who recently won a Marathon race from Evanston to Grant Park. Good luck to both of them!
