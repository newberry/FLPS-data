p. 3 The musical association, the Batalka Academy of Musical Art, gave a concert last week at the Auditorium, directed by Prof. Geo. Kampas.

Mr. Kampas and his ninety-three pupils, most of them Greek boys and girls, received applause and congratulations from all the American music masters who gathered these for the occasion. He as solist, performed a most difficult selection, the Ave Maria of Shubert - Whilhelm, with such technique that every one was astonished.
