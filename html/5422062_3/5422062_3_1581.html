The Greek heavyweight champion wrestler, James Londos, whose strength and dexterity are attracting the admiration of his opponents, and also of the crowds who witness his matches, will wrestle on November 5th the Italian champion wrestler, Gardini, reputed to be a formidable athlete. This match will take place in the Coliseum at Fifteenth Street and Wabash Avenue and is expected to be one of the biggest contests ever held in Chicago. A large attendance is assured.
