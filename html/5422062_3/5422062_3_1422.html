With her theme "What Greek Women Contributed to Mankind and Civilization," Mrs. Kaskas, teacher in the afternoon classes of St. Basil School, delivered a lecture, last Monday, at the international exposition of the City Women's Club, 360 North Michigan Ave.

Ladies representing practically all nationalities took part in the exposition, at which culinary samples were exhibited. The Greek table of the exhibition, due to the activities of Mrs. Kaskas and Mrs. Alexander, presented an exceptionally rich variation of food.

A group of Grecian girls danced various Greek dances to the delight of the gathering.
