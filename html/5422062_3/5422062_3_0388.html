Tonight, at 8:30 P. M., in the Bowen Hall of the Hull House, a concert is being given by the Greek baritone Christ Nicholaou. Mr. Nicholou will be assisted by other Greek artists and two young American ladies Miss Howler and Miss Smith, who are known in musical circles as first class sopranos.

Mr. Nicholaou will sing "The Prologo of Paliaci," "Cavatina of Faust", "Aria of Carmen," "The Shepherd Boy," "Gero Demos" (Old Demos) and "Sypna Agapi Mou Glykia" (Awaken My Sweet Love).
