p. 5.- One of the most progressive and energetic Greek organizations of Chicago is the Megalopolitan Club. Two years ago this club built one of the finest gymnasiums in Greece. Yesterday an additional $1,250 was voted for expansion and other improvements. Nor is this the only work accomplished in the two years.

At the recent elections the following officers were elected:

S. Katsantonis - president

P. Asimakopoulos- vice-president

T. Karatsaferis - treasurer

I. Karampatsos - secretary

Trustees are: G. Christofilopoulos, N. Basilakos, P. Kontogiannis, A. MPakas, C. Korelas, E. Lellos, D. Koutsoulis, and D. Zerbas.
