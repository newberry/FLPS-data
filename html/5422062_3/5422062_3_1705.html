p. 5.- The Cretan Society of Chicago and of the nearby states, held their elections last Sunday at the Sherman Hotel and the following were elected:

For Chicago: Styl. Saridakis, President; Emman. Lydakis, Vice President; Dr. J. Volicos, Treasurer; P. Gerakaris, Secretary; D. Kokogiannis, Dean.

Out of town Commanders: George Stefanakis, Chicago Heights; Geo. Vidalakis, Joliet; Spyro Glabedakis, Gary, Ind.; Nicholas Skarakis, Kenosha, Demetrios Zeakis, Davenport, Iowa.

The Cretan Society of Chicago and the adjacent states are branches of the Pan-Cretan Fraternity which is a national organization.
