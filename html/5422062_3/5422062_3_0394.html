p. 5.- The Greek Ladies Philomusical Society will hold a reception in the Club Room, Art Institute, tomorrow, Sunday, from 3 to 6 P.M., in honor of Constantine Pougialis, a distinguished painter, who was awarded prizes in two expositions. Mr. Pougialis has some of his paintings on view at the Art Institute, at present. The program will include a lecture, music, and refreshments. Free admission.
