p. 1.- The new ordinance forbidding peddlers on the city streets has gone into effect. This naturally affects the Greeks, because they are very numerous in this trade. The Greeks have voiced a protest against this ordinance, but to no avail.

Hope hasn't been given up yet. They have taken the matter to court and are preparing another protest. A meeting was held at Horan Hall last Sunday for this purpose. The principal speaker was Andrew Vlachos who urged the Greeks to stand together and fight.

The Greek Ambassador, Mr. Lambros Koromilas was also there. Other speakers were Mr. Karagiannis, president of the movement, and Messrs. Tsambas, Drabilas, and D. Eutaxias.
