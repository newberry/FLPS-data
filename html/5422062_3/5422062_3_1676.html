p. 5.- The newly elected officers of the Greek Masonic Lodge, who will be installed Dec. 10th are as follows:

Stavros Nostrantas, Master; P. S. Stavropoulos, Senior Warden; Paul Alexander, Junior Warden; N. Protopapas, Cashier; and Andy Zeimet, Sec'y. John Lampos, K. Tsitiolis, N. Govis and Aristotle Kolias, were installed in the remaining offices.

The installation will take place at the Masonic Temple, 32 W. Randolph St.
