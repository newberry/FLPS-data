P. 6 - The Pan Dramatic Company will present the play "The Adopted Daughter and the Bandit Chief Krikelas" at the Blackstone Theater on Sunday, May 18, at 8:30 P. M. The cast has been trained by Mr. Paul Telegan, dramatic instructor, and Miss Manou and Mrs. Katanogia have the leading parts. It is an idyllic drama in three scenes by Ieannes Botsares. Tickets are on sale at the Blackstone Theater,at the Atlantis Bookstore, and at Mouzakiotes Brothers' store.
