p. 5.- Before leaving Chicago for New York, the beautiful Miss Alice Diplarakou, appeared and talked to an American audience once more. This time "Miss Europe" gave a lecture at North Western University in Fisk Hall.

To the more than five hundred people, seventy-five of whom were Greeks, "Miss Europe" was presented by the Dr. Morley, Professor of Classical Languages, who emphasized the fact that "Miss Europe" is not only beautiful in body, but in mind, spirit and soul as well.

Miss Diplarakou's theme, "The Delphian Festivals", captivated the audience and to many it revealed the perpetual reincarnation of ancient Greek philosophy and arts.

Miss Diplarakou's lecture was honored by all the professors of archeology and classical studies, among them, Dr. John Scott, famous Professor of Homerics, gratulated Miss Diplarakou.

Afterward "Miss Europe" received personal congratulations from everyone.
