September 15, 1933.

Dear Mr. Lambros:

The Royal Italian Ministries for Foreign Affairs and for the Royal House in Rome, Italy, have requested me to convey to you the sincere thanks of His Majesty the King of Italy and of His Excellency Premier Mussolini for the copies of the Greek Star sent by you containing articles on the transatlantic fliers of Italy.

Yours very truly,

Mr. P. S. Lambros,Publisher,

The Greek Star,

7720 Sheridan Road,

Chicago, Illinois.

G. Castruccio,

Royal Italian Consul Geberal.
