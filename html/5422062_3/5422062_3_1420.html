p. 5.- On Feb. 26, the St. Andrews women had a very enjoyable gathering in the hall of the church.

Mr. Lambros, president, in a few words introduced the mandolinists, Messrs. Bournas, Nikolopoulos, Papajohn, Koubarakou, and Diogenis. Mr. Benetatos presented Mrs. I. Tanagra Kopadis who spoke on "Women's Position in Civilization." Mr. Papageorge sang a few select songs.

Refreshments were served after the program. Everybody left with the impression that such association and gatherings are delightful and that there should be more of them.
