More than one hundred and twenty-five of the finest Greek people of the city attended the lecture and tea given in Ida Noyes Hall of the University of Chicago last Sunday afternoon. It was sponsored by the Greek Women's University Club. The speaker was Dr. Nicholas D. Cherouis, professor of chemistry and well-known scientist, who spoke on the subject, "Our Synthetic Civilization"..... At the close of his address twelve young girls, high school graduates, were introduced to the audience. They were the pledges of the Club.

Tea and cake were then served so that everyone might meet Dr. Cherouis.
