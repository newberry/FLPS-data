Last Sunday, the Logan Square Club held an athletic meet. Five Greek boys, members of the Greek Athletic Club of Chicago, scored victories. Mr. Spyros Vorres is president of this fine Greek organization.

The Logan Square Club offers its congratulations to these five young men for their victories. Their names are John Costopoulos, A. Tsekinis, J. Droulias, G. Klarinikos, and W. Sheriki.

Saloniki heartily applauds these lads and their fine club.
