p. 5.- The United Gapan Chapters are preparing for us a theatrical performance to be given on April 21st, at the Eighth Street Theater. The play chosen is, "Tyhi Tis Maroulas" ("The Luck of Maroulas").

Thousands of Chicagoans have seen this play in Athens in the old days and they will be glad to see it again performed by the Gapans.
