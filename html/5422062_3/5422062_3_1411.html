p. 5 Dr. Nicholas D. Cherkonis gave a lecture last Friday before the Greek Professional Men's Club of Chicago. The theme of his lecture was "My Impressions on my Trip to Greece". The chairman, Dr. P. Chronopoulos, opened the assembly by annoucing that a series of lectures had been arranged to be presented to the chapter.

He then introduced Dr. Cheronis who was heartily applauded. His lecture, which was both interesting and informative, held the undivided attention of every listener till the very end. Everyone present at the Sherman Hotel last Friday hastened to congratulate and thanked Dr. Cheronis for this wonderful lecture.
