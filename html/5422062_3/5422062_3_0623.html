p. 5 At the Civic Theatre, last Sunday, the school PLATO gave a theatrical performance titled ,"MARIA DOXAPATRI", performed by boys and girls and it was crowned with great success.

At the close of the performance, over fifty girls attired in snow-white native costumes danced the famous and historic dance of "ZOLOGOU" which was applauded for many minutes. The effect was both magnificent and sentimental.

Proceeds go to the treasury of the school. "PLATO"
