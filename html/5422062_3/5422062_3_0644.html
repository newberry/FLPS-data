A triumphal presentation by Mario Rotziron for the benefit of St. George's Greek School, Sunday, July 21, at 8:15 P. M. In cooperation with the artistic couple, Mr. and Mrs. Th. Pofanti, and the Greek Mandolin Orchestra of Chicago under the able direction of Mr. P. Vourna.

Two hundred Greek boys, accompanied by a grand orchestra, will sing Sakelarides' enchanting song, "The Gypsy Witch."

Auditorium Theatre.
