The well-to-do and enterprising Govostis Brothers, who already own and excellent lunchroom on West Lake Street, have recently opened a new exclusive lunchroom at 3951 1/2 West Twelfth Street. We congratulate our progressive and ambitious fellow countrymen and wish them great profits.

Messrs. Leonidas and George Siavelis have opened a new and exclusive lunchroom near the busiest intersection of the South Side, at 828 East 63rd Street. We wish our good friends much profit.
