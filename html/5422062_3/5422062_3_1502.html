P. 3--Under the auspices of the Pan-Hellenic Society of Chicago public lectures will be delivered next Sunday in the Auditorium of Hull House. Attorney F. Papakostas, LL. D., will lecture on "Pages from Our National History, with a Summary of the Dark Era, 1453-1821." Attorney Pan Bacos will deliver a lecture on "Our Duty to Our Country."

The Philharmonic Society has undertaken to furnish the audience with popular and patriotic orchestral selections and songs. A galaxy of amateurs composes this Society.

Spyros Maurelis will recite various poems, including "The Vigil of Crete." Admission is free to all, and no collection will be taken.
