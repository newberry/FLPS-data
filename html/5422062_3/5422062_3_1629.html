Next Monday at the Aragon, a Grand Ball will be given by the Greek Legionaires of Chicago, on the occasion of Armistice day. The proceeds will be used for the erection of a monument in honor of the Greek-American veterans. The diners will be honored by the recently elected Senator James Hamilton Lewis and other prominent Americans.

The success of the Ball and Dinner is secured and it is the duty of all Greeks to be present to honor the brave soldiers, who honored the Greek name in the American Army.
