P.3--In order to hear and honor their distinguished compatriot Dr. Apostolos Apostolopoulos, a statesman of Greece and publisher of a nationalistic newspaper, the Taniate Society of St. George has called a special meeting for the occasion, to take place next Sunday, July 8, at 3 P. M. in Thompson Hall, 75 Randolph Street.

Dr. Apostolopoulos, who last Sunday delivered a Pan-Hellenic lecture at the meeting of the Greek community of Chicago in Hull House, will lecture this time on "The Affairs of Kynouria, " the native county of the members of the Society of St. George.

Although the lecture will be on the local affairs of Kynouria, those who missed the opportunity to hear the eminent visitor last Sunday will do well to attend this meeting and hear the Greek statesman speak.
