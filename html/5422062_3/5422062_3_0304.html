p. 4- Last week the recently organized Association of Chicago Greek Confectioners held its first election of officers, and the following persons were elected: John Manousopoulos, president; Demetrios Floros, vice-president; Christ Christakos, treasurer; Basil Varelas, secretary, and Basil Yannios, G. Chicnias, D. Michalopoulos, G. Sekokes, D. Konstantinou, and [gap]. Bolas, members of the board.

The next meeting of the Association will be held on July 19 to discuss important matters concerning the trade.
