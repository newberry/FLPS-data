The young ladies Greek society "New Generation", on July 21st will give a county frolic at romantic Pilsen Park,26 Albany St.

The young girls, who compose the society, always strive to present something new and unique and this time will provide unusual entertainment for everyone who takes part in the frolic.

They have something new and altogether suprising. The Greeks will be amazed to find that until now the "Greeks have no word for it."

The girls chose this site, for the frolic on account of its romantic surroundings. Probably they have something up their sleeve.
