p. 3.- To all members!

All members of the Greek Confectioners Association are invited to a general meeting on Friday, October 2, at 2 o'clock at 75 E. Randolph St.

An important discussion will be held on "The Chocolate Market."

We want everyone present as this is a very important matter.

Chicago, Sept. 29, 1908

President

K. Loomos

Secretary

B. Giannios
