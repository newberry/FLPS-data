p. 2- Next Wednesday night, May 10, at the North Side Turner Hall, the Greek amateurs of Chicago will perform the popular comedy, E Lyra tou Yeronikola (The Lyre of Old Nicholas), for the benefit of the Greek church.

The members of the cast are: P. Shioris, N. Lymberopoulos, Miss Prenshis, J. Cocalis, P. Polymenacos, B. Kontaxis, C. Kondiles, J. Karolocos, and Geo. Matalas.
