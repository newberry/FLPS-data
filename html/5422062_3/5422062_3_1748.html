The Eptanisian club gave a dance last Sunday at the La Salle Hotel. The hall was crowded due to the efforts of the Board of Directors. There was a huge profit from the dance which will go to the National Hero of Athens fund. The crowd proved that every Greek affair can be a success if organized the right way. The committee was:

George Kokalis, president

A. Chonoromaras, vice-president

A. Pagoulatus, secretary

I. Zerbos, treasurer

A. Mourikos- rec. secretary
