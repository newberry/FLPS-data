p. 2.- According to the final decision of the artistic committee of 7, composed from distinguished painters, sculptors and other artists, the six members mentioned below were chosen from the total of twelve final contestants as the first six winners of the Grand Prizes of the 1935 contest.

No. 44 (Miss Helen Litsardakis) having 5 votes for the first prize and 2 votes for the third prize, has been selected as the "Grecian Queen of 1935" and given the "First" prize.

No. 61 (Miss Sophie Pappas) having 2 votes for the first prize, 4 votes for the second prize and 1 vote for the fourth prize, has been selected as the "First Princess" and given the "Second" prize.

No. 62 (Miss Dina Papangelis), "Second Princess" given "Third" prize.

No. 1 (Miss Mary Antona) as "Fourth" prize winner.

No. 14 (Miss Demetra Collias) as "Fifth" prize winner.

No. 73 (Miss Sophie D. Stamos), sixth prize winner.
