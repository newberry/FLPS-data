The well-functioning society Messenia, which from time to time gives lectures of enlightenment and progress, tomorrow, (Sunday) at 2 P. M. will have a lecture given at Hull House.

George Kyriakopoulos, attorney, former Assistant State's Attorney, will deliver the lecture on "The History of Messenia and What It Has Contributed to Greek Civilization."

All who are interested in lectures of this kind are invited to attend. Admission free.
