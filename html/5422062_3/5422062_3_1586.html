The outstanding athletes of the Greek-American Olympic Club are constantly winning new laurels for themselves. In their final contests the following emerged victorious: in the 115 pound class, H. Metropoulos and A. Tsekinis; in the 125 pound class, J. Vorres, H. Metropoulos and A. Tsekinis; in the 135 pound class, Spyros Vorres.....

First prize was given to H. Metropoulos, who threw his opponent in fifteen minutes. Third prize was won by A. Tsekinis.

New matches will be held on March 2 and 3. All sport fans are invited to attend.
