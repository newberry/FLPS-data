Much praise is due to the organizer of the concert, Nicholas Matsoukas; Demetrios Papageorge, Director of the choir; the girls and boys comprising the choir, and the Rev. Niketas Kesses, who with melodious voices and technique, sang the responses (Antiphones)..

Participants in the choir were:

Sopranos: Misses Bertha Florou, Angelika Lari, Angelika Kourla, Adamantia Govasti and Elizabeth Tsirigou.

Anti-sopranos: Misses Sophia Bexi, Bertha Lymberi, Lora D. Lymberi, Maria Ch Lymberi, Metaxia Govasti, and Mrs. Sophia Bexi.

Tenors: Christ Georgopoulos, Peter Sarelas, Nicholas Monemvasitis, E. Granias, Demetrios Charakas and Dem. Karambelas

Baritones: Andrew Spadis, Spyrus Stamos, Constantine Karambelas, Peter Karteroulis and St. Galeneas.

Basses: Athanasios Kokinos, Demetrios Michalopoulus, Anastasios Nosis and Demetrius Karistakis.

The concert was given at the Byzantine Exposition. The Byzantine Exposition was sponsored by the University of Chicago.
