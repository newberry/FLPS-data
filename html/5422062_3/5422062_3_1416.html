p. 5.- Mr. K. Argyropoulos (Constantine Argoe) gave his lecture last Monday night. Not only were there many Greeks present, but Americans also. His speech was marvelous. Mr. Gekas, who introduced him, spoke of Mr. Argyropoulos with great respect. His lecture lasted over an hour and held the interest of everyone present.

After his lecture everybody wanted to meet him and congratulate him. We are proud of our countrymen.
