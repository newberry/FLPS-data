The Greek Students' Club of Chicago, working jointly with the Professional Men's group, has planned to give a series of lectures. The lectures will be on modern trends of thought and science.

They invite all those who would like to be one of the lecturers to send in their names and the topic of their lecture. A list of the lecturers and their subjects, will be drawn up as soon as all names come in.

Please send this information to the Greek Student Association "Plato", c/o Dr. P. Harris, 11 North Ashland Boulevard, Chicago.
