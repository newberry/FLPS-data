p. 4 Kimisis tis Theotukou Church is preparing a theatrical performance for Sunday, May 25 at the Civic Theater, Wacker Drive and Washington Blvd. Students of Platow School will participate in the play "Maria Doxa Patri" directed by T. Popantis. All performers will be dressed in Byzantine costumes. Aside from the play, there will be a varied program the girls of the school will dance Greek, P. Bournas will play the nandolin, A. Kambas' orchestra will play, Miss P. Haloulos will dance and Misses Filiutis will sing. Everyone who comes will be more than satisfied with the performance.
