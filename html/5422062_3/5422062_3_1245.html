P. 6- With delight the Greek community in Chicago greets the new monthly periodical Panhellenic, published by Miltiader Papademetriou, Doctor of Philosophy and a journalist of long standing.

The Star with real joy welcomes the new magazine and wishes success to its illustrious publisher and editor Dr. Papademetriou. It further wishes for it proper support by the Greek community.

The Panhellenic is a beautifully illustrated magazine with a complete repertory of selected articles. It really deserves undivided support.
