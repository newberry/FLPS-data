Entertainment and dance to be given by the Assiciations, St. George, of Kastretans, Sunday night, Nov. 15th, at Plasterers Temple Hall, 330 S. Marshfield avenue. (Kastri is a town in the Peloponnesus or Southern part of Greece--Translator). The thirty-fifth anniversary of the Association will be celebrated, and it is to expected that not only the 2,000 Kastretans of Chicago will attend, but hundreds of other of our nationals who will spend the evening and enjoy themselves with our fellow Kastretians.
