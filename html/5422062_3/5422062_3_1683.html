p. 5.- Amaltheia, the society of Cretes of Chicago and suburbs will give its first annual picnic, June 21st, at Grand View Gardens, 6401 W. Grand Avenue.

The aim of the society is to impart Greek habits and customs to the new generation. Of special importance to the Cretes is the ceremony for the holiday of Kleidonou which will take place the day of the picnic. The program of the day will include various athletic games.
