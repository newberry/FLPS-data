The Stenagmoi Tou Daphne (The Sighs of Daphne), a poetic collection of our pastoral and idyllic poet, Lazaros A. Lazarides, has been recently published and printed by the Saloniki press in an artistic and attractive volume.

Mr. Lazarides is already known to the American public by his previous poetic publications; recently a most favorable critique of his last work was made by Professor Phoutrides of Harvard University.

The price of the book is twenty-five cents, and is sold by A. G. Katsoulis, P. O. Box 478, Hibbing, Minnesota. It is mailed free of charge.
