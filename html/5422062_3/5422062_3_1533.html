p. 5 The Lord Byron Chapter of the Sons of Pericles is feverishly preparing to meet in a basket ball contest the Centaurs, next Saturday night, at Hull House.

It is expected that the game will be fought with so much enthusiasm that both sides claim the victory in advance. The Centaurs are headed by Ph. Kollias.

These Greek-American boys of both sides are ready and willing to show their parents and the rest of the Greeks in Chicago how competent they are not only in their studies but also in athletic games.

Everybody is invited to come and witness the contest. Price twenty five cents.
