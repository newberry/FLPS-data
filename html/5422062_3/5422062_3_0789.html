The Stereoladitan picnic at Polonia Grove was a huge success last Sunday. Hundreds of our Greek countrymen attended. The Board of Directors worked hard to make it a success and they were rewarded. They are as follows: President, L. Diamesis; Vice-President, J. Gekas; Second Vice-President, G. Pelonis; Secretary, J. Pezaitis; Treasurer, L. Patrinos; and Counselors: S. Kanzos, B. Bouranas, L. Lucas, G. Papadopoulos, G. Patargias, K. Mougias, N. Katsadimas, K. Papadimitrios, E. Loukopoulos, O. Tourlakis, and P. Salopoulos.
