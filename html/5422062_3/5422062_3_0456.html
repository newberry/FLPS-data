Fun! Fun! Fun!

Who is the present creator of laughter and joy among Chicago Greeks, and, with his inimitable satire and keen mind, held the Athenians spellbound for many years, at the unforgettable pond? The man who was designated, King of Laughter; the artist, Theodoropoulas, is in town with his shadow show, Karagiozis.

The garden of the known Coffeehouse, Panellenion; 843 W. Polk St., will become for Chicago what the Pond was for the Athenians.
