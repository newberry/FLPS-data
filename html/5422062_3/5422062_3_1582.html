On March 24, the Greek Post of the American Legion sponsored a wrestling program at the Casino Theater. Three pairs of Greek wrestlers took part in the bouts; among them were the nationally known George Basil and Spyros Vorres.

It would be a reflection on us if we did not express our thanks to Mr. D. Pantelis, a Chicago lawyer, who made this event such a success by his energy and sincere interest. We also wish to thank the young men who took part in the bouts.

The Post plans to sponsor another program of this kind in the near future.

George Koletis

Chairman of Publicity Committee
