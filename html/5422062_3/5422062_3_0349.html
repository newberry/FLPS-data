p. 4. That part of our community which loves the finer things in life and appreciates good music will have an excellent opportunity to hear Maria Parissi, soprano, in a song recital on Sunday at the Concert Hall of the Lyon and Healy Building on Jackson and Wabash.

The program includes seventeen selections in all, and Mrs. Parissi, with her exceptional musical talent will give us all an enjoyable afternoon.

Included in Mrs. Parissi's program will be our beloved singer, Andrea De Parry. We should all be present, as the proceeds will be donated to a fund for the needy Greeks of Chicago.
