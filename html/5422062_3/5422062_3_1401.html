p. 4.- Evsebia, the Women's Club of St. Basil church, is planning a series of Sunday lectures at the church, 733 S. Ashland Blvd.

The first lecture will take place Sunday, December 14, at 3 P.M. Dr. S. Nittis will speak on "Magic in Medicine". He is a doctor of medicine at the University of Chicago and taught for three years at the University of Michigan.

Admission is free!
