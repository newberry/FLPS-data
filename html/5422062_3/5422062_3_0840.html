George A. Cokins, president of St. Basil's Church has announced that the climax of their Mardi Gras will be a popularity contest in which young Greek ladies of Chicago will participate.

The winner will be crowned Queen on the night of the ball. The Queen will have five attendants. She will have the choice of a trip to Greece or a Sedan.

Over 200 girls have already entered the contest. We are certain that many more will do so before the judging takes place.
