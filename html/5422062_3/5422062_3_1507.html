P. 2 - Dr. and Mrs. Geo. Koremelas have arrived here from St. Leuis for a three-week visit. They were received by a committee of prominent Greeks of the community.

Dr. Keromelas, who is a specialist in tubercular diseases, came to America as the representative of the Greek government at the Universal Medical Convention in St. Louis, where he made a deep impression by his specialized knowledge.

Next Sunday the learned Greek will lecture to a Greek audience on "Phthisis (tuberculesis) and Phthonos (ill will)."
