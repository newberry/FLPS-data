Last Thursday, in the hall of Chapter No. 46, of the Greek organization, Ahepa, a lecture on Venereal Diseases was given by the well known doctor, Thomas Marantis' The speaker was introduced by the President of the chapter, George Porikos, a lawyer.

Dr. Marantis' speech was very analytical and instructive. The manner in which he opened his theme showed that his studies on the subject are accurate and thorough. In his lecture, with the aid of illustrations, he explained in detail the complications of venereal diseases.
