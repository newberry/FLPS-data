The well known Juvenile Kampas Violin Orchestra, composed of thirty children, their ages from 8 to 16, appeared in a variety of programs last month with significant success.

They played at the Trianon Ballroom, March the 10th, before an audience of 6,000 people, and also at the Steuben Club, March 14, and the Cameo Room of the Morrison Hotel, March 25, Mr. Kampas is a westsider.
