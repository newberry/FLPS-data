p. 1.- The desire of the Greeks of Chicago will be fulfilled shortly. The famous tragedienne, Marika Kotopouli, will be in Chicago the first part of February.

Monday, Feb. 9th will be the date and Aragon the place, where every art loving Greek will have the rare opportunity to see the famous tragedienne in "Electra". This will be the only drama Miss Kotopouli will play in Chicago, therefore we urge everybody to pay respect to this goddess of Greek classical drama, Marika Kotopouli.
