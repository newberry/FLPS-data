At the last meeting of the Thrakomikrasiatikon society, "Homer", election of officers for 1932 took place. L. Kelavos is president; Dr. Johannides, vice-president; K. Pantoleon, treasurer; Dr. Haralampides, secretary and P. Kaskas, recording secretary; Advisors are: P. Proskinitupoulos, A. Baxevanis, G. Vanias, P. Mikes, and A. Polikardos.
