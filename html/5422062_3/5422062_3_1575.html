Elections of the Greek Olympic Athletic Club took place on the 18th day of January and the following were elected:

Polychrones Malapanes, President; George Malliris, Vice-President; Menelaos Karras, Treasurer; N. K. Smirlis, General Sevretary; John Lyvaios, Recording Secretary; John Malapanes, Chairman; Geor. Kantalas, Dean, the following are members of the Council. Panagiotis Simadis, Alex Xeros, Nicholas Athanasopoulos.

The gymnasium of the club is at 825 West Polk St., 5th floor, hours 1 P. M. to 10 P. M.
