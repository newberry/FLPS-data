Last Friday, June 13, the chair of St. Constantine Church, consisting of thirty eight young ladies, held elections for the coming year. Those elected were Mary Simadis, president; E. Andritsopoulos, vice-president; Tasoula Petrakis; secretary, and Olympia Katsambis, treasurer. Rev. Marcus Petrakis addressed the choir and praising the young ladies and their director, G. Demopoulos, for their good work.

Basiliki Ch. Katsambis.
