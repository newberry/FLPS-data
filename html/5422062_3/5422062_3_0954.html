The Commerce Court of Arbitration, 10 South La Salle Street, Chicago, Illinois, June 3, 1922.

My dear Mr. Lambros,

Permit me to take this opportunity to thank you for permitting me to have a copy of the Greek Star containing the comparison between the funeral oration of Pericles over the heroes of the Peloponnesian War and Lincoln's Gettysburg speech. I read your comparison with great interest, and it inspired me to compare the original oration by Pericles with the Gettysburg speech. It is indeed astonishing that there is so close a resemblance. Your treatment of the subject is certainly instructive.

With kindest personal regards, I remain

Yours very truly,

J. Kent Greene
