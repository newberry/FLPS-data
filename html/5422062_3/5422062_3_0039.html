p.2- The bank nearest to the Greek colony of the West Side, the Mid-City Trust and Savings Bank, has taken into its service as manager of its Greek department Mr. George Stathopoulos.

Mr. Stathopoulos took his master's degree in economics at the University of Lucerne in Switzerland, and is proficient in many European languages. He has acquired experience in all lines of business by working in various European commercial houses. During the last two years he has been employed by the First National Bank of Chicago, from which he now comes to be the manager of the Greek department of the Mid-City Trust and Savings Bank.
