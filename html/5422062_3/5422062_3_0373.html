Mr. Themy Georgi, the well known Greek tenor, who has achieved many artistic triumphs in the Vienna Theatre of Melodrama and also in other big cities of Europe, will give on Sunday afternoon, March 25, 1923, in Cohan's Grand Opera House, a recital truly Greek in characteristic.

This is the first time that the Greeks of Chicago have had an opportunity to listen to truly Greek music sung by a Greek artist so distinguished and it will arouse sweet memories of the homeland.

The Greek songs which our artist will sing are the works of such distinguished composers as Samara, Sakellarides, Kalomiris, Hatzapostolos and Xanthopoulos.

Tickets will be sold at popular prices.
