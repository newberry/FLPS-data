The readers of the Greek Press undoubtedly read the article in the previous issue announcing that Mr. and Mrs. Minas Booras were to take part in an operatic performance in the World's Fair Theater. The opera was "The Legend of the Piper," written by Eleanor Everest Freer.

Mrs. Marion Booras sang the role of the heroine, Barbara, and Minas Booras sang the role of Claus. Both of these artists are well known to the Greeks of Chicago. Mr. Booras gave a wonderful performance, his baritone voice being in perfect condition. As usual, the freshness and beauty of the voice of Marion Booras captured the hearts of the audience, who called for encores with enthusiastic applause.
