P.2--Selected tobacco from Greece, that is, from Kavala, Agrinion, Xyromeron, and Tripolis, has been received by the importing house of Kapantais and Company, 338 South Halsted Street. It is packed in fifty-dram packages, each with a large book of cigarette paper, the finest made in Greece.

This consignment comes from Seretopoulos Brothers, Tripolis, who provide the royal court with tobacco. Tickets of the Navy lottery are also sold for sixty cents at the same place. If you wish to enjoy a smoke of the finest tobacco sold, you will find it at Kapantais and Company's, Chicago, Illinois. We ship tobacco to any part of the United States, postage prepaid.
