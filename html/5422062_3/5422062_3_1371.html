At the North Shore chapter of Ahepa, southeast corner of Broadway and Wilson Ave., a debate will be held by the Association of Greek Students on Monday at 7 P.M. The subject will be, "Is it to the interest of Greece to take part in the Pan-Balkan Union?" Messrs Vasileos Belogiannis and George Livanos will take the affirmative and Messrs Andrew Kaser and Paul Kokkinakis the negative.
