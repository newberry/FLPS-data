On May 9, the Greek Olympic Athletic Club of Chicago elected the following officers M. Karamesinis, president; G. Koustis, vice-president; G. Koutalis, treasurer; N. Smirlis, secretary; J. Lymbaios, recorder; and E. Pavlatos, manager. Trustees are S. Borris, P. Malapanis, C. Tsoulos, G. Malliris, and B. Pihos.
