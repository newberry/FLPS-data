On July 18th at the Hall of Ahepa, 190 North State St, Professor George E. Melonas, Doctor of Archeology of the National University in Athens Greece, and now holding summer classes at the University of Chicago, will deliver a lecture in Greek on the theme "Grecian Archeology.

Professor Melonas will be introduced by Prof. Carl D. Buck who teaches modern Greek at the same University. Free admission to all.
