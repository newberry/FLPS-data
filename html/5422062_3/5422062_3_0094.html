p. 1- It is better late than never. The much-needed cooperation among the thousands of Greek confectioneries in Chicago is at last a matter of fact.

With great delight we hear that the Chicago Greek candymen have combined in a huge organization entitled "Greek Confectioners' Association of Chicago." That is progress; over 6,000 candy stores under one roof. Bravo!

In the election held last Sunday the following officers were chosen:

George Chiaquoris, president

S. Phasseas, vice-president

J. Adinames, secretary

K. Johnson, treasurer.

2

The exact number of Greek confectioneries in Chicago is not known. Some wholesalers, claim that there are 6,000 or 7,000, others say there are over 10,000; anyway it is a tremendous force, and if it is properly exercised, the Greek candymen will be noteworthy.

Those who are not yet members of the Association will do a service to themselves and to their trade by applying for membership. If you are united and strong, the syndicate, corporation, or chain-store will not be able to take your business away from you. And the only way to keep the wolf from the door is to be organized, not for to-day only but for tomorrow as well. A word to the wise.
