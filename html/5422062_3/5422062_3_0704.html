Beauty Contest, Gloryfying the Grecian Girl

For the Selection of a Queen of Beauty, and Two Princesses, From The Most Beautiful Grecian Girls of Chicago. The Winners will Take Part in the Greek Movies That Will Be Produced In Chicago Studios, Monday evening, February 18.

Luxurious prizes will be given to the beauties selected by an award committee of most prominent Greek ladies and gentlemen and artists.

This will be the biggest Greek fete that the Greek people of Chicago have ever seen.
