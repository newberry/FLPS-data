The noted scholar, Mrs. Kalliope Theodoropoulos, has arrived in our community from New York. Mrs. Theodoropoulos is the wife of the lawyer and publisher, Miho Theodoropoulos, and is a woman distinguished for her elaborate education. She is visiting the various Greek communities and studying them closely. Her impressions will be published in Parnassos, the periodical of the Greek savants in America.
