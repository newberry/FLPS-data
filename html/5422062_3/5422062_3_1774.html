p. 5.-The Announcement is made to all fellow-countrymen from Achaea and Elia that our society passed the following decisions in its meeting on Sunday, March 14:

(1) The society shall be educational, social, and mutually beneficial.

(2) After a wide discussion on the sallent points the purpose of the society, a committee of seven was elected to lay out the constitution which is to be submitted before the membership at the next meeting for approval.

(3) The next meeting will take place on March 28th, at Hull House, at which time voting on the articles of the constitution and election of officers will the place.
