p. 3.- At a special session, the State of Illinois voted to take part in the World's Fair to be given in Chicago in 1933. Governor Emmerson will head the organization with Paul Demos, prominent Greek lawyer, other directors are Noble Brandon Judah, former U.S. Ambassador to Cuba; Geo. F. Harding, former County treasurer; U. G. Herrman, former Librarian; Anthony Czarnecki, Collector of Customs and J.F. Cornelius, President of the Academy of Fine Arts!
