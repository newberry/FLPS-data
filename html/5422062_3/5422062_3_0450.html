To-night the melodramatic company of Mrs. Pantopoulos, which has given us a series of presentations, winds up with the splendid play of the memorable Kokos, "The Lyre of Old Nickolas". The proceeds of this evening's performance will go towards the erection of a school in Arbuna of Kalabreta.

THE GREEK MELODRAMA.

Next Friday the protagonists and champions of the Greek melodrama will give us an artistic presentation which the Greeks of Chicago rarely have the opportunity of enjoying.

It is a really melodramatic presentation in which the Greek artists will appear dressed in national costume and will present the act "Marco Botsaris" and the pathetic song "Gero Demo" and the other beautiful songs of this Greek opera.
