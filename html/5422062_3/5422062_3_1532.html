The members of the Greek American Legion George Dilboy Chapter No. 13 will present a boxing program on Friday, February 5, 1932, in cooperation with the Greek Olympian Athletic Club. The bouts will take place at the Ashland Auditorium, 328 South Ashland Blvd. (corner Van Buren St.)

We are asking the Greeks to sponsor this affair just as they have sponsored many other ones. They will not be disappointed.

George Dilboy Chapter.
