Children of Zakinthos, not to be outdone by their elders are calling a meeting on Sunday, November 30, at the Cafeteria at Hull House, in order to start a Zakinthian club. All Zakinthians are urged to attend. A committee has been temporarily set up. It is composed of S. Tsouloufis, J. Mitakis, N. Varzos, A. Mouzakis, and C. Aktypis.
