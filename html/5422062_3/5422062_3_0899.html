We wish to announce to all organizations of Chicago and environs that we have set Sunday, June 28, as the date for our picnic at Forest Park.

To avoid duplications and conflicts in our affairs, animated by brotherly feelings, we hereby inform all organizations with which this society wishes to cooperate of the date of our picnic so that they may arrange their affairs for different dates, and that thus we may help one another to make our affairs successful, assisting our local and general aims and purposes.

With brotherly love,

The Educational Society of Kastriton.

642 South Wells Street.

Chicago, Illinois.
