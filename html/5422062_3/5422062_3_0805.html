The Greek community of the South Side is giving on Friday August 15, its twentieth annual picnic at Chernauka's Grove, Archer Avenue and 79th St.

This picnic in the neighborhood of St. Constantine Church will be attended by South Siders and by people from all parts of the city who want to help the church. Wines, roast lambs, and other Greek foods will be served. There will be dancing all day long as well as other forms of entertainment.
