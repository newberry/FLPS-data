p. 5.- The philanthropic society, Eptanision, celebrates its anniversary tomorrow, Sunday, at the church of Holy Trinity.

A sumptuous dinner will follow at the West Side Auditorium, Racine and Taylor Sts.

This philanthropic society which was founded a good many years ago, performs its humanitarian duty quietly, and is functioning very successfully in funds, harmony, and administration.

Members composing this society are natives of the enchanting Aegean Sea.
