Next Sunday, June 24th, all the natives of Kastri and Saint Peter are holding a picnic at Kolze's Electric Park, 6353 Irving Park Blvd. A considerable sum has been collected for the construction of a road between Tripoli-Kastri and Saint Peter. Although much progress has been made there is still some money required to complete the job. This is the purpose of this picnic.

All money raised will be sent for the completion of this road.

Come all, and let us make a summer resort out of our native town, to increase its trade, to honor it, and to be honored by it.
