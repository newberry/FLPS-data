Northwestern University Settlement

Noble and Augusta Streets, Chicago, Illinois.

Mr. P. S. Lambros,

The Greek Star,

130 North Wells Street, Chicago, Illinois.

My dear Mr. Lambros:

Ever since the February 17th edition of your paper came to me, I have meant to write to you and to thank you just as an American citizen for that splendid Lincoln article. I did express myself at our meeting Monday, but I wanted to put myself on record in writing. It was a beautiful testimonial, and I have been eager to tell you so.

2

Hoping to see you at our meeting on Wednesday, I am

Very cordially yours,

Harriet E. Vittum, head resident
