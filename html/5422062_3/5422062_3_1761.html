p. 4.- The Greek Society Phallanthos, which was founded a year ago and is composed of natives from the twelve towns of Phallanthos, will give, tomorrow, Sunday, a symposium with dancing, at the West Side Auditorium Annex Hall, 1010 South Racine Avenue.

The object of the symposium and dance is for the purpose of getting acquainted. All the families from Phallanthos county and their friends are invited to participate in the affair, which according to the program of the organization committee will be an unforgettable event.
