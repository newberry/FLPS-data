p. 3.- Mr. E. Fousianis deserves hearty congratulations for his presentation of the Greek movies last Friday night.

The thousands of Greeks who saw the first presentation stated that it far exceeded their expectations. They asked to have it shown again, so that others might have a chance to see it. The thousands who saw the second performance seemed more delighted if possible, than those who saw the first.

This successful reception by the Chicago Greeks has prompted Mr. Fousianes to give a third performance on March 13.
