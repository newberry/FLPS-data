p. 2.- This brilliant and educational meeting took place at the residence of the past president, Mr. Nick Mason, at 1746 Lawrence Avenue, January 9, 1927, at 4 o'clock in the afternoon, under the chairmanship of the president, T. M. Bishop.

Disposing of the business part of the program first of all, the president then introduced Mr. G. A. Kyriakopoulos, a prominent attorney and assistant prosecuting attorney of our city, to the members and guestsas the speaker of the day. Mr. Kyriakopoulos spoke very efficiently for a whole hour on the subject of "The Kingdom of Pontus."

Once again Mr. G. Kyriakopoulos proved to be a born historian. He held his audience in such a fervor and astonishment that they expressed the desire of having him join the club and enjoy the privilege of his companionship.

2

The speaker was good enough to answer all questions asked following his speech.

The club was also honored with the presence of the following guests: Messrs. John D. Morton, Th. Kalpakis, and Christ J. Pallas, all from the AHEPA, North Shore Chapter No. 94.

The following were then officially initiated for membership: Messrs. E. Castritsis, S. Photiades and J. Sphondouris.

After the singing of the Pontus Song, the meeting was then adjourned and refreshments were served by the hostess.

T. M. Bishop.
