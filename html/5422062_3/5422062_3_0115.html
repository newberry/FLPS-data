Mr. Themis Haliotis and Mr. D. Arahovitis opened a new and up-to-date printing establishment last week.

It is located at 775 South Polk Street, and is called the "Odyssia".

The owners have installed the finest equipment and are able to take care of all the printing requirements of the community. These two men are highly respected and well known; therefore, it is unnecessary for us to recommend them to the community.

We congratulate them and wish them every success in their new location.
