Hundreds of Greeks belong to the Free Masons, and their numbers are ever increasing. Installation of new officers of the Greek chapter took place Thursday at the La Salle Hotel.

The newly elected members are as follows:

Van A. Nomikos, Worshipful Master

Stephen G. Kostran, Senior Warden

Peter S. Stauropoulos, Junior Warden

Nicholas G. Protopapas, Treasurer

Andy M. Zeimet, Secretary

George J. Georgandas, Chaplain

Paul L. Alexander, Senior Deacon

John L. Lampos, Junior Deacon

Constantine N. Chioles, Senior Steward

Nicholas S. Gouess, Junior Steward

Aristotle N. Collias, Marshal

Armin P. Baur, Organist

Thomas J. Kataras, Tyler
