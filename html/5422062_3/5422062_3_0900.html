Today, tomorrow and Monday, in the Greek school Socrates, 742 Sibley St., a grand bazaar will take place for the benefit of the school.

Brother-Greeks and merchants, have donated many and various articles to be sold at the bazaar.

Ladies and young girls of our community are ready to overflow the market with rare and beautiful hand embroideries.

The object of the bazaar is to maintain and upkeep the school, therefore, no one will be absent from this three day festival.
