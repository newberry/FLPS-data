The dramatic performance presented by the members of the Epsilon Phi Epsilon sorority last Tuesday was a tremendous success.

The play was entitled "Bridegroom Sought in Chicago," and was written by the well-known writer of our community, James Mihalaros. The young men and women in the cast performed their roles with enviable ability. Of course, credit for the superiority of the performance must be attributed, in part, to the patient and skilled supervision of J. Athanasopoulos, who was in charge of the production.....
