The Greek Men's Professional Club of Chicago sponsored a lecture by Doctor Spyros Soter. This lecture was presented last Sunday and was one of the best received talks of the series.....

Dr. Soter spoke on "Surgeons and Surgery," a topic of great interest to the average dudience because so few laymen know anything about this fascinating subject.....

Speaking in perfect Greek, Dr. Soter made it clear he had comprehensive knowledge of his subject.....Although the subject was a technical one, the speaker explained himself so capably that all of the hundred or more people in the audience clearly understood his explanations.
