To-morrow, Sunday, for the first time the all Greek Basketball tournament will be held at De Paul, Webster and Sheffield Aves.

The committee, which organized the first annual basketball competition, selected the best teams for the purpose. They are as follows: The Omega and Alpha club, Milwaukee, Wis., which has won two annual victories since it was formed three years ago; the Sons of Pericles, of Sophocles chapter, Gary, Ind., which holds first place in local games of that city. This group is composed of Greek students attending high schools in Gary; the chapter Ypsilantis of Chicago, headed by Sam Sotes, who is one of the best athletes of Hyde Park high school.

The Lane Indians, from the recently organized chapter Greek Center, of the Sons of Pericles, composed of students of West Chicago and headed by Elias Stergios and B. Mavromatis; the Delta and Lambda recently organized and composed of graduates of the Military School, Howe and the Centaurs of Hull House, 2which are headed by Varellas, B. Galanopoulus and D. Kitsopoulos.

The games of these seven teams will be played under the auspices of Mr. John Raklios, President of Raklios and Co.

After the games four trophies will be awarded. The cups will be presented by Mr. John Raklios, Deligiannis Bros, the Chicago Pie Co. and Mr. John Askounis.

It is the first time that athletic teams, whereby made up of Greek boys, will compete on such a large scale, and undoubtly the Greeks of Chicago and suburbs will be delighted to meet them.
