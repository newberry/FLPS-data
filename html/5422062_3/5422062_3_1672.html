Last Wednesday, G. Papadopoulos, Supreme President of the Karpathian Educational Progressive Club, whose headquarters are in Pittsburgh, installed a Chicago chapter of this organization. Andrew Karzas who is a Kapathian donated his offices and the Trianon ballroom.

After business formalities were over officers were elected. They are President, Dr. -. De Meur; Secretary, E. Karatianos; Treasurer, B. Aslaniois; and Advisors, J. Chrisos and B. Karagiannis.

The club has five chapters in the united States. Its purpose is to build schools and hospitals in Karpatha, Greece.
