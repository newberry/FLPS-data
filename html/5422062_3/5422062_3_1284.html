The Byzantine choir of the Church of the Annunciation, under the direction of the well known musician, D. Papageorge, will sing, for the second time Byzantine hymns over the Chicago Tribune Radio Station, W.G.N., this afternoon, at 2:45 P.M. When, for the first time the choir of Mr. Papageorge sang on the ocassion of a request by the management of the Chicago Greek Daily, the W.G.N. Station received more than 700 telegrams of congratulations from all parts of America.

The poet, Mr. D. Mihalaros, will speak at the same time in English on the subject "Greek Easter Sunday."
