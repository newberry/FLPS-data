The Greek Professional Men's Club has resolved to give from time to time lectures by various professional men.

The first of these lectures will take place May 2nd, 9:00 p.m. at the Sherman Hotel, with N. Cheronis as lecturer, on "My Impression of Greece." Mr. Cheronis,Doctor of Chemistry, visited many parts of Greece during his sojourn there, and he is known as a great patriot.

Undoubtedly his lecture will be of great importance and interest to all.
