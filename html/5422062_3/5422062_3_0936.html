p. 1.- A movie will be shown in the auditorium of the school of St. George Church this coming Sunday. The film is Eros Nika ("Love Wins"), and Aihmalosia Tou Lambrou Ypo Ton Liston ("The Capture of Lambros by the Bandits"). Archrev. Daniel Golemis will explain the plot of the picture. The performance will start at 3 o'clock. Admission is fifty cents for adults and twenty cents for children. Proceeds will go toward the school fund.

A musical program by Aliki Nikolaou will follow. All North Siders are expected to be present at this gathering to see this wonderful show, hear this wonderful program, and help this wonderful cause.
