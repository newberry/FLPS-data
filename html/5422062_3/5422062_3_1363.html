The Greek Professional Club of Chicago considers it an honor to let the public know that on Saturday evening, October 22, at 9:30 P.M., a debate will take place under its auspices on the subject, "Will Hellenism of America Disappear?"

Mr. Paul Tzavaras will support the affirmative, while Mr. Spiros Kotakis will take the negative.

Both debaters are qualified to handle this question since both have been living continuously for over a quarter of a century in Chicago. They are journalists and have followed and studied Hellenism in all its expressions in America.

\2

There is no doubt they will give us a fine treat on Saturday evening when they will argue and present the reasons for their respective positions on the subject. Admission 25 cents.

Respectfully yours,

The Greek Professional Club of Chicago.
