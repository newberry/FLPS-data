The Greek Olympic Athletic Club of Chicago announces that those who desire to train themselves for participation in the Olympic Games to be held next spring in Los Angeles, California, may report at the headquarters of the Club, 825 West Polk Street.

This opportunity is exceptional because the athletes will be prepared by trainers expert in all track and field games, thanks to the Club's contract with one of the best gymnasiums of the City of Chicago.

Take advantage of this exceptional offer of the Club for your participation in the World Games of next year, thus continuing the example of our ancestors, which has been followed ever since by all civilized nations.
