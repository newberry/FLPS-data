Ahepa chapter 46 invites the Greek people of Chicago and suburbs to attend a lecture given by Prof. Harold R. Willoughby of the University of Chicago.

The topic of the lecture is "Calligraphy of the Greek Handwritings of the Epech of Paleologoe".

We call the attention of the Greek public to the fact, that Thil-Hellenic Professor Willoughby is an authority on the subject, and the lecture will be a very educational lesson from a historic-national point of view.

The lecture will take place, June 4, at 190 N. State Street. Free admission.

The Educational Committee.
