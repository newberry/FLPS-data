p. 2 - Tomorrow evening, Friday, March 27, at 8:10 P.M., through radio station WGN, a Greek radio program will be presented in which the Byzantine quartet will be heard, under the direction of Mr. G. Papageorge. The quartet will sing "Ipermacho Stratigho Ta Niketeria" (Fighting for Victory) , an old Byzantine hymn.

Mr. Papageorge will sing a popular Greek folk-song. A short talk will be given by Mr. D. Michalopoulos on "The Future of Greece", and Mr. D. Bulukas will speak on Greek-American youth.
