p. 5.- Amidst solemnity and magnificence the installation of the newly elected officers of the Masonic Greek Lodge, took place last Thursday at the La Salle Hotel.

The new officers are Evangelos Nomicos, Master; Stauros Kastrandas, Senior Warden; P. S. Stavropoulos, Junior Warden; A. Zemet, Secretary; and Nicholas Protopapas, Treasurer. The rest of the officers are P. Alexander, K. Tsiolis, J. Lambos and Aristotle Kolias.

Dance followed the ceremony and lasted until late.
