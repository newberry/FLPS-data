The following latter from the Joint Distribution Committee was received by the People's Relief office:

"Dear Mr. Schuchter:

In answer to your question as to the methods by which the various "landsmanchaften" (societies of countrymen) can send their collected funds to their home towns in Europe, for distribution in these towns through reliable local men, I can inform you that the Joint Distribution Committee can accept the money and transport it to the towns designated by the societies. We can also publish the name of the sending society in the town being given relief....."
