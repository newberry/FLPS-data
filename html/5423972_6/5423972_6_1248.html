A free dispensary has been opened at 572 West Twelfth Street, at the corner of Jefferson Street, where doctors will examine sick women and children every day from ten to twelve in the morning, and men who are sick, from seven to eight in the evening.

During the same hours, dental service will be given at half price, and teeth will be extracted free.
