At the monthly meeting of the Directors of Jewish Training School, held on Wednesday evening, the following donations were received: D.E. Frank, $500; B. Mergentheim, $100; Kendal Social and Dramatic Club, $50; Excelsior Club, $75; Hebrew Ladies' Aid Society of La Porte, Ind., $30; Classes II, III, and IV, of the Sinai Congregation, $13.85.

Among the recent visitors at the Jewish Training School were Mr. Dapperich and Mr. Vogel, Directors of the German-American Seminary and Engelmann School, of Milwaukee, who came here to familiarize themselves with the methods of the School for the purpose of introducing them in their institutions.
