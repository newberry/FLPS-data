A letter was sent to Dr. Boerne Bettman by Julian W. Mack, Secretary, informing him that at a meeting of the United Hebrew Charities, a resolution was adopted establishing the West Side Dispensary and appointing Drs. Bettman, Zeisler, Friend, Morgenthau, Greensfelder, Weber, Frankenthal, Sydney, Kuhn, Goodkind, Carey and Engelman as the Dispensary staff.
