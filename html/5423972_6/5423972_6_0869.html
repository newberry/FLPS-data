"From the teeasurer's report of the A.J.C., for the past fiscal year--"

The total collections a mounted to $219,402.

The principal beneficiaries were the M.R. Hospital,$63,000, the Jewish Aid Society for Relief, $53,500, the Chicago Home for Jewish Orphans, $21,300, the Jewish Training School, $13,550 and the Home for Jewish Friendless, $14,400.
