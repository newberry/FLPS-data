The Chicago Hebrew Institute's gymnasium supplies many Jewish athletes in the fullest sense of the word. The teachers of the gym show that they know the athletic laws and whenever they are challenged, they come out victorious.

The track team of the Chicago Hebrew Institute has had a great victory in the All Clubs meeting, at West Park, No. 2. Seven clubs sent fifty athletes to this meeting. The athletes of the Institute received 62 1/2 points; the Greek-American get 42 1/2 points; the other club received very little.
