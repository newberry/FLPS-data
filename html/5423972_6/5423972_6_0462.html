A ten-year-old Jewish boy of Chicago has become a serious rival of little Sammy Rzerzewski [Reshevsky] for the title of child chess genius. The little Chicago chess genius is Ralph Molitnikoff of 1902 South Leavitt Street, who won the inter-settlement championship at a match game at Hull House. The little boy won against thirty players.

Mr. Edward Lasker, the chess champion of Chicago, was the referee. He praised the child's remarkable playing very highly.
