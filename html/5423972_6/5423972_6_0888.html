From the Annual Report of the U.H.C., submitted at the meeting held on May 15, 1906:

The total number of cases handled by the Relief Department was 3248, and the total number of persons assisted, 13,743.

The Employment Department received 1167 applications, and work was procured for 828.

At the West Side Dispensary, 26,668 patients were treated during the year. The Hospital handled 1880 patients.

The Hospital Endowment Fund amounts to $227,800. and the Relief Endowment Fund, $22,500.
