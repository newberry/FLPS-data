Mandel Memorial Dispensary Completed.

The Mandel Memorial Dispensary, a project long looked forward to, will be formally opened Saturday, the 4th of April. The dispensary is located at Maxwell and Waller Streets. The dispensary was made possible by a grant of $35000 by Emanuel Mandel and and supplemented with necessary funds by the Jewish Aid Society.

There will be a special consumptive clinic under the supervision of Dr. Theodore B. Sachs and a dental clinic under the constant supervision of a dentist.

Already the dispensary has the largest childrens milk station in the middle west, having supplied 13000 bottles of milk to children within the last month.
