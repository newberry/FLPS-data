From the Treasurer's Report of the A.J.C. on April 30, 1906:

The total receipts for the year were $150,484. The total disbursements were $145,850. The principal beneficiaries were U.H.C. for Relief, $38,000; U.H.C. for the hospital, $30,500; the Chicago Home for Jewish Orphans, $20,000; and the Jewish Training School, $16,500.
