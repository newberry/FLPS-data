A debate between the Left and the Right, will be held Friday evening, May 9th. Seymour Stedman and Dr. H. Batt from Detroit will debate the following resolution.

Resolved: That the Socialist Party of America should cease agitating for immediate reforms through legislation.

Dr. H. Batt, associate editor of the Proletarian says, "Yes" and S. Stedman, "No." The debate is sure to interest all lovers of liberty and all thinkers who are interested in the most vital problems of the day. Every Socialist friend and sympathizer should not fail to attend.

Auspices, City Central Committee.

Jewish Socialist Branches.
