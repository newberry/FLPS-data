At a meeting of the Board of Directors Julius Rosenwald presented to the A. J. C. for the Endowment Fund 417 shares of Sears Roebuck Stock with a market value of over $50,000.

Mr. Rosenwald agreed at some time within five years, upon the request of the Board, to purchase these certificates and pay for the same the sum of $50,000 with accrued interest at the rate of 6% per annum from the date of the last dividend received.
