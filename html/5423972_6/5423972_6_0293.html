A large crowd is expected tonight at the Congregation Beth Itzchok of Albany Park, where Harry A. Lipsky, president of the Middle Western Zionist organization, will speak on "The International Situation and Zionism".

Cantor Kritz will sing Zionist songs. Miss Chirkoff will play the piano. Attorney Julius Savitsky, the Zionist leader of Albany Park, will be chairman. He will represent the Zionist organization of that district.
