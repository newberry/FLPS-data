Kosher Hospital to be Built.

Directors Have Decided to Break Ground Shortly for the Hospital Structure.

At a very enthusiastic meeting of the West Side Auditorium, yesterday evening, the Kosher hospital directors decided to start building in the very near future.

The building committee, in its report presented its entire plan and they decided to accept bids for construction of the foundation and first floor. Bids can be sent to the chairman of the building committee, Mr. A. Lurie.
