The Jewish Educational Alliance offers an opportunity to all those who wish to become citizens to join a class under the supervision of the Department of Labor and the Board of Education of the city of Chicago. This class will hold its first session November 8, at 7:30 P. M. The Board of Education will send one of its regular school teachers to instruct the students and to offer all the assistance needed to make out the necessary papers and become citizens of the United States.

Diplomas jointly issued by the Department of Labor and the Board of Education will be given to all those who complete the course. When this diploma is presented before the court where one is sworn in as a citizen, no further questions will be asked.
