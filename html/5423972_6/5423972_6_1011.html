Monteficre Council, N. U. will give an informal dance at its hall on Monday night.

The West Side Ladies Aid Society will give a ball for the benefit of the poor on March 3, at the West Chicago Club-House.

The Sabbath School Children of Zion Temple are rehearsing for an Operetta to be given at the West Chicago Club-House during Purim.

The Annual meeting and election of officers of the Young Mens' Hebrew Charity Association will be held Monday night, March 2, The place of the meeting will be in the Sinai Temple Vestry room.
