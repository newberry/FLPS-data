The annual meeting of the Young Men's Hebrew Charity Association was held on Monday night at Sinai Temple. The financial reports gave the receipts for the year as $111,366.59. The total amount expended, including donations, was $102,684.14. Balance is $8,682.45.

The report from the Labor Bureau showed that the finest record thus far had been made, in providing work for applicants. Ninety percent were given employment.
