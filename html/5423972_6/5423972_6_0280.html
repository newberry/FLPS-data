Twelve members attended the first meeting of the newly formed Contemporary Problem Club, Sunday afternoon, September 29, in the Hillel Room. Plans and suggestions for the conducting of this group were presented by the chairman and the members. A discussion as to the exact purpose and aim that shall motivate the club, took place and the members were requested to present at next meeting a carefully planned program for the carrying out of the club's purpose.

The next meeting will be held on Sunday, October 13,at 2:30 P. M., in the Hillel Room. Any person over eighteen, who is interested in contemporary Jewish problems is eligible.
