Volunteers for a Tag Day.

Douglas Park Day and Night Nursery - Sunday, May 2nd.

All the Jewish women of Chicago, who are willing to help in this noble and sacred work, to raise a larger sum of money for the Douglas Park Day and Night Nursery on the Tag Day of Sunday, May 2nd, should volunteer and make it possible for the homeless and unfortunate children who are knocking at the doors of other institutions, to have a home.

Women, be prepared for volunteer work. Stations will be announced in the next issue.
