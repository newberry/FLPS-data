In regard to the Michael Reese Hospital, in 1935, 143,064 hospital days' care was given,14,153 Nursery Days, of which 76, 980 was totally or partly free, and 140,225 dispensary visits were made at Mandel Clinic of which 133,104 were totally or partly free.

During the year the Hospital cooperated with the Illinois Emergency Relief in both the in-patient and out-patient departments accepting for care many non-Jewish patients as well as Jewish patients who were clients of the Commission.
