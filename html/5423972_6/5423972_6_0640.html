The annual tag day for all Jewish charity institutions in Chicago will take place tomorrow. Among the charity institutions for which money will be collected are: The Douglas Park Day and Night Nursery, Daughters of Zion Day and Night Nursery, and Daughters of Jacob Nursery.

The following Jewish institutions will participate in the tag day: Douglas Park Nursery, Chicago Consumptive Aid Society, Daughters of Zion Nursery, Chicago Ladies Aid Society, Independent Sisters of Charity, United Sisters of Charity, Northwest Side Sisters of Charity, Daughters of Jacob Nursery, Daughters of Israel, Independent Ladies Aid Society, Bread for the Poor Aid Society, American Ladies Aid Society and Daughters of Zion Society.
