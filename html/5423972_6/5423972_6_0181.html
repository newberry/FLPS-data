The Chicago College of Mohelim (Circumcisers), organized under the laws of the State of Illinois by Drs. A. P. Kadison, J. Ginsburg, M. Loevenson, and Attorney E. N. Solina, is the first of its kind in the world. The college will enlist well known surgeons and Rabbis as instructors and will employ modern scientific methods. Tuition will be free.
