Richard J. Greenberg of Chicago sailed on July 11, on the President Roosevelt to represent the United States at the Olympic games, which are to be held in Amsterdam this month.

Mr. Greenberg was a member of the C. A. A. relay team that broke the world's record last year on the Coast. He was also a member of the C. A. A. water polo team that won the National Water Polo Championship this year. Mr. Greenberg is considered the best water polo guard in America and one of the best 50 yard men in the country.
