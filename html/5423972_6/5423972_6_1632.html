Reception of the Orphan Home's Sewing Circle.

The first annual reception of the South Side Marks Nathan Orphan Home Sewing Circle will take place tomorrow afternoon, 2:15 P. M. at the Isaiah Temple, 45th and Vincennes Ave. A very interesting program has been arranged. The following will participate in the program:

Dr. Joseph Stolz - Address, Mr. Harold John - Violin Solo, Mrs. M. Mozler - Recitation, Mrs. N. Rosenthal - Vocal Solo, Mr. Ralph Ginzburg of Los Angeles, Cal. - Violin Solo.

We hope many will take advantage of this very fine program. Admission Fee is nominal - Refreshments will be served.
