Conservative Judaism Expounded

At the Five O'clock Forum held on Sunday, December 25, Rabbi Max Kadushin of the Humboldt Boulevard Temple, gave his views on "What Is Conservative Judaism?"

Rabbi Kadushin explained that the Conservative group looked upon Judaism as a civilization, and not merely as a revelation or a religious--philosophical system. He developed this view and indicated just how such a view is needed right now, for the purpose of preserving the higher values of Judaism for the modern Jew.
