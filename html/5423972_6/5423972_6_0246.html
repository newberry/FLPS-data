The Executive Committee of the Jewish Manual Training School, met on Wednesday evening last. The Committee on School, presented its report, which was taken up.....First, that there be organized for the months of July and August, a summer school to be opened in the parsonage adjoining Sinai Temple, same to be divided into four departments, to wit. Industrial, Kindergarten, Kitchengarten and Religious.

The most important step adopted was that the Building Committee (was authorized to) receive bids and look up a building site between 12th and 16th Streets, and from Canal Street westward, say not less than one hundred and two hundred feet, and not to exceed $20,000 for the immediate erection of the school edifice.
