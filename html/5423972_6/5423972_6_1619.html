Many faults were found with the children's institutions, and the bad sides were severely criticized in a report to Cook County Judge Owens, by the Board of Visitors which was appointed last April to investigate these institutions.

Among the thirteen which are assailed there are two Jewish institutions: The Jewish Home Finding Society with offices in the Schiff Building-at 720 W. 12th Street, which was criticized for keeping their records in a very negligent manner. The Home for the Friendless Jewish Children - at 53rd Street and Ellis Avenue, iscriticized for assuming no responsibility for the children after they leave the institution.
