At a conference of the welfare workers and the Ladies Home for the Aged Society which was held July 3, in the Hebrew Institute, where many directors of the Federation were present, it was determined to organize, under the name of the Ladies Home for the Aged Committee, the charitable women whose task shall be to assist the Federated Jewish Orthodox Charities in collecting money and creating new subscribers.

After a lengthy discussion the following resolutions were adopted:

1. All the women who were present shall compose a committee whose task shall be to assist the Federated Orthodox Jewish Charities.

2. The new women's committee, first of all, shall undertake collection of the outstanding debts due the Federation.
