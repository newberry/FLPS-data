At a meeting of the Board the attention of the Directors was called by Leo F. Wormser to Senate Bill # 344 passed by the State Legislature requiring permits from the dept, of Public Welfare for the solicitation of funds for charitable purposes.

Mr. Wormser announced that the bill had been placed before the governor for his signature and he recommended that the A. J. C. join other philanthropic organizations in appearing before the Governor on June 27 to ask that this bill be vetoed.

Mr. Wormser was then designated to represent the A. J. C before the Governor.
