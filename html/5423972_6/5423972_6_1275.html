Joseph Schaffner, founder of the famous clothing firm of Hart, Schaffner and Marx, has left an estate of three million dollars and personal property to the amount of $75,000. The larger part of his estate will be divided among his wife and three children. He also leaves $20,000 to the Michael Reese Hospital, and $12,500 to the Northwestern University School of Commerce, which he founded. All of this became known Friday when his will was filed in the Probate Court. Mr. Schaffner died April 19.
