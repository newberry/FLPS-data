On Tuesday evening January 25, will mark a historic occasion in the annals of Chicago Jewry. The Zionist Organization of Chicago will dedicate a bust of Dr. Theodore Herzl, which they are presenting to the Herzl School.

In honor of the occasion a life size painting of the immortal Zionist leader will be dedicated at the school. Mayor William E. Dever will receive the two gifts in behalf of the city, and W. M. Roberts, principal of Herzl School, in behalf of the school. Max Shulman will represent the Zionists of Chicago.
