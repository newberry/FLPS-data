The Josephine Club, was opened on November 7, 1909, at 219 S. Wood St., for the purpose of offering a home to Jewish immigrant girls, alone in Chicago, who needed the protection of home life and companionship. The club is a private philanthropy and is supported by memberships at $30 per year. The girls pay pro-rata of their weekly earnings. The present home is located at 4805 Forrestville avenue.
