From the annual report of the U. H. C., submitted at the meeting of the Board on May 16, 1905.

The total number of cases assisted during the past year was 3,101 and the total number of persons, 11,997.

1,193 applications for work were received and jobs were secured for 669 of the applicants.

At the West Side Dispensary, 25, 812 patients were treated. The Hospital handled 1,925 cases and the Hospital Dispensary, 5,460 patients.

The Hospital Endowment Fund is now $215,300 and the Relief Endowment, $22,500.
