From a report issued by the directors of the U. H. C. a deficit is shown to exist in every department, the total being $4,433.

The Relief department granted assistance to 14,428 persons, an increase of 50% over the preceding year. The Employment Bureau procured work for 601 out of 797 applicants. The Hospital treated 1,239 Jewish patients. The Hospital Dispensary handled 16,244 Jewish cases. The West Side Dispensary treated 23,172 cases. The Sheltering Home cared for 75 children.
