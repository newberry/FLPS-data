The last Amalgamated educational meeting which was held at the Ashland Auditorium last evening, was a great success. More than 5,000 members of the "Amalgamated" filled the Auditorium and roaringly applauded President Hellman and Secretary Schlossberg, who were the principal speakers at the meeting.

Schlossberg spoke about the working class in general, and Hellman surveyed the present condition of the Amalgamated, and its negotations with the employers.
