The Sabbath School for Girls held at the Waller Street Talmud Torah will be open every Sunday afternoon. All Jewish children are welcome to join the classes.

This school is supervised by the Council of Jewish Women. Mrs. H. J. Davis will act as chairlady [of the program to be held] next Sunday evening. A play will be presented entitled "The Eternal Song". Mr. S. Rubenstein and Miss Tania Mesirow will take the principal roles.
