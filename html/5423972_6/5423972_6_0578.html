Three Chicago institutions will benefit to the extent of almost $1,000,000 by the will of Adolph J. Lichtstern, Chicago Board of Trade operator.

The University of Chicago will receive between $300,000 and $400,000; the Jewish Charities of Chicago, between $150,000 and $200,000; and the Michael Reese Hospital between $150,000 and $200,000. The charity bequests are left outright, with no trust funds or other modifying provisions.
