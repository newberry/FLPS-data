Today at 2 p. m. the cornerstone for the new sanitarium was laid with due and fitting ceremony. It will be the most sanitary and modern hospital in Chicago. The sanitarium will be an Eden for those unfortunates who suffer from the advanced stages of consumption.

They will have all the necessary comforts to ease their sufferings.

The sanitarium is being built at the corner of 50th (west) and Belmont Ave.

Officiating at the ceremony were: United states Judge Julian Mack, Dr. Emil G. Hirsch, Israel Kovan, Rabbi Saul Silver, Dr. Theodore B. Sacks, Judge Harry M. Fisher, Barnett Horwich, and Dr. George B. Young.
