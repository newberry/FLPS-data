The report (of the School Committee) states further, that the class is divided into four classes, the first of which consists of forty-eight pupils - being the confirmation class taught by the superintendent, Dr. Hirsch - the second class consists of fifty-one pupils and is in charge of Mr. Henry Cohen; the third and fourth classes have seventy-one pupils and are taught by the eight young lady volunteer teachers....all of whom are graduates from the Religious Norman School, created and taught exclusively by our worthy superintendent.
