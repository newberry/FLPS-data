From the annual report of the United Hebrew Charities. In the relief department they assisted, with the past year, 2,826 cases, comprising a total of 11,133 persons. In the Employment Bureau, work was procured for 642 applicants.

At the West Side Dispensary 20,523 patients were treated, 22,873 prescriptions were filled. The sinking fund now amounts to $186,950 and the relief endowment fund to $21,500.
