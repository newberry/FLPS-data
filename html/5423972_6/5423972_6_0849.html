At a meeting of the Board of Directors, E. G. Foreman, M. E. Greenebaum, and A. J. Pflaum were present as a committee from Michael Reese Hospital with reference to the proposed gift of $300,000 by the heirs of Mrs. Nelson Morris for the Children's Hospital. The following statement was submitted, cost of maintenance of hospital $41,299 per year, income per year $41,257. Permission was granted the Board of Michael Reese Hospital to accept the gift.
