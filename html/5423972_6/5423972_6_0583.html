The United drive for $4,000,000 was launched with an appeal to 50,000 Jews this week.

Julius Rosenwald has accepted the office of honorary chairman. Sol Kline is associate chairman. Marcy J. Berger is secretary, and Alfred K. Foreman, treasurer.

Chicago institutions will be materially aided by this fund, including Michael Reese and Mount Sinai Hospitals. Educational, vocational and social work at the Jewish People's Institute will be extended.
