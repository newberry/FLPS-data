The annual meeting of the Y. M. H. C. A. was held at Sinai Temple.

The reports showed that the organization had at present, the largest membership in its history, the number being 677. The financial secretary's report gave the receipts for the year as $34,876. The Charity Ball was responsible for $29,711 of this amount.

The sum of $29,866 was donated to 36 institutions, the amounts ranging from $50 to the Salvation Army, to $5,000 for the Michael Reese.

Alfred S. Austrian was elected president, and Horatio S. Simons, treasurer.
