A summer outing camp and convalescent home for the Jewish children of the congested districts will be opened about July 15, by the Chicago section, Council of Jewish Women, at the village of Western Springs, near Hinsdale. A three acre track, with a large brick building, has already been purchased for this purpose.....The property will be owned and managed by a separate corporation, the Summer Outing and Convalescent Home, Chicago Section, Council of Jewish Women.

The business office of the corporation is at 4947 Vincennes Ave.
