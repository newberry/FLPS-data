A very interesting lecture has been arranged for a meeting of the Clara DeHirsch and Kadimab Gates of the Order Knights of Zion at the Lessing Club House, 447 West Millar Street tomorrow evening. Mr. Israel Kahn will lecture on the "Jewish Question" now one of the big problems before the Russian "Duma".

Professor Goldschmidts Orchestra will provide the musical entertainment. Mr. William Tanger will render a few vocal selections. Lecture starts promptly at 8:00 P.M. Discussion and dancing to follow.
