The North-West Young Men's Hebrew Association's Athletic Department announces that they have signed up fifty men to try out for the baseball team, the talent being so good and so large that it will be a hard task to choose the regular men. A schedule has been arranged with some of the best teams outside of the city.

The Y. M. H. A. Social Department announces their "April Follies" Dance in conjunction with the Y. W. H. A to take place at the Humboldt Park Refectory April the 8th. Don't miss the follies.
