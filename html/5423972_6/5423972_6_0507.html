Contributions totaling $11,000 have been received for the Dr. Theodore Sachs' Memorial Fund. The largest contributors were:- Julius Rosenwald, $5,000, and Mrs. Keith Spalding, $5,000.

It has been decided that the memorial is to be a building erected upon the grounds of the Edwards Sanitarium belonging to the Chicago Tuberculosis Institute at Naperville, Illinois.

The total fund to be raised is $200,000, half of which is for the building and half for its maintenance.
