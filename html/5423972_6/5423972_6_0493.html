The trustees of the estate of Jacob Franks have announced a bequest of $100,000 as a memorial to Bobby Franks.

One-half of the $100,000 will be devoted to improving the property of the American Boys' Commonwealth, in two ways. The first undertaking will be a new clubhouse to replace the present structure. The second project will be a new athletic field at the summer camp of the organization at Wooster Lake, Illinois, to be known as the Robert Franks Memorial Field.
