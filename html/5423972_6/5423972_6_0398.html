Mr. Adolph Fainbach who is managing Mr. Asch's tour of the West, has announced that Sholom Asch, world renown yiddish writer, will be in Chicago on the 28th of this month. Mr. Asch will speak on two occasions and many societies are planning to give him a great reception. From Chicago,Mr. Asch is scheduled to go to St. Louis where he will speak on the 1st and 2nd of May.
