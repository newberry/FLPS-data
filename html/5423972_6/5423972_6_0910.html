The Jewish agriculturalists Aid Society has resolved to issue certificates of credit in denominations of $10 each to the amount of $100,000. The certificates are to bear interest at the rate of 3% per year and to be redeemable at the option of the society or at the expiration of a certain number of years. The money is to be used for the incorporate purposes of the society, namely to aid Jewish poor to take up farming as a vocation.
