The will of Mrs. Henrietta Rosenfeld, who died July 24th, has been probated. Charitable bequests were left to various institutions, among them the following: Michael Reese Hospital, $15,000, United Hebrew Charities, $6,000, Home for Aged Jews, $5,000, Jewish Training School, $2,000, South Side Ladies' Sewing Society, $1,000 and Chicago Aid and Relief Society, $1,000.
