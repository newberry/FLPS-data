The Sokol Slavsky gymnastic association made some very thorough preparations for a tournament which is to commemorate George Washington's birthday. The tournament will take place in the hall of the Ceskoslovanske Podporujici Spolky (Czecho-Slavonic Benevolent Societies) on 18th and May Streets.....

Among the artists who will perform on this occasion is the famous Mrs. Marenka Havelka, who is a member of the Lawndale Theater. An interesting novelty on the program is the gymnastic poem, "Pad Tyrana" (The Downfall of the Tyrant); the words of the poem will be accompanied by appropriate gymnastic exercises.....
