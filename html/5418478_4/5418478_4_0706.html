p. 1, Col. 3 - We are bringing to the attention of Bohemian parents the reopening of Bohemian Class School, which will be opened as usual, beginning tomorrow.

The classes will be held on Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays from 4 P.M. to 6 P.M. on Saturdays, from 9 A. M. to 12 A. M. and on Sundays only from 9:00 A.M. to 11:00 A.M.

The subjects taught are: Reading out loud, enunciation. Writing and translating by use of the Bohemian and English dictionary; domestic and foreign history, singing and dramatics, and moral teaching in the rational sense.

We are certain the parents will not hesitate sending their children to our school. The tution fee is but 25 cents a week which can be afforded by any of our Bohemian parents. It was decided by the executive board of the school to have Mr. Frank Veverka as teacher.
