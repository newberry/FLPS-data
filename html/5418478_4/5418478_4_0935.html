P.1 - The management of the well known welfare and educational institution, Hull House,located at Halsted and Polk Streets, has requested Prof. Thomas G. Masaryk to give a lecture in Hull House Hall. Mr. Masaryk,accepted the invitation, and the topic he has decided to speak on is: "The Present Situation in Bohemia." The lecture will be given in the Bohemian language this coming Saturday. Admission is free,and every one is welcome.

Countrymen, living in the neighborhood of Hull House, and in other Bohemian communities, will attend the lecture in large numbers.
