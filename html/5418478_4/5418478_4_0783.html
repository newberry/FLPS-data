There will be a lecture today, Sunday, November 26, 1922. It will be delivered by Mr. Vaclav J. Petrzelka, the official spokesman of the Svobodna Obec (Bohemian Free Thought Community), in the Jubilejni Husuv Dum (John Huss Memorial Hall), 4236 West 22nd Street, and will....deal with the causes that led up to the battle of Bila Hora [A. D. 1620] and its effect upon the Bohemian nation. Doors open at 2 P.M. There will also be a program of music. Everybody is welcome.
