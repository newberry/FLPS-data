In the athletic contests arranged and held under the auspices of the West Side Parks, Saturday August 6, in Douglas Park, the following of our countrymen were among the winners:

James Stastny, broad jump, gold medal; Charles Klocar, hammer throw, gold medal; John Klocar, high jump, gold medal; Joseph Smidl, high jump and run, gold medal; John Martini, pole vault and hammer throw, silver medal. One hundred athletes participated in these contests.
