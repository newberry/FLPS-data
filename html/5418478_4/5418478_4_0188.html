On December 1, 1911, the Cesko-Americka Tiskova Kancelar (Bohemian-American Press Bureau) will begin the third year of its existence. The executive committee of the Bohemian-American Press Bureau, at its last meeting held November 23, made an agreement with the present office manager of the Bureau, Dr. Jaroslav E. S. Vojan, for another year, that is, until November 30, 1912.
