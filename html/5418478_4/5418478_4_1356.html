P.1, Col.4--Several new sport organizations have recently formed in the Bohemian sections of Chicago.

A baseball club, which will be known as the Original Crawford Baseball Club. The charter is to be made up by the Bohemian lawyers firm of Novak and Pollak and the incorporators of the club, whose names will appear on the charter are, Mr. T. Valenta, Mr. A. Pitt and Mr. R. Pacal.

The other is the Bohemian Catholic Foresters Baseball League, which will be sponsored by the Foresters organization of Chicago.

League competition will be open to all the chapters of Foresters in Chicago. Mr. A. Kral will be the leagues president and Mr. A. F. Sobetka the secretary.
