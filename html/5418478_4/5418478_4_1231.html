The competition of the Gymnastic Union, composed of Bohemian, Croatian, Slovak, and Polish Sokol groups, which took place in the gymnasium of the Sokol Havlicek-Tyrs yesterday morning, was quiet but purposeful. Over one hundred Sokols took part in the competition which lasted from 8:30 A. M. until way past noon. The Sokoldom stresses physical education in its system, and what was shown in this respect yesterday would fill every friend of the Sokols with genuine pleasure.

The competition consisted of drills on the apparatus, both for teams and for individual gymnasts.....
