The Spolek Ceskych Lekaru (Bohemian Medical Society) will hold a meeting at 9 P. M. tonight in the Ceska Beseda (Bohemian Club). Since all members of the society are expected to attend this meeting, our countrymen are advised to ask for medical consultations, wherever this is possible, before the doctors' meeting. It will be impossible to get medical help while the meeting is in progress.
