The next to the last lecture of Mr. Vaclav J. Klofac, the well-known leader of the Bohemian National Socialist party and a member of both the Austro-Hungarian and the Bohemian parliaments, was held last night in the hall of Sokol Tabor at West 13th Street and South 41st Avenue. While the attendance was not more than satisfactory, the moral success of the lecture was complete.....

[Translator's note: The speaker dealt again with the Slavonic nations of the Balkan Peninsula, their wars, etc.]
