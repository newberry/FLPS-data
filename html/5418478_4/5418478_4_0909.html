Our famous traveler and ardent national worker, Mr. E. St. Vraz, delivered a lecture yesterday in the Bohemian-American Hall on the topic "Picturesque Travels through Bohemia and Moravia". The Hall was crowded with our public who always welcome the lectures of Mr. Vraz.....

Yesterday's lecture was particularly interesting because Mr. Vraz's colorful narration was accompanied by 250 stereopticon slides, many of which were brand new and shown for the first time.....
