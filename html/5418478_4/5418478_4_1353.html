At the Shooting competitions held Sunday at the picnic of the United Hunting Clubs in Leafy Grove, Willow Springs, the following prizes were awarded: First prize to Club Orel; Second prize to Club Pilsen; Third prize to Club Turner; Fourth prize to Club Campers; Fifth prize to Club Ceskych Myslivcu; and Sixth prize to Club Troy.

In the individual competitions, the following were successful: First: Rudolph Sevic; Second: Alois Fiala; Third: John Soukup; Fourth: Vojta Simak; Fifth: Joseph Pondelicek.
