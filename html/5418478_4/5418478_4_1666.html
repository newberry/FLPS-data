The Dvur Svateho Vaclava cis. 399 Katolickych Lesniku (Saint Wenceslaus Court No. 399 of the Order of Catholic Foresters) is preparing for the annual ball to take place tomorrow night in the Cesko-Americka Sin (Bohemian-American Hall), 1436 West 18th Street. Anybody who knows this popular organization will wish to attend the ball, for the entertainments arranged by the Court are known to be excellent. The committee on arrangements has taken great pains to make the event as attractive as possible. The ball will begin at 7:30 P. M., and the admission is fifty cents per person including government tax.
