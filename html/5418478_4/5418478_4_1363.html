An agreement was signed, yesterday, at Devil's Lake, Wis., according to which, Jan Rut (Jack Root) is to meet Marvin White of Louisville, Ky. The fight is to take place in the principal city of Kentucky, Aug. 27th.

The fighters will receive sixty per cent of the gross receipts and are guaranteed at least $2,000. Tim Hurst will be the referee and Will Douglas the promoter. Louis Hausman signed the agreement for Root, who will immediately begin training for the fight.
