The Court Jan Komensky No. 14 of the Cesko-Americti Lesnici a Lesnice (Bohemian-American Order of Foresters) has arranged a splendid entertainment and picnic which is to take place tomorrow in Pilsen Park, West 26th Street and Albany Avenue, at 4 P. M. The name of this Court is a sufficient guarantee that the entertainment is one of the best. Mr. Cermak's orchestra will furnish music.....
