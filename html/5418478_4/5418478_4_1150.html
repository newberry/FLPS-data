In Sokol Chicago Hall, a basketball game was played last night, between the Sokol Chicago team and the Sokol Karel Jonas team. The number of people who came to see the game indicates that the interest in basketball among our Bohemian people is increasing. The basketball game was attended by approximately 300 spectators, who were rewarded with an interesting sight. However, from the very beginning of the game, it was apparent that the Sokol Karel Jonas team could not compare with its adversary, and at the end of the game this was confirmed. The Sokol Chicago team won 61 to 19.
