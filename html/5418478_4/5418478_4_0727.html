You are hereby respectfully requested to send a report, not later than December 15, 1903, to the secretary of the Bohemian National Cemetary, Mr. Josef Filip, 397 West 18th street, in which we desire the following information: Where are your school premises; street and number? How many times weekly and at what time are classes held? How many pupils does your school have and who is the teacher? The National Cemetary Committee needs this information for the allocation of the $1,000.00 which was allowed at it's last meeting. In the name of the Bohemian National Cemetary Association, John Pech, chairman, Jos. Filip, Secretary.
