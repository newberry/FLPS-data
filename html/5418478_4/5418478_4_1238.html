Our readers have had several opportunities to read about the proposed merger of two sokol units, the Sokol Tabor and Sokol Slovanska Lipa. Today, they have an opportunity to read that it has been accomplished.....

There were several proposals concerning the name the new unit should adopt....Thus it will be called Sokol Tabor Slovanske Lipy (camp of the Slavic Linden).....As soon as all details are taken care of, the former Sokol Slovanska Lipa will move into the building of the Sokol Tabor on De Koven Street....
