The Ceska Korespondencni Skola (Bohemian Correspondence School) at 1825 Blue Island Avenue, has just begun its second year of activity. It has been considerably enlarged, a circumstance which proves its usefulness and popularity. To the original list of courses in the English language, now improved and reedited, the following courses have been added: business accounting, accounting for farmers and craftsmen, bookkeeping, and commercial law.....
