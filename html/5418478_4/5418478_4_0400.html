This is the beginning of a new school year, and Czech parents are advised, therefore, to enroll their children in a free thought school. This is the first and foremost patriotic duty of all conscientious and progressive parents.

The registration in the Mikolas Ales [Bohemian Free Thought] School will be on Saturday, September 2, 1922 from 9 A.M. to 3 P. M. The school is located at 5064 West 30th Place, Cicero, Illinois. The registration in the Jan Amos Komensky [Bohemian Free Thought] School will be on Sunday, September 3, 1922, from 9 A.M. to 3 P.M. This school is located at 900 North Western Avenue, Chicago.
