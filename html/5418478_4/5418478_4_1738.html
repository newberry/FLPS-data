The members of the Sbor Bile Lilie cis. 68, Jednota Ceskych Dam (White Lily Lodge No. 68 of the Bohemian Ladies' Union) are reminded that the regular meeting of the Lodge, which was to be held this Sunday, has been postponed because of a banquet to be given in honor of the convention delegates of lodges of the Sesterska Podporujici Jednota (Bohemian Sisterhood). The banquet is to be given by the Grand Lodge of the Bohemian Ladies' Union in honor of the Jednota [convention] delegates. Our meeting is thereby postponed until August 31, 1922 (Thursday).

Mrs. Frantiska Pokorny, president;

Mrs. Katerina Gruenwald, secretary.
