At a special meeting of the "Society of Bohemian Journalists," which was held Monday, it was resolved to rent club rooms in the Schlitz building, corner Blue Island Avenue and 19th Street, so the next regular meeting will be held in that building.

It was also decided to subscribe to all local American newspapers, Review of Reviews,Mc Clures,Scientific American;from out of town, Kvety, Osvetu, Zlatou Prahu and Zoon. The club manager, J. E. Havlicek, Editor of Denni Hlasatel, will furnish individual members of the society with a key to the club premises. The premises will be arranged to suit the purposes of the society.
