The Chicago delegation to the great Sokol festivities staged on the occasion of the fiftieth anniversary of the first Sokolska Jednota (Sokol Union) in the United States, returned from St. Louis full of praise for the wonderful success of this important national holiday.

The celebration took in two full days, Saturday and Sunday......

The Sokol St. Louis deserves full recognition for its progress and development during the fifty years of its existence.....
