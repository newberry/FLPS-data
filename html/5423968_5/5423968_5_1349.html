We have already informed our readers that the Polish Album Company, recently established in Chicago, is preparing a number of artistic photographic albums for exhibition at the Lwow Fair. The Lwow Fair Committee has consented to exhibit these albums in the Polish-American Pavilion, and the Polish Album Company is very busy finishing them. These albums will contain photographs of Polish buildings, business houses, outstanding American Poles, etc.

The secretary of the Polish Album Company has informed us that the photographs of St. Stanislaus Kostka church, Holy Trinity church,and St. Mary's of perpetual Help church, Bridgeport, are ready. The photographs of the convent and school of the Sisters of the Holy Family of Nazareth, the high school of St. Stanislaus Kostka parish, and the school of St. Mary of Perpetual Help parish ( in Bridgeport) are almost ready.
