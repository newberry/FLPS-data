The Saint Stanislaus Kostka Parish bazaar opened last night with many Polish societies in attendance.

Contributions were as follows:

Joseph Izydorczyk, 41 Tell Place, two laughing doves and cage; Pauline Zblewsska, 77 Cleaver Street, a large flower; Antoinette Szumilowska, 557 Holt Street, musical automat; Philip Kopczynski, 593 Holt Street, two large ferns; Valentine Szymanski, 606 Noble Street, one canary; William Slupienski, 635 Noble Street, three pairs of shoes.

Sincere thanks are extended all donors.

The Committee.
