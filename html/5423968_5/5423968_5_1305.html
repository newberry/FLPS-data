Silvester Wleklinski has left in our office $6.75, collected as follows for the Polish Pavilion:

Reverend Matthew Krawczunas, $2; Peter Krawczunas, $1; S. Salas, $1; K. Andrusis, $0.50; Mr. Ostrowski, $0.25; and Sylvester Wleklinski, $2.

To the noble donors we extend a hearty God bless you.
