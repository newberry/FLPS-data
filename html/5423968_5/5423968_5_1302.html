We have received the following information:

"Lwow, Poland October 13, 1894.

"It gives me great pleasure to inform you that our Polish-American Pavilion at the Lwow Exposition, not the building but our exhibits, was unanimously awarded an Honorary Diploma, which is the highest award of the Exposition.

"Besides this, Mr. Slominski was awarded two medals, silver and bronze; Mrs. Bienkowski of Chicago, a bronze medal; Dr. Kociell, a gold medal; Mr. Kulinski of New York, a silver medal for his iron products; Mr. Buczkowski, a silver medal for his harness, etc.

2

"A complete list of awards will be published later on.

"Adam Szwajkart Manager of the Polish-American Pavilion at Lwow."
