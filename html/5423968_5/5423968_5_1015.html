The St. Cecilia Men's Choir of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish presented S. Zahajkiewicz's five-act musical drama, "Jaskinia Beatusa" (Beatus' Cave), last night at the Polish Hall. A large attendance witnessed the play.

The leading roles were played by J. Nering [sometimes Nehring], Mrs. Anna Jozwiakowska, J. Kikulski, W. Gorecki, Mrs. M. Gorzynska, Vincent Jozwiakowski, J. Kondziorski, Mrs. R. Koppa, Miss K. Drzonek, J. Czekala, Miss A. Stas, A. Barwig, J. Grabowiecki, S. Piasecki, Miss C. Borkowicz, Miss W. Barwig, and Miss K. Murkowska.

The play was under the direction of S. Zahajkiewicz and A. Kwasigroch.
