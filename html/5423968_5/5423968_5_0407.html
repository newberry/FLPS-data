The women's choir, Kalina, will stage a splendid concert on Sunday, Feb. 26th, in the Polish Roman-Catholic Union Hall at Milwaukee and Augusta Sts. The committee promises that the program will be excellent.

Miss Cecilia Kwiatkowska, very seldom active in public, will appear as a solo violinist. Miss Kwiatkowska is a student of Professor Chapek, who has appeared with great success under the direction of Mr. Chapek at the Auditorium in downtown Chicago.
