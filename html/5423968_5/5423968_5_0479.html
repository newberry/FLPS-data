The Bay City Symphony Orchestra, a musical society of Chicago, is arranging three large symphony concerts at the Washington Theatre. At one of these concerts, that of the 15th of December, Mrs. Rose Kwasigroch, our famous singer, will be the main soloist. Mrs. Kwasigroch will render six selections; four with piano, two with orchestral accompaniment.

She will sing in Polish, English, German, Italian and Latin. Knowing Mrs. Kwasigroch to be a singer of high calibre, we are positive her audience will be pleased. This will add another leaf to her wreath of fame.
