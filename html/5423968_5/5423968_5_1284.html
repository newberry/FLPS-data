Donations of cash and merchandise continue to flow in for the coming St. Hedwig Parish Fair, according to the committee. Out of the twenty-two persons who made contributions recently, two gave three dollars in cash and the remainder gave valuable gifts.

Louis Kliwer gave a watch valued at twenty dollars. A music box valued at thirty dollars was given by Stanislaus Snigocki. P. Trandel contributed a white goat.

The St. Hedwig Parish Fair committee extends sincere thanks to all donors.
