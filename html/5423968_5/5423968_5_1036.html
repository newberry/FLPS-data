A new Polish dramatic club has just been organized at St. Hedwig's parish. The officers of the club are John Lasinski, president; John Wachowski, secretary; and Joseph Wachowski, treasurer. The next meeting will be held Sunday, March 4, 2 P. M., at Marcinkowski's Hall, Robey Street and Webster Avenue. All those wishing to join are invited.

John Wachowski, secretary.
