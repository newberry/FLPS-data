The Kosciusko Guard Society (under the guidance of St. Florian) of St. Michael Archangel Parish staged an interesting play "Krotki i Dlugi" (Short and Long) last night. The theme of the drama was based on the thieves of Chicago. All the amateur players were at their best.

Despite inclement weather, the parish hall was filled to capacity; some had to stand.

It is requested that this organization present more plays of this type in the future. They deserve a hand for their fine work.

One of the Audience.
