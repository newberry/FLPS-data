Mr. Antoni Mallek of Chicago, well known for many years in the field of music published a church song book of Polish songs for two voices and the organ. This is the first series, others will follow.

We hope this song book will be received gladly by the parochial schools so that Polish children will praise God in their own language. Mr. Antoni Mallek, who is a well known musician is also an organist at the Polish Holy Trinity Church, and a conductor of the Polish Singers' Union. He may be assured, that he contributed new laurels to his merits in the field of Polish songs in America.
