The newly organized Polish Variety Theatre already has concentrated in its group the best known first class actors, also newcomers and amateurs. The theatrical group made a decision to perform regularly during the whole winter season and rehearsals are already started.

The first performance will be at the Walsh Hall, corner of Noble and Emma Street, on August 20, 1911. This performance will consist of a five act play with the title "Emigracija Chlopska," (Emigration of the Peasant).

After this the next performance, August 27, will be an excellent farce in three acts, "Dobry Numer," (Good Number).
