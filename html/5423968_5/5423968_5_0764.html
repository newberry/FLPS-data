A musical and literary program was given by the Young Men's Archbrotherhood Sunday, May 9, at the St. Stanislaus Kostka parish hall. The entertainment was as follows:

1. Opening, Francis Czastka

2. Song, Francis Czastka

3. Declamation, "In Honor of Polish Priests," Valentine Roszkowiak

4. Song, "Hail, Mazurs," St. Stanislaus Kostka Men's Choir

5. Declamation, "It Is Forbidden to Us," Miss F. Jazdziewska

2

6. Song, "May Dawn," St. Stanislaus Kostka Young Ladies' Choir

7. Recitation, "Omen," Simon Sikorski

8. Song, "My Dear God," Men's Choir

9. Declamation, "I Am Proud That I Am a Pole," John Bombera

10. Speech, Valentine Roszkowiak

11. Tribute to the Reverend Joseph Gieburowski

12. Song, "God Save Poland"

Sincere thanks are expressed to Mr. Chojnacki, director of the lower choir (sic) of St. Stanislaus Kostka's parish, and to the singers. Thanks are also 3extended to Miss Frances Jazdziewska for her splendid recitation.

Valentine Roszkowiak, president

August Bach, recording secretary
