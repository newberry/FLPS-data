The Pulaski Hall Building Society has printed and distributed its financial report for the year 1895. Total income amounted to $9,276.16. Repairs $276.25. Interest payment was $2,982.59, and repaid on loan $4,109.50. Cash balance in the hands of the treasurer is $458.78 (sic). All together, $12,767.80 has been repaid on the loan, leaving a balance still due of $472.60.
