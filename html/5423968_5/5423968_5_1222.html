More contributions for the St. John Cantius Parish benefit bazaar have been received.

Forty-seven persons donated $54.92; the highest contribution was ten dollars and came from J. Cielusta. The central Lumber Company gave five dollars and Peter Kiolbassa, one dollar. Twenty-five cents was the smallest donation.

Fifty persons gave goods valued at over one hundred dollars. Bread, flowers, birds, ham, sausage, beer, pop, wine, chickens, and other commodities are listed.
