We are mentioning for the second time the fact that the Poles in Chicago would like to witness on the stage a Polish opera.

I judge that with the material strength we have here, we could present Moniuszkis' creations.

To support us in this event we could ask Mrs. Kwasigroch, Smulska, Nering, J. Kowalska and Messrs. Kowalski, from South Chicago; Szymanski, Kondziorski, Nering and many others. Our public delights in stage shows, especially if they consist of singing.

The organization of organists should confer upon this matter as soon as it is possible for them.
