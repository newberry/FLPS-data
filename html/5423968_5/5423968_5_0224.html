At the annual meeting and election of directors of the Milwaukee Avenue State Bank, the following were elected to the board: John P. Hanson, William Johnson, Peter Kiolbassa, M. A. LaBuy, Issac Hansam, James P. Sherlock, John F. Smulski, and Soren D. Thorsen. The officers elected for the ensuing year were: Paul O. Stensland, president; F. H. Herhold, vice president; Charles E. Schlytern, cashier; and H. W. Hering, assistant cashier.
