On Sunday evening, April 29th, 1928, at Schoenhofen Hall, Milwaukee and Division Sts., lovers of music will have an opportunity to hear the work of well-known artists, who will present beautiful, tho difficult selections, under the direction of Mr. Casimir Jasinski.

The Polish people should support the efforts of Mr. Jasinski who has, for many years, devoted his untiring efforts towards the development of a Polish sentiment for music in our youth.

The concert begins promptly at 8:15 P.M.
