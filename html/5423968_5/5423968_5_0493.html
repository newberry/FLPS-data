A Polish concert performance will take place this Saturday, November 23, in the Walsh Hall. This concert's institutor is Mary Merkl, a well known and successful singer before Chicago audiences. Beside Miss Merkl there also will be a company of selected artists who will do their part, in making this a real artistic recreation for the listeners.

This will be an important event displayed for the benefit of the Polish Polonia here in Chicago.

After the concert a ball will be held.
