A meeting of the Dramatic Circle of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish was held last night.

The president, Mr. John Czekala, made a very comical speech, which caused much laughter.

Reverend Eugene Sedlaczek read the new constitution of the Dramatic Circle, which was approved. Other important matters were then discussed, and a committee was selected to choose a play and arrange a date for the next theatrical performance. The regular monthly meetings of the Dramatic Circle will be held on the second Thursday of every month.

Six new members joined the Circle at yesterday's meeting.
