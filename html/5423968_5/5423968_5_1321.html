At a meeting held yesterday afternoon, officials of the societies in Saint Stanislaus Kostka Parish agreed unanimously to hold a fair for the benefit of the orphanage on Division Street. The fair will start on October 20.

All Polish and Bohemian parishes are invited to co-operate, inasmuch as the orphans should be supported by all the people. We are certain that no parish, either Polish or Bohemian, will refuse to do its share.

Further information concerning this fair will be given after the next meeting.

B. Clarkowski

Secretary of the Fair.
