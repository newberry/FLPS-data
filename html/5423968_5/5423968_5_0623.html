Miss Jadwiga Czarnecka, a sympathetic singer, will participate in the concert conducted by Mr. J. Kowalski, a pianist. This fete will be concluded in the P. R. C. V. Organization's hall, located at Augusta and Milwaukee avenues, this coming week, Oct. 26, at 8 P. M. This famous Polish songstress, Miss J. Czarnecka, is the daughter of a prominent Chicago lawyer, Mr. K. B. Czarnecki. Miss Czarnecka will sing solos consisting of various operatic and domestic airs.

Miss Czarnecka will appear in another concert sponsored by the Halka Choir, Sept. 13, in Schoenhofen Hall.
