The Holy Trinity Parish fair opened yesterday in a large hall on the second floor of the new school building. This hall is beautifully decorated and is filled with merchandise up to the ceiling.

Reverend Casimir Sztuczko opened the fair with a short speech, after which the Chopin Choir sang patriotic songs.

Throughout the whole afternoon and evening, the fair was full of people. The raffle wheels rattled. All kinds of merchandise were raffled off. People enjoyed themselves immensely. Indications are that the fair will be a success.
