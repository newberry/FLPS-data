The Dramatic Circle of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish has donated one half of its net profit from a play, "Two Orphans," staged on January 10, to the Holy Family Orphanage. The contribution amounts to thirty-two dollars.

Sincere thanks are extended to J. Jarzembowski for offering free transportation to and from town in order to deliver costumes used in the play. This kind gesture made possible a savings of two dollars, which should be considered as a direct donation on the part of Mr. Jarzembowski.
