A concert arranged with the aid of Thaddeus Sleszinski, director of Davis Square park, at 44th Street and Paulina, will be given at this park, at 3:30 P. M. The program will be executed by Polish talent as follows:

Agnes Nering, Soprano solo; Thomas Orzada, violin; Stanley Mrozinski, piano, and many others. Children under fourteen years of age must be accompanied by adults.
