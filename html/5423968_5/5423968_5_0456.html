In memory of Anthony Mallek, our oldest worker, organist, and teacher in Holy Trinity Parish, Chicago, Illinois.

Sincere grief has enveloped our entire parish and along with it the Pulaski Club.

We members, gathered together at our meeting of February 5, upon hearing the sad news of the death of Anthony Mallek, were plunged in sorrow at the loss of one of the oldest workers of Holy Trinity Parish and of the Pulaski Club, the father of Polish song in America.

We members of the Pulaski Club express our deep sympathy to the family of the deceased in the name of the Club.
