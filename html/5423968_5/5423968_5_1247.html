The success of the Fair for the benefit of the St. John Cantius parish seems to be assured.

Many novel articles were purchased for distribution at the Fair. Besides the chance wheels, there will be a "Negroes Table" and two "David Slingshots," where for a few cents one can win a valuable article.

The committee received a suggestion that a pair of goats be bought to raffle at the Fair, but the committee decided against the idea because of the inconveniences involved.

Those who feel tired may have a bite and a drink at low prices at the "buffet bar". Buy all you can eat today--tomorrow it will be free.
