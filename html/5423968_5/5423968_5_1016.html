The Dramatic Circle of the Polish Patriotic Organization presented yesterday "The Children of Israel," by Szczesny Mahajkiewicz, in the St. Michael Archangel Parish, South Chicago. A capacity crowd turned out to acclaim the amateurs' performance, which truly reached the artistic heights. All the humor and pathos [of the drama] was enacted to perfection. The singing was under the direction of A. Kwasigroch, choirmaster of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish.

The author of the play, S. Mahajkiewicz, was given an enthusiastic ovation when he appeared on the stage. The audience shouted, "Long live the director 2of the Dramatic Circle!" Mr. Mahajkiewicz, after a short speech, concluded with "Long live the Poles of South Chicago!"
