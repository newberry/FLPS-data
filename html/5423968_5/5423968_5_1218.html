Contributions for the Saint John Cantius Parish benefit bazaar continue to be received by the committee. Twelve persons have donated $19.50. Reverend J. Piechowski's five dollar donation was the largest single gift. The smallest was twenty-five cents. Others gave cigars, books, bakery goods, etc.

Sincere thanks are extended to all.
