The Stanislaus Moniuszko choir and Dramatic club is sponsoring a dance and choir singing on October 27, 1912, at Klacela Hall, 19th and Leavitt Sts.

The committee has arranged a splendid and very interesting program for this evening.

The Moniuszko choir and Dramatic club deserves the support of the Poles of Chicago and vicinity, therefore we assume that the hall will be crowded to capacity.
