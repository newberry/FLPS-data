The following donations to the Fair for the benefit of the St. John Cantius parish were received:

The St. Barbara Fraternal Benefit Society of the St. Stanislaus Kostka parish donated ten dollars.

The St. Francis Society of the same parish donated ten dollars.

The Altar Boys' Society of our parish was present at the Fair, and donated the sum of ten dollars from its treasury.
