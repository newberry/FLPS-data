On Monday April 29th, an unusual rehearsal will be given by the Chopin Choir No.1, of the Polish Singing Societies, in Walsh's Hall at 8: o'clock. It is the duty of every Chopinist to attend, with the intention of joining the society in the near future.

The following singing rehearsal will take place Thursday 2nd day of May in Walsh's Hall, Division and Cleaver St. Those wishing to expand this symphony choir, should come and hear it sing, and in all probability will join.
