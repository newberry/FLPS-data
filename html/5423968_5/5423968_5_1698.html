The Holy Trinity Literary Circle opens its 26th season, tomorrow evening at 3:30 P.M. in the Holy Trinity Auditorium, at Division and Cleaver Sts. The circle will present a playlet entitled, "Krysia W. Krynicy" followed by recitation, singing and many musical numbers.

The Rev. Zubowicz, who recently returned from a journey through Poland, will give a lecture on his experiences which should prove very interesting.

Our friends, as well as the general public are cordially invited to attend. Admission is free.
