Lovers of classical music will have a great treat when they attend the famous Opera "Il Trovatore", which will be staged to-morrow at St. Stanislaus Kostka Hall. "Il Trovatore" is the master-piece of Verdi, the most famous of Italian composers.

The leading role of this opera will be sung by Mrs. Agnes Nering, the Polish artist, whose every stage appearance leaves a memorable impression with her listeners.

The St. Agnes Young Girl's Society branch of the local Alma Mater Association will make its first public appearance, the latter part of the evening.
