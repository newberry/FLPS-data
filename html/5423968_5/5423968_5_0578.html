Mlle. Janina Butkiewicz, well-known classic and Polish folk-dance artist, will be one of many noted artists to appear at the dance being given at the Hotel LaSalle April 2, for the benefit of St. Mary of Nazareth Hospital.

Mlle.Butkiewicz is distinguished for her French, Russian, Spanish, Polish and Oriental dances. Mlle. Butkiewicz will reside in Chicago, and is engaged to appear with Mme. Junk's troupe of the Chicago Grand Opera.
