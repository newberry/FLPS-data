Mr. Wojciech Kossak accepted the invitation of the Polish Arts Club, to be the guest at a meeting called in his honor, and has sent in his application as a sustaining member of the club.

Mr. Kossak is residing at present, at a winter resort in Aiken, South Carolina, where he is busy painting potraits and other pictures. From there he will leave for Long Island and Port Chester, to do some work before coming to Chicago.

Since the Polish Arts Club has also been planning to arrange a reception for Madame Janina Burska, it may be possible to honor both friends of the club, at the same time. Announcements will be made as soon as more definite plans have been arranged.
