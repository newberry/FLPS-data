Inexorable death has taken from us a great musician of the common people, who brought up a whole singing generation of young Poles in America, whose patriotic songs have lighted the burdens of the Polish immigrants in the free land of Washington for a quarter of a century.

Polonia has lost a worthy citizen, the church has lost one of the best religious poets, the family has lost a most eminent representative, and the singers and musicians have lost their best bard.

Sincere sorrow touches us to the depths. We will miss him for a long, a very long, time! Honor to his spirit; respect for his tireless work and great deserts!

May God reward him with heaven, may his song and memory live in Polish hearts for ages!

To the grieving family we send our heartfelt sympathy.

St. Hyacinth's Choir

Rosalie Barwig

L. Ligman

J. Kikulski
