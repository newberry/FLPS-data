A concert in which a number of Polish church choirs have organized for the benefit of the "Polish Sisters Convent" will be held, November 21, 1897 in the large hall on Bradley Street with the assistance of a large orchestra. The program will be as follows:-

1. "Polonais" Kurpinshi------Orchestra

2. "Gloria" Mozart-----------Mixed choirs with the accompaniment of the Orchestra under the direction of A. J. Kwasigroch; solo part will be rendered by Miss Wanda Barwig.

3. Song dedicated to Nicholas Copermicus------Miss Rozalia Bieszka, Jos. Reich, Pianist

4. "Song of Poland"------------Words by Zahajkiewicz, Women's choir, orchestra under the direction of E. Wiedeman; solos by Miss Agnes Wojtallwicz(Soprano) and Miss Martha Kosinski

5."William Tell"--------------Rossini Orchestra. (alto)

6."Barcorola"-----------------Song duet Misses Anna Muchowsha & Lucia Gizeszkowsha, S. J. Kujawski, pianist.

7. Waltz from O pera "F A D E T T E"--------Soprano solo Miss Wanda Barwig, A. J. Kwasigroch, Pianist.

8. "Krakowiak" from "songs of our Nation"-Male choir, orchestra under the direction of A. Mattek.

Soloists Mr. John Jarosz and Stanislau Pliszka, (tenor) Mr. John Kondziorshi, baritone.

9 " War Signals"--------Wronski-Orchestra

10. Mazurek from the opera "Duch Wojewody" by Grossman-Solo by Miss Agnes Wbjtalewicz, Mr. A. Mattek, pianist.

11."Excelsior" duet, Miss Agnes Glomb and Mr. William Dombek, E. Wiedeman, Pianist.

12. "Duch Wojewody" opera selection, mixed choirs- orchestra under the direction of A. J. Kwasigrich, soloists soprano, Miss Agnes Glomb, Soprano; Stanislaw Mattek, Tenor, Mr. John Kondziorski, baritone.

The concert will begin at 8 o'clock P.M.

Tickets are .25c, .50c and $1.00.

A. Mattek,

E. Wideman,

A. J. Kwasigroch,Committee.
