"Dwie Sieroty" [Two Orphans] was presented yesterday (Sunday) at Pulaski Hall, by Company C of the Pulaski Volunteers, before a full house. The hard work of the amateurs was aptly displayed on the stage, and their reward came when the curtain fell on the last act, for the applause was more than enthusiastic.

The audience realized that this was the finest play that was ever presented on the stage of Pulaski Hall. The crowd and the financialreturns were indicative of the success of the play.
