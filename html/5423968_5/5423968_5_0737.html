The concert of Mr. John A. Mallek, with Mrs. M. Smulski and Miss B. M. Stevens, in Association Auditorium Hall, was very successful. The hall was filled with the people that attentively studied the technique of our famous artist, Mr. J. Mallek.

The listeners were surprised by the wonderful voice of Mrs. Smulski, and the skill of other artists, who after repeated applause, accommodated with encores.
