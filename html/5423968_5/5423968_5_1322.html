It was a good day at the fair yesterday. Even a group of Irish merchants came to visit it and enjoyed themselves immensely.

Yesterday, which had been set aside for the Holy Rosary Sodality from Saint John Cantius Parish, was a successful day.

The billy goat raffled at the fair refused to leave the hall, proving once again the truthfulness of the Polish proverb, "Stubborn as a goat". And perhaps it was not stubborness. Maybe he liked the fair. Who knows?
