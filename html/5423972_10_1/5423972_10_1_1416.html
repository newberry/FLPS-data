Henry Gersoni was born in 1844 in Wilna, Russia. In 1869 he came to America. In 1876 he accepted a call to the pulpit of the Congregation B'nai Sholom of Chicago, holding this position until 1880. While in Chicago, he edited a weekly paper in English and German called, "The Jewish Advance". In 1881 this paper was discontinued and Gersoni published and edited in Chicago a monthly magazine under the name of "The Maccabean".
