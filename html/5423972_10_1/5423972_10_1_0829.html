Solomon L. Sulzberger, president of the Enterprise Paint Manufacturing Company, died on July 23, He was born in Philadelphia, Pa., February 5, 1857. In 1876, he came to Chicago and in 1893 engaged in the manufacture of paint.

Mr. Sulzberger was formerly president of the Standard Club, and also of the Jewish Aid Society, and he was also on the directorate of the Jewish Charities of Chicago.
