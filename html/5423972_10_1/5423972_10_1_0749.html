James Rosenthal, president of R. I. Terwilliger & Company, died on Friday.

He was born in Linshitz, Poland, October 17, 1865, and came to Chicago in 1890. He was a member of the Odd Fellows, a member of the Independent Order B'rith Abraham, the Masons, B'nai B'rith, Elks, and was also a member of the B'nai Abraham Zion Temple.
