Archie H. Cohen was appointed Master-in-Chancery of the Circuit Court of Cook County at a meeting of the judges of that court, held on June 24.

Mr. Cohen was born on October 16, 1889, in Philadelphia, Pa. He was educated in the public schools of Chicago and received his L. L. B. degree from John Marshal Law School.

He is a member of the Chicago, Illinois, State, and American Bar Associations, Crescent Lodge No. 895, A. F. and A. M., Temple Chapter, Ramah Lodge. Mr. Cohen was formerly president and is now ex-officio, a member of the board of directors of the Young Men's Jewish Charities.
