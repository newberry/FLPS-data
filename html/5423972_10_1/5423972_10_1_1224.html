Mr. Morris Iglovitz, 14th and Sangamon Streets, died suddenly on the eve of Yom Kippur, at the age of sixty-six. He was buried the same day he died at the Anshe Knesses Israel Cemetery.

Mr. Iglovitz was one of the first Jewish settlers of Chicago. He was born in Warsaw and was a very learned man. There remains his widow, Rachel, and seven children Herman, Henach, Samuel and Aaron Iglovitz, and Mrs. Robinson, Mrs. Cohen, and Miss Emma Iglovitz.
