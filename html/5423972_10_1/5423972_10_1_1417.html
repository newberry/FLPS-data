Cosman Eisendrath died Thursday, February 21st at his home, 207 Erie Street. The deceased was a native of Dorsten, Westphalia, and came to Chicago fifty years ago. For many years he was among the leading grocers of the City and founded the house now doing business under the name of H. Regensburg's Sons.

He was a prominent worker in the order of the B'nai B'rith.
