Rabbi Aaron I. Messing, of Temple Bnai Sholom, died last night. The funeral will take place today from the home of his daughter, Mrs. H. H. Mitchel, 4450 Magnolia Avenue.

The deceased was a great educator. He settled in Chicago soon after the Civil War, when he founded the Congregation of which he was the rabbi until the last day of his life.

He was born in Prussia seventy-six years ago. He leaves five daughters and three sons.
