Mr. William Wilhartz, senior member of the law firm of Ringer, Wilhartz and Hirsch, died on Sunday. He was 63 years of age.

Mr. Wilhartz was born in Louisville, Kentucky. At the time of his death, he was general counsel for the Chicago Real Estate Board. He was president of the Chicago Jewish Orphan's Home, and a member of the board of trustees of Chicago Sinai Congregation, and he was a director of the Standard Club.
