Mr. Leo Fox was born in Oettinger, Bavaria, Feb. 2, 1844. At the age of 13 he arrived in America. He came to Chicago in 1887, and was elected Vice-President of the International Bank, which liquidated several years ago. He is a member of Sinai Congregation, and has been one of its directors for ten years. He is also associated with the Standard and Sunset Clubs. He is treasurer of the Jewish Training School, to which office he was elected ten years ago. He was a director of the U. H. C., and treasurer of the Congress of Religions.
