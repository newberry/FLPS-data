Rev. J. Feuerlicht was born in Hungary in 1857. Under the guidance of his father who was one of the most learned men in the country, he received his early education. In 1875 he entered the Rabbinical School at Pressburg, which is recognized as the highest seat of theological learning in Hungary. He came to America in 1882, and secured his first position as Rabbi in Congregation Moses Montefeare, in Chicago, where he served until 1890.

In 1903 Rev. Feuerlicht left the ministry with the determination to devote the remainder of his life to institutional work for the last four years he has been supt. of the Home for the Aged in Chicago.
