Mrs. Carrie L. Strauss, widow of the late Leopold Strauss, passed away yesterday at the age of 64, at the Lexington Hotel. Mrs. Strauss was a great worker in philantrophy and charity work here in Chicago. She was a member of several Jewish Charity Organizations in which she was active for the last fifteen years.

She was one of the founders and first president of the Jewish Orphans Home.

She was born in Germany in 1847, and was brought to the United States three years later.
