Louis Bomash is a well known figure in business circles as well as a leader in Jewish communal and philanthropic life. He was one of the first directors who helped found the Marks Nathan Jewish Orphan Home and one of the earliest organizers of the Federated Jewish Charities.

Mr. Bomash is a member of the Chicago Real Estate Board; the B'nai B'rith; and the president of the oldest orthodox congregation, Beth Hamdrash Hagodel, on the South Side.
