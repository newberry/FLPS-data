Max Epstein, a Chicago Jewish millionaire gave this amount to Chicago University - for an art institute to spread and develop art.

Epstein is one of the new millionaires in Chicago. He came here as a poor young man in 1891 and now he is one of the chief owners of the American Tank Car Corporation. His first contribution to the University was $225,000 about three years ago, to establish a dispensary.

Mr. Epstein was born in Cincinnati, in 1875, and is a great lover and collector of art.
