A. M. Rothschild died suddenly on July 28th. Born in Germany in 1853, he came to this country in 1856. His parents were poor and they settled in Davenport, Ia. where a son operated a retail store.

When the fire swept Chicago, the two brothers saw their opportunity. They came here and opened a clothing store on W. Madison St. In 1895, Nelson Morris and his sons became silent partners and the department store of A. M. Rothschild and Co. was opened at State and Van Buren Sts.
