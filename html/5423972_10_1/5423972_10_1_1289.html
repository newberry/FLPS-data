Members of the Bar and Judges of the courts attended the unveiling of the portrait of the late Adolph Moses, lawyer, in Judge Dupuy's Courtroom, in the County Building, last Saturday afternoon.

Among the speakers were: Justice Orrin, N. Carter of the Illinois Supreme Court, former Judge Philip Stein, partner of Mr. Moses, and Joseph H. Defrees, President of the Chicago Bar Association.

The portrait of Mr. Moses was the contribution of many of his friends and associates.
