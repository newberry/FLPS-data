Mr. Albert Fishell was born in Blowitz, Bohemia, on June 13, 1844. In April 1890, Mr. Fishell brought his family to Chicago. He is a radical reformer and a member of Sinai Congregation. In April 1896, he was elected president of the Sinai Congregation and held the office for three consecutive terms. He is now a member of the Board of Directors. Mr. Fishell was a director of the Russian Refugee Society of Chicago.
