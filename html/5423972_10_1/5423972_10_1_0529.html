The local office of the American Red Cross for the last few months has been searching for a Mr. J. Brasner, supposedly of 120 W. 12 St. But there is no such person at that address.

The Red Cross has a letter for him from Lieutenant Bernard Brasner, Landshturm 20, at present war prisoner in Krasniarsk, Siberia. The letter reads as follows:

"I am well and hopeful. Best regards to Ludwia's and your own family."

The letter can be obtained in the Courier's office.
