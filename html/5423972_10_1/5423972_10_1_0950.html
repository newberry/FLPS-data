The appointment of Major Willism J. Mack, a brother of Federal Judge Julian W. Mack, as secretary of the American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee was recently announced.

Major Mack practiced law in Chicago until the entrance of America in the World War, and later assisted Major Willard Straight in organizing the unit to direct the War Risk Insurance, and allotment work throughout the American Expeditionary Forces in Europe. He was assigned to the office of the Judge Advocate General with the rank of Major.
