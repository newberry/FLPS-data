Dr. Aaron A. Loewenheim died at his home, 3142 Rhodes Avenue on Monday, March 18. Dr. Loewenheim came to Chicago from Germany fifteen years ago, and took charge of the Anshe Emeth Congregation on the North Side. He remained there as a rabbi for five years, and then was appointed superintendent of the Chicago Home for Jewish Orphans. He held this office until the removal of the Home to Sixty-second Street and Drexel Avenue.
