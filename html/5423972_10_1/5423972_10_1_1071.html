This morning at 9:00 A.M. the funeral of the well-known west side business man, Solomon Klein, will the place at Temple Isaiah, 45th Street and Vincennes Avenue. Mr. Klein one of the Jewish pioneers in Chicago business, died September 17, 1919. Mr. Klein was president of the L.Klein Department Store and of the 12th Street Store, the two largest department stores in the West Side.

Mr. Klein was born in Germany 61 years ago and came to America with his parents at the at the age of 14. In 1880 he founded the Klein Department Store and since then he rose in wealth until he become one of the most successful businessman in Chicago.
