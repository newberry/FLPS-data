Simon J. Straus died on Sunday of a heart attack. He was born on August 18,1865 and was president of Straus Bros. Co., farm merchants, and of Straus Bros. Investment Co.

Many farmers relied upon Mr. Straus for advice regarding their land and crops. To them and to his associates and employes he was known as "S. J."

He was a member of the Standard Club, the B. P. O. E., Masonic Lodges and the B'nai B'rith.
