As beneficiaries named in the testament of Emanuel Frankenthal, co-founder of the Metropolitan National Bank, are the following institutions: St. Luke's Hospital, $2,000; The Jewish Old People's Home, $2,000; Michael Reese Hospital, $2,000; the Jewish Orphanage, $1,000; the Jewish School for Handicrafts, $1,000; and the Home for Friendless Jewish Women and Working-Girls, $1,000. The rest of his total wealth of about $1,000,000 will be divided according to his will among his six surviving children.....
