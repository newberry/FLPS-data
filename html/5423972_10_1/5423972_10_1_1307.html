Louis Bauer, 70 years of age, president of the firm of Bauer & Black, died at his home, 4901 Grand Blvd., Friday, Jan. 1, 1909. The deceased was born in Wingarten, Germany, and came to America in 1866. He settled in Chicago in 1889. He was a member of the Chicago Lodge, A. F. and A. M., the Oriental consistory and Medinah Temple.
