The will of Mrs. Nannie Loewenthal, Widow of Berthold Loewenthal, was filed in the Probate Court? She left $18,000. to ten hospitals and homes of Chicago.

The following institutions were benefitted by her bequests: Michael Reese Hospital, $3000; Jewish Training School of Chicago, $3000; Home for Aged Jews of Chicago, $3000; Chicago Home for Jewish Orphans, $3000; Chicago Home for Incurables, $1000; Chicago Lying-In Hospital; and Dispensary, $1000; Home for the Friendless, $1000; Chicago Orphan Asylum $1000; and the Home for Jewish Friendless and Working Girls, $1000.
