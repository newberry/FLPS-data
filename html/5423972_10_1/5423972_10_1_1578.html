Siegmund Florsheim died the 24th of March, at the age of 60. He was born near Frankfort, Germany and came to America when a young man.

In 1863 he opened a retail shoe store on N. Clark St., and a few years later went into the wholesale trade. The firm of Greensfelder, Florsheim and Co. was one of the largest shoe houses in Chicago for years. About two years ago, the partnership was dissolved, Mr. Greensfelder retiring.
