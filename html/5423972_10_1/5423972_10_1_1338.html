Nathan Mayer, one of the pioneers of Chicago, died Sunday, November 18, at his home, 4404 Greenwood Ave., at the age of 75. The deceased was born in Durkheim, Bavaria, and came to Chicago in 1854. In 1855 he went into business. He was a charter member of Sinai Congregation. For many years he was Director in Michael Reese Hospital and U.H.C.
