Moses Bensinger, president of the Brunswick-Balke-Collander Co., died on October 14. He was born in Louisville, Ky. in 1839 and in 1865 was married to Miss Eleanore Brunswick. Mr. Bensinger became identified with his father-in-law in the manufacture and sale of billiard and pool tables and became president of the corporation in 1890.

Mr. Bensinger was a prominent member of Sinai Congregation of which he was a director. He was also a director of Michael Reese Hospital. He was active in the Standard and Lakeside Clubs.
