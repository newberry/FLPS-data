Harry Berkman has been again selected to act as judge for the annual Midwest Intercollegiate Gymnastic Conference, to be held at the University of Minnesota, March 20 and 21. Mr. Berkman has been acting in this capacity for 17 years.

He led a team throughout Europe in 1908 and they participated in the International Gymnastic Conference held in Germany.

Mr. Berkman was the originator of athletics at the Jewish People's Institute, where he was athletic director for 15 years. He induced Chicago philanthropists to donate $250,000 towards building a modern gymnasium for the J.P.I.
