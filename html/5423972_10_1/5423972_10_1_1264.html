Senator Samuel A. Ettelson, is one of the men responsible for the enactment of the Direct Primary Law. He firmly believes that the people are entitled to a direct vote in the selection of the nominees for every public office. He has drafted a bill to be presented to the Legislature giving the people the right to select delegates to the National Convention to nominate a President of the United States.

Mr. Ettelson is also the author of the bill to procure a Public Service Commission over all public utilities. As a result of this measure, a legislative investigation commission has been appointed.
