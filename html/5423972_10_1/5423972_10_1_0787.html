Mr. H. L. Meites was appointed Superintendent of Water. The department of which Mr. Meites will be in charge transacts $15,000,000 business every year.

Mr. Meites is the founder and editor of the Chicago Jewish Chronicle, which under his care has grown to be the widest read English-Jewish weekly in Chicago. He is also secretary to the Jewish Historical Society of Illinois, and has for years been active in Zionist and other cultural and philanthropic work.
