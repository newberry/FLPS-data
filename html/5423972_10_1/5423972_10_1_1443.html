Joseph Weissenbach is a Chicagoan, born in April 1875. He studied law at the Chicago College of Law. In 1896 he was appointed Assistant State's Attorney under Chas. Deneen and he filled this office creditably until 1900 when he resigned to form a partnership with Willard McEwen. Mr. Weissenbach is the author of a work entitled "Crimes and Litigations of the Russian Jew in the United States."

He is secretary of the Jewish Training School. He was financial secretary and is now a director of the Lakeside Club.
