Henry L. Burman has been appointed master-in-chancery by the judges of the Superior Court. In assuming his new office, Mr. Burman has resigned as assistant corporation counsel.

He was born in Roumania in 1903, and came to Chicago at the age of three. Mr. Burman has been active in civic work and is a member of the Chicago Bar Association; the Isaac Cutler Masonic Lodge; and the Covenant Club.
