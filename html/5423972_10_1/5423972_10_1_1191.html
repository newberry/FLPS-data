Isaac Hess, pioneer packer is dead at the age of 74. He was born in Germany in 1840 and came to the United States in 1854. In 1862 he settled in Chicago and began his career as a peddler. He was an intimate friend of Nelson Morris who later became his brother-in-law. Mr. Morris and Mr. Hess set up independent packing plants which merged in 1898. Mr. Hess was the uncle of the new minister to Sweden, Ira Morris.
