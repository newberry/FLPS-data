Mr. Samuel Straus was born at Kirchheimbolanden, in Rhein Pfalz, on January 22, 1823. He came to Chicago in July, 1852. He joined the K.A.M. in 1854 and was often requested by the Board of Administration to assist in the reading of the prayers, especially on New Year's Day, and the Day of Atonement. He died in Chicago, July 8, 1878.
