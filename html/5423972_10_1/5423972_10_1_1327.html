Meyer E. Lindauer, active in the early business life of Chicago, died in June 7. He was born in Germany 71 years ago and came to the U.S. when a boy of 14. He arrived in Chicago in 1858 and, with his brother, Benjamin, established the firm of Linda uer Bros., wholesale furnishings. For many years Lindauer Bros. was the largest business of its kind in the east after a period of 30 years, in which the brothers amassed a fortune, they retired from active business life. Meyer Lindauer was one of the organizers of the Standard Club.
