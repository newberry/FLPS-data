Edward Rubovits was born in Sebes-Kellener, Hungary, November 26, 1840. He came to America in 1867, and to Chicago in 1868. Mr. Rubovits was for many years a stationer and printer.

He died October 21, 1905. During the last five years of his life, he was superintendent of the U. H. C. He was also a member of Sinai Congregation.
