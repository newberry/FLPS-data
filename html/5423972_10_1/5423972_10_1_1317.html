Siegfried H. Kirchberger was born February 12, 1848, in Nieder-lahnstein, Germany. He came to America at the age of five, and settled in Chicago in July 1887.

He was associated with Moses Born and Oscar Rosenthal under the firm name of Moses Born and Company, until his death, October 12, 1902. He was an active member of Sinai Congregation, and was a liberal contributor to our charitable institutions.
