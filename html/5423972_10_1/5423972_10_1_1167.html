Prof. Julius Stieglitz, of the Dept. of Chemistry at the University of Chicago, has been made chairman of the department. He has been connected with the department since its founding and is an associate editor of the Journal of the American Chemical Society. He is also a member of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences as well as of the International Commission on Annual Tables of Constants. He has been for several years, Director of Analytical Chemistry and Director of the University Laboratories.
