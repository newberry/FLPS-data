Harry A. Lipsky has been appointed Election Commissioner by County Judge Edmund K. Jarecki.

He was born in Chicago on April 22, 1875, and was educated in the city's public schools. He has been the general manager of the Daily Jewish Courier since 1910, and was a member of the Board of Education (1911-1917). He has been president of the Cook County Civil Service Commission since 1918, and chairman of the Committee on Education of the Chicago Association of Commerce (1917-1918. He was also a member of the Milk Commission in 1912.
