Isaac J. Robin, one of Chicago's pioneers, died at the age of seventy-five years, on May 9.

Mr. Robin was one of the first of the Russian intelligentsia to come to America thirty-five years ago, and entered the drug business. Mr. and Mrs. Robin's home was the rendezvous of the progressive Russian youth who came to Chicago in the late eighties (80's) and early nineties (90's) to seek their fortune.
