Chicago lodges of the Jews order B'nai B'rith gave a ball at North Side Turner Hall, last evening, for the benefit of the night school conducted by Prof. Bamberger at Taylor and Judd streets for the instruction of Russian refugees in the English language. One hundred couples participated. The Committee on Arrangements consisted of Messrs. Sigmond Zeisler, Samuel Taussig, E. Rubovits, M. Guthman, and N. Kean. It is thought $1,000 was realized for the school, which is attended by four hundred refugees of all ages.
