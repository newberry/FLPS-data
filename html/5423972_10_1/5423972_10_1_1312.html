Levi Mayer was born in Richmond Va. in 1858, and came to Chicago in 1864. He took up the study of law and, after having been admitted to the Bar, made rapid strides in his profession. He is now one of the foremost lawyers, not only in Chicago, but in the United States. He is the senior member of Mayer, Meyer, and Austrian, one of the most prominent law firms in Chicago. Mr. Mayer has organized a number of the largest corporations and trusts and is the attorney for many of the biggest business houses in the city. He is a member of Sinai Congregation.
