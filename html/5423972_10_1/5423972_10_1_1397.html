The last will and testament of the late Oscar Rosenthal, 3333 Michigan Ave., bequeaths to the United Hebrew Charities of Chicago the amount of $7,500 to be used as an endowment. The annual interest on this amount is to be expended in carrying on the work of the organization. The following institutions were given $1,500 each - Michael Reese Hospital, Chicago Home for Jewish Orphans, German Old People's Home, the Home for Aged Jews, Jewish Training School, Chicago Home for the Friendless, and the Chicago Home for Incurables.
