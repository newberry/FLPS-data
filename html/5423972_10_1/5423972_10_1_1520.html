Isaac Rubel died Tuesday morning at his home, 3341 Wabash Ave. Mr. Rubel was 69 years of age and had lived in Chicago for forty-five years. He was born in Bavaria, Germany, and came to this country in 1853. He settled in Chicago and became interested in the hardware trade. He was a member of the firm of Rubel Brothers for a number of years, and about fifteen years ago began the manufacture of furnaces and ranges in his own name.
