Louis Schiff, 93, pioneer, retired coal and furniture dealer, died yesterday at his home.

He was born in Russia in 1835, and came to Chicago in 1863. For many years he made his home on the site of the old Bridewell, at Polk and Wells streets.

Mr. Schiff was one of the first organizers of the Beth Hamedrash Hagodol, the first orthodox congregation in Chicago.
