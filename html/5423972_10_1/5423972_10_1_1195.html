The Associated Jewish Charities of Chicago, 30 North La Salle Street, is given $1,006 and a bequest of $500 is made to the Chicago Home for the Friendless, 5059 Vincennes Avenue, in the will of Henry Greenebaum, admitted to probate yesterday. His estate is valued at $13,000. Helen and Lewis Bergman, 4637 Ellis Avenue, grand-niece and grand-nephew, are bequeathed $5,000 each. Mrs. Emma Foreman, 5231 South Michigan Avenue; Mrs. Bertha Kantowicz, 5234 East End Avenue, and Mollie Seckbach, 5706 South Michigan Avenue, nieces, were given $1,000 each.
