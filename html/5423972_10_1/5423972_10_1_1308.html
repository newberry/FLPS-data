Abraham Strauss was born in Sousenheim, Germany, Sept. 24, 1829. He came to America in 1858, and arrived in Chicago in 1866.

He was engaged in the wholesale clothing business under the name of Simon, Meyer, Strauss & Co., which was changed to Meyer, Strauss & Goodman, and later, to Strauss, Yondorf & Rose. He remained a member of the latter firm up to the time of his death, which occured on April 8, 1894.

He was a charter member of the Sinai Congregation and a member of the Board of the U. H. C.
