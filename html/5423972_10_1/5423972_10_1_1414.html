David M. Pfaelzer was born November 23, 1853, at Laudenbach. He came to America in July, 1872, and for a number of years has been engaged in the wholesale clothing business. He is an active member of K. A. M., and is on the Board. He is superintendent of the Sabbath School. Mr. Pfalezer is a contributor to the Associated Charities and a member of the Standard Club.
