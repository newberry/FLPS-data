Isaac Kahn Friedman, Chicago author and magazine writer, died on Tuesday. He was 61 years old.

Mr. Friedman was born in Chicago, and graduated in 1893, from the University of Michigan, where he was a member of Phi Beta Kappa, and he later took a post-graduate course at the University of Chicago.

He wrote special articles for Chicago newspapers and for magazines, and in 1908, went to the Orient as a special correspondent for the Chicago Daily News.
