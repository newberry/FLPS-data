Mr. Henry Hart was born in Eppelsheim, Rhenish Hessia, Feb. 17, 1850. In 1858 he came with his parents to America. Mr. Hart is a member of the well known wholesale clothing firm of Hart, Schaffner and Marx. He is vice-president of Sinai Congregation, a member of the Standard and Hamilton Clubs, a director of the Home for Aged Jews, and vice-president of the Associated Jewish Charities of Chicago.
