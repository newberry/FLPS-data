The well known Philantrophist, Mr. Leon Mandel, who died November 4th left an estate of over $3,000,000. His Widow and two sons are the executors of his will which he made Sept. 26, 1910.

He left $50,000 for Jewish Charities and a Workers fund of $50,000. Other bequests ranged from ten thousand to a thousand dollars to his friends and old employees in the Department.Store.

His two Sons will remain in full control of the big Department Store.
