Lewis Hecht, one of the oldest real estate men in Cook County and for a period, president of the Cook County Real Estate Board, left an estate of over $200,000.

He bequeathed $1,000 to the Hebrew Benevolent Society, and a trust fund of $50,000 to be used as a perpetual endowment in honor of his father and mother and to be known as The Solomon and Rachel Hecht Memorial Fund. The fund is to be governed by the Associated Jewish Charities of Chicago.
