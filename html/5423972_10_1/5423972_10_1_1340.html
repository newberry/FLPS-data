Myer S. Emrich is the Independent Candidate for Probate Court Judge. He was born in Baltimore and came to Chicago in 1888. He attended Northwestern University, where he studied law. He was admitted to the Bar in 1891.

Mr. Emrich has built up a lucrative practice, and includes among his clients the Retail Furniture Dealers Association. He is a member of B'nai B'rith Hillel Lodge, and other prominent organizations.
