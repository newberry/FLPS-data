Morris Einstein, one of the founders of the Michael Reese Hospital, Sinai Congregation, and the Standard Club, died at the age of 90. He was born in Wurtemburg, Germany, and emigrated to the United States at the age of 16.

Mr. Einstein was a pioneer resident of Chicago, coming here from Joliet, in 1863. He was engaged in the clothing business, from which he retired 30 years ago.
