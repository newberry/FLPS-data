Adolph Kraus of Chicago, president of the International B'nai B'rith organization, has left Berlin to begin a comprehensive official visit to the great Jewish Oder throughout Germany, Bohemia, Hungary, Austria, and France.

Distinguished attention has been accorded to him by the German B'nai B'rith which gave a banquet in his honor. It was attended by the president of 43 local organizations from all parts of the country.
