Isaac Wedeles, president of Steele-Wedeles Co., passed away early Wednesday morning. He was a native of Austria, and was born in 1824. He came to America in 1858 and settled in Chicago soon after. He entered the grocery business and acted as clerk for several firms until 1862. In that year he became associated with Max and Bernhard Steele and the three entered into partnership. Their first store was located at 551 Clark St. where they remained for a number of years. Both Max and Bernhard Steele died in the early nineties, and Mr. Wedeles became the active head of the firm. He was a member of Sinai Congregation.
