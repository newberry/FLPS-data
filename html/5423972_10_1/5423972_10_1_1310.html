Bernhard Cahn was born in Partenheim-Rhein-Hessen. He came to America in 1852, and to Chicago in 1857. He was for many years a member of the wholesale clothing house of Cahn, Wampold, and Co. Mr. Cahn was on the Board of various charitable institutions, including the United Hebrew Charities.

Mr. Cahn died May r, 1908.
