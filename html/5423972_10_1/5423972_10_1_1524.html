Samuel Tausig, prominent commissioner broker and well known in Jewish circles in Chicago, died at his office, 42 River St., Friday afternoon, March 18th. The deceased was born in Jersey City, forty-six years ago. His brokerage business during the last few years had been confined to dealing in rice, sugar and molasses. He was an ex-president of the Commercial Council of the National Union, and also a past-president of the District Grand Lodge No. 6, I. O. B. B.
