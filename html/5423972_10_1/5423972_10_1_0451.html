Attention all immigrants from Wilna and the neighboring vicinity! A memorial meeting in memory of the Wilna religious martyrs who perished in the gruesome destruction in Wilna will take place Sunday, July 13, at two o'clock in the afternoon in the Douglas Park Auditorium, Ogden and Kedzie Avenues. Noted speakers will address the meeting, among them being K. Marmar, Leon Zolotkoff, Rabbi Shonfieds and many others.

Rabbi Nisan Budzinsky will present the eulogy. Chazan Halperin will recite the memorial prayer. All immigrants from Wilna and the neighboring vicinity are expected to attend.
