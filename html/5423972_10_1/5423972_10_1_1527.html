Frederick W. Straus died on Wednesday morning. By his death, the Jewish community loses a staunch and loyal member. He was born in Rhenish, Prussia in 1833. He came of this country in 1853 and moved to Chicago in 1883. Since that time he had been engaged in the banking, mortgage, loan and real estate business under the firm names of F. W. and J. Straus, and Straus Bros. and Co. He was a member of Sinai Congregation, the Standard Club and of the Masonic and B'nai B'rith fraternity.
