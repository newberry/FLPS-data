Herman Eliassof was born in 1850 in Vilna. He came to America in 1872 and to Chicago in 1873, where he became the first minister of Beth-El Congregation.

He has been connected with K. A. M. for 12 years as teacher and is now principal of the Sabbath-school. He is considered to be one of the best Hebrew scholars in the country and a writer of poems in the pure classic Hebrew.
