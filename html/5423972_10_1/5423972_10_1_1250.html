Mr. Jerusalimsky Passes Away.

Mr. Marcus Jerusalimsky, father of Miss Bertha R. Jerusalimsky, Business Manager, in charge of the Woman's Department of the Chicago Hebrew Institute, died recently after a short illness. The deceased is survived by the widow and five children. Mr. Jerusalimsky was a resident of this City many years and was a well-known personality, especially on the West-Side and in Zionistic circles, to which cause he rendered valuable service.
