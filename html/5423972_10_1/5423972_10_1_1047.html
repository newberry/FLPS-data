Joseph G. Snydachker, capitalist and collector of rare painting, died on Oct. 13. He was born in Chicago on Sept. 2, 1863.

Mr. Snydachker was president of the Pacific American Fisheries Co., the Puget Sound and Alaska Shipbuilding Yards Co., the Hawaiian Pineapple Co., and the firm of Scotten & Snydachker.

He was part owner of the Woods Theater and office building. His collection of paintings is estimated to be worth $1,000,000.

Mr. Snydachker was a member of the Chicago Board of Trade and was at one time a Director.
