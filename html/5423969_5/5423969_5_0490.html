The Oak Grove Lutheran Ladies' Seminary has been reorganized. At the special meeting held to discuss the Seminary, the following recommendations were made: That an appeal be made for contributions to meet the expenses of the Seminary, and to pay off old debts; That a new school building be built; That the following students be ordained: Louis Hansen, H. M. Hemingsen, Lars Tryseland, Inguald Thvedt, and Knud O. Lee.

It was reported that the debts of the Seminary were practically liquidated.
