Chicago Norwegians will celebrate Thanksgiving day in a spirited manner. There will be special religious services by every church of every denomination. Parties will be held in all welfare institurions: The Deaconess' Home; Old Peoples Home; Childrens Home; and Thabita Hospital.

The Norwegian Logan Square Baptist Church will have a special Thanksgiving sermon at 10:30 A.M. A choir composed of three different church choirs will do the singing; three ministers will lead the sermons and offers will be taken. The amount collected to be turned over to the Norwegian Childrens Home.
