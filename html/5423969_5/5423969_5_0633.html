Trinity Church:- Elling Eilsen preached first Lutheran sermon on American soil in Chicago in 1839. In the '40s a congregation was formed and in 1857 Trinity church was formally organized at Indiana and Peoria Streets, a location then out on "the prairie."

Bethlehem and Bethany churches were split from this church by dissenting groups.

As late as 1905 the services in Trinity Church were carried on half in Norwegian and half in English.
