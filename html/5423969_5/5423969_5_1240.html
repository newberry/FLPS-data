F. C. Ronning, 3323 Hirch St., is seventy years old today. He was born in Trondhjem, came to American in 1896, and has lived in Chicago ever since. He is a contracting painter, and people from far and wide have sought him on account of his reliability.

Mr. Ronning is one of the oldest members of the Norwegian Singing Society and has a large circle of friends who are extending to him their best wishes for the day. He is a man of rugged health, and in spite of his age he is as lively and strong as a youngster.

His wife, Alma, nee Tornkvist, was born in Sweden.
