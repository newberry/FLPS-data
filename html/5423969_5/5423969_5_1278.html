The well known baritone soloist, Joel Mossberg, recently returned from a very successful concert tour, from which our Norwegian Colony welcomes him with open arms. Mr. Mossberg, beside being our favorite soloist, is our dean of conductors in male chorus circles.

The recent concert tour was a financial and artistic triumph, and Scandia congratulates Mr. Mossberg and his ultra-capable accompanist, Paul Hultman, a pupil of the great Scharwenka.
