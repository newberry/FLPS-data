Rev. George Albert Gullixon was born in Bade, Iowa, December 19, 1866. He came to Chicago, in July, 1902. He accepted the pastorate of the Norwegian Evangelical-Lutheran Church, St. Paul, Wisconsin. Rev. Gullixon is a member of the board of the Home Mission of the Eastern District of the Synod of the Norwegian-Lutheran Church of America. He was elected to this office in 1895 and was reelected in 1904, and is still holding this position. He is also vice-president of the Lutheran Home Finding Society of Illinois for dependent children.
