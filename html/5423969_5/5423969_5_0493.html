The Norwegian Lutheran Synod has missions among the Eskimos, the Indians, the Negroes, and the Mormons. In Africa the Synod operates the Schreuder or Zulu Mission.

At the annual conference of the Synod, reports were given for the Theological Students' Mission Society....the orphan asylums, children's homes, homes for the aged, the church extension committee, and the pension committee.

After the several reports were delivered, the conference adjourned until tomorrow.
