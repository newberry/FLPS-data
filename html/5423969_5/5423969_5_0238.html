Andrew Haagensen was editor of the Methodist church paper Missionaeren, 1870-1877, of the church organ Den Kristelige Talsmand, 1880-1884, 1890-1897. He was author of a trenchant volume entitled "Methodism and Lutheranism Compared;" also "The Norwegian and Danish Mission History" and illustrated Bible history, all in the Norwegian language.

(These church papers and books were printed and edited in Chicago, Ill.) J.E.
