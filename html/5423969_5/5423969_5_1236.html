Peter J. Hoffstad, 2444 Ballou St., the owner of a large photographer's business, and a man widely known among the Norwegians in Chicago died on Wednesday, Dec.26, in his home after a protracted illness. He was suffering from heart trouble and certain complications of illnesses.

Mr. Hoffstad was born at Vesteraalen, Norway, in 1864. Nineteen years old, he emigrated to America, and in 1890 settled in Chicago. His business is at present one of the largest of its kind in Chicago.

Mr. Hoffstad was an active man, interested in every worthwhile movement of which he was aware. Especially was he interested in everything Norwegian.

He is survived by his wife and seven children.
