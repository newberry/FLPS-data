The Norwegian Lutheran Mission Society of Chicago held its regular annual meeting last Tuesday at St. Paul's Lutheran Church. The president, Reverend Lars Harrisville, was chairman, and Reverend M. L. Nesvig served as secretary.

The usual reports were read and accepted. The traveling financial secretary, S. K. Lund, reported donations totaling $1,800. This sum was used to pay the balance due on the lot on Kedzie Avenue. The plans for the new home were shown and discussed.
