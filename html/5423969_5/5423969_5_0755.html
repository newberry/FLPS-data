The Rev. Nils M. Vlvisaker, minister of the Church of Our Saviour,is going to Camp Logan, Houston, Texas to start work as camp chaplain and representative in the army of all the Lutheran churches in America. He has received the names and home addresses of a number of the boys at the camp, and is going to look them up. A well attended farewell dinner was given in his honor by the trustees of the church he has served so long, and a liberal money gift, the proceeds of a collection was given him as a farewell gesture. Rev. Ylvisaker is not leaving the church for good, he expects to return after the war.
