Pastor Andrew Haagensen, founder of the Norwegian Division of the Methodist Episcopal Church in America, passed away at the Evanston Hospital on Wednesday. He was born in Sarpsborg, Norway and came to America in 1877. He founded the Norwegian M.E.Church of Evanston in 1882 and served as its pastor for a number of years. Rev. Haagensen held several national offices in the Norwegian Division of the M.E. Church. He was seventy-five years of age at the time of his death.
