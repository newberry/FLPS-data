p.4..................One of Chicago's Norwegian colony's prominent and very popular members, Mr. Nicolay Grevstad (editor of Skandinaven) has been signally honored by President Taft. Mr. Grevstad has been appointed Embassador to Paraguay and Uraguay, S.A., and the confirmation of his appointment by congress is a foregone conclusion. We congratulate our colleague and extend to him our best wishes for a pleasant South American sojourn, and continued success in his every endeavor.
