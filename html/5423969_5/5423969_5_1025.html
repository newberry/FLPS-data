E. H. Hobe, Norwegian consul at St. Paul, Minnesota, was in Chicago on Thursday in the interest of the Norwegian-American Line. He will return to Chicago in a week to start a four-month campaign for the sale of Norwegian-American Line stock. It is expected that another million crowns' worth of the stock will be sold here. Mr. Hobe will tour Illinois, Iowa, Wisconsin, Minnesota, North Dakota, South Dakota, Montana, and the Pacific states, visiting Norwegian colonies in these states.
