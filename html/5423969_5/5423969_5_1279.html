Mr. and Mrs. Wm. Gerner entertained in honor of the Norwegian artist, Henrick Lund, last Saturday evening.

About forty guests (Norwegian, Swedish, and Danish) were invited, and all enjoyed an excellent program and a tasty supper. The Swedish group entertained with a number of Swedish songs, and the guest of honor pleased all with his humorous remarks, readings, and imitations.

Speakers of the "Punch Bowl" included Dr. Doe, Dr. Holmboc, Birger Osland, and others.
