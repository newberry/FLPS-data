The advocate, James A. Petersen, moved his office thirteen years ago to suite 1313 on the thirteenth floor in the Chambers of Commerce Building. On April 13th he celebrated his 13th anniversary in room 1313 on the 13th floor. He ordered thirteen boxes of cigars, each box containing thirteen of the best handmade cigars, which he presented to thirteen of his best friends. Mr. Petersen is not afraid of the evil number thirteen.
