The Norwegian Lutheran Mission Home Society held its biannual meeting at St. Paul's Church last Tuesday evening. The secretary gave a report from the last meeting; eighteen new members were accepted, and three life members joined.

It was reported that the Girls' Society at the Deaconess Home had donated a new piano to the Mission Home. The discussion centered around the issue of paying for the lots that had recently been bought. One woman pledged $100.
