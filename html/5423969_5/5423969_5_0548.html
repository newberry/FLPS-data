On Sunday, March 28, Reverend Ludvig Hope addressed the Scandinavian Young Men's Christian Association on the topic of "Sanctification".

Reverend Solberg spoke on the "Comparison between Justification and Sanctification". Reverend Ellestad....and several others took part in the discussion.

The chorus of the Young Men's Christian Association sang several numbers. The hall was crowded at each of the meetings.

At the Monday meeting a large audience gathered to hear Reverend Ludvig Hope preach.

2

In the evening Reverend Hope spoke first; he was followed by a number of his fellow clergymen.
