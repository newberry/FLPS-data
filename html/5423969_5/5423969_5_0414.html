Another Norwegian church, the Wesley Methodist Church in Irving Park, is to be dedicated tomorrow at 3:00 P. M., with Bishop McDowell heading the ceremonies.

In addition to the dedicatory ceremonies, there will be two festival services, morning and evening. The congregation formerly had their church home in the old Maplewood Avenue Church at Le Moyne St.), but sold the property, which is now a Jewish Synagogue. O. H. Wilsar is the pastor of the new church.
