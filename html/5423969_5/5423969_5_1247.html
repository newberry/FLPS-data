Hauman G. Haugan, director of the State Bank of Chicago was elected honorary member of Norsemens Singing Society of Chicago on his 75th birthday. It is the oldest singing society and Mr. Haugan has always had the singers interests at heart.

Mr. Haugan was the first president of Norsemens Singing Society of La Crosse, Wisconsin; the oldest singing society of America.

Mr. Haugan was unable to be present at the regular meeting, so the officers of the society called upon Mr. Haugan.
