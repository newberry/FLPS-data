Enoch Strom's Funeral took place Sunday afternoon from his residence at 58 West Huron Street. A great mass of people attended; quantities of flowers were sent and one large floral piece from the Norwegian Relief Association of which he had been president and treasurer for years.

Pastor Quello officiated at the service at his home and he was burried at Rose Hill Cemetary where the Ben Hur Lodge officiated. All the prominent Norwegians were present. He was born in Stavanger, Norway in 1855.
