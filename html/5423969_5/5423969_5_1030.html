p.7... Mortensen and Crook, Steamship Agents, on the northwest side, report an unusually large number of reservations to Norway. Many Chicagoans are taking their first trip to the country of their birth, and a number of Norwegians, American born, out proud of their ancestral land, are curious to visit its historical spots, and see Norway for the first time. For the older travelers, it will be a trip to see the mother; for youngsters a visit to the grandparents.
