The news that Roald Amundsen discovered the South Pole swept like fire through our Norwegian Colony, and became not only the main subject of all conversation, but created a new form of greeting, i.e., "Gratulerer Med Amundsen" (congratulations on Amundsen).

The Norwegian club called messages of congratulation to Roald Amundsen and King Haakon of Norway.

King Haakon replied with the following cablegram:

Kristiana, Norway

Mar. 10, 1912.

Norwegian Club

Chicago

Thanks for kind telegram. Felt sure all Norwegian would rejoice at good \2news.

Haakon, Rex

Last night the Club held a meeting for members only. After the business session came lunch, with music, singing, and readings to complete another enjoyable evening.
