The Norwegian-Danish Methodist congregation of Moreland was organized twenty-five years ago. Immediately after organizing, the young congregation began the construction of a church at Indiana Street and 50th Avenue. The building was dedicated on August 15, 1886.

The group that organized the congregation is still very active in the Church. The present Church is located at Ontario Street and 51st Avenue.

The oldest pastor in the Norwegian-Danish Pastor's Conference, Reverend O. L. Hansen, will preach in the Church next Sunday.
