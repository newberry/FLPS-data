Last Sunday, the laying of the cornerstone of the new Zion Church was performed in the usual festive spirit. The Zion Church was organized in 1884 by Reverend O. P. Vangness. The following pastors have been attached to the Church: O. P. Vangness, 1887; H. Rosenquist, 1887-90; John Halvorsen, 1890-92; Christian Anderson, 1892 to date.

The new Church will cost $40,000, and will have a seating capacity of one thousand. The building committee consisted of J. W. Wilson, P. A. Ruseth, Ole Bredesen, Ole Glsruig, C. J. Hosgaard, and Reverend Christian Anderson.
