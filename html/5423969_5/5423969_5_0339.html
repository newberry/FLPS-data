The great tent where this mission is conducted is located at the corner of Armitage and Hamlin Avenues. This is the only Norwegian-Lutheran tent mission in Chicago. Meetings are held every Sunday at 4:00 P.M. and at 8:00 P.M. Meetings are also held on Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays at 8:00 o'clock P.M.

The sermons are invariably very good, by various ministers of note within our churches. Besides there is excellent singing and music.

The mission is sponsored by our Lutheran churches in Chicago; It welcomes everyone and deserves the support of everyone.
