Last Thursday night a number of Scandinavians gathered in Martin Thanes' home at 1045 North Fairfield Avenue, and organized a new church for Logan Square. The church called Pastor J. H. Meyer, former minister of Zion's Church, until further notice the church services will be held in Kimball Hall every Sunday at 4:00 P.M. and in Sharlous Hall every Sunday at 10:30 A.M.
