The Lutheran Church of the Redeemer, corner of Samon and Bernice Avenues, was dedicated yesterday. Reverend J. Nordby, president of the Norwegian Lutheran Church of America, presided. The Lutheran Church of the Redeemer was formerly the Irving Park Lutheran Mission. The first meeting of the congregation was held at the Grayland People's College, Milwaukee and Irving Park Boulevard, in the fall of 1913. The present pastor is Reverend Elias Rasmussen.
