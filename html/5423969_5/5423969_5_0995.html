Scandia enjoyed a visit by two distinguished men yesterday; they were Roald Amundsen, discoverer of the South Pole, and Mr. Lee Keedic of New York, manager of the Amundsen lecture tour through the United States and Canada. Mr. Keedick informs us that Mr. Amudsen's Chicago lecture will be held in Orchestra Hall under the auspices of the Chicago Geographical Society, and that the prices for seats will range from fifty cents to two dollars. In New York Mr. Amundsen will be introduced by Admiral Peary.
