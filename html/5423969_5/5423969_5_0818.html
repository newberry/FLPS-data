The Federated Norwegian Lutheran Young Peoples Societies of America, Inc., consists of Norwegian-Lutheran youth societies and individuals who are members of a Norwegian Lutheran congregation. The purpose of the Federation is to awaken the desire for culture through music, art, literature, and lectures. It is compulsory to use the Norwegian language at lectures and at affairs.

The Federation publishes Norsk Ungdom (Norwegian Youth). Eilert Hjemseth, 1622 North moody Avenue, is the editor, and Olaf Fjeloe is business manager. The paper is published in Chicago.
