P.O.Skaaden, the well-known Norwegian Chicago pioneer,passed away Tuesday, Dec. 11, 77 years old. The deceased was born in Oier's Gudbrandsdalen, Norway in 1840. He was one of the widely known Norwegians in Chicago. For more than forty years he carried on a business as carpenter contractor. For more than fifty years he was a member of our Savior's church. During the past eight or nine years he has been rather of poor health.
