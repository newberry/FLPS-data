The Maplewood Avenue M. E. Church yesterday held a very successful welcome social for their minister, the Rev. Paul Hangan, who is now starting his second year of service to this congregation.

During the past year, Mr. Hangan has gained unusual popularity, both within and outside the congregation, and this popularity was strikingly evident in the cordial reception given. A number of other ministers were present, and they joined with the church members in making the pastor feel that his work in the past had been worthwhile and appreciated.
