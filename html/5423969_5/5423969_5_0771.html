At the last meeting of the Norwegian National Committee, the members resolved to give a grand party for Nicolay A. Grevstad, editor of Skandinaven. Mr. Grevstad has been appointed United States Minister to Uraguay, and will leave shortly for South America. A committee, consisting of A. Abrahamson, George Kramer, C. Solberg, Miss R. Howe, and Mrs. John C. Thye, has been formed to arrange for the party. Mr. Grevstad will receive a grand send-off.
