The last session of the Norwegian Youth Congress was the most active and interesting of the entire Congress. The Choral Union concert was a revelation, and the young choir did the job in a grand way. It was directed by F. Melius Christiansen and Carl A. Sperati, assisted by Miss Alpa Lindaas, Soprano; Adolph Olsen, Violin; and the St. Olaf College Band.
