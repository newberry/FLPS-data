Two young men well-known among the Norwegians of Chicago, Axel R.Olsen, formerly a member of Sleipner, together with the noted skating artist, Ole Bjerke, a member of the Ski Club, Norge, will start tomorrow on their way to France. Both of them have joined the United States Engineers Civic Division. Axel Olsen used to be chief carpenter in the United States Navy, and received honorable discharge in 1907 from the United States Steamship Ohio.
