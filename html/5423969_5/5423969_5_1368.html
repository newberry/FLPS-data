Monticello Lodge, No. 847, I. O. O. F., gave a pleasant social in Bjorgvin Hall last Saturday evening, in honor of the oldest member of the lodge, Mr. John Latas. He has belonged to the order for fifty-six years.

The program was excellent, and all those participating performed well. After the program, a banquet, in no may inferior to the program was given. After the banquet, there was dancing to the music by O. C. Schmidt's orchestra.
