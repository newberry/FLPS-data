Surprising News in Anshe Sholom Synagogue, Ashland Blvd. and Polk St.

All countrymen of Mariampol and Calvary are invited to come this Saturday to the Anshe Sholom Synagogue, where the Bar Mitzwah (Confirmation) of Mr. and Mrs. Henry Levy's son, Samuel Isaac, will take place.

In honor of this happy event the beloved young singer and Cantor, David T. Cohen will lead prayers this Friday evening and Saturday morning.
