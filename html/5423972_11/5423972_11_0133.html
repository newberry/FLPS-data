Our delegate, Aaron Tikotzsky has returned to Chicago. He has with him 350 letters and photographs for natives of Krinik, Horodock, Bialystock, Yolovk, Sokol, Zabludor, etc.

Sunday, August 22nd at 4:00 P. M. in Educational Alliance, 1249 N. Wood st. There our delegate will pass out the letters and present a report of what he has seen.
