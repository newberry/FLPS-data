Good tidings to all countrymen of Mazir and environs.

We announce that the women of our synagogue Agudath Achim Anshe Mozir, 1612 So. Hamlin Ave. have donated a Scroll of the law (Sefer Torah) to our congregation.

We will meet at Maltz Hall, 3512 W. 16th st., Dec. 12th at 11:00 a.m. for a reception.
