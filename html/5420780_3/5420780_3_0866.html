Mr. Kristian Baun was born in Vesting, Denmark, in 1887. He became a school-teacher. In 1887 he left for America. His first position in this country was as editor for two Danish newspapers in Michigan.

Next he preached in a Unitarian Church, which he left to enter a Unitarian college. After obtaining the degree of Doctor of Divinity, he secured a position as correspondent for the Danish paper Pioneer, and at the same time became editor of the Scandinavian Farm Journal in Minneapolis.

Mr. and Mrs. Baun celebrated their golden wedding anniversary October 8, 1936. They have visited Denmark four times.

Mr. Baun holds the degrees of Bachelor of Science and Master of Pharmacy.
