Ebenezer Kvindeforening (Ebenezer Ladies' Society) was organized in 1916 with a board of five members. The aim is to help the pastor, the congregation foreign and inner missions, the Childrens' Home and the needy Danes. The flower committee visits sick people at the hospital and in their homes.

The society meets every 2nd and 4th Thursday of the month in the church basement. It costs 20 cents a month to belong to the society. The organization has about 30 members at present.
