Another young Dane, Peter Damgaard, has died. He was only twenty-three years old. He died of typhoid fever, which he contracted in Cuba, where he went with the First Illinois Infantry Regiment.

He was born in Kolding, Denmark, in 1875. He came to this country with his parents in 1884. After about ten years on a farm the family moved to Chicago, where Peter Damgaard joined the Army.

Mr. Damgaard was buried with full military honors at Oakwood Cemetery.
