page 110.. De Danske Folkedansere (Danish Folkdancers) was started June 5, 1913. Its aim is to further Scandinavian folk-dances in America. Its president is A.S. Karkow, 3717 Wrightwood Ave. Frederikke Sorensen, secretary. The fee is $1.00 for every month in which dancing lessons are given by Mrs. Tousing-Thomsen. The ladies pay only 50 cents per month. Passive members pay 50 cents per year.
