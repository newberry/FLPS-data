Henry L. Hertz, who for many years has been Chief Clerk of the Board of Review was appointed last Monday (March 25), by President McKinley to the post of Internal Revenue Collector for the First Illinois District. His predecessor was Fred E. Coyne, who, beginning April 1 is to be the new postmaster of Chicago.

Mr. Hertz is appointed for four years at a salary of $10,000 per year.
