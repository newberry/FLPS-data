On the occasion of the death of King Frederik VIII, Consul Bech invited a number of countrymen for a conference at his office, and it was decided to arrange a memorial service in Trimtatis Church, Cortes St. and Francisco Ave. A committee of arrangements was formed, with Dr. May Henius as chairman, and Pastor Blichfeldt as secretary.

The service took place yesterday in the presence of a distinguished audience, in which were by special invitation, the consuls of foreign countries.

Pastor Adam Dan officiated and spoke in Danish while professor D. K. Dodge, of the University of Illinois, delivered an address in English.
