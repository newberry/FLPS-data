Last Tuesday, at a luncheon in the Hotel Morrison, a Gold Star Banner was presented to the Foreign Language Divisions.

The inscription on the Danish Banner, accepted by president John C. Christensen, was as follows:

"Presented to the Danish Division, April 27, 1919, by the United States Government Loan Organization in memory of those Americans of Danish blood in the Service of the United States of America who made the supreme sacrifice in the War."
