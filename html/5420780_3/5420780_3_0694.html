It has pleased His Majesty King Haakon of Norway to confer the honor of "Knight's Cross of St. Olav, 1st Degree" to our friend and countryman, composer and conductor, Carl Bush ,in recognition of his thirty years of work in promoting Scandinavian music in the United States.

During the Harmonien Singing Society's visit in Denmark it has pleased His Majesty King Christian X to confer the honor of "Knight of the Danish Flag" to the society's conductor,Mr. Joel Mosberg. The King conferred the same honor on the society's oldest active member, Mr. Jac. A. Hoass.
