While Dr. Henius sticks to his lantern slides and has made quite an enviable record in that line, his daughter Mrs. Gerda Holmes and her husband, the well-known actor Rapley Holmes, have signed a contract with the Essanay Film Mfg. Co. of Chicago and will in the near future be seen here on the silver screen.

And furthermore, as one of the youngest disciples of this new art, the doctor's grand-daughter, little Miss Elizabeth Henius has already had roles in several films.
