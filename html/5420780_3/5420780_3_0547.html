Dansk Sammensluttet Ungdom (The Danish United Youth) has two local societies in Chicago:

Trinitatis Menigheds Ungdomsforening (Trinitatis Congregation Young People's Society) has seventy-five members and emphasizes gymnastics.

St. Stefans Menigheds Ungdomsforening (St. Stefan's Congregation Young People's Society) was founded in 1907. This organization practices gymnastics and has a library of two hundred books.
