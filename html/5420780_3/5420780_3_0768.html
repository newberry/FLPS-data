p.4.col.4.....Danish and other Scandinavian organizations, especially the socialistic groups, have been active in raising funds for relief of those affected by the general strike in Sweden. Collections at meetings and by subscriptions, in sums ranging from $1.20 to $62.50, have swelled the total to date to $1,040.53. Many meetings and petitions are still to be reported. We heartily say, "Well done."
