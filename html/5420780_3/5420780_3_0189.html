As previously announced, a collection of Scandinavian art will be exhibited at the Art Institute of Chicago beginning February 25. Danish, Norwegian, and Swedish societies will be represented at the opening ceremony.

The American-Scandinvian Foundation will be represented by its secretary, Dr. Henry Gaddord Leach, formerly secretary to Doctor Ezan, United States Minister to Denmark. During his stay in Doctor Leach acquired a broad knowledge of Scandinavian art and literature, and is going to give lectures on these subjects in Chicago and other cities, to which the exhibition will be moved successively.
