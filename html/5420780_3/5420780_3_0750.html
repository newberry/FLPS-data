The student singers will arrive in Chicago at 9:00 A.M. Sunday May 21, at the Baltimore & Ohio Depot, 5th Avenue and Harrison Street.

Welcoming speech by the president of the welcoming committee, Henry L. Hertz. Song by Danish Norwegian and Swedish singing societies. Possibly the students will sing.

From the depot, by automobile to the Auditorium Hotel, by the beautiful Lake Michigan.

All Danes in Chicago will meet at the depot before 9:00 A.M.; there is room for thousands. All Danes are requested to wear a red-white ribbon on their coat lapel.
