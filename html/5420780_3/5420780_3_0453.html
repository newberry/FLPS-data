N. P. Lang, Lutheran minister, was born at Ostofte near Maribo, Denmark, January 3rd, 1868. He graduated in 1894 from Dana College, Blair, Neb.; studied theology at Trinity Seminary, Blair, Neb., and at Lutheran Seminary, Chicago; was ordained minister in 1897; and has been professor of Theology at Trinity Seminary, Blair, Neb., since 1909.

(Rev. N. P. Lang is now the head of the United Danish Church here in Chicago, and lives at 29ll Logan Blvd.)
