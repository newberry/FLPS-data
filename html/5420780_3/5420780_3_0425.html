"Women's Missionary Society of the Ebenezer Danish Evangelical Lutheran Church of Chicago" was organized May 19, 1932 by Mrs. Rev. C. M. Videbeck. Mrs. H.P. Petersen was elected president pro tem, and Mrs. Arnold Petersen, secretary pro tem. The final constitution of the society was approved by the church council January 16th, 1933.

The meetings are held in the various homes. The attendance has been from 40 to 50.

At a meeting in July, 1934, it was decided to join the Syncdical W. M. S., which then was only three years old having a total of 53 societies with a membership of 1,674.
