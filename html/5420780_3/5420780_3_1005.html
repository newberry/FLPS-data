Henry L. Hertz was born in Copenhagen, Denmark, November 19, 1847. He came to Chicago in 1869. He was, for a while, employed in a bank; a few years later he was Clerk of the Criminal Court.

In 1886 he was elected State Treasurer. From 1901 to 1912 he was connected with the Chicago Tax Department.

He operated the only "Brewers School" in Chicago.
