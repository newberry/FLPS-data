The folk dancers and athletes of the St. Stefan Youth Society gave a demonstration of old Danish dances and modern Danish gymnastics last Thursday in Hamilton Park, at the request of the Park Commission. The performance was attended by a large American audience, including a number of Chicago public school teachers.

The demonstration was very successful, and earned much applause and favorable comment.

Much of the credit, as far as the dances are concerned, should go to their able instructor, Miss Dagny Pedersen.
