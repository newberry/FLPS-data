Dansk Folkesamfund i Amerika (The Danish People's Society in America) has two societies in Chicago:

1. Chicago South Side. President, Christian Hansen, 3951 Prairie Avenue.

2. Chicago West Side. President C. C. Christensen, 2944 Logan Block.

Dansk Folkesamfund i Amerika on September 30, 1912, had 30 life members and 341 annual members [in Chicago].
