In the Danish Evangelical Lutheran Trinitatis Church, on Bickerdike Street, a convention is to be held all this week. Danish ministers from all over the United States are expected. It is believed that forty ministers and seventy lay delegates will attend. A split is feared if an understanding cannot be reached regarding the interpretation of the Bible.

Pastor O. S. Kirkeberg is chairman of the convention and Mr. M. Rasmussen is vice-chairman. Pastor A. Dan of Minneapolis is secretary, and Pastor Gravengaard of Sioux City is assistant secretary. The meeting is scheduled to last the entire week.
