The Danish Ambassador to the United States, Count Carl Moltke, arrived in Chicago April 13. He was met at the station by Consul Geo. Beck, Dr. Max Henius, and ex-Consul C. H. Hansen. The next evening a banquet was given in his honor at the Red Star Inn, at which about eighty persons were present, among them Consul P. B. Nelson and Ivar Kirkegaard of Racine.
