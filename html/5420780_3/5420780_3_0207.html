The district committee of the Danish-American Society recently invited leaders of all Danish societies, lodges, and congregations to a meeting at the Bismarck last night, for the purpose of discussing what ought to be done in order to make the Danish student singers' concert in Chicago a success.

According to a cable from Copenhagen, the student singers are due in Chicago Sunday morning, May 21. They will stop in Chicago for two days and then go on to Racine.
