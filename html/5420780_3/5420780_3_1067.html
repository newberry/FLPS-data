One of the oldest and best known Danish businessmen in Chicago, Rasmus J. Jensen, died last Wednesday at the age of 62.

Mr. Jensen, who was born in Denmark, came to Chicago about 1870 and for 20 years conducted a jewelry business at the corner of Milwaukee and Morgan, from where he later moved to Milwaukee and Robey.

He was an interested member of many Danish and American societies and very popular, due to his straightforward and jovial character.
