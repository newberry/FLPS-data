The Danish Press Club was the initiator of the new "Constantin Brun Fund" here in Chicago. The following officers were elected: S. J. Heiberg, president; P. H. Kunst, secretary.

The purpose of the fund is to give Danish-Americans in Chicago a chance to go to Denmark, either for recreation or study. In the following issue of Danish Times, we will give a list of those suggested to be the first to benefit by this fund.
