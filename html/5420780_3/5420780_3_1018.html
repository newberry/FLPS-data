Rolf Rasmussen, owner of a shoe store, was born at Silkeborg on October 8, 1869. He came to America in 1891, and started his own business in 1896. He was Supreme Treasurer for the Danish Brotherhood in 1906, and was re-elected in 1910 and 1915. He represented the Brotherhood at the dedication of the Rebild National Park in 1912. He is director of the Dansk-Amerikansk Selskab (Danish-American Society). His address is 3155 North California Avenue.
