When the Chicago delegates came to the dedication of Rebild Park left for Denmark, they took along an American silk flag, which was hoisted during the festivities, and later presented to the Danish King.

According to a dispatch to American newspapers, the flag is now waving over the Royal Castle Marselisborg, and Mr. Magnus Holm, who was the custodian of the flag during the voyage, has received a letter from the King, expressing his appreciation of the gift and signed "Christian Rex."

It is strongly suspected that Mr. Holm now is on the waiting list as a candidate for the next Cross of the Knight.
