On June 23rd, the St. Hans' festival will be celebrated at Finstad's Place, North and California Avenues, for the benefit of the Danish Old People's Home.

(St. Hans' festival day is a very famous Danish day. At night you see bonfires on every hill in Denmark; the young people dance around the fire until far into the night. Everywhere you hear songs, music and laughter. This is the well-known Scandinavian midsummer night festival, celebrated on the longest day of the year.) J.E.
