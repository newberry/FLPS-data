During the years 1861-1870, 17,153 persons emigrated from Denmark to America; 1871-80, 31,771 persons; 1881-90, 88,132 persons;1891-1900, 50,231; 1901-05, 33,968 persons; 1904, 8,525; 1905, 8,970 and in 1906, 7,741 persons. This makes a total of 246,491 men, women, and children emigrated from Denmark to America since 1861.
