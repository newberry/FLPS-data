According to an ad, a society has been started with the name: "Den Slesvigske Understottelses Forening." (The Schleswig Mutual Benefit Society. )

According to Revyen, Feb. 13th, 1897, this society is one year old and has about 150 members among the Danes on the North West Side.

(When Schleswig, after the war of 1864, came under German administration, many Danes from Schleswig left for the United States rather than become German citizens.)
