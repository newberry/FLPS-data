Mrs. James Thorson, the founder of the Danish Old People's Home in Chicago, died March 23, and was buried March 25. Mrs. Thorson was the one who, in 1891, originated the idea of a home for the old Danish people. With a small flock of Danish women and the blessing of God, she realized her dream before her death, sixteen years later.

Mrs. Thorson will always be remembered as the mother of the Home.
