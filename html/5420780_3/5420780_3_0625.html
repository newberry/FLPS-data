The first golden wedding on the South Side among Danes, was celebrated June 13 by Mr. and Mrs. Tom Jorgensen, in Walhalla Hall. Three hundred guests were present. The banquet was arranged by the Society Valkyrien, of which Mrs. Jorgensen is a member.

Mr. Jorgensen and his wife came to Chicago from South Jutland in 1881 and for several years kept a boarding house for young Danes. They have five sons and three daughters, all of whom are married and living in Chicago.
