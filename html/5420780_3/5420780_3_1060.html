At the meeting of the Scandinavian Medical Society of Chicago last Thursday in the Swedish Glee Club, one of America's leading medical men, Professor Frank Billings, of the University of Chicago, gave a lecture on a technical subject, at the conclusion of which the medicos partook of a first class supper.

At the table Professor Billings in a deep-felt speech paid tribute to the memory of our countryman, the late Dr. Christian Fenger, who he said had modernized American medicine, and he further stated that what he himself is today, he owes to Dr. Fenger.

Other speakers were Dr. Svenning Dahl and Dr. Holmboe. Dr. Carl Nielsen presided.
