The following Danes lost their lives in the Eastland disaster:

Arnold M. Green, 1323 N. Artesian Avenue; Niels Pedersen; Adolph Locke, son of Christ Locke; Anna Clausen and her daughter Ella (Mr. Clausen had stayed home to care for their youngest child); Rose and Carrie Hasen, sisters; Harald Hansen, son of Theodor Hansen; Martha Fick.

Emil Meilstrup was saved. He was in the water for a long while. His watch had stopped at 7:30, which showed that that was the time the accident happened.
