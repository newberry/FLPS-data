The two Danish actors from Copenhagen, Kolling and Herskind, will give a play February 10, in Walhalla Hall on the South Side. They arrived in town February 1. Since their arrival they have been informed that the directors of Enna Conservatory of Music in Des Moines, Iowa, have made both of them Doctor of Music of that institution. Professor Emil Enna, director of the institute, is a cousin to the famous Danish composer Aug. Enna.
