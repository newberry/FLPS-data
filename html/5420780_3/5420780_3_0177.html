Dania was started on November 23, 1862, and is the oldest Danish society in Chicago. Its aim is entertainment and mutual aid in case of sickness and death. It has 225 members. It meets the first Saturday and third Wednesday in the month. It owns its own building, at 1651-1653 North Kedzie Avenue.

During 1914 and 1915 Dania is going to conduct a dancing school in order to attract the young people.
