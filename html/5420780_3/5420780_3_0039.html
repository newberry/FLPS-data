The general strike is still going on in Sweden. The Swedish Strike Relief Committee has received 939,993.53 kroner from German workers, 352,325.71 kroner from Danish workers, and 44,866.57 kroner from sympathizers in the United States.

From the Socialist headquarters in Chicago $5,054.63 had been sent by November 13. Of this sum the Scandinavians of Chicago contributed $2,657.01.
