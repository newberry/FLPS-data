A central organization for all Danish societies in Chicago is now a fact. The official name of the organization is The Central Committee of the United Danish Societies in Chicago. Niels Juul is president, Christ Norager is secretary, P. Kristensen is treasurer, L. O. Larsen is vice-president, and Mrs. Toft is financial secretary.

This organization represents about twenty-five or twenty-six Danish societies, with two or three delegates from each society.
