P. Nissen was born in Emmelef near Tonder, March 3, 1862. Due to German oppression he left for America when 17 years old. He was good at making all kinds of fine instruments. In 1892 he started Nissen's Business College on the corner of Milwaukee and Chicago Avenues. From 1892 to 1895 this college was attended by more than 2,000 students, Americans, Germans, and Scandinavians.
