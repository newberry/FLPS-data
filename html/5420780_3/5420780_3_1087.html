J. A. Riis from New York, a personal friend of President Roosevelt, was invited by Dania to attend a banquet in his honor. On this occasion he was also made honorary member of Dania. J. A. Riis is a Dane by birth and a correspondent for The Sun, New York. He is well-known for his work among the poor of that city.

(The Danes of Chicago were later to name a park after him.) J.E.
