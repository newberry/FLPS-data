Michael F. Blichfeldt, Lutheran minister, was born September 4, 1875, at Balling Parsonage near Skive, Denmark. He received the degree of Bachelor of Arts, at Aalborg Latinskole, 1893, and that of Bachelor of Divinity in 1899. He taught at Lyngby Realskole in 1900. He was ordained for the ministry in 1900. He was pastor at Sheffield, Illinois from 1900 to 1902, and served in other Danish churches until 1909. He was teacher in the public schools of Copenhagen, Denmark,from 1909 to 1911. He was assistant at the Newberry Library in 1911. He has been pastor of St. Ansgar's Danish Church, Chicago, Illinois, since May, 1912.
