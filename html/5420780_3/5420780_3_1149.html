John P. Hansen, a well known cigar manufacturer, was born March 22nd, 1843 in Tonder, Denmark. He came to America in 1864, and to Chicago in 1867. He opened his own factory in 1872. He is one of the organizers of the American Varnish Co. He was also a promoter of the Milwaukee Ave. State Bank and a member of the board of directors.

Mr. Hansen has been an honored member of the Knights of Pythias since 1873. In politics he is a Republican.
