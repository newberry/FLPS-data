The board of directors of the Danish-American Association, at a meeting on April 16, decided to invite Danish lecturers to speak in America.

Mr. F. Borgbjerg will be asked to come over in July for a two-month stay, during which he is to give thirty lectures on national economy.

Holger Begtrup, superintendent of the People's High School, will be asked to start a hundred-day lecture tour September 1. He will give fifty lectures on Danish schools.

The author Jeppe Aakjaer will be asked to begin a hundred-day lecture tour September 15. He will speak on Danish poetry.
