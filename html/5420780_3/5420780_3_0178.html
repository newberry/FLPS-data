Sons of Denmark celebrated its 20th anniversary last Sunday, with a banquet in Metropolitan Hall.

Mr. A. B. Larsen, the president, acted as toastmaster, and introduced Dr. Max Henius, honorary member of the "Sons," as the first speaker. In his speech, Dr. Henius paid a heartfelt tribute to the Society's accomplishments during the past twenty years.

Then followed speeches for America, for Denmark, and for the ladies. The dining room and the table were tastefully decorated, and the food and service excellent.

There was no hitch in the arrangement, and in observing their 20th anniversary, the Sons of Denmark lived up to their well-established traditions.
