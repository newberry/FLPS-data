The United Danish Church held its eighteenth annual meeting at Greenville, Michigan, June 4-9, 1914.

This denomination now has 21,722 members. There are 6,442 Sunday school pupils and 840 teachers. During the year 1,416 were baptized, 756 confirmed, 485 were buried, and 470 couples were married. The income during the year has been $28,685.46, and the expenses have been $24,884.57.
