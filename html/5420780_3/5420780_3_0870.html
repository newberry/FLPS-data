Mr. N. P. Jensen, owner of the Sunset Dairy Company, 1929 California Avenue, was born in Odder, Denmark, in 1882. In 1906 he came to America. He found work in the milk business, and opened his own dairy in 1910. In 1929 Mr. Jensen sold his business to the Bowman Dairy Company, and opened a new dairy on California Avenue.

Mr. Jensen's wife is a Dane. They have two daughters, fifteen and twenty-one years old. The younger is a student at the University of Chicago. The Jensens live in a beautiful home in Oak Park.

Mr. Jensen is a Mason.
