Next Sunday members of the Siloam Church will celebrate the dedication of a new pastoral residence and have arranged a fine programme for the occasion. The choir under the direction of M. Edward Arnesen will be assisted by solists Rev. Conrad Engelstad and Miss Marvyl Larson. Violinist Miss Ligne Elquist and pianist Miss Ester Arnesen will assist also.

The Building Committee has been eagerly at work and, as evident, their work has been crowned with success. Members of this committee are: Rev. J. Simonsen, chairman; E. Dick; H. Jensen; H. Theilgaard; Albert Nielsen; A. Hzldahl, treasurer, and Rudolph Ostergaard, secretary.
