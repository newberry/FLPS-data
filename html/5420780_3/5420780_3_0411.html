The Trinitatis Ladies' Society now has sixty-five members; during the year 1936 four of the members died.

Twenty-two meetings have been held. The bazaar of October 21 gave a profit of $420.43; the total income has been $820.07.

At the annual meeting, January 11, 1937, Mrs. Rebecca Andersen, 4535 North Bernard Street, was made president, and Mrs. A. V. Teisen, 5332 North Spaulding Avenue, secretary.

Mrs. Rasmussen Kuld, 1809 Fifth Avenue, Wausau, Wisconsin, was made honorary member of the Society.
