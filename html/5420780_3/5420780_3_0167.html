The Danish-Americans recently organized the Jacob A. Riis League. Its purpose is to promote the American patriotic ideal among the Danish-Americans. The first meeting of the league was held at the Atlantic Hotel. The local board will consist of Dr. Max Henius, president; Sophus Neble, vice-president; Carl Antonsen, secretary; Jens C. Hansen [Norwegian], president of the Security Bank of Chicago, treasurer; John C. Christensen, Henry L. Hertz, Captain William Hovgaard, United States Navy. The national office of the League will be in Chicago.
