The Danish-American author, candidate of Philosophy, Carl Hansen from Tyler, Minn., came to Chicago to read several stories in humorous Danish. He came to this country in 1888 and has ever since lived in Tyler where he first worked as a veterinarian, then as postmaster, town clerk and justice of peace. He has written several stories about Danish emigrants in the Danish language. See Revyen, Jan. 22, 1898, page 1.
