George Dupont-Hansen was born in Chicago March 16, 1871. His father, a hardware merchant, was a native of Denmark, and had come to Chicago in 1867.

While still very young, Mr. Dupont-Hansen went to Copenhagen, Denmark, to acquire a musical education. In his sixteenth year he became totally blind. In 1892 he returned to the United States, and became organist at the Danish Trinity Church, a position which he still occupies. He teaches piano, organ, and harmony. He has written many compositions, which have been sold in Europe and the United States.

In 1925 he was knighted by the Danish king. He was married to Miss Johanne Nielsen in 1909.
