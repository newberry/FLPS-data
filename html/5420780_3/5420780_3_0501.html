Den Forenede Danske Evangeliske Kirke (The United Danish Evangelical Church) held its convention in Blair, Nebraska.

Den Danske Lutherske Kirke (The Danish Lutheran Church) held its twentieth convention in Marinette, Wisconsin. The Reverend Eriksen and the Reverend Kirkeberg, M. Rasmussen, S. N. Nielsen, T. Christensen, and M. Sorensen were sent from Chicago.

[Translator's note: The first church group is very puritanical in its view on life. The last group represents the Grundtvig movement in Denmark, mostly known in America for its people's high schools.]
