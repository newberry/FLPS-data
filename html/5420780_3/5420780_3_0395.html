A central organization for all Danish societies in Chicago is now a fact. The official name of the organization is: The Central Committee of the United Danish Societies in Chicago. Niels Juul, President; Christ Norager, Secretary; P. Kristensen, Treasurer; L. O. Larsen, Vice-President, and Mrs. Toft, Financial Secretary.

This organization represents about twenty-five or twenty-six Danish societies with two or three delegates from each society.
