During the year 1935-36 the Trinitatis Danish Church has held 52 Danish and 49 English services, and the congregation has listened to 16 lectures.

Eighteen children have been baptized, 16 have been confirmed, 16 couples have been married, and there have been 34 funerals, of which 7 were of members of the church. Three hundred and fifty-six have participated in the Lord's Supper.

The congregation has 238 members. The church board is the same, and Mr. Claus A. Ohlrich, 2702 North Lamon Avenue, is the president.
