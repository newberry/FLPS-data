Henry Ockenholt, born in Denmark April 14, 1845. Arrived in Chicago in 1873, where he now is running a carpet cleaning establishment, at 952 Milwaukee Ave. Has been a member of the Society Dania for twenty years, and has held the office of president for seven years. He is also one of the founders of the Danish Aid and Relief Association. He is also an influential member of the Danish Lutheran Church. He was married to Bertha Wiese in Denmark in 1873. They have only one daughter, Ida, who has just graduated from Wells School. Mr. Ockenholt is a Democrat.
