Denmark Lodge No. 112, Knights of Pythias, has bought lots 114 and 115 in Block N, Mt. Olive Cemetery, or 830 square feet. The whole affair was arranged by the following committee: P. J. Noer, J. P. Olsen, A. Johnsen, Louis Andersen, John Rosenberg, and J. W. Hertz. The cost was $415.

This space is going to be used for the interment of members of the Lodge.
