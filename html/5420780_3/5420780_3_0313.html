The sixth Danish National Festival was celebrated on June 7, 1914. It gave a profit of $1,500-1,600. It was decided to give $600 to the Aid and Relief Fund, to the Danish Old Peoples Home $360, to the Danish Children's Home $250 and to "Dr. Fenger's Memorial Fund" $100.

Jos. Jensen is the President of the National Committee for 1915 and Mr. R. Egebergh, secretary. To this committee, 37 Danish societies in Chicago have sent a delegate each.
