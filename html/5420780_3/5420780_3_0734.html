Julius Andersen, chairman of the Danish National Committee in Chicago, received yesterday the following letter from the secretary of the King of Denmark: "The king has asked me to convey to you his hearty thanks for the beautiful plaque presented to him by Chicago Danes, and for the detailed report on the National festival celebrated by Danish societies in Chicago.

"His majesty is very happy to see the love and loyalty with which Danes from the homeland, preserved the memory of Denmark."
