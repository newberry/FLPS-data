Four Danish societies, Daneborg, West Pullman, Danish Brotherhood Lodges No. 61 of South Chicago and No. 107 of West Pullman, and the Hundred Men's Society of Grand Crossing, had a united banquet at Schlitz Hall, at the corner of 115th Street and Michigan Avenue. More than one hundred couples were present.

Mr. C. Slack was toastmaster. C. Petersen spoke for Denmark, Martin Andersen for America, Carl W. Larsen for the ladies, and Mr. W. Kikkebusch for cooperation between the different Danish societies in Chicago. Mr. Kikkebusch is the Supreme Vice-President of the Danish Brotherhood of America.
