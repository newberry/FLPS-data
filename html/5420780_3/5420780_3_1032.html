Christian Botker, Danish editor, was born at Gunslev, Falster, Denmark, March 4, 1866. He was graduated as a public school teacher from Blaagaards Seminarium at Copenhagen, in 1885. He wrote for Aftenbladet. He was assistant editor (Redaktionssekretaer) at Soro Amtstidende and Venstrebladet, Copenhagen, 1889-91. He worked at a terra cotta factory at Perth Amboy, 1891-92. He was editor of Chicago-Posten, 1892-1894, and has been publisher and editor of Revyen since 1895.
