"De Danske Vaabenbrodre" (The Danish War Veterans Society) started April 2, 1876. Aim: To keep alive the mutual memories from the years in which they were soldiers in the Danish or American army. Meets every 2nd and 4th Wednesday in Wabansia Hall, 1710 N. California Ave. July 1, 1913, one hundred twenty-five members were present. Cash on hand $500. The Library consists of six hundred volumes.
