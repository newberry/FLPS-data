Another old settler, James Thorsen, 72 years old, 836 North Irving Avenue, died November 4. He came to this country in 1844 and prospected for gold for some years. He returned to Denmark in 1853 and went into business in Copenhagen. In 1869 he again left for America and finally settled in Chicago. His wife is founder of the society for the erection of a Danish Old People's Home here in Chicago. He leaves one daughter and three sons, all of whom hold good positions.
