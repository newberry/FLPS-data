The Central Committee has arranged a big evening for the Danish actress, Mrs. Elizabeth Riis, on Aug. 15th. This will be an affair backed by all the Danish societies in Chicago. She will read several poems and a Danish play. There will be a concert and a dance afterwards. On the same page is found a good picture of Mrs. Riis.

In Revyen, Aug. 14th, we are told that Mrs. Riis has travelled 5,000 miles and appeared before large crowds of people her twenty evenings here in Chicago. Like last time she is staying with the Orckenholt family.
