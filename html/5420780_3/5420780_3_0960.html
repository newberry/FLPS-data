page 7 column 6-7. "Dr. Peter C. Clemensen came to Chicago in 1893 as a poor boy. He had practically no education when he arrived. For a time he worked in a minor capacity on the Chicago Daily News. He was very studious when a boy and his constant aim was to improve himself. Eventually he took up medicine and has now reached the pinnacle of success in his chosen profession."

"Dr. Clemensen was a member of the board of education for several years and also member of the park board. He built the Jackson Park hospital.
