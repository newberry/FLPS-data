Judge Gibbons has asked Bookkeeper L. Nielsen to audit the books of the West North Avenue Building and Loan Association to see how everything is since Mr. Rusthoy left town. The society had taken in $78,266.22 and had a surplus of $53,825. The shareholders had $73,238 in the business. It is estimated that Mr. Rusthoy has stolen between $5,000 and $10,000. The judge has made Mr. Stensland receiver of the Association.
