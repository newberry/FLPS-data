The Danish Church had its annual meeting in Ringsted, Iowa, from June 9 to June 14, 1914.

This denomination has 100 congregations with 78 churches and 56 parsonages, and 31 schools or meeting houses. The Church has 20,629 members, of whom 6,009 give regularly to the Church throughout the year.

There are 81 ladies' aid societies with 2,483 members, 52 young people's societies with 2,717 members, 59 Sunday schools with 216 teachers and 2,222 pupils.

Through the year 928 have been baptized, 431 confirmed, 388 couples have been married, and 341 buried.
