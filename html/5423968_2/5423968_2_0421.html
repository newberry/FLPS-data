Free courses, to be held every evening except Saturday and Sunday, are going to be offered in the Chicago public schools beginning Monday, October 18.

In Saint Adalbert Parish classes will be open to Poles at the Froebel Public School, 21st Street, between Robey Street and Hoyne Avenue. Classes are going to be conducted by Mr. Jezierny.

F. Milecki,

1003 W. 18th Street.
