The 'monk's stupefier,' Dziennik Chicagoski, informs us that Mr. Piotrowski, an attorney for the Polish Roman-Catholic Union, lives in a magnificent palace which has colored windows that cost $4,500. There are beautiful mirrors and $2,500 tables, also Oriental rugs that cost $1,250 each, etc.

Hm! We are not surprised at all that Mr. Piotrowski lives in a palace, that he buys luxurious furniture, rugs, etc. The more law-suits the Polish Roman Catholic Union has, the bigger the hole in the treasury of the "Pious Organization," and the more expensive shall be the furniture of Mr. Piotrowski.

That is the natural outcome.
