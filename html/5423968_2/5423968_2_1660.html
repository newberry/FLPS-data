The Businessmen's Association of Town of Lake has collected $450.25 in cash and $567 in food for the needy families of the Stockyards' strikers. The township of Hegewisch has donated $25 toward the cause.

Peace prevails throughout the packing house district. The strikers are keeping at a safe distance from the affected concerns and are patiently awaiting the dawn of their victory.

The strikers' committee is making an effort to get material support from the various Polish organizations and clubs.
