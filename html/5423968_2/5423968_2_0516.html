These schools of immorality should be closed forever. The saloonkeepers are bringing dishonour and disgrace to the Polish name as they are contributing to the delinquency of monors by selling them liquor.

They are also responsible for the brawls, fights and arrests by not refusing to sell alcholic drinks to all those that are nearly drunk. The majority of court cases are the result of drunkenness and the only people who are benefited are shyster lawyers.

What we people need is more emotional maturity to treat life more seriously: morality should be our objective: from that we are still far off.
