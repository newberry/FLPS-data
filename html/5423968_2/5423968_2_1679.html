At the final meeting of the year an election of officers was held last night, in the Butchers' Union (sic), Local 116. The following members were elected: Joseph Skopek, president; O. Parkus, vice -president; W. Cieslewicz, secretary pro tempore; Walter Bartkowiak, financial secretary and cashier. Among other things, it was decided that the strike in the Stockyards would be continued by all members to the very end.
