Sincere thanks are expressed to all priests, to all Societies of St. Stanislauz Kostka's, St. Hedwig's, St. Hyacinth's, Holy Trinity, and St. Michael the Archangel's parishes, and to all the guests who took part in the solemn ceremony of the blessing of the bells of St. John Cautius' parish on Sunday, April 11. May God bless the parents of those who acted as godmothers and godfathers of the bells. After the Easter holiday special services will be held as a means of expressing our thanks, first to God for blessing us with such a beautiful Sunday for this affair, second to the parents of the children who acted as godfathers and godmothers, and third, to all who took part in the event.

In order to anticipate inquries the following information is given. The largest bell, St. Anne, weighs 5785 pounds and cost $ 1157.20. The second bell, St. John the Raptist, weighs 2841 pounds and cost $578.20. The third bell, 2St. Hedwig, cost $357.80 and weighs 1889 pounds. Cost of all the mountings for all three bells amounted to $287.00, making the total cost of the bells $2370.20.
