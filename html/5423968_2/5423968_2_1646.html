The stockyards district is quiet at present. One can see from time to time policemen on duty to protect those who are working. Meetings are held as usual in Slowacki, Mickiewicz, and Pulaski Halls.

Donations for the poor families of the strikers may be sent by money order addressed to Mr. Bakowski or Mr. Bartkowiak, 1700 West 48th Street. Many of the stockyards strikers are in great need. Those of our countrymen who desire to help them are asked kindly to send their remittances to the above-mentioned persons at Slowacki Hall, 1700 West 48th Street.
