A new industrial organization, the Polish Blacksmiths', Locksmiths', and Machinists' Society, was established recently. The first meeting will be held Sunday, December 8, at Lechert's Hall. The founders of the society were Messrs. Wojna, Glowacki, Rudzinski, Olszewski, Szymanski, and others. Its purpose is mutual aid and work on the national front.
