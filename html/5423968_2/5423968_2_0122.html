Mr. J. H. Strauss, who for four years filled the position of school instructor with the St. Agnes Church in South Bend, Ind., resigned his duties there to accept a similar post with St. Stanislaw B. M. Church in Chicago, Ill.

Mr. Strauss, in accepting his position with St. Stanislaw B. M. Church, stated that with his regular duties there he would establish a sort of high school for older children. Mr. Strauss received thanks for his splendid services, from his former sponsors, and parents.
