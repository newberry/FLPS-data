The Holy Trinity Parish which was organized 6 years ago, is now conducting an adult evening school where the English language is being taught. The Holy Trinity parish committee is doing everything in its power to make this School a complete success, and is desirous of teaching the English language to every Pole.

The course, this year, will begin on October 2nd and will contine to April, 1912. Classes are held from 7 to 9 P.M. every Monday, Wednesday and Friday. Those interested can now register at the parish office until October 2nd, when registrations will be taken in the class room. All are welcome.
