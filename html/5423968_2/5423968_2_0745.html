Sunday, November 27, confirmation services were held at St. Mary Padgalene's Church. The services were officiated by Bishop McGavick. He was assisted by Fr. Kachdowski, who served as deacon, and Fr. S. Bubacz, who was the sub-deacon.

Some of the parishioners who were confirmed were: Michael Gertych, William Ruzek, Misses Antoinette Burdelak and S. Swiderska.

A number of local priests assisted in the services. The church was packed to its capacity.
