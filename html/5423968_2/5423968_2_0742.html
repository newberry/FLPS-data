The yearly Forty Hours Devotion services were held on November 29, 30, and December 1 at Holy Trinity Parish. During the entire services devout parishioners filled the church to capacity. A large number of school children set a fine example by their attendance, and hundreds of people went to confession and received the Blessed Sacrament.

All the sermons during the services were delivered by the renowned Rev. Dr. Thomas Hisicki of Williamsport, Pa. His brilliant command of Polish brought 2more listeners each time he spoke. He will be remembered for a long time to come, for he is a true son of Poland.

Among the many visiting priests were: Rev. Anthony Zubowicz, C.S.C., pastor of St. Hedwig's Parish in South Bend, Indiana; Rev. A. Kadziela, assistant at St. John de Kanty's Parish in Indiana Harbor, Indiana; Rev. F. Jagielski of East Chicago, Indiana; and many others.
