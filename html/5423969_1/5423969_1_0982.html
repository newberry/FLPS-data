Last night a group of Norwegians organized the Norwegian Al Smith League. The officers and members of the new organization are: Einar Garaff, president; George M. Kraemer, vice-president; Dr. S. H. Nannestad, Henry Melhoos, Hans Torgersen, Marius Kirkeby, Christian Mathisen, Ragnvald Grondahl, Emil Bjorn, Captian Jens A. Doe, and Otto Oie.
