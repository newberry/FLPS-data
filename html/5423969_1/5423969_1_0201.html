The fall semester of the Norwegian-Danish Seminary opened with a successful festival last Wednesday.

The new faculty includes O. L. Hansen (Dane), Reverend Frederick Ring, T. M. Hauge, Reverend H. P. Berg. The principal, Dr. N. E. Simonson, addressed the students.

It was reported that two new teachers have been added to the staff this year: Reverend C. W. Schevenius, from Tuley High School, and Herbert Hansen, who will teach English.

The Narrona Literary Society, a students' organization, was mentioned as one of the best of its kind among the Scandinavian colleges.
