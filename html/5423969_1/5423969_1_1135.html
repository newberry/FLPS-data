Several Scandinavians have been nominated to run in the November election. Following is a brief mention of the candidates:

Congressman Lundin (Swede) is the Republican candidate for re-election to Congress.

George Edmund Foss is also a candidate for re-election to Congress. He is the man who proposed the creation of postal savings banks. He has served in Congress for sixteen years.

John Kjellander is running for the office of Clerk of the Criminal Court; he is now City Sealer. He cleaned up the "short-weight" bakers.

Axel Chytraus (Swede) is running for Judge of the Superior Court.

The following are candidates for seats in the General Assembly: Charles S.Ryberg 2(Dane) and Fred G. Erickson (Swede), Twenty-first District; Niels Juul (Dane), Twenty-third District; Herman H. Breidt, Andrew O. Silveson, and Charles L. Fjeldstack, Twenty-fifth District.

The following are also candidates: Lewis Rinkor, County Judge; Carl R. Chindblom, Oscar R. Hillstrom, and Gustav Dreyer, County Commissioners.
