The Scandinavian Shoemakers' Union had a most pleasant social last Sunday afternoon at Nora Hall, on the occasion of the installation of new officers for the Union.

Several members of the Norwegian Glee Club were present and, after partaking of an excellent meal, gave a number of vocal numbers. Mr. Harry Dahl sang several comic numbers which were loudly applauded. Several speeches were made in honor of the Union and its president, and the most jovial atmosphere pervaded the social.
