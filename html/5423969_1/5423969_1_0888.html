It pays, at times, to be a Socialist. The Scandinavian Socialists of Chicago now have a summer colony at Fox Lake, where quite a number, mostly Norwegian and Danish, are enjoying the fine weather amid pleasant surroundings. The colony was made possible by the generosity of Dr. K. F. Sandberg, who gladly placed his spacious estate at the disposal of the Scandinavian Socialist group. It pays to be a member of some organization, even the Socialist party.
