Olaf Tveritmoe, prominent Norwegian-American labor leader, is in Chicago in the interest of the trade unions. Mr. Tveritmoe, a former Chicagoan, (now has his home in San Francisco) is on his way to Alantic City to attend the National Convention of the American Federation of Labor.

Mr. Tveritmoe was recently arrested in Los Angles and accused of complicity in the Mc Namara Bros., bombing affair there but the confessions of the brothers exonerated him.
