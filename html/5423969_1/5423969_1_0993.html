Last Thursday afternoon a group of representative Norwegian women organized a new political club, The Logan Women's Civic Club.

The Club will meet once a week in the Norwegian Club.

The following well-known women are on the board: Miss Nora Bjornstad, president; Mrs. Henry Stoltenberg, first vice-president; Mrs. Louis B. Bergersen, second vice-president. Mr. Alfred Michaelson has been invited to address the Club.
