p.8............Much intereat is being shown in all our Norwegian organizations, in the forthcoming appointments, by Mayor Carter H. Harrison, of new members of the school board. The Dovre Club presents Chas. O. Sethness a former member of the board, the Humboldt Women's Club presents Mrs. Cecelie Gerner. Mrs. Lillian Bendixen is also a candidate, basing her qualifications on seven years of service to Chicago as a teacher. Her father, Ingvald Olsen was our first Norwegian member of the board, (1872).
