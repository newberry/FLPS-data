The Twenty-eighth Ward Norwegian Republican Club's mass meeting at Wicker Park Hall was well attended by the Scandinavian voters. Judge Oscar M. Torrison, one of the Republican candidates, spoke at length on the issue: "Cleaning Out the City Hall".
