The Scandinavian Democratic league of Illinois held its regular meeting in the hall of the Svithiod Swedish Singing Society, Wrightwood Avenue near Clark Street. Henry Ericsson [Swede], the president of the League, presided. It was decided to support Carl W. Larsen and Joseph Anderson for County Commissioners, E. F. Anderson for Senator from the Eleventh Senatorial District, and John Waage for Senator from the Twenty-fifth Senatorial District. In addition to the candidates mentioned, the Harrison-Dunne slate will be supported.
