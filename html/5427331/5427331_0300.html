On June 8th, 1912, the first edition of the Chicago Slovak Daily was issued by Editor H. Thiele who does not know Slovak very well himself.

His assistant is Emil Mally who is a Chicago-born Slovak and also does not know the Slovak tongue well.

The Slovak language used is so poor that the paper is not fit to be called a daily but a nightly at which time a paper like that should be read. Upon reading the first issue there were numerous mistakes in the paper from page one to page five.
