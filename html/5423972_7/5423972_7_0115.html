The Board of Directors of the Home met last Monday night and effected a permanent organization by electing the following officers: President, Morris Rosenbaum; Vice-President, E. Frankenthal; Recording Secretary, Harry Hart; Treasurer, B. Lowenthal. A Financial Secretary will be elected later.

A charter has been granted to the organization, under the name of "The Home for Aged Jews," the incorporators being M. Rosenbaum, Nelson Morris, and Levy. A special committee is now engaged in drafting a constitution and by-laws, which will be submitted to a general meeting, as soon as completed. Contributions are still being sent in, although no organized effort is being made to collect them.
