The official opening of the dining room took place yesterday in the Home for the Aged, which was contributed by the famous Jewish philanthropist, Samuel Phillipson. The dining room cost Mr. Phillipson about $4,000.

The dedication was celebrated by a banquet, arranged by the directors of the institution, in honor of Mr. Phillipson and a large crowd consisting of community workers expressed their gratitude to the philanthropist through suitable speeches and a poem written to Mr.and Mrs. Samuel Phillipson.
