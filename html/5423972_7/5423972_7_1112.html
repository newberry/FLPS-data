All Jewish trade unionists are invited to come to the People's Relief Bazaar. Tonight is dedicated to the United Jewish Trades. The program is as follows:

1. Orchestral Music

2. Morris Siskind, chairman

3. Isabelle Yolkovsky, pianist

4. Frank Sherman, tenor

5. William Z. Foster, speaker

6. Baby Sylvan Cohen, song and dance

7. David Schoenfeld, a student of Alexander Zhnkovsky, will play the violin.
