All junk dealers are expected to attend an important mass meeting tonight at eight o'clock in the Douglas Park Auditorium in the interests of the Red Cross. A representative of the government will be present at the mass meeting so that a report may be given to the federal authorities on the work that the Jewish junk dealers are doing in behalf of the Red Cross, that is, in behalf of the government.

Judge Harry M. Fisher will be the main speaker, and he has an important message to deliver to the Jewish junk dealers. The other speakers will be Rabbi Saul Silber and Mr. B. Horwich. The Douglas Park Auditorium is located at the corner of Kedzie and Ogden Avenue.
