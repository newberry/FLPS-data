The City Council has designated Monday, September 11, as a general tag day for the associations of charity in Chicago. Alderman Anderson of the Thirty-third Ward is chairman of the Federated Charities Committee, which is in charge of the tag day. Mrs. I. Benjamin is one of the vice-presidents, Miss Ellen Babcock is secretary, and George B. Saxe is treasurer.

The following Jewish societies received permission to participate in the tag day:

Chicago Consumptive Aid Society,

Shmuel Aizik Bikur Cholem Society,

Douglas Park Day and Night Nursery,

Chicago Ladies Gmilas Chasodim Society,

Independent Sisters of Charity,

Sisters of Helping Hands Society,

2

Northwest Side Sisters of Charity,

Bnos Jacob South Side Nursery Society,

Humboldt Daughters of Israel Society,

Bread to the Poor Ladies Aid Society,

Chicago Ladies Aid Society.

Daughters of Zion Nursery and Infant Home lost its right to participate the tag day because it had a tag day of its own during June.

Very tagger will wear a card with an inscription: "Have A Heart".
