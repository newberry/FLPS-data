Paris. (Received in Chicago, June 27)--Justice Louis D. Brandeis, in the company of Mr. De Haas and other prominent English Zionists, arrived here today.

To my question: How did Zion fare in the Peace Conference, Mr. Brandeis answered that the Palestinian program was adopted in its entirety just as the Zionist Organization had expected.

Justice Brandeis, by the way, mentioned that Chicago has not fulfilled its duty to the Goulah (Redemption) Fund this year.

Here in Paris it is reported that about fifty thousand Jews lost their lives in the Ukraine.
