The "Miriam Club" at 482 East 42nd Street near Grand Boulevard has just enlarged its quarters by the acquisition of another flat. The club now has a membership of twenty eight girls and have just accepted six more making a total of thirty four. The girls of the "Miriam Club" pay only two and one half dollars per month for which they receive a home with all necessary conveniences. The house modern installations and has a choice location with very fine exposures. The street car takes you right there.

Aside from these conveniences, the club also manages to find employment for their own girls who lose their positions. Free medical care is extended to all members of the club. It is indeed worthwhile for every girl to go and see what this club has to offer.
