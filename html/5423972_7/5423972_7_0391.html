The Women of the Douglas Park Day Nursery will parade Today.

The 1,200 women members of the Douglas Park Day Nursery will gather at Ashland Boulevard and 12th St. today at 2 P. M. to participate in the procession of mourning for the victims of Polish pogroms. This decision had been taken at their last meeting. The Nursery urges the members of the nurseries in the Northwest Side and of Englewood to follow suit.
