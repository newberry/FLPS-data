The $250,000 drive for new subscriptions for the Jewish Charities of Chicago ended last night with a banquet in honor of the workers, at the Standard Club. The full sum had been raised.

Jacob M. Loeb announced that in a few weeks a drive will be launched to raise $2,500,000 for a nurses' home at the Michael Reese Hospital, a South Side hospital, and for a branch in the Lawndale district of the Peoples Institute. The workers promised to co-operate in order to make this coming drive a success.
