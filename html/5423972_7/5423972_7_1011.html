At the last session of the conference of the Joint Distribution Committee held in Chicago, it was decided to send five of the fourteen million dollars that are planned to be raised throughout America, to the Jewish sufferers in Soviet Russia.

The campaign will start about November 1st.

The People's Relief is urged to join this general campaign. Mr. David Brown of Detroit was elected Chairman of the Executive Committee of the campaign.

The Chicago Committee to raise the quota, determined for Chicago, consists of Julius Rosenwald, Honorable Chairman; General Abel Davis, Chairman; Charles Rubin, Vice Chairman; James H. Becker, Vice Chairman; Marcy E. Breger, Secretary; Julius Harris, Assistant Secretary; and M. E. Greenebaum, Treasurer.
