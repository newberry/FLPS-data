Purim Charity - Food and clothing campaign.

United Purim charity drive, March 20th to 27th, 1921, for food, clothing, soap, medicine and books, by the Peoples Relief Committee, for the Ukranian Society and for the Lithuanian-Lotvian Society. Cut out coupon and mail to campaign headquarters. This will inform us, 1. If you have a bundle. 2. If you wish to be a volunteer. 3. If you can serve with your car, truck or wagon for the week, March 20th to 27th, 1921.
