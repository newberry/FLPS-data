The main building:

The main-building, extending 190-ft. along Taylor Street, 60-ft. deep, with a wing running north 85x40 ft., all four stories in height, contains the People's Synagogue, branch reading room and delivery station of the Chicago Public Library, the Hebrew Library, Recital Hall, Offices of the Administration Club, and Class-rooms, trade school and commercial college class-rooms, Daily Hebrew and Sabbath School-rooms, Museum for Jewish Language, Art, Literature, and Customs.
