More than two hundred and fifty women of the Chicago Hadassah gave a luncheon and reception yesterday afternoon at the Blackstone Hotel in honor of the Zionist delegation, headed by Nahum Sokolow.

Mr. Sokolow, Miss Sokolow, Colonel Peterson, Judge Hugo Pam, Max Shulman and Miss Lillian Frankel addressed the gathering. Mrs. Bertha Reed was chairman. Judge Harry M. Fisher made an appeal for support of the work of the Hadassah which maintains the medical units in Palestine.
