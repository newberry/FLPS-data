The Peoples Relief carried through the resolution, of the last conference, to open a special department, which will devote its entire work to organizing relief activities in Indiana, Michigan, Wisconsin, Iowa, Missouri, and Nebraska, under the direction of the Peoples Relief Committee.

Mr. Shucter, who recently returned from Europe, where he was a representative of the Peoples Relief Committee, was appointed manager of this department (Western Department).

There is no doubt that Mr. Shucter's ability will prove very beneficial to the Relief.
