The Civic and Citizenship Departments of the Institute, under the supervision of competent attorneys, announces that ex-service men will be accommodated without any charge any evening of this week in filling our applications for the bonus.

Two notaries are always on hand to certify to any document without charge.

A sufficient supply of applications is always on hand to serve as many as may call.

The service of filling out first or second papers and general information as to citizenship is continued as usual.
