Krinik is waiting impatiently.

The Krinik representative is due to arrive in Krinik any day, from Chicago. He carries with him, for Krinik, Harada and vicinity, a sum of $30,000.

A cable received from Krinik stated that everyone is awaiting him impatiently. We also received an appeal for help, and a list of the 1,300 families being helped by the Krinik Council.

At the meeting this Sunday at 4 o'clock, at the Educational Alliance, Wood and Division St., the names of the 1,300 families will be read. Tickets will also be distributed for the Krinik and Harada benefit performance, at the Empire Theater, Tuesday May 18th.
