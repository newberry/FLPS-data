An organization council was established through the organizing delegates who gathered April 20th.

The aim of the council is to create close connections with the Hias in order to spread and strengthen its relief work and to assist in obtaining the necessary financial remedies that this institution needs to be able to carry on its local and universal activities.

Mr. H. Reznick was elected chairman of the council.

The delegates were greeted by Adolph Copeland, President of the Chicago Hias, and by dozens of other prominent Jews.

2

It was decided to call another meeting in the near future and to invite all Jewish Organizations of Chicago to send their delegates.
