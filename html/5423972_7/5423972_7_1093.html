Country men of Sudilkov, Shepetevka and vicinity. Attention.

Our delegate, Mr. Buchman is about to leave. A meeting is called today, Sunday at 2 o'clock sharp, at the Hebrew Institute Room 38.

Bring the money, affidavits, and letters, which you want to send to your relatives. Do not be late!

Your relatives are now more in need then ever - do not lose this opportunity to help them.

There will be a committee at Mr. Y. Lone's home, 1022 S. Oekley Blvd. Mon. Tues. Wed. Thurs, evening from 7 to 10 p.m. for the purpose of giving information and taking money and affidavits.

Do not forget that we have an opportunity to send clothes and shoes - rather \2clothes for the destitute.

Sincerely yours,

Sudilkov, Shepetevka Relief Society

S. Glassman, Sect'y

4105 Kenmore Ave.
