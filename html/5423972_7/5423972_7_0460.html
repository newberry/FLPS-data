The officer's of the Amalgamated General Board, Sidney Rissman and Charles Bauer, have forwarded $8,000 to the Mayor's Committee, which is raising money to help the flood victims of the South, as a contribution from the members of the organization.

The $8,000 was raised by the members in the shops through voluntary contribution, at the suggestion of the General Board.

The owners of the clothing factories assisted the Amalgamated in the collections. The workers themselves raised $8,000 in a short time.

The Joint Board of the Amalgamated, at a meeting of delegates from all locals, Thursday, had contributed $250 for the Ex-Patients Home in Denver, Colorado.
