Old Constantine Countrymen, Province of Velin and vicinity.

A mass meeting Sunday. Nov. the 28th, at 2:30 p.m. in the Workmen's Circle School 1224 South Albany Avenue.

At the last meeting we have elected brother Stahl as a delegate to tour Constantine, Kasmine, Krisilov, Kaltchin, Lubin, Esterpola and all the surrounding villages. He will take affidavits, letters and private money. The old Constantine Relief will be responsible for the money and everything else, which will be sent with our delegate.

It is the duty of every countryman of old Constantine and vicinity in whom beats a Jewish heart, to come and render aid in this important work.
