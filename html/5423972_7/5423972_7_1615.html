The Ashland district committee of the federation was organized Wednesday evening and will operate on the system employed by the Lawndale district. At present the committee consists of Rabbi Samuel S. Cohen, of the Zion Temple; Ben Cohen, Wm. Farber, S. Ginsberg, Max Karshack, Harry Marks, Benjamin B. Morris, Paul Rissman, Nathan Rosentweig, Max Schulman, Rabbi Saul Silver, Dr. I. Trise, Joseph Weil, and M. Zideman.

It was decided to call a meeting of the residents of the Ashland district next Wednesday for actual organization and work for the federation.
