The combined Rovner and Rajona Relief sent a half million marks by telegraph to begin the construction of cooperative stores and homes for children. Arrangements were made that a delegate should go to Rovna as soon as possible. All Rovner and Rajona friends are cordially invited to come on Sunday October the 31st, 5 o'clock in the evening.

To the National Socialist Institute to select a suitable delgate. Whoever promised a donation or dues please pay, for otherwise we will be compelled to send a committee to collect it. Arrangements for a ball which we will soon announce will also be made.
