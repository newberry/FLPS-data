BEQUESTS AND GIFTS. (Above $1,000). From Jews to Jewish and non-Jewish Institutions in the United States. August 25, 1900, to August 10, 1901. In Chicago.

Leon Mandel gives $25,000 to the University of Chicago.

The children of Jacob and Hannah Rosenberg give $25,000 to the Michael Reese Hospital of Chicago.

Abraham Slimmer, of Waverly Iowa, gives $20,000 to the Beth Moshav Zekeinim of Chicago.
