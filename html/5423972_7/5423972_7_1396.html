The hospital unit committee has called an annual meeting of all rabbis and presidents of all the synagogues of the city for tonight at 6 P.M., in the Hebrew Institute, Room 31. All the unfinished business of the previous meeting will be continued. Every president is requested to bring in the money from the "Sacred Vow" which was brought in on the Special Day of Prayer, also the pledges and the names of the contributors.

[Editor's note: Earlier article tells that Jews of Chicago answered the President of U. S.'s plea for assistance in war time by establishing a day of prayer and donations.]
