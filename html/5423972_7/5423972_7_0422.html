A committee of the Social Service, and the District Committee of the Workmen's Circle, gave yesterday the sum of $650, to five charitable institutions in Chicago.

The last convention of the Workmen's Circle gave the District Committee $500. for this purpose, and $150. was given by the Social Service Department of the branches of the Chicago Workmen's Circle.

The following institutions were the beneficiaries:

Mount Sinai Hospital, $150; Rest Haven, $150; Chicago Consumptive Aid, $100; and Douglas Park Day and Night Nursery, $100.
