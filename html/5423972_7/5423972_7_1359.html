To all the shop chairmen of the cloak shops: You are instructed by the Joint Board, to collect from all the workers, 10 per cent of their last week's wages, for the war sufferers in Europe.

Judges, Fisher and Horner, have made arrangements with the bosses to help in this work.

This applies to all workers without exceptions - cutters, trimmers, operators, pressers, baisters, and button sewers.

By order of the Joint Board Cloak-makers Union.

Local 5 - 18 - 81. Louis Gold, Secretary.
