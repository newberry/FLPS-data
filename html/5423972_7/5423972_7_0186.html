A Jewish inn was recently opened at 1431 Sawyer Avenue. The inn is the center of the young Chicago poets from the Young Chicago group, from the artists' group, and from the group of young radical Jews.

The inn is also a sort of community center. Mr. Rashkes, the leader of the Idneskom in Russia, gave a report there last Saturday night on his trip to Soviet Russia. The first semiannual conference of the Workers Culture Society will take place at the inn, this Sunday, January 13.
