Every Jew, is anxious, at present, to hear a true report from his old home, a report which will in detail describe the life, the suffering, and the hopes of the starving Jews in Poland, Lithuania, Ukrainia, and Roumania.

Sholem Asch is certainly able to fulfill this need. He, the great observer of life, has seen things that a simple onlooker would miss.

If you wish to hear this report.....You must secure tickets for the mass meeting given by the People's Relief, Saturday, July 19 in the Empire Theater.....

Sunday morning, July 20, Asch will address the People's Relief Conference to be held in the West Side Auditorium.
