Saturday September 15th, by the Russian members of The Industrial Workers of the World a lecture will be given. The subject: "Under What Conditions Could Socialism Be Realized". The lecturer is Comrade, D. Stranden, who recently arrived from Soviet Russia.

The lecture will be held, in the South Side Childrens' School (large hall) 1231 S. Morgan St. Beginning exactly at 7:30 P.M. Admission free. Come in masses.

The Committee.

(Ed. Note--No year shown, probably in the '20's),
