Miss Abbot who is at the head of the Department of Sociology in the University of Chicago is writing a book about the position of foreigners in Chicago.

Her assistant, Miss Zaborskaya, is studying the conditions prevailing among the Russians, and her research work will be incorporated into Miss Abbot's book.

In connection with her research work, Miss Zaborskaya has visited the South Side School for Children, one of the largest Russian schools in Chicago, and has found it to be an excellent school.
