H. T. Anderson will erect a three-story building at 2113-17 Birchwood Avenue at an estimated cost of $40,000. The drawings have been prepared by H. A. Anderson, architect.

A. W. Anderson has contracted for the brickwork on a three-story brick building which is to be erected at 2051 Farragut Avenue at an estimated cost of $40,000.

The firm of Hanson Brothers has contracted for the construction of an industrial plant which the Borden Farm Products Company will erect at 2825-37 North Washtenaw Avenue at an estimated cost of $141,000.
