The Chicago Lodges of The"Order of Vasa" celebrated their 30th anniversary Sunday, Oct. 31st, by a concert in the Oriental Consistory. Appearing on the program were Mme. Marie Sundelius-Zendt, celebrated soprano from the New York Metropolitan Opera Co., The Scandinavian String Quartet, and The Swedish Glee Club. The anniversary address was delivered by Harry A. Lund, former Grand Master of the Order.
