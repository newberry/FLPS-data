The Swedish Engineering Society of Chicago has just issued its yearbook for 1929 which, among other information, contains a complete membership list.

It has a total of 376 members, distributed as follows: 7 honorary members, 35 life members, 188 active members, 6 junior members, 39 passive members, 77 corresponding members in the United States and 24 in other countries.

The ladies' auxiliary consists of 40 members, with Mrs. E. J. Ericsson as president.
