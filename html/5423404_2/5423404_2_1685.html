The Kronoberg's Regiment Music Corps held its concert last Sunday at Orchestra Hall under the auspices of The Swedish National Society. The hall was filled to capacity. After two numbers had been rendered by the Harmony Singing Club and the Ladies' Quartet, Linnea, the "Kronobergarne" made their appearance on the platform, and were greeted with applause. The corps played several marches, Swedish folk melodies, pieces from Operas, and last the Kronobergarnes march, and the retreat of the Swedish Army. A telegram was sent to King Gustav during the concert.
