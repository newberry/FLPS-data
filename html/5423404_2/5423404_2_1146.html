Mr. Justinus Lysell, well-known travelling agent for Svenska Tribunen, has just been granted the general agency for Chicago and the West by the Conserve Manufactory of Sweden.

The company is to be congratulated on its selection, because a better representative than Mr. Lysell would undoubtedly be hard to find. Persons who wish to procure the company's choice conserves, may send their orders to Mr. Lysell in care of Svenska Tribunen.
