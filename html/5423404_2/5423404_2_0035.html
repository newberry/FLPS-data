Among the many Chicago Swedish-Americans appointed as members of the San Francisco relief committee by Mayor Dunne, we note the names of some of our most prominent Swedes: C. F. Erikson, L. A. Larson, A. J. Lofgren, P. O. Stensland, and H. A. Hangan.

Our well-known S. E. Erickson was appointed a member of the Illinois State committee by Governor Deneen.
