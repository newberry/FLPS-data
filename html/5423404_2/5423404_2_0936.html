The regular monthly meeting of the Swedish Typographists' Union No. 247, I.T.U., was held last Friday. It was resolved to arrange for a picnic at Leunberg's Farm, La Grange, on Sunday June 30.....

Chas E. Dahlberg, the retiring cashier was given a vote of thanks for long and faithful service,-----and in addition was presented with a medal in gold and $40.00 also in gold.-----After the business meeting, supper was served.
