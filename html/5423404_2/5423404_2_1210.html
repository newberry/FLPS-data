With great rejoicing we have received the news that our famous operatic mezzosoprano, Mme. Julia Claussen, will re-appear in Chicago at a concert to be given March 12th, at the Studebaker Theatre, under the auspices of the well-known concert impressario, F. Wight Neumann. As usual, her repertorie will include a group of Swedish songs, in addition to compositions by Brahms, Rachmaninoff, Stradella, and others.
