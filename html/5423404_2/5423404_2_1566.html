This choir, which is composed of 300 voices, has been organized with a view to participating in the 75th Anniversary Jubilee of the Chicago Swedish Baptist Churches, which will be held in conjunction with the General Baptist Conference in August. Mr. John E. Spann, who conducted the 50th Anniversary Jubilee Choir 25 years ago, is now the choir's honorary conductor.

Mr. Folke Anderson, the celebrated tenor from the Royal Swedish Opera in Stockholm, will be one of the soloists at the concert on June 7.
