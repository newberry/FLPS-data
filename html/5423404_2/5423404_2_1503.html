America's most prominent Swedish Comedian, is now commencing his 19th annual tour of appearances, assisted by a large cast.

Appearing in Chicago:- On the South Side: Carpenter's Hall, 6414 S. Halsted St., Sunday, March 15, 8 P.M. On the West Side: Maccabee Temple 5711 W. Chicago Ave., Wednesday, March 19, 8 P.M. On the North Side: Belmont Hall, Thursday and Friday, March 19-20, 8 P.M. Dancing after the program.
