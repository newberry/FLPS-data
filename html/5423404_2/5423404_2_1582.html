A Grand Concert for the benefit of the building fund of the "Swedish Baptists' Old Peoples' Home" will be given at Orchestra Hall, Oct. 19th. The "Swedish Choral Club," conducted by Edgar E. Nelson, will be the main attraction. Assistant soloists will be Mrs. Minnie Cedargran-Jernberg, violinist; Mrs. Jennie E. Peterson, soprano; and Harry T. Carlson, organist.
