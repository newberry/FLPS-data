This is the name of a comedy that will be given by the Swedish Dramatic Society at the Garrick Theatre on Sunday evening, October 24th.

The role of Uncle Brasig will be played by Ernest Lindblom. Other parts will be played by Prof. John R. Artengren, Ernest Behmer, Ida Anderson, Werner, Schcker, Rollins, Fernquist, Miss Larson, Sigris Lindberg, Signe Mortenson, Melen, Miss Milton, and other well known actors. Last summer the Swedish National Societies purchased the American rights to the play, and has since then obtained copyright from Washington, D. C.
