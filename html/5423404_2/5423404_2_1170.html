The Western Steel Protecting Glove Company, and the United States Watch Company, were incorporated last week under the laws of the State of Illinois. Both of them are Chicago concerns, the former being capitalized for $10,000, the latter for $50,000. The officers of the former organization are Emanuel Burkross, August W. Julin and Anders G. Hoggren. The incorporation documents of the latter concern were signed by A.E.W. Peterson, Arthur Lawrence and Wm. W. Anderson.
