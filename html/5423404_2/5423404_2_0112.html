Today, as we observe the wild greed for money, observe highly honored officials arrested for embezzlement, forgery, and conspiracy, observe Governors defending the worst crooks, attempting to justify their indefensible crimes; observe ministers accepting blood money and, without a blush, explaining that henceforth they will accept money from divers quarters without first inquiring how derived, then the thought forces itself upon us that Mammon is the God of our day and of our people, and Satan is the supreme lord of this world.
