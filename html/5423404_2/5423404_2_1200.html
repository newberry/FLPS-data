The Apollo Musical Club of Chicago has chosen as its new conductor, to succeed the veteran Harrison M. Wild, who is retiring, our countryman Edgar A. Nelson.

Mr. Nelson, who professionally is the president of the Bush Conservatory of Music, is already conducting three of the best known most talented choruses in Chicago and the Middle-West, namely: The Swedish Choral Club, the Chicago Sunday Evening Club Chorus and the Marshall Field & Co. Chorus.
