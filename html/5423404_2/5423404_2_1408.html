The fourteenth annual Swedish Artists' Exhibit is in progress at the Swedish Club, 1258 N. La Salle Street. It was formally opened last Saturday, April 10th, by Consul C. O. de Dardel. A musical program will be presented. Forty-seven artists are represented at this exhibit and the number of works shown including sculpture, total 129. A sum of $300 will be awarded in prizes among the several groups represented.
