Two new Swedish enterprises came into existence in Chicago during the past week, and both were incorporated under the laws of our State. One of them is the Excelsior Hardened Copper Company, which is capitalized for $500,000., and the officers of which are Peter Johnson, John Seaburg and Seth F. Collins. The other one is the Carlstedt Piano Company, the officers of which are A.B. Carlstedt and G. H. Carlstedt and K.L. Carlstedt.
