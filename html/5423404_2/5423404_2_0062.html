The Honorable Samuel E. Erickson, State Representative from the district that embraces the City of Chicago, was tendered a testimonial banquet last week in the Swedish Assembly Hall, 120 East Chicago Ave. Toastmaster was John A. Linn, who during the course of the evening called upon such notable guests as Sheriff Gilbert, Congressman Washburne, Judge Willits, and others, to express the sentiments of appreciation of Non. Erickson's successful work in the State Legislature at Springfield. As a token of such appreciation, Non. Erickson was presented with an expensive gold watch.
