Last Sunday Afternoon, the Nordic Singing Club gave its usual fall concert in Pythian Temple, assisted by Miss Minnie Cedargreen, violinist; Miss Doris Trafford, pianist; William Dahlen, tenor; Reinhold Hurtig, baritone. Every seat in the large hall was occupied. The audience was well pleased with the well-chosen program, the artists who took part in it, and with the songs by the club.
