The Swedes are the first foreign nationality group to use the new, but yet uncompleted, Civic Opera House for an independent affair of any sort. This is an assured fact by the Swedish Glee Club of Chicago and the American Daughters of Sweden now having definitely rented the opera house for a joint gala concert, to be held there on November 24.

As a coincident we may mention that a Swedish organization was also the first foreign language group to use the Auditorium Theatre after its completion in 1893.
