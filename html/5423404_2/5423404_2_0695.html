Architect G. E. Pearson has made the drawings for a two-story brick building which will be erected by R. Arends at 2053 North Avers Avenue at an estimated cost of $12,000.

Engineer Axel K. Sjolander will build a three-story brick house at 1430-32 Rascher Avenue, and J. A. Lundstrom & Company has contracted for the brick-work on the building, which is estimated to cost $35,000 when completed. E. Benson is the architect.

Architect F. Lindquist has delivered the drawings for a three-story brick building, which A. J. Cohen will build at 3035 Eastwood Avenue at an estimated cost of $18,000.
