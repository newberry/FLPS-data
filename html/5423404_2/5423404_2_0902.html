The contracting firm of Anderson & Windblad has won the contract for the brickwork on a warehouse building which is to be erected at 13520-42 South Park Avenue for the Black Products Company. The contract involves $182,000. A. G. Lund is the architect.

The Lindquist Construction Company has been awarded the contract for the brickwork and carpentry on a building to be erected for B. R. Ringwald at 8090-92 South Chicago Avenue. The contract calls for $20,000.
