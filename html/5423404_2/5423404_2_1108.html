The Patent Office, Washington, D. C., has recently issued patents to the following Scandinavians residing in Chicago: Frank T. Anderson: a lock; Victor Erickson: rotating steam engine; Peter O. Svensen: baton for police officers; Andrew D. Hultquist: book fastener, also loose-leaf binder; John K. Norstrom: automatic telephone system; Swan Nelson: piano mechanism; Andrew J. Sorenson: piano pedal guard; Alfred Stromberg: switch board.
