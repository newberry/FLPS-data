Mr. Albert Alberg, the prominent actor and author, will go to Sweden shortly to pass his remaining years there. His many friends here have decided to give a farewell play in his honor, as a recognition of his long activity among the Swedes here. The farewell performance will take place at the Grand Opera House, Sunday, April 16. On that occasion, the German dramatist Herman Suderman's great play "Honor," will be presented for the first time...
