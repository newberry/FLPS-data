At the Grand Lodge meeting Oct. 14, it was decided, on a motion made by the grand secretary, Nels J. Lindskog, to subscribe $10,000 for the Fourth Liberty Loan, and that this sum should be credited to the Swedish Division. The grand secretary has personally sold bonds for $6,900. The following subordinate lodges have subscribed as follows:

Society "Thor" $100.00
Lodge "Thelma" 50.00
" "Brage" 500.00
" "Svea" 100.00
" "Ivar" 100.00
" "Odin" 500.00
Lodge "Valhalla" $100.00
" "Trudvagn" 50.00
" "Thorbjorn" 50.00
" "Yngve" 50.00
" "Harold" 100.00

These amounts have all been duly credited to the Swedish Division.
