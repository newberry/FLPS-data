Mayor Carter H. Harrison has appointed Mr. Henry Ericsson, one of Chicago's most prominent building contractors, Building Commissioner in Chicago.

Ericsson was born in Sweden, where he obtained his education in the building line. He emigrated to America in 1881, and came to Chicago in 1883. He has built many skyscrapers here. Among them are: Manhattan, Harvester, Borland, Hunter, Pugh Terminal and Warehouse, which is the newest building in the city, and many others.
