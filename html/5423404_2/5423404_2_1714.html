The highly esteemed singers Joel Mossberg and Margaret Slattery will give a concert Sunday, May 7, at 4 P.M. at Kimball Hall, assisted by Nicoline Zedeler, violinist, and Mr. George Krebs. The well-selected program offers compositions from several of the most renowned composers of our time and contains no less than fourteen numbers. We are fully convinced that all of these will be given masterful interpretation.
