The Mendelssohn Conservatory of Music recently held its final musical feast with a concert at the Globe theatre. Eight female students of Swedish descent presented a concert program, consisting of compositions by Rubinstein, Mendelssohn, Massenet, Bizet, Schumann, Tschaikowsky, Verdi, and Liszt.

The degree of Master of Music was conferred upon three piano students, and the degree of Bachelor of Music upon five piano students.
