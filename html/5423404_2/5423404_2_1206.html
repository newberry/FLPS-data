The Uptown Conservatory of Music is the name of a new school of music, which will be formally dedicated and opened for public instruction, next Friday.

The organizer and managing director of the new enterprise is our countryman, Mr. Paul Hultman, the well-known pianist, and the staff of teachers includes such famous names as Mlle. Frances Ingram, the contralto from the Metropolitan Opera Co., Ted Fiorito, Dan Russo and others.
