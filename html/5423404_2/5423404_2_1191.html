A collection of paintings by Alfred Jansson, who is especially noted for his winter scenes, is now on exhibition at J. W. Young's art gallery in the Fine Arts Building, 410 South Michigan Avenue. It opened yesterday, and a reception for specially invited art patrons was held last night.

Jansson himself is visiting in Sweden at present, and the show was arranged by his wife and the owner of the art gallery. The exhibit includes fifteen canvases, and will close December 9th.
