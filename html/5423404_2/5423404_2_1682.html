A grand concert has been arranged by the Swedish Singers' Society of Chicago for December 13, at the Auditorium, This large choir, under the direction of Prof. Ortengren, is now holding rehearsals, as a number of new singers will take part at the coming event. Among the outstanding and talented performers will be Madam Norelli, and Prof. Hugo Hermann, violinist.

The proceeds will be used to help defray expenses of the trip the choir is to make to Sweden in 1910.
