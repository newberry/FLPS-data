The "Chicago Scandinavian String Quartet" which is composed of the following well-known virtuosi: Frederik Frederikson, 1st violin; Lorentz Hansen, 2nd violin; Ebba Sundstrom, viola, and Ninian Waerner, cello, has been engaged to play at the concert to be given Sunday, Humboldt Part Commanding Temple. The quartet has also been engaged to play at a concert Friday of next week, at Kimball Hall.
