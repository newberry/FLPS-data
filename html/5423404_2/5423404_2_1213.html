The first appearance in Chicago over a long period of our own celebrated opera singer, Mme. Marie Sundelius, will take place Sunday, Feb. 1st, at the Studebaker Theatre. In addition to a group of songs by well-known Swedish composers, the program will include such world-famous names as Haendel, Faccio, Benati, Debussy, Stravinsky, and others.

Madame Sundelius, upon concluding her 1923-24 engagement at the Royal Opera in Stockholm, was awarded the Literis et Artibus medal by King Gustavus IV. Immediately up her return to New York she was engaged by the Metropolitan Opera Company.
