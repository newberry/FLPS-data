During the last four years she has been taking the younger roles in the Swedish theatre and has become one of the public's favorites. In the near future she will leave Chicago to take up residence in Stockholm. Her last appearance will be as Agnes Brand, in John Jolin's masterful play, "Pay and Tears," to be given Sunday, Feb. 16th, when, probably she will say farewell to the Swedish theatre going public in Chicago. Joel Mossberg will play Olifant. Svithiod Singer's Club will be the student choir.
