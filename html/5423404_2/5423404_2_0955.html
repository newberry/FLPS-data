The well-known Swedish piano manufacturing firm, Newman Brothers Company, has opened a new display and sales room at 859 Belmont Avenue, managed by Mr. J. W. Ong. Located in the heart of Lake View, this store is easily reached by the many Swedes living in that part of the city, and who are planning to buy a piano.

Newman Brothers Company is one of the largest piano manufacturing concerns in Chicago, and its instruments are known all over the country for their beauty and excellent qualities, truly fine examples of Swedish workmanship. The name alone is a guaranty of quality, and prices are low because there are no middlemen.
