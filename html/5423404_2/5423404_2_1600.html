The Swedish Glee Club's winter concert given at the Swedish Club, last Sunday afternoon, under the direction of William Nordin, may be referred to as an outstanding success. Works by the following Swedish composers were given by the choir: Alfred Berg, C.A. Brolen, Ejnar Eklof, Felix Korling, Stenhammar and Ivar Widen. Soloists were Mlle. Ebba Frederickson, our celebrated violin virtuoso, and Leola Aikman, soprano.
