The Swedish Theatrical Society's first presentation for the season will be given at the Grand Opera House on Sunday, October 25. The drama to be presented is "Life in the Country," a play in five acts, written by Frans Hedberg.

The play has been imported direct from Sweden, where for years it has been seen again and again and has won great acclaim. It contains an excellent exposition of everyday life in the country districts, and the characters are drawn true to life.
