The Swedish-American Women's Club of Chicago gave further evidence of their loyalty when they held a Bunco Party at Belmont Hall to augment the amount already raised for an ambulance, which they intend to send to France. The fund will be futher augmented from the proceeds of a Military Ball, which will be held January 15. The Red Cross nurses who are in charge are: Kathleen Hill, Elsie Lindbloom, Elisa Belener, Edith Shogren, Margaret Nelson, Josephine Holmgren, and Anna Maja Bengtson.
