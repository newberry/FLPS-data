The Swedish National League gave its first major concert at the New Medinah Temple; 4,500 people attended. Mme. Julia Clausen, the opera star, assisted. The baritone, Gustaf Holmquist, also appeared on the program. The Swedish Singer's League, under the direction of Joel Mossberg, sang the old Swedish songs of the homeland. The Singer's League, consisting of two hundred voices, is the best Swedish chorus in America.
