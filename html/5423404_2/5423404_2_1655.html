The Nordic Singing Club held its annual meeting January 8, and elected the following officers: chairman: John Brunzell; vice-chairman: A. H. Hurtig; secretary: Fritz Holmer; treasurer: Emil Sommanson; librarian: Otto Sodergren. Delegates are: C. H. Bergeen, and J. A. Bromberg, who also are trustees together with Otto Sodergren. Wm. Dahlen was re-elected director.

The club will participate at the singers feast at Minneapolis, Minn. next summer.
