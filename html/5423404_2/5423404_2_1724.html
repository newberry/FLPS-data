For the benefit of the Mission Society's hospital in Bowmanville a song festival was held in the Auditorium on November 5. A. L. Nystrom was president of the committee; John Hogfeldt was secretary; C. O. Beroth was treasurer; A. L. Hvassman was elected director. As a result of the song festival, the committee has now turned over to the Mission Society's hospital the sum of $1,950, the total net proceeds from the festival.
