Consul John R. Lindgren has resigned his position as cashier from the State Bank of Chicago. In his place, the directors have appointed S. A. Henschen. For nineteen years, Mr. Henschen who is still a young man, worked in this bank as assistant cashier. He was born in this country of Swedish parents and was given a Swedish cultural background. He studied at Uppsala University in Sweden. Consul Lingren still retains his position as vice-president of the bank.
