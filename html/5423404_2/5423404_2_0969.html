The annual stockholders' meeting was held last Tuesday at 108 La Salle Street, and the report indicates that it is in a strong financial position. The Association has now been in existence for sixteen years, and holds first mortgages to the amount of $207,375, while invested capital and paid-up stock has reached the sum of $195,677.22, and the dividend account balances with $22,229.80. According to these figures, the organization is among the most secure and dependable of its kind in the city. It has enjoyed a steady growth right along, and both officers and members are well satisfied with its present status.
