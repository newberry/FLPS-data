This will be a great and historic evening

in the interest of the Gustavus II Adolohus Ter-Centennial Memorial

Tickets $.50 tax exempt.

Auspices of the Chicago Districts Lutheran Augustana Synod

Orchestra Hall

Friday, Feb. 20, 1925 at 8:15 P.M.

Three large choruses from the churches of the Chicago districts will sing a group of songs and join in a united grand chorus.

Chairman - Doctor Peter Peterson

Speaker - Doctor Julius Lincoln

Short Addresses by Dr. C.E. Hoffsten and Rev. Joshua Oden.
