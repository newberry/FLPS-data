Linnea, the Swedish-American ladies' quartette, gave a concert in Schiller Hall last Saturday evening, and it was a success in every way. The popular quartette consists of the following members: Mrs. Signe Mortensson, director, Miss Lovendahl, Miss Hakanson and Miss Jansson. The many songs presented were delivered with excellent style and revealed high musical understanding.

As far as we know, there is no Swedish ladies' quartette in Chicago except the Linnea...and we gladly welcome any public concert by this quartette.
