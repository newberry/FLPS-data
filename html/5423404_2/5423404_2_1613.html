The well-known Swedish violinist, Bruno Esbjorn, gave a concert yesterday at the Kimball Hall, Edgar A. Nelson was the accompanist.

The program was as follows:

1. Sonata No. 1 in E Minor.............Peteron-Berger.

2. Lento Allegro Molto Adagio,.............(Scherzando, Vico con Grazia) (First time in America)

3. Chaconne.............Bach.

4. Concerto No. 4 in D Minor.............Viextemps.

2

5. Capricco Nos. 23, 13, 14,.............Pagnini-Esbjorn.

6. Romance, Valse Capriccio.............Wienlawski.

7. Vaggsang, Mazurka.............Toraulin.
