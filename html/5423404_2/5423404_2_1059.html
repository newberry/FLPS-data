A new Scandinavian bank is to be opened shortly at the corner of Randolph and LaSalle Streets. A number of prominent Scandinavian business-men are said to have bought stock in the new bank. It is to be a State bank and the name is to be "Union Bank of Chicago."

Fourteen licenses for erection of new buildings have been issued to Scandinavians during the past week. List of those to whom licenses have been issued, together with description and cost of the buildings, given in the present issue of the Nyheter.
