On October 11 in Butler House met a large gathering of Swedish women to pledge their loyalty to the United States. Mrs. Kellogg Fairbanks of the Woman's National Defence Committee delivered and address. Other speakers were Mrs. Julia Haita, a Swedish woman who now lives in Belgium. Charles S. Peterson also addressed the gathering.

The theme of the meeting was that powerful support must be given to our Army and Navy for the successful prosecution of the war. The women decided to gather funds for the purchase of an ambulance, which was to be given to the American Red Cross.

Mrs. Shelia Mybsman was elected secretary, Mrs. Martha Hale cashier. Many committees were formed for the purpose of carrying on the solicitation for funds for this purpose.
