The great singing festival in Riverview Park held last Sunday was a great success. Ernest Lindblam directed with Emil Bjorn. A great number of Swedish songs were ably rendered. The Norwegian Ladie's Choir, Grieg, directed by Mrs. Husely sang one Swedish and one Norwegian number, which brought great applause.

Norwegian dances featured part of the program. Arrangements were made by Swedish Glee Club, Svithiod Club, North Star Singing Club, Iduna, Jephyr, Bellmanchair, Norwegian Glee Club, Norwegian Worker's Society, Danish Worker's Society and Danish Harmony.
