A Church Charity Concert, with Knut Ohrstrom as soloist, will be given by the "Fyrbaken" (The "Beacon Light") Aid Society in the First Swedish Baptist Church, corner of Addison street and Maple Square avenue, Wednesday, Oct. 14th, 8 P.M.

Mr. Ohrstrom, at this concert, will be assisted by Mrs. Ebba Sundstrom-Nylander, eminent violinist. Proceeds of the concert will go to the Widows and Orphans Fund.
