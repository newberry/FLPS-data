Carl E. Trodgard has been appointed a member of the Board of Health, at Springfield, by Governor Richard Yates. He served in this capacity but a short time, and has recently been requested by the Governor to take over the duties of Food Inspector in Illinois. The latter position is of a more lucrative nature, and is the position which Mr. Trodgard occupied during Governor Tanner's term of office. Mr. Trodgard is active in Swedish-American circles.
