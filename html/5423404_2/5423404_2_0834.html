Ivar Ericson, who for the last nine years has been the manager of the fashionable Fleischman Floral Co's store, Jackson Blvd. and Michigan Ave., has now severed his connections with this concern and opened his own business at 1714 W. North Avenue.

Mr. Ericson, prior to his coming to the United States, studied horticulture in France and Germany, and is often asked to lecture before horticultural societies.
