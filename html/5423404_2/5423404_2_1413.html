The landscape painter Martin Lundgren is currently represented at three art exhibits in this city. At the Palette and Chisel Club he is exhibiting "Willow Screen", number 30 in the catalogue. At the exhibit which has been arranged in connection with the Illinois Products Exposition, at 666 Lake Shore Drive, one finds his canvas "Willow Trees," catalogue number 92, and in the No Jury Exhibit, in the galleries of Marshall Field and Company, his "Winter Landscape" can be seen.

It will be remembered that Lundgren has for many years participated in the annual art exhibit in the Swedish Club.
