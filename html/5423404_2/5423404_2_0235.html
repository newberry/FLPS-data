Erick Johnson who has served in the army since the beginning of hostilities, is now visiting us. He has spent a great deal of time in England,partly for training purposes, and then, because of wounds. Before being wounded Johnson was at the front an entire year. He has been decorated several times for bravery. He was wounded once in the head, once in the left eye, and has lost the sight of it. Also wounded in both arms - a bullet is still lodged in his right arm.
