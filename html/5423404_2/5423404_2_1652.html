The Svithiod Singing Club held its annual meeting at the club's hall last Friday. The following officers were elected to serve for 1914: Chairman, John E. Ericson; vice-chairman, Eric B. Dauis; recording secretary, Herman Olson; financial secretary, Arvid Hagberg, and treasurer John P. Friedlund. Eric E. Hall and Carl Swanson were elected members of the Board. The former delegates to the Swedish National Society were re-elected.

The chorus sang under the direction of Professor Mossberg.
