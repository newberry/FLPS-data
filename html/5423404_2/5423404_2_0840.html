"The Dirigold Corporation", which is the United States subsidiary of the Swedish mother company, has opened offices and display rooms at 70 E. Jackson Boulevard. The public demand for the Dirigold products has necessitated this addition to the already existing sales organization in Minneapolis, Minn.

As previously explained in detail, Dirigold is the metal alloy discovered by our eminent countryman, Von Malborg. It possesses the dual features of having the appearance and beauty of gold and the hardness and flexibility of steel.
