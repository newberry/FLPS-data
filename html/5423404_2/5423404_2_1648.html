Extra meeting held, on Sunday, A.M., at the hall of the Svithiod Singing Club. The attendance was large, and all the questions taken up were discussed with an interest and enthusiasm which bodes well for the affairs of the society in the new year. It was decided that all the singers of the society meet at the same hall on the first Sunday in every month for practice and business meeting, while meetings of the delegates from the local units of the society meet as usual...After the meeting the members went, together to Rose Hill Cemetery...where flowers were placed at the grave of the singer, Olaf Nelson.
