The Swedish American Art Exhibit will be held in the Swedish Club during the next week - One hundred twenty-seven works of art will be exhibited; one hundred three of these are paintings and twenty-four, works of sculpture.

The famous painter, Arvid Nyholm, has several paintings. The Marine painter, Chas. E. Hallberg, has six pictures. The Nature painter, Alfred Jansson has a considerable display.

Birger Sandgen has several canvasses, too, so has his pupil, Oscar Bronsse Jacobson.

Charles S.Peterson, bought the first painting on the opening night. The general opinion was that the exhibit was much better than in former years.
