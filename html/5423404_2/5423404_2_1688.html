The concert at St. Ansgarius Church last Thursday night was, as expected, very successful and well attended. On the well chosen program appeared the following: Mr. Gustave Holmguist, bass soloist; Miss Maud Robberts, mezzosoprano, and Miss Dolly Randolph, violinist. Miss Carin Lindskog was at the piano.

Pastor Lindskoz mentioned the golden wedding jubilee the Swedish king and queen celebrated recently in Stockholm, Sweden.
