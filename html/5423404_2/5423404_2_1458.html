p.11.....The annual exhibit of Chicago Artists will be held, beginning next month, at the Art Institute.

Among the marine paintings is "A Summer Morning on Lake Michigan," by Chas. E. Hallberg; Alfred Jansen, the landscape painter has a collection of five paintings; Arvid Hyholm, a portrait of Le Roy Goddard; C. Schneidau, one of Frank G. Gustafson. Other portrait painters showing are: Edw. W. Carlson, Eugene M. Franzen, Carl R. Krofft and A.F.Fellander.

In the sculpture section we find three works by Frank G. Gustafsen, three by Agnes V. Fromen and three by A.F.Tellander.

Admission is free Wednesday, Saturday and Sunday afternoons.
