A Scandinavian concert will be held by the Chicago Grand Opera Company at the Auditorium Theatre, Sunday afternoon, January 12, when the following will appear on the program: Julia Claussen, Rudolph Ganz, Joel Mossberg, N. B. Emanuel director, the opera orchestra and a male chorus of one hundred voices from Svithiod, Nordstjernan, Harmoni, and Zephyr Singing Clubs.
