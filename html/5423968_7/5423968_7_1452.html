Contributions collected by Mr. Thomas Krolik and Mr. Jacob Mucha: Pulaski Mutual Fire Insurance Company, $100; Mr. Max Drzemala, $20; seven other contributions, ranging from fifty cents to one dollar, $6--total $126.
