On February 28 (Sunday) a benefit entertainment was staged for the Holy Family Orphanage at Schoenhoffen's Hall, Milwaukee and Ashland Avenues, by the Carol Chodkiewicz Society. The net proceeds from this affair, one hundred and twenty dollars, was turned over to the Orphanage on March 11 by the committee, Paul Nawrot, Peter Niemczewski, and Francis Hoffman. The Society has also promised continued support of this institution.

Sincere thanks are extended to this society for this generous contribution. May God bless the members a hundredfold.

Sisters of Notre Dame.
