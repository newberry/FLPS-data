The following individuals have given Christmas contributions for the destitute Polish children at the Holy Family Orphanage:

Reverend John Kasprzycki, pastor of St. John Cantius Parish $15.00
Ignatius Mikitynski 5.00
Mr. Burda 10.00
Joseph Marson 5.00

Mr. Bieszka gave a pail of caramels; Mr. Rys, a barrel of flour; and Mrs. Barbara Niemczewska, two hams. God bless all donors.

Sister Superior
