The following contributions have been received from the basket which was placed for public donations at Lazarowicz's Grocery Store, 283 Center Avenue, by the Holy Family Orphanage: 35 pounds of sugar, 4½ pounds of coffee, 2 pounds of rice, 1 pound of raisins, 1 pound of starch, ¼ pound of candy, 1 peck of apples, and 8 bars of soap.

Sincere thanks are expressed to Mr. Lazarowicz, who was the first grocer to deliver the contributions from his basket, and to all other donors.

Sisters of Notre Dame.
