The following letter has been submitted by Walter Pacholski for publication:

"On Sunday, April 23, the Polish National Catholic Society of Thaddeus Kosciusko was organized in St. Michael Archangel's Parish in South Chicago. The officers elected for the first year are Joseph Budziak, president; John Bolka, vice-president; W. Pacholski, secretary; Albert Stefanski, financial secretary; Anthony Szykowny, treasurer; and Joseph Golombieski and Casimir Jordan, auditors.

"Meetings of the society will be held on the first Sunday of each month at Retmanski's hall. The initiation fee for the present time is two dollars; after six months it will be raised to five dollars."
