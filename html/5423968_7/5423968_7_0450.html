The late Dorothy Swinkowska, a widow, bequeathed a hundred dollars in her last will and testament to the Polish Hospital. The money was consigned to the Reverend Father Lange, pastor of St. Josaphat's parish.

The Reverend Adolph Nowicki of St. Michael's parish has contributed ten dollars.

Anthony A. Scherman has given ten dollars to Mr. Mucha for the same cause.

Sincere thanks are extended to all donors.

Sisters of Nazareth
