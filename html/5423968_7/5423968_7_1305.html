Announcement of the activities of the Polish Young Ladies Charity Association of Chicago, which was just received, shows that this young Organization is engaged in relief work which is meeting with amazing results.

For a period of one year, from April 1, 1908 to April 1, 1909, $529.45 was distributed among 301 relief cases; of which $407.00 was used for the payment of rent, for home-less families, and to aid 23 widows and their children.
