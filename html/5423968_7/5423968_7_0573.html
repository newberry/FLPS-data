The Women's Society for the Benefit of the Resurrection Nursery sent the following report of last Sunday's (April 1) tag day for the benefit of the institutions conducted by the Resurrection Sisters, with the request that it be published.

"The counting of the contents of the boxes took place at the Sister's Home immediately after the tagging, and the sums collected were entered beside the name of each tagger.
