It is known to the public that the Brothers Youth Organization of St. Joseph and the Sisters Rosary Society of St. Stanislaus proposed after the Christmas holidays a bazaar to raise funds for the Polish Orphans' Home in Avondale.

After checking the books the figures show a gross income of $1117.85, the expenses amounted to $327.06, which left a sum of $790.79 for the orphans.

We are taking this opportunity to thank all the people for their splendid support given in behalf of the orphans. May God bless their kind hearts.

Rev. Father J. Ziemba C. R.

and the Committee.
