Donations for the poor Polish families of Nebraska continue to pour into the office of Peter Kiolbassa from all sides. The St. Vincent de Paulo Society of South Chicago has given $15.85. Joseph Nowak, secretary, 8939 Muskegon avenue, has turned the money over to Mr. Kiolbassa. [List of individual donors omitted in translation.]

It is reported that Mr. Kiolbassa has sent two hundred and sixty dollars to Nebraska today.
