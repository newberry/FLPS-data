The Catholic Order of Women Foresters, Lodge 54, of St. John Cantius Parish, has donated five dollars for the Polish Hospital. The Young Men's Brotherhood of the Holy Countenance has offered five dollars. Paul Dabrowski of St. Mary of Perpetual Help Parish has also contributed five dollars. Also, a donation of ten dollars has come from Reverend Stanislaus Nawrocki, pastor of the St. Mary of Perpetual Help Parish.

Sincere thanks are extended all donors.

Sisters of Nazareth
