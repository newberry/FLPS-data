The St. George Parish, a Lithuanian parish, has given four dollars for the Polish orphans at the Holy Family Orphanage. The money was turned in by the pastor, Reverend M. Krawczunas. Three dollars and twenty-five cents has also been contributed by the Women's Sodality, Rose 59, Branch 3, of the St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish.

Sincere thanks are extended all donors.

Sisters of Notre Dame
