A meeting was held yesterday (Sunday) afternoon at the parish hall by the Welfare Society of St. Hedwig Parish. John Krajecki, president of the organization, opened the activities, and in a few words explained the purpose of the gathering. He also explained how this welfare group should help the poor by raising funds, getting contributions, etc.

The Reverend John Piechowski, pastor of St. Hedwig Parish, spoke in favor of the Society. Stanislaus Glomski, John Konczynski, Michael Moszczynski, and others also spoke.
