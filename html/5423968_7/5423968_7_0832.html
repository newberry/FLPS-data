The Polish Social Club, which was organized not so long ago, has just opened its headquarters at 695 Milwaukee Avenue. Clubroom comfort may now be enjoyed by all members. There is a piano and a billiard table; also, select drinks are for sale.

The Club will be opened to members on Saturday, February 9, and daily thereafter from 6 P. M. to 1 A. M; Sundays from 3 P. M. to 1 A. M.

Julian Czupka, Manager
