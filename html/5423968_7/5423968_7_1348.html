The local clothing firm, The National Company, Ashland and Milwaukee Avenues, has contributed men's wearing apparel valued at $150 to the Polish Welfare Association of the Sixteenth Ward. The executive committee, composed of Peter Kiolbassa, president, and T. Gordon, secretary, expresses sincere thanks to this clothing company for its generous contribution.

According to the secretary the following donations have been received:

For January $42.25
Mrs. W. Barzynska 5.00
Mrs. Barbara Niemczewska 1.00
F. Bieszki 3.00
Thomas Krolik 2.00
W. Nowaczewski 5.00
Total $58.25

Further material and financial help should be sent to the Polish Welfare Association of the Sixteenth Ward, F. Bieszki, manager, 602 Noble Street.

The poor people needing assistance will be interviewed by the secretary, T. Gordon, at 636 Noble Street.
