The Catholic Order of Women Foresters, Lodge 54, of St. John Cantius Parish, has donated five dollars for the Holy Family Orphanage. Four dollars has been given by the Women's Sodality, Rose 7, Branch 4, of the same parish. The Women's Sodality, Rose 6, Branch 1, of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish, has contributed $1.80.

Sincere thanks are extended all donors.

Sisters of Notre Dame
