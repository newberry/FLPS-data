At its last meeting, the Executive Committee of the Polish National Alliance accepted a request of the Benevolent Society of Chicago and assigned $25 for that purpose from the current fund. In exchange, the society has sent in, in care of the Executive Committee, a certain number of order forms which, when presented by those in need, will entitle them to some help from that society.

These orders are in the hands of the Relief Department where also those in need should apply for help from that source.
