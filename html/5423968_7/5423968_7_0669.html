The St. Peter and Paul Society of St. Stanislaus Kostka Parish donated ten dollars to the Holy Family Orphanage.

The Women's Sodality of the same parish gave three dollars and seventy-five cents.

A two dollar contribution was received from J. Rejch of Bridgeport (a community of Chicago), organist of the St. Mary of Perpetual Help Parish.

Heartfelt thanks are extended to all generous contributors.

Sisters of Notre Dame.
