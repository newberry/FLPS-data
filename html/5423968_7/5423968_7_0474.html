The Polish Hospital is again in need of many things.

It needs a medicine cabinet but has no money to buy it. Lack of this cabinet causes a lot of trouble and inconvenience....

The medicine containers have to be placed on the floor, where bottles are broken, get dirty, etc.

Isn't there some kindhearted person who will supply this deficiency and help the good Sisters who devote their lives to the relief of the suffering of others?

We appeal to our generous-minded citizens: Who will donate such a medicine cabinet to the Sisters? Who will be so kindhearted?
