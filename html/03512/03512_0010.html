p. 2- The Rev. W. C. Roberts presented the report of the adjudicators on the last contest of the day before, and announced that the Rev. Ebenezer Edwards of Winersville, Pa., who contested under the nom de plume of William Penn, was the winner.

Then the baritone soloists sang against each other for all they were worth.

Joseph P. Burns of Wilkesbarre, Pa. was declared the champion baritone.
