P. 3 – Welsh newspaper, The Columbia, printed in Chicago, August, 1891- J. W. Jones, proprietor and M. A. Ellis, A. M. editor. Editorial and composing rooms at 315-317 Dearborn St. Paper, pages printed alternately in Welsh and English, devoted to general news, and social, religious, musical and literary happenings among the “Cymru”. Special correspondents maintained in all Welsh centers. Started three years earlier at Emporia, Kansas.
