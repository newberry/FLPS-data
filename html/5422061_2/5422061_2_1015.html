Most of the language groups of Chicago are arranging concerts for the benefit of the Joint Emergency Fund under the direction of the Chicago Daily News.

The Hungarian group will arrange a program in conjunction with the Ukrainian group on November 22, at 3 P.M. at the Apollo Theatre. The Hungarian committee members are Sandor Radanovits, Inatio Izsak, and Joseph Kaszab. Seats are priced: Box, $2.50; Orchestra, $2.50 and $1.50; Balcony, $1.50 and $1.00; Second Balcony, 75¢ and 50¢.
