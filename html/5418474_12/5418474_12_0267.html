One of the oldest and best known German settlers of Chicago, Philip Petrie, died yesterday afternoon at his home, 273 N. State Street at the age of 76 years.

The funeral will take place Wednesday at 2:00 P.M. from the St. Joseph Church. The deceased was born in 1814, Neunkirchen, (Germany), and came in 1839, with his wife to Chicago. He is leaving five sons, of whom one, Charles Petrie, is secretary of the City Fire Department.
