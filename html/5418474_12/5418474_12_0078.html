Professor Moritz Zeisler, the noted actor, has been engaged by the Chicago Conservatory as teacher of oratory and dramatic science. Professor Zeisler will give an introductory lecture at the Auditorium Tuesday night, and on this occasion he will give recitations of poems by Shakespeare, Schiller, Goethe, Scheffel, Leander, Petoesi, Baumbach, Trojan and Moskowsky.

Tickets are sold by Brentano, 204 Wabash Avenue.
