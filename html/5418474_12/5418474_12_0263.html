At Uhlich's Hall, the Hamburg Club, held its monthly, popular Ladies Evening, which is regularly given on every second Tuesday of the month. The former inhabitants of the Hansa City and their enjoyed the musical recitals and lively dances. A special dance event was the staging of a "Cotillons" with a distribution of medals and presents, which Mr. Gustav Wolff had arranged. Mr. E. Steinhoff and J. Neumann were the assistants on the committee of arrangements.
