John P. Altgeld, Judge of the Superior Court, handed in his resignation intending to withdraw to private life, stating that he is tired of public life and wishes to give his undivided attention to his private interests.

However, some who are acquainted with his affairs, are claiming, that Mr. Altgeld is quietly preparing himself to run at the next election as candidate for Governor on the Democratic ticket.

Judge Brentano will temporarly occupy Mr. Altgeld's position.
