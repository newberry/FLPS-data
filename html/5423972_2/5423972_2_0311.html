A committee consisting of the officers of the Jewish Shelter Home will accept applications and distribute tickets to all needy, Jewish families or single men for the Passover holiday. Tickets will also be distributed for the Seder [The religious ceremony and feast celebrated on the first and second nights of Passover] meals at the Shelter Home. Nobody will be admitted without tickets. The Shelter Home office will be open Sunday from 1 P.M. to 6 P.M. and on weekdays from 9 A.M. to 12 noon.
