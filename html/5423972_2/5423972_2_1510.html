Exactly what interests the Rothschilds have in an opposition hotel is not apparent, but it is said in Washington that if the action of Hilton is an "entering-wedge to the proscription of Jews in America," the great bankers will withdraw from the Syndicate and oppose American credit in Europe.

Seligman is in an unenviable position. Hilton kicked him out the door, and his friends are jamming him down the chimney.
