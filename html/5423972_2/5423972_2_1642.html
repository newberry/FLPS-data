The jubilee ceremony of Roosevelt Road - the new name for Twelfth Street, will be both artistic and impressive; no such sight has ever been presented on the West Side.

Wednesday, July 2, at 6:00 P.M.

Roosevelt Road will be beautifully decorated and lighted with colored lights, parade of floats, beautiful girls will dance to the rhythmn of jazz bands.

Music and Fireworks.

The parade will begin at 7:00 P.M. at Albany & 12th St., west to Crawford, North to Madison, east to Ashland, south to 12th St., west to Independence Blvd.

(Adv.)
