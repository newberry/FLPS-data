Morris Joseph, president of Congregation Bnai Zion and one of the richest Jews in Chicago, gave a banquet in his home Wednesday evening in honor of the delegates to the Roman Catholic Congress which is being held in Chicago. The purpose of this banquet was to foster better relations between Jews and Roman Catholics. Rabbi Abram Hirschberg of Temple Sholom spoke on this subject.

Among the prominent Jews present at the banquet were Mr. and Mrs. Julius Rosenwald, Judge Henry Horner, Judge Hugo Pam, Judge [Joseph] David, Michael Rosenberg, and Alderman [Jacob] Arvey.
