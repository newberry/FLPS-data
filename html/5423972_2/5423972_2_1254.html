Julius Rosenwald has offered a donation of $25,000 for a Y.M.C.A. building for Negroes, on condition that $75,000 more is raised by popular subscription. This same offer he extended to every other city in America. The time-limit for raising the $75,000 is set by Mr. Rosenwald at five years.

At the same time, Mr. N. W. Harris, head of the Harris Trust and Savings Bank, offered $25,000 for a Chicago building, provided $125,000 is raised within six months.
