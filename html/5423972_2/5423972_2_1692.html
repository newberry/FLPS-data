Local 12 of the Novelty Leather Workers Union has celebrated a great victory.....

The gains are: a 44 hour week, wages to be paid on a weekly scale rather then on piece work basis, a raise for one class of workers from $23 to $35 per week, and a raise for a second class from $21 to $32 per week.

These conditions have been won for 98% of the trade, only two shops remaining outside the agreement: The Columbia Legging Co. and the Western Legging Co. Strikes will be called in these two shops.....

Mr. I.Gordon is the President, Sam Polensky is the business agent of Local 12.....
