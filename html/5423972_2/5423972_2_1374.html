An informal discussion was then had upon the suggestion of Benswanger, that the Public Worship Committee take into consideration the propriety and expediency of eliminating from our services on holidays, the reading of lengthy prayers in Hebrew, with a view of being thoroughly consistent in our practices, having abolished the teachings of Hebrew in the Sabbath School and the reading of the Bible in Hebrew and bringing our services in harmony and in consonance with the spirit and tendencies of the age.
