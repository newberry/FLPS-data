The opening of the new building of the Liberty Trust and Savings Bank, was held last Saturday, Nov. 6th.

The Liberty Trust, the oldest and largest state bank in the Douglas Park neighborhood is one of the largest, strongest, and most successful banks in the city.

The resources of the bank are almost $7,000,000,the increase in deposits in the last year was over $2,000,000.

The officers of the bank are, Walter Hyman, pres., Adolph S. Hillquist, vice-pres., Jacob London, cashier, etc.
