The Cap Makers Union has voted to strike, beginning Monday July 26th. The issue is piece work versus weekly wage. The following are the shops affected: Alper Witz, Atlas, Fried, Eagle, Feldstein and Tessler, Gourd and Brothers,Hobbing, Hirsch and Weingrad, Marvel, Rabener Brothers, Rex, Rubenstein and Gordon, Siegal Brothers, Tessler and Son, Fishman's, Levenson Brothers, Meyerowitz, Parrot and Mercantile, S. B. Pollack, R. Label, Supreme, and United.

In 14 shops, where weekly work is maintained, there will be no strike. These shops do not belong to the Association.
