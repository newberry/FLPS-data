The Sears-Roebuck Y. M. C. A. has invited Rabbi Leon Fram of Temple Judea to conduct a Bible class composed of Christians and Jews.

This is the first time in Chicago and perhaps in all America, that a rabbi has been asked to hold a Bible class under the auspices of the Y. M. C. A. It is an evidence of broad-mindedness in which Gentile and Jew might well rejoice.

Rabbi Fram received the Rabbinical degree in 1920 and has been the spiritual leader of Temple Judea since his graduation from the Hebrew Union College.
