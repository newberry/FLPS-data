Dress Makers Celebrate Great Victory; Largest Concerns Sign Agreement with the Union.

The dressmakers of Chicago celebrated a great victory yesterday. At 10 A.M., they walked out on strike and at noon most of them returned to work triumphantly.

The Bosses Association undersigned the agreement before several workers had time to leave their work.

The workers returned to work under Union conditions...44 hours per week and union scale and other conditions.

The only workers on strike now are from shops that were until now unorganized.

The union will carry on their fight until they win.
