The first mass meeting in the campaign of the 44 hour week, will be held tonight at the Amalgamated Hall, 1579 Milwaukee Avenue.

Well known speakers, in Jewish, English and Polish will speak on the opportunities that the waist-makers, dress, kimono, white goods workers, cutters and pressers have now, to win the 44 hour working week.

All the workers of these trades should come in masses to join the union and help these demands materialize sooner. Most of the branches in this organization have already won the 44 hour week.
