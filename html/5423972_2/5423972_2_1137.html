Last night, the Valley gang again set forth on its wild escapades in the Jewish quarter. These drunken hoodlums, riding in an automobile, stop wherever they see a Jew, and give their unfortunate victims a brutal beating.

Yesterday about 2 P. M., one of the bandits jumped from the auto at 13th Street near No. 1516, and assualted Mr. Louis Switzky, 40, inflicting a cruel beating on him. His screams attracted a large crowd of people, but the bandit leaped into the car and escaped.

Mr. Switzky received several wounds on the head, and was taken to the County Hospital. The police have not caught any members of the gang.
