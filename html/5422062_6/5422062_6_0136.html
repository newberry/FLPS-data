p. 1.- The holiday which was arranged in honor of the Ahepa and Gapa visitors to Greece was a most successful and enthusiastic event. Thousands thronged to the stadium at Athens where the officials and the leaders of society in Athens were present. Mr. Venizelos made a great speech welcoming the visitors.

The entire program was carried through without a halt. The thoroughness and perfectness surprised the visitors. In the evening a banquet was given by the American Greeks.
