Out of nineteen beautiful girls who took part in the 1930 beauty contest in Paris, the Grecian girl, Miss Alice Diplarakou, was chosen as beauty queen to wear the crown of "Miss Europe" for 1930.

The newly crowned beauty will sail for Rio De Janeiro to compete for the "Miss Universe" title. She is eighteen years old with chestnut hair, classical appearance and the figure of Artemis (Diana). She loves sports, dislikes cosmetics, and is the daughter of a wealthy lawyer.
