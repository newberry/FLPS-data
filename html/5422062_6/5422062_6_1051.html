Another regional society has been added to the already lengthy roster in Chicago. This time it has been formed by people from the town of Kakouria in the State of Mantinea. Although there are only a few people here from this little town, the smallness of their numbers did not dampen their patriotic ardor to perform some beneficial service for their home town.
