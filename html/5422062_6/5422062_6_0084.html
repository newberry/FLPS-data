For over a month various Greek organizations have tried to bring Miss Europe (Aliki Diplarakou) to Chicago. Of all these invitations one had to be accepted and the lucky lot fell to the Women's Club of St. Andres.

Miss Diplarakou telegraphed that she would be present at their dance on Dec. 10. One she is brought to Chicago, Miss Europe will not find it easy to leave, because 65,000 Greeks are eagerly waiting to please her at the Aragon on Dec. 10.
