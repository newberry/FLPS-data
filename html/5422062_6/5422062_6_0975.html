John Mangel (Venizelos), was born at Afison, Sparta, Greece and come to the United States in 1884. He is the "Grand Old Man" of the Hellenic Community of Chicago and has always been ready to help those of our people who were in need. Being that he operates a string of Flower Stores in the heart of downtown Chicago, he never fails to donate every Easter all the necessary flowers that the Greek Churches of Chicago need. He is a member of Chicago Chapter No. 46 and an ardent supporter of Ahepan principles.
