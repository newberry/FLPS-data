p. 5.- Tomorrow, at the Palmer House, State and Monroe, the Greek Professional Club is giving a banquet of welcome for the Greek Ambassador, Harry Simopoulos, and of celebration for Greece's century of freedom. His Holiness, Damaskinos Korinthias, will accompany the ambassador.

Aside from these distinguished guests, our beloved Jane Addams had consented to address the audience.

Although all places are reserved, those wishing to be present can call the secretary and make arrangements to be there.
