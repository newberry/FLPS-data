Every once in a while we get a chance to tell of the work being done in Greece by various Chicago organizations.

One such undertaking a school built in Kerasitsas, Tegeas. The Kerasitsan Organization of Chicago contributed the funds for this enterprise. This club has been in existence since February 26,1922 and has done much good for its country.

This progress is not to be stopped. The Brotherhood is continually making new plans. It is to be congratulated on such splendid work.

The Board of Directors are: C. Malliris, president; J. Tzathas, secretary; S. Tzathas, treasurer. P. Lambrakis, A. Pikras, N. Spiropoulos, and F. Malliris are the trustees of the Brotherhood.
