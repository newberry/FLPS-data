Mr. Pavlos Koutsiouris and his wife, Helen, daughter of Nicholas Palivos have just returned from Greece where they remained all fall. They visited Mr. Koutsiouris' home-town, Rizas, Tegeas and Baltetsia, Mrs. Koutsiouris' fathers's home and from there to Larrisan, Mrs. Koutsiouris' mother's home. They returned by way of Europe on the Majestic. Welcome!
