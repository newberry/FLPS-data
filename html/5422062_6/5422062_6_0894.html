p. 5- A letter of appreciation from the Panhellenic Association for the Prevention of Tuberculosis, of Athens, Greece, has been received by the Panhellenic Society of Chicago for its generous contribution of $200.

This sum was sent by the Chicago organization to the Metropolitan of Athens for this Greek institution which combats the dreaded disease of tuberculosis.

The letter reads as follows:

Mr. President:

Kindly accept the deep gratitude of the Panhellenic Association for the Prevention of Tuberculosis for your philanthropic and generous contribution of forty pounds sterling, which was handed to us by His Eminence the Metropolitan of Athens, Greece.

N. Hadjemichalis, President.

B. Patrikios, Secretary.
