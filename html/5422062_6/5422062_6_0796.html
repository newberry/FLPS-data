The Greek community of Chicago offers its hospitality to the representative of His Majesty's Government, Lysimachos Kaftazoglou, who is in Chicago to observe and study commercial methods for Greek imports.

The distinguished visitor who was enthusiastically received by Mayor Harrison and the Civic Authorities, will remain here four or five days, at the Auditorium Annex, Suite 1748. Mr. Kaftazoglou will receive every Greek who wishes to see him from 10 A. M. to 1:30 P. M.
