The committee of the Greek Liberals of Chicago, composed of 125 prominent Greeks, appeals to all patriotic Greeks who love Mother-Greece and Hellenism to take part in the forthcoming general rally to express our confidence in Eleutherios Venzelos for leading Greece to war on the side of the Allies.

This general meeting will take place on May 13, and all who are in favor of the above-mentioned cause are requested to come and bring their friends with them.
