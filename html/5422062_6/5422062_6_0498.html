By special cablegram from the Greek foreign office to the Washington Greek consulate Mr. Vasilios Mammonas, who was recently appointed Greek consul general in Chicago, has been promoted and transferred to Washington, D. C., to be supervisor of all Greek consulates in America.

Our sincere congratulations and best wishes for his success and for his continued advancement!

It is said that Mr. Mammonas will remain here for a few days to make a few changes in the consulate and to see that it is operating according to regulation requirements.
