The president of the Greek Restaurant Association received a telegram of thanks from E. Venizelos upon sending him a congratulatory telegram on his victory at the elections in Greece.

The telegram read as follows:

John Kokkalis

Pres. of the Greek Restaurant Ass'n.

Chicago.

With deepest thanks.

E. Venizelos.

A similar telegram was sent to J. Flores, President of the Confectioners Association.
