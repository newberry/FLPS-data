In response to a telegram sent to Venizelos by 12,000 Chicago Greek Liberals assuring him and his National Government of support, Mr. Venizelos answered as follows:

"Liberals of Chicago:

Accept my deep appreciation and thanks for your telegram to me. Your patriotic sentiments gave tremendous moral support to the endeavors of us Greeks, united under the temporary Government, who are striving to restore our national honor and prestige and fulfill Greece's obligation to our protectors, the Allies."

Venizelos.
