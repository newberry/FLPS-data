Dear Mr. Lambros:

It was a great pleasure to meet you and your charming wife last evening. Mother and I hope that we shall have an opportunity to see you again before we leave.

Thank you so much for sending me your kind letter of greeting and the clippings from the Greek Star.

It was very nice of you to say all the kind things that you said about me, and I want you to know that I greatly appreciate your thoughtfulness.

Sincerely yours,

Aliki Diplarakou, "Miss Europe."
