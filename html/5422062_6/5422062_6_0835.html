The Athletic club is giving an affair on Saturday night, August 21, at Hull House, in honor of the Patriarchate of Constantinople. An interesting program has been arranged. Admission is 50c, 75c and $1.50. Among those on the program will be A. Mandilaris, B. Benetsanakis, and P. Petropoulos. The Graeco-Turkish situation will also be discussed. Everyone is invited to attend.
