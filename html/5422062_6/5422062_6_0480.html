p. 5-On January 21, 1923, the Selasia Association, through the good offices of the executive committee and patriotic members, gave a dance at the White City Auditorium. A large crowd attended the dance, and the net proceeds were $620 after expenses had been deducted. This sum was sent to Athens for the benefit of the refugees.

Our hearty congratulations to our good friends, who are always willing to help a worthy cause!
