The Greeks of Chicago will hear, with joy, that the Athenian Bank, the second such credit establishment in Greece, has opened offices in Chicago, where the Greek people of Chicago and other Mid-western cities will be able to conduct all their commerical dealings with Greece, sending money home, and depositing savings.

The Chicago branch of the Athenian Bank is managed by Mr. Sotiris Demopoulos, in whom his countrymen can be certain to find faithful service concerning any transaction they wish to make.
