p. 2.- The highest Greek representatives of Hellenism in America, Lambros Koromilas, minister of Greece to Washington, has finally arrived in our fair city. We are proud and happy to have such a distinguished man in our midst.

Mr. Koromilas is held in high esteem by all the Greeks in the world for the part he played in the Macedonian problem. His visit to America holds a great significance for us. It shows that Hellenism has progressed to such an extent that it is considered necessary to send a minister as representative of our country in Washington. Mr. Koromilas' visit will bring together the Greeks of Chicago into such a strong bond as will not be easily broken by outside influences.
