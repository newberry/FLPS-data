With the many brilliant outings our organizations have been giving, we must also include the first picnic of the Psaraion Organization of America. According to the number of people present we would say that financially the affair was a huge success.

The Psaraion Organization has sent 500,000 Drachmas to Psari, a town of Messinia for improvements and for building a school. This is one organization that accomplishes in a big way that which it sets out to do.
