p. 1. The Greek Information Bureau, of Washington D.C., informed us that the Government of Greece appointed two eminent committees to study the French and American Constitutions.

High officials of Pangalos government are very much in favor of adopting the American Constitution. The current of Greek-Americanism will prevail. The hundreds of thousands of Greek-Americans visiting Greece have been instrumental in pointing out the benefits and advantages that can be enjoyed under an American form of Government.

Mr. Pangalos is determined to restore constitutional harmony out of the chaos.

Many governmental officials have been dismissed for having Communistic proclevities.
