Patris, a Greek daily published in Pythos, Elis, Greece, authorized Mr. Tom Asimacopolous, 107 South Wells Street to solicit subscribers here.

For the annual subscription of $7, the publication offers, besides the delivery of the daily, any service that could be extended to its subscribers in the United States.

Those who wish to get this daily, should apply either directly to Greece or tp Tom Asimacopoulos, 107 South Wells Street, Chicago.
