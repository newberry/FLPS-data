Up to the present time the American Lithuanians have donated many photographs to the Lithuanian Science Society's Museum, which is to be built in Vilnus. Reverend Serafinas, Reverend A. Skrypkus, Reverend K. Ambrozaitis and photographer Stankunas--all Chicagoans; Reverend K. Skrypkus and photographer S. Mazrimas, from Westville, Ill.; Reverend Petravicius, East St. Louis, Ill.; Reverend Augulis, Dorrisville, Ill.; Reverend Gadeikis, Kenosha, Wis.; Reverend Krusas, Waukegan, Ill.; Reverend J. Gervickas and V. Stasevicius, Grand Rapids, Mich., etc., have already contributed.

In the name of the Lithuanian Science Society of Vilnus, I extend my profound thanks to all the above-mentioned donors.
