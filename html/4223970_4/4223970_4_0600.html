The Lithuanian Alliance of America, Branch 36, has authorized K. Satkauskas and Motiejus Bleizgis to collect money for the national museum of Vilnius [Vilna]. Mr. Satkauskas will take charge of the collection in Chicago, and M. Bleizgis in South Chicago, Gary, Indiana Harbor, and other Chicago suburbs where Lithuanians are located. The honesty of the men selected is guaranteed by V. Paplauskas, owner of the building at 922 West 35th Place.
