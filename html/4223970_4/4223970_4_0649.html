On Christmas Day at the home of Mr. Kulesius, Mrs. Anastazia Kulesius suggested making a collection for the revolutionary movement in Russia. The guests agreed and a collection of $4.50 was made.

On the second Christmas Day at the house of Mr. Marsunas, at his son Stephen's name's day celebration, a collection gave $2.50. Mr. M. G. Valaskas gave $1.25. Total $8.05. The money was sent to the revolutionary fund.

Bleka. The Fin.
