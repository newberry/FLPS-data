The Association of Chicago Lithuanian Dramatic Societies sent 272 Rubles on January 12 to the Lithuanian Educational Society in Vilna, Lithuania, for the relief of war refugees. This sum was realized from two benefit affairs held by the Association on December 19 and 20 at Olsevskis hall in the new Milda Theatre building, 31st and South Halsted Streets. The following plays were staged at these affairs: "Uzburtas Kunigaikstis" (The Enchanted Duke), and "Gaires" (Signs). Detailed financial statements of these affairs will be published later.
