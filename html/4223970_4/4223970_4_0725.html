One of the most important problems of the Lithuanian public is to help the poor Lithuanian young students, because every nation must have its own national leaders in various branches of science. The Daumantas Society exists in Europe to help the poor Lithuanian students. The majority of the Lithuanians in America do not know about this society, nor do they know where to send the money donated for that cause. For this reason donations are coming slowly for the help of the students in their higher education.

The American Lithuanians should organize a Daumantas branch to collect 2 the money, in order to help our students in Europe, where they are seeking higher education.

I don't want to believe that there are in America such Lithuanians as would refuse to donate for the poor students who are striving to gain education. I am promising to donate a few cents every month to the poor students fund. I believe that many of our compatriots will support this great cause and donate as much as they can for this noble work. By the way, in America there is a clerical society, Motinele (The Little Mother), the aim of which is to help the students. But unfortunately, we cannot come to any agreement with the clergy as to the helping of the students. The clergy do not take into the Motinele Society those who can do their own thinking; they only accept blind fanatics, and they support clerical students only. From such clerical students the nation will have no benefit. The clergy want to support their students in order to have more exponents of their own class, because the clergy hate and despise the civil intelligentsia.
