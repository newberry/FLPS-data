Lodge 194 of the Lithuanian Alliance is located in Cicero. Anyone may join it--man, woman, or child. At its last meeting the Lodge donated five dollars from its meager treasury to the cause of Lithuanian independence, and the members added $22.60 more. The money and the names of the donors have been sent to the organ of the Lithuanian Alliance of America, the Tevyne.

The Lodge also selected a committee of three members to solicit donations for the Lithuanian cause. It is believed that this committee will be able to do a great deal. Lietuva will undoubtedly help us in this matter. We have only to work together, in perfect harmony, and our efforts will be more successful than they have been in the past.
