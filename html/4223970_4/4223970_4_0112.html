Last Sunday, on Bridgeport, there was a consecration of the new Lithuanian church. The church was consecrated by the Polish priest Brazinski. He was assisted by four Lithuanian priests. The rector Kraucszunas, his assistant and two priests Peza and Jaksztis, were the assistants. The Rev. Jaksztis from Worcester, Miss., delivered the sermon.

The new church will be three times larger than the old one.
