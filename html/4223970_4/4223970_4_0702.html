At the meeting of Mr. J. Mockus on January 29, Mr. M. Janasievicz sponsored the collection for Lithuanian affairs. He collected $4.25. The money is donated to our brothers who are at present fighting in the revolutionary ranks against the Russian despotic tsar.

I wish that all the Lithuanians would follow this example to help our brothers to go forward with the revolution in Russia.

J . Ilgandas

3108 S. Halsted St.

Chicago, Illinois.
