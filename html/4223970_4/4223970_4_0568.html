Many Chicago Lithuanian societies are making substantial donations from their treasuries for the Lithuanian Day (Tag Day) on November 1, 1916.

Some of the societies that have already made donations are: the Grand Duke Algirdas of Lithuania Society, fifty dollars; the Farmers of Lithuania Society, $392.96, the proceeds from the tenth anniversary commemoration of the Society; the Lithuanian Workers' and Citizensâ€™ Benefit Club, ten dollars from its treasury and $14.35 raised by a collection among the members, making a total of $24.35; the Association of Lithuanian Brothers, twenty-five dollars; the United Lithuanian Societies of the West Side, five hundred dollars. This is the largest donation thus far. In addition, member societies of this Union are making individual donations.
