At its meeting held last Friday night, members of the local White Rose Club decided to make a donation to the cause of Lithuanian independence. The total donated was $152.75.

After the Club's business was finished, Mr. L. Paukstis moved that a donation be made to the cause of Lithuanian liberty, and Mr. L. Gelezinis suggested that the Club donate the hundred-dollar Liberty bond which was in its treasury. Mr. Gelezinis' suggestion was approved by the other members present. Then several good patriots took up a collection among the members and thus increased the donation to the amount mentioned above.
