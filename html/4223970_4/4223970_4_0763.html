Simonas Lelesius, 50 years old, died in the People's Hospital January 20. He immigrated here from the village of Gudines, Mariampoles County, District of Suvalku. He lived in America thirty years. It is said that he was the first Lithuanian to have arrived in Chicago. In the early days of the newspaper Lietuva, the late Lelesius was one of its staff. He was not married.
