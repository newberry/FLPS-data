April 30, the Lithuanian Theatrical Society, Ruta-Rues, presented a two-act comedy, "The Warden's Matchmaking," at Hull House Theater.

After the performance, the Ruta Choir, under the direction of Mr. Lankelis, sang several songs. The public liked the girls' quartet best.

The Ruta Society wants to establish a library in the vicinity of 18th street and Union Avenue. Tue Ruta Society was organized by young Lithuanians, in order to bring cultural activity to the young and the old Lithuanians of this vicinity.

The Ruta Society holds its meetings every Thursday, 7:15 P.M. at the Baravicius Hall, 17th and Canal street. All Lithuanians are invited to join.
