On August 29, 30, and 31, the Lithuanian Catholic Students'Association of America will hold its second convention in Chicago. The students and the public are impatiently awaiting this celebration, which will be held at St. George's Parish Hall, in Bridgeport.

The convention will begin on August 29, at 9 A.M., with a mass at St. George's Church.

Students and guests who will take part in the convention, should please be on time to hear the mass.

During the three evenings of the convention, the students will give performances in St. George's Parish Hall, 32nd Place and Auburn Avenue.
