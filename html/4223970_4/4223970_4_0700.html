Mr. A. Balciunas has collected from the Chicago Lithuanians the sum of $15.25.

Friends! Take this as an example and try your best to make collections in other cities for the suffering victims who have fought for our fatherland Lithuania.

Remember that during this revolution many families have lost their bread-winners. Many are dying of starvation at home; others are dying in prison. By contributing each of us a few cents, the many donations will make a large sum of money, even though each has donated only a few cents.

2

This will be a great help to the martyrs. Let us show that we love our fatherland, that we are striving for the liberty of our nation! Let us prove to be loyal children of our former dukes Keistutis and Vytantas! Let us help the martyrs of the revolutionary movement in Lithuania!

All the contributions and the names of the contributors will be published in Lietuva and in the Workers’ Voice.

To both the collector and the contributors to this donation, in the name of the Workers' Voice, I am extending hearty thanks.

J. Ilgandas

Representative of the Workers’ Voice

3108 S. Halsted St., Chicago.
