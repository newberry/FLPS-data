As announced last spring, there is a movement being made by Direct Echoes to bring closer together the various clubs of young people.

Although we did not take any definite steps during the summer months, we still have this movement on our program, and we shall begin to act now.

At present we are corresponding with and discussing our plan with the following well-known Lithuanian young folks’ clubs:

2 Birute, a choral club, which claims a high percentage of young folks in its membership; Golden Stars, a very active men's club, featuring sports; Kulturos Ratelis (Cultural Circle), more popularly known as the K R's, whose membership consists of a limited number of Lithuanian young women; Lambda Gamma Kappa, an enterprising group of wide awake young men; Ruta Gay Girls, representing the younger set of young women; Marquette Maroons, a branch of the Lithuanian alliance of America organization, a young folks' group that is always doing something.

We should like to hear from any other Lithuanian young folks’ club or associations.

We shall soon arrange a get-together.
