We read in the newspapers the appeal of Meszkutis to the American Lithuanians for help. Brother patriots! We, the Westville Lithuanians, got together a few men and decided to make donations to our brother in Siberia. Also we elected Mr. A. Olszewski as secretary. We believe that there will be more collections made for our patriots in Siberia.

Our men donated $3.75; two girls gave 25 cents each, total $4.25. It seems to us that our girls began to understand the patriotic work; they do not want to stay behind the men. For this our girls are worthy of praise.

A. A. Apanaitis

Westville, Illinois.
