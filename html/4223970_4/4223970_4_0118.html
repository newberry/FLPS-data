Last Sunday, Aug. 27, the Society of the Province of God had its banquet. We are proud to say that the banquet was very successful.

The new Pulaski Hall, 800 S. Ashland avenue was packed with a Lithuanian and Polish audience. The music, under the direction of Mr. Hens, was fine. There were many young people at the dance.

The Society of the Province of God never thought that this banquet would be such a success. A profit was made of over $100.00.

Therefore, all Lithuanians should belong to the Catholic societies, as they benefit all of us.
