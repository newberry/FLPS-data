....Members of this organization feel the loss of Aldona Gulbin, President, who resigned from the L.J.L. at a meeting held Sept. 29th because her work does not allow her to attend meetings regularly. She will be missed, for she put all her efforts into building up Lithuanian Junior League to what it is today. Her resignation caused the following changes in officers: President Dan Dambrauskus, Vice-president Ignatius Nestokus, while other officers remain the same. Many thanks to those who attended our social last week. All present, I was told enjoyed themselves. A committee has already been appointed to arrange a gala Halloween party, date of which will be announced in the near future. We invite members of other organizations to attend our affairs. We in turn will attend yours.
