Let us stop and think upon this question: To what sort of high schools should we send our children as soon as they complete their elementary course?

To give you a general reply does not seem to be such an easy task because the answer would not please everybody. There are certain circumstances which everybody should consider before deciding upon this matter so important to your children.

The child, after completing his elementary course, has the opportunity to choose from a great selection, that is, private institutions, public high schools, parochial, also a vast number of colleges.

We recommend for the parents and guardians to send their children to Polish schools after completing their grade school.
