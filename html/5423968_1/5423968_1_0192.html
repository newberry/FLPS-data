Ella Flagg Young, the superintendent of schools, notified the Polish National Allian that the Polish language will be taught in every high-school in Chicago having a minimum group of 20 students who wish to learn the language.

At the present time, the Tuley High School class is in the process of organization. All students interested in enrolling should apply at the principal's office of any high school in Chicago, between 8 and 12 a.m. and from 2 to 5 p.m.
