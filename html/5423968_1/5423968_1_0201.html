The women's department of the Polish National Alliance is very active. The summer schools organized by this department are meeting with great success and have an enrollment of 200 children.

Mothers who understand the advantage of having their children learn the Polish language, are sending their children to these schools willingly. The term is six weeks; three hours daily with a 15 minute recess. The classes are divided into two grades, in the lower they are instructed in reading and writing, and in the higher history, geography and Polish literature are taught.
